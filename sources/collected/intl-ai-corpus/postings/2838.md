## Description complète

Started in 2016, FreedomCare (FC) is a Healthcare company
that is innovating and revolutionizing the way home care is
delivered. We bring dignity and support to patients needing
care in their homes and to the caregivers who care for them.
We’re the largest consumer-directed home care agency in NY
State, and are continually expanding our areas of focus
including operations in other states and tech-based
innovations.

We pride ourselves on our values which drive the level of
care that we deliver to our patients:

- Here For You (An attitude of service, empathy, and
  availability)
- Own It (Drive and ownership)
- Do the Right Thing (High integrity)
- Be Positive (Great attitude and a can-do positive approach
  to challenges)

Join our team and make a positive impact on the lives of
others! We are looking for an Assistant Manager of Training
Delivery & Excellence for our People & Culture team.

Must be willing to travel: This role is remote and requires
occasional travel to any FreedomCare location nationally
when necessary.

Department & Position Overview:

As we scale, we’re focused on improving how work gets done
across operations and service delivery.  AI will play a key
role in reducing manual effort, improving decision making,
and driving more consistent outcomes across these workflows.
FreedomCare is a healthcare organization operating in a
highly regulated environment, how we design and apply AI
must also align with compliance, accuracy, and auditability
requirements.  We’re looking for a highly technical
individual contributor to own how AI is applied across the
business.

The AI Prompt Engineer will focus on designing, building and
optimizing prompts, agents and AI driven workflows
implemented at the company.  You will work across teams to
integrate AI into real business processes to streamline
processes, improve output quality, and reduce operational
friction.  This hands-on role is expected to both build and
improve solutions.

Everyday You Will:

Apply AI to Core Business Workflows

- Identify and implement AI solutions across key areas
- Improve how teams handle high volume, repetitive tasks by
  embedding AI into existing processes
- Partner with business leaders to prioritize and solve
  operational bottlenecks

Prompt Engineering & AI Optimization

- Design, test and refine prompts to drive accurate,
  consistent, and high-quality outputs from AI systems
- Build and optimize AI agents and copilots used by internal
  teams
- Analyze output quality and continuously iterate to improve
  performance, reliability, and alignment with business
  needs

Build & Integrate Solutions

- Develop tools, automations and systems to support AI-driven
  workflows
- Integrate AI into existing platforms and tools using APIs
  and scripting
- Connect data sources and systems to enable end-to-end
  automation

Drive Measurable Impact

- Define and track metrics to evaluate AI output quality and
  workflow performance (i.e. turnaround time, accuracy,
  throughout and team efficiency)
- Analyze results to identify gaps and continuously improve
  prompts, agents and workflows
- Quantify impact of AI solutions and communicate results to
  stakeholders
- Ensure solutions are scalable, reliable, and aligned with
  compliance requirements

Enable Adoption Across Teams

- Partner closely with cross-functional teams to effectively
  implement solutions
- Train teams on how to use AI tools and embed them into daily
  workflows
- Document best practices and standardize how AI is used
  across the business

What Success Looks Like

- You’ve identified and implemented high impact AI use cases
  within core workflows, reducing manual effort or
  turnaround time
- Teams are effectively using AI-enabled workflows you’ve
  built, with clear improvement in output quality and
  consistency
- You’ve established baseline metrics (i.e. accuracy,
  turnaround time, throughout) and can clearly demonstrate
  where
- AI is driving improvements AI outputs are not only faster,
  but reliable and consistent within a regulated
  environment, minimizing rework and downstream issues
- You’ve created a framework to approach identifying, testing,
  and scaling AI use cases across the business

Ideal Candidate will Possess

- 5+ years of experience in software engineering, automation
  or technical solution roles
- Hands-on experience applying AI or LLMs to real world
  workflows (not just experimentation)
- Strong technical skills (Python, APIs, scripting, system
  integrations)
- Experience building internal tools, automations, or workflow
  solutions
- Ability to analyze outputs systematically improve
  performance
- Experience working with high-volume operations

Nice to Have

- Experience with automation platforms
- Familiarity with AI agents, copilots, or workflow
  orchestration tools
- Experience working in healthcare, service operations, or
  other regulated environments
