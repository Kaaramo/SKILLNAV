## Description complète

About HASH

At HASH, we're building infrastructure to solve information
failure and help everybody make the right decisions. Our
open-source platform helps firms integrate both structured
and unstructured information into knowledge graphs that
support simulating, optimizing and automating processes.

We’re building towards world models from a radically
different perspective to most AI labs. Our founders include
Joel Spolsky (former founder of Stack Overflow; acq. $1.8bn,
and Trello; acq. $425m), Jude Allred (former CTO of Glitch)
and Dei Vilkinsons (former CEO of Soho Strategy).

About the role

In the last 6 months we've seen significant growth and are
now scaling fast. We're hiring AI Solution Engineers in
London and Berlin to help build real-world applications for
our customers utilising HASH's technology.

This is a highly technical, customer-facing role for someone
who enjoys operating at the intersection of AI, systems
thinking, and problem-solving. You’ll work closely with
customers to understand their goals, translate complex
business problems into robust technical solutions, and help
them realize value quickly.

Requirements

- Strong technical ability, with experience shipping software,
  or building AI systems in production: prior AI/ML
  engineering, data engineering, applied AI, or product
  engineering experience is a hard requirement
- Ability to work directly with customers and own complex
  problem-solving end to end: you'll interface with external
  stakeholders and will need to draw upon social
  intelligence, business context, and technical insight in
  your work — demonstrating clear, structured communication.
- Strong judgment, high agency, and a bias toward execution:
  comfort operating in fast-moving environments with a high
  degree of ambiguity
- Genuine interest in helping customers succeed, not just
  shipping technology: results-oriented and impact-
  motivated; many of our engagements are priced based on the
  results we deliver, as is compensation in this role.

What you'll do

In essence, you'll talk to customers, act as a technical
architect, and write code / develop solutions yourself (both
solo and within a wider team):

- Partner with customers to understand their workflows, data,
  and operational challenges
- Design and build AI-powered solutions using HASH, especially
  for enterprise use cases
- Translate ambiguous business problems into structured
  technical plans and deployable systems
- Work across knowledge graphs, structured and unstructured
  data, LLM-based workflows, and automation
- Lead technical discovery, solution design, implementation,
  and rollout with customer stakeholders
- Act as a trusted advisor to customer teams, from operators
  and technical teams through to executives
- Work closely with product, engineering, and research to
  shape the platform based on real customer needs
- Help define repeatable solution patterns, delivery
  playbooks, and best practices as we scale

Why HASH

- Work on first-principles AI research tied to a large and
  important real-world mission
- Join at a moment of rapid growth, with outsized scope and
  influence
- Help build a leading frontier AI lab in Europe with global
  ambition
- Work alongside founders who have previously built and exited
  businesses worth billions of dollars
- Be part of a team that moves fast, is enthusiastically open
  source, and is in it for the long-term

Benefits

Our team have founded and sold billion-euro companies from
scratch. We offer leading equity-weighted total
compensation, including competitive salaries and tax-
advantaged options.

We meet together quarterly in-person for whole-team
retreats, provide engineers with generous learning and tech
budgets, and recognise and reward output.

This role is primarily bonus/equity incentivized and pays
£70,000-£120,000 base.
