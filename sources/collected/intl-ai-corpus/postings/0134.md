## Description complète

Legal teams are drowning in repetitive work. The rest of the
business is stuck waiting. Flank is changing that.

We are operating at the cutting edge of AI development,
building the future of autonomous agents for the enterprise.
We have raised a $10M Series A led by Insight Partners (US),
with continued support from Gradient Ventures (Google’s AI
fund), HV Capital, and Speedinvest (EU).

This isn't a "nice-to-have" tool for small startups. We are
already deploying Flank agents to multinational enterprises
with 100,000+ employees, completely automating workflows
that were previously impossible to solve. Our agents work
24/7, operating under supervision and within guardrails
customers define, and routing only exceptions to legal for
review. Every request and action is transparent & auditable,
ensuring customers have full visibility over their automated
workforce.

We’re seeking an experienced AI Engineer to drive our
applied AI initiatives.

We’re a fast-moving start-up where you’ll work closely with
product, design, and commercial teams to ship real AI into
production — not just research experiments.

You will be tasked with leveraging emerging technologies and
a team of legal experts to improve the intelligence and
autonomy of our agents.

This might start with building an evals framework in
collaboration with our team of lawyers, it might spawn a
hypervisor that governs every step of our agentic system and
that “knows what good looks like”, and it might end with us
building the first alignment program for legal AI systems.

What you’ll be doing:

- Drive Applied Research: Drive applied research into LLMs and
  agentic systems, rapidly prototyping, validating, and
  shipping production-ready capabilities.
- Stay Ahead of the Curve: Stay hands-on with the latest
  advances in foundation models, LLM-as-a-judge, agentic

  into the product.

  - Experience with deep learning frameworks (PyTorch,

  - Hands-on experience deploying ML/LLM models in production

  - Solid understanding of data pipelines, APIs, and distributed

  - Knowledge of prompt engineering, RAG, fine-tuning, and
    embeddings for LLMs.
  - Experience with cloud platforms (AWS, GCP, or Azure) and
