## Description complète

About Skiffra

Skiffra is building the intelligence layer for the physical
world. We translate complex, real-world environments into
clear, actionable data. While most AI companies focus on
digital industries, we design AI native orchestration
systems for the sectors that extract, move, and build
ecosystems around us. We turn messy, high stakes operations
into intelligent, adaptive workflows that improve decisions
in real time. Our first proving ground is mining and natural
resources, but the platform is modular by design and built
to scale into any industry where decisions are expensive and
the consequences are real.

The Role

As a Founding AI Engineer, you will be the architect of our
core intelligence. This is a role for a high-leverage
builder who can move fluidly between prompt engineering,
backend systems, and model optimization. You will design the
"brain" of Skiffra, moving agentic workflows from
whiteboards to production-grade systems that handle real
enterprise data at scale. This is a founding-level role for
builders who value shipping over dogma and real-world impact
over demos.

This role will be based in the San Francisco Bay Area or Los
Angeles, CA.

What You’ll Do

- Architect & Build Agentic Systems: Design, prototype, and
  harden production-grade orchestration frameworks that
  leverage LLMs, persistent memory, and tool-integration to
  navigate complex, non-linear enterprise tasks
- Build the Data Flywheel: Implement sophisticated RAG
  (Retrieval-Augmented Generation) pipelines, ensuring high-
  fidelity context retrieval from fragmented data sources
- Evaluate & Iterate: Build internal benchmarking and
  evaluation suites to measure and improve model
  reliability, grounding, and latency
- Strategic Modeling: Architect high-performance intelligence
  stacks by making pragmatic trade-offs between Frontier
  Reasoning Models (GPT-5, Claude 4 Opus) for long-horizon
  agentic planning, Fine-tuned Open-Weight Models (Llama 4,
  Mistral 3) for data sovereignty and low-latency inference,
  and Deterministic Heuristics for reliable, cost-efficient
  guardrails
- Full-Stack Ownership: Work directly with founders to
  translate ambiguous operational "messes" into clean,
  scalable, AI-powered software
- Cross-Collaboration: Partner with product and engineering to
  turn ambiguity into working software

What We’re Looking For

- 7+ years of Systems Engineering: You have a deep background
  in building production software. You understand that "AI"
  is only as good as the infrastructure surrounding it
- Modern AI Fluency: Deep practical experience with the
  transformer-based stack: embeddings, vector databases,
  tool-calling, and context window management
- Python Power-User: You are comfortable building entire
  systems, not just experimenting in notebooks
- Reliability Mindset: You have strong instincts for failure
  modes (hallucinations, API timeouts, "infinite loops" in
  agents) and how to build guardrails against them
- Clear communicator with founder-level ownership mindset

Nice to Have

- Experience in building complex, multi-step workflows using
  LangGraph, LlamaIndex, and CrewAI
- Hands-on experience with Fine-tuning and PEFT (LoRA/QLoRA)
  to adapt open-weight models for domain-specific tasks
- A background in Enterprise Operations (Industrial,
  Logistics, Fintech, or CRM workflows).
- Strong opinions on where AI creates value and where it’s
  just expensive noise

How We Work

We value speed, clarity, and technical rigor. We favor
simple systems over clever ones, prototypes over over-
planning, and ownership over handoffs. You’ll work directly
with founders and ship things that matter quickly.

Location & Work Model

This is a hybrid role with regular in-person collaboration
in Los Angeles or the San Francisco Bay Area. We are not a
remote-only company.

Work Authorization

Candidates must be authorized to work in the United States.
We are not able to offer visa sponsorship for this role.
