## Description complète

Founding AI Engineer

Location: Europe (Fully Remote) Compensation: €80K–€120K +
up to 4% equity Stage: Early-stage startup — Founding team
role

About our Client

Our client is on a mission to revolutionize enterprise
software efficiency — not by iterating on existing systems
but by transcending them with seamless, intelligent AI
workforce automation. Our platform replaces fragmented
systems with a unified ecosystem of AI Agents and AI Teams
that execute tasks cross-functionally, dramatically cutting
tech and operational waste (by 30–40% within just 4–12
weeks)

Backed by luminaries across AI and enterprise, we’re
building the future of work: autonomous, scalable, and
profoundly impactful.

Role Overview

We’re looking for a Founding AI Engineer—a full-stack
Python-savvy AI practitioner who thrives building next-gen
AI applications. You'll work at the crossroads of
engineering, product, and vision, shaping how our platform
empowers businesses with AI that acts, thinks, and executes.

In this role, you will:

- Architect and build AI Agent and multi-agent systems using
  LLMs and Python-based frameworks.
- Develop full-stack features—from model orchestration to
  production deployment.
- Own and shape technical direction alongside leadership, with
  tremendous scope and autonomy.
- Ensure that our platform delivers instant ROI and
  transformative efficiency to enterprises.

What You Bring

Must-Have

- Solid engineering experience (2–5+ years) with strengths in
  Python and full-stack development.
- Hands-on experience building AI agents, multi-agent systems,
  or LLM-powered workflows.
- Familiarity with deployment tooling, orchestration, model
  integration, or RAG-style pipelines.
- Excitement for solving tough problems in fast-paced, early-
  stage environments.

Nice-to-Have

- Experience with AI marketplaces, “AI App Stores,” or
  generative agent frameworks.
- Previous exposure to agent orchestration, prompt
  engineering, or evaluation frameworks.
- Startup background, ideally with roles that combined
  engineering and product leadership.

Why Join?

- Significant Equity (~4%) in an early-stage startup — you're
  joining early, owning impact.
- Radical Mission — we're not just optimizing; we're
  redefining enterprise software completely.
- Autonomy + Influence — you'll shape architecture, product,
  and deployment strategy.
- Remote-friendly in Europe, with a passionate, mission-
  aligned leadership team.
