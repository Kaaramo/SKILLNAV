About Censia

This is an opportunity to join a rapidly growing startup
with an incredible foundation and huge ambitions. Censia is
an AI-powered Talent Intelligence Platform that organizes
talent data, enabling enterprises to make unbiased,
predictive, and intelligent decisions about talent
acquisition and talent management. Our solutions extend
across the entire talent lifecycle, shifting the focus from
manual, error-prone processes to automated, predictive
intelligence. We believe there is a better way to make
talent decisions and that AI will disrupt all aspects of
Human Capital. Come join us and be part of the next
generation of Talent Intelligence.

Role Overview We are seeking a versatile Full-Stack Engineer
with expertise in Node.js, React, and experience in AI and
Large Language Models (LLMs). This role involves developing
scalable applications that enhance our talent intelligence
platform, contributing to both front-end and back-end
development, and integrating AI-driven features. Key
Responsibilities

- Design, develop, and maintain robust front-end applications
  using React.
- Build and optimize back-end services and APIs with Node.js
  and Express.
- Integrate AI and LLM capabilities into the platform to
  enhance functionality.
- Collaborate with cross-functional teams to define, design,
  and ship new features.
- Ensure the responsiveness and performance of applications
  across devices.
- Implement security best practices and data protection
  measures.
- Participate in code reviews, testing, and debugging to
  maintain high-quality code.

Qualifications

- Bachelor’s degree in Computer Science, Engineering, or a
  related field.
- Proven experience as a Full-Stack Engineer or similar role.
- Proficiency in front-end technologies: React, HTML, CSS,
  JavaScript.
- Strong back-end development skills with Node.js and Express.
- Experience with AI and LLMs, including integration of models
  like GPT.
- Familiarity with databases such as MongoDB or PostgreSQL.
- Understanding of RESTful APIs and web services.
- Excellent problem-solving skills and attention to detail.
- Strong communication skills and ability to work in a
  collaborative environment.

Preferred Qualifications

- Experience with cloud platforms like AWS .
- Knowledge of containerization tools such as Docker.
- Familiarity with CI/CD pipelines and DevOps practices.
- Understanding of data privacy regulations and compliance.
