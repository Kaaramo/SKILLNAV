## Description complète

About this role

Role Brief:

Are you interested in building AI systems that fundamentally
transform how software is engineered in a global financial
institution? Do you like working at the speed of a startup
while solving highly sophisticated, enterprise-scale
problems in a highly complex environment?

At BlackRock, we are seeking a VP of AI Platform Engineering
to lead the design and delivery of enterprise AI
capabilities that power technologist enablement, and next-
generation platform services. This leader will combine deep
technical expertise in modern AI systems with strong
engineering execution and organizational leadership. We
recognize that strength comes from diversity, and will
embrace your unique skills, curiosity, and passion while
giving you the opportunity to grow technically and as an
individual. With over USD $11 trillion of assets we have an
exceptional responsibility, our technology empowers millions
of investors to save for retirement, pay for college, buy a
home, and improve their financial well-being. As an AI
engineering leader at BlackRock, you will help define how AI
is safely, securely, and effectively embedded into our
engineering ecosystem at scale.

About the Team:

Our Platform Engineering division builds and operates the
foundation system that power Aladdin and BlackRock’s
enterprise technology landscape. We are leading BlackRock’s
evolution toward an AI-native engineering organization. We
are fundamentally reimagining how software is built,
reviewed, tested, modernized, and operated through
intelligent systems.

The AI Platform Engineering team is not simply integrating
AI into existing workflows; we are redesigning those
workflows altogether. The team drives step-change
improvements in developer enablement, large-scale
modernization, and enterprise AI governance. We operate at
the intersection of AI innovation and enterprise-grade
reliability, shaping the future of engineering across
thousands of developers and multiple business domains. The
team defines what AI-powered engineering looks at scale
inside one of the world’s most sophisticated financial
technology environments.

Responsibilities:

- Design scalable AI architecture leveraging LLMs, RAG, fine-
  tuning, and model optimization techniques
- Establish evaluation frameworks to measure model quality,
  hallucination control, and precision/recall trade-offs
- Lead the design and implementation of scalable AI platform
  capabilities used across the software development
  lifecycle
- Partner with product and security teams to ensure AI
  solutions are safe, compliant, and production‑ready
- Drive delivery through ambiguity by breaking down complex
  problems into clear plans and milestones
- Lead buy vs. build decisions, evaluating third‑party
  solutions alongside internal capabilities
- Identify technical risks and dependencies early, with
  mitigation and escalation where needed
- Coordinate across multiple engineering teams and time zones
  to deliver shared outcomes
- Translate complex AI capabilities into clear business values
  and communicate progress, trade‑offs, and risks to senior
  stakeholders
- Lead and develop a team of engineers and senior ICs,
  providing coaching and technical mentorship
- Foster a culture of ownership, accountability, and
  continuous improvement
- Support the growth of emerging leaders by delegating
  responsibility and increasing scope over time

Requirements:

- Strong experience building and operating production AI
  systems, including LLM‑based workflows
- Solid understanding of modern AI practices such as prompt
  engineering, retrieval‑augmented generation, fine‑tuning,
  and evaluation
- Proven experience designing and delivering shared platforms
  or services at scale
- Deep technical fluency in Java and Python with hands-on
  experience building scalable, production-grade AI and
  platform services
- Experience designing APIs and operating services in a cloud
  environment
- Experience working with distributed Cloud environments
- Strong communication skills and the ability to collaborate
  effectively across teams
- Experience working in Agile development teams.

Nice to Have

- Experience working on developer productivity or platform
  engineering initiatives
- Prior people‑management experience, including leading small
  to mid‑sized engineering teams
- Familiarity with enterprise governance, security, and risk
  considerations for AI systems

culture of collaboration and apprenticeship that enriches

model to accelerate your learning and onboarding experience
here at BlackRock.
