Our vision is to transform how the world uses information to

- AI Solution Development: Build and deploy AI-driven tools

- Integration & Enablement: Connect AI agents with enterprise

- Process Automation: Identify repetitive tasks and design
  intelligent automation solutions to improve efficiency.

- Collaboration & Training: Partner with cross-functional

- Governance & Compliance: Collaborate with cybersecurity
  teams to enforce data protection, threat modeling and e
  nsure all AI solutions adhere to security, ethical, and
  compliance standards.

- Security & Identity Integration

- Ensure secure agent interactions using encryption,
  authentication, and authorization mechanisms.

- Integrate agents with identity platforms (e.g., OAuth2,
  OIDC, SAML, Entra ID, AD FS, etc.) for secure access
  control.

- Continuous Improvement: Collect feedback, iterate solutions,
  and contribute to a culture of innovation and scalability.

Required Skills & Qualifications:

- Bachelor's or master's degree in computer science , AI,
  Machine Learning, Data Science or related field.

- 3 years to 6 years of experience in software development or
  AI/ML engineering.

- Strong understanding of AI concepts , machine learning
  pipelines , and automation tools .

- Hands-on experience or coursework in Copilot Studio , UiPath
  , and SharePoint .

- Knowledge with cloud AI platforms: Azure AI Foundry, AWS
  Bedrock, and/or Google Vertex AI .

- Knowledge with scalable context management solutions
  including retrieval-augmented generation (RAG ), semantic
  chunking, and memory persistence for long-horizon tasks

- Implement multi-agent workflows for complex tasks such as

  solving.

- Ability to design, implement, and manage context-aware
  systems that leverage Model Context Protocol (MCP) to
  maintain continuity across user interactions, including
  prompt engineering, memory management, and context window
  optimization for large language models.

- Familiarity with cloud platforms , API integrations , and
  data quality validation .

- Familiarity with agent orchestration frameworks and multi-
  agent systems using A2A (Agent to Agent).

- Knowledge of common development languages

- .Net , C#, Python, Java, API development, SQL, PowerShell,
  Unix shell scripting

- Excellent problem-solving skills and ability to work in
  fast-paced, agile environments.

- Strong communication skills for cross-team collaboration.

Preferred Attributes:

- Exposure to generative AI , prompt engineering , or agent-
  based systems .

- Interest in AI governance, security, and ethical frameworks.

- Ability to adapt quickly and deliver "quick wins" while
