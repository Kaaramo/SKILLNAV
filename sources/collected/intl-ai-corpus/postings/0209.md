## Description complète

Manifest is seeking an experienced Senior Backend Engineer -
AI with a passion for building high-performance, intelligent
systems that power modern AI-driven platforms. In this role,
you’ll lead the design and scaling of backend services and
data pipelines to support advanced analytics, machine
learning workflows, and intelligent automation across the
platform. You’ll work in a fast-paced, data-intensive
environment where your contributions will have immediate and
significant impact. The impact you will have:

- Architect and implement AI-native backend infrastructure,
  including scalable data pipelines and intelligent services
- Partner with AI/ML teams to bring machine learning models
  and LLMs into production at scale
- Design resilient, high-throughput systems that enable
  intelligent workflows and automated decision-making
- Influence product direction and system design with a strong
  backend perspective on AI capabilities
- Contribute to a culture of innovation, performance, and
  reliability across the engineering team

As a Senior Backend Engineer - AI, you will:

- Build infrastructure that powers real-time AI applications
  and intelligent APIs
- Develop high-throughput systems that support AI/ML workloads
  and model inference pipelines
- Collaborate with cross-functional teams (ML engineers,
  product, business development) to bring intelligent
  features to production
- Design modular, scalable architectures that support AI
  system evolution over time
- Contribute to performance monitoring, testing, and
  reliability best practices in AI systems
- Help maintain a culture of engineering excellence,
  mentorship, and technical ownership

What it is essential that you will bring to the team:

- 8+ years of backend software engineering experience,
  preferably with AI/ML or data-intensive systems
- Proven success scaling distributed systems that support
  large-scale inference, data processing, or intelligent
  services
- Deep understanding of data pipelines, API design, and
  backend architecture for AI applications
- Experience collaborating with machine learning or data
  science teams to productize models
- Ability to learn new technologies quickly, with a focus on
  building systems that enable AI innovation
- Strong communication and cross-team collaboration skills

Bonus but not required:

- Experience deploying or scaling LLMs, fine-tuning models, or
  integrating generative AI into products
- Awareness of modern tooling and platforms supporting AI/ML
  (e.g., LangChain, Hugging Face, Ray, etc.)
- Experience with Typescript, Python, and Go
- Experience with SQL and NoSQL databases
