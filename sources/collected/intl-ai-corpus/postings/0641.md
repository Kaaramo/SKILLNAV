## Description complète

About Qumis

Qumis is a venture-backed AI startup building the most
advanced platform for reading and interpreting insurance
policies. We help P&C insurance professionals—claims
advocates, brokers, underwriters, and legal teams—move
faster, make better decisions, and reduce reliance on siloed
expertise. Our customers include some of the largest
insurance organizations in the U.S.

We combine legal-grade natural language understanding with
structured analysis and intuitive workflows to modernize one
of the most complex and outdated parts of the insurance
industry: reading the policy. Our vision is a world where
anyone can read any insurance policy like a lawyer.

We’re a small, high-trust team of engineers, designers, and
insurance/legal experts. We move fast, operate with
autonomy, and hold each other to a Michelin-star standard.
We value grit, speed, ownership, clarity of thinking, and
deep curiosity. If you're energized by building at the edge
of AI, transforming massive legacy industries, and working
with people who care deeply—welcome.

About the Role

We're hiring a Founding Staff AI Engineer to build and ship
production-grade AI that powers the core workflows of Qumis.
You’ll partner closely with the CTO and co-founders to
design, implement, and own every part of our AI
pipeline—from unstructured document ingestion to interactive
LLM outputs that shape legal-grade decisions.

It’s a product-critical role for someone who thrives in
ambiguity, ships fast, and is excited to be the technical
owner of a new AI stack inside a real-world, high-stakes
domain.

You’ll play a key role in shaping our architecture, roadmap,
and engineering culture. Your work will touch every
customer, every week.

What You’ll Do

- Build and ship production AI features end-to-end—from R&D to
  SaaS integration, observability, and iteration
- Design and deploy advanced document understanding systems,
  including RAG pipelines, clause segmentation,
  summarization, risk detection, and more
- Create scalable APIs that deliver real-time coverage
  analysis, comparisons, and recommendations inside a modern
  SaaS UI
- Engineer robust data workflows across OCR, PDF ingestion,
  parsing, normalization, and embedding generation
- Own evaluation and observability, from automated accuracy
  pipelines to human-in-the-loop systems that maintain trust
  and reliability
- Design for explainability—develop transparent workflows that
  help legal, claims, and compliance professionals
  understand what the AI sees and says
- Collaborate across disciplines with designers, PMs, and
  domain experts to translate insurance workflows into AI-
  powered systems
- Set technical direction—you’ll shape our infra, tooling, and
  AI product roadmap from the ground up

What We’re Looking For

This role blends the ambition of a research scientist, the
urgency of a product engineer, and the ownership of a
founder. You’ll be our first AI hire, helping architect and
scale our AI systems from the ground up. You should bring
most or all of the following:

- 6–10+ years of experience building and deploying production
  machine learning systems, with a focus on NLP, document
  intelligence, or unstructured data
- Strong track record shipping ML models into production—API
  design, observability, iteration, and scaling included
- Deep experience with RAG pipelines, multi-step LLM
  workflows, and vector databases (e.g., Pinecone, Weaviate,
  Qdrant)
- Comfortable working with complex, messy documents (e.g.,
  insurance policies, contracts), including parsing, OCR,
  clause-level segmentation, and normalization
- Strong software engineering background, with experience in
  fullstack dev, containerization (Docker), and DevOps/MLOps
  best practices
- Ability to design secure, compliant systems in regulated
  environments (insurance, legal, healthcare, or finance)
- Experience architecting agentic workflows and AI-native
  products that go beyond simple chatbot interfaces
- Product-first mindset—you build models to solve real user
  pain, and love working closely with cross-functional teams
- Operates like a founder—driven, hands-on, scrappy, and
  thrives in fast-paced, zero-to-one environments with
  extreme ownership

Why Join Qumis

- Backed by top-tier venture capital funds including Armory
  Square Ventures, MTech Capital, Grand Ventures, and Alumni
  Ventures, and strategic angels in AI and insurance
  including the founder and CEO of Chicago insurtech unicorn
  Kin Insurance
- Used and loved by leading brokerages and claims teams to
  transform how work gets done including multiple top 15
  brokerages with tremendous expansion potential
- Shape the future of how generative AI transforms one of the
  world's most complex, critical industries
- High-ownership role on a high-standards founding team with
  grit, urgency, and big ambition
- Build a generational company—the kind of opportunity that
  only comes once in a career

We've Got You Covered

- Life Insurance (company-paid), plus supplemental options,
