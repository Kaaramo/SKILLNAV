## Description complète

Change the way the world travels

Be part of the GetYourGuide journey and connect people with
unforgettable travel experiences worldwide. Since 2009,
millions of travelers have booked unique activities with us
in over 12,000 cities. Our headquarters in Berlin is
supported by 16 other local offices across the globe.

Ready to join a diverse community of over 850 fellow
explorers dedicated to revolutionizing the travel experience
industry? Check out our careers site to learn more.

Team mission

Since 2020, GetYourGuide has established a Machine Learning
(ML) Platform team, providing the tools and best practices
to help the Data Products team build and maintain
production-ready ML/AI systems. The team's mission is to
enable our diverse teams and ML/AI use-cases via self-
service tools and automation. More recently the team was
renamed as AI Platform Team as a way to better reflect our
ambition to become an AI-first organization.

As a Senior MLOps Engineer, you'll join our AI Platform Team
and have a chance to make an enormous impact across all of
our data products. You'll work closely with our Data
Products teams to build systems that enable ML/AI algorithms
to go to production. If you are interested in contributing
to the success of a product that is used by millions of
travelers around the world then keep on reading.

Your mission:

- You'll design and implement systems and tools for scalable
  and more efficient machine learning solutions.
- You will work to increase the amount of data products in
  production reliably, reduce the time to production, and
  reduce operational costs.
- You'll be partnering/collaborating with product teams to
  bring advanced algorithms into production, following MLOps
  best practices.

Your toolkit:

- Software Engineering: You have strong fundamentals in
  Computer Science and a polyglot mindset. Python is your
  language of choice; you are able to migrate between
  various programming languages though. Delivering clean
  code and investigating new tools, languages, and practices
  is your passion.
- Data Engineering: previous experience in organizing and
  querying data at scale data warehouses, tools like Spark
  are not novelty for you
- Data Science & MLOps: Data Scientists are close stakeholders
  for you. You share a genuine interest in Artificial
  Intelligence and Machine Learning and are proficient in
  applying MLOps best practices as scale.
- Business Experience: You possess the ability to prioritize
  and manage stakeholders; they know you for your good
  communication and collaboration skills.
- Vision: Your ideas turn into reliable and maintainable code.
  A track record of outcome-driven work gives evidence of
  this. You are experienced in operating at scale; you have
  a cloud DevOps / infrastructure mindset.
- Autonomy: Your mindset can be described as autonomous,
  proactive and customer-oriented. You own your projects.
- Teamwork: You strongly believe that a team is more than the
  sum of its members. Mentoring and sharing knowledge are
  fulfilling activities for you.

Extras that give you an edge:

- Prior experience in scale-up environments with various
  technologies
- Interest in LLMs and the new challenges of Generative AI
