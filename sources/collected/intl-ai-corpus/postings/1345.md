Role & Responsibilities

VideoAmp is seeking a Senior AI Data Engineer  to join our
Linear Data Processing (LDP) team. This role focuses on
building production-grade AI and LLM-powered systems
directly into large-scale data pipelines that support
audience measurement, currency, identity, and metadata
workflows across linear and digital media.

You will apply LLMs and classical ML to real-world linear TV
problems: network-specific schedules, hourly viewership
logs, program metadata, and commercial schedules that vary
widely by partner, format, and quality.

- Design and operate AI- and LLM-enabled data pipelines
  processing billions of linear and digital records
- Build and productionize LLM-driven systems for concrete LDP
  problems, including:Network & Program Schedules
- Hourly Viewership Logs
- Commercial & Ad Spot Metadata
- Combine LLMs with rules, heuristics, and ML models to ensure
  deterministic, auditable outcomes
- Own the end-to-end AI lifecycle, including:
- Integrate LLMs responsibly into batch and event-driven LDP
  workflows, balancing accuracy, latency, and cost
- Build human-in-the-loop workflows for high-impact or low-
  confidence cases
- Collaborate closely with Measurement, Identity, Library, and
  Product teams to translate ambiguous data issues into
  scalable AI solutions
- Provide technical leadership through design reviews,
  mentorship, and architectural guidance

What We’re Looking For

- 4+ years of experience in AI/ML engineering, applied ML
  systems, or data engineering
- Demonstrated experience shipping LLM-powered systems into
  production
- Strong understanding of:
- Proficiency in Python and SQL; strong software engineering
  fundamentals
- Experience working with large-scale data platforms and
  distributed systems
- Ability to design AI systems that meet measurement-grade
  reliability, explainability, and auditability requirements

Strong Plus

- Experience working with TV schedules, viewership logs, or

- Familiarity with linear TV measurement or media data

- Experience applying AI in compliance-sensitive or customer-
