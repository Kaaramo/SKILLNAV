## Description complète

About Loop: At Loop, we’re revolutionizing restaurant
operations with AI-powered automation. Our technology helps
restaurants streamline their workflows, reduce costs, and
enhance customer experiences—all through intelligent
automation. We’re a fast-moving, early-stage startup on a
rapid growth trajectory, and we need a technical leader to
build and scale our engineering team from the ground up.

Role Overview:

As a Staff Product Engineer, you will own a broad technical
scope spanning frontend, backend, and AI systems. You’ll
design, build, and scale features across the full product
stack — from user-facing UI to robust backend services and
AI-driven components. You’ll work directly with product,
design, data, and leadership to deliver high-impact
solutions that push our platform forward.

This position is ideal for engineers who love building
across the stack, enjoy solving complex problems, and want
to deeply influence both product direction and technical
architecture.

Key Responsibilities:

- Full-Stack Development: Lead design and implementation of
  scalable frontend architecture using modern web
  technologies. Translate UI/UX designs into responsive,
  accessible, and performant applications. Build and
  maintain backend systems and APIs to power core product
  functionality. Ensure smooth integration between frontend
  interfaces and backend services.
- Architecture & Best Practices: Contribute to high-level
  architecture decisions across the stack.Create scalable,
  maintainable systems with high code quality. Apply
  critical thinking to propose innovative solutions and
  continuously improve technical processes.
- Cross-Functional Collaboration: Partner with product
  managers, designers, and engineers to define and
  prioritize roadmap features. Participate in customer-
  focused discussions to understand pain points and
  translate them into technical solutions. Mentor and guide
  team members, championing best practices and high
  engineering standards.
- AI Systems: Collaborate on AI-driven features, integrating
  models or intelligent workflows into platform
  components.Help design systems that support data
  pipelines, model inference, and AI feedback loops.

Qualifications:

- 6+ years professional experience building production-grade
  software.
- Solid backend experience — building services, APIs, and
  database integrations with Python, Django, or comparable
  stacks.
- Demonstrated ability to work across frontend and backend
  contexts with clean, maintainable code.
- Experience with version control, deployment pipelines, and
  performance optimization.
- A proactive mindset and ability to work independently in a
  fast-paced startup environment.

Nice to Have:

- Experience building or working with AI agentic workflows,
  including orchestration, tool usage, and multi-step
  reasoning systems.
- Strong frontend expertise in React.js, including performance
  optimization and scalable component architecture.
- Ability to think beyond pure engineering — strong business
  and product acumen.
- Demonstrated curiosity and interest in working backwards
  from the customer, identifying real pain points, and
  translating them into technical solutions.
- Passion for building end-to-end features that create
  measurable product impact.

Why Join Us?

- Impact: Drive major product areas from inception to
  production.
- Growth: Learn and contribute to both frontend, backend, and
  AI systems.
- Ownership: Shape architecture and influence product
  strategy.
- Team: Work with passionate engineers and product thinkers
  solving real problems.
