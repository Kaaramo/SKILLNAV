Why you should join us

About the role

Solstice is building the industry’s leading AI platform to
help pharmaceutical brands generate compliant, compelling
content at scale. As our Founding AI Design Engineer, you’ll
be the creative force behind how that content looks, feels,
and communicates visually, bringing together classic design
tools and modern AI workflows to build what’s next in pharma
marketing.

You won’t just be designing assets but you’ll help architect
the creative system, collaborating closely with our
engineering team to build tools that turn human creativity
into scalable, AI-assisted output. From Illustrator and
InDesign to Figma and prompt-based generation, you’ll shape
a platform that fuses the craft of visual design with the
power of automation.

This is a unique opportunity for a multidisciplinary
designer who thrives at the intersection of creativity and
technology. You don’t need to be an engineer, but you should
be deeply curious, imaginative, and fluent in the tools
shaping modern creative workflows.

What you’ll be working on

- Work closely with engineers to shape the underlying system
  that translates visual intent, brand guidelines, and user
  inputs into automated creative output.
- Design visual assets for HCP and DTC audiences using tools
  like Illustrator, InDesign, Photoshop, and Figma,
  including concepts for leave-behinds, social graphics,
  digital ads, and branded materials.
- Contribute to designing UI/UX flows that support prompt-
  driven creative tasks and enable non-designers to create
  on-brand visuals at scale.
- Help shape how LLMs interpret and apply design patterns —
  through feedback, curation, and visual prompt refinement.
- Establish creative standards that balance brand guidelines,
  aesthetic impact, and pharma-specific compliance needs.

You might be a fit if you…

- Have 3+ years of experience in graphic, visual, or brand
  design, ideally at a marketing agency or in-house at a
  healthcare/pharma company.
- Are highly skilled in Illustrator, Photoshop, InDesign, and
  Figma, with an eye for clean, modern, scalable design.
- Have familiarity with HTML (e.g., email layouts, landing
  pages, or understanding design constraints in digital
  environments).
- Have a strong visual portfolio that showcases your ability
  to turn messaging into high-impact visuals across multiple
  formats.
- Are tech-savvy and AI-curious — you're not an engineer, but
  you’re comfortable experimenting with LLMs, creative
  automation, and prompt-driven workflows.
- Love the details — typography, spacing, layout, and the
  emotional feel of visual communication.
- Are excited to build something from 0 → 1, and help define
  what design looks like in the AI era of healthcare.
- Communicate directly and clearly, and thrive in fast-paced,
  collaborative environments.

Bonus points for…

- Experience working with pharmaceutical brands, especially
  with knowledge of MLR processes or regulated design
  constraints.
- Familiarity with prompt engineering or optimizing LLM
  outputs for visual tasks.
- Experience creating visual systems for content at scale —
  whether in marketing, branding, or publishing contexts.
