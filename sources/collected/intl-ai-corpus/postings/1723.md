## Description complète

About us

Visia is revolutionizing the recycled materials supply chain
by providing processors with real-time, actionable insights
into their material streams through our AI-powered platform.
By pairing this intelligence with custom-built change
management, we drive critical outcomes like prohibitive
removal, material audits, and automated KPI tracking. Our
solutions have delivered measurable results, with customers
seeing throughput increases of over 300%. Our mission is to
be the single source of truth for materials in the waste
stream, enabling a transparent, traceable, and automated
supply chain that empowers recyclers to achieve
transformative improvements.

What we're looking for

Join us in revolutionizing the way we approach
sustainability through innovative technology. We're looking
for engineers who share our passion for making a positive
impact on the environment. If you're excited about building
impactful tech from the ground up and being a part of a team
that's making a real difference, this role is for you.

Responsibilities

- Design, develop, and maintain new features across Visia's
  engineering services such as our client-facing web and
  mobile apps, computer vision pipeline, and IoT software
- Maintain system reliability by monitoring, debugging, and
  resolving technical issues to ensure smooth operations
  across multiple platforms
- Collaborate with other engineers to write well-documented
  and maintainable code that supports our mission of
  building technology for a greener future
- Participate in cross-functional team meetings and contribute
  to Visia's overall product roadmap, helping to shape the
  future of recycling and green tech

Who you are

- You have 1+ years of full-time work experience in Software
  Engineering and a passion for using technology to drive
  positive change
- You have strong programming skills in one or more commonly
  used programming languages (we use Python, TypeScript, and
  Go among others)
- You are familiar with or have experience with any of the
  following:
  - Full stack development
  - Web/Mobile app development and design
  - UI/UX best practices
  - Building and scaling data streaming pipelines
  - Integrating with (ingesting → condensing → conveying) large
    amounts of data like images, logs, metrics, alerts, etc.
- Knowledge of MLOps or computer vision techniques is a plus
- You are comfortable working with at least some of the
  following technologies and are eager and able to learn
  others as needed:
  - Mobile/Frontend development (React / React Native or similar
    frameworks)
  - Cloud computing services (Google Cloud Platform, AWS, or
    Azure)
  - CI/CD, IaC and containerization tools (Docker, Terraform,
    Github Actions)
  - Database languages, tools, and systems (SQL, dbt, Postgres,
    ClickHouse)

What you might work on at Visia

- Building customer-facing applications to facilitate alerting
  on prohibited items in a material stream
- Developing internal tooling for remotely monitoring the
  health of, configuring, and controlling our fleet
- Accelerating machine learning workflows by optimizing data
  pipelines and enabling seamless data transfer between edge
  devices and the cloud
- Working directly with our customers and product team to
  build features

Benefits

- Salary: $110,000 - $180,000 + options
- Health, Vision, Dental + 401(k)
- Flexible + Unlimited PTO
- Remote + Asynchronous
