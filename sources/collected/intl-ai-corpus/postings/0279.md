## Description complète

Being sick or injured is painful. Getting better shouldn’t
be.

Most industries have been transformed by new technology.
Healthcare is still waiting — and you can help change that.

Our mission is simple but ambitious: make healthcare
accessible, transparent, and effortless for all. We put the
consumer at the center, move fast and partner with forward-
thinking providers to deliver software that makes care
better, faster, and more human. This is work with real-world
stakes — and you can help lead the way.

As a key member of our engineering team, you’ll design and
build systems that directly impact people’s lives. You’ll
tackle complex, high-scale challenges in an industry that
desperately needs innovation, using technology to reimagine
the patient experience from the ground up.

Solv Health is seeking an experienced engineering leader to
architect and build the core infrastructure powering our
suite of AI/LLM applications. You will help define best
practices around model evaluation, monitoring, testing, and
deployment, ensuring our AI services run reliably at scale
and deliver measurable value to patients and providers.

If you’re passionate about building the AI foundations that
can transform healthcare, we’d love to hear from you!

What You’ll Be Doing

- Build and launch new, AI-native products that are
  transforming Solv’s core platform - powering intelligent
  workflows, automation, and personalized experiences for
  both patients and providers
- Build systems for rigorous evaluation of model quality:
  establish metrics, automated test suites, and benchmarks
  for prompt performance
- Develop observability & monitoring frameworks for production
  models, integrating logging, alerting, drift detection,
  and performance dashboards
- Champion best practices in versioning, CI/CD, and artifact
  management for AI assets, partnering closely with
  security, compliance, and devops teams
- Collaborate closely with product managers and client-facing
  teams to align AI features with user needs

What You Bring

- 6+ years of software engineering experience, with a minimum
  of 2 years building production AI/LLM applications
- Deep understanding of AI/ML concepts, including foundation
  models, embeddings, vector search, prompt engineering, and
  RAG architectures, paired with solid computer science
  fundamentals
- Proven track record defining and rolling out evaluation/test
  frameworks and monitoring systems for large-scale machine
  learning or LLM services
- Expertise in cloud-native infrastructure (AWS), container
  orchestration (Kubernetes), and CI/CD tooling (e.g.,
  GitHub Actions) complemented by strong web development
  skills in React/Typescript/Python.
- Strong leadership & communication skills, with experience
  guiding technical strategy, driving alignment across
  stakeholders, and mentoring peers
- Stay abreast of the latest advancements in AI (new model
  releases, tools, research) and evaluate their potential
  benefit to our platform. Help define our company’s AI
  strategy and roadmap by identifying new use cases and
  capabilities that keep our product at the cutting edge.
- Problem-solving attitude with a proactive approach to
  learning new technologies and innovating solutions to
  difficult problems
- Curiosity and a learner attitude

Bonus Points

- Experience in healthcare technology or enterprise SaaS
  product environments. Direct knowledge of clinical
  workflows, healthcare interoperability standards (FHIR,
  HL7), or prior work on healthcare AI products is a plus.
