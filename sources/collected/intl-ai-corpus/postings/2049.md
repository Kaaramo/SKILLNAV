Summary

eClerx is looking for a AI Developer with  5-7 years of
experience including 2-4 years of experience in AI
Development space  experience in designing and deploying
production-grade GenAI applications. This role focuses on
leading advanced analytics initiatives for the Money
Movement business by architecting scalable RAG and agent-
based solutions that drive productivity and highly
customized outcomes.

The ideal candidate will have strong expertise in Python,
AI/ML frameworks (PyTorch, TensorFlow, LangChain), MLOps,
and cloud-native architectures such as Kubernetes. You will
design and optimize agentic architectures, implement
training and feedback loops, ensure compliance and security
standards, and mentor team members on advanced AI solution
development. Strong architectural thinking, scalability
experience, and stakeholder collaboration skills are
essential.

Responsibilities:

- Lead analytics to deepen core areas for Money movement
  business through different analytical solutions with a
  focus on leveraging Generative AI capabilities to enhance
  productivity and build highly customized solutions.
- Architect, design, and lead the development of production-
  grade Generative AI applications and complex Agentic
  solutions.
- Evaluate different agentic architectures and evolving
  industry practices to select the optimal approach for
  business problems.
- Design and implement agent training, performance
  measurement, correction, and feedback processes, and
  create/articulate new architectural options.
- Ensure solutions meet stringent compliance, security, and
  performance management standards.
- Collaborate with and advise business stakeholders on the
  scope and capabilities of Generative AI solutions.
- Mentor other data scientists in the team, training them in
  advanced Agentic solution development and software
  engineering best practices.

Eligibility Requirements

- Experience: 5–7 years of hands-on experience with Python,
  Snowflake, and SQL Server, including data modeling,
  performance tuning, and working with structured and semi-
  structured data (data extraction, transformation, and
  analysis). Mandatory exposure to Data Science and
  operating compute environments such as Kubernetes
  services.
- AI / GenAI: Proven experience building AI-driven insights
  from enterprise data. Strong understanding of RAG
  architectures; Graph RAG expertise is mandatory. Must-have
  experience building NLQ (Natural Language Query)
  interfaces.
- Integrations: Hands-on experience integrating and supporting
  data ingestion from Genesys Cloud, Salesforce, and
  internal/external APIs. Skilled in monitoring, debugging,
  and optimizing data pipelines and data flows.
- Cloud & Automation: Proficient in using cloud services for
  data ingestion and automation, leveraging Python scripting
  and/or orchestration tools.
- Data Governance: Exposure to data quality, governance, and
  automation best practices.
- Programming: Strong software engineering skills in Python;
  Java and React JS are preferred.
- Genesys (Preferred): Knowledge of Genesys PureCloud
  APIs/SDKs and Architect Flow is a plus.
- Delivery / Transformation: Experience supporting large-scale
  platform transformations and managing multiple parallel
  development initiatives with effective prioritization.
- Ways of Working: Comfortable working in an Agile delivery
  model.
- Soft Skills: Excellent communication, stakeholder
  management, influencing, and negotiation skills; able to
  collaborate effectively across teams beyond direct
  reporting lines.
- Education: Bachelor’s or Master’s degree in Computer
  Science, Information Systems, or a related field.
