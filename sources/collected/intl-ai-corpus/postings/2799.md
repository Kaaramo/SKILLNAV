## Description complète

Artificial Intelligence (“AI”) Solutions Engineer

Cooley is seeking an Artificial Intelligence (“AI”)
Solutions Engineer to join the Practice Engineering team
within the Innovation department.

Position summary: As the leading technology law firm, Cooley
is determined to become a leader in the digital practice of
law. Cooley Innovation embraces a culture of customer
service excellence, and all members of the department are
expected to move this agenda forward. To that end, under the
direction of the Manager of Product Engineering, and
collaborating directly with practitioners, technical teams,
and innovation stakeholders, the Artificial Intelligence
(“AI”) Solutions Engineer leads process automation
initiatives by driving prototype-based discovery and
building functional workflow demonstrations that validate
repeatable, end-to-end processes—from defined inputs through
tool orchestration to outputs that meet specified acceptance
criteria. This includes developing multi-step automations
that combine deterministic scripting with AI workflows,
including advanced prompting, retrieval-augmented generation
(RAG), and agentic techniques. This role partners across
multiple domains including Practice Innovation, Knowledge
Management, Software Engineering, as well as resource
attorneys and other practitioners across a range of offices
and practice areas. Specific duties and responsibilities
include, but are not limited to, the following:

Position responsibilities:

- Responsible for the management, design and development and
  testing of legal workflow automations, form-building
  solutions and expert knowledge systems
- Elicit attorney/practitioner requirements, map end-to-end
  process steps, define decision points and failure modes,
  and produce clear acceptance criteria and test cases that
  make the workflow measurable and reviewable
- Rapidly build and iterate working proofs-of-concept that
  validate feasibility of repeatable processes, combining
  deterministic logic (rules/scripting) with AI steps
  (prompting, retrieval, tool-use/agents) to produce usable
  outputs aligned to acceptance criteria
- Working with Knowledge Management and practitioners,
  create/maintain the workflow’s “source of truth”
  (playbooks, templates, matter-type guidance, clause
  libraries, FAQs), implement retrieval strategies and
  citation/traceability expectations, and partner with
  attorneys to ensure substantive content accuracy and
  ongoing governance of knowledge updates
- Evaluate AI and technology components; design measurable
  validation workflows (including Python-based integrations)
  to test use cases, assess feasibility, and generate
  stakeholder-ready findings
- Build RPA workflows and API integrations to measure and
  report adoption and performance; deliver executive-facing
  analytics that enable leadership advocacy and decision-
  making
- Design and maintain knowledge applications, including
  working with Knowledge Management and attorneys to ensure
  substantive legal content is accurate
- Coordinate training, support, documentation and resource
  material in support of processes and systems
- Engage with end-users to gather feedback, observe and map
  routines and processes
- Create and maintain project planning documentation,
  including business end-user requirements, user and
  administrative workflows and quality assurance procedures
- All other duties as assigned or required

Skills and experience:

Required:

- After orientation at Cooley LLP, exhibit proficiency in the
  Microsoft Office suite, iManage and other firm
  applications
- Ability to work extended and/or weekend hours, as required
- Ability to travel, as required
- 3+ years  direct applicable experience (e.g., delivery of
  GenAI-powered workflows and solutions)
- Experience with API-driven integrations, workflow
  automations, document automation (e.g. excel and docx),
  and generative AI
- Experience working with MCP servers and building agentic
  workflows using MCP and non-MCP tools
- Experience designing and implementing retrieval-grounded
  generation in permissioned environments
- Proficiency extracting from and creating common enterprise
  file formats like docx, pptx, xlsx, pdf, etc.

Preferred:

- Bachelor’s degree
- Working knowledge of business analysis, knowledge
  management, information systems or other-related field
- Experience working in professional services, or a legal
  services environment with preference to experience in
  AMLAW 200 law firm or in-house legal department with
  greater than 50 attorneys
- Knowledge of legal concepts related to the business of the
  firm and the legal industry
- Familiarity with agile development methodologies
- Experience engaging with end-users to gather feedback,
  observe and map routines and processes
- Ability to deliver AI-enabled workflows within
  confidentiality/approved-AI policy constraints and
  governance expectations
- Excellent attention to detail
- Excellent analytical skills
- Excellent time management and project management skills,
  with ability to work independently to meet objectives and
  deadlines
- Ability to work as a member of a client-service oriented
  team in a professional, approachable manner
- Strong customer focus, with an emphasis on efficient and
  effective communications
- Solid relationship management, presentation and
  collaboration skills
- Unwavering ability to handle and maintain confidentiality
  regarding firm information, projects, client data
- Ability to work independently and take initiative while
  ensuring compliance with team standards in development,
  delivery and support of business systems
- Entrepreneurial by nature
- Ability to organize, prioritize and coordinate multiple
  activities often under tight timelines
- Ability to drive projects to completion and achieve goals
- Team-player with collaborative spirit
