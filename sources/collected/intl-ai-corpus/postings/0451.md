## Description complète

About Us:



Magna Legal Services provides end-to-end legal support
services to law firms, corporations, and governmental
agencies throughout the nation. As an end-to-end service
provider, we can provide strategic advantages to our clients
by offering legal support services at every stage of their
legal proceedings.

Job Description:



Job Title: AI Engineer



Position Summary:



Magna Legal Services is seeking a highly skilled AI Engineer
to join our growing technology team. The ideal candidate
will have hands-on experience building and deploying AI/ML
solutions in production environments, with a focus on
applied AI in legal, medical, or professional services
contexts. This is a fully remote role, offering the
opportunity to make a direct impact on innovative AI
initiatives that support our mission of empowering legal
professionals with smarter tools.

Key Responsibilities

- Design, develop, and deploy AI/ML solutions that support
  Magna’s legal and medical platforms.
- Translate business needs into scalable machine learning
  models and automation workflows.
- Work closely with data engineers, software engineers, and
  product managers to bring AI products from prototype to
  production.
- Maintain, optimize, and monitor deployed AI systems to
  ensure reliability, accuracy, and compliance.
- Integrate AI solutions into existing platforms, including
  natural language processing, summarization, and document
  quality control tools.
- Conduct research and stay current on emerging AI trends,
  tools, and frameworks that can be applied to legal
  services.
- Collaborate with cross-functional teams to identify
  opportunities for automation and innovation.

Qualifications

- Bachelor’s or Master’s degree in Computer Science,
  Engineering, Data Science, or related field.
- 3+ years of experience in AI/ML engineering with a strong
  track record of building and deploying production-grade AI
  systems.
- Proficiency in Python and experience with common AI/ML
  frameworks (TensorFlow, PyTorch, Hugging Face, scikit-
  learn, etc.).
- Strong experience with NLP (summarization, entity
  extraction, classification, embeddings, LLMs).
- Experience deploying AI models via Azure cloud services
- Familiarity with MLOps practices: model versioning,
  monitoring, CI/CD pipelines.
- Strong problem-solving skills with the ability to work
  independently in a remote environment.
- Excellent communication skills and ability to explain
  complex AI concepts to non-technical stakeholders.

Preferred Qualifications

- Experience in legal tech, health tech, or other regulated
  industries.
- Familiarity with data privacy, compliance, and ethical
  considerations in AI.
- Experience integrating AI into SaaS platforms and workflow
  automation systems.
- Familiarity with reviewing and building dotnet applications.

What We Offer

- Be part of a forward-thinking organization transforming the
  legal services industry through AI.
- Collaborate with a talented, diverse, and mission-driven
  team.
- Competitive compensation and benefits package.
- Flexible work environment.
