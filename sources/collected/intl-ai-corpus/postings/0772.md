## Description complète

Teamified works with top enterprises and digital native
businesses in Australia helping them build their remote
teams in India, Philippines and Sri Lanka. We pride
ourselves with hiring great teams to work on exciting game
changing technology. Teamified currently has 200+ engineers,
testers, product managers etc. working across 20+ partners.
We focus on uplifting the way organisations build and manage
their remote teams through great working relationships,
trust, integrity, culture and hiring processes. Additional
to this we are building our own technology product
offerings. We strive to deliver the best outcomes for our
customers, our partners and our people.

We are seeking a talented and experienced Senior AI Data
Engineer to join our dynamic team. As a Senior AI Data
Engineer, you will be responsible for designing, developing,
and implementing AI solutions to enhance our products and
services. You will work closely with our principal software
engineer and platform software engineers to create cutting-
edge AI models and algorithms.

Key Responsibilities:

- Design and develop our Teamified AI data architecture & API
  using Python (Django Framework) and other relevant python
  libraries

- Manage our RDBMs [SQL], NoSQL [MongoDb] and Vector
  [Pinecone] databases and conduct proper data governance &
  research

- Collaborate with our leadership, product experts and
  software architect to integrate AI solutions into our
  Teamified Platform

- Optimize AI augmentation models, context builders and prompt
  builders for performance, scalability, and efficiency.

- Conduct research and stay up-to-date on the latest
  advancements in AI and data engineering.

Requirements:

- Bachelor's or Master's degree in Computer Science,
  Information Technology, or a related field.

- 3+ years of experience in AI data engineering.

- Proficiency in Python and Django Framework and other
  relevant tools and technologies.

- Good-to-have skills: RAG Orchestration Pipelines, LangChain,
  Vector Databases, PineCone,

- Strong problem-solving skills and attention to detail.

- Excellent communication and teamwork skills.

- Ability to work independently and collaboratively in a fast-
  paced environment.

Benefits:

- Flexibility in work hours and location, with a focus on
  managing energy rather than time.
- Access to online learning platforms and a budget for
  professional development
- A collaborative, no-silos environment, encouraging learning
  and growth across teams
- A dynamic social culture with team lunches, social events,
  and opportunities for creative input
- Health insurance
- Leave Benefits

If you are passionate about AI and data engineering and have
the skills and experience we are looking for, we would love
to hear from you. Join Teamified and be part of a team that
is dedicated to innovation and excellence. Apply now!
