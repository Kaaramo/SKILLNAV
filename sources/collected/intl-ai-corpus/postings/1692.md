### Raisonnement classification (LLM upstream)

This role focuses on enabling developers to adopt AI tools
and creating guardrails for AI development, rather than
building or training models directly. The position is about
operationalizing AI adoption across the software development
lifecycle through training, governance, and enablement
strategies - supporting others who work with AI rather than
working on AI systems directly.

## Description complète

We are a leading global asset management firm with over
3,000 employees across 20 offices in 15 countries; we help
millions of investors around the world pursue their
financial goals.

We hire critical thinkers. People who thrive in a
collaborative culture like ours where we solve real problems
while building the future of finance.

You

- Are excited to be part of a vibrant engineering community
  that values diversity, hard work, and continuous learning.
- Love solving complex real-world business problems.
- Recognize that cross-functional collaboration is a core
  component of success for the team.
- Believe there are multiple ways to solve most technical
  problems and are willing to debate the trade-offs.
- Have become a stronger engineer by making mistakes and
  learning from them.
- Are a doer, someone who wants to grow their career and gain
  experience across technologies and business functions.

We

- Continuously invest in a high-performance and inclusive
  culture, in which a diversity of backgrounds, experiences
  and viewpoints are celebrated and valued.
- Encourage career mobility, so you can benefit from learning
  different functions and technologies, and we gain the
  benefits of your experience across teams.
- Run technology pro bono programs that help the non-profit
  community and give our engineering community opportunities
  to volunteer and participate.
- Offer education reimbursements and ongoing training in
  technology, communication, and diversity & inclusion.
- Embrace knowledge sharing through lunch-and-learns, demos,
  and technical forums.
- Consider our people to be our greatest asset—we will help
  you learn what PIMCO Technology has to offer so you can
  participate in activities that benefit your career while
  delivering impactful technology solutions.

Role Overview

The AI Developer Enablement Lead will own the end-to-end
enablement strategy of  “AI for Tech," helping teams adopt
and operationalize AI development tools across the SDLC. You
will work closely with engineering leaders and delivery
teams as a senior, hands-on role, enabling developers
through practical training, clear guardrails, and a
lightweight playbook that helps teams move fast.

Requirements

- Bachelor's or Master's degree in Computer Science,
  Engineering, or a related field
- 10+ years of software development experience. Preferably in
  financial services
- Demonstrated ability to teach and influence across teams
- Practical experience applying AI tools to software
  development especially beyond coding - testing,
  monitoring, etc.
- Strong written communication
- Experience with CICD and DevOps tools
- Forward thinker who can filter the noise to find what is
  useful and applicable
- Familiar with enterprise governance of AI systems
