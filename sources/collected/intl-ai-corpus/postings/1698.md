## Description complète

About Us

Narmi is how community financial institutions unlock the
very latest capabilities in digital banking, account
opening, and lending so they can move faster, tap new growth
opportunities, and be where banking is going. Since our
founding, Narmi has enabled the movement of billions of
dollars and the opening of hundreds of thousands of accounts
for banks and credit unions across the U.S. The results
speak for themselves: our customers have grown deposits,
revenue, and customer satisfaction, with one of the first
institutions powered by Narmi recognized by Bankrate and
NerdWallet for delivering the #1 digital banking experience
in the country.

About the role

AI is easy to demo. It’s hard to ship. We’re hiring someone
to ship it.

Narmi powers digital banking for community banks and credit
unions serving 130+ million Americans. These institutions
operate in regulated environments where inconsistent AI
output isn’t a nuisance; it’s a compliance risk. That means
the bar for shipping is high, and the pace needs to be fast.

We’re hiring a Staff AI Engineer to own end-to-end delivery
of AI-powered features into production across dozens of our
customers environments. You’ll build the features, build the
reliability infrastructure that makes them shippable, and
establish the standards that make the next feature faster to
deliver than the last.

This is a horizontal, Staff-level role. You will influence
how AI is built across the company.

At Narmi, how we work is just as important as what we build.
We call this The Narmi Standard:

- High Ceiling: You go beyond the obvious, take initiative,
  and hold yourself to high standards with care.
- Low Ego: You put the mission above ourselves, share credit
  freely, and lead with curiosity.
- Alignment to Mission: You believe that helping community
  financial institutions win against Mega Banks and FinTechs
  is a worthy endeavor, and will use that belief as a
  northstar to anchor your efforts to that goal.
- High Commitment: You follow through, roll up your sleeves,
  and keep the bar high for yourself and others.

What you’ll do:

- Own AI features from ideation to prototype to production
  release under our customers environments, and enable easy
  monitoring of success
- Embed closely with customer teams, understand their needs,
  and guide the adoption of what to build
- Codify working patterns into tools, playbooks, or building
  blocks that others can use
- Build evaluation and regression systems that validate the
  model and prompt changes before release
- Design monitoring that detects output degradation within
  hours, not days



Shipping is the goal. The infrastructure you will help us
build enables us to ship at pace in a regulated environment.

What you’ll bring:

- 5+ years of software engineering experience
- Direct experience shipping GenAI or agentic systems into
  production and supporting them afterwards
- Write and review production-grade code across frontend and
  backend using Python, JavaScript, or comparable stacks
- Experience building evaluation, monitoring, or ML
  infrastructure, and opinions on what makes these systems
  work well
- Deep understanding of non-deterministic system behavior and
  failure modes
- Strong backend engineering skills
- Ability to operate across engineering, product, and
  compliance

Nice to have:

•  Fintech or regulated industry experience

•  White-label SaaS platform experience

We are not looking for someone learning AI on the job. We’re
looking for someone who has already seen it break and been
there to fix it.

Impact

•   Accelerate the pace at which AI-powered features reach
production

•   Shorten model rollout cycles across the partner fleet

•   Increase partner trust and adoption of AI capabilities

•   Build the reliability foundation that makes speed
sustainable

You will be the reason we ship aggressively without shipping
recklessly.
