## Description complète

This is a fully remote role within the United States.

We’re looking for a driven, entrepreneurial Senior Data
Science Engineer, AI to join our team and help eliminate the
financial complexity of healthcare. We value creative,
outside-the-box thinkers that back their ideas with
technical knowledge and action.

Responsibilities:

- Work cross-functionally with Product and subject matter
  experts to conceptualize, prototype, and build data
  solutions
- Unify and augment multi-source healthcare datasets (e.g.,
  claims, contract rates, demographics) into high-quality,
  Analytics-ready data models that power internal and
  external products
- Build and maintain data engineering systems that support AI
  use cases, including scalable ingestion pipelines, feature
  generation, and downstream products
- Contribute to building, maintaining, and testing data
  pipelines
- Draft internal and external technical documentation
- Seek and prioritize technical and product feedback from
  internal customers
- Iterate quickly with an eye towards value

What you'll bring to the role:

- Bachelor’s degree, or equivalent experience/knowledge. We
  are happy to work with strong candidates with non-
  traditional educational backgrounds
- 4+ years of experience developing data models, pipelines,
  and end-to-end analytical solutions using Python and SQL
- Familiarity with modern AI tooling and frameworks (e.g.,
  OpenAI APIs, vector databases like Pinecone/Weaviate,
  embedding workflows, RAG architectures)
- Understanding of evaluation strategies and monitoring for
  LLM-based systems (e.g., hallucination mitigation, eval
  frameworks, feedback loops)
- Ability to design data systems with scalability,
  performance, and cost efficiency in mind, particularly for
  compute- and data-intensive workloads
- Strong grasp of data architecture and engineering practices,
  including ETL/ELT workflows, orchestration tools (e.g.,
  Airflow, dbt), and cloud-based storage (e.g., S3,
  Redshift)
- Entrepreneurial mindset: you prioritize tasks with an eye
  for evolving business needs. You can problem solve
  independently
- Comfortable working remotely in a collaborative, technical
  team

Bonus points:

- Revenue cycle, healthcare payments and/or medical billing
  experience
