## Description complète

LeanData helps the world’s fastest-growing companies
automate, simplify, and accelerate revenue.

We are seeking a GTM Engineer (Data & AI) to be the lead
architect of our autonomous revenue engine. This is a
critical role focused on building the Data and Context
layers that serve as the "brain" for our AI strategy. We
aren't just looking for someone to manage tools, we want a
builder who can accelerate our agentic GTM automation, where
workflows don't just follow rules, but reason through
signals to power the entire customer journey.

You will be empowered to use your technical depth to design,
test, and scale a system that eliminates manual friction and
turns the funnel into a high-velocity machine. This is a
highly cross-functional role where you will work directly
with GTM VPs to define their growth strategies.

Reporting into the Head of Revenue Operations, you will have
a seat at the table with the C-suite to help define the
future of RevOps in the GTM tech industry.

This is a remote opportunity.

What you will be doing:

- Architect the GTM Intelligence Foundation. Design and manage
  a unified data layer that merges structured
  (DataLake/APIs/MCPs) and unstructured signals
  (Calls/Emails/Chats) for agentic reasoning and consumption
- Engineer Agentic Workflows. Build autonomous systems that
  sit in the execution path of the funnel, blending
  probabilistic AI reasoning with deterministic logic to
  automate complex decisions across Marketing, Sales, and CX
- Lead our predictive modeling efforts. Own the logic and
  technical setup for scoring and propensity models ensuring
  they are integrated into the agentic orchestration layer
- Prototype and Scale GTM Innovation. Rapidly build and test
  new AI-native concepts and turn the successful ones into
  standard operating procedure

Requirements:

- Engineering-First Mindset. 5+ years in GTM Systems, RevOps,
  or Data Engineering with a focus on technical workflows. A
  technical degree or equivalent background is preferred
- Context Orchestrator. You have built complex data flows
  across Data Warehouses, APIs, and MCP servers and have
  hands-on experience building the context layers for AI
  judgment
- Track record of shipping GTM logic, like scoring, routing,
  and lifecycle automation, that measurably moved the needle
  on pipeline or retention.
- Hands-on experience with modern orchestration platforms and
  understand how to orchestrate LLMs, enrichment waterfalls,
  and agentic workflows.
- Possess a structured approach to problem-solving and a
  natural instinct to simplify and automate manual processes
  rather than adding complexity

Why work at LeanData:

- LeanData covers employee insurance premiums up to 90%
- Stock options in LeanData for all full-time employees
- Flexible PTO
- 401K plan
