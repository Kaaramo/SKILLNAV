At Iron Mountain we know that work, when done well, makes a

Are you curious about being part of our growth story while

Job Summary

Iron Mountain is seeking a dynamic and experienced AI
Engineer to join our Operations team. In this role, you will
be responsible for designing and deploying production-ready
Machine Learning models and Artificial Intelligence agents
that optimize complex logistics workflows and drive
measurable cost reductions.

You will join a high-impact team dedicated to bridging the
gap between operational business challenges and scalable
technical solutions, directly influencing our supply chain
and field operations.

What You’ll Do

In this role, you will:

- Develop End-to-End AI Solutions: Design, build, and monitor
  production-ready Machine Learning models and automated
  agents to streamline multi-step operational decision-
  making.
- Optimize Operational Workflows: Collaborate with Finance,
  Facilities, and Information Technology teams to build
  robust Extract, Transform, Load (ETL) pipelines and
  integrate predictive tools into existing warehouse and
  logistics software.
- Govern AI Safety and Compliance: Ensure the responsible
  deployment of all systems by performing rigorous model
  validation, maintaining strict data governance, and
  mitigating risk or bias in automated decisions.

What You’ll Bring

The ideal candidate will have:

- 5+ years of applied experience in Artificial
  Intelligence/Machine Learning engineering or advanced
  analytics, preferably within logistics, supply chain, or
  transportation industries.
- Advanced technical expertise in Python and SQL, with proven
  experience scaling "agentic" workflows and Retrieval-
  Augmented Generation (RAG) solutions using platforms like
  LangChain or n8n.
- Proven ability in strategic problem-solving and rapid
  prototyping, with the communication skills necessary to
  explain complex model outputs to non-technical business
  leaders.
- A Bachelor’s degree in a quantitative field such as Computer
  Science, Data Science, Engineering, or Mathematics.
- United States Citizenship is required.

  balance.
- Comprehensive health, wellness, and retirement plans to
  support your long-term well-being.
- Opportunities for continuous learning and professional
  growth in a cutting-edge technical environment.
