## Description complète

The Squarespace Developer Experience team is looking for a
Senior Software Engineer, Developer Tools to develop tools
that help engineers ship code more efficiently. Our
Developer Experience team empowers our engineering
organization with internal platforms and automation that
streamline development, debugging, and deployment workflows.
We are a collaborative, empathetic, and technically curious
team working across a modern cloud infrastructure. You will
work closely with engineers across Product, Platform, and
Infrastructure teams, and while you will not have direct
management responsibilities, you'll have opportunities to
mentor others and influence team strategy.

This is an opportunity for you to drive the next generation
of internal tooling at Squarespace. You will lead the AI
developer tooling guidance and adoption that directly
improves developer productivity across the engineering
organization. We are looking for someone with a passion for
AI developer tools, who enjoys advocating for their use and
promoting best practices and adoption within a large
engineering organization.

This is a hybrid role working from our NYC office 3 days per
week. You will report to the Developer Tools Team Manager
based in New York.

You'll Get To…

- Promote best practices and the adoption of AI developer
  tools
- Advocate for AI utilization across multiple teams, taking
  ownership of AI adoption strategies and providing expert
  guidance and training to peers on integrating AI tools
  into daily workflows
- Build and support tools to make the life of developers at
  Squarespace easier.
- Work with other engineers to negotiate, design and implement
  solutions that improves their efficiency, with an emphasis
  on frequent iterations.
- Write high-quality code with industry best practices in
  mind. (Resilient architecture, automated testing, etc.)
- Focus on finding scalable, reliable, automated solutions to
  meet the engineering department's scale.
- Sponsor the growth of your team and teammates.

Who We're Looking For

- Experience evaluating and advocating for AI developer tools.
- 8+ years of experience writing software in mainstream
  programming languages such as Go, Python or Java.
- Experience programming in JavaScript or TypeScript.
- Experience communicating updates and resolutions to users
  and other partners.
- Appetite for solving problems, supporting developers, and
  learning new technologies.
- Experience with containers, Kubernetes, Prometheus, or other
  CNCF software is a big plus.
- Be comfortable working in a team distributed across multiple
  time zones
- Degree in Computer Science or equivalent industry
  experience.
