## Description complète

About Wolf Games

Wolf Games is building the platform for generative
entertainment universes — living, ever-expanding worlds
where creators, players, and AI collaborate in real time.
Our first universe, Public Eye, turns everyone into a
detective. Across a suite of crime-solving games, players
collect evidence, crack cases, and rise through the ranks of
a connected detective program. We're creating a future where
game development and player experience are tightly connected
through intelligent systems that learn, adapt, and surprise.

About the role

We're looking for a QA Engineer to build and manage AI-
driven QA systems that autonomously verify, play, and
bulletproof our mobile HTML5 games. This is a role for
someone who sees QA as an engineering discipline —
prioritizing AI tooling, automated playthroughs, and script
generation over manual clicking.

You will transform traditional manual testing into a highly
automated, continuous pipeline of AI bug hunts, autonomous
playthroughs, and AI code reviews. As Wolf Games scales to
produce daily live content, our testing surface area is
exploding. Traditional manual QA cannot keep pace with an
AI-driven development pipeline that ships daily. We need
someone who builds AI systems to test what our AI systems
build.

The time you save through automation goes directly into what
humans do best: evaluating game feel, tuning, balance, and
"finding the fun." You'll be the human arbiter of taste
while your AI agents handle the regression testing.

You will report to the Director of Engineering and work
closely with engineering and creative leadership to:

- Design and deploy AI agents capable of autonomously playing
  through our HTML5 games to validate core loops,
  progression logic, and edge cases.
- Build an automated "AI Bug Hunt" system that continuously
  explores daily builds to identify physics glitches, UI
  overlaps, and rendering anomalies across mobile browser
  environments.
- Integrate QA deeply into Wolf's AI-driven development
  process, writing the test parameters, prompts, and
  acceptance criteria that our AI coding tools use to
  generate game features.
- Implement automated AI code reviews that scan incoming pull
  requests for performance bottlenecks, memory leaks, and
  mobile web anti-patterns before human review.
- Develop automated systems to simulate and test device
  fragmentation — iOS Safari memory limits, Android Chrome
  touch latency, variable network speeds.
- Embed AI QA gates directly into the CI/CD pipeline, halting
  builds that fail automated visual or state-based checks.
- Run structured internal play-test sessions for rapid
  prototypes, translating subjective design feedback into
  objective, testable parameters.
- Reduce manual regression testing by 90% through AI-augmented
  end-to-end testing.

What you'll bring

- Extensive experience shipping and maintaining mobile browser
  / HTML5 games. This is non-negotiable.
- Proven track record testing browser-based games built with
  Pixi.js, Phaser, Three.js, or custom WebGL engines.
- Deep familiarity with LLMs, AI agents, and prompt
  engineering. You know how to instruct an AI to review
  code, write test scripts, or analyze game logs.
- Strong scripting skills in TypeScript/JavaScript and
  experience with modern testing frameworks (Playwright,
  Cypress, Puppeteer) combined with AI-assisted workflows.
- Solid understanding of TDD methodologies and how to apply
  them in an AI-code-generation environment.
- Highly developed taste for games — strong intuition for what
  makes a game fun and the ability to articulate design
  feedback constructively.
- Deep empathy for the player experience, prioritizing issues
  based on how heavily they disrupt the core gameplay loop.
- An automation-obsessive mindset. If you have to manually
  test something twice, your instinct is to build an AI
  agent or script to do it forever.
- Exceptional communication skills — your bug tickets and AI
  test prompts are precise, clear, and immediately
  actionable.
- Comfort operating in a high-velocity environment where
  builds happen daily and traditional QA timelines do not
  exist.

Nice to have

- Experience configuring visual regression tools for
  WebGL/Canvas environments.
- Background training or fine-tuning AI models for testing
  applications.
- Prior work embedding QA automation into CI/CD pipelines at
  scale.
- Experience with AAA-level QA rigor applied to mobile or web
  game contexts.

Why Wolf Games

We are a small, creative, and collaborative team that values
curiosity, craftsmanship, and experimentation. We believe
small wins lead to big wins — and that the right QA systems,
built by the right engineer, can let a small team ship at
startup speed with AAA quality. If you're excited to
industrialize quality assurance through AI while defending
the fun, we'd love to hear from you.
