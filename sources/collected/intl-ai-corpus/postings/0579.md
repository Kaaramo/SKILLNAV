## Description complète

POSITION SUMMARY

We are seeking a highly motivated and skilled AI Platform
Engineer to join our growing technology team. This role is
pivotal in bridging the gap between our business departments
and the transformative power of AI. You will be responsible
for the operational management, integration, and
optimization of our AI platforms, primarily AWS Bedrock and
Azure AI Foundry. A key aspect of this position will be
administering and scaling our use of third-party integration
tools like Zapier, n8n, and Tines to empower teams across
the company to build AI-driven workflows securely and
efficiently.

The ideal candidate is a hands-on engineer with a DevOps
mindset, a passion for automation, and a strong
understanding of how to make sophisticated AI tools
accessible and impactful for non-technical users.

The Senior AI Platform Engineer is a proficient and
autonomous owner of the platform's core components. They
lead small-to-medium-sized projects and begin to specialize
in more complex AI services and integrations.

- Scope of Impact: Owns medium-sized projects and features for
  the platform; begins influencing teams that consume the
  platform.
- Key Responsibilities:
- Example Project: Building a reliable, automated process for
  a product team to securely access and use a new generative
  AI model, complete with cost monitoring and usage alerts.

REQUIRED SKILLS AND EXPERIENCE

- Bachelor’s degree in Computer Science, Engineering, or a
  related field, or equivalent practical experience.
- 3+ years of experience in a DevOps, SRE, or Cloud
  Engineering role, with a strong focus on automation.
- Hands-on experience with major cloud platforms, with deep
  expertise in AWS and familiarity with Azure.
- Proven experience working with managed AI services (e.g.,
  AWS Bedrock, Amazon SageMaker, Azure AI Services, Google
  Vertex AI).
- Demonstrated proficiency in administering and building
  complex workflows in at least one major low-code/no-code
  automation platform (Zapier, n8n, Tines.com, Make, etc.).
- Strong scripting and programming skills, particularly in
  Python and/or Bash.
- Experience with Infrastructure as Code (IaC) principles and
  tools, especially Terraform.
- Excellent communication skills with the ability to explain
  complex technical concepts to non-technical stakeholders.

Preferred Qualifications

- Experience working in the FinTech, lead generation, or

- Familiarity with C#/.NET environments.
- Experience with API design, security, and management.
- Relevant cloud certifications (e.g., AWS Certified DevOps
  Engineer, AWS Certified Generative AI Developer -
