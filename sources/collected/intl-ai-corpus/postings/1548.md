About this role: Wells Fargo is seeking a Senior Software
Engineer (Data Engineering/Generative AI) The Finance

Technology is a collaborative, cross-functional, Agile
organization that is looking for independent thinkers

through Gen AI, data delivery and data management. In this

- Lead moderately complex initiatives and deliverables within
  technical domain environments

- Design, code, test, debug, and document for projects and

- Lead projects and act as an escalation point, provide
  guidance and direction to less experienced staff

Required Qualifications:

  following: work experience, training, military experience,

Desired Qualifications:
