## Description complète

Airbnb was born in 2007 when two hosts welcomed three guests
to their San Francisco home, and has since grown to over 5
million hosts who have welcomed over 2 billion guest
arrivals in almost every country across the globe. Every
day, hosts offer unique stays and experiences that make it
possible for guests to connect with communities in a more
authentic way.

The Community You Will Join:

The Business Technology Enablement and Innovation team,
within the BizTech organization, leads the mission to
deliver innovative technology, empowering our employees to
utilize technology productively, driving connection, and
scale support for employees. We bridge the gap between
traditional IT and engineering teams by providing quick
solutions, consultations, and guidance beyond traditional
support, with a focus on enhancing workflows and tools
utilizing AI.

The Difference You Will Make:

We are seeking a world-class Business Systems Engineer to
join our dynamic team. You will be responsible for providing
quick solutions utilizing internal tools and agentic AI, to
support and assist teams in enhancing their day to day
workflows, overseeing the end-to-end delivery of system and
tool changes as required, and providing support to those
solutions. Your expertise will help provide solutions to
stakeholder challenges, promote industry best practices, and
maintain data integrity and accuracy within our platforms. A
high degree of focus on practical AI and understanding the
current state of industry as AI technology changes is
critical.

A Typical Day:

A typical day for a Business Solutions Engineer at this
level involves a mix of strategic ownership, technical
delivery, and cross-functional collaboration. Their
activities often include:

- Feature Support: Managing product feature lifecycles, which
  includes responding to production support issues and
  handling change requests.
- Stakeholder Alignment: Engaging directly with business
  partners to define and agree upon necessary changes,
  covering requirements gathering, design, delivery
  timelines, and testing plans.
- Platform Enhancement: Focusing on optimizing and enhancing
  their team's platforms, seeking out opportunities for
  process improvement and automation using technical and
  analytical skills.
- Technical Delivery: Taking ownership of the partial delivery
  of system or tool changes, including the execution of
  configuration updates and building low to medium
  complexity solutions and bug fixes.
- Information Gathering and Strategy: Working with cross-
  functional teams to translate their needs into effective
  platform solutions, and using data and reporting to
  suggest actionable insights for data-driven decisions.
- Independent Execution: Operating with minimal supervision,
  demonstrating advanced knowledge of business principles,
  and independently creating critical deliverables such as
  detailed process flows, controls documentation, and
  project plans for single-stakeholder projects.
- Proactive Risk Management: Collaborating with team members
  to manage and prioritize tasks based on deadlines and
  proactively documenting delivery risks and recommending
  mitigation strategies.

Your Expertise:

- 6+ years of professional experience  with proven enterprise-
  grade systems implementations, IT consulting or similar
  field leveraging advanced platform integrations
- Proven experience with Agentic AI workflows and platforms
- Demonstrated experience designing and implementing custom
  business solutions and integrations using enterprise
  platforms including Contentstack and taxonomy platforms,
  with Graphite as preferred platform
- Knowledge of data modeling, business intelligence tools
  (e.g., Tableau, Looker), and enterprise reporting best
  practices.
- Skilled at stakeholder engagement and cross-functional
  collaboration in large global organizations.
- Adept at contributing to complex projects, simplifying
  technical concepts, and promoting operational excellence.
- Excellent written and verbal communication skills,
  especially in critiquing technical designs and leading
  workshops.
- Exemplifies strong communication fundamentals.
- Proficient in designing and implementing data management
  strategies to ensure data integrity.
- Identifies opportunities for product improvements and
  standardizes processes, obtaining necessary buy-in from
  stakeholders.
- Experience with productivity tools such as Google Workspace,
  JIRA, Asana, Slack, etc.
