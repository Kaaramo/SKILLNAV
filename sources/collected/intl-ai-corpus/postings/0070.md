About the job

Join a Billion-Dollar Mission in the Making

Subject is reimagining education from the ground up. We’re
backed by Kleiner Perkins, SoftBank Opportunity Fund, Owl
Ventures, and founders of category-defining companies. Now
we’re building the most ambitious engineering team in edtech
— and we’re looking for mission-focused superstars to join
our team.

We’re building generative AI solutions that support grading,
teacher insights, data analysis, student engagement, and
personalized learning at unprecedented scale and speed. If
you’re a full-stack senior engineer with experience in
AI/LLM development and agent orchestration, this is your
moment.

What You’ll Own

You’ll be driving core agentic AI systems and continue their
evolution into best-in-class products for student and
teacher support, performance analytics, real-time grading,
and the personalization of education for every student. This
is not just maintenance — it’s rethinking what’s possible.

Responsibilities

AI Product Ownership: Drive our agentic AI infrastructure
for grading, data synthesis, student feedback, and more.
Evolve prototype systems into high-scale, production-ready
solutions.

LLM and Agent Orchestration: Implement, extend, and optimize
AI agents using tools like LangChain, semantic search,
vector databases, and OpenAI APIs. Design orchestrators that
drive multi-step, human-in-the-loop workflows.

Hands-On Engineering: Write production-level code across
front-end (Next.js, TypeScript) and back-end (Node.js,
Postgres). Deploy through Docker, Vercel, and cloud services
(GCP/AWS).

System Thinking: Architect scalable APIs, pipelines, and
infrastructure for LLM interaction, data ingestion, grading
workflows, and analytics dashboards.

Collaboration & Velocity: Partner with product, data, and
content teams in rapid sprints. You’ll work face-to-face
with senior leaders daily. Think fast. Move faster.

Quality & Leadership: Champion engineering standards while
balancing technical debt and speed. Mentor mid-level
engineers and help build a high-trust engineering culture.

You Bring

4+ years experience in full-stack engineering, with real-
world ownership of production systems.

Deep understanding of prompt engineering, LLM APIs (OpenAI,
Claude, Gemini), vector stores (e.g., Pinecone, Weaviate),
and agentic frameworks (LangChain, AutoGPT, or similar).

Experience with orchestration of multiple AI agents and
human-in-the-loop decision-making flows.

Frontend fluency in Next.js, React, TypeScript; backend
confidence in Node.js, Postgres.

DevOps skills in Docker, CI/CD, and cloud (AWS or GCP).

Startup hunger: You’ve shipped with incomplete specs, found
creative solutions, and worn many hats.

Speed and grit: You operate at championship pace, make
decisions quickly, and iterate faster than anyone you know.

Cultural Fit

We are a championship team — not a family. We care, but we
perform. We win together.

You thrive in intensity. 60+ hour weeks? In-office every
day? That’s your edge.

You believe impact beats pedigree. Your resume is just the
starting point — your results will speak loudest.

Mission-Driven Tech That Changes Lives

At Subject, we’re building tools that directly help students
graduate high school, improve outcomes, and unlock
opportunities. Every line of code has impact. Every model
deployed touches real learners. We’re not in this to
participate. We’re here to change the future — and if you
are too, we want to talk.
