## Description complète

Capital Technology Group provides expert consulting services
software development, digital transformation, human-centered
design, data analytics and visualization, and cybersecurity.

Our multidisciplinary teams use agile methodologies to
rapidly and incrementally deliver value in close
collaboration with our clients. For over a decade, we have
been trusted by both federal and commercial clients to solve
complex, mission-critical business challenges. The quality
of our work has been recognized by our partners and peers
through our inclusion in the Digital Services Coalition, a
group of forward- thinking firms recognized for excellence
in delivering IT services.

Description

Capital Technology Group (CTG) is on a mission to modernize
and innovate the way the federal government delivers
software. We are passionate about our work, dedicated to our
clients, and committed to a culture of continuous learning
and growth. For this role specifically, we are seeking
individuals to help support high-impact, civic tech within
the federal government.

As an integral part of the program, the Lead AI/ML Engineer
leads the team in evaluating new or emerging technologies
using prototypes and/or proof of concepts, analyzes and
communicates the benefits and risks in implementing
solutions using the new technologies, and lead teams to
support the adoption of new technologies across the
enterprise. The Lead AI/ML Engineer will also provide
technical leadership by supervising and mentoring junior IT
specialists.

Client Requirements: applicants MUST BE US Citizens and be
able to obtain Public Trust clearance

Responsibilities

- Leads the team in evaluating new or emerging technologies
  using prototypes and/or proof of concepts
- Analyzes and communicates the benefits and risks in
  implementing solutions using the new technologies
- Shall have the ability to perform as a developer,
  operations, and security engineer in cloud environments
- Lead teams to support the adoption of new technologies
  across the enterprise
- Provides technical leadership by supervising and mentoring
  junior IT specialists

Requirements

- Bachelors Degree
- 10 years of relevant experience
  - With at least 3 years of experience deploying production
    enterprise applications in AWS that use AI/ML
- Strong proficiency in Python
- Experience with machine learning frameworks such as
  TensorFlow or PyTorch
- Solid data manipulation and analysis skills using pandas
- Experience working with cloud platforms, with AWS preferred
- Must be able to get up-to-speed quickly on new projects and
  technologies
- Strong communication and problem-solving skills
