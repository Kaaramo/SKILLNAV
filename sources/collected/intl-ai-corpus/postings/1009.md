## Description complète

This role combines leadership, project management &
technical depth with a strong understanding of AI technology
and multiple enterprise systems. You will lead design,
planning, and cross-functional execution for the building of
Agentic AI solutions; serving as a bridge between business
stakeholders and technical teams, ensuring that solutions
are built and supported in alignment with enterprise goals
and they deliver measurable value. You will work with teams
handling Technology Platforms, AI Engineering, technical
product design & business innovation.

You will have the opportunity to stay on the cutting edge of
technology research and to put your skills to practical use
by building Agentic AI & technology solutions. You will be
able to see the results of your efforts ripple throughout
the company and our systems in real time. You will design
systems, own your code, and your work will have a very
direct, measurable impact on the core metrics of the
organization.

Responsibilities

Strategic & Functional Leadership

Define and drive the functional roadmap for agentic AI
initiatives across business domains.

Participate in AI Agent design discussions, contributing
business context, functional logic, and user-centric
perspectives. Lead structured workshops to define As-Is and
To-Be process landscapes, leveraging design thinking and
process modeling. Collaborate with infrastructure, security,
and architecture teams to ensure the build of scalable &
compliant Agentic AI solutions.

Business Analysis & Product Ownership

Act as the owner for Agentic AI workflows, owning the
backlog & delivery roadmap. Translate operational insights
into structured process maps, KPIs, and transformation
goals. Author functional requirement documents, user
stories, and acceptance criteria for agile development.

Agentic Process Design

Architect end-to-end agentic workflows for processes,
including data ingestion, match logic, exception handling,
and reconciliation. Collaborate with AI/ML experts to define
agent skills, autonomy levels, and decision boundaries.

People & Project Management:

Serve as the primary liaison between business operations and
technical teams to ensure alignment and value realization.

Manage and mentor a team of engineers Oversee project
execution, timelines, and resource allocation across
multiple initiatives. Represent the Agentic AI team in
leadership forums and cross-departmental planning sessions.

Cross-Functional Collaboration Work closely with:
- AI Engineers & Data Scientists – to define agent
  capabilities and validate performance
- Product Managers & UX Designers – to align features with
  user needs
- Enterprise Architects & DevOps Teams – to ensure scalability
  and deployment efficiency
- Business Stakeholders & SMEs – to define use cases and
  measure impact
- Change Management Teams – to support adoption and training

Pre-requisites for the role (Skills, Work Experience)

- Proven experience in strategic planning, business analysis,
  and people leadership
- Strong communication and stakeholder management skills
- Ability to translate business needs into functional designs
- Passion for enabling transformative AI solutions that drive
  business impact

And, while not necessarily an expert, you should have a good
knowledge of some of the following platforms & technologies:
- Cloud & DevOps: Microsoft Azure Functions, Logic Apps, Azure
  DevOps

- AI Concepts: LLMs, generative AI, prompt engineering, ML
  lifecycle

- AI Platforms: Microsoft AI Foundry (highly preferred), Azure
  ML or Copilot Studio
- Enterprise Systems: Databricks, Snowflake, UiPath, SAP HANA,
  Salesforce,
