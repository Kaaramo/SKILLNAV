### Raisonnement classification (LLM upstream)

This is a hands-on AI/ML engineering role with direct
responsibility for building and deploying AI systems,
including agentic systems, RAG implementations, and
production ML pipelines. The role involves designing and
implementing AI solutions using LangChain, LangGraph, prompt
engineering, and orchestrating LLM workflows on GCP Vertex
AI, while also deploying traditional ML systems like
recommender systems and forecasting models.

## Responsabilités

- Partner with stakeholders to scope problems and identify the right solution, whether leveraging existing AI tools or building custom workflows and solutions

- Build and maintain production ML pipelines and services for non-GenAI use cases including recommender systems, customer segmentation models, and marketing optimisation modules
- Develop APIs and microservices for AI/ML solutions, ensuring security, scalability, and observability
- Implement CI/CD for ML services, write infrastructure as code, and monitor for model/data drift and performance
