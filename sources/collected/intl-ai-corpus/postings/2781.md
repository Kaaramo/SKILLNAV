## Description complète

About Us

Selector is building an operational intelligence platform
for digital infrastructure. By adopting an AI/ML-based
analytics approach, the platform provides actionable multi-
dimensional insights to network, cloud, and application
operators. It enables operations teams to meet their KPIs
through seamless collaboration, search-driven conversational
user experience, and automated data engineering pipelines.

Our solutions are used by leading Telecoms, Media Providers,
Retail, and Professional Sports organizations across the
world. Our novel approach and rapidly expanding footprint
put us in the unique position for continued growth to become
a category leader. To fuel our growth, we are seeking
passionate, high-energy, results-oriented individuals to
join our team.

Our mission is to deliver world-class solutions on behalf of
the large enterprise. Supported by leading investors,
Selector is uniquely positioned to deliver a world-class
solution to address large enterprise requirements across the
globe.

Job Overview

We are looking for a highly skilled Test Automation Engineer
to join our Engineering team. You will play a pivotal role
in ensuring the reliability, performance, and scalability of
our network observability solutions by designing,
developing, and maintaining automated testing frameworks.
You will collaborate closely with engineering, SRE, DevOps,
Sales Customer Support teams to improve test coverage,
accelerate delivery, and enhance product quality.

Key Responsibilities

- Design, develop, and maintain robust automated test
  frameworks and test suites for network and observability
  platforms.
- Define test strategies for functional, integration,
  performance, scalability, and regression testing.
- Collaborate with engineering teams to understand
  requirements, design test cases, and identify potential
  risks.
- Integrate automated tests into CI/CD pipelines to enable
  continuous testing and faster delivery cycles.
- Analyze test results, identify root causes of failures, and
  provide actionable feedback to development teams.
- Drive quality initiatives across the platform, including
  test coverage metrics, automation metrics, and reporting.
- Work with observability tools (logs, metrics, traces) to
  validate network behaviors and ensure platform
  reliability.

Requirements

- Bachelor’s or Master’s degree in Computer Science,
  Engineering, or related field.
- 2+ years of experience of hands-on experience in networking
  and load testing.
- Strong programming skills in languages such as Python, Java,
  or Go.
- Experience with test automation frameworks (e.g.,
  Selenium/Playwright, Pytest, Robot Framework, JUnit).
- Preferred: Experience with network technologies, protocols
  (TCP/IP, BGP, SNMP), and observability tools (Prometheus,
  Grafana, ELK).
- Experience integrating tests with CI/CD pipelines (Jenkins,
  GitLab CI, or similar).
- Strong analytical, debugging, and problem-solving skills.
- Excellent communication skills and ability to influence
  quality practices across teams.

Preferred Qualifications

- Experience with large-scale distributed systems and cloud-
  native architectures.
- Knowledge of container orchestration (Kubernetes, Docker)
  and microservices testing.
- Familiarity with network performance testing and monitoring.
- Experience with test data management and simulation of
  network traffic.
