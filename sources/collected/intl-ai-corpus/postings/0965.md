## Description complète

Senior Software Engineer – AI (Materia AI)

We’re building the next generation of AI‑powered systems for
tax and accounting professionals, and we need a Senior
Software Engineer who can architect high‑performance backend
platforms that make real‑world AI possible. If you thrive in
fast‑moving environments, love solving complex
distributed‑systems challenges, and want to see your work
directly power intelligent, production‑scale features, this
role is for you.

About the Role

As a Senior Software Engineer – AI, you will design, build,
and optimize backend services that power AI‑native
experiences across our Materia‑inspired product ecosystem.
You will play a key role in shaping the architecture,
performance, and reliability of the systems that orchestrate
LLM interactions, serve high‑traffic APIs, and process
large‑scale content.

Own Backend Architecture for AI‑Native Products

- Design and implement backend services using Python, FastAPI,
  and PostgreSQL.
- Build distributed systems that support real‑time AI
  interactions and high‑volume data flows.
- Define architectural patterns, service boundaries, and
  system interfaces for our AI platform.

Engineer High‑Performance Services

- Build APIs and microservices supporting AI agents,
  orchestration engines, and content pipelines.
- Optimize performance across concurrency, caching,
  throughput, and large‑scale document processing.
- Ensure backend systems scale seamlessly under demanding
  compute and data workloads.

Integrate AI Components Into Production Systems

- Connect backend services to LLM providers such as OpenAI and
  Anthropic.
- Implement orchestration logic, safe execution flows, request
  routing, and latency‑tuned real‑time AI capabilities.
- Productionize AI components with robust error handling,
  guardrails, and observability.

Raise the Engineering Bar

- Lead architecture discussions, code reviews, and key
  technical decisions.
- Champion engineering best practices in testing, resiliency,
  observability, and maintainability.
- Support CI/CD pipelines, DevOps workflows, and cloud
  automation patterns.

Collaborate Across Product & AI Teams

- Work closely with ML engineers, product managers, and
  designers to translate requirements into scalable
  solutions.
- Turn conceptual AI features into reliable backend
  capabilities used by thousands of professionals.

About You

Required Qualifications

- 4+ years of backend engineering experience building scalable
  APIs and microservices.
- Strong proficiency in Python, FastAPI, PostgreSQL, and AWS-
  based cloud services.
- Solid understanding of distributed systems, architecture,
  and data modeling.
- Strong debugging skills and experience delivering
  production-grade systems.
- Excellent communication and cross‑functional collaboration
  abilities.
- Comfortable in fast‑paced, iterative environments with
  evolving requirements.
- Curiosity about AI technologies and enthusiasm for
  integrating them into real systems.

Preferred Skills

- Experience with AI‑driven systems or agent architectures.
- Familiarity with embeddings, vector search, or RAG-style
  retrieval systems.
- Strong opinions on backend testing, automation, and
  operational quality.
- Experience with modern LLM APIs or orchestration frameworks.
