## Description complète

Why you should join us

Solstice is redefining how life sciences organizations
commercialize their products. We are building a commercial
engine that allows pharmaceutical marketers to take control
of their content supply chain. Some reasons for why you
should join us are:

- Rapid growth: Over the past year, we have been working with
  some of the top life sciences manufacturers as well as
  marketing agencies and are working with over 50+ pharma
  brands.
- Frontiers of technology: We’re building applications and
  rapidly iterating technical approaches at the frontiers of
  AI, experimenting with LLMs and new frameworks. We have V2
  of the platform currently deployed to customers but are
  incorporating new, advanced frameworks to our solution
  aggressively.
- Top-tier investors: We’ve raised $3.5mm+ from investors like
  Twelve Below, Virtue and the founders of Datavant,
  Commure, and Paradigm to supercharge our growth and build
  an elite team of engineers and operators.

Building anything great requires commitment and dedication.
We’re looking for someone who has the fire to prove others
wrong 🚀

About the role

We are in search of an ML operations engineer who has the
drive required to be a part of a founding team looking to
tackle a difficult but meaningful problem. You’ll be
responsible for the end-to-end lifecycle of our AI
systems—from experimentation to production deployment and
ongoing optimization.Beyond development, you will be
collaborating closely with co-founders Yiwen and Aris on all
facets of the startup as we grow Solstice in the coming
years. Reach out to aris@solsticehealth.co to learn more.

What you’ll be working on:

- Leverage prompt-engineering, retrieval-augmented generation,
  model experimentation, and benchmarking to optimize
  performance of Solstice’s platform on real biopharma
  marketing data.
- Wrangle proprietary commercial & marketing brand data into
  complex LLM pipelines to produce high-quality, compliant
  life sciences marketing content.
- Implement ML Ops best practices, including CI/CD pipelines
  for ML, model versioning, automated testing, and
  monitoring.
- Developing LLM eval infrastructure to store, analyze and
  iterate on LLM outputs and the feedback we receive from
  clients
- Scope, architect, and build SOTA agents and pipelines while
  supporting customers directly.
- Flex across the full stack (frontend, backend, infra) to
  build whatever is necessary to support our customers.

Some ideal traits we’re looking for

- Engineering chops. Relevant experience building systems &
  deploying enterprise-grade products
- Independent thinking. While we believe specific skills can
  be taught, we need someone who has the ability to make
  decisions and drive results on their own.
- Strong proficiency in full-stack web development
  technologies such as Python, FastAPI, Docker, AWS
- Experience working with LLMs such as GPT, Claude and Gemini
  to create LLM-based workflows, RAG Pipelines and agentic
  solutions. (Either formally or through
  projects/experimentation)
- Superb communication and collaboration skills. We believe in
  direct communication and willingness to provide as well as
  accept criticism.
- Unreal work ethic. Diamonds were not made overnight.

Benefits

- Medical insurance
- Relocation support
- Opportunity to work at a high-velocity and high-energy
  startup
- Visa sponsorship (O-1) for internationals
