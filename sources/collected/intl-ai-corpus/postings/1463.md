## Description complète

About Lucidya

Lucidya is a fast-growing SaaS company building AI-powered
customer experience solutions for enterprises across the
MENA region. Known for our deep expertise in Arabic-first
AI, we help organizations understand, serve, and engage
their customers at scale.

We are now launching a new AI Agents business unit, focused
on building AI Agent platforms and solutions to solve
critical pain-points in Customer Experience - securely,
responsibly, and at scale.

About the Role

As an AI Engineer specialising in Voice Agents, you will be
responsible for designing, building, and deploying
intelligent Voice Agents that solve real business problems.
The Voice Agents is a key strategic pillar for the Agent AI
business unit.

This role goes beyond experimentation - you will help take
Voice Agents from concept to production, ensuring
performance, reliability, and business impact.

You will have the opportunity to showcase your ability to
build from 0 to a real solution that drives value

What You’ll Do

AI & Agent Development

- Design and implement real-time voice agents using ASR, TTS,
  and LLM-based reasoning.
- Evaluate Voice Agent providers, focussing on reliability and
  stress testing
- Build multi-turn, stateful voice conversations with
  interruption handling (barge-in), retries, tool-use and
  fallbacks.
- Build evaluation for AI Agent performance, ensuring that AI
  Agents are working as expected
- Finetune and adapt STT (Speech to Text), TTS (Text to
  speech) and LLMs for different dialects
- Contribute to the development of the Lucidya AI Agent
  platform, ensuring that Voice capability is embedded in
  the platform

Production & Scalability

- Deploy AI models into production environments with a focus
  on scalability, performance, and reliability.
- Collaborate on MLOps practices to support continuous
  integration, monitoring, and improvement.
- Work with streaming architectures (WebSockets, gRPC,
  SIP/WebRTC).
- Analyse call logs and transcripts to continuously improve
  performance.
- Cross-Functional Collaboration
- Work closely with software engineers, product managers, and
  domain experts
- Translate business problems into effective AI solutions with
  measurable outcomes.

Governance & Quality

- Ensure AI solutions comply with ethical standards, data
  privacy requirements, and legal regulations.
- Document models, algorithms, workflows, and decisions
  clearly for internal and external stakeholders.
- Stay up to date with the latest AI research, tools, and best
  practices; and apply them where valuable.

RequirementsWhat we’re looking for

Technical Skills

- 3+ years of hands-on experience building and deploying Voice
  Agents, with a proven track-record of achieving business
  value
- Strong programming skills in Python (Java is a plus)

- Experience deploying AI models into production or
