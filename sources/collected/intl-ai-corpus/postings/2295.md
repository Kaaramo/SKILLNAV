## Description complète

Summary

Within Guidewire’s Business Technology (BizTech)
organization, you’ll join a global team that powers our
internal systems and employee experience through secure,
scalable platforms and data-driven solutions. BizTech’s
mission is to enable Guidewire’s growth and transformation
with innovative, resilient technology, strong governance,
and a focus on AI fluency and operational excellence. You’ll
collaborate across engineering, security, and business teams
in a culture that values speed, agility, and continuous
improvement. As a Senior Software Engineer – AI, you will
design, build, and operate our next-generation AI/ML agent
platform and RAG capabilities that support internal teams
and, ultimately, the P&C insurers we serve. You will combine
deep backend engineering skills with hands-on experience in
GenAI, LLM orchestration, and enterprise data integration to
deliver secure, production-grade AI services. This is a
highly visible role that advances BizTech’s Agentic Platform
and GenAI enablement priorities while contributing to
Guidewire’s broader mission of transforming the way the
world’s P&C insurers do business through cloud, analytics,
and AI-powered innovation.

Job Description

What you’ll do

- Design, develop, and operate scalable microservices that
  power the runtime and serving layer for AI/ML agents and
  RAG components, with an emphasis on reliability, security,
  and performance.
- Implement and maintain LLM orchestration frameworks (e.g.,
  LangChain, LangGraph, AWS Strands, Google ADK) and
  integrate them with Guidewire’s core and internal
  applications to enable robust agent workflows.
- Build and optimize secure data ingestion and indexing
  pipelines for RAG, leveraging vector databases (such as
  Pinecone, Weaviate) and graph databases (such as Neo4j) to
  support complex enterprise use cases.
- Develop secure, scalable data connectors and tool interfaces
  for systems like Oracle, Salesforce, Workday, and
  Snowflake, ensuring compliance with data governance and
  Responsible AI principles.
- Collaborate closely with Security and other stakeholders to
  embed strong access controls, auditability, and privacy
  into AI/ML services, balancing innovation with risk
  management.
- Design and implement automated evaluation frameworks (evals)
  to measure LLM performance, reliability, and accuracy, and
  integrate these into CI/CD pipelines for continuous
  improvement.
- Contribute to BizTech’s Agentic Platform and GenAI
  enablement roadmap by optimizing engineering workflows
  (e.g., via coding assistants such as Claude, Cursor,
  Gemini Code) and championing AI fluency across the team.
- At Guidewire, we foster a culture of curiosity, innovation,
  and responsible use of AI—empowering our teams to
  continuously leverage emerging technologies and data-
  driven insights to enhance productivity and outcomes.

What you’ll bring

Required

Demonstrated ability to embrace AI and apply it to your
current role as well as data-driven insights to drive
innovation, productivity, and continuous improvement.

- 5+ years of professional software engineering experience
  building backend services or internal platforms, ideally
  in a cloud-native environment.
- Strong programming skills in Python and/or
  TypeScript/Node.js for building automation, orchestration,
  and backend services supporting AI/ML workloads.
- At least 2 years of hands-on experience designing,
  implementing, and deploying solutions using Generative
  AI/LLM technologies (e.g., via AWS Bedrock, Google Vertex,
  or similar).
- Practical experience with LLM orchestration and RAG/agent
  frameworks (e.g., LangChain, LangGraph, AWS Strands,
  Google ADK), including tool calling and multi-step
  workflows.
- Hands-on work with vector databases (such as Pinecone,
  Weaviate) and graph databases (such as Neo4j), including
  schema design, indexing strategies, and query
  optimization.
- Experience integrating LLM-based applications with large-
  scale, complex enterprise data sources and APIs (e.g.,
  Oracle, Salesforce, Workday, Snowflake).
- Solid understanding of AWS services relevant to AI
  platforms, including EC2, S3, IAM, and container
  orchestration (Docker, Kubernetes), plus CI/CD practices
  for automated deployment of AI/ML services.
- Strong collaboration, communication, and problem-solving
  skills, with the ability to work across security, data,
  and business teams in a fast-moving, outcome-oriented
  environment.

Preferred

- Experience designing MCP (Model Context Protocol) or similar
  servers for secure tool invocation, data access, and API
  integration in agentic architectures.
- Prior work implementing or extending automated LLM
  evaluation frameworks (evals), including test design,
  metrics selection, and integration into pipelines.
- Familiarity with data governance, Responsible AI practices,
  and security-by-design approaches for AI/ML systems,
  especially in regulated or enterprise settings.
- Exposure to observability tooling (logs, metrics, traces)
  for AI workloads and experience debugging issues across
  data pipelines, models, and orchestration layers.
- Experience contributing to internal platforms or “citizen
  developer” tooling that enables broader teams to safely
  build on AI capabilities, aligned with BizTech’s GenAI
  enablement goals.

Your Impact

We believe in clarity and setting you up for success. In
your first 6 months, you will take ownership of key
components of BizTech’s Agentic Platform, stand up or
enhance microservices and RAG pipelines in production, and
partner with Security to ensure robust controls around
enterprise data and AI tools.

You’ll define and implement evals that make LLM performance
measurable and trustworthy, while improving engineering
workflows through automated tooling and coding assistants.
As your work scales, you will help Guidewire become the most
efficient, trusted vertical SaaS company by enabling secure,
reliable AI agents that enhance employee experiences and
accelerate the transformation of how P&C insurers operate.
