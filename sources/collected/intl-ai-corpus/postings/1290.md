## Description complète

Working as a coder at Hudson River Trading (HRT) means
working on a small, highly productive, and efficient team.
You may see "trading" in our name and assume you'll be
relegated to some dark corner to code for the "business,"
but that could not be further from the truth!

At HRT, an automated trading firm based in NYC, the code you
write *is* our business, so it is everyone's job to make
sure our developers have everything they need to do their
jobs well.

As a member of the AI Tools team, you will be responsible
for increasing productivity across the company by
integrating the newest AI tools into our users’ workflows.
Whether it’s for a Python developer writing a web app, a C++
developer speeding up order execution, a researcher
investigating trade signals, or even Business Development
meeting with vendors - we are focused on deploying what
works.

This is an exciting opportunity to help build and define the
AI tooling ecosystem at HRT. You will have to navigate the
rapidly evolving options in this space, and adapt them to
our own internal systems. You might be fixing auth one day,
comparing vendors the next, and coding a new MCP the third.

Responsibilities

- You’re one of the top devs in your current organization, as

  helpfulness to other devs
