F5 is seeking a highly skilled and innovative AI/ML Engineer
to join our Employee Experience (EX) team. In this role, you
will be at the forefront of transforming our digital
workplace by designing, developing, and deploying
intelligent solutions. You will focus on building AI Agents,
implementing Large Language Model (LLM) integrations, and
creating automation workflows that streamline business
processes for our People, Finance, and Legal functions. This
role is instrumental in moving our employee experience from
traditional support to a proactive, AI-driven ecosystem.

Responsibilities

- AI Solution Development: Design and build end-to-end AI/ML
  solutions, with a heavy focus on Generative AI, LLMs
  (e.g., OpenAI, Anthropic, Gemini), and RAG (Retrieval-
  Augmented Generation) architectures to support employee
  productivity.
- AI Agent Architecting: Develop and deploy autonomous and
  semi-autonomous AI Agents to automate complex cross-
  functional workflows across platforms like ServiceNow,
  Workday, and Salesforce.
- Model Integration & Engineering: Integrate AI capabilities
  into existing business systems using APIs and
  orchestration frameworks (e.g., LangChain, LlamaIndex).
  Optimize model performance and cost-efficiency for
  enterprise-scale deployment.
- Data Engineering for AI: Collaborate with data teams to
  design and implement data pipelines, vector databases, and
  fine-tuning datasets to improve the accuracy and relevance
  of EX-specific AI models.
- Engineering Excellence: Champion best practices for AI
  development, including prompt engineering, model
  evaluation, monitoring for "hallucinations" or bias, and
  maintaining the security and privacy of employee data.
- Cross-Functional Collaboration: Partner with Product
  Managers, Business Systems Analysts, and UX designers to
  identify high-impact AI use cases and translate them into
  technical prototypes and production-ready features.
- Innovation & Research: Stay current with the rapidly
  evolving AI/ML landscape, evaluating new tools,
  frameworks, and research to keep F5’s Employee Experience
  at the cutting edge of technology.

Qualifications

- 5+ years of software engineering experience, with at least
  2+ years focused on AI/ML development.
- Proven expertise in building applications powered by Large
  Language Models (LLMs) and implementing RAG patterns.
- Strong proficiency in Python and experience with AI/ML
  frameworks (e.g., PyTorch, TensorFlow, Scikit-learn) and
  orchestration libraries (e.g., LangChain, Haystack).
- Experience with Vector Databases (e.g., Pinecone, Milvus,
  Weaviate) and managing structured/unstructured data for AI
  applications.
- Hands-on experience deploying AI solutions in cloud
  environments (e.g., AWS, GCP, Azure) and utilizing MLOps
  practices for model monitoring and versioning.
- Familiarity with enterprise platforms such as ServiceNow,
  Workday, or Salesforce and their native AI/ML capabilities
  is a significant plus.
- Solid understanding of API design (RESTful services) and
  integrating AI into complex, multi-system enterprise
  architectures.
- Excellent problem-solving skills and the ability to navigate
  the ambiguity of emerging technologies.
- Effective communication skills, with the ability to explain
  complex AI concepts to both technical and non-technical
  stakeholders.
