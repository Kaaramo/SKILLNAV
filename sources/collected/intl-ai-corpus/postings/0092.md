### Raisonnement classification (LLM upstream)

This role is about building infrastructure and tooling to
enable OTHER engineers to use AI tools more effectively -
not building or deploying AI models/products itself. The
focus is on developer productivity, setting up AI coding
workflows (Cursor, Claude Code, MCP servers), and creating
the platforms/frameworks that allow the engineering
organization to leverage AI, which falls squarely under ai-
support.

## Description complète

Join us in redefining the creator economy with AI

Fanvue is one of the fastest-growing creator monetisation
platforms globally. We’re an AI-powered, creator-first
platform helping creators connect, engage, and earn directly
from their audiences at scale. Following our recent Series
A, Fanvue has surpassed $100M+ in annual recurring revenue
with triple-digit year-on-year growth, supporting hundreds
of thousands of creators and millions of fans worldwide.

As Fanvue scales, how we use AI inside engineering is
becoming a critical advantage. This role exists to ensure AI
meaningfully accelerates developer velocity, feature
delivery, and code quality, while being deployed
responsibly, safely, and consistently across the codebase.

🎯 The Role

We’re hiring an AI Developer Tooling Engineer to own how AI
is applied across Fanvue’s engineering organisation. This is
a highly hands-on role focused on making AI a force
multiplier for every engineer, not an uncontrolled risk.

You’ll help define the AI coding stack, build the
infrastructure and tooling that supports it, and enable the
team to ship faster through parallelisation, agents, and AI-
assisted workflows, while maintaining high standards for
quality, security, and maintainability.

🚀 What You’ll Do

- Align the Fanvue codebase with modern AI coding tools such
  as Cursor and Claude Code
- Help define, standardise, and roll out Fanvue’s AI-assisted
  development stack across IDEs, PR workflows, and CI
- Build and operate MCP servers and AI-ready infrastructure to
  enable reliable, context-aware code generation
- Enable parallelised development workflows where engineers
  review and ship AI-generated code confidently
- Establish best practices for AI-assisted coding, reviews,
  and refactoring across the team
- Create and maintain documentation, playbooks, and training
  to drive adoption and safe usage
- Track and report measurable improvements in developer
  productivity such as PR cycle time, throughput, and code
  quality
- Continuously evaluate emerging AI developer tools and
  vendors, with clear success criteria and kill switches

👀 Who You Are

- Strong software engineering background with TypeScript in
  production environments
- Deep hands-on experience with AI coding tools such as Cursor
  and Claude Code, with exposure to Aider, Cline,
  CodeRabbit, or GitHub Copilot
- Experience running MCP servers and related tooling such as
  Context 7, Puppeteer, or MCP Compass
- Comfortable creating, managing, and deploying agent skills
  across real-world codebases
- Strong understanding of LLM fundamentals including RAG
  pipelines, embeddings, tokenisation, fine-tuning, and
  performance trade-offs
- Confident working with GitHub workflows, CI/CD pipelines,
  and secure coding practices
- Highly autonomous, experimental, and comfortable owning
  ambiguous problems end to end
- Able to demonstrate real, measurable impact from applying AI
  to engineering workflows

✨ You’ll Thrive Here If

- You’re excited by making engineers dramatically more
  effective, not just building tools for their own sake
- You enjoy experimentation, fast iteration, and turning
  emerging tech into production-ready systems
- You think in systems and workflows, not just features
- You’re comfortable being accountable for outcomes, not just
  ideas

⚠️ You’ll Struggle Here If

- You prefer narrowly scoped roles with fixed roadmaps
- You’re uncomfortable making judgment calls around AI safety,
  quality, and trade-offs
- You need constant direction or heavy process to operate
  effectively

🌍 Why Join Fanvue?

- Play a critical role in defining how AI is used responsibly
  at scale inside engineering
- Direct impact on developer velocity, feature delivery, and
  product quality
- Work closely with senior engineers and leadership across the
  company
- Competitive salary and benefits package
- Unlimited holiday
- Remote working
- Flexible hours, according to when you perform best
- Budget for growth and wellbeing

⭐ Fanvue is for Everyone
