How You'll Create Possibilities

Key Responsibilities:

Model Development & Deployment

- Build and fine-tune machine learning and deep learning
  models for NLP, computer vision, and recommendation
  systems.
- Develop APIs and microservices for model inference and
  seamless integration into enterprise applications.
- Implement scalable training and inference pipelines using
  distributed computing frameworks on Google Cloud Platform.

AI Engineering & Integration

- Leverage Agent space to design, deploy, and manage
  intelligent agents supporting enterprise workflows.
- Apply retrieval-augmented generation (RAG), embeddings, and
  vector search techniques for enhanced model performance.
- Integrate open-source and commercial LLMs (e.g., OpenAI,
  Anthropic, Hugging Face models) into GCP-hosted
  applications.
- Implement monitoring, evaluation, and continuous learning
  pipelines for AI solutions.

Collaboration

- Work closely with data scientists, platform engineers, and
  business teams to translate use cases into AI-powered
  solutions.
- Contribute to reusable libraries, frameworks, and best
  practices for enterprise AI development.

What You'll Bring to Our Team

Qualifications Required:

- 3–5 years of experience in AI/ML engineering or applied
  machine learning.
- Proficiency in Python (preferred) and experience with ML
  frameworks
- Hands-on experience with Google Cloud Platform (BigQuery,
  Vertex AI, AI/ML pipelines, Pub/Sub, etc.).
- Strong understanding of embeddings, vector databases, and
  LLM integration.
- Experience deploying AI models in cloud or hybrid
  environments.

Preferred:

- Familiarity with Agent space and agentic orchestration
  frameworks.
- Hands-on experience with CI/CD for ML (MLOps).
