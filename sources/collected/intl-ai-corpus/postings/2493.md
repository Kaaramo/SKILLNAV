This role is squarely ai-first: the developer builds,
trains, fine-tunes, and deploys deep learning models (CNNs,
RNNs, Transformers), fine-tunes LLMs with LoRA/QLoRA,
develops RAG pipelines, and designs multi-agent workflows
using LangChain/LangGraph/CrewAI. All core responsibilities
involve hands-on work with AI/ML models and agentic systems.
