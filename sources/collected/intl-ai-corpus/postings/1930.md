About the Role

Pragmatike is hiring on behalf of a fast-growing AI startup
recognized as a Top 10 GenAI company by GTM Capital, founded
by MIT CSAIL researchers.

We are searching for a Senior UI / Frontend Engineer with
strong, hands-on experience building high-quality, high-
performance user interfaces for complex, data-intensive AI
products used by Fortune 500 customers.

This role is ideal for an engineer who cares deeply about
UX, performance, and maintainable frontend
architecture—someone who can own features end to end,
collaborate closely with design and backend teams, and
deliver polished, production-ready interfaces at scale.

What Youll Do

- Build and maintain modern, production-grade web interfaces
  using React and TypeScript.
- Translate product requirements and design systems into
  clean, scalable UI components.
- Own complex frontend features end to end, from
  implementation to production support.
- Optimize UI performance for large datasets, real-time
  updates, and high-usage dashboards.
- Collaborate closely with product, design, and backend teams
  to ship user-facing features.
- Establish frontend best practices around state management,
  testing, and code quality.
- Improve frontend architecture, tooling, and developer
  experience.
- Debug and resolve production UI issues across browsers and
  devices.

What Were Looking For

- 5+ years of experience as a Frontend / UI Engineer.
- Strong proficiency with React and TypeScript in production
  environments.
- Solid understanding of modern frontend architecture and
  component-based design.
- Experience building responsive, accessible, and high-
  performance user interfaces.
- Familiarity with frontend build tools, bundlers, and CI/CD
  workflows.
- Ability to collaborate effectively with designers and
  backend engineers.
- Comfortable owning features and shipping to production.

Bonus Points

- Experience building data-heavy dashboards or real-time web
  applications.
- Familiarity with modern state management solutions (Redux,
  Zustand, React Query).
- Experience with design systems and component libraries.
- Exposure to AI-driven or ML-powered products.
- Startup experience or comfort working in fast-moving,
  ambiguous environments.

Why This Role Will Pivot Your Career

- Research pedigree: MIT CSAIL founders with deep AI and
  systems expertise.
- Customer impact: Build interfaces used by Fortune 500
  clients.
- Industry momentum: Alumni behind major acquisitions
  (MosaicML Databricks, Run:AI NVIDIA, W&B CoreWeave).
- Funding & growth: Oversubscribed seed round, next funding
  planned for 2026.
- Ownership: Lead UI features for mission-critical products.
- Quality & craft: Work on frontend problems that prioritize
  performance and UX at scale.
