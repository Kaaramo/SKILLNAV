## Description complète

Job Description SummaryWe are looking for an AI Engineer
with strong software engineering and system design
fundamentals to build and deploy production‑grade Generative
AI systems in a MedTech environment. In this role you will
work closely with product, clinical, service, and operations
stakeholders to translate real‑world workflows into
scalable, reliable AI‑powered applications. You will design,
build, integrate, and operate LLM‑based systems, including
RAG, Knowledge Graphs, Graph RAG, and Agentic AI workflows,
and deploy them on AWS using SageMaker and Bedrock.

Job Description

Roles and Responsibilities

- Apply strong software engineering principles to design,
  build, test, deploy, and operate AI‑powered systems
  end‑to‑end.
- Design clean, scalable system architectures, including APIs,
  services, and data pipelines, with a focus on reliability,
  observability, and security.
- Make sound engineering trade‑offs across latency,
  scalability, cost, fault tolerance, and maintainability.
- Design and implement LLM‑based solutions for real‑world,
  production use cases.
- Build Retrieval‑Augmented Generation (RAG) pipelines,
  including:
  - Document ingestion and chunking strategies
  - Embeddings and vector search
  - Retrieval, reranking, grounding, and response synthesis
- Implement Knowledge Graph and Graph RAG approaches to enable
  reasoning over structured and unstructured enterprise
  data.
- Build Agentic AI systems capable of planning, reasoning, and
  executing multi‑step workflows.
- Implement agentic workflows using tool/function calling,
  memory, context management, and guarded execution.
- Design systems where agents orchestrate workflows and LLMs
  act as the reasoning and decision layer.
- Work embedded with stakeholders to understand workflows,
  validate AI outputs, and iterate quickly from prototype to
  production.
- Integrate AI capabilities into real applications (backend
  services, user‑facing tools, copilots, operational
  systems).
- Own solutions in production, including monitoring,
  debugging, and continuous improvement.
- Build and deploy AI services on AWS, leveraging:
  - AWS SageMaker for model training, hosting, and pipelines
  - AWS Bedrock for foundation models, agents, and knowledge
    bases
- Implement CI/CD, containerization, and MLOps practices for
  AI systems.
- Ensure secure access, governance, logging, and monitoring
  aligned with enterprise and healthcare requirements.
