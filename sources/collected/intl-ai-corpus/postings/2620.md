### Raisonnement classification (LLM upstream)

This is fundamentally a DevOps/SRE role that supports AI/ML
workloads rather than directly building or training models.
The engineer builds infrastructure, CI/CD pipelines, and
deployment automation that enables ML systems to run in
production, and integrates AI-assisted tools into DevOps
workflows (AIOps), but does not design, train, fine-tune, or
implement AI/ML models themselves.
