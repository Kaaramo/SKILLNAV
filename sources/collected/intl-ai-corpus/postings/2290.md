## Description complète

Principal AI Forward Deployed Engineer





Location:                   Remote, USA



Employment Type: Full-Time



Compensation:        $210,000 - $262,000 (Range applies to
US candidates only) + Benefits/Variable Comp - Range may
vary based on experience.



Benefits Offered:    Vision, Medical, Life, Dental, 401K











Summary



As a Principal AI Forward Deployed Engineer at OneStream,
you are the senior-most individual contributor within the AI
Forward Deployed Engineering program. This role drives
sustained AI adoption at OneStream’s most strategic
enterprise accounts by combining deep finance domain
expertise with AI fluency and customer advisory capability.



You operate as a trusted advisor to senior finance
stakeholders (CFOs, FP&A VPs), owning AI use case roadmaps,
leading adoption and change management strategy, and
translating non-deterministic AI outputs into actionable
business language. You partner with FDE team members, AI
Delivery, AI Engineering, and OneStream’s partner ecosystem
to ensure customers achieve measurable ROI from SensibleAI
products within OneStream implementations.





The Principal AI FDE shapes engagement models, defines
reusable playbooks, and informs hiring profiles as the FDE
program scales. This is a foundational role that sets the
standard for how OneStream’s AI Forward Deployed Engineering
function operates at the enterprise level.





Primary Duties and Responsibilities



Customer AI Adoption & Advisory



- Serve as the primary strategic advisor to senior finance
  stakeholders at assigned accounts, owning the AI adoption
  roadmap from initial deployment through sustained
  operational ownership.
- Participate directly in the design and delivery of AI use
  cases at assigned accounts, including solution
  architecture, data pipeline configuration, and
  implementation oversight — particularly during initial
  deployment and expansion phases.
- Lead adoption and change management strategy for AI-enabled
  finance processes, ensuring customer organizations can
  confidently own non-deterministic AI outputs
  independently.
- Design and facilitate executive workshops, business reviews,
  and readouts that connect AI capabilities to measurable
  financial outcomes (forecast accuracy, cycle time, process
  efficiency).
- Engage directly with customer finance and data teams to
  deliver, validate, and refine AI solutions within the
  OneStream ecosystem.



Use Case Strategy & Expansion



- Identify and validate expansion use cases within assigned
  accounts by translating business objectives into
  actionable AI implementation plans aligned to the
  SensibleAI product portfolio.
- Build 24-36 month AI roadmaps per account, sequencing use
  cases by business impact, technical feasibility, and
  organizational readiness.
- Partner with Sales and Account Management to position AI
  expansion opportunities during renewal and upsell cycles,
  activating revenue from inside the account.
- Evaluate cross-functional process and forecasting
  opportunities that extend AI value beyond single-function
  deployments.

FDE Program Development & Cross-Functional Collaboration

- Shape the FDE engagement model by producing reusable
  playbooks, engagement templates, adoption benchmarks, and
  change management frameworks that scale across the
  program.
- Serve as an escalation point within FDE engagements,
  mentoring other FDE team members and partner resources on
  complex engagement patterns.
- Coordinate with AI Delivery to transition validated
  solutions into scalable production delivery workflows.
- Partner with AI Customer Success & Advisory to support
  strategic accounts during and after FDE engagement.
- Support AI Partner Operations by enabling partners with
  field-tested patterns, training content, and quality
  standards.
- Channel field learnings, product gaps, and customer signal
  directly to AI Engineering and Product teams to inform
  roadmap priorities.

Commercial & Strategic Impact

- Support strategic sales opportunities by shaping AI solution
  feasibility, delivery approach, and customer readiness
  assessment.
- Contribute to pre-sale positioning of AI capabilities for
  qualified accounts, including executive-facing materials
  and discovery sessions.
- Drive measurable outcomes: accelerated time-to-value,
  expansion ARR, Net Revenue Retention improvement, and
  durable customer self-sufficiency.





Required Education and Experience



- Bachelor’s degree in finance, accounting, business, computer
  science, data science, or relevant combination of
  education and experience.
- 8–15 years of experience in finance transformation, FP&A
  advisory, CPM/EPM consulting, or enterprise financial
  software delivery.
- 3–5 years of experience with AI/ML concepts applied to
  financial planning, forecasting, or analytics workflows.
  Deep ML engineering expertise is not required; AI fluency
  and the ability to translate AI outputs into business
  context is essential.
- Demonstrated success leading change management workstreams
  on ERP, EPM, or financial systems transformation programs.
- Experience in customer-facing advisory roles with direct
  engagement at the C-suite or VP level (CFO, VP FP&A, VP
  Finance).
- Track record of identifying and activating expansion
  opportunities within enterprise accounts through trusted
  advisory relationships.
- Experience at a Big 4 firm, Global Systems Integrator, or
  enterprise software vendor in a finance-focused consulting
  or advisory capacity.





Preferred Education and Experience



- Direct experience with OneStream, Oracle EPM, SAP BPC/SAC,
  or comparable CPM/EPM platforms.
- Experience building or advising on AI-enabled finance
  offerings at a consulting firm or software vendor.
- Familiarity with the competitive dynamics of vendor
  selection during Finance Transformation programs.
- Prior experience in a Forward Deployed Engineering,
  solutions consulting, or embedded advisory role.
- Experience influencing product roadmaps based on field
  deployment learnings.
- Proficiency in Python and/or C#.
- 1-3 years of software engineering experience, with a focus
  on data science, AI, or machine learning concepts or
  relevant combination of education and experience.
- Strong SQL; data modeling and ETL/ELT; REST APIs,
  identity/security basics.







Knowledge, Skills, and Abilities



- C-suite presence - can hold a room with a CFO or FP&A VP;
  translates complex AI concepts into business value.
- AI fluency - can explain non-deterministic outputs, model
  limitations, and accuracy tradeoffs in business terms
  without requiring deep technical implementation skills.
- Use case design - can build a 12–24 month AI roadmap, not
  just implement a single sprint.
- Change management instinct - reads organizational readiness,
  identifies adoption blockers, designs interventions.
- Hypothesis-driven approach; comfort operating with
  ambiguity; strong documentation and communication habits.
- Cross-functional collaboration with Product, Engineering,
  Delivery, CS, Sales, and Partners; ability to give and
  receive design feedback.
- Design for reuse; produce playbooks, frameworks, and assets
  others can adopt; contribute to internal standards.
- Apply data privacy and governance guidelines in solution
  design and customer advisory.
- Comfortable engaging and presenting to C-level executives on
  a regular basis.
