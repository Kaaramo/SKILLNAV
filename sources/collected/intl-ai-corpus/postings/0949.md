Mission Statement:

The Service excellence team is a part of the Common Shared
Services organization and supports multiple functions
(including Finance, SCM, HR Operations, and Trade Transport
and Logistics amongst others). As AI Innovation Engineer
within the Business Process Automation team, you will be
part of a dynamic team focused on enabling the CSS functions
to deliver their services with greater efficiency and
effectiveness.

Your Responsibilities:

- Design AI-powered solutions, including the integration of AI
  Agents, to handle complex, repetitive, or decision-driven
  tasks.
- Create and optimize generative models such as GANs
  (Generative Adversarial Networks) and VAEs (Variational
  Autoencoders) for various applications, including content
  creation, data augmentation, and more.
- Multimodal AI Integration: Develop AI solutions that
  integrate multiple data modalities (e.g., text, image,
  audio) to create cohesive and intelligent systems.
- Develop and implement POCs using MaaS platforms (e.g., Azure
  OpenAI & Google Vertex AI) and AI Agents to showcase their
  potential value.
- Configure, train, and deploy AI Agents to interact with
  systems, perform decision-making, and automate tasks
  requiring cognitive functions.
- Leverage MaaS platforms to deploy, monitor, and fine-tune AI
  models for Shared Services automation.
- Build APIs and pipelines to integrate AI capabilities into
  existing tools and workflows.
- Work closely with cross-functional teams to gather
  requirements, test solutions, and roll out AI-driven
  systems.
- Translate technical concepts into actionable insights for
  both technical and non-technical audiences.
- Collaborate with business analysts and stakeholders to
  analyze Shared Services workflows and identify high-impact
  automation opportunities.
- Stay ahead of trends in AI, including advancements in MaaS
  platforms, Large Language Models (LLMs), and natural
  language interfaces.
- Living Hitachi Energy’s core values of safety and integrity,
  which means taking responsibility for your own actions
  while caring for your colleagues and the business.

Your Background:

- Bachelor’s or Master’s degree in Computer Science,
  Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning, or a related
  field.
- 5–7 years of experience in AI and machine learning, with
  exposure to generative models, AI Agents, or multimodal
  AI.
- Proficiency in Python and familiarity with AI frameworks
  such as TensorFlow, PyTorch, or Keras.
- Strong analytical and problem-solving skills with a passion
  for tackling real-world AI challenges.
- Good communication and teamwork skills, with the ability to
  explain technical ideas to diverse audiences.
- Experience working in collaborative, cross-functional
  environments.
- Familiarity with project management tools and agile
  methodologies is a plus.
- Proficiency in both spoken and written English is required.
