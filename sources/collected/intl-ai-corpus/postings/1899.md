MAIN PURPOSE OF THE ROLE

This is a hands‑on engineering role at the intersection of
cloud platforms, software development, and AI enablement.

applying strong engineering discipline to deliver secure,
scalable and sustainable patterns for MUFG.

You will be expected to work autonomously, manage ambiguity,
and adapt rapidly to evolving business needs while
collaborating with application and infrastructure teams to
deliver secure, compliant AI and cloud solutions.
