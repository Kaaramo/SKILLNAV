### Raisonnement classification (LLM upstream)

This role implements and configures the ServiceNow AI
platform, building solutions using pre-built AI capabilities
like Now Assist, AI Search, Virtual Agent, and Predictive
Intelligence rather than training or fine-tuning models. The
developer works with existing AI/GenAI features within the
ServiceNow ecosystem to create customer service workflows
and automation solutions, which is supporting AI
functionality rather than building AI systems directly.
