### Raisonnement classification (LLM upstream)

This role focuses on building data pipelines, web scraping
infrastructure, and data management systems that power
speech models - it's clearly working NEAR AI but not ON the
AI models themselves. The engineer creates the datasets that
ML Engineers use, handles data infrastructure, and optimizes
data workflows, which is classic AI-support work enabling ML
teams rather than direct model development.

## Description complète

As a Software Engineer - Data & AI, at Speechmatics, you’ll
design and manage the data pipelines that power our state-
of-the-art speech models. You'll be work at the intersection
of infrastructure, engineering, and research to create
large-scale, high-quality datasets efficiently. If you love
solving complex technical problems and optimising systems
for real-world AI applications, this is the role for you.

What you’ll be doing:

- Develop and maintain scalable web scraping solutions to
  extract data from multiple sources
- Design, implement and maintain scalable data pipelines for
  ingestion, transformation, and storage of large datasets
  for high-performance AI models.
- Ensure data quality and integrity through validation,
  cleaning, and normalisation processes.
- Manage data infrastructure, including databases, and cloud
  storage solutions.
- Optimise performance of scraping scripts and pipelines for
  speed, reliability, and scalability.
- Monitor and troubleshoot data workflows reliability and
  efficiency.
- Identify new opportunities to source or generate relevant
  datasets.
- Stay compliant with data privacy and security regulations
  when handling sensitive or proprietary data.
- Collaborate closely with our ML Engineers to shift the
  cost/throughput/quality frontier, delivering richer data
  at bigger scale and lower cost to power our next-
  generation models.
- Collaborate with others on the ML team and Speechmatics
  leadership to craft the roadmap for our data work to power
  excellency in voice AI.

Who we are looking for:

- Extensive industry experience in software/data engineering.
- Strong proficiency in Python and SQL.
- Hands-on experience with web scraping, crawlers and
  awareness of the latest web frameworks, and protocols to
  improve scraping efficiency
- Professional experience with a major cloud provider.
- Knowledge of data pipelines, ETL (extract, transform, load)
  processes.
- Exposure to data for machine learning.
- Familiarity with data compliance standards and privacy-
  preserving data methods.
- Experience with on-prem infrastructure.
