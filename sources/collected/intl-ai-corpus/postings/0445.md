## Description complète

At WISEcode, we're a well-funded, dynamic startup. WISEcode
makes personalized nutrition a reality based on the facts –
what truly is in our food — and marries this data with your
preferences. Powered by AI and the world’s best food and
nutrition database, WISEcode embraces the fact that we are
all different, have different dietary needs, objectives, and
preferences, and should not be at the mercy of market spin
or “expert” opinion. Every one of us should have the
information we need to make the best decisions for
ourselves.

The Opportunity

WISEcode doesn’t hire for incremental impact — we hire for
force multipliers. Our Principal Engineers are restless
builders who transform complexity into clarity, elevate
every team they touch, and create architecture that
accelerates the entire company. These are strategic
technologists who see systems where others see fragments,
who bring long-term thinking into every decision, and who
translate bold ideas into elegant, scalable solutions that
endure beyond their original use cases.

About the Role

The Principal Engineer (PE) is WISEcode’s highest-impact
individual-contributor role — a technical leader who
combines deep hands-on engineering with clear architectural
ownership and accountability across major parts of the
platform. PEs write excellent code, but also define system
boundaries, long-term technical direction, and engineering
standards that influence the entire organization over time.

While this Principal Engineer role operates at a platform
and systems level, this position is anchored in WISEcode’s
AI, intelligence, and reasoning layers and requires deep,
opinionated expertise in designing systems that combine
probabilistic intelligence with deterministic software
foundations.

A WISEcode PE is both strategist and builder. You will:

Translate product ideas, domain concepts, and company vision
into intelligent systems that reason over food, ingredients,
nutrition, and user preferences.

Design and implement AI-powered features end-to-end,
including model selection, orchestration, inference
pipelines, confidence handling, and integration with product
workflows.



Guide teams through decisions on model tradeoffs, data
quality, evaluation strategies, observability,
explainability, and long-term system evolution.

Influence without authority — shaping engineering culture,
mentoring senior engineers, and raising the organizational
bar for how intelligence is built and trusted.

PEs at WISEcode operate as a flexible, high-impact bench.
You may embed temporarily into a product, data, or platform
team to accelerate an AI-heavy initiative, resolve
architectural ambiguity, or establish a new intelligence
capability. You’ll then step out, leaving behind reusable
patterns, clear interfaces, and higher organizational
confidence in intelligent systems.

Because of their breadth, PEs typically work closely with
the CTO and maintain visibility across products, data
systems, and infrastructure. Your success is measured not by
individual model performance alone, but by the reliability,
interpretability, and usefulness of intelligence across the
entire WISEcode ecosystem.

How Principal Engineers Operate at WISEcode

Systems thinkers with depth. PEs bring T-shaped skills: deep
expertise in AI systems, model integration, and intelligent
workflows, combined with broad understanding across data
modeling, software architecture, product experience, and
infrastructure.

Architects who execute. You can design an inference
pipeline, integrate LLMs or ML models into production
systems, implement evaluation and monitoring loops, and
understand how these components must evolve over time as
data, models, and use cases change.

Product-savvy technologists. PEs help shape product
direction, not just implement requirements. You anticipate
how intelligent behavior affects user trust, explainability,
personalization, and long-term product differentiation, and
help frame product decisions through technical clarity.

Pragmatic long-term thinkers. You balance speed of
experimentation with architectural integrity. You know when
probabilistic systems are appropriate and when deterministic
computation is required.

Multipliers. You leave teams stronger: clearer intelligence
patterns, better evaluation practices, safer AI usage, and
faster future delivery that does not depend on your constant
involvement.

AI Skills and Leadership

• WISEcode is an AI-native company. Every engineer is
expected to use AI well, but PEs set the standard. You will:

• Design AI systems that combine LLMs, traditional ML,
rules, and deterministic logic into coherent, testable
workflows

• Establish practices for evaluation, monitoring, and
iteration that make intelligent systems reliable and
trustworthy over time.

• Understand how data quality, prompt design, retrieval
strategies, and system architecture affect model behavior

that aligns with WISEcode’s mission of consumer trust and
empowerment.

AI is not a bolt-on at WISEcode — it is a horizontal
capability. PEs ensure intelligence is embedded thoughtfully
into products, workflows, and decision-making without
sacrificing correctness or clarity.
