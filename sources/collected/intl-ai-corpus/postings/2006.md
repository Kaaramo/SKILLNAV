About Blackbird:

Blackbird Labs is a loyalty and payments platform
establishing meaningful connections between the world’s best
restaurants and their customers.

Served up in 2022 by Ben Leventhal, co-founder of Eater and
Resy, Blackbird offers a fully customizable loyalty
programming platform for restaurants and a consumer app
designed to make every guest feel like a bonafide VIP no
matter where they dine. Backed by Union Square Ventures,
a16z, Amex Ventures, Variant Fund, and other forward-
thinking investors, Blackbird is out to revolutionize the
restaurant industry.

About the Job

Blackbird is looking for a Senior Platform Engineer to own
the platforms and tooling that make our engineering team
fast, reliable, and AI-augmented. You'll be responsible for
the infrastructure powering APIs that handle payments,
loyalty, and blockchain transactions for thousands of
restaurants, as well as the developer experience layer that
helps our engineers ship with confidence every day.

This is a high-leverage role. You'll own our AWS
infrastructure, CI/CD pipelines, observability stack, and
the emerging AI-assisted development workflows that are
becoming core to how we build software. You'll act as both a
technical owner and a multiplier: shipping platforms that
keep production humming while accelerating the velocity of
every engineer on the team.



Responsibilities

- Own and evolve our AWS infrastructure (Terraform)
- Maintain and improve CI/CD pipelines
- Drive observability across the platform, keeping systems
  performant where it matters most
- Build and expand AI-assisted developer workflows; you'll own
  this surface area
- Remove friction from shipping: local dev environments,
  Gradle optimization, build caching, and whatever else is
  slowing us down
- Push for the right technical investments at the right time;
  we're moving fast and the landscape keeps changing



Requirements

- 5+ years building and operating production systems on AWS
- Strong Terraform proficiency
- Hands-on experience with container orchestration (ECS
  Fargate or similar), CI/CD design, and deployment
  automation
- Comfortable in the JVM ecosystem: Spring Boot, Gradle, JVM
  tuning; Kotlin expertise not required
- Working knowledge of PostgreSQL operations and performance
- Experience with observability tooling (Datadog, CloudWatch,
  PagerDuty, or equivalent)
- Python or Bash for scripting and automation
- High ownership instincts; this is a small team, scope is
  fluid, and production will occasionally need your
  attention

Nice to Haves

- You actively use AI coding agents (Claude Code, Copilot,
  Cursor) and have opinions about what actually works
- Experience with blockchain infrastructure, web3 systems, or
  HashiCorp Vault
- Background building developer platforms or internal tooling
  other engineers rely on daily
