## Description complète

As our next AI Engineer, you’ll play a key role in helping
us build the UK’s go-to home improvement marketplace. From
bridging the gap between cutting-edge AI research and real-
world application, your work will help us move forward by
building the intelligent systems that simplify home
improvements for millions - fast, fair, and without the
faff.

Forget long lists, here’s what you’ll actually spend your
time on:

- Delivering production-ready AI solutions that drive results
  for our customers and trades.
- Spotting ways to improve how we work by identifying where AI
  (and where not AI) can solve complex business problems.
- Collaborating with cross-functional product teams to
  integrate intelligent workflows into our wider ecosystem.
- Making sure every interaction with our platform is smarter,
  faster, and leaves people feeling confident in
  Checkatrade.

It’s not about ticking every box. It’s about turning up
curious, ready to crack on. You’ll do well here if you
bring:

- Production AI Experience: A proven track record of
  designing, building, and deploying AI-powered solutions
  (not just training models, but making them work at scale).
- Programming Proficiency: You’re a pro with JavaScript or
  TypeScript and comfortable with modern software
  development frameworks.
- AI Fundamentals: A strong grip on algorithms, evals, agentic
  workflows, and MCP. You understand the trade-offs of
  different model types and know when a simpler solution is
  better.
- Solid Engineering Roots: Deep knowledge of design patterns,
  CI/CD pipelines, and version control.
- Communication Skills: The ability to translate complex
  technical concepts—like model performance or system trade-
  offs—to anyone from a fellow dev to a non-technical
  stakeholder.

We believe great work deserves great rewards. Here’s just a
taste:

- Competitive salary + annual bonus
- Employee Share Program
- Health, well-being, and learning support
- Gym membership, Smart Tech Scheme, and Cycle Scheme
- Birthday/Special day leave
- Buy/Sell holiday scheme
- 1 week’s paid charity leave and much more!

How we’ll get to know each other

- Final stage with cross-functional Stakeholders
