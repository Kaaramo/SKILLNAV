This role is about using AI tools as a force multiplier for
software engineering and consulting - not building,
training, or deploying AI models. The role focuses on
applying AI to improve software delivery practices,
observability, and documentation while working as a
consultant helping clients with AI-native transformation.

software architecture and delivery, not model development.

About the role and team:

This role is primarily a client facing role, focused both on
solving client problems and AI native transformation of
their own teams, however you will play a varied role across
all elements of our business, from pre-sales through to
marketing and thought leadership - you’re not afraid to lean

You have excellent consulting skills - you love

to lead them towards a better outcome. You care deeply about
software craftsmanship,  designing and building and shipping

multiplier to amplify your own impact and raise the bar for
the teams you work with. You’ve got a real appreciation of

ensure safe and ethical applications and You enjoy helping
simplify problems, make decisions, and have a bias to build

love using your experience to help others succeed through
mentoring and coaching.

What you’ll be doing:

- Work closely with client teams to understand their context,

- Share learning with both client teams and CreateFuture

  Deliver external thought leadership at conferences and
