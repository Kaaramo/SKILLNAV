About Coursera

Coursera was founded in 2012 by Stanford professors Andrew
Ng and Daphne Koller to make world-class learning accessible
to everyone, everywhere. Today, over 190 million learners
and 375+ university and industry partners use our platform
to gain skills in fields like AI, data science, technology,
and business. As a Delaware public benefit corporation and
Certified B Corp, we’re driven by the belief that learning
can transform lives through learning.

Why Join Us

At Coursera, we’re looking for inventors, innovators, and
lifelong learners ready to shape the future of education.
You’ll help build global programs and tools that power
online learning for millions turning bold ideas into real
impact. People who thrive here are customer-first builders
who move fast, simplify ruthlessly, and iterate relentlessly
on the metrics that matter.

We’re a globally distributed team and let you choose the
best way you work, whether it's from home, a Coursera hub,
or a co-working space near you. Our virtual hiring and
onboarding make it easy to join us and start making an
impact from anywhere. If you’re ready to make a global
impact, scale unique products exclusive to Coursera, and
expand your career horizons, apply below.

Job Overview:

We’re seeking a Senior Full-Stack AI Engineer to join a
small, cross-functional team focused on building AI-native
learning experiences from the ground up. This is a high-
impact role where you’ll create innovative, personalized
learning features, experiment with new AI capabilities, and
move fast from concept to prototype to production.

You’ll work closely with designers, product managers, and
other engineers in a maker-driven environment where everyone
contributes directly to building the product. The role
emphasizes practical use of Generative AI—both in the
features we ship and in how you work every day.

This role is an in-person role in our Mountain View office 3
days a week.

Responsibilities:

- Build and ship full-stack features using React (or similar),
  Node.js, and Python.
- Create AI-driven learning experiences—including hyper-
  personalization, adaptive content, and intelligent
  assistance—by leveraging Generative AI frameworks, APIs,
  and orchestration tools.
- Use AI proactively in your development workflow for
  scaffolding, refactoring, and testing code—while
  critically reviewing AI-generated output for quality and
  performance.
- Rapidly prototype, test, and iterate on ideas based on user
  feedback.
- Collaborate across disciplines to integrate AI capabilities
  into seamless, intuitive user experiences.
- Implement and maintain robust testing, deployment, and
  monitoring for AI-enabled features.

Why You’ll Love This Role

- Impact: Help shape the future of personalized education for
  millions of learners worldwide.
- Velocity: Work in a fast-moving environment with short
  iteration cycles and room for bold experimentation.
- Innovation: Build AI-native features and workflows from
  scratch, unencumbered by legacy constraints.
- Culture: Join a collaborative, hands-on team where your
  ideas can go from sketch to shipped in weeks.

Qualifications:

- 5 + years of professional experience in full-stack
  development (React/Next.js, Node.js, Python) and working
  with cloud environments (AWS, GCP, Azure).
- Knowledge of Generative AI concepts and experience applying
  them to create practical, user-facing applications.
- Hands-on experience using AI coding assistants (e.g., GitHub
  Copilot, ChatGPT) to speed up development without
  compromising quality.
- Understanding of RESTful API design, databases (SQL/NoSQL),
  and modern application architectures.
- Bonus: familiarity with retrieval-augmented generation
  (RAG), embeddings, or vector databases.
- Strong communication skills, curiosity, and a passion for
  pushing the boundaries of what’s possible in online
  learning.
