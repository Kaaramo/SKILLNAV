## Description complète

About Deductive AI

Deductive is building AI SRE Agents trusted by leading
global software companies to troubleshoot and resolve
complex production incidents. Our mission is to free
engineers and organizations from the burden of constant
firefighting, enabling them to focus on building and
achieving their greatest potential.

We are a team of world-class AI researchers and engineers
with backgrounds spanning UC Berkeley, IIT, Meta, Google,
Amazon, Databricks, and ThoughtSpot. We combine deep
academic research with hands-on industry experience to push
the boundaries of what AI can do for reliability
engineering.

Role Overview

As a Forward Deployed Engineer, you’ll work directly with
customers to help them realize the value of Deductive during
POCs and rollouts. You’ll learn how their teams handle
incidents and reliability challenges, then adapt and extend
our AI platform to fit their needs. This role blends
technical problem-solving with customer collaboration,
configuring and fine-tuning Deductive to deliver clear,
measurable outcomes. You’ll work closely with our Go-to-
Market and Engineering teams, serving as the voice of the
customer.

What You’ll Do

- Own and drive successful deployments of Deductive, guiding
  customers from scoping and integration through production
  readiness and measurable impact.
- Configure and optimize Deductive deployments to fit the use
  cases in customer environments and deliver value quickly.
- Act as a trusted technical partner to customers, helping
  them navigate observability and reliability challenges
  while shaping effective practices.
- Build automations and scripts that extend Deductive across
  observability platforms, codebases, and knowledge systems.
- Collaborate with Product and Engineering to translate
  customer insights into product enhancements and reusable
  best practices.

What We’re Looking For

- 3+ years of experience as Software Engineer, Platform
  Engineer or Site Reliability Engineer
- Strong fundamentals and expertise in cloud infrastructure
  (e.g. AWS, GCP, Kubernetes) and observability (e.g.
  Datadog, Grafana, Prometheus, OpenTelemetry)
- Strong programming skills to build automations using one or
  more scripting languages (e.g. python).
- Proven track record in fast-moving, ambiguous situations
  where both precision and urgency matter.
- Strong Plus: Experience working directly with customers in
  roles like Forward Deployed Engineer, Sales or Solutions
  Engineer.

Why Join Us

At Deductive, you’ll have the opportunity to:

- Join a team of world-class researchers and builders.
- Help shape how AI transforms reliability and software
  engineering and make history.
- Work directly with some of the world’s most advanced
  engineering organizations.
- Be part of a fast-growing startup where your contributions
  directly shape the product and customer success.

We offer highly competitive compensation, equity, and
benefits.
