### Raisonnement classification (LLM upstream)

This is a frontend engineering VP role at BlackRock that
uses AI tools to accelerate development (code generation,
testing, domain learning), but the core work is building
frontend systems and frameworks. The role does not involve
building, training, fine-tuning, or deploying AI/ML models.
AI is used as a development accelerator tool, similar to
using Copilot or ChatGPT to assist coding. The primary
responsibility is frontend platform engineering for Aladdin.

## Description complète

About this role

Do you enjoy solving complex engineering challenges,
experimenting with new approaches, and working in an
environment that combines the discipline of an enterprise
platform with the curiosity and pace of a startup?

At BlackRock, we look for engineers who are excited to push
the boundaries of modern frontend development - from
advanced UI frameworks to high‑performance micro‑frontends
and platform‑level tooling that sits at the heart of
investment workflows handling real financial outcomes.

In Core UI Engineering, you’ll work on systems that operate
at global scale and evolve the foundations of our web
platform. This includes building reliable, performant user
experiences, shaping shared frameworks, and increasingly
exploring how AI‑assisted workflows can accelerate how we
design, code, and deliver value. We are evolving toward
purpose driven, outcome oriented product engineering, where
humans guide and validate AI generated output while taking
full responsibility for product, engineering, and business
outcomes.

Our technology supports Portfolio Management teams
overseeing over $14 trillion in assets, and the products you
build will sit at the heart of tools used daily by investors
making decisions that impact millions of people worldwide.
It’s engineering work with real reach, real complexity, and
real impact.



Why Join Aladdin Engineering?



Aladdin is BlackRock’s investment platform, used across the
firm and by many of the world’s largest financial
institutions. It combines rich analytics, portfolio
management workflows, trading capabilities, and operational
tools into a single ecosystem.

As part of Aladdin Engineering, and specifically our Core UI
engineering efforts, you will help build the next generation
of our web platform. This includes:

- Modern UI frameworks used by thousands of users

- Highly scalable micro‑frontend architectures

- Performance‑critical components powering real‑time portfolio
  decisions

- Developer tooling that improves reliability, observability,
  and speed

- Early experimentation with AI, workflows assisted code
  generation and testing

- Evolving standards and best practices for teams across the
  company

- We take pride in engineering excellence, in delivering value
  quickly, and in continuously adapting our processes as new
  technologies emerge.



What You’ll Be Doing



- Collaborate with colleagues across UK, US, India, and other
  global offices.

- Build highly available, resilient, and performant frontend
  systems and frameworks used by thousands of users.

- Contribute to the evolution of Aladdin’s multi‑asset trading
  and portfolio management capabilities, using AI to
  accelerate domain learning and solution design

- Shape product direction together with Product, Design, and
  Engineering leadership.

- Mentor and guide frontend engineers, helping cultivate a
  culture of curiosity, inclusion, and technical excellence.

- Work in an Agile setup where autonomy, ownership, and
  accountability matter.

- Use AI proactively from day one, both as a development
  accelerator and as a tool you validate and refine to
  ensure highquality outcomes.

- Ability to combine generalist problemsolving with strong
  engineering fundamentals, guiding AI tools while retaining
  human ownership of outcomes.

- Design elegant solutions from idea to production, with
  strong engineering discipline and early foundations to
  keep human and AIgenerated code reliable, clear, and free
  of technical debt.



What We’re Looking For

- AI 1st engineers, who has experience using AI development

Nice to Have / Great to Learn Here
