The Opportunity:

The Citizen Developer (Finance) identifies, designs, and
builds low-code automation solutions—primarily Robotic
Process Automation (RPA)—to streamline finance processes
such as accounts payable/receivable, reconciliations,
journal preparation, reporting, and controls. This role will
also expand into AI-enabled process improvement, leveraging
approved AI capabilities (e.g., intelligent document
processing, generative AI assistance, and AI-driven workflow
enhancements) to improve decision support, reduce manual
effort, and increase process insight—while adhering to
governance, security, and compliance standards.

Job Description

The Opportunity:

What Your Impact Will Be:




- Identify and prioritize automation opportunities across
  Finance (AP/AR, close, reconciliations, reporting, master
  data, audit support), including candidates for AI
  augmentation (e.g., document understanding, variance
  narratives, triage, classification).
- Build and maintain RPA workflows using approved low-code
  tools (e.g., UiPath, Power Automate, Automation Anywhere,
  Blue Prism) and associated connectors.
- Partner with Finance and IT/CoE to pilot and scale AI-
  enabled automations such as:
  - Intelligent document processing (IDP) for invoices,
    statements, and support documentation
  - AI-assisted email/case triage and routing
  - Automated narrative generation for reconciliations/variance
    explanations (with human review)
  - Knowledge search/Q&A over approved finance procedures and
    policies
- Document future-state processes (SOPs, process maps, user
  stories, acceptance criteria) including AI “human-in-the-
  loop” checkpoints.
- Implement strong exception handling, audit logs,
  monitoring/alerting, and operational runbooks for both
  bots and AI-assisted workflows.
- Execute unit testing and support UAT; assist with deployment
  and hypercare; support ongoing enhancements and incident
  resolution.
- Ensure solutions meet SOX/internal controls, data privacy,
  model/data usage policies, and security requirements
  (including access controls and segregation of duties).
- Track and report automation value (hours saved, cycle time
  reduction, error reduction), and for AI initiatives
  include quality measures (e.g., accuracy, exception rate,
  reviewer effort).
