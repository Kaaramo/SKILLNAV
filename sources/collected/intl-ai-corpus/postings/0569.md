## Description complète

Snowflake is about empowering enterprises to achieve their
full potential — and people too. With a culture that’s all
in on impact, innovation, and collaboration, Snowflake is
the sweet spot for building big, moving fast, and taking
technology — and careers — to the next level.

The Opportunity: Accelerate the AI Data Cloud Journey

The Account Engineer is a high-impact, cross-functional role
designed to accelerate the customer journey from initial
acquisition to successful platform adoption and expansion on
the Snowflake AI Data Cloud. You will contribute to this
mission by driving initial utilization, leveraging modern
AI-powered tools and coding agents to ensure technical
success.

This role bridges the critical gap by helping new and early-
stage customers achieve rapid implementation, quick value,
and a world-class experience on the Snowflake platform. You
will be the dedicated customer technical partner,
responsible for driving technical enablement and supporting
the overall activation and adoption of our customers initial
use cases.

This is a fantastic opportunity for technical professionals
to develop deep expertise in cloud data architecture, make a
measurable impact on customer success, and contribute to one
of the fastest-growing technology companies.

Key Responsibilities and Deliverables

The Account Engineer manages a critical portfolio of new and
early-stage customers, and is accountable for accelerating
their time-to-value and achieving sustained platform growth.
Operating as a Full-Stack Technical Resource, this role
blends deep hands-on expertise with the strategic acumen of
a business leader.

Strategic Ownership and Value Creation

- Lead Your Account Micro-Business: Take ownership of the
  technical success and consumption trajectory within your
  assigned portfolio, driving adoption metrics in your
  assigned accounts, acting as a single point of contact for
  all customer needs.
- Driving Outcome-Based Utilization: Apply an outcome-based
  selling mindset to customer engagements, focusing on how
  the Snowflake AI Data Cloud helps them solve complex data
  challenges and unlock maximum business value—not just
  product features.
- Strategic Advisory and Guidance: Serve as a trusted
  strategic advisor immediately following the initial
  contract close, providing guidance on data strategy,
  technology stack integration, and ensuring a swift,
  successful activation of Use Cases.
- Deliver Technical Value with AI: Deliver technical value
  efficiently by leveraging cutting-edge AI-powered
  development tools and automation to accelerate solutions
  and implementation guidance, helping customers move faster
  to production.

Technical Execution and Collaboration

- Architectural Leadership: Provide customers with critical,
  Snowflake-specific foundational guidance, including
  Architectural Best Practices, Workload Isolation &
  Warehouse Utilization, Resource Monitoring & Alerts,
  Budgeting, and RBAC/Security Setup.
- Cross-Functional Collaboration: Collaborate effectively with
  the Account Team to highlight risks and establish and
  nurture trusted relationships with internal Snowflake
  teams (e.g., Professional Services, Support) and external
  partners to ensure seamless resource orchestration.
- Develop Scalable Programs: Create and deliver reusable,
  high-impact programs, such as Hands-on Labs (HoLs) and
  webinars, to educate a wide customer audience on specific
  platform features and industry best practices.
- Technical Feedback Loop & Innovation: Act as a key feedback
  loop from the field to influence future product
  development, working closely with Product Management and
  Engineering teams.
- Expert Support and Triage: Efficiently triage a broad array
  of incoming customer inquiries, assisting the field team
  with demos, troubleshooting, and identifying opportunities
  to qualify potential business value for the Expansion
  team.

Required Qualifications

We are seeking highly skilled and motivated technical
professionals eager to launch their next career chapter with
us.

- Technical Aptitude: Hands-on experience with SQL is
  required.
- Data & Cloud Knowledge: Strong foundational understanding
  and experience with data architecture, data analytics, and
  the core concepts of major cloud platforms (AWS, Azure,
  Google Cloud).
- Growth Mindset: A strong desire to learn, grow your
  technical skills, and become an expert in the Snowflake
  platform.
- Communication Skills: Excellent written and verbal
  communication skills with the ability to communicate
  technical concepts clearly and effectively to diverse
  audiences.
- Professional Skills: Ability to handle multiple projects
  simultaneously and possess Project Management skills to
  track, manage, and drive customers to an agreed-upon
  timeline.
- Education: University degree in computer science,
  engineering, mathematics, or related fields, or equivalent
  practical experience in a technical role.

What Sets You Apart (Preferred Qualifications)

- SnowPro Core certification
- Proficiency in additional coding languages, particularly
  Python, Java, or Scala, especially in a data or
  application development context.
- Experience with modern data technologies (e.g., CI/CD
  tooling, orchestration tools) or a basic understanding of
  Data Science concepts.
- Prior experience or strong interest in leveraging AI-powered
  development tools and coding agents to increase solution
  delivery efficiency.
