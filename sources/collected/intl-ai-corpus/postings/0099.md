## Description complète

Applied AI Engineer - New York

Location: New York City Innovation Hub (In-person)

About This Opportunity

We're assembling an exceptional team of AI engineers in NYC.
The kind of people who’ve shipped breakthrough AI products
that millions actually use. This isn’t about office
amenities or typical startup perks (although we have those
too!). It’s about working alongside some of the most
talented engineers and product minds in AI, solving
fascinating problems at scale.

You’ll collaborate in-person with a carefully selected group
designed for breakthrough thinking, backed by the resources
and reach of Automattic’s ecosystem spanning WordPress.com,
WooCommerce, Tumblr, and more. Think of it as an elite AI
lab with the distribution and impact of Automattic’s
ecosystem.

What You'll Do

- Ship user-facing AI features across Automattic’s product
  ecosystem; from content creation tools to e-commerce
  recommendations to messaging experiences.
- Build and iterate rapidly on AI-powered products that
  directly impact millions of users.
- Collaborate intensively with PMs, designers, and engineers
  in a fast-paced environment optimized for innovation.
- Experiment and scale successful AI innovations from
  prototype to production across multiple products.
- Push the boundaries of what’s possible when exceptional AI
  talent works together.
- Stay current with AI developments that unlock new user
  experiences.

You'll Thrive Here If You

- Have shipped AI features that users actually use; production
  experience over theoretical knowledge.
- Are exceptionally talented; you’re the person other great
  engineers want to work with.
- Are full-stack capable; comfortable from model APIs to user
  interfaces.
- Move fast without breaking things; prototype quickly while
  building for scale.
- Work best collaboratively; your best ideas come from working
  directly with teammates.
- Think product-first; you know great AI is invisible to
  users; it just makes their experience better.
- Are drawn to working with other exceptional people rather
  than just seeking comfortable work environments.

Technical Experience We Value

- Production experience with LLMs (APIs or custom
  implementations) at meaningful scale.
- Full-stack development (we use PHP, Python, and JavaScript,
  primarily).
- Building AI-powered user interfaces that perform well at
  scale.
- Machine learning fundamentals and staying current with rapid
  field developments.
- Track record of technical leadership or influence on high-
  performing teams.

Bonus Points

- Developed consumer AI products that scaled to a significant
  number of users.
- Experience with WordPress, e-commerce, or messaging
  platforms.
- Open source contributions showing your thinking.

Why This Role Matters

Shape how AI transforms products used by hundreds of
millions people across the planet. From helping creators
tell better stories to enabling merchants compete
effectively. You'll tackle fascinating challenges across our
entire ecosystem with the backing of a profitable, mission-
driven company.

Salary range: $130,000-$250,000 USD

We are searching for high-caliber candidates with the skills
and qualities to have a net positive for Automattic. Pay

can bring.

About Automattic

Now in our 20th year, we’re  the people behind
WordPress.com, WooCommerce, Tumblr, Simplenote, Jetpack,
Longreads, Day One, PocketCasts, and more. We believe in
making the web a better place.

We’re a distributed company with 1500 Automatticians in
nearly every corner of the globe, speaking 110 different
languages. Enriched by this diversity, we’re united by a
singular mission: to democratize publishing, commerce, and
messaging so anyone with a story can tell it, anyone with a
product can sell it, and everyone can manage their
communications from a single source. In short, we help
maintain a balance in society, creating and continually
refining powerful tools people can use to compete
fairly—regardless of income, gender, politics, language, or
where they live in the world.


We believe in Open Source, and the vast majority of our work
is available under the GPL.
