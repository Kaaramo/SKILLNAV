### Raisonnement classification (LLM upstream)

This is a backend engineering role focused on building
infrastructure and services that power AI applications. The
engineer works ON prompt engineering, model integration, and
implementing multi-agent architectures (A2A protocols, MCP
servers) rather than training or fine-tuning models
themselves. While hands-on with AI systems, the work centers
on orchestration, observability, and production deployment
of AI capabilities built by others.

## Responsabilités

- Design, develop, and maintain scalable and fault-tolerant backend services that power AI applications
- Apply advanced prompt engineering techniques to optimize model performance, manage context windows, and ensure high-quality responses
- Implement model integration patterns to connect and orchestrate various external and internal services
- Develop Agent-to-Agent (A2A) protocol communication strategies for complex multi-agent architectures
- Implement systems for Managing Conversational Protocols (MCP servers) and A2A protocol for long-running, stateful interactions
- Define and implement observability standards for AI applications including metrics for latency, cost, token usage, drift, and user-perceived quality
- Collaborate with ML Scientists and Data Scientists to transition prototypes and models into production-ready, high-availability services
- Mentor junior engineers and champion best practices in backend development and MLOps
