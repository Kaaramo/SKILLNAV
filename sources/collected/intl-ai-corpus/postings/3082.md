## Description complète

We’re seeking someone to join our CDRR Technology team as a
Sr AI Developer, in Non-Financial Risk Technology to design,
build, and support production-grade AI applications and
agentic LLM solutions using Python and modern ML/LLM
frameworks.

In the Technology division, we leverage innovation to build
the connections and capabilities that power our Firm,
enabling our clients and colleagues to redefine markets and
shape the future of our communities.

This is Director position that develops and maintains
software solutions that support business needs.

Since 1935, Morgan Stanley is known as a global leader in
financial services, always evolving and innovating to better
serve our clients and our communities in more than 40
countries around the world.

What you’ll do in the role:

- Build systems using LLMs and multimodal models, retrieval
  and vector databases, and orchestration frameworks (e.g.,
  LangChain/LangGraph, MCP), integrated with internal tools
  and data.
- Own the full lifecycle: discovery, prototyping, validation,
  deployment, monitoring, iteration, and support.
- Track new model releases, vendor updates, and emerging
  patterns in agentic AI.
- Run targeted experiments with new capabilities and share
  timely recommendations that influence roadmaps and
  standards.
- Implement solutions for environment configuration
  (Dev/QA/Prod) and, build processes while documenting
  efforts and experiences with a focus on educating others
  to ensure reliability and ongoing supportability of the
  platform.
- Write production-quality code and integrate APIs across
  internal platforms and external providers (e.g., Google,
  OpenAI, Databricks, AWS, Microsoft).
- Make pragmatic architectural decisions balancing speed,
  scale, security, reliability, and cost.
- Build reusable components, templates, and playbooks that
  accelerate future development and reduce friction for
  downstream teams.
- Participate in technology evaluations and play an active
  role in ongoing strategy based on technology trends, best
  practices and industry standards.
- Bachelor's degree in computer science, or a related field,
  or equivalent experience.

What you’ll bring to the role:

- 6+ years of hands-on development experience in Python and
  LLMs such as OpenAI, ChatGPT, Claude, and/or Copilot.
- Evaluate model capabilities and select appropriate AI tools
  based on workflow needs, risk profile, and operational
  constraints.
- Design guardrails for agentic systems, including tool
  safety, security controls, data handling, and human-in-
  the-loop review as needed.
- Partner with Standards, Legal, Security, and GRC to ensure
  compliant data use, responsible deployment, and audit-
  ready practices.
- Establish monitoring and incident response patterns to
  detect issues and maintain trust and reliability at scale.
- Basic Linux and Windows command-line knowledge
- Experience with DevOps practices and CI/CD technologies
  including Jenkins, Stash, GitHub, Git, Jira (or
  equivalents).
- Experience with Kubernetes (EKS/AKS/GKE/OpenShift) in a
  clustered environment.
- Track record of having worked on, and been involved in, the
  deployment and management of infrastructure at scale.
- Able to troubleshoot, problem solve and be an analytical
  thinker.
- Strong interpersonal, written, and verbal engagement skills
  ensuring an ability to communicate at all levels across
  multiple project aspects.
- Focus on good time-management, task prioritization and an
  ability to meet agreed upon deadlines.
- Confidence in your skills, a self-starter and a team player
  possessed with a high level of commitment and enthusiasm.
- Familiarity with, or experience working in, an Agile
  environment leveraging Kanban or Scrum.
- Thorough understanding of security architecture principles,
  design, and engineering best practices.
