## Description complète

Stuut is transforming accounts receivable for B2B
companies—making collections smarter and faster for
companies that have historically relied on manual processes
that are labor intensive and costly. Our platform is gaining
traction with finance teams across industrials, chemicals,
and manufacturing sectors from Fortune 10 brands to scaling
midmarkets. We're backed by top-tier investors including
a16z, Khosla, Activant, 1984 Ventures and Page One.

The Role

We’re hiring an AI Sales Engineer to partner closely with
Sales, Forward Deployed Engineers, and Product to drive
successful evaluations and adoption of Stuut’s AI-powered
cash flow automation platform. You’ll play a critical role
in guiding midmarket and enterprise customers through
complex technical discovery, solution design, and proof-of-
value—ensuring they understand how Stuut delivers measurable
business impact.

This is a high-impact, customer-facing role for someone who
thrives at the intersection of technical problem-solving,
business context, and execution. You’ll translate customer
challenges into technical solutions, design ERP
integrations, support product launches, and influence buying
decisions—while collaborating deeply across Sales, Product,
and Engineering.

What You’ll Do

- Serve as the technical partner to Sales and Forward Deployed
  Engineers throughout the sales cycle, from discovery to
  close.
- Lead technical discovery sessions and product demonstrations
  tailored to real customer workflows and data.
- Design and support proof-of-concept engagements with clear
  success metrics (e.g. DSO reduction, collection rate lift,
  automation ROI).
- Architect integration approaches for customer ERP
  environments (SAP, Oracle, NetSuite, Dynamics) in
  collaboration with IT, finance, and operations teams.
- Develop technical proposals, solution documentation, and ROI
  models aligned to customer requirements.
- Support new product launches by contributing to go-to-market
  collateral, training internal teams, and partnering with
  early customers.
- Surface customer and technical insights that inform platform
  improvements and product strategy.
- Build trusted relationships with customer technical
  stakeholders and represent Stuut in technical discussions
  and industry forums.

You Might Be a Fit If You…

- Have 4–7 years of experience in Solutions Engineering, Sales
  Engineering, pre-sales, or technical consulting within B2B
  SaaS.
- Are comfortable supporting complex, enterprise sales cycles
  and influencing six-figure-plus deals.
- Have strong familiarity with ERP systems and financial
  software ecosystems (SAP, Oracle, NetSuite, Dynamics).
- Understand APIs, data integration patterns, authentication,
  and enterprise architecture concepts.
- Have experience with iPaaS tools (e.g. MuleSoft, Boomi,
  Workato) or adjacent integration technologies.
- Can communicate complex technical concepts clearly to both
  technical and non-technical stakeholders.
- Bring curiosity around AI/ML, LLMs, and agentic
  workflows—and a desire to learn quickly.
- Thrive in high-growth startup environments that reward
  ownership, collaboration, and bias toward action.
