## Description complète

About Voodoo

Founded in 2013, Voodoo is a tech company that creates
mobile games and apps with a mission to entertain the world.
Gathering 800 employees, 7 billion downloads, and over 200
million active users, Voodoo is the #3 mobile publisher
worldwide in terms of downloads after Google and Meta. Our
portfolio includes chart-topping games like Mob Control and
Block Jam, alongside popular apps such as BeReal and Wizz.

This position is fully remote within CET ±3h.

Team

Joining our Live games team means collaborating with gaming
industry experts on globally renowned, enduring games while
embracing exciting new entrepreneurial ventures. With over
seven billion downloads worldwide and a portfolio of more
than 10 resilient hybrid games generating more than $20m per
year, we are the world's largest and most successful hybrid
publisher.

You will be joining one of our Live Studios, working on
Hole.io, one of Voodoo's top games.

Voodoo is always looking for the next "unfair advantage." We
are currently seeking an AI visionary to join our Live
Studios for a high-impact, one-month mission. Your goal is
to help us leverage cutting-edge technology to optimize our
production at scale, ensuring our developers and artists
spend less time on repetitive tasks and more time on what
truly matters: making the game fun.

Role

We are looking for an AI Engineer to conduct a comprehensive
audit of our development and creative workflows. Rather than
just following trends, you will take a pragmatic, results-
oriented approach to identify where Artificial Intelligence
can concretely speed up our delivery cycles and enhance our
creative output.

- Audit & Identify: Analyze our current game development
  lifecycle—from asset creation and coding to QA and
  marketing—to find bottlenecks that can be solved by AI.
- Strategic Roadmap: Develop a clear, prioritized plan of AI
  integrations (LLMs, Generative Art, Auto-testing, etc.)
  that offer the highest ROI for our studio.
- Workflow Optimization: Design custom AI-driven workflows and
  tool recommendations that allow our teams to automate
  "busy work" without sacrificing quality.
- Hands-on Prototyping: Demonstrate proof-of-concepts for AI
  tools that can be integrated into our Unity-based
  production environment.
- Knowledge Transfer: Empower our senior leadership and
  technical teams with the knowledge to maintain and evolve
  these AI strategies long after your mission is complete.

With this role, you will have a direct impact on the
efficiency of a world-class gaming studio. Your expertise
will help us cut through the noise and implement AI
solutions that provide a tangible competitive edge in the
casual markets.

Profile

- AI Expert: Deep, up-to-date knowledge of the AI landscape,
  including LLMs (GPT, Claude, local models), Generative
  Media (Stable Diffusion, Midjourney), and AI-driven coding
  assistants.
- Game Dev Context: A strong understanding of the game
  development process (Unity/C# environment preferred) to
  ensure your recommendations are technically feasible.
- Efficiency Obsessed: A track record of implementing AI
  solutions that have demonstrably reduced production time
  or costs in previous organizations.
- Analytical & Pragmatic: Ability to distinguish between "AI
  hype" and tools that provide genuine utility for a fast-
  moving gaming studio.
- Strong Communicator: Excellent English skills and the
  ability to explain complex AI concepts to both technical
  developers and creative leads.
- Independent & Proactive: Experience working in short-term
  consultancy roles where you are expected to deliver high-
  quality results with minimal hand-holding.

Benefits

- Other benefits according to the country you reside
