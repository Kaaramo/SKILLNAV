This role involves directly configuring, deploying, and

Agentic Workflows) on the ServiceNow platform, including
prompt configuration, intent modeling, and AI agent-to-agent

intents, and designing agentic workflows — making it ai-

POSITION SUMMARY

CVS Health is seeking a ServiceNow AI Engineer, a platform
engineering role responsible for enabling, scaling, and
operating enterprise AI capabilities across the ServiceNow
platform. This role focuses on Now Assist and Moveworks
integration, delivering secure, governed, and reusable AI
services that can be consumed consistently across HRSD,
ITSM, ITOM, and broader ESM domains.

Operating within the ServiceNow Platform Engineering
organization, this role emphasizes configuration‑first AI
enablement, standardized patterns, and guardrails—ensuring
AI solutions are scalable, measurable, and aligned with
enterprise security, privacy, and governance standards.

Role Scope & Operating Model

- Scope: Platform‑wide (shared AI services across all
  ServiceNow products)
- Model: Central Platform Engineering (not embedded in a
  single product team)
- Primary AI Capabilities: Now Assist Skills, Now Assist
  Agents, Now Assist Agentic Workflows, Moveworks Agent to
  Agent Integration, AI Control Tower, Now Assist for
  Creator
- Engineering Approach: Configuration‑first, with selective
  scripting and integration development as needed
- Success Metrics (Examples)

- Adoption and enablement of Now Assist and Moveworks across
  ServiceNow domains
- Reduction in manual handling time through AI assistance
- Self‑service containment and deflection improvements
- Stability and upgrade safety of AI configurations
- Compliance with AI governance and audit requirements

What we expect of you

1) Enterprise AI Enablement (Now Assist & Moveworks)

- Configure, deploy, and operate Now Assist capabilities
  (Skills, Agents, Agentic Workflows, Agent to Agent
  Integration, AI Control Tower, Now Assist for Creator).
- Integrate and align Moveworks with ServiceNow experiences,
  ensuring seamless handoff, consistent intents, and
  enterprise‑approved patterns.
- Develop within platform‑level AI standards for enterprise
  re-use and scalability.
- Support Citizen Developer model for AI Enablement.
- Support ESM SDLC process (Development, Testing, Code
  Migration, Deployment)

2) Platform Architecture & Reusable Patterns

- Design and maintain reusable AI configurations, including
  Skills and Agents.
- Define reference architectures for common use cases across
  AI configurations.
- Ensure AI solutions align with ServiceNow platform
  architecture, performance expectations, and upgrade
  compatibility.

3) Configuration‑First Engineering with Selective Scripting

- Leverage out‑of‑the‑box and configurable AI features
  wherever possible to maximize maintainability and upgrade
  safety.
- Use scripting and custom logic only when required to extend
  capabilities, integrate systems, or enforce enterprise
  guardrails.
- Build and maintain integrations using Integration Hub, APIs,
  and event‑driven patterns to support AI‑initiated actions.

4) Responsible AI, Security, and Governance

- Responsible AI Governance to ensure:
  - Appropriate data access boundaries
  - Role‑based controls and logging
  - Human‑in‑the‑loop and escalation patterns
- Support architecture reviews, AI technical governance
  boards, and risk assessments for AI features.
- Document AI designs, data sources, and controls to support
  audit and compliance requirements.

5) Measurement, Optimization, and Observability

- Support the definition of and monitoring of platform‑level
  AI KPIs.
- Continuously tune prompts, intents, and configurations based
  on usage data and outcomes.
- Partner with product teams to validate AI value realization
  across domains.

6) Platform Operations & Lifecycle Management

- Establish operational runbooks for AI capabilities,
  including monitoring, incident response, and problem
  management.
- Manage AI feature lifecycle across environments (lower →
  production), including testing, releases, and upgrades.
- Evaluate new ServiceNow AI features and vendor enhancements,
  providing recommendations for platform adoption.
