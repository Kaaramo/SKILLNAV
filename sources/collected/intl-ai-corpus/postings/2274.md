We are seeking an AI/ML Engineer to join the Edwin AI team.

Here's a closer look at this key role:

- LLM & NLP-Powered Insights for Observability: Build and
  optimize large language model (LLM)-based solutions that
  generate human-readable incident reports, root cause
  explanations, and remediation suggestions. Leverage
  retrieval-augmented generation (RAG) to surface relevant
  documentation, logs, and knowledge base insights for IT
  teams.
- AI-Powered Search & Observability Context Awareness: Develop
  semantic search and context-aware retrieval solutions to
  help IT teams quickly find relevant data, logs, and
  metrics.
- Scalability & Deployment in Cloud Environments: Optimize AI
  models for real-time performance in distributed, cloud-
  native environments (AWS, GCP, Kubernetes, etc.).
- Collaboration & Thought Leadership: Work cross-functionally
  with platform engineers, product teams, and data
  scientists to align AI-driven features with customer
  needs.
- AI first attitude: Stay ahead of AI/ML advancements

What You'll Need:

- 2-3 years of experience in AI/ML engineering, with a focus
