Founded in 1972, Atari is one of the world’s most iconic
consumer brands and a pioneer in the video game industry,
known for creating classics like Pong, Asteroids, and
Centipede. Today, Atari Inc. continues to build on its
legacy by developing games, hardware, and experiences that
honor the past while driving innovation for the future.

Over the past two years, we've been building Atari India, a
growing team that plays a critical role in supporting our
global operations. We're proud of the team we've assembled
so far, and we’re just getting started. As part of a lean,
high-impact organization, the team in India works closely
with colleagues in North America and Europe on projects that
move the company forward. Whether you're helping launch a
new game, keeping our infrastructure secure, or supporting
day-to-day operations, your work here matters. Join us as we
continue to grow Atari India and build the future of a
legendary brand.

About the Role

We're seeking a Senior AI Engineer to lead cutting-edge
AI/ML solutions across multiple initiatives. You'll combine
hands-on development with strategic thinking, working on
projects from intelligent automation to predictive analytics
while shaping our AI strategy.

Responsibilities

- Design, develop, and deploy production AI/ML models for
  intelligent automation, predictive systems, pattern
  recognition, and decision support
- Research and experiment with state-of-the-art AI techniques
  (deep learning, NLP, computer vision, RL) to identify new
  opportunities
- Build and optimize ML infrastructure including training
  pipelines, model serving, data preprocessing, and
  monitoring frameworks
- Develop transformation systems that process structured data,
  understand patterns, and enable intelligent cross-platform
  automation
- Integrate LLMs using LangChain, LlamaIndex, and similar
  frameworks to create conversational systems, document
  processing, and automated workflows
- Design agentic AI systems that autonomously interact with
  tools, APIs, and services to accomplish multi-step tasks
- Build integrations between AI systems and third-party
  applications via RESTful APIs, webhooks, and event-driven
  architectures
- Collaborate with product, engineering, and data science
  teams to identify AI opportunities and integrate
  capabilities
- Establish ML best practices including versioning, A/B
  testing, monitoring, and continuous improvement processes
- Stay current with AI research, evaluate emerging
  technologies, and recommend strategic investments
- Mentor junior engineers, conduct code reviews, and build
  strong AI engineering culture

Requirements

- 5+ in AI/ML engineering with proven track record deploying
  production AI systems, especially LLM-based applications
  and intelligent automation
- Deep expertise in LangChain, LlamaIndex, or similar LLM
  frameworks, with strong understanding of prompt
  engineering, RAG, and AI orchestration
- Strong software engineering skills in Python,
  JavaScript/TypeScript, RESTful APIs, microservices, and
  cloud platforms (AWS/Azure/GCP)
- Production experience with LLMs including prompt
  engineering, fine-tuning, RAG implementation, and managing
  API limits/costs
- Experience building agentic AI systems and orchestrating
  workflows that integrate multiple AI models and external
  services
- Excellent problem-solving, communication skills, and ability
  to work in cross-functional teams

Bonus Points

- Experience with major LLM providers (OpenAI, Anthropic,
  Google, Azure OpenAI) and vector databases (Pinecone,
  Weaviate, ChromaDB) for RAG
- Open-source contributions to LLM frameworks, autonomous
  agents, or AI orchestration tools
- Production-scale conversational AI, chatbots, or assistants
  with error handling and context management

Preferred Qualifications

- Master's or Bachelor’s in Computer Science, AI, or related
  field with focus on ML, NLP, or software engineering
- Experience with ML frameworks (PyTorch, TensorFlow, scikit-
  learn) for custom model development and fine-tuning
