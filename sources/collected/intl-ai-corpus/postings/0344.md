## Description complète

About us:

Solirius Reply, part of the Reply Group, delivers technical
consultancy and application delivery to our clients in order
to solve real world problems and allow our clients to
respond to an ever-changing technical landscape. We partner
closely with our clients, embedding our consultants into
their businesses and providing a bespoke service, allowing
us to truly understand our clients’ needs.

It is this close collaboration with our clients that has
enabled us to grow rapidly in recent years and will drive
our ambitious future growth plans. We currently have over
300 consultants working with a variety of clients from both
the public and private sectors, such as the Ministry of
Justice, Department for Education, FCDOS, UEFA,
International Olympic Committee and Mercedes Benz.

We operate as a flat organisation and believe in trusting
and supporting our team to operate independently. We pride
ourselves on being specialists at what we do, making the
most of our consultants’ expertise in their fields to
provide a best-in-class service to our clients. All our
consultants have opportunities to work on a range of
different projects, providing a broad range of knowledge on
which to develop their careers and progress in the direction
they choose.

About you:

You are a motivated and adaptable professional with a strong
analytical mindset and a passion for using technology to
solve real-world problems. You enjoy working in
collaborative, agile teams and take pride in delivering
high-quality solutions that make a tangible impact. With
strong communication skills and a consultative approach,
you’re comfortable engaging with clients, understanding
their needs, and translating them into effective outcomes.
You understand and align with Solirius Reply Values.

The Role:

We are looking for an experienced AI Engineer to join our
team here at Solirius. You will join a multidisciplinary
practice comprising Data Scientists, Data Engineers, and
Software Engineers, developing and delivering exciting
projects with a fantastic team of technology experts.

Your primary focus will be designing, architecting, and
deploying robust AI solutions and pipelines that enable
advanced analysis and Generative AI capabilities, often in
highly regulated environments. Either drawing on existing
expertise, or with some development, you will be capable of
advancing further to design complex, multi-agentic systems
that solve real-world problems.

Additionally, you will act as a technical champion for
modern development practices, utilising and coaching others
on AI-assisted and agentic coding techniques to elevate our,
and our clients’, engineering velocity.

Requirements

Key Responsibilities:

- Core AI Solution Design: Design, build, and maintain
  scalable AI architectures and pipelines that support
  business requirements, ranging from predictive modelling
  to Generative AI applications.
- LLM Integration: Operationalise Large Language Models (e.g.,
  GPT, Llama, Mistral) and NLP workflows (BERT, topic
  modelling) for production environments.
- Agentic & Multi-Agent Systems: Design and orchestrate
  autonomous agentic workflows (using frameworks like
  LangChain, AutoGen, or CrewAI) that can reason, plan, and
  execute tasks across multiple tools.
- Production Engineering: Work collaboratively with Data
  Engineers to ensure model deployments are supported by
  robust data storage, vector databases, and cloud
  infrastructure.
- AI-Assisted Development (AICA): Act as a practitioner and
  mentor for AI-assisted coding. You will champion the use
  of tools (e.g., Claude Code, Cursor, Copilot) and help
  upskill internal teams and clients on agentic coding
  workflows.
- Advisory & Strategy: Provide technical consulting on AI
  product development, helping clients understand the
  distinction between standard ML and agentic AI.
- Continuous Learning: Stay updated with the latest trends in
  the AI engineering field, such as the modern AI stack,
  synthetic data generation, and Explainable AI.

Key Skills/Experience:

- Engineering Foundations: Strong proficiency in Python and
  robust software engineering principles, with experience
  operating as part of engineering teams.
- Generative AI & RAG: Proven experience working with LLMs and
  designing Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG)
  architectures, including knowledge of semantic search and
  vector retrieval.
- AICA Proficiency: Experience using AI-assisted and agentic
  coding tools to accelerate development, with the ability
  to articulate and teach these methods to others.
- Consulting Aptitude: Passionate, motivated, and enthusiastic
  about developing technology solutions with the ability to
  explain complex AI concepts to non-technical stakeholders.

Bonus Skills/Experience:

- Agentic Systems: Experience designing and orchestrating
  autonomous agentic workflows (e.g., LangGraph) and
  managing agent state/memory.
- Cloud: Experience working with cloud infrastructure (Azure,
  AWS, or GCP).
- Agile Delivery: Experience working in an Agile development
  environment.
- Data Engineering: Proficiency in SQL and relational
  databases (e.g., PostgreSQL, DuckDB). Experience with the
  modern data stack, building data ingestion pipelines and
  working with ETL and orchestration tools (e.g., Airflow,
  Luigi, Argo, dbt), big data technologies (Spark, Kafka,
  Parquet), and web frameworks for model serving (e.g. Flask
  or FastAPI).
- Data Science: Familiarity or experience with classical NLP
  techniques (BERT, topic modelling, summarisation),
  statistical analysis, and knowledge graphs.
- Deep Learning: Familiarity or experience with Deep Learning
  (TensorFlow, PyTorch).
