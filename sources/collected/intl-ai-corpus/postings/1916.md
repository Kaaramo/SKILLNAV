### Raisonnement classification (LLM upstream)

This is a support role that helps customers USE AI/ML
infrastructure, not build AI systems. The role involves
troubleshooting customer issues with Kubernetes, GPU, and
GradientAI workloads, architecting solutions for customers,
and serving as a technical advisor - but the work is
enabling customers to run their AI/ML workloads on
DigitalOcean's platform, not directly building, training, or
deploying AI models.
