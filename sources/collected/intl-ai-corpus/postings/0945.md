About Us

Automation Anywhere is the leader in Agentic Process
Automation (APA), transforming how work gets done with AI-
powered automation. Its APA system, built on the industry’s
first Process Reasoning Engine (PRE) and specialized AI
agents, combines process discovery, RPA, end-to-end
orchestration, document processing, and analytics—all
delivered with enterprise-grade security and governance.
Guided by its vision to fuel the future of work, Automation
Anywhere helps organizations worldwide boost productivity,
accelerate growth, and unleash human potential.

Our Opportunity

As an AI Senior Solutions Engineer at Automation Anywhere,
you will bridge the gap between strategic selling and hands-
on technical delivery, serving as both a trusted advisor and
a technical implementer for our most complex AI-driven
automation initiatives. Unlike traditional pre-sales roles,
you will embed deeply with customers throughout their
automation journey—from initial discovery through
implementation—designing, prototyping, and deploying
production-grade solutions that demonstrate the
transformative power of Agentic Process Automation.

You will spend significant time on-site with customers,
understanding their business challenges at a granular level,
rapidly building proof-of-concepts, and delivering custom
integrations that showcase real business value. Your work
will directly influence both customer success and product
evolution, as you serve as a critical feedback loop between
the field and our engineering teams.

You will make an impact by being responsible for:

Customer Engagement and Technical Discovery:

- Deep Customer Embedding: Invest significant time embedded
  with customers, gaining thorough understanding of their
  business processes, technical infrastructure, pain points,
  and strategic objectives

  proposing innovative solutions that address both immediate
  needs and long-term digital transformation goals
- Trusted Technical Advisor: Build strong, long-term

  AI agents, and intelligent process optimization

Solution Design and Hands-On Implementation:

  proof-of-concept solutions quickly, demonstrating real-
  world applicability and ROI potential using Automation
  Anywhere's platform alongside complementary technologies
- Custom Development and Integration: Develop production-grade
  bots, AI agents, and workflow automations tailored to
  customer-specific requirements, including complex
  integrations with enterprise systems (SAP, Salesforce,
  ServiceNow, Workday, etc.)
- Hands-On Technical Delivery: Roll up your sleeves to debug,
  optimize, and deploy automation solutions directly into
  customer environments, ensuring seamless integration with
  existing infrastructure
- Architecture Design: Design scalable automation
  architectures that leverage hyperscaler capabilities (AWS,
  Azure, GCP), incorporating RAG, LLMs, OCR, and other
  advanced AI technologies

Value-Based Selling and Business Alignment:

  automation

Product Evolution and Knowledge Leadership:

  customers and product/engineering teams, providing
  detailed feedback on product gaps, enhancement

- Internal Knowledge Sharing: Document best practices,
