## Description complète

About Us

Herald builds digital infrastructure for commercial
insurance. Today we provide developers a single API to get
quotes for insurance products from multiple carriers.
Tomorrow we want to build anything that helps developers
connect their applications to the insurance ecosystem. We
think buying insurance should be as easy as buying anything
else online and we want your help!

We’re a team of insurtech veterans with experience at At-
Bay, Kin, and Insurify. We’ve raised our Series A funding
from top-tier VCs (Lightspeed, Brewer Lane,  Afore,
Underscore) along with a panel of insurtech founders (At-
Bay, Marble), insurance executives (CRC), and early
employees of other successful API infrastructure companies
(Plaid, Alloy, Stytch).

The Role

We are seeking a highly skilled Frontend Engineer to join
our team and take ownership of building our application from
the ground up. This role requires a strong foundation in
modern frontend development, architectural decision-making,
and hands-on coding expertise to establish scalable,
maintainable solutions.

Key Responsibilities

- Project Ownership: Lead the development of a new web
  application from scratch, setting up the architecture,
  tooling, and best practices.
- Architecture & Design: Define and implement frontend
  architecture, ensuring scalability, performance, and
  maintainability.
- Feature Development: Build robust, user-friendly, and
  responsive interfaces using React, TypeScript, and modern
  libraries.
- Collaboration: Work closely with product managers,
  designers, and backend engineers to deliver seamless user
  experiences.
- Quality & Testing: Establish automated testing strategies
  (unit, integration, end-to-end) to ensure high code
  quality.
- Continuous Improvement: Optimize application performance,
  maintain CI/CD pipelines, and stay current with industry
  trends.

What You Bring

- 1+ years of professional experience in frontend development
  with strong expertise in React.
- Experience building production web applications and
  contributing to project setup or new feature development.
- Proficiency in TypeScript, JavaScript (ES6+), and/or modern
  React (Hooks, Context, etc.).
- Interface with our Python Flask backend to integrate APIs,
  debug data flows, and extend functionality as needed.
- Write backend logic and make Postgres queries to support
  frontend features.
- Familiarity with styling libraries and UI frameworks such as
  TailwindCSS, Material UI, Styled Components, or Storybook
  for component-driven development.
- Excitement to contribute in a fast-paced startup environment
  and help take products from 0→1.

Nice to Have

- Experience with Next.js or other SSR frameworks.
- Background in cloud platforms (AWS, GCP, Azure) and DevOps
  practices.
- Knowledge of micro-frontend architectures and integration
  strategies.
