## Description complète

Job Title: Lead Quality Engineer - Automation & AI

Location: Bexhill/Leicester/London – Hybrid

Welcome to Hastings Direct

We’re a digital insurance provider with ambitious plans to
become the best and biggest in the UK market. We’ve made
huge investments in our pricing and data capabilities over
the past few years, along with nurturing our 4Cs culture.

We provide insurance for over four million customers, but we
know there’s even bigger opportunity out there – our
Pricing, Data and Analytics community value curiosity,
collaboration and constructive challenge.

We are always looking for new ideas and diverse perspectives
to question established thinking and drive meaningful
change. Great pricing is built on trust, innovation and
precision, so our aim is to ensure customers receive a fair
and accurate price based on their individual risk,
supporting fair outcomes, while delivering sustainable and
profitable growth for our company.

Pricing is more than just a number – it’s a strategic
capability. At the heart of Hastings is deep risk insight –
continually improving how we assess, segment and price risk
through data and analytics.

The Role:

We are seeking a senior, hands-on QA leader to drive
technological transformation of testing across our pricing
platforms. This role is responsible for improving quality,
pace, and confidence of delivery through modern testing
techniques, automation, and AI-enabled approaches, while
leading and developing a high-performing QA function. The
role operates across our in-house based rating engine and
Azure/GitHub-based engineering landscape. Working closely
with pricing engineers, ML engineers, and underwriting
teams, you will modernise how testing is designed, executed,
and governed, reducing duplication of effort and materially
lowering post-live issues.

This role combines hands-on technical involvement, strong
people leadership, and strategic ownership of testing
capability, tooling, and environments.

Key Responsibilities

- Lead end-to-end QA strategy and execution across all
  underwriting lines of business, ensuring testing supports
  high-quality delivery at pace.
- Design, implement, and continuously evolve the QA strategy
  covering pre-live testing, post-release monitoring, and
  live issue investigation, with a focus on improving
  quality, pace, and confidence of delivery.
- Provide hands-on technical leadership in reviewing test
  strategies, automation design, and investigation of
  complex or high-impact defects, including interrogation of
  rating behaviour and data inputs.
- Lead the strategic and practical use of test automation,
  ensuring automation is targeted, maintainable, and focused
  on the highest-risk areas.
- Drive improvements in automation and tooling to reduce
  manual effort and strengthen preventative quality
  controls, applying AI-assisted techniques where they
  provide clear, measurable benefit.
- Work closely with pricing engineers and ML teams to apply
  AI-assisted testing techniques where they add value,
  ensuring solutions are governed and suitable for a
  regulated environment.
- Own post-release quality activities, including monitoring,
  alerting, and validation of controls, ensuring issues are
  detected early and feedback loops are effective.
- Improve the efficiency, stability, and reliability of QA
  test environments to enable comprehensive data testing
  with minimal environment-related failures.
- Support incident RCA activity by providing testing insight,
  evidence, and analysis, and ensure learnings are embedded
  into improved testing practices.
- Line-manage QA Leads and the Automation Manager, providing
  clear direction, prioritisation, coaching, and performance
  support.
- Ensure QA activities are focused on preventing customer harm
  and supporting compliance with regulatory and internal
  governance requirements.
- Maintain strong communication and organisational discipline
  to manage multiple initiatives and report progress, risks,
  and outcomes clearly and consistently.
- Develop and maintain QA documentation, standards, and
  artefacts to ensure testing practices are consistent,
  auditable, and aligned with internal and regulatory
  requirements.
- Define and track quality metrics such as defect trends, test
  effectiveness, automation coverage, and release readiness
  to support data-driven decisions.
- Support recruitment and development of QA talent through
  active hiring involvement, regular one-to-one engagement,
  mentoring, and creating an environment where team members
  can perform and grow.

Skills, Knowledge and Expertise

- Bachelor’s degree in Computer Science, Computer Engineering,
  or a related field, or equivalent practical experience.
- Strong experience leading QA or test engineering teams in
  complex, data-driven or regulated technology environments.
- Hands-on technical capability, with the ability to write,
  read, and review code and actively contribute to test
  design and automation decisions.
- Strong proficiency in Python, including writing and
  reviewing automated tests and debugging failures in
  complex rules-based or decisioning systems.
- Proven experience designing and maintaining test automation,
  including API and integration testing using frameworks
  such as pytest, with working knowledge of UI tools such as
  Selenium, Playwright, or Cypress where appropriate.
- Experience applying AI-assisted testing tools to improve
  coverage, efficiency, or defect detection, with clear
  understanding of governance and control requirements.
- Practical experience working with Git/GitHub, CI/CD
  pipelines, and Azure-based engineering environments.
- Strong understanding of modern testing practices, including
  risk-based testing, shift-left quality, and end-to-end
  assurance.
- Experience working with test environments and test data,
  including validation of complex inputs and outputs using
  SQL and data comparison techniques.
- Familiarity with test management and defect tracking tools
  such as Azure DevOps and producing clear, meaningful
  quality metrics.
- Excellent leadership skills, with experience managing senior
  QA roles and automation specialists.
- Strong stakeholder-management and influencing skills, with
  the ability to balance technical, commercial, and
  governance considerations.
- Excellent problem-solving skills, with the ability to
  navigate complex pricing and engineering challenges and
  deliver robust solutions.
- Strong communication skills, able to clearly explain
  technical quality risks and solutions to both technical
  and non-technical stakeholders.
- Experience working in Agile delivery environments and
  collaborating closely with engineering, data, and ML
  teams.
