## Description complète

We celebrate diversity and encourage individuals of all
genders (m/f/d) to apply.

We’re on the lookout for an motivated Junior AI Engineer to
join our Engineering team at our office in Berlin. Join us
in building the largest AI enabled fintech for healthcare!

As part of our commercial AI division at Nelly, you'll be
one of the pioneers automating manual tasks for our
commercial teams. In a small, agile team, you'll work on
developing practical AI systems and agentic solutions - from
rapid prototypes to production ready applications. This role
is ideal for a recent graduate with a strong technical
foundation who's eager to apply their knowledge in real-
world scenarios, working on innovative GenAI projects while
maintaining a pragmatic, product-focused approach.

About Nelly

At Nelly, we’re revolutionizing medical practice management
with AI-driven automation and seamless digital workflows.
Our innovative solutions - from best-in-class patient
journeys, AI enabled patient relationship management and
billing to state of the art payment solutions - eliminate
painful workflows and improve financial outcomes making
healthcare administration smarter, faster, and 100% digital.

To fuel our mission, we just raised €50M in our Series B,
backed by top-tier investors like Cathay, Notion, Lakestar,
GFC, b2venture, and Motive Partners. With this momentum,
we’re on a mission to build Europe’s largest fintech in
healthcare - bringing best-in-class financial and
operational solutions to over 1 million medical practices
serving millions of patients daily.

Join us now if you’re driven by impact, excited by fast-
paced innovation, and ready to shape the future of
healthcare!

Joining us, you will

- Design, build, and deploy agentic systems into production by
  leveraging AI advancements, including RAG and ReAct to
  develop effective solutions.
- Work on highly applied projects with real-world impact,
  following a "start small, learn fast" approach where every
  solution is built with practical outcomes in mind.
- Evaluate, select, and integrate AI tools, frameworks, and
  platforms to ensure scalability, efficiency, and
  compliance.
- Embrace a fast-paced, agile environment where outcomes and
  quick progress are achieved through experimentation and
  rapid iteration.
- Support user interviews, contribute to product planning, and
  collaborate with cross-functional teams to drive impactful
  projects.

What you'll bring

- A degree in computer science, software engineering,
  mathematics, physics, or a related field.
- Python programming skills and first experience with agentic
  frameworks (ie.  LangChain, LangGraph, or similar)
- Understanding of cloud-native development, ideally AWS.
- A solid intuition for how modern LLM agents and agentic
  systems work.
- A self-motivated and creative problem-solver with a passion
  for pushing the boundaries of AI in healthcare and
  fintech.
- A proactive and independent work ethic, balanced with strong
  team collaboration skills.
- Proficiency in both English and German

What we offer

- Competitive salary
- Work in a great team of talented and highly motivated people
- Dynamic environment, steep learning curve, and extensive
  development opportunities
- Part of an exciting FinTech startup in the German healthcare
  market
- Responsibility, independent work, and high flexibility
- Generous budget for learning and development
- First-class work equipment and flexible working hours
- Deutschlandticket or Swapfiets rental bike
