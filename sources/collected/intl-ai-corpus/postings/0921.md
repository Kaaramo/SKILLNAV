Company Description

Assent is the leading solution for supply chain
sustainability tailored for the world’s top-tier,
sustainability-driven manufacturers. Hidden risks riddle
supply chains, many of which weren't built with
sustainability in mind. That's where we step in. With
insights from experts, Assent is the tool manufacturers
trust for comprehensive sustainability.

We are proud to announce that Assent has crossed the US$100M
ARR milestone, granting us Centaur Status. This
accomplishment, reached just 8 years following our Series A,
makes us the first and only Certified B Corporation in North
America's SaaS sustainability industry to celebrate this
milestone.

Our journey from $5 million to US$100M ARR in just eight
years has been marked by significant growth and
achievements. With our $350 million US funding led by Vista
Equity Partners, we're poised for even greater expansion and
are on the lookout for outstanding team members to join our
mission.

Hybrid Work Model

At Assent, we proudly embrace a remote-first work model,
valuing the flexibility and autonomy it provides our team.
We also acknowledge the intangible benefits of occasional
in-person workdays. For team members situated within 50
kms/31 miles of our five global offices in Ottawa, Eldoret,
Penang, Columbus, Pune and Amsterdam, you can expect to come
into the office 1-3 days a week. Similarly, those near our
co-working spaces in Nairobi and Toronto are encouraged to
work onsite once a month.

Job Description

We are seeking an experienced Senior AI Engineer with deep
expertise in designing and building advanced AI/ML systems,
Large Language Models (LLMs), Retrieval-Augmented Generation
(RAG), and agent-based architectures. This role is ideal for
a highly skilled technologist with 10+ years of software
development experience (including 3+ years in AI/ML) who
thrives at the intersection of systems engineering, cloud
infrastructure, and cutting-edge AI and machine learning
applications.

As a Senior AI Engineer, you will architect, implement, and
optimize AI-driven solutions for enterprise-scale
environments. You will collaborate closely with cross-
functional engineering, product, and research teams to
integrate intelligent features into products and services.
You will also play a key role in operationalizing AI
systems, ensuring reliability, security, and performance at
scale.

The Senior AI Engineer is a systems-oriented innovator who
is passionate about building reliable AI platforms, pushing
the boundaries of applied AI, and driving measurable
business value through innovation.

Key Requirements & Responsibilities

- Design and Develop AI Systems: Design, build, and optimize
  advanced AI solutions including LLMs, RAG pipelines, and
  agent-based architectures.
- Operationalize AI Agents: Deploy, orchestrate, and manage
  multi-agent systems for enterprise-scale use cases,
  leveraging standards and frameworks like MCP, LangChain
  Agents, CrewAI, AutoGen, or A2A/ADK.
- Agent Lifecycle Management: Establish practices for agent
  state management, role coordination, monitoring,
  retraining, and rollback to ensure reliability and
  accountability.
- Autonomy Guardrails: Implement policies, controls, and
  observability to bound agent behavior, prevent runaway
  actions, and align with Responsible AI standards.
- Integration Architecture: Connect agents with APIs,
  microservices, and message-driven systems to orchestrate
  seamless collaboration across agents, humans, and
  enterprise services.
- Responsible AI & Security: Apply governance, fairness,
  security, and compliance controls to ensure safe, ethical,
  and regulatory-aligned agent operations.
- Collaborate Cross-Functionally: Partner with product
  managers, designers, and engineers to co-create AI
  features that deliver business value and improve user
  experience.
- Drive Continuous Innovation: Stay ahead of emerging trends
  in AI engineering, evaluate and adopt emerging
  tools/frameworks to strengthen the AI platform strategy.
- Be familiar with corporate security policies and follow the
  guidance set out by processes and procedures of Assent.

Qualifications

We strongly value your talent, energy and passion. It will
also be valuable to Assent if you have the following
qualifications

- Master’s or Bachelor’s degree in Computer Science or
  Software Engineering.
- Industry Experience: 8+ years of professional software
  development, including 3+ years in AI/ML systems (LLMs,
  RAG, agent-based architectures).
- Programming Excellence: Expert-level coding skills in
  Python, C# and Angular/JavaScript, and experience with
  coding assistants.
- Agentic AI Tooling: Hands-on experience with standards and
  frameworks such as MCP, A2A/ADK, LangChain, CrewAI, and
  AutoGen.
- Cloud Expertise: Strong working knowledge of AWS
  (preferred), Azure, or GCP; comfort with cloud-native
  services for LLM workloads.
- Systems Thinking: Deep understanding of event-driven
  architectures, distributed systems, message brokers
  (Kafka, Rabbitmq, Solace PubSub+), and microservices.
- DevOps Fluency: Familiarity with CI/CD, Docker, and
  infrastructure-as-code; able to collaborate with DevOps
  teams to ensure scalable and reliable agent operations.
- Responsible AI Awareness: Understanding of AI security,
  fairness, governance, and compliance frameworks.
- Product Orientation: Ability to translate business
  challenges into AI/ML solutions, define use cases, and
  engage in early-stage ideation and problem-solving.
- Collaboration & Communication: Strong ability to engage with
  cross-functional teams, influencing product direction
  while articulating technical trade-offs.
- Innovation Mindset: Curiosity and passion for applying
  emerging AI/ML techniques to enterprise integration
  challenges.
