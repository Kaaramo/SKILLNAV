THE ROLE

We’re looking for an AI Engineer (Technical Product Owner)
to help us build the system behind a new internal tool for
Meta ads.

This tool won’t just generate copy - it will learn from
performance data, help our team test variations at scale,
and get smarter over time. You’ll lead the technical side:
designing the system, building the first version, and
helping shape what it becomes.

This role is a mix of product thinking and backend
engineering. You’ll work closely with our founders and
editors to solve real problems, make calls on what’s worth
building, and create a system that actually helps us grow.

RESPONSIBILITIES

- Build the full backend for our internal AI ad engine.
- Design how creative elements (hooks, angles, formats,
  personas) get combined, tested, and improved.
- Set up clear feedback loops using Meta ad performance data.
- Make decisions about structure: workflows, queues,
  callbacks, system logic.
- Work with editors to understand what’s useful in day-to-day
  creative work.
- Choose the right MVP path - build small, test fast, improve
  often.
- Keep things simple and scalable as we grow.

SKILLS & EXPERIENCE

- Solid backend experience (Python, FastAPI or similar).
- Experience building with LLMs and asynchronous systems.
- A track record of launching usable tools (internal or SaaS).
- Strong product judgment — you know when something’s worth
  building.
- Comfort working independently, making decisions, and owning
  results.

Bonus if you:

- Have built tools for marketing, ads, or growth teams.
- Know how Meta or TikTok ad workflows function.
- Can explain a project you decided to stop — and why that was
  the right call.
