## Description complète

Are you ready to shape the future of intelligent automation?
We’re seeking a visionary AI Engineer to join our high-
performing team. In this pivotal role, you’ll lead the
development, deployment, and ongoing optimization of
cutting-edge AI and Generative AI solutions that drive
innovation and operational excellence across the enterprise.

You are:

- A self-starter who can work independently and as part of a
  team to implement and operate new technologies that help
  Guardian Life advance its Automation & Artificial
  Intelligence objectives.
- Driven by a desire to deliver outstanding customer service
  and a rock-solid stable automation platform, you will be
  successful through successful operation of our automation
  platforms.

You have:

- Bachelor’s degree or equivalent in Computer Science,
  Computer Engineering, Computer Applications, Information
  Systems or Information Technology
- 7+ years of experience in working with AWS Services,
  Enterprise Architecture best practices, system engineering
  and security.
- 7+ years of development experience or significant knowledge
  of software engineering best practices across the
  development lifecycle, including agile methodologies,
  coding standards, code reviews, source management, build
  processes, testing, and operation
- 5+ years of hands-on experience with Python, AI Frameworks,
  AIOps
- Familiarity with Containerization and Orchestration
  technologies, Sagemaker
- Strong software troubleshooting, problem solving and system
  analysis skills
- Willingness and capability to learn new technologies quickly
- Experience working with remote teams in a matrixed
  environment
- Mentor junior team members and foster a culture of technical
  excellence

You will:

- Be part of a dispersed team both offshore and onshore for
  operation of process automation products & solutions
- Implement engineering best practices to ensure scalability
  and uptime
- Have an understanding of the Industry standard security best
  practices
- Track and report on platforms performance and availability
  metrics
- Be involved in all stages of the model development lifecycle
  from design to development, testing, and deployment.
- Design and implement scalable and reliable systems to AI
  applications
- Collaborate with software engineers, data scientists, and
  DevOps teams to design and implement technical designs and
  solutions
- Stay up-to-date with new technologies and industry trends,
  and continuously improve the platform to meet the changing
  needs of the company

Location:

- New York, New Jersey or Pennsylvania 3 day onsite hybrid
- Up to 10% travel within US
