## Description complète

At F5, we strive to bring a better digital world to life.
Our teams empower organizations across the globe to create,
secure, and run applications that enhance how we experience
our evolving digital world. We are passionate about
cybersecurity, from protecting consumers from fraud to
enabling companies to focus on innovation.


Everything we do centers around people. That means we obsess
over how to make the lives of our customers, and their
customers, better. And it means we prioritize a diverse F5
community where each individual can thrive.

Responsibilities

- Design, develop, and deploy services powered by the latest
  AI models to enable agent-driven workflows for F5
  products, ensuring scalability and ease of use.
- Build intelligent agents and design workflows utilizing GPT
  models for automation and advanced capabilities in F5
  systems.
- Maintain, optimize, and support F5’s internal AI systems,
  including GPT-based implementations, ensuring high
  availability and scalable performance.
- Collaborate with product teams, IT departments, and vendors
  to integrate AI-driven agents and workflows across diverse
  system environments.
- Develop observability tools, monitoring systems, and
  automation frameworks to enhance AI model reliability.
- Coordinate with platform teams to improve CI/CD environments
  with AI-focused automation and ensure best practices.
- Continuously explore emerging AI technologies and frameworks
  to integrate innovative solutions into F5 products.
- Ensure compliance with architecture standards and security
  policies while addressing audit inquiries.
- Define development lifecycle processes and workflows to
  streamline AI-driven application creation.
- Provide technical leadership and establish guidelines for
  integrating AI technologies and agentic workflows.

Skills and Requirements

- AI Expertise:
  - Experience with deploying and optimizing AI tools such as
    GPT models and integrating agentic workflows.
  - Familiarity with GPT architectures (GPT-4.x, related APIs)
    and reinforcement learning for intelligent systems.
  - Knowledge of AI libraries and frameworks (TensorFlow,
    PyTorch, Keras, Hugging Face Transformers, etc.).
- Development & Automation:
  - Proficiency in programming languages like Python,
    JavaScript, Go, or Shell scripting.
  - Hands-on experience with containerized architectures
    (Kubernetes, Docker) and cloud platforms (Azure, AWS, or
    GCP).
  - Expertise in CI/CD pipelines and infrastructure automation
    with tools like Terraform, Ansible, and Helm.
- Data & Integration:
  - Familiarity with relational and non-relational databases
    (PostgreSQL, MS SQL, MongoDB) and API integrations
    (RESTful APIs).
- Security & Governance:
  - Understanding of ethical AI principles and security best
    practices for AI model deployment.
  - Experience with organizational governance policies and
    compliance standards.
- Observability & Scaling:
  - Experience with monitoring and observability tools like
    Prometheus, Grafana, or Datadog.
  - Knowledge of distributed computing frameworks for scaling AI
    workflows (such as Apache Spark, Airflow, or Dask).
- Soft Skills:
  - Strong communication skills to interact effectively with
    technical and non-technical teams.
  - Self-motivated with analytical problem-solving capabilities
    and a collaborative mindset.

Preferred Qualifications

- Bachelor’s degree in Computer Science, AI, Machine Learning,
  or a related field with 2+ years of relevant experience;
  or advanced degree with AI expertise.
- Familiarity with AI-powered conversational systems (RASA,
  Dialogflow, LangChain) and agent-building workflows.
- Proficiency in Kubernetes and OpenShift for container
  orchestration.
- Knowledge of CI/CD tools (GitLab, Jenkins) and MLOps
  practices for optimizing AI model lifecycle.
