## Description complète

AI & Automation Engineer (3 Roles to build Workato Workflow
Automation for Sales, Marketing, and G&A) – Hyderabad, India

The Opportunity

Nasuni is hiring 3 AI & Automation Engineers, one each
focused on Sales, Marketing, and G&A, to join our Enterprise
Business Systems team in Hyderabad.

If you are passionate about building intelligent
automations, AI agents, and modern integrations creating
significant business impact, this is your chance to own
outcomes, shape strategy, and build at enterprise scale.

You will work at the center of Nasuni’s next generation of
automation, leveraging Workato, Agentic AI, LLMs, MCPs, and
modern SaaS ecosystems, to help our business grow faster,
operate smarter, and scale globally.

Primary Responsibilities:

What You’ll Do (All 3 Roles)

- Design, develop, test, and deploy integrations, automations,
  and AI agents using Workato (including Agent Studio &
  Workato GO).
- Translate business requirements into scalable workflows,
  recipes, and agentic solutions.
- Partner with global teams and incorporate feedback
  effectively across time zones.
- Adhere to governance standards and engineering best
  practices.
- Recommend and implement process improvements aligned to
  enterprise architecture.

Role Specializations

- AI & Automation Engineer – Sales

Help automate and accelerate the revenue engine. Build
solutions that support Salesforce, Gainsight, Clari, Gong,
Tackle, Workspan, Zift, and more. Ideal for someone who
loves optimizing sales workflows, pipeline development, and
data-driven forecasting.

- AI & Automation Engineer – Marketing

Empower Marketing with intelligent automation across
HubSpot, Google Analytics, 6Sense (or similar), and modern
MarTech ecosystems. Perfect for someone excited about AI-
powered pipeline generation, attribution, and marketing
analytics.

- AI & Automation Engineer – G&A

Improve efficiency across HR, Finance, Accounting, and
Operations. Work with tools like Outlook, Zoom, Slack,
Greenhouse, procurement/expense systems, and core corporate
apps. Great for someone who enjoys company-wide impact and
cross-functional optimization.

What You Bring / Requirements

- Bachelor’s degree in Business, Accounting, Finance, Computer
  Science or Engineering or related field, or similar work
  experience.

- 1 to 3+ Years in Workflow Automation or similar Enterprise
  SaaS AI Automation environments
- 3 to 5+ Years overall experience.
- Experience integrating and automating business systems that
  support Sales, Marketing, Accounting, or Finance and a
  good understanding of how the software is used by the
  business.
- Completed or Planning to complete Workato Foundations Level
  1 & 2 + Workato Agentic AI Basics
- Workato Foundations Courses can be taken here:
  https://academy.workato.com/page/workato-foundations
- Demonstrated expertise in designing and using data object
  models and relational and non-relational databases, with a
  track record of delivering data integrations that reliably
  move and transform data between systems.
- Familiarity with LLMs, MCPs, and AI-driven automation
  patterns
- Demonstrated in-depth knowledge of APIs, JSON formatting,
  and SQL for building and maintaining integrations.
- Skilled in Python, JavaScript, or Ruby, with additional OO
  languages (e.g., Java, C#) considered a plus for building
  AI automation systems.
- Experience with Ruby (Workato’s core language) and
  proficiency in Python and JavaScript to build and extend
  automation connectors efficiently.
- Experience integrating modern SaaS platforms for Sales,
  Marketing, Finance, Accounting, or HR Systems.

Preferred

- Hands-on experience with Workato Agent Studio, Workato GO,
  and MCP servers
- Experience with other IPaas systems like Mulesoft, Boomi, or
  others is helpful.
- Background in high-growth B2B SaaS (Ability to iterate
  quickly, meet deadlines, and release tools in agile
  sprints)
- Able to think critically and independently to make decisions
  and deliver high-quality code efficiently.
- Additional Workato AI certification
- Experience with cloud storage platforms such as Nasuni,
  Google Cloud, Microsoft Azure, AWS, Snowflake, or similar
  systems is a plus.
