Job Description

We are seeking an Agentic AI Developer to build intelligent,
autonomous AI systems that can reason, retrieve knowledge,
and act across multiple tools and data sources. The role
focuses on developing multi-agent pipelines, RAG systems,
and real-time AI-driven applications.

Responsibilities

- Design and develop agentic AI systems using LLMs, tools, and
  orchestration frameworks.
- Build RAG pipelines for structured and unstructured data.
- Implement multi-agent workflows for document understanding,
  OCR, and data extraction.
- Build APIs and services using Python and FastAPI for AI
  pipelines.
- Evaluate and improve model accuracy, latency, and
  reliability

Qualifications

Required Skills

- Strong proficiency in Python and SQL.
- Experience with LLMs, RAG, and multi-agent systems.
- Hands-on with LangChain, LangGraph or similar frameworks.
- Experience with vector databases and OCR tools.
- Experience deploying models on cloud platforms (Azure
  preferred).
- Knowledge of prompt engineering and agent reasoning
  patterns.
- Familiarity with cloud deployment and Docker.

Good to Have

- Exposure to cloud platforms (GCP) .
- Exposure to MLOps experience.
- Knowledge of CI/CD tools.
