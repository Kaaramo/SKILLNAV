## Description complète

About Coralogix

Coralogix is a modern, full-stack observability platform
transforming how businesses process and understand their
data at scale. We are a high-growth, 100M+ ARR business
building the revenue infrastructure to match our ambition.
The companies winning right now are not the ones with the
biggest sales teams — they are the ones with the smartest
revenue systems. This team builds those systems.

Role Overview

This is a builder role. You will own the automation and AI
layer of our GTM Engineering stack — designing and deploying
the workflows that make our revenue engine intelligent,
automated, and self-improving. You will work with workflow
orchestration tooling (currently n8n), LLM-based AI
development tools (currently Claude Code), and a Snowflake-
powered data foundation.

Equally important: you are expected to stay current with the
automation and AI tooling landscape and proactively evaluate
alternatives. If you find a better orchestration platform, a
more capable AI coding tool, or an integration approach that
meaningfully outperforms what we are using today — bring the
case. Tool recommendations based on evidence are a core part
of this role.

Your success metric is straightforward: how many manual GTM
processes have you eliminated this quarter? Every workflow
you build should free up time for sellers, provide better
intelligence for leaders, and improve the accuracy of our
forecasting and pipeline systems.

This role is not about maintaining workflows that already
exist. It is about building ones that do not yet exist and
making the business meaningfully faster as a result.

What you will build

Automation & AI Workflows

- Design and deploy AI-enabled automation workflows across the
  GTM stack using workflow orchestration tooling (n8n is our
  current platform — evaluate and recommend alternatives as
  appropriate)
- Build LLM-powered tools for pre-call research, deal
  intelligence, pipeline gap analysis, and automated
  executive briefings — using AI coding assistants such as
  Claude Code or equivalent
- Integrate LLM capabilities into operational revenue
  workflows — prompt engineering, output parsing, confidence
  scoring
- Build Gong engagement gap alerts, forecast variance analysis
  workflows, and SE utilization tracking automations
- Develop and maintain event-driven, API-to-API integration
  patterns connecting Salesforce, Gong, Snowflake, Slack,
  and Marketo

Internal Tools & Applications

- Rapidly prototype and ship internal GTM tools using AI-
  accelerated development — Claude Code, GitHub Copilot, or
  equivalent AI coding assistants
- Build lightweight frontend interfaces for internal sales and
  revenue teams where self-service replaces manual requests
- Develop backend services and APIs to extend CRM and GTM
  platforms beyond their native capabilities

Revenue Infrastructure

- Build services for pipeline analysis, forecasting support,
  and deal intelligence that feed leadership dashboards and
  regional reporting
- Develop data pipelines and transformations that move GTM
  events from source systems into Snowflake for downstream
  analytics
- Design compounding automation systems — each workflow built
  on shared logic and data foundations so value multiplies
  over time

- 5–8+ years of backend engineering experience — Python,
  Node.js, or similar as primary language
- Strong experience building RESTful APIs, microservices, and
  event-driven integrations
- Hands-on experience with workflow automation and
  orchestration tools — n8n, Zapier, Workato, or equivalent
- Demonstrated experience using AI coding assistants (Claude
  Code, GitHub Copilot, GPT-based tools) as a core part of
  development workflow — not experimentation
- Frontend development experience (React or similar) —
  sufficient to ship functional internal tools independently
- Experience working with Salesforce APIs and platform
  integrations
- Working knowledge of the end-to-end lead-to-cash process—
  you understand how leads originate,progress through the
  sales cycle, get quoted, and convert to recognised revenue
  well enough to build automations that serve each stage of
  the GTM motion, not just individual tasks in isolation.
- Experience building automations or tools in service of
  Revenue Operations or Sales Operations teams— you
  understand what workflows they run, where manual effort
  exists, and how to systematically eliminate it.
- Familiarity with cloud platforms (AWS, GCP, or Azure) and
  modern deployment workflows
- Strong data modeling and database knowledge; experience
  working with or querying Snowflake a plus
- Ability to leverage LLMs for prototyping, debugging, and
  workflow automation design
- Bachelor's degree in Computer Science, Engineering, or
  related technical field

The Non Negotiables

- You use AI tools every day in your development workflow —
  not occasionally
- You can point to automations you built that measurably
  eliminated manual work
- You are comfortable working across multiple systems
  simultaneously without losing track of data integrity
- You understand lead-to-cash end-to-end— you can articulate
  how a prospect moves through the sales cycle to become
  recognised revenue, and you build automations that serve
  that entire journey, not just point-in-time tasks.
- You ship fast, iterate based on feedback, and do not wait
  for perfect requirements

Cultural Fit

We’re seeking candidates who are hungry, humble, and smart.
Coralogix fosters a culture of innovation and continuous
learning, where team members are encouraged to challenge the
status quo and contribute to our shared mission. If you
thrive in dynamic environments and are eager to shape the
future of observability solutions, we’d love to hear from
you.

Coralogix is an equal-opportunity employer and encourages
applicants from all backgrounds to apply.
