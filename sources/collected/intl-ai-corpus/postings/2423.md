## Description complète

At praxipal, we are building an AI-powered healthcare admin
workforce to address the global shortage of medical staff.

Medical assistants are too valuable to spend their days
chasing callbacks, cleaning up schedules, sending repetitive
messages, or wrestling with invoicing and documentation
workflows. Staff should spend time on patients. That’s why
we build Luna.

Our AI receptionist, Luna, answers and automates phone calls
and she’s loved by more than 100 medical practices. Right
now, we’re expanding her from calls to end-to-end patient
communication across any channel. Over the next years, we’ll
grow her into an AI worker that automates all administrative
processes in medical practices, embedded directly into the
systems teams already use.

We’re backed by one of Europe’s leading investors and are
one of the fastest growing healthtech startups in Germany.
Our team has previously worked and studied at Palantir,
Amazon, SAP, the University of Cambridge, and Hasso Plattner
Institute.

We are hiring an AI Automation Engineer to build automations
and internal tools that make our go-to-market organization
faster and more scalable.

The Role

This is a hands-on role with end-to-end ownership of
automations, tools, and reporting that enable Sales,
Marketing, and Customer Success. You’ll work directly with
our founders, GTM operators, and other engineers to identify
bottlenecks and manual work and turn them into reliable
systems. If you do things right, you can help us grow twice
as fast.

What You’ll Do

You’ll build the systems that make our GTM organisation run.

- Identify friction across Sales/Marketing/CS and remove it
  through automation
- Automate GTM workflows (n8n, Zapier, custom software, etc.)
- Build internal tools (lead enrichment, web crawlers,
  outreach personalization, etc.)
- Build and maintain our data lake, as well as analytics and
  automated reporting (campaign performance, funnel
  tracking, pipeline health, etc.)
- Create demos and prototypes for new products and new markets
- Build and maintain our core business systems (billing,
  commissions, invoicing, etc.)

What We’re Looking ForMust-Have

- Strong hands-on software engineering experience in
  TypeScript, Python or Rust
- You have built, shipped, and owned software end-to-end
- Comfortable working with non-technical stakeholders on
  technical topics
- You enjoy operating in ambiguity and turning messy real-
  world processes into pragmatic systems
- Prior experience in startups or scale-ups
- You’re excited to work on-site in Berlin in a fast-moving
  startup in build-mode

Nice-to-Have

- Experience with web automation tooling (Selenium,
  Playwright, Puppeteer)
- Experience improving operational processes through
  automation
- Familiarity with modern data stacks (Postgres, Grafana,
  PostHog, etc.)

How We Work

- High ownership: You own the systems you build and the
  outcomes you create.
- Hands-on and pragmatic: You focus on solutions that work,
  even when constraints are messy or imperfect.
- Humble and collaborative: Team fit and attitude are non-
  negotiable.

Location & Compensation

- Location: Berlin (100% on-site)
- Competitive compensation: €80,000–€120,000 total
  compensation (cash + equity)
- Office perks: A beautiful workspace in Berlin Mitte,
  complete with a ping pong table, PlayStation, and VR setup
- Team bonding: Regular get-togethers, happy hours and
  offsites
- Health and productivity: Free dinners, subsidized lunches
  and sponsored gym subscription
