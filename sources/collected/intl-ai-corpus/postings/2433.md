## Description complète

Position Summary

 We’re hiring our first AI Sales Engineer to be the face of
Raylu and make our AI research platform indispensable to
customers. You’ll own the full customer journey - from first
demo to daily power user - while sitting at the center of
the company as the bridge between customer workflows and our
product roadmap.

Responsibilities:

- Discovery, Demos & Onboarding: Join sales calls, understand
  investor workflows, run tailored demos, and configure
  Raylu so customers see value quickly in trials and
  onboarding.
- Usage, Metrics & Engagement: Set up dashboards, track
  product usage, and proactively reach out when engagement
  drops or when customers need guidance on AI features and
  best practices.
- AI Product Expertise & Feedback: Master Raylu and its AI/LLM
  capabilities, including prompt and workflow design. Turn
  customer feedback into clear product requirements that
  influence our roadmap.
- Growth & Expansion: Support renewals and expansions by
  quantifying value delivered and uncovering new AI use
  cases and teams to roll out Raylu to.
- Foundation Building: Help build the GTM / customer function
  from 0 to1 - playbooks, processes, and operational
  frameworks - working closely with Partnerships, Sales, and
  Product.

Who You Are:

Experience:

- 2–5 years in: sales engineering, customer success/account
  management at a hyper-growth B2B startup, or finance
  (PE/VC), consulting, or similar.

Mindset & Skills:

- Customer-centric with strong product sense: quickly
  understand investor workflows and translate needs into
  clear requirements.
- Comfortable with AI/LLMs: excited to learn tools, refine
  prompts, and become a product expert at the
  technical–business intersection.
- Scrappy and action-oriented: structure ambiguous problems,
  juggle multiple accounts, and move fast in a startup
  environment.
- Excellent communicator and relationship builder with
  executives, managers, and analysts.
- Revenue-minded: comfortable discussing expansion and
  contracts while always prioritizing customer value.

Nice to Have:

- Prior experience building CS or Sales Engineering motions
  from 0 to 1.
- Deep interest in startups, funding, and private market
  investing workflows.

Logistics:

- Compensation: $140K - $190K salary, $50K-100K equivalent in
  new hire equity (4 year vest)
- Location: New York City. We are a fully in-office team
  working out of Midtown Manhattan Monday through Friday. We
  allow for WFH days when anyone is traveling, but we do not
  allow for permanent remote work.
- Benefits:
  - Generous health, dental, and vision insurance
  - 401k with 3% automatic contribution (no vesting)
  - Paid Lunches
  - Wellness and Citi Bike benefit
