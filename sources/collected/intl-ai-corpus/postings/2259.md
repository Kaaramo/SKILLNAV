📐 About the role

As an AI engineer at WRITER, you'll be at the forefront of
shaping how enterprises harness superintelligence. This
isn't just about theory; you'll be building tangible AI
solutions that power the future of work for hundreds of the
world's leading companies. Your work will directly impact
the performance, scalability, and ethical alignment of our
cutting-edge LLMs and AI agents, enabling businesses to
unlock unprecedented levels of productivity and innovation
with AI that is truly grounded in their data.

This role can be hybrid in our London hub. You'll report to
the head of AI engineering.

- Architect, develop, and deploy high-performance, scalable AI

  features, translating complex research into practical,

  scientists, and product managers to define technical

  MLOps tools for managing the AI lifecycle.
