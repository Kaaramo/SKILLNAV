## Description complète

Job Description:

Vertex is seeking an exceptional AI Product Engineer to
support in the development of cutting-edge AI solutions for
both external customers and internal workflows.

The AI Product Engineer will be responsible for
conceptualizing, designing, and implementing production-
ready AI products utilizing machine learning and generative
AI technologies. This role requires a unique blend of
technical expertise, domain knowledge, and entrepreneurial
spirit.

This role offers an exceptional opportunity to gain hands-on
experience in building AI products, making it ideal for
those aspiring to found AI companies in the future. The
successful candidate will be at the forefront of
implementing best practices for AI product development,
focusing on creating stable, truthful, and helpful systems.

Join us in shaping the future of AI-driven tax solutions!

Key Responsibilities:

- Develop and deploy AI-driven products from concept to
  production
- Create proof-of-concept systems and scalable prototypes
- Manage cross-functional teams to bring AI solutions to life
- Collaborate with stakeholders to identify pain points and
  design effective solutions
- Stay abreast of the latest ML/GenAI advancements and apply
  novel techniques

Required Qualifications:

- Proven experience in end-to-end product development and
  deployment
- Strong background in machine learning and generative AI
- Proficiency in full-stack development (backend, frontend,
  and UI/UX design)
- Ability to understand and implement techniques from ML/GenAI
  research papers
- Experience in solo product shipping and development
- Excellent communication skills for stakeholder management
- Exceptional ability to lead discussions to understand
  workflows and business logic of operational processes and
  tools
- Please include a portfolio showcasing your work in AI.
  Examples may include completed projects, research papers,
  links to GitHub repositories, or case studies
  demonstrating your experience with AI technologies such as
  machine learning, natural language processing, computer
  vision, or generative AI

Preferred Qualifications:

- Consulting experience in relevant domains
- Background as a technical founder or strong entrepreneurial
  aspirations
- Familiarity with tax software and related workflows
- Portfolio or work samples are highly preferred

Skills and Attributes:

- Self-starter with the ability to identify and create
  necessary tools and resources
- Adept at building and managing internal teams
- Strong problem-solving and analytical skills
- Visionary mindset with attention to detail

Other Qualifications

The Winning Way behaviors that all Vertex employees need in
order to meet the expectations of each other, our customers,
and our partners.

- Communicate with Clarity - Be clear, concise and actionable.
  Be relentlessly constructive. Seek and provide meaningful
  feedback.
- Act with Urgency - Adopt an agile mentality - frequent
  iterations, improved speed, resilience. 80/20 rule –
  better is the enemy of done. Don’t spend hours when
  minutes are enough.
- Work with Purpose - Exhibit a “We Can” mindset. Results
  outweigh effort. Everyone understands how their role

- Drive to Decision - Cut the swirl with defined deadlines and
  decision points. Be clear on individual accountability and

- Own the Outcome - Defined milestones, commitments and
