### Raisonnement classification (LLM upstream)

This is a customer-facing implementation role focused on
configuring and deploying Ironclad's CLM platform for
customers. While they work with AI features (smart import,
clause extraction, playbooks), the role is about
implementing existing AI capabilities rather than building,
training, or fine-tuning AI/ML models. The work is enabling
customers to use AI products, not developing AI systems.
