We are seeking a highly skilled Senior Technology Engineer

RequirementsKey Responsibilities

- Design, build, and maintain containerized applications using

- Integrate and optimize AI inference engines such as Triton

- Implement robust monitoring and alerting frameworks using
  Prometheus and Grafana.

  future roadmap of AI platform capabilities.
