Job Description

As an AI Software Engineer in our Data Science team, you
will help design and deliver scalable backend and cloud
systems that power our AI-driven products and services.

You will design and build scalable, production-grade systems
and APIs, with a focus on integrating modern AI capabilities
(via APIs from OpenAI, Anthropic, Google, etc.) into real-
world applications.

We are not training models from scratch — instead, you will
work on agentic AI systems, orchestration workflows, and
cloud-native architectures that bring AI into production at
scale. In this newly formed work stream, you will have the
unique opportunity to lay the foundations and set the right
technical direction.
