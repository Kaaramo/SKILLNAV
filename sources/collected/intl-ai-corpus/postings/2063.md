At SAFE Security, our mission is bold and ambitious: We Will

We’re looking for a seasoned Senior Software Development
Engineer (SDE III) with deep expertise in Analytics and AI

Core Responsibilities:

- Technical Leadership: Lead and mentor developers, fostering
  a culture of innovation, quality, and continuous
  improvement.
- AI & Analytics Development: Design, build, and deploy
  scalable AI-driven analytics solutions, leveraging
  advanced AI techniques, including Retrieval-Augmented
  Generation (RAG), AI agents, and foundational models.
- Architecture: Collaborate with cross-functional teams to
  architect scalable, secure, and performant AI-driven
  microservices.
- Cloud and AI Integration: Utilize cloud services (primarily
  AWS Bedrock, Lambda, EC2, S3, RDS) to develop efficient,

  (e.g., OpenAI, Anthropic Claude, Amazon Titan),

  Engineering, or a related field.

  Augmented Generation (RAG), AI Agents, and deploying these
