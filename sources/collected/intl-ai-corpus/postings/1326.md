## Description complète

About the Position:

Bluefish is looking for a technically strong, customer-
facing Senior Customer AI Engineer to support enterprise
customers throughout their AI Marketing efforts—from
implementation and integration through optimization and
expansion. This role sits at the intersection of account
management, AI solutioning, and technical delivery.

You will act as a trusted technical partner for customers,
ensuring smooth transitions from sales to account ownership,
supporting complex integrations, and helping customers
unlock measurable value from Bluefish’s AI platform. The
ideal candidate is equally comfortable discussing APIs and
data pipelines as they are explaining AI-driven outcomes to
non-technical stakeholders.



What You'll Be Doing

- Support the technical relationship for assigned customer
  accounts, serving as the primary point of contact for
  integrations, configurations, and advanced technical
  questions.
- Support RFP and RFI responses, expanding technical sections
  and helping streamline internal processes for scale and
  consistency.
- Guide and support customer integrations, including APIs,
  data feeds, product catalogs, and other platform
  dependencies.
- Design and support custom technical solutions, such as
  dashboards, data exports, and bespoke workflows tailored
  to customer needs.
- Partner with customers on complex optimization measurement,
  automation, and performance validation to ensure
  measurable business impact.
- Collaborate closely with product, engineering, sales, and
  customer success to surface customer feedback and inform
  roadmap priorities.
- Help identify opportunities for account expansion and deeper
  platform adoption through technical use cases and
  optimization strategies.



Qualifications

- Minimum of 5+ years of experience in a Technical Account
  Manager, Solutions Engineer, Customer Engineer, or similar
  client-facing technical role, ideally in SaaS, AI, or
  marketing technology.
- Strong hands-on experience with APIs, data feeds, and system
  integrations.
- Solid AI and AI/ML proficiency, with the ability to clearly
  explain complex technical concepts to non-technical
  stakeholders.
- Working knowledge of databases, data management, and
  analytics concepts.
- Proven ability to drive value and adoption in Fortune 500 or
  global enterprise accounts.
- Comfortable operating in ambiguous environments and building
  or improving processes as the company scales.
- Exceptional communication skills-written, verbal, and
  visual-with the ability to engage stakeholders at all
  levels.
- A consultative, customer-first mindset with a track record
  of delivering outcomes.
- Comfort working in fast-paced, startup environments with
  cross-functional collaboration.
