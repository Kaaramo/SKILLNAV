## Description complète

Job Title: Senior AI Engineer (Rapid Prototyping & Analytics
)

Prompt is a mission-driven startup looking for an
experienced Senior AI Engineer to help push forward an
ambitious set of AI initiatives in healthcare, with a
particular emphasis on speech and language processing.

Prompt delivers highly automated and modern B2B enterprise
software to rehab therapy businesses, their teams, and most
importantly the patients they serve. We’ve established
ourselves as the go-to platform in the space and are rapidly
growing our market share by delivering software people love.

As we scale, AI is becoming increasingly central to how we
build products and how we operate internally. We’re looking
for a Sr. AI Engineer who can operate broadly across the
team, with a particular strength in designing, evaluating,
and monitoring end-to-end AI systems. Comfort choosing,
implementing, and improving outcome metrics is essential.
This role will contribute to product-facing AI as needed,
while also playing a key role in rapidly ideating and
developing internal AI tools that improve efficiency,
decision-making, and quality across Engineering, Design,
Sales, Marketing, Support, Finance, and Operations.

This is a high-ownership role focused on iteratively
building systems, partnering closely with stakeholders, and
ensuring our AI tools deliver real value.

Responsibilities

- Drive Prompt’s mission to improve healthcare through modern
  technology including AI
- Lead AI projects from ideation → architecture → production →
  iteration until tools are widely adopted and loved!
- Design, build, and deploy end-to-end AI systems across both
  traditional ML and LLM-based workflows
- Partner with stakeholders across the company to understand
  workflows, define success criteria, and deliver practical
  solutions
- Supporting internal as well as product-facing AI initiatives
  as needed
- Design and implement evaluation, monitoring, and
  observability metrics (accuracy, cost, latency, and
  business impact)
- Build guardrails around LLM pipelines including validation
  and human-in-the-loop workflows
- Develop rapid proofs-of-concept and prototypes, then harden
  them into reliable systems
- Help grow and lead the AI team, including training and
  development of junior team members

Qualifications

- 4+ years of experience building AI/ML systems in industry or
  academia, with meaningful production ownership
- Strong experience building and operating AI systems end-to-
  end, not just models in isolation
- Strong experience with LLM-based systems (programmatic
  prompt optimization, evaluation, prompting, structured
  outputs, RAG, multi-step workflows)
- Solid understanding of evaluation and measurement for AI
  systems (offline metrics, online monitoring, regressions,
  cost tracking)
- Ability to work closely with non-technical stakeholders to
  translate workflows into effective AI solutions
- Strong software engineering fundamentals and ability to ship
  maintainable, production-grade systems
- Track record of benchmarking and iteratively improving
  accuracy, cost, and latency
- Excellent communication skills, with the ability to explain
  complex AI concepts to non-technical stakeholders
- Python (>3.9)
- Docker (Basics)
- AWS familiarity (S3, RDS, EC2) preferred
- Strong problem-solving mindset and ability to operate
  effectively in ambiguous, fast-paced settings
- Willingness to learn and deeply understand the healthcare
  sector, specifically physical therapy, occupational
  therapy, and speech therapy
- Please only apply if you are interested in a heavy code
  contribution role; this is not an engineering management
  position, though growth opportunities exist over time

Nice-to-Haves

- Experience building internal AI tools or platforms used
  across multiple business functions
- Experience designing evaluation harnesses for LLM workflows
  (golden datasets, regression tests, human review loops)
- Experience setting up analytics and dashboards to track AI
  usage, outcomes, and ROI
- Familiarity with workflow automation and business systems
  (CRMs, support tools, analytics platforms)
- Experience with FastAPI, Streamlit, Pydantic, SQLAlchemy,
  PostgreSQL
- Experience with retrieval systems (vector search, hybrid
  search, grounding and citation strategies)
- Experience building healthcare software and/or data
  processing pipelines
