## Description complète

Summary

Guidewire is seeking a highly technical AI Workflow
Automation Engineer to join our Professional Services
organization in Bangalore. Unlike a traditional client-
facing consulting role, this position is dedicated to
empowering our own global community of Professional Services
developers and consultants. Your mission is to build the
"internal engine" of our delivery organization. You will
architect and implement AI-driven agents, automated coding
assistants, and workflow orchestration tools that eliminate
manual toil for our developers. By leveraging LLMs and
agentic frameworks, you will transform how Guidewire
software is configured, integrated, and deployed, making our
professional services teams faster, more accurate, and more
efficient.

Job Description

- Developer Workflow Automation: Identify bottlenecks in the
  Guidewire implementation lifecycle and design AI-powered
  agents to automate repetitive developer tasks (e.g., code
  generation, unit test creation, configuration mapping).
- Internal Tooling & Orchestration: Build and maintain custom
  internal tools and LLM-based applications using frameworks
  like LangGraph, or LangChain to assist consultants in
  their daily delivery work
- AI-Enhanced DevEx: Collaborate directly with Professional
  Services developers to understand their pain points and
  iterate on AI solutions that improve their "Developer
  Experience" (DevEx)
- Prompt Engineering & RAG for Delivery: Develop and optimize
  RAG & GraphRag pipelines that tap into Guidewire’s
  internal documentation, best practices, and codebase to
  provide developers with instant, context-aware assistance.
- Standardization & Governance: Create reusable AI modules and
  prompt libraries that can be shared across global delivery
  teams to ensure consistent implementation quality
- Technical Mentorship: Act as an internal subject matter
  expert, training and guiding Professional Services
  developers on how to effectively use AI tools and
  integrate them into their local development environments
- At Guidewire, we foster a culture of curiosity, innovation,
  and responsible use of AI—empowering our teams to
  continuously leverage emerging technologies and data-
  driven insights to enhance productivity and outcomes.

Qualifications

- Education: Bachelor’s or Master’s degree in Computer
  Science, Software Engineering, or a related technical
  field.
- Experience: 5+ years of software engineering experience,
  with a strong emphasis on automation, developer tools, or
  internal platforms. At least 1 years of hands-on
  experience with AI/LLM integration.

Technical Proficiency:

- Expert-level Python/Java skills for building automation
  scripts and backend services.
- Deep experience with LLM orchestration frameworks
  (LangChain, LlamaIndex, LangGraph).
- Strong knowledge of LLM APIs and prompt engineering
  techniques.
- Experience with coding assistants (Claude Code)
- Demonstrated ability to embrace AI and apply it to your
  current role as well as data-driven insights to drive
  innovation, productivity, and continuous improvement.

Engineering Excellence:

- Proven track record of building robust, maintainable
  software and internal tools. Familiarity with CI/CD, Git
  workflows, automated testing, and
  Monitoring/Observability.
- Domain Context: (Preferred) Experience with enterprise
  software ecosystems and the challenges of complex
  configuration and integration.

Preferred Qualifications

- Cloud Infrastructure: Experience with AWS
  (Bedrock/Sagemaker) for deploying internal AI services.
- DevOps/MLOps: Experience in building and scaling internal
  platforms or developer productivity tools.
- Guidewire Knowledge: Prior experience with Guidewire
  InsuranceSuite or Guidewire Cloud is a significant plus
  but not required.
- AI/ML: 3+ years of experience as a Data Scientist
