AI Engineer

The Opportunity:

Design and implement effective AI solution architecture

You Have:

- 2+ years of experience with artificial intelligence, data
  science, machine learning engineering, software

  engineering, including in an academic or professional

- Experience with generative and agentic AI technologies, such

  C#, or Java

  microservice design or edge computing

- Experience with modern cloud computing technologies,
  including Docker
