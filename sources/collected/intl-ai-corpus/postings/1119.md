### Raisonnement classification (LLM upstream)

This role is an AI Platform/MLOps position focused on
building infrastructure and tooling for other teams to
deploy and operate AI systems. The engineer creates shared
services (LLM gateway, evaluation frameworks, monitoring)
and self-service tools but does not build, train, or fine-
tune models themselves.

## Responsabilités

- Design and operate scalable AI infrastructure for LLM inference, prompt management, and evaluation pipelines supporting billions in premium flow
- Build self-service tools, SDKs, and APIs that enable product teams to move from prototype to production faster
- Instrument production AI/ML workloads with standardized logging, tracing, and evaluation metrics for full observability coverage
- Implement intelligent routing, caching, and provider optimization via LLM gateway to reduce AI compute costs
- Drive adoption of shared platform services including LLM gateway, evaluation frameworks, and monitoring across AI features
- Champion developer experience through comprehensive documentation and responsive support for internal customers
