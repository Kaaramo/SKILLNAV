## Description complète

BrightHire is a category-creating, high-growth, Series B
software company with a mission to give everyone the hiring
experience they deserve.

We deliver on this mission by transforming the way many of
the world’s leading companies build exceptional teams. We
created the Interview Intelligence category, and our clients
include some of the world’s most innovative companies—Canva,
OpenAI, Ramp, Hubspot—up to the Fortune 500.

Location

Remote

About the Role

You will own the end-to-end delivery of many of our biggest
AI features. Working in lockstep with Product and Design,
you’ll move quickly to design, build, and refine features
throughout the entire stack from prompt engineering and
evals through the backend, all the way to frontend
interactions. You’ll focus on the output quality of your AI
features and make sure users get accurate, on-brand AI
outputs fast. You’ll work on real features people use every
day—things like:

- Automatic interview coaching that provides tailored feedback
  and guidance based on an interviewer's recent interviews,
  with customizable content.
- An AI Candidate skills matrix that displays structured
  summaries mapped to key skills aligned with the job
  description.
- An AI assistant that allows users to instantly query any
  detail about a candidate or interview using natural
  language.
- Customizable interview summaries that enable customers to
  create fully personalized AI-generated summaries using
  their own structure and criteria.
- …and more

What You’ll Bring

- You have real world experience building generative AI
  applications end to end, building prompt chains, eval
  harnesses, vector search, and more.
- You know how to make LLMs useful in a product. You
  understand what they are good at and bad at, and you are
  able to harness their strengths.
- You have plenty of experience building across the stack in
  Python and Javascript/Typescript, especially building out
  the backend logic, data models, and architecture.
- You think deeply about how AI features work for end users
  and can make decisions with user empathy.
- You’ve worked with both structured (SQL) and unstructured
  data (raw text) and know how to transform them and utilize
  them together.
- You care about personality, tone, accuracy, safety, and
  speed as integral to the AI features you build.
- You have product instincts and work well with design.
- You move fast and independently, but you know when to pair
  and when to pull others in.

About our team

- You’ll have the opportunity to work on high-impact projects
  in small, autonomous squads, with the flexibility to lead
  initiatives or focus as an individual contributor
  depending on your goals and interests.
- Our developer experience is thoughtfully designed, with fast
  CI (< 10 minutes), 1-click deploys, strong observability,
  and a clean codebase that enables you to move quickly and
  confidently.
- Our culture supports sustainable, focused work with fully
  remote roles, regular working hours, no-meeting
  Wednesdays, and flexible time off to recharge when needed.
- Our team is composed of smart, collaborative, and genuinely
  kind people, creating an environment where you can learn,
  grow, and do your best work.
