We believe it takes great people to create a great product.

based on them, too. Since 2010, Pipedrive has been on a

We are looking for a Principal AI/ML Scientist & Engineer to

advanced research and production-grade systems. Your impact
will be measured through technical leadership, leverage, and
the real-world business outcomes you enable.

Your new adventure:

- Lead and mentor a team of Applied Scientists and AI/ML
  Engineers, fostering a culture of technical excellence and
  scientific rigor
- Lead the technical strategy for AI/ML-based solutions
  focused on extracting actionable value and insights from
  both structured CRM data and unstructured content

- Apply deep scientific intuition to the design of AI systems,

  technical capabilities
