## Description complète

About Amperos Health

Amperos Health is the leading provider of multi-modal RCM
agents empowering healthcare clinics to collect more
revenue, faster. Founded in 2023 and backed by Uncork, Neo,
Nebular.vc and strategic angels from OpenAI, Stripe, and
Twilio, we're on a mission to revamp the bridge between
healthcare providers and payers. Our vision is to equip
healthcare providers with an AI workforce that reduces
administrative burden and accelerates revenue.

About the role

As an AI Engineer on our research team, you’ll work on our
hardest research and AI infra problems. You’ll both help
prototype new products and also work on turning prototypes
into a high quality AI product. Hard problems include
passing the healthcare billing Turing Test - would an
insurance agent who is interacting with our AI or a
healthcare biller who is reviewing the work of our AI think
our AI is a human, and not an AI? More concretely, you will
be working to improve our AI agent’s quality so it acts more
human-like and collects more accurate and detailed results
from insurance.

What you’ll do

- Main focus is on improving models, LLM orchestration, prompt
  optimizations, evaluation framework, and AI infra
- Enable agent/workflow product engineers
- Own and raise the bar on AI quality
- Own ultimate vision of end to end AI orchestration of
  modules
- Work on hardest research problems (ie. Anti-AI)
- Mainly a mix of ML, data science, and applied LLM work.
  Heavy Python.

Who we’re looking for

- You like reading the latest LLM research papers
- Deep experience in implementing and building AI agents
- Eager to try out the latest releases (ie. o1, OpenAI voice
  mode, Claude Computer-Use) to see how it can be used in
  real-world healthcare billing applications
- Worked with complex distributed systems with many async
  operations
- Excited about pushing the boundaries of conversational AI
  technology
- Can balance quick experimentation with systematic evaluation
- Very high Python mastery
