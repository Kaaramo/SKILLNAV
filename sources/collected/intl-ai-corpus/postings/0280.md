## Description complète

Who we are:

Tria Federal delivers digital services and technology
solutions that support the health and safety of veterans,
service members and civilians. For two decades, federal
agencies have relied on Tria companies to advance their
critical missions and modernize their systems, so that they
can uphold their commitment to the American people. Today,
we are pushing the boundaries of possibility through
partnerships and investments in artificial intelligence and
emerging technologies, developing solutions for the biggest
challenges that government will face tomorrow.

We are proud to employ and support military veterans who
bring mission-first mindset, technical expertise, and
leadership qualities that strengthen our work. Veterans,
transitioning service members, and military spouses are
strongly encouraged to apply.

Tria Federal is assembling a small, high-autonomy team of
Junior AI Engineers to design and build agentic systems,
with outcome focused intelligent software agents that
reason, act, and collaborate to solve real-world problems
for federal missions.



This individual will help create systems that automate
knowledge work, enhance human decision-making, and improve
digital experiences for millions of Americans, from veterans
and clinicians to public safety professionals.



As part of Tria Labs, you’ll shape how generative and
agentic AI redefines mission delivery, developing tools and
systems that make digital civic services smarter, faster,
and more human-centered.



This is a great opportunity to turn generative AI into
agents that act, not just respond, and perform where it
matters the most.



***Must be a U.S. citizen due to the security clearance
required for this position.



Responsibilities:

- Build intelligent agents that plan, reason, and interact
  with users, MCP Servers and APIs, using evolving agentic
  frameworks to orchestrate complex business workflows.
- Engineer end-to-end products — from Python or Node.js
  backends to React or Angular frontend applications that
  embed these agents into secure, mission-critical
  environments.
- Integrate and experiment with leading AI platforms and
  Foundational Models to design multi-agent workflows and
  retrieval-augmented solutions.
- Prototype rapidly, test ideas, and push from concept to
  pilot within an agile, builder-driven team.
- Apply responsible AI and security-minded design, as we build
  systems that interface with sensitive data and secure
  platforms (SSDF awareness a plus).

Qualifications:

- 2–3 years of hands-on experience building and deploying
  full-stack applications (Python, TypeScript, Node.js,
  React/Angular).
- Practical experience with LLM APIs and generative AI
  frameworks, ideally having built or experimented with
  agentic systems, chat interfaces, or AI copilots.
- Understanding of data and API orchestration, including
  prompt management, evals, RAG, context handling, and
  connecting AI reasoning to real systems.
- Exposure to AWS or other cloud platforms for deploying and
  monitoring modern applications.
- Curiosity, autonomy, and the drive to make AI tangible, you
  thrive in small, multidisciplinary teams where initiative
  matters.
