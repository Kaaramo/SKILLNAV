Company Description

Job Description

- Preferred location is NYC, but open to candidates located in
  the East Coast

Role Overview

The Solution Architect serves as the technical lead and
platform owner for the AI Adoption initiative. This role
designs and governs the AI Landing Zone, oversees
integrations, ensures security and compliance, and provides
technical direction for all AI use cases and platform
components. The Solution Architect ensures that the AI
platform is scalable, secure, interoperable, and aligned
with IT standards, supporting both pilot development and
long-term institutionalization.

Key Responsibilities

AI Platform & Landing Zone Architecture

- Architect the AI Landing Zone, including Azure OpenAI, Azure
  AI Foundry, Power Platform, Microsoft Fabric, and
  Microsoft Copilot services.
- Ensure the platform is secure, compliant, and designed to
  scale across academic, administrative, research, and
  healthcare environments.
- Define and enforce governance, identity/SSO integration, and
  infrastructure requirements.

Security, Compliance & Responsible AI Controls

- Implement architectural controls to support FERPA,
  accessibility (WCAG/508), privacy, and institutional
  policy requirements.
- Ensure solutions incorporate responsible AI checks, fairness
  validation, and observability.

Integration & Data Architecture

- Design integration patterns with core client systems (SIS,
  LMS, SSO, APIs, data sources).
- Collaborate with the Data/Technology Lead to define secure
  data pipelines, connectors, and access patterns.
- Ensure interoperability and reliability across departments
  and technical environments.

Use Case Technical Design & Feasibility

- Evaluate technology options (Azure OpenAI, Copilot, Power
  Platform, Fabric) for each use case.
- Define technical requirements, constraints, and
  architectural components for low, medium, and high-
  complexity use cases.
- Provide technical guidance to developers and ML engineers
  during design, build, and deployment.

Platform Maturity, Monitoring & MLOps

- Establish CI/CD pipelines, monitoring dashboards, cost
  transparency mechanisms, and lifecycle management
  processes.
- Set standards for performance, security, reliability, and
  operational readiness.
- Support the evolution of the platform from early pilots to
  scalable enterprise services.

Technical Leadership & Quality Assurance

- Review prototypes, solution designs, integrations, and
  deployments for technical completeness and architectural
  alignment.
- Ensure consistency of implementation across teams and
  phases.
- Provide coaching and guidance to development and engineering
  teams.

Collaboration & Engagement

The Solution Architect works closely with the Functional
Consultant to ensure technical designs meet functional
needs, with the Data/Technology Lead on integrations and
data architecture, and with developers on implementation.
The role supports the Project Manager with technical
sequencing and risk mitigation and engages governance groups
and ITS stakeholders as needed.

Qualifications

Required Skills & Experience

- Expertise in cloud/enterprise architecture, preferably
  across Azure AI services.
- Strong understanding of identity, security, compliance
  frameworks, and data governance.
- Experience designing scalable AI or cloud platforms and
  integrating enterprise systems.
- Ability to evaluate technical feasibility, optimize
  performance, and implement MLOps/CI-CD.
- Strong communication skills for translating complex
  technical concepts to non-technical stakeholders.

Good to have skills: AI Application Solution Design
(Strong), Metasonic, Microsoft Azure Artificial Inteligence
and Machine Learning (Expert), Microsoft Fabric (Expert)

Additional Information

Gross (includes base + bonuses), depending on skills and
years of experience.
