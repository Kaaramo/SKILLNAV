## Description complète

Toast creates technology to help restaurants and local
businesses succeed in a digital world, helping business
owners operate, increase sales, engage customers, and keep
employees happy.

Toast is looking for a hands-on Staff Software Engineer to
join the AI & Strategy Engineering team. In this role, you
will be a primary builder of 0-to-1 tools designed to
revolutionize how our sales teams operate. This isn't about
maintaining legacy systems; it’s about writing the code and
building the intelligence models that eliminate friction
across the Go-To-Market stack.

Working under a Senior technical leader, you will be
responsible for the "heavy lifting" of backend engineering
and Machine Learning implementation. You will bridge our
custom quoting software and Salesforce with intelligent,
agentic workflows that allow sales users to make faster,
data-backed decisions.

A day in the life (Responsibilities)

- Hands-on Execution: Spend the majority of your time "hands-
  on-keyboard," architecting and coding high-performance
  backend services and ML pipelines.
- 0-to-1 Development: Build and prototype new internal
  products from scratch that leverage LLMs and Agentic AI to
  automate account research, lead prioritization, and
  complex quoting logic.
- Core Intelligence Models: Design and implement ML models
  that provide real-time recommendations to sales users,
  reducing manual entry and increasing deal velocity.
- Infrastructure Integration: Develop the backend connective
  tissue between our custom quoting engine, Salesforce, and
  internal data lakes to ensure a seamless, low-latency end-
  to-end experience.
- Technical Problem Solving: Act as a domain expert to solve
  complex synchronization and architectural challenges
  across the GTM stack, ensuring systems are scalable and
  resilient.
- Quality & Mentorship: Drive engineering excellence through
  rigorous code reviews, automated testing, and by modeling
  best practices for SE1-SE3 engineers on the team.
- Build Great Products: Deliver significant core capabilities
  that have broad impact; take high-level goals and turn
  them into sequenced, production-ready code.
- Problem Solve: Anticipate shifts in product needs and build
  flexible, decoupled backend systems that can evolve with
  our AI strategy.
- Deliver Quality: Ensure the reliability of GTM tools by
  building deep observability into every service you ship.

What you'll need to thrive (Requirements)

- Deep Backend Expertise: Extensive experience in backend
  languages (Java, Go, Python) with a track record of
  building complex, distributed systems.
- Applied AI/ML Skills: Practical, hands-on experience
  deploying Machine Learning models and building with LLMs
  (e.g., LangChain, RAG architectures, or Model Fine-tuning)
  in a production environment.
- Technical Complexity: Experience building or deeply
  integrating with complex enterprise software, specifically
  custom quoting engines or CRM internals (Salesforce
  Apex/LWC).
- Systems Architecture: Ability to design event-driven
  architectures and manage data flow across disparate
  systems with high integrity.
- Education: Bachelor’s or Master’s degree in Computer
  Science, or a related technical field.

What will help you stand out (Nonessential Skills/Nice to
Haves)

- Experience building internal "Sales Tech" or "Fintech" tools
  that directly impact revenue.
- Familiarity with AWS data services (SageMaker, Lambda, SQS).
- A passion for removing "human-in-the-loop" friction through
  intelligent automation.
