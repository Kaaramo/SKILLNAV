Marmon Technologies India Private Limited

As a part of the global industrial organization Marmon

Under general direction, designs, develops, troubleshoots

new products. Develops software and tools in support of

Provides direction to team members and is a key contributor.

to consult for other projects. Provides coaching to junior-
level employees. Role typically requires between 5 and 8
years of experience.

Position Overview

We are looking for a Senior AI Engineer who doesn't just
design AI solutions but builds it. You are a unique blend of
a technical architect, and a versatile full-stack developer.
In this role, you will lead the charge in developing
sophisticated agentic solutions and multi agent
architectures, taking projects from a conceptual whiteboard
to a production-ready cloud environment. This is a hands-on
position; you will be expected to code, orchestrate, and
deploy while mentoring others and navigating complex
business requirements.

Mandatory Skills

• Deep expertise in RAG processes, prompt engineering, and
fine-tuning. Experience with orchestration frameworks (e.g.,
LangChain, AutoGen, CrewAI, Microsoft Agent Framework).

• Full-stack proficiency (Python/Node.js, modern frontend
frameworks, and API design).

• Expertise in vector databases (Pinecone, Milvus, Azure AI
Search, Cosmos DB) and handling diverse data formats for AI
ingestion.

• Strong understanding of cloud-native services (AWS, Azure,
or GCP).

Desired Skills

• Proven experience in multi-agent systems and agentic
workflows.

• Develop strategies for diverse data types (structured,
unstructured, vector) to optimize AI performance and RAG
accuracy.

• Work directly with stakeholders to capture requirements,
lead technical discovery meetings, and translate "business
problems" into "AI solutions."

• Understanding of multimodal models, including services
that generate embeddings for diverse file types such as
videos, CAD drawings, and more.

Opportunities for Growth

• Architect next‑generation AI ecosystems by driving
innovation in multi‑agent systems, autonomous workflows, and
enterprise‑scale orchestration patterns.

• Expand into AI technical leadership, influencing platform
strategy, establishing engineering standards, and mentoring
a growing team of AI and full‑stack developers.

• Own end‑to‑end AI product delivery, partnering directly
with business leaders to shape roadmaps and translate
complex challenges into scalable, high‑impact solutions.

• Deepen expertise in advanced multimodal AI, including
emerging embeddings, complex data pipelines (video, CAD, 3D,
sensor data), and cutting‑edge cloud-native model hosting.

• Grow into a thought leader in applied AI, presenting best
practices, guiding experimentation, and shaping
organizational adoption of new LLM, RAG, and agentic
technologies.

Required Qualifications • Professional-level Cloud
Certifications (e.g., AWS Solutions Architect, Azure AI
Engineer) and specialized AI/Agentic certifications are
highly preferred. • You are comfortable leading meetings,
managing expectations, and pushing back on technical
feasibility when necessary. • While you can work
independently, you prioritize the success of the team,
offering guidance and code reviews to fellow engineers. • 5+
years of software engineering experience, with at least 2
