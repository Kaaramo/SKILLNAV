## Description complète

We are an applied AI lab building end-to-end software
agents.

We're the makers of Devin, the first AI software engineer,
and Windsurf, the AI-native IDE. Together, they represent
our vision for collaborative AI teammates that enable
engineers to focus on more interesting problems and empower
teams to strive for more ambitious goals.

Our team is small and talent-dense. Among our founding team,
we have world-class competitive programmers, former
founders, and leaders from companies at the cutting edge of
AI including Scale AI, Palantir, Cursor, Waymo, Tesla,
Lunchclub, Modal, Google DeepMind, and Nuro.

Building Devin and Windsurf is just the first step—our
hardest challenges still lie ahead. If you’re excited to
solve some of the world’s biggest problems and build AI that
can reason on real-world tasks, apply to join us.

About the Role

Cognition’s AI Enablement Engineers help teams adopt Devin
and integrate agentic workflows into their day-to-day
development. They combine deep technical expertise,
consulting intuition, and a teacher’s mindset to guide teams
through real work—building software alongside them while
teaching how to use Devin to do the same.

As one of the first AI Enablement Engineers, you’ll also
play a key role in shaping how the program scales
globally—turning individual training engagements into
repeatable playbooks, digital learning content, and partner-
driven enablement models that allow Cognition to reach
hundreds of thousands of engineers worldwide. You’ll help
define how AI Enablement becomes a product in itself:
structured, measurable, and continuously improving in
impact.

This role is perfect for someone who has strong engineering
fundamentals, enjoys working directly with customers, and
finds deep satisfaction in teaching others and unlocking
their potential—while also thinking systematically about how
to scale those learnings to the next 100 teams.

In this role, you will:

- Lead interactive programs for enterprise engineering teams
  (live workshops, pair programming sessions)
- Guide customers through installing, configuring, and
  optimizing Devin and its associated tools (DeepWiki, MCP
  integrations, etc.)
- Pair-program with engineers at varying experience levels,
  helping them build, debug, and automate real projects
- Identify high-ROI workflows for Devin across engineering,
  QA, support, data, and product teams
- Create enablement materials, best practices, and shared
  playbooks based on customer learnings
- Work with leadership to scale AI Enablement into a
  repeatable and productized offering—through digital
  learning content, partner certifications, and scalable
  training models
- Collaborate with Cognition Engagement Managers and
  Engineering teams to continuously refine onboarding and
  provide feedback on new features

Requirements for the Role:

- Degree in a STEM field or equivalent hands-on experience
- 3+ years as a software engineer or technical consultant with
  strong coding proficiency (Python, JavaScript/TypeScript,
  or similar)
- Proven ability to communicate complex technical topics to
  diverse audiences
- Strong customer-service orientation and ability to thrive in
  interactive, people-facing environments
- Excellent verbal and written communication skills
- Demonstrated ability to learn and adapt exceptionally fast

You might excel if you…

- Have experience leading technical workshops, internal
  enablement programs, or developer onboarding initiatives
- Have deployed or integrated LLM or agent-based systems in
  production settings
- Are energized by teaching, coaching, and helping others
  reach “aha” moments
- Have previously founded or joined early-stage startups where
  autonomy and execution speed were critical
