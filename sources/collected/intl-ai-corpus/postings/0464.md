## Description complète

Role:

We are seeking an AI Engineer to work within the office of
the CIO for fundamental equities at Point72 to apply the
latest AI technologies to solve real world problems and
streamline day-to-day operations. This role partners
directly with L/S equity portfolio managers, analysts, and
business leadership to build innovative solutions to improve
efficiency and research quality across the equities
platform.

The ideal candidate is a creative problem solver with a
collaborative and commercial mindset and the ability to
develop modern AI technologies.

Responsibilities:

- Partner closely with investment professionals to understand
  their workflows and process and translate those into high
  impact AI solutions.
- Design and build AI‑driven research tools and workflows,
  with a focus on large language models (LLMs),
  retrieval‑augmented generation, and agentic systems.
- Build GenAI solutions, leveraging both internal models and
  external APIs.
- Apply emerging AI capabilities pragmatically, balancing
  innovation with robustness, speed, and adoption.
- Drive solutions from concept to production, including
  prototyping, evaluation, deployment, and user feedback
  loops.
- Collaborate with the AI platform engineering team to ensure
  solutions are scalable and well-integrated with existing
  systems.
- Be on the frontier of applying new AI technology,
  continually assessing new models and techniques for
  commercial relevance.

Requirements:

- Bachelors, MS, or PhD in machine learning, computer science,
  statistics, or other quantitative discipline.
- Academic or industry experience with data analysis, NLP, or
  applied machine learning beyond LLMs.
- Cutting edge knowledge of LLMs and modern AI systems,
  including prompt engineering, fine‑tuning, evaluation,
  tool‑use, and retrieval‑based architectures.
- Strong ability to tackle open‑ended and complex problems and
  develop practical, deployable solutions.
- Proficiency in Python, SQL, and general software engineering
  principles.
- Experience building internal platforms or tools with heavy
  end user interaction and feedback.
- Ability to work cooperatively in a team-oriented, fast-paced
  environment
- Strong communication skills and ability to collaborate with
  non‑technical but highly sophisticated end users.
- Strong commercial mindset.
- Intellectual curiosity and an interest in financial markets.
- Commitment to the highest ethical standards.

About Point72:

Point72 Asset Management is a global firm led by Steven
Cohen that invests in multiple asset classes and strategies
worldwide. Resting on more than a quarter-century of
investing experience, we seek to be the industry’s premier
asset manager through delivering superior risk-adjusted
returns, adhering to the highest ethical standards, and
offering the greatest opportunities to the industry’s
brightest talent.  We’re inventing the future of finance by
revolutionizing how we develop our people and how we use
data to shape our thinking. For more information, visit
www.Point72.com/working-here.
