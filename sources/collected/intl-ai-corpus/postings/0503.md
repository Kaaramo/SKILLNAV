### Raisonnement classification (LLM upstream)

This role focuses on building data infrastructure,
pipelines, and platforms that enable AI/ML workloads (RAG,
feature stores, vector databases) rather than directly
building, training, or fine-tuning models. The engineer
supports data preparation and deployment for generative AI
applications but does not work hands-on with model
development, inference, or LLM fine-tuning.
