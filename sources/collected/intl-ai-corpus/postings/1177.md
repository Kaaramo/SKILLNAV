WHY THIS ROLE IS IMPORTANT TO US

In SimCorp, we help some of the biggest financial
institutions in the world be successful by simplifying their

IT systems, processes, and financial knowledge. High
quality, performing, secure, and cost-effective software is

You will be working with a team of about 12 people located

Product Development team functions as a center of enablement
for artificial intelligence (AI), providing knowledge and
guidelines to other teams, as well as working hands on
implementing and operating solutions with AI.

WHAT YOU WILL BE RESPONSIBLE FOR

The Lead Software Engineer at SimCorp, at the IC5 grade, is
responsible for leading and driving the design, development,
and implementation of complex software solutions. This role
involves collaborating with cross-functional teams to
develop high-quality software that meets business
requirements and technical specifications. The Lead Software
Engineer takes ownership of key engineering projects,
provides technical guidance to junior engineers, and ensures
that software is delivered on time and within scope. This
position requires advanced problem-solving skills, expertise
in software engineering practices, and the ability to work
in a collaborative and fast-paced environment.

We work team-based, so your primary responsibility is as a
team member. You need to make sure the team delivers on its
commitments, that it’s a good place to work for you and your
teammates, and that it continuously improves.

Daily tasks are for example:

- Developing plugins and tools for generative AI models,
  Agentic AI solutions.
- Going from proof-of-concepts to high quality, production
  ready solutions.
- Integrating AI functionality with modules and Agents
  developed by other teams
- Lead the design and development of software solutions,
  ensuring they meet business needs and technical
  requirements
- Provide technical leadership and mentorship to engineering
  teams, cultivating a collaborative and high-performance
  environment
- Collaborate with product management, design, and quality
  assurance teams to ensure seamless integration and
  delivery of software solutions
- Identify and resolve complex technical issues, ensuring
  high-quality solutions that are scalable, reliable, and
  performant
- Ensure adherence to software development best practices,
  including coding standards, testing, and documentation
- Take ownership of key features and components, driving their
  design and implementation from concept to delivery
- Continuously assess and improve engineering processes,
  recommending new tools and technologies to enhance
  productivity and software quality
- Participate in code reviews, ensuring that the team follows
  best practices and maintains high standards for software
  quality
