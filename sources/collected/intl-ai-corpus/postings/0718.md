About the Role:

We are looking for a Senior Software Development Engineer in
Test (SDET 3) with strong expertise in AI tools and testing
AI-driven products. This role requires a hands-on individual
who can design and execute test strategies for AI systems,
automate intelligently, and mentor junior team members. You
will work independently, drive tasks to closure, and play a
key role in ensuring the quality of our AI initiatives.

Requirements:

- 5+ years of overall experience in QA/SDET roles
- Proven experience working with AI tools (e.g., OpenAI,
  Claude, or similar LLM platforms)
- Strong understanding of testing AI/ML products and their
  unique challenges
- Hands-on knowledge of RAG pipelines and their testing
  methodologies
- Proficiency in automation frameworks (e.g., Playwright,
  Cypress, Selenium, or similar)
- Experience with API testing (Postman, REST, GraphQL, gRPC)
- Strong problem-solving skills with the ability to work
  independently
- Mentorship and leadership skills in guiding peers or junior
  engineers

- Design, develop, and maintain automation frameworks and test
