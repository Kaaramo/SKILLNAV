About Rhythms

Rhythms is building the AI business execution system that
enables enterprises to become AI-native—fundamentally
rethinking how organizations operate, execute, and scale.
We're founded by entrepreneurs who built and sold Chronus
(acquired by PE) and Ally.io (acquired by Microsoft). We've
raised a $26M seed round from top investors including
GreenOaks, Madrona, and Accel, alongside CXOs from global
technology leaders—earning GeekWire's 2024 Deal of the Year
and named a 2025 AI Innovation of the Year finalist.

Rhythms transform how enterprises become AI-native by
connecting strategic planning and OKRs directly to day-to-
day execution and real-time progress tracking. Built on
multi-agent AI architecture, our platform connects with
Slack, Teams, Jira, Linear, and other daily tools to
automate operational workflows, surface predictive insights
and risks, eliminate manual status updates, and build
organizational memory that grows smarter over time.
Enterprise customers choose us because we're making agentic
AI reliable enough to run critical business operations..

The Role

As part of a small, high-impact engineering team building
our AI operating system. You'll work across the full product
— from understanding customer problems, to designing
solutions, to shipping production code daily.

You'll own features end-to-end across multi-agent
architectures, LLM orchestration, and user-facing
experiences that directly impact enterprise customers.
You'll join customer calls, hear feedback firsthand, and see
your work change how organizations operate — in real time.

- AI-First Builder – Deep expertise in production-ready AI
