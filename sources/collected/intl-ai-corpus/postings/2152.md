## Description complète

About this role

At BlackRock, technology is fundamental to how we serve
clients and operate at scale. Aladdin—our industry‑leading,
end‑to‑end investment management platform—embodies this by
combining deep financial expertise with advanced data and AI
capabilities.

We are seeking a Director of AI Platform / Engineering to
provide technical and strategic leadership within the
Aladdin AI Engineering organization. In this role, you will
be responsible for shaping the direction of Aladdin’s AI
platform, leading high‑impact engineering initiatives, and
ensuring the delivery of scalable, reliable, and responsible
AI solutions across investment, operations, and data science
workflows.

This role requires a blend of hands‑on technical depth,
architectural leadership, and people and delivery
leadership. You will work closely with senior engineering
leaders, product managers, data scientists, and business
stakeholders to translate AI strategy into production‑grade
platforms and services.



Key Responsibilities

Technical & Architectural Leadership

- Set the technical vision and architecture for Aladdin’s AI
  platform, including LLM‑powered and agentic AI systems.

- Lead the design and evolution of scalable AI/ML platform
  services, APIs, and microservices that support Aladdin’s
  global user base.

- Drive architectural decisions across distributed systems,
  ensuring reliability, scalability, security, and
  operational excellence.

- Establish and enforce engineering standards, design
  patterns, and best practices across AI platform teams.

- Ensure AI solutions are built with strong considerations for
  governance, observability, resilience, and responsible AI.

Delivery & Execution

- Own delivery outcomes for multiple concurrent initiatives,
  balancing near‑term execution with long‑term platform
  evolution.

- Partner with product management to define roadmaps,
  prioritize investments, and align execution with business
  outcomes.

- Oversee production readiness, including design reviews, risk
  assessments, release management, and operational support.

- Drive continuous improvement in engineering workflows,
  quality, and delivery predictability.

People & Organizational Leadership

- Lead, mentor, and develop senior engineers, technical leads,
  and managers.

- Build a strong engineering culture centered on ownership,
  rigor, collaboration, and technical excellence.

- Support hiring, onboarding, performance management, and
  succession planning.

- Act as a senior technical voice across leadership forums and
  architectural reviews.

Stakeholder & Cross‑Functional Collaboration

- Partner closely with platform, product, data science, and
  infrastructure leaders.

- Communicate complex technical concepts clearly to senior
  leadership and non‑technical stakeholders.

- Represent Aladdin AI Engineering in cross‑organizational
  initiatives and governance forums.



Required Qualifications

AI / ML‑Specific Qualifications

- Proven experience leading teams that build AI‑driven
  platforms and applications, including solutions leveraging
  large language models (LLMs), AI Agents, agent tools and
  agent skills.

- Strong hands‑on familiarity with LLM frameworks such as
  LangChain, LlamaIndex, Semantic Kernel, or equivalent.

- Experience with Google Agent Development Kit (ADK) or
  comparable agent frameworks, including designing,
  orchestrating, and operating agent‑based systems in
  production.

- Deep understanding of agentic AI architectures, including
  multi‑agent coordination, tool invocation, and stateful
  reasoning workflows.

- Experience with prompt engineering and prompt tuning at
  scale, including performance, reliability, and safety
  optimization.

- Strong understanding of ML model evaluation, monitoring, and
  behavioral drift management in production systems.

- Experience integrating vector databases (e.g., Faiss,
  Chroma) into AI system architectures.

- Solid understanding of MLOps practices, including model
  lifecycle management, deployment pipelines, and
  observability.

- Experience leveraging AInative code editors and IDEs (e.g.,
  Copilotenabled IDEs, agentassisted development
  environments, or similar AIaugmented tooling) to improve
  developer productivity, code quality, and delivery
  velocity.

- Experience establishing best practices and guardrails for
  the responsible use of AInative development tools,
  including considerations for security, IP protection,
  compliance, and code quality.

- Ability to evaluate and guide adoption of emerging AIpowered
  developer tools, balancing innovation with
  maintainability, governance, and platform standards.

- Strong awareness of responsible AI principles, governance,
  and risk management when deploying AI systems in regulated
  environments.



Core Engineering & Leadership Qualifications

- B.S. or M.S. in Computer Science, Engineering, or a related
  field.

- 10+ years of software engineering experience, including
  significant leadership of large‑scale, distributed
  systems.

- Proven track record of technical leadership, owning platform
  architecture and multi‑team delivery.

- Deep expertise in object‑oriented programming, particularly
  Java and Python.

- Strong experience designing and operating scalable APIs,
  microservices, and distributed systems in production.

- Extensive experience with cloud platforms (Azure preferred;
  AWS or GCP acceptable).

- Strong understanding of event‑driven architectures,
  messaging systems (e.g., Kafka), and asynchronous
  processing.

- Experience with containerization and orchestration
  technologies such as Docker and Kubernetes.

- Hands‑on experience with relational and NoSQL datastores,
  including Cassandra.

- Strong grasp of Agile delivery models, SDLC governance, and
  operational excellence.

- Demonstrated ability to influence across teams and senior
  leadership.
