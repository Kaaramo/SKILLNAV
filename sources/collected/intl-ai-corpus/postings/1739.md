## Description complète

We are seeking a  Lead Product Development Engineer –
Generative & Agentic AI to define, architect, and deliver
next-generation GenAI and agentic AI–powered product
platforms that drive measurable business and customer
impact. This role is designed for highly experienced
engineers who have successfully led complex, production-
grade GenAI and agentic AI systems and who operate as both a
technical authority and a strategic product partner across
the organization.

In this role, you will own the architecture and evolution of
GenAI, agentic AI, and Model Context Protocol (MCP)–based
systems. You will shape the AI roadmap, mentor senior
engineers, and ensure that AI capabilities are scalable,
secure, cost-efficient, and responsibly deployed across
multiple product lines.

About You – experience, education, skills, and
accomplishments

- 7 years of experience in software engineering and AI/ML,
  with proven delivery of GenAI and agent-based systems in
  real production environments.
- Strong leadership experience building and scaling large,
  enterprise-grade GenAI solutions embedded into products.
- Expert proficiency in Python, distributed systems, APIs,
  microservices and overall system design.
- Hands-on expertise with LLMs, agent frameworks (LangChain,
  AutoGen, CrewAI, LlamaIndex, etc.), and agent
  orchestration patterns.
- Deep knowledge of RAG architectures, embeddings, vector
  databases (FAISS, Pinecone, Milvus, Weaviate), and
  advanced retrieval and evaluation techniques.

It would be great if you also have:

- Experience leading AI copilots, agentic platforms, or multi-
  product agent-based systems.
- Familiarity with Model Context Protocol (MCP) and context
  standardization approaches.
- Familiarity with AI governance frameworks, compliance, and
  regulated environments.
- Contributions to internal AI platforms, open-source
  projects, or industry thought leadership

What will you be doing in this role:

- Define and drive the technical vision for GenAI, agentic AI
  and MCP-based capabilities across multiple product lines.
- Lead prompt engineering, memory management and MCP-based
  context orchestration for scalable AI systems.
- Design and optimize RAG, enterprise knowledge and MCP-backed
  context platforms, including secure data ingestion and
  governance.
- Guide the integration of GenAI and agentic AI features into
  core products, APIs and enterprise platforms.
- Drive production readiness covering reliability,
  observability, performance, and responsible / safe agent
  behaviour.

What You’ll Build

- Company-wide GenAI, Agentic AI, and MCP-based product
  platforms
- Scalable RAG, agent orchestration, and context management
  frameworks
- Enterprise standards for AI quality, safety, and reliability
- A strong technical leadership bench for the next generation
  of GenAI engineers
