### Raisonnement classification (LLM upstream)

This role is focused on optimizing and deploying traditional
deep learning models (CNNs, Transformers) on edge/embedded
devices for computer vision tasks. It involves model
optimization techniques like quantization, pruning, and
distillation for real-time inference on constrained
hardware, rather than building LLM/agent-based systems. This
is classic ML/DL engineering work targeting edge deployment.
