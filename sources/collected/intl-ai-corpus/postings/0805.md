## Description complète

Onebridge, a Marlabs Company, is a global AI and Data
Analytics Consulting Firm that empowers organizations
worldwide to drive better outcomes through data and
technology. Since 2005, we have partnered with some of the
largest healthcare, life sciences, financial services, and
government entities across the globe. We have an exciting
opportunity for a highly skilled AI Engineer to join our
innovative and dynamic team.

Candidates are ideally located in Bangalore or Pune, with
flexibility to consider other major metros such as Hyderabad
or Chennai. This role requires working onsite two days per
week.

AI Engineer | About You

As an AI Engineer, you are responsible for designing and
implementing cutting-edge AI solutions that drive innovation
and efficiency. You thrive in fast-paced environments and
are passionate about solving complex problems using machine
learning and AI technologies. You are a self-starter who
enjoys working independently as well as collaborating with
cross-functional teams. Your curiosity and drive to stay
ahead of emerging technologies make you a valuable
contributor to any AI initiative. You take pride in building
scalable, secure, and impactful AI systems that deliver real
business value.

AI Engineer | Day-to-Day

- Architect and develop AI-powered services, tools, and
  products that align with business goals and technical
  requirements, ensuring scalability, reliability, and
  performance.
- Lead end-to-end project execution, from initial concept and
  design through development, testing, and deployment, while
  maintaining high standards of quality and efficiency.
- Build and integrate AI models using Azure OpenAI and other
  Azure AI Services, applying best practices in model
  training, fine-tuning, and production deployment.
- Collaborate with stakeholders across teams to translate
  business needs into clear technical specifications,
  ensuring alignment throughout the development lifecycle.
- Stay current with emerging AI technologies and tools,
  proactively identifying opportunities to enhance existing
  systems or introduce new capabilities.
- Work independently and within global teams, contributing to
  a collaborative and innovative environment while managing
  tasks with minimal supervision.

AI Engineer | Skills & Experience

- 5+ years of experience in AI/ML engineering or related
  fields.
- Strong programming skills in Python and SQL, with hands-on
  experience in ML libraries and UI development using
  Streamlit or JavaScript frameworks.
- Deep expertise in Microsoft Azure, including Azure AI
  Foundry, Azure OpenAI, and related services.
- Solid understanding of CI/CD pipelines, version control
  systems (Git), and Azure DevOps workflows.
- Experience working with both open-source and proprietary
  LLMs, including Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG)
  implementations.
- Knowledge of secure API/web service development and data
  protection best practices.
- Excellent communication skills and a collaborative mindset
  for working across teams and time zones.
