We’re hiring a Senior AI Engineer to lead the design,
development, and refinement of AI agent systems that power

This role goes beyond building models — you’ll drive system-
level innovation, mentor peers, and shape how our AI
copilots tackle domain-specific challenges at scale. You’ll
work at the intersection of cutting-edge research and
engineering execution, ensuring reliability, robustness, and
real-world impact.

Data Creation & Intelligence

- Architect synthetic data pipelines to strengthen model

- Lead development of complex evaluation datasets (especially

- Build scalable systems for automated data verification and
  pipeline orchestration

Model Response Analysis & Monitoring

- Define rubrics and frameworks for evaluating nuanced,
  ambiguous AI tasks
- Build monitoring and optimization systems for continuous
  production feedback loops
- Create tools that empower domain experts to collaborate in
  evolving datasets & model behavior

System Innovation & Scale

- Research and implement novel approaches for long-context
  handling, cascading error reduction, and structured
  outputs
- Advance prompt engineering, fine-tuning, and control
  techniques for greater reliability
- Drive the design and evaluation of production-grade, agentic
  AI systems at scale

Core Skills

- Strong programming skills in Python
  (Go/JavaScript/TypeScript a plus)
