## Description complète

At Ciena's Blue Planet, you'll be part of a dynamic team
pushing the boundaries of network automation. Your role as
an AI Engineer will involve designing and developing AI
agents using cutting-edge frameworks, fine-tuning large
language models, and implementing advanced techniques like
Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG). You'll also leverage
cloud platforms and databases for efficient AI solution
deployment and data handling.

Responsibilities

- Design and develop AI agents using frameworks like
  LangChain, LangGraph, Langflow, and MCP Servers.
- Fine-tune and optimize large language models (LLMs) such as
  GPT models and Llama for diverse applications.
- Implement RAG techniques and integrate vector databases like
  Qdrant and ChromaDB.
- Enhance AI agent operations with tools like langfuse and
  litellm for robust security and guardrails.
- Utilize cloud platforms (AWS, Google Cloud, Azure) and
  databases (Postgres, Neo4j, Clickhouse) for scalable AI
  solutions.
- Optimize workflows and data processing with technologies
  like Apache Airflow, Redis, Mlflow, Minio, and Apache
  Kedro.
- Apply prompt engineering techniques and CI/CD processes for
  efficient development.
- Collaborate with cross-functional teams to align AI
  solutions with business goals.

Qualifications

- Minimum 3-5 years of experience in AI engineering with Java
  proficiency.
- Expertise in AI frameworks (LangChain, LangGraph) and fine-
  tuning LLMs.
- Strong knowledge of vector databases and AI agent security
  protocols.
- Familiarity with cloud platforms, database technologies, and
  prompt engineering.
- Experience with CI/CD processes and tools like Apache
  Airflow, Redis, Mlflow, Minio, and Apache Kedro.
- Bonus: Experience with PySpark for large-scale data
  processing.
- Knowledge of emerging AI frameworks and cloud-native tools
  for AI deployment.
- Advanced understanding of AI agent optimization and
  collaborative systems.
- Interest in contributing to open-source AI initiatives and a
  passion for driving AI innovation.
- Professional certifications in data science or machine
  learning are a plus.
