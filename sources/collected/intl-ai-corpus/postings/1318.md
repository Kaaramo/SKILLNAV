## Description complète

About Particle

Particle Health is revolutionizing healthcare data analytics
and interoperability. Our mission is to unlock the power of
medical records in an intelligent platform that focuses
health back on the patient.

Through our modern data platform, we enable healthcare
innovators—ranging from value-based care providers and
payers to digital health companies and health systems—to
access standardized, real-time patient data. By bridging
fragmented data silos, Particle empowers organizations to
tackle critical use cases such as care coordination, risk
stratification, patient identity management, and regulatory
compliance with ease and precision.

Our solutions of Snapshot, Signal, Navigator, and Workbench
address the complex challenges of healthcare data analytics
and interoperability, helping our customers transform data
into actionable insights, improve patient outcomes, and
streamline workflows.

About the Role

As a Senior Software Engineer II, Backend at Particle
Health, you’ll play a key technical leadership role in
building, scaling, and evolving the core services that power
our healthcare data platform and improve healthcare for
millions of patients. You’ll work primarily in Golang (with
some Python) to design and operate robust, secure, and high-
performance APIs that enable customers to reliably access
and utilize healthcare data at scale.

Beyond owning complex backend systems, you’ll help shape how
the engineering organization builds software, including
advancing the team’s use of modern tooling — particularly
AI-assisted development tools — to improve code quality,
developer velocity, and system reliability. This role is
ideal for an experienced backend engineer who pairs strong
technical judgment, architectural thinking, and systems-
level ownership with curiosity about improving engineering
practices and outcomes.

You’ll collaborate closely with Product, Data, and
Engineering teammates to deliver high-quality solutions in a
highly regulated, mission-driven environment, operating with
meaningful ownership and influence across the backend
platform. This role is best suited for engineers who have
operated at a senior, staff, or technical lead level and are
comfortable owning large, business-critical backend systems.

Location & Work Schedule

This is a hybrid role based in New York City. The team works
in-office two days per week , so applicants must be located
within commuting distance and comfortable with in-person
collaboration.

What You'll Do

- Design, develop, and maintain RESTful APIs and backend
  services using Golang (and some Python).
- Build scalable, secure, and performant services that handle
  healthcare data with strong privacy, security, compliance,
  and reliability requirements.
- Leverage AI-assisted development tools to improve
  productivity, debugging, testing, documentation, and code
  quality; help define best practices and guardrails to
  ensure correctness, security, and maintainability
- Integrate backend services with data pipelines and
  processing workflows.
- Take end-to-end ownership of complex systems, including
  architecture, implementation, observability, operational
  health, and long-term reliability
- Lead or meaningfully contribute to architectural discussions
  and long-term technical planning, balancing near-term
  delivery with long-term platform health
- Identify and implement measurable improvements to system
  performance, efficiency, scalability, and reliability
- Mentor engineers and contribute to improving engineering
  standards, tooling, and development practices.
- Partner closely with Product to translate requirements into
  durable technical solutions, including supporting customer
  integrations and production use cases

What We SeekRequired:

- 8+ years of backend engineering experience, with sustained
  focus on APIs and distributed systems in production
  environments
- Strong production experience with Golang; comfortable
  working in Python when needed.
- Significant experience using AI-assisted development tools
  (e.g., Copilot, Cursor, Claude Code) to implement
  features, improve engineering workflows, with sound
  judgment around quality, risk and maintainability.
- Deep understanding of RESTful API design, scalable service
  patterns, and backend architecture.
- Experience with SQL and/or NoSQL databases and cloud
  environments (ideally GCP).
- Demonstrated ability to design, build, evolve, and operate
  complex systems independently, including making
  architectural tradeoffs
- Strong debugging, analytical, and communication skills.
- Bachelor’s degree in Computer Science, Engineering, or
  equivalent practical experience.

Nice to Have:

- Experience with healthcare data standards (FHIR, HL7, CCDA).
- Familiarity with data engineering pipelines or ETL
  workflows.
- Hands-on experience with Docker, Kubernetes, and modern
  CI/CD tooling.
- Experience working in GCP.
- Infrastructure-as-Code experience, particularly Terraform.
- Experience with event-driven architectures and message
  queues.
- Knowledge of API security patterns (OAuth, JWT).
- Familiarity with healthcare compliance frameworks (HIPAA,
  SOC 2).
- Experience helping define best practices or internal

What You Bring:

  grow domain expertise
- A collaborative mindset with an interest in mentoring and

- A strong sense of initiative and ownership across complex

- A continuous-improvement mindset, including curiosity about
