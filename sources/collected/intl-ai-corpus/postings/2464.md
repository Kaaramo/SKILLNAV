## Description complète

We are looking for a highly skilled Senior Data Engineer to
join our Data Engineering chapter within the Technology
Platform domain. The ideal candidate will have strong
expertise in real-time data streaming, modern data
platforms, and AI/ML operationalization
(AIOps/MLOps/LLMOps).

You will be responsible for designing, building, and
optimizing scalable data pipelines and enabling advanced
analytics and AI-driven solutions.

RequirementsKey Responsibilities

- Design and implement real-time data streaming pipelines
  using Kafka and related technologies
- Build and maintain scalable data engineering solutions on
  Cloudera platform
- Develop and optimize ETL/ELT pipelines for large-scale
  structured and unstructured data
- Collaborate with data scientists to deploy and manage ML
  models (MLOps/LLMOps frameworks)
- Implement and manage CI/CD pipelines for data and ML
  workflows
- Work with OpenShift/Kubernetes for containerized deployments
- Ensure data quality, governance, and security best practices
- Monitor, troubleshoot, and optimize data workflows and
  infrastructure
- Partner with cross-functional teams including product,
  engineering, and analytics

Required Skills & Experience

- 6+ years of experience in Data Engineering
- Strong hands-on experience with Apache Kafka (Real-Time
  Streaming - RTS)
- Expertise in Cloudera Data Platform (CDP) or similar big
  data ecosystems
- Experience with CI/CD pipelines (Jenkins, GitLab, etc.)
- Hands-on experience with OpenShift/Kubernetes
- Strong programming skills in Python/Scala/Java
- Experience with distributed data processing frameworks
  (Spark, Hive, etc.)
- Good understanding of data modeling and data warehousing
  concepts

Preferred Skills

- Experience in AIOps, MLOps, or LLMOps
- Exposure to AI/ML lifecycle management and deployment
- Knowledge of cloud platforms (AWS/Azure/GCP) is a plus
- Experience in banking or financial services domain

Soft Skills

- Strong problem-solving and analytical thinking
- Excellent communication and stakeholder management
- Ability to work in agile and fast-paced environments
