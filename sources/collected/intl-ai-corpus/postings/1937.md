### Raisonnement classification (LLM upstream)

This role focuses on developing computer vision and
perception systems using traditional ML/DL techniques
(calibration, sensor fusion, SLAM, 3D vision) for aerospace
applications. While it involves ML-based vision techniques,
it emphasizes classical computer vision and robotics
perception rather than LLMs, generative AI, or agent-based
systems.

## Responsabilités

- Own end-to-end development of perception pipelines for robotic and mobile platforms including mobile robots, hand-held, and airborne systems
- Implement GPU-enabled solutions for indoor and outdoor localization, mapping, detection, and segmentation using cameras, LiDAR, IMUs, and satellite navigation
- Design perception rigs and associated lifecycle software including calibration and health management for field applications
- Use rapid-prototyping techniques to model and simulate perception systems with software and hardware in the loop testing
- Collaborate with ML teams to produce scalable perception solutions suitable for large dataset acquisition while minimizing labeling needs
- Work collaboratively with international teams of software development and research engineers
- Help adopt new tools and technology, perform proof-of-concepts, and support implementation engagements
