### Raisonnement classification (LLM upstream)

This is a Value Engineering / Solution Consulting role at an
AI-driven company (Celonis), but the role itself is
customer-facing consulting focused on demonstrating business
value, process improvement, and ROI analysis. The engineer
uses Celonis's AI-powered platform as a tool to help
customers, but does not build, train, fine-tune, or deploy
AI/ML models directly. The work is about applying existing
AI-powered products to solve customer problems rather than
developing AI systems.
