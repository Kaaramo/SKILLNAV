About Arc

Arc is building the first AI-native business cash management
platform. We empower high-growth companies to manage,
analyze, and act on their financial data through a unified
cash management, treasury, and debt-financing
platform—powered by AI.

Founded in 2021, Arc is backed by world-class investors
(Series A), supports thousands of technology companies as
customers, and manages billions of dollars in transaction
volume. With offices in NYC and SF, we operate on a
Monday–Wednesday in-office model (Thu–Fri optional in-office
or WFH).

We’re at an inflection point.

Large enough to matter; small enough to innovate.

The Role

We are seeking a Applied AI Engineer to join Arc’s

and delivery of Arc’s AI-powered products across both cash
