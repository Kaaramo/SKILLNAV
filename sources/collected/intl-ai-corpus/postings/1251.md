## Description complète

FE fundinfo is the leading provider of fund data in the UK
and specializes in the collection, validation, dissemination
and analysis of fund information within data feeds,
documentation, web pages and Internet-based fund selection
tools. As part of a multibillion-pound equity business owned
by HG Capital, FE fundinfo is an aggressively growing
company, through sales and acquisitions, with operations in
the UK, Chennai, Hong Kong, Brno, Singapore, Zurich,
Luxembourg and Australia.

Technology is at the centre of FE fundinfo and how it
delivers investment fund data and advice to global clients.
Teams take a DevSecOps approach to delivery, owning services
through the software lifecycle. Our cloud first, code first
approach enables rapid response and adaptation of solutions.
The AI Data Engineer - AI will be part of the AI team that
builds and maintains the AI products and services that
enable FE fundinfo to leverage the power of artificial
intelligence and machine learning to transform the business
and deliver value to the customers.

Your key responsibilities as a Senior AI Developer will be
to:

- Designing and implementing agent‑based AI systems,
  multi‑step reasoning workflows, and LLM‑orchestrated
  automation pipelines.
- Building scalable AI services using LLM orchestration
  frameworks, inference APIs, and modern RAG architectures.
- Developing retrieval systems, tool‑calling flows, memory
  management processes, and end‑to‑end RAG pipelines.
- Applying LLMOps/MLOps practices, including prompt
  versioning, evaluation processes, monitoring, telemetry,
  and cost optimization.
- Work with Databricks and MLflow for experimentation,
  telemetry, and AI observability.
- Developing AI‑enabled applications using React, Next.js,
  Python (FastAPI), or .NET Core while integrating AI with
  enterprise systems.
- Ensuring reliable cloud deployments, CI/CD automation, and
  production‑level operational standards across all AI
  services.

To join us as a Senior AI Developer you will need the
following experience and skills:

- You must have hands‑on experience building agentic AI
  systems or LLM‑powered workflows using modern frameworks
  and tools.
- You will have a strong understanding of RAG architectures,
  embeddings, vector search, memory patterns, and
  tool‑calling interfaces.
- You should bring practical knowledge of Databricks, MLflow,
  or similar LLMOps/MLOps toolchains for experimentation and
  observability.
- You must be proficient in React, Next.js, TypeScript, and
  backend development using Python (FastAPI) or .NET Core.
- You will have experience working with Azure cloud services,
  CI/CD pipelines, Docker, and relational database
  technologies such as PostgreSQL or SQL Server.
