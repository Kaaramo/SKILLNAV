## Description complète

Role Overview:

As Cambiar expands its national portfolio of education
innovation initiatives, we are scaling our digital
ecosystem—integrating systems, data, and tools and building
products that power our mission. We are seeking a Staff
Software Engineer, AI/ML Focus to join our Technology team,
reporting to the SVP, Technology. This role is critical in
building the next generation of our products and internal
tools.

You will be a lead software engineer skilled at AI and
product development; passionate about using technology to
drive positive social impact. In this role, you will take
significant ownership of technical projects and building
tools and products directly or guiding the work of product
development contractors/vendors in partnership with various
Cambiar product teams. The ideal candidate brings deep
technical acumen and user-focussed problem solving, combined
with the ability to move fast and lay sound groundwork for
future scale. This role requires balancing operational
excellence with the flexibility and creativity that drive
innovation.

What you’ll do:

This role requires hands-on execution, a builder mindset and
expertise in AI and software development (esp backend and
data systems). You will be building tools and products to
enable both internal efficiencies and external products; in
partnership with product teams and where relevant dev
vendors.

Core Responsibilities:

- AI Product Development: Lead the design, prototyping,
  development, and deployment of new user-facing products
  and internal tooling, with a heavy emphasis on leveraging
  Artificial Intelligence both within the product and in
  product development. Implement AI applications/models and
  tuning/RAG where needed. Build products/features, starting
  with MVPs and iterating based on user feedback, while
  establishing reusable patterns for future development.
- System Architecture, Health & Sustainability: As part of
  broader product development, build and ensure systems are
  reliable, secure, performant, and aligned with business
  objectives leveraging both existing or (where needed)
  helping build new organizational infrastructure and data
  systems. Support the health and sustainability of the
  codebase—notice and advocate for patterns that allow code
  to be flexible, scalable, and approachable to other
  engineers.
- Communication, Collaboration & Iteration: While leading and
  owning AI product development and related aspects,
  communicate and collaborate closely across multiple
  roles/stakeholders eg. with Product Management to scope
  and execute product features per prioritization and
  roadmap or with Software Development Vendors to
  design/build/maintain parts of the product. Lean into our
  value of rapid learning cycles—gathering and improving
  product per constant feedback.
- Security & Compliance Champion: Champion security and
  privacy, including looking for ways to make it easier to
  safeguard FERPA/COPPA compliance for sensitive data and
  incorporate security best practices. Implement data
  privacy and security best practices within products and AI
  applications.

This Role Is for You If…

- You bring 5+ years of software engineering experience with
  2+ years of hands-on AI/ML product development ; including
  developing new web/mobile and AI applications from scratch
  with rapid iterations. We value proven ability to ship AI
  features over years of general experience; including
  leveraging AI for automations and product experience with
  LLM tools eg. LangChain.
- You possess backend expertise and proficiency in building
  scalable, robust APIs and systems; including microservices
  architecture and agile development methodologies.
- You thrive in dynamic, entrepreneurial settings, balancing
  structure with adaptability and creative problem-solving.
  You possess a comfort with a startup mindset, taking
  initiative, and dealing with ambiguity.
- You can collaborate effectively with cross-functional
  partners in product, data, policy, and more.
- You are an excellent communicator who can translate and
  motivate complex technical topics into accessible,
  actionable insights for diverse audiences.
- You have some core tech/AI skills:  Python, experience with
  LLM applications (LangChain/similar), and modern web
  frameworks (AWS/GCP). Nice to have: Experience with our
  specific stack (Rails, React, PostgreSQL)
- Experience in education, nonprofit, or social impact
  organizations (EdTech) is a plus.
