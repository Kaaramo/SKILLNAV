About Fusemachines

Founded in 2013, Fusemachines is a global provider of
enterprise AI products and services, on a mission to
democratize AI. Leveraging proprietary AI Studio and AI
Engines, the company helps drive the clients’ AI Enterprise
Transformation, regardless of where they are in their
Digital AI journeys. With offices in North America, Asia,
and Latin America, Fusemachines provides a suite of
enterprise AI offerings and specialty services that allow
organizations of any size to implement and scale AI.
Fusemachines serves companies in industries such as retail,
manufacturing, and government. Fusemachines continues to
actively pursue the mission of democratizing AI for the

communities and helping organizations achieve their full

As an AI Automation Engineer, you will play a pivotal role
in delivering high-quality, tailored AI and automation
solutions that directly address client challenges and
generate tangible value. You will lead technical
implementation efforts, support ongoing engineering for
retainer clients, and help build scalable, productized
solutions that can be deployed across multiple clients.

Key Responsibilities

- Design, develop, and deploy tailored AI and automation
  solutions based on client-specific needs and objectives.
- Translate ambiguous business problems into scalable, end-to-
  end AI workflows using Python, n8n (or similar tools),
  OpenAI APIs, and vector databases.
- Build full end-to-end AI automations, including triggers,
  LLM logic, API calls, data storage, error handling,
  monitoring, and iteration.
- Design and deploy AI agent systems, including multi-step
  workflows that call tools, interact with APIs, and operate
  autonomously.
- Integrate with third-party AI tools and platforms to solve
  real-world problems such as customer service queues, phone
  routing systems, voice agents, and agent-driven workflows.
- Act as a technical partner for retainer clients, owning
  solution delivery, refinement, and ongoing maintenance.
- Own the deployment, iteration, and continuous improvement of
  AI agent systems across multiple client teams.
- Create technical documentation, client demos, and internal
  playbooks to support reuse, scalability, and knowledge
  sharing.
- Stay current on emerging AI tools, libraries, and
  methodologies to continuously improve solution quality and
  delivery speed.

Success in This Role Looks Like

- Solutions delivered to clients consistently meet or exceed
  expectations and show measurable business impact (e.g.,
  time saved, cost reduced, conversion increased).
- Retainer clients view you as a go-to engineering
  partner—relying on your expertise to expand AI usage and
  optimize existing workflows.
- You bring creativity and a strategic approach to problem
  solving and your solutions designs.

Qualifications

- 3 to 5+ years of experience in software engineering or
  applied systems development.
- Demonstrated project experience building AI-driven and
  automation-based solutions using tools such as GPT APIs,
  workflow orchestration platforms, and embeddings.
- Hands-on experience building end-to-end AI agent workflows,
  including phone trees, customer support bots, voice
  agents, or API-driven AI agents.
- Experience with Model Context Protocol (MCP) servers, or
  strong interest and ability to ramp up quickly.
- Familiarity with modern AI-native toolchains, such as n8n,
  Make, Vapi, Zapier, LangGraph, LlamaIndex Agents, OpenAI
  Assistants API, or similar platforms.
- Strong experience with Python and related frameworks,
  including prompt engineering, RAG pipelines, and system
  integration patterns.
- Proven ability to translate ambiguous business goals into
  autonomous AI workflows and production-ready
  implementations.
- Experience supporting client relationships and managing
  multiple priorities in parallel.
- Self-directed, collaborative, and comfortable balancing deep
  technical builds with fast, iterative prototyping.
- Prior consulting experience or work in fast-paced startup
  environments is a plus
