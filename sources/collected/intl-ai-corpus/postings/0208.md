## Description complète

About The Role:

As a Solutions Engineer, at Buzz Solutions, you will play a
pivotal role in driving the adoption of our AI-powered
solutions, PowerAI and PowerGUARD, within the electric
utility sector. This role combines pre-sales technical
consulting (60%) and post-sales customer enablement (40%),
ensuring a seamless experience from initial engagement
through solution adoption through AI / ML based projects.

This is a high-impact role for someone who thrives on
solving complex problems, loves working directly with
customers, and enjoys strategic thinking and hands-on
execution. You will be the face of Buzz Solutions for some
of the nation’s largest utilities, guiding them through
adopting and integrating our AI solutions. You will engage
in deep business and technical discovery, creatively
position our value in their workflows, and collaborate
closely to maximize their success. We are looking for
someone who is self-driven, resourceful, and eager to
grow—someone who sees this role as an opportunity to
demonstrate their expertise, shape the future of AI in
utilities, and make a real industry impact. If you enjoy
working on a mix of technical challenges, client engagement,
and strategic problem-solving, we want to hear from you.

Key Responsibilities

Sales Support:

- Gather customer requirements to understand and document our
  clients' needs and challenges.
- Architect and demonstrate AI/ML-powered solutions that meet
  client needs, including computer vision, data ingestion,
  analytics, and API integrations
- Collaborate closely with Account Executives to strengthen
  relationships and drive new business by leading the
  RFI/RFP process.
- Lead the design and deployment of end-to-end ML and computer
  vision workflows and solutions for utility clients and
  their use cases
- Collaborate with customer technical teams during deployment
  to ensure seamless integration, guide best practices, and
  optimize solutions for long-term success.
- Develop tailored solutions that meet customer requirements.
- Complete Vendor Security Audits required by clients to
  ensure the security and compliance of our solutions with
  industry standards.
- Answer technical questions from clients regarding our
  technology, features, and solutions.
- Create compelling demos, visualizations, and technical
  presentations that clearly communicate the value of our
  AI-driven platform to both technical and non-technical
  audiences
- Provide comprehensive training sessions with clients, both
  virtually and onsite.
- Contribute to successfully implementing and integrating our
  solutions into clients’ workflows.

Travel Requirements:

- This role includes periodic travel to client locations and
  industry events. Candidates should be prepared for travel
  that may vary in frequency and duration based on project
  requirements.
- Travel will primarily be domestic (within the U.S.), with
  occasional international trips.
- Candidates should be flexible and available for occasional
  short-notice travel to meet client and project deadlines.
- All travel expenses, including transportation,
  accommodation, and meals, are covered by the company.
- A valid passport is required, and candidates should be able
  to obtain any necessary visas for international travel.

Minimum Qualifications:

- 5+ years of experience in a solutions engineering or sales
  engineering role, ideally supporting AI/ML or data-driven
  software products
- Proven track record of building and operationalizing AI/ML
  workflows and solutions
- Strong understanding of AI/ML concepts - neural network
  architectures, convolutional neural networks (detection,
  segmentation, classification based supervised learning
- High-level proficiency with computers and business software,
  including CRM systems, presentation tools, and
  productivity software (e.g., Microsoft Office Suite,
  HubSpot, Canva, etc.)
- Background working in and/or a solid understanding of
  cybersecurity and information security standards,
  including ISO 27001 and SOC2.
- Proven ability to build and strengthen customer
  relationships, guiding them through technical discovery
  and solution adoption.
- Excellent verbal and written communication skills to
  articulate complex technical concepts clearly to both
  technical and non-technical audiences.
- Strong problem-solving skills with the ability to understand
  customer needs and provide tailored solutions.
- Experience with scripting or automation (e.g., Python, VBA)
  to improve personal efficiency and productivity.

Bonus Skills:

- Experience selling, buying, or integrating Esri products
  like ArcGIS.
- Experience working in the Electric Utility Industry or Power
  Engineering Industry.
- Experience working as a field technician or field engineer
  in the utility industry, especially as a substation
  technician, utility inspector, or lineman.
