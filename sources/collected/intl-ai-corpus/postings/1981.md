## Description complète

Multiverse is the upskilling platform for AI and Tech
adoption.

We have partnered with 1,500+ companies to deliver a new
kind of learning that's transforming today’s workforce.

Our upskilling apprenticeships are designed for people of
any age and career stage to build critical AI, data, and
tech skills. Our learners have driven $2bn+ ROI for their
employers, using the skills they’ve learned to improve
productivity and measurable performance.

In June 2022, we announced a $220 million Series D funding
round co-led by StepStone Group, Lightspeed Venture Partners
and General Catalyst. With a post-money valuation of $1.7bn,
the round makes us the UK’s first EdTech unicorn.

But we aren’t stopping there. With a strong operational
footprint and 800+ employees, we have ambitious plans to
continue scaling. We’re building a world where tech skills
unlock people’s potential and output.  Join Multiverse and
power our mission to equip the workforce to win in the AI
era.

The Opportunity

Multiverse's mission is to equip the workforce to win in the
AI era. As Senior AI Solutions Engineer, and a key technical
contributor within our AI Transformation engineering squad,
you will play an important role in transforming our business
to deliver on that mission. You will lead the development
and delivery of AI-powered solutions within the squad,
moving beyond the theoretical to ship production-ready
systems. We are looking for an experienced engineer who
thrives on making AI work in the real world and operates
with confidence at the intersection of system design and
advanced AI implementation. If you are passionate about
shaping the future of work and education, join us at
Multiverse.

Key Responsibilities

Build and maintain autonomous agent-based solutions that
generate real impact to our internal teams, enabling them to
better serve our customers

- Design and deliver AI solutions: Partner with technical and
  business teams to develop new AI solutions
- Own complex squad initiatives: Take responsibility for high-
  impact projects from design through to deployment, user
  adoption, and iteration
- Drive technical quality: Lead code reviews, contribute to
  design decisions, and identify trade-offs to maintain
  architectural integrity and system reliability
- Share expertise and raise the bar: Play active role in
  ensuring squad keeps pace with technological advances,
  mentor peers, and contribute to improving ways of working
  within the team
- Collaborate cross-functionally: Ensure that solutions meet
  user requirements, deliver impact across the organisation,
  and remain aligned with other engineering squads

About You

- Proven Senior-level experience: 3–5 years in software
  engineering, with strong understanding of Applied AI
  fundamentals and experience working in cross-functional
  product teams
- Expertise in:
  - System architecture: Proven ability to design, implement,
    and maintain complex RAG pipelines and solution
    architectures, ensuring low latency and high relevance
    in information retrieval
  - AI Orchestration: Proven experience in developing
    embeddings, MCPs, and agentic workflows, enabling
    robust, secure and scalable AI-driven systems
- Required engineering skills: Proficient in Python and
  TypeScript, with experience building APIs, microservices,
  and cloud-native applications
- AI-enabled productivity: Comfortable leveraging AI-assisted
  development tools to enhance your own and the squad's
  output
- Growth mindset: You're curious, open to feedback, and
  excited to share what you learn while contributing to an
  inclusive, high-performing culture.
