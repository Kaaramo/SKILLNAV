## Description complète

About Reedsy

We’re here to give authors the tools and resources they need
to create beautiful books. Our marketplace gathers the
industry's best publishing professionals — the likes of Neil
Gaiman's editor, Nora Roberts' book marketer, and GRRM’s
cover designer.

We’ve grown to a community of 5,000,000 authors, and
transformed the way people write and publish their book.
Want to hop on board and help us get to our next
destination? Cool, keep reading!

Responsibilities

At Reedsy, our goal is to build the best ecosystem for
writers, supporting them at every stage of their creative
journey. Over the years, we've developed a suite of
products, including a marketplace, book discovery platform,
writing app, central authentication system, and email
marketing tools.

As a publishing startup, we're thoughtful about how we
integrate AI. As an AI Engineer, you'll help us empower
authors to do more — enhancing creativity, not replacing it.
You’ll focus on building intelligent features that support
storytelling and streamline workflows.

In this role, you will:

- Design and implement AI-powered tools for authors (e.g. a
  narrative co-pilot, visual storyboarding assistant, etc.);
- Improve internal workflows by automating repetitive tasks
  and enhancing UX across our products;
- Build internal tools for AI development, including
  evaluation benchmarks and automated testing;
- Collaborate closely with engineers and operations teams to
  identify high-impact AI opportunities.

Requirements

- Proven experience integrating AI/ML into real-world
  products, with ownership of the full lifecycle — from
  modeling and evaluation to deployment and go-to-market
  execution.
- Experience working with large language models (e.g., prompt
  engineering, fine-tuning, model selection, retrieval-
  augmented generation)
- Familiarity with orchestration tools (e.g., LangChain,
  LlamaIndex, or custom chaining frameworks)
- Knowledge of hallucination detection and mitigation
  strategies
- Hands-on experience with cloud platforms (preferably Google
  Cloud Platform) and scalable AI deployment
- Familiarity with embeddings, vector databases, and
  Elasticsearch
- Experience with AI developer tooling (e.g., Cursor,
  intelligent CI/CD, AI-powered QA)

Bonus Points

- Fluent in multiple languages. Reedsy welcomes applicants of
  all cultures and backgrounds.
- A good dose of ambition, a willingness to learn, and a great
  sense of humor

Benefits

- Work from home
- Paid paternal and maternal leave
- Annual team retreat

Salary range:

70k-120k EUR - 0-0.2% equity
