About the Role

We are seeking an experienced Sr. Developer for Enablement
(C4E) delivery team. This role is responsible for delivering
the technical foundations of Citco's AI capabilities through
reusable patterns, frameworks, and components. The role
requires hands-on technical experience, focusing on building
high-performing applications that enable AI adoption across
Citco. The ideal candidate will bring deep AI engineering
expertise while fostering a culture of innovation and
technical excellence.

Responsibilities

Your Role:

- Build and maintain core AI infrastructure, APIs, and cloud
  integrations
- Implement LLM/GenAI solutions, vector stores, and embedding
  strategies
- Collaborate with cross-functional teams to enable AI
  adoption
- Ensure code quality through testing, CI/CD, and DevOps
  practices

Qualifications

About You:

- 5–8 years of software development experience
- Strong Python, FastAPI, and REST API skills
- Hands-on experience with LLMs, GenAI, or vector databases
- Cloud experience (AWS, Azure, or GCP)
- Experience with React, CI/CD, and Agile practices
- Strong problem-solving and communication skills

Nice to Have:

- AWS Bedrock or Azure OpenAI experience
- Event-driven cloud-native architecture
- AI governance and compliance awareness
