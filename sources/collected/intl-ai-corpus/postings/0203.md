## Description complète

Job Title: AI Engineer

Division: Front Office Technology

Reports To: Quantitative Developer AI

Location: London

Department Overview:

We are part of the Front Office Technology team responsible
for delivering advanced AI and machine learning solutions
that enable the business through LLM-powered applications,
including retrieval-augmented generation (RAG) systems,
agentic process automation, and scalable internal AI tools.
The team collaborates closely with Quant Research, Data
Technology, and Rapid Application Development, forming the
central AI capability that supports both research and
operational efficiency across the firm.

Role Overview:

As BlueCrest continues to integrate AI across its front-
office and research workflows, we are seeking a Junior AI
Engineer to join our central FO Tech Team. This position
sits at the intersection of technology and innovation,
contributing directly to how AI is applied to enhance
trading, research, and operational efficiency across the
firm.

This role offers a rare opportunity to work hands-on with
cutting-edge large language models (LLMs) and emerging AI
architectures while helping to shape the foundations of
enterprise-scale AI adoption. You’ll design and implement
frameworks that make advanced AI capabilities widely
accessible across teams — enabling improved knowledge
retrieval, intelligent process automation, and enhanced
decision support.

You’ll collaborate closely with Front Office Technology,
Quant Research, and business teams to develop robust AI
systems that align with the firm’s strategic innovation
goals. The successful candidate will play a key role in
building, deploying, and maintaining scalable AI
infrastructure — ensuring that the firm can securely
leverage generative and analytical AI to support
quantitative researchers, analysts, and operational users.

In this role, you’ll gain exposure to real-world financial
applications of AI, contribute to the firm’s software
development lifecycle, and help shape the tools and
standards that underpin responsible, high-performance AI
engineering at BlueCrest.

Experience Required:

Essential:

- Degree in Computer Science, Engineering, or related
  technical field, or equivalent industry experience.
- Proficiency in Python and experience with frameworks such as
  OpenAI Agents SDK, LangChain, LlamaIndex, or Hugging Face
  Transformers.
- Understanding of RAG and LLM orchestration techniques.
- Experience working with APIs, vector databases (e.g.,
  Qdrant, Pinecone, FAISS, Weaviate), and embedding models.
- Familiarity with cloud platforms (AWS, Azure) and
  containerized environments.
- Strong grasp of software engineering fundamentals — version
  control, modular design, testing, and documentation.
- Exposure to agentic AI systems or multi-agent orchestration
  frameworks.
- Experience building internal tools, SDKs, or developer
  platforms.
- Experience with MLOps, CI/CD for models, and monitoring
  pipelines.

Desirable:

- Previous experience working in financial markets or
  investment firms.
- Interest in financial technology or data-rich enterprise
  environments.

About You:

In this role you must be self-motivated and able to learn
quickly. This is a highly technical role, you need to be a
curious and motivated technologist with a strong interest in
how AI can transform workflows across complex organisations.
You thrive in collaborative, fast-moving environments and
are eager to learn. You are comfortable with hands-on
engineering, take initiative in problem-solving, and enjoy
the challenge of scaling new technologies responsibly.

BlueCrest is committed to providing an inclusive environment
for its workforce. As an employer, we provide equal
opportunities to all people regardless of their gender,
marital or civil partnership status, race, religion or
ethnicity, disability, age, sexual orientation or
nationality.
