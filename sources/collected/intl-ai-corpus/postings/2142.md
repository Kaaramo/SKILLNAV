## Description complète

Innovation starts from the heart. At Edwards Lifesciences,
we’re dedicated to developing ground-breaking technologies
with a genuine impact on patients’ lives. At the core of
this commitment is our investment in cutting-edge
information technology. This supports our innovation and
collaboration on a global scale, enabling our diverse teams
to optimize both efficiency and success. As part of our IT
team, your expertise and commitment will help facilitate our
patient-focused mission by developing and enhancing
technological solutions.

This role is responsible for understanding business
gaps/opportunities that can be resolved using AI/ML
solutions. Design, develop and test quick AI/ML prototypes
that addresses identified business gaps.

How will you make an impact:

- Provide highly complex design, development and deployment of
  scalable and efficient AI/ML models both for prototyping
  purposes as well as for production deployment.
- Collaborate with cross-functional teams (data scientists,
  software engineers, product managers, business leads) to
  define requirements and ideate solutions using AI/ML
  frameworks.
- Participate in agile development processes, including sprint
  planning, daily stand-ups and demo prototypes or milestone
  updates when necessary
- Drive research and experimentation to explore new AI/ML
  techniques, tools, and frameworks.
- Build end-to-end data pipelines for collecting, processing,
  and analyzing large-scale datasets.
- Ensure code and model development are well-documented and
  follow best engineering practices.
- Improve and optimize the performance of models, ensuring
  robustness, scalability, and efficiency.
- Other incidental duties

What you'll need (Required):

- Bachelor's Degree in Engineering or technical field.
- 4 years experience in Software Development or Engineering
  demonstrably solid performance in a role working with
  cross-functional teams (within IT and/or within the

  implementations Required
- Proficiency in programming languages such as Python, Java,
  C++, or similar.
- Experience with machine learning libraries and frameworks
  (e.g., TensorFlow, PyTorch, Scikit-learn, Keras, etc.).
- Strong understanding of machine learning algorithms,
  statistics, and data structures.
- Experience with cloud platforms such as AWS or Cloud

  storage.
- Familiarity with big data technologies like Hadoop, Spark,

- Solid experience with version control systems (Git) and
  agile development methodologies.
- Experience with deep learning techniques (e.g., CNNs, RNNs,
  GANs, etc.).

  Apache Kafka, Airflow, etc.
