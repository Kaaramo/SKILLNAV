About KX

KX software powers the time-aware data-driven decisions that
enable fast-moving companies to outpace competitors,
realizing the full potential of their AI investments. The KX
platform delivers transformational value by addressing data
challenges related to completeness, timeliness and
efficiency, ensuring companies understand change over time
and can achieve faster, more accurate insights at any scale,
cost-effectively.

KX is essential to the operations of the world's top
investment banks, aerospace and defence, high-tech
manufacturing, healthcare and life sciences, automotive and
fleet telematics organizations. The company has established
offices and a robust customer base across North America,
Europe, and Asia Pacific.

Overview Of The Role

Own the end-to-end technical design for large-language-model
(LLM) and generative-AI solutions across NVIDIA, AWS, Azure
and GCP stacks. You will translate business use-cases into
secure, scalable architectures, lead reference
implementations, and mentor delivery teams for enterprise
deployments—especially in financial-services environments.

- Shape multi-cloud architectures for training, fine-tuning

  Vertex AI).

  CPU clusters (TCO & carbon impact).Build production

  clients.
