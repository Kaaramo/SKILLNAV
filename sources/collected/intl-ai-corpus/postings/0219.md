### Raisonnement classification (LLM upstream)

This is a security engineering role focused on protecting AI
systems, not building them. The candidate will evaluate AI
threats, design security controls, implement guardrails, and
secure AI infrastructure rather than develop, train, or
deploy AI models directly. While they work with AI
technologies like LangChain and vector databases, their
primary function is enabling secure AI usage for others
through security architecture and controls.
