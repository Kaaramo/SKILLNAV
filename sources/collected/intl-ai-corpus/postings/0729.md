## Description complète

Role/Responsibilities:

We are seeking a NLP engineer for a NLP research team within
Point72. The ideal candidate has a track record of
developing modern NLP solutions, including the use of LLMs,
agents, RAG, open-source models fine-tuning and deployment,
etc.

Responsibilities include:

- Exploring and combining rich internal and external textual
  datasets
- Building GenAI solutions, leveraging both internal models
  and external APIs
- Formulating research hypothesis to derive alpha
- Post-training models for specific tasks
- Benchmarking LLM approaches internal tasks

We believe the investment world offers unique opportunities
to use modern NLP methods. We are a team ideally situated
for that, having access to both internal and commercial
datasets, with an end-to-end research philosophy and direct
portfolio impact.

Requirements:

- Bachelor, Master’s or PhD graduate or candidate in computer
  science or another quantitative discipline
- Experience in NLP, particularly LLMs.
- Proficient in Python, SQL and general software engineering
  principles (github, testing, etc.)
- Data science stack familiarity
- Interest in financial markets
- Collaborative mindset
