than directly building, training, or fine-tuning models. The
engineer creates the foundation (pipelines, APIs, indexing,

function, which is classic ai-support work.

At Statista, we’re all about facts and data, for we are the
world's leading business data platform. By providing
reliable and easy-to-use data as well as various data
analytics products and services, we empower people worldwide

Founded in Hamburg in 2007, we have quickly grown into a
global company with offices in major cities such as London,
New York, Berlin and Tokyo. And we still have a lot of

but also keeps creating new development and career
opportunities for our employees.

We value and celebrate our diverse culture. You are welcome

Are you ready to join us?

Your Job

As an Automation Engineer focused on insights and retrieval,
you will design powerful, intelligent data solutions to fuel
Statista’s next-generation analytics and AI products. Take
ownership of scalable pipelines, advanced retrieval systems,
and automated insight delivery—helping transform how data is
connected, indexed, and leveraged across our products and
partner platforms.

- Design Intelligent Data Pipelines: Build and optimize
  scalable, high-performance pipelines that seamlessly
  connect databases, schemas, and advanced retrieval logic
  for both structured and unstructured data.
- Automate Insights Delivery: Develop automation systems for
  rule-based retrieval and dynamic content generation,
  enabling real-time, large-scale insight delivery—from
  simple queries to AI-powered outputs.
- Empower AI Workflows: Ensure data and content are
  efficiently stored, indexed, and available via robust APIs
  and partner integrations—powering next-gen AI features
  like Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG).
- Innovate and Optimize: Analyze and address bottlenecks,
  always seeking opportunities to advance Statista’s data
  automation strategy and improve content workflows for
  greater scalability and performance.

Your Skills

- Data Engineering Expertise: Experience in DataOps, Data
  Engineering, or backend development with a strong record
  of building scalable systems.
- Python & SQL Mastery: Advanced skills in Python and SQL for
  robust data processing, automation, and backend
  integration.
- API Development: Familiarity with FastAPI or similar
  frameworks, able to build efficient, user-friendly APIs
  for data access and automation.
- Cloud Platform Proficiency: Hands-on experience with AWS (or
  other cloud platforms), enabling scalable deployments and
  data solutions.
- Modern DevOps: Strong understanding of Docker, CI/CD
  pipelines, and Infrastructure-as-Code (IaC) for secure,
  reliable, and maintainable infrastructure.
- AI & NLP Skills: Experience with AI/ML libraries or
  frameworks (LangChain, RAG systems) and a genuine
  curiosity about LLMs, model evaluation, and autonomous AI
  agents.
- Continuous Innovation: Eager to stay ahead of fast-moving
  trends in prompt engineering and intelligent data
  retrieval, bringing new ideas and technologies into your
  daily work.
