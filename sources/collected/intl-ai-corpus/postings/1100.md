Job Summary

We’re looking for creative thinkers with hands-on experience
in Agentforce, AI agent development, and intelligent process
automation, who thrive in a collaborative, fast-paced
environment. As a Senior Application Engineer specializing
in Salesforce Agentforce AI at PointClickCare, you’ll
architect and deliver state-of-the-art AI agents and
automations that transform system workflows. You’ll be
empowered to experiment, prototype, and deliver solutions
that directly shape the future of our technology landscape,
gain exposure to the latest AI technologies and dedicated
time for professional development and certifications as you
create AI Agents using Agentforce and other AI tools.

To succeed in this role, you need to be an autonomous and
accountable team player with a continuous learning mindset
and understand what it means to be a member of a high
performing team. You bring hands-on experience building
Agentforce AI Agents and over 5 years of experience and
knowledge designing, developing, and maintaining
configuration and custom applications that integrate with
Salesforce or other connected apps.

As a Senior Application Engineer (Salesforce Agentforce AI)
at PointClickCare you will also build intelligent process
automations, test automations, and debug standard to complex
issues related with Apex code, JavaScript, Python or code
from other programming languages as it relates to the
applications you are developing for. You will develop
technical documentation, be part of our DevOps maintenance
crew and peer review builds developed by other team members
and vendors. You will provide training and consultation
related to the systems and processes developed by the team
to our business partners and support both transformational
projects, high priority enhancements and resolve standard to
high complexity incidents to quickly deliver value to the
business.
