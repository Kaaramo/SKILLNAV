## Description complète

About HouseAccount

At HouseAccount, we’re building the modern home services
platform, starting with the handyman. We connect homeowners
to trusted local providers and make it simple to book,
manage, and pay for everything their home needs in one
place. By combining vetted pros, transparent pricing, and
AI-powered automation, we’re turning home maintenance into
something homeowners don’t have to think about.

What You’ll Do

You’ll lead the effort to make HouseAccount truly AI-first,
designing, prototyping, and deploying intelligent systems
that power how we match, message, and manage home services.
You’ll work closely with our product and growth teams to
identify repetitive workflows and convert them into smart,
automated agents that scale what we do every day.

A typical day might include:

- Building and fine-tuning workflows with OpenAI APIs,
  Anthropic Claude, or Mistral models
- Using frameworks like LangChain, LlamaIndex, or Vercel AI
  SDK to connect multiple models and data sources
- Designing retrieval and memory systems that help agents
  reason across internal data (CRM, outreach logs, provider
  data)
- Prototyping automations using Zapier, n8n, or custom
  Python/Ruby scripts before productizing them
- Collaborating with engineering to connect model outputs back
  to our Rails app via clean APIs
- Experimenting with prompt architectures, tool use, and
  context management to improve performance and accuracy

You’ll work across the stack, but your focus will be
building and scaling agentic systems that make our
operations and product smarter.

What Matters to Us

- You’ve built real things with OpenAI or similar APIs -
  agents, copilots, automations, or internal tools
- You understand how to translate messy real-world processes
  into structured logic for AI systems
- You’re comfortable working across data pipelines, APIs, and
  backend logic to get something working end-to-end
- You thrive in fast, iterative environments and care more
  about getting something live than perfecting it in a doc
- You’re deeply curious about how AI can simplify human work
  and scale impact

Who You Are

- 3 to 7 years of software or AI engineering experience,
  ideally in a startup or applied AI setting
- Proficient in Python (FastAPI, Flask, or Django) and
  comfortable building integrations in JavaScript or
  TypeScript
- Familiar with LangChain, LlamaIndex, or similar frameworks
  for orchestration
- Experienced with vector databases like pgvector, Pinecone,
  or Weaviate
- Familiar with workflow automation tools such as Zapier or
  n8n
- Bonus: you’ve been a founding or early engineer and thrive
  in the 0 to 1 stage

Why This Role Matters

You’ll be one of the first engineers shaping how AI is built
into the DNA of our product and operations. This role sits
at the intersection of engineering, product, and applied
research - bringing intelligent systems from idea to
production and defining how we scale smarter.
