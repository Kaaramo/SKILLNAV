## Description complète

Nansen is a leading blockchain analytics platform that
empowers investors and professionals with real-time,
actionable insights derived from on-chain data.

The Opportunity:

Nansen is leveling up its strategic capabilities in
Artificial Intelligence (AI) and Machine Learning (ML). We
are actively seeking a highly skilled and motivated Senior
AI/ML Engineer to join our team. In this role, you will
develop and monitor AI models to analyze vast amounts of
blockchain data, identifying patterns and insights that
drive decision-making for crypto investors. Your
contributions will be key to advancing Nansen's mission of
surfacing the signal and creating winners in the future of
finance.

As a Senior AI/ML Engineer at Nansen, you will play a
crucial part in enhancing our platform's capabilities,
enabling us to offer a cutting-edge product to our users.
Your expertise will not only influence the direction of our
technology but also solidify Nansen's position as a leader
in blockchain analytics.

Key Responsibilities:

- Lead the design, implementation, and use of AI/ML models to
  process and analyze blockchain data, ensuring high
  performance and scalability.
- Collaborate with product managers and engineers to integrate
  AI/ML models into Nansen's platform, enhancing its
  analytical capabilities and improving user experience.
- Stay abreast of the latest developments in AI/ML and
  blockchain technologies, applying innovative approaches to
  solve complex challenges.
- Mentor junior engineers and promote best practices in AI/ML
  development across the team.
- Work closely with cross-functional teams to align AI/ML
  initiatives with business objectives and customer needs.

Requirements:

- Proven experience as an AI/ML Engineer or similar role, with
  a strong portfolio of successful AI/ML projects.
- Expertise in machine learning frameworks (e.g., TensorFlow,
  PyTorch) and programming languages such as Python.
- Familiarity with modern AI models, APIs, and SDKs (e.g.
  OpenAI, LLMs)
- An ability to work full-stack with data; some data
  engineering experience is a big plus
- Strong analytical skills and the ability to work with large,
  complex data sets.
- Excellent communication skills, with the ability to
  collaborate effectively in a remote work environment
  across multiple time zones.
- A passion for blockchain, crypto, and Web3 technologies.
- Have an AI-first mindset.  At Nansen, AI is more than a tool
  - it's a mindset.  An ideal candidate is one who
  enthusiastically embraces AI tools and techniques to
  streamline processes and elevate outcomes.

What We Offer:

- Competitive salary and generous equity.
- Remote work environment with a flexible schedule.
- A team that values data and AI; our founders are data
  engineers and data scientists
- The opportunity to work from our Tech Hubs (flight &
  accommodation paid for) in Singapore, Bangkok, London &
  Oslo.
- A company culture that values transparency, speed, courage,
  and curiosity.
- Opportunities for personal and professional growth as the
  company scales.
- Exposure to a global network of industry experts, partners,
  and influencers.
