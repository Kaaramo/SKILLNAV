## Description complète

About LiveFlow

LiveFlow is building the next-generation accounting and
finance platform - enabling lean finance teams to run
massive enterprises.

We’ve raised over $21M from top-tier investors including YC,
YC Continuity, Valar, Seedcamp, WndrCo, Moonfire, and
Bradley Horowitz (VP Product, Google).

Today, thousands of companies rely on LiveFlow to streamline
their financial workflows, with hundreds of 5/5 ratings on
G2.

Our founding team previously worked at Revolut, one of
Europe’s fastest-growing fintechs.

Why Join Us

You’ll help shape not just the product - but how we build
it.

We’re a team that values ownership, speed, and thoughtful
iteration. We’re looking for people who ask great questions,
challenge assumptions, and care deeply about building
something customers genuinely love.

You’ll work closely with product, design, and customers,
operating with a high degree of autonomy - while
contributing to a culture that’s collaborative, ambitious,
and constantly evolving.

The Role

We’re hiring Software Engineers to build AI agents that
power the next generation of LiveFlow.

This is a full-stack role at its core - but with a strong
focus on designing and developing agent-driven systems that
are embedded directly into the product.

You’ll work on AI-powered features, thinking deeply about
how agents behave, interact with financial data, and deliver
reliable, high-impact outcomes for users.

This isn’t about bolting AI onto existing workflows - it’s
about reimagining how financial software operates with AI at
its core.

What You’ll Work On

- Building AI agents that are deeply integrated into the
  product
- Designing systems that combine LLMs, workflows, and
  structured financial data
- Developing a real-time, collaborative financial workspace
- Designing and scaling APIs and backend systems
- Integrating with multiple financial data providers
- Crafting simple, intuitive abstractions for complex
  financial workflows
- Ensuring high standards of data security, reliability, and
  compliance

How We Use AI

In Engineering:

- We experiment aggressively with tools like Cursor, Claude,
  and PR-based AI agents
- We’re investing in Claude skills, sub-agents, and custom
  command workflows
- Engineers are encouraged to bring their own tools, test new
  approaches, and share what works
- We optimize for speed, leverage, and iteration, not rigid
  processes

In the Product:

- We’re building AI features that are deeply embedded into
  financial workflows
- Focus is on accuracy, usefulness, and real customer impact
- We care about outcomes over hype - everything we ship needs
  to work in production

Our Stack

- Backend: Elixir / Phoenix
- Frontend: Next.js, TypeScript
- Infrastructure: AWS, Terraform

We don’t expect experience in everything.

If you’ve worked with functional languages, frameworks like
Rails/Django, or modern frontend stacks, you’ll ramp
quickly. What matters most is your ability to learn, adapt,
and build.

What We’re Looking For

- Strong software engineering fundamentals (frontend, backend,
  or full-stack)
- Experience building or experimenting with AI agents, LLM-
  powered systems, or workflow automation (or strong
  interest in doing so)
- Product mindset - you care about users and outcomes, not
  just implementation
- Ability to operate in fast-paced, ambiguous environments
- High ownership and willingness to wear multiple hats
- Strong intuition for when to follow best practices - and
  when to rethink them

Perks & Benefits

- Competitive salary + equity
- Health insurance
- Pension plan
- Latest tech (MacBook, monitors, etc.)
- Learning budget (books, courses, conferences)
- Unlimited vacation

Interview Process

- Intro call
- Technical assessment
- Technical interview
- Career history interview
- Founder interview
