The role

Design, develop, and implement production-grade AI models,
agents, and algorithms using Agentic AI frameworks

Establish LLMOps/MLOps practices (CI/CD, versioning,

Ops observability (latency, accuracy, cost, drift/bias).
Troubleshoot complex production issues across models,
agents, tooling, and integrations; drive root‑cause analysis

What you’ll bring

Requirements (Must‑Have)

Bachelor’s or master’s in computer science, Artificial
Intelligence, Machine Learning, or related field. 7+ years
