Are you ready to make your mark in the world of Formula 1?

At Formula 1, we are passionate about delivering the world’s

Everything you see on screen or at an event comes via our

Role Overview:

We are looking for an AI, ML, Data Science Engineer to join
our Innovation & Technology team. Reporting into our Chief
Engineering & Innovation Officer, this role will design,
build, and deploy intelligent data systems and AI/ML
solutions.

Main Duties and Responsibilities:

AI & Machine Learning Development

- Design, develop, and deploy machine learning models for:
- Predictive analytics (performance trends, operational
  forecasting)
- Computer vision (video analysis, racing systems, media
  enhancement)
- Natural language processing (fan insights, content
  intelligence, knowledge systems)
- Anomaly detection (trackside operational reliability, racing
  systems telemetry monitoring)
- Build scalable inference pipelines for real-time and batch
  processing.
- Implement model monitoring, drift detection, and retraining
  workflows.
- Rapidly prototype and validate ideas to demonstrate value
  before scaling solutions into production.
- Balance short term delivery with longer term platform
  thinking, prioritising speed to value and learning.
- Operate comfortably with incomplete requirements, shaping
  problems collaboratively with business stakeholders.
- Continuously research emerging data, AI, and systems
  technologies across adjacent and non adjacent industries
  (e.g., aerospace, defence, healthcare, manufacturing,
  finance).
- Translate external best practices and proven solutions into
  the unique constraints and opportunities of Formula One.

Data Engineering & Architecture

- Design and maintain robust data pipelines integrating:
- Telemetry and timing systems
- Video and image data
- Event operations data
- Commercial and digital engagement data
- Develop APIs and services to expose analytics capabilities
  to internal stakeholders.
- Ensure high availability and low latency for race-critical
  systems where required

Cross-Functional Collaboration

- Proactively identify high impact data and AI opportunities
  by engaging directly with teams across the business:
- Broadcast & Media
- Commercial & Marketing
- Cybersecurity and IT Infrastructure
- Motorsport Operations
- Trackside Operations
- Translate business and sporting requirements into technical
  solutions.
- Present findings to both technical and executive audiences.
- Build trusted relationships across technical and non
  technical teams to become a go to partner for data  and AI
  enabled problem solving.

Governance, Compliance & Security

- Implement responsible AI practices.
- Support data privacy, governance, and regulatory compliance.
- Collaborate with cybersecurity teams to secure data
  pipelines and AI systems.
