## Description complète

AI Demo Engineer

Location:  Remote, United States

Employment Type: Full-Time

Compensation: $115,000.00 - $132,500.00 (Range applies to US
candidates only) + Benefits/Variable Comp/Equity - Range may
vary based on experience. 

Benefits Offered: Vision, Medical, Life, Dental, 401K

Summary

As an AI Demo Engineer at OneStream, you’ll build and
maintain demo applications and content that showcase our AI
capabilities  and the broader OneStream platform. You’ll
partner with Sales, Presales, and Platform teams to turn
real customer use cases into  clear, hands-on demos that
highlight business value. Day to day, you’ll prepare sample
data, script repeatable demo flows, and  incorporate new
product features as they launch. You’ll gather feedback from
field teams and customers to refine assets, and  contribute
lightweight documentation and runbooks so others can reuse
your work.

Primary Duties and Responsibilities

- Create and continuously enhance OneStream’s AI-focused demo
  assets to enable shorter, more successful sales cycles and
  increased deal sizes.
- Stay current on new OneStream features and incorporate them
  into demos.
- Translate complex business challenges into clear, compelling
  demo content and technical narratives.
- Build and manage data pipelines using C#, SQL, and Python
  programming languages.
- Collaborate with Sales and Presales to tailor demos for
  partners, prospects, and customers.
- Contribute documentation, runbooks, and reusable sample
  content for the field.

Required Education and Experience

- Bachelor's degree in computer science, data science, or
  relevant combination of education and experience.
- 0-1 years of industry experience in business intelligence,
  applied machine learning and analytics.
- 2-5 years of experience in Application and/or Database
  Management including being a lead in innovative demo
  design strategies, and deploying applications.
- Familiarity with corporate, enterprise, or financial
  planning, consolidation, and management systems
  (OneStream, Hyperion Financial Management, SAP, etc.).
- Proficiency in:
  - SQL.
  - Data Visualization via OneStream, Microsoft Excel, Power BI,
    Tableau, Qlik Sense, etc.
  - Basic exposure to C# and/or Python.
  - OneStream Knowledge.

Preferred Education and Experience

- 1 year of OneStream implementation consulting experience,
  with a preference in leading software implementations.

Knowledge, Skills, and Abilities

- Ability to clearly translate complex concepts and apply them
  to modern business.
- Ability to clearly define and logically resolve complex
  problems.
- Strong organization skills to lead and deliver complex
  projects on schedule with advising support.
- Ability to multi-task and prioritize ongoing activities
  based on importance.
- Quick learner who can understand and self-teach new
  technologies.
