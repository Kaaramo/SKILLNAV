## Description complète

Our vision is to transform how the world uses information to
enrich life for all.

Micron Technology is a world leader in innovating memory and
storage solutions that accelerate the transformation of
information into intelligence, inspiring the world to learn,
communicate and advance faster than ever.

Key Responsibilities

- Architect, design, and implement AI/ML-driven workflows for
  DRAM/NAND memory subsystems and controllers.
- Improve design and layout efficiency through AI-based
  automation, leveraging Python and LLMs (e.g., Claude,
  Gemini).
- Perform block- and chip-level simulations, debugging, and
  functional verification.
- Collaborate closely with design and CAD teams to develop and
  customize tools that accelerate design qualification and
  turnaround time.
- Apply industry-standard EDA tools such as Cadence Verisium,
  Virtuoso Layout Suite, Siemens Solido (Variation-Aware
  Design), and other leading tools for synthesis,
  simulation, parasitic estimation, and layout-aware
  optimization.
- Develop standard cell layout automation using Python and
  SKILL programming.

Required Skills – Data Science & AI

- 4–8 years of proven experience applying AI/ML techniques in
  the semiconductor design domain (analog, digital, layout,
  and/or verification).
- Strong foundation in Mathematics of Optimization and core
  machine learning algorithms, including neural networks.
- Hands-on experience implementing reinforcement learning (RL)
  or related optimization techniques for design or EDA
  problems.
- Practical experience building LLM-based solutions,
  including:
  - Fine-tuning
  - Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) pipelines
  - Pre-training or adapting LLMs for domain-specific use cases

Required Skills – Silicon Design

- Strong understanding of CMOS fundamentals, device physics,
  timing, power, and memory architectures.
- Experience with simulation and verification environments
  such as Virtuoso, Xcelium, ModelSim, Solido, or equivalent
  EDA platforms.
- Working knowledge of SystemVerilog and UVM methodology.
- Familiarity with Cadence and Synopsys toolchains is highly
  desirable.
