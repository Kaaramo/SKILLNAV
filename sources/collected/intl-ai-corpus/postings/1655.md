Job Title: Principal Engineer – Agentic AI Insurance Core

Location: India (Remote / Location-Agnostic) Travel: ~1 week
per month to Mumbai HQ Reporting to: CTIO or Enterprise
Architect

1. Mission: Achieve Total Technology Sovereignty

Lead the engineering of a fully proprietary, Agentic AI-
powered insurance core that eliminates dependency on
third‑party SaaS systems. You will drive a multi-phase
“SaaS‑replacement roadmap” built on Vertex AI, Gemini 1.5
Pro, BigQuery ML, GKE, and Apigee.

Phase 1 — AI Product & Pricing Engine (Current Priority)

- Build the Agentic Product Engine to configure complex health
  insurance products and dynamic pricing logic.
- Replace rigid SaaS rating engines by architecting an Agentic
  Quotation System capable of multi-variant, real‑time
  quoting.

Phase 2 — Agentic Sales & Proposal Stack

- Develop the Agentic Proposal System for automated data
  capture, form understanding, and intent
  recognition—replacing traditional CRM modules.
- Engineer the Agentic Underwriting Engine using LLMs and
  Document AI to automate medical and financial scrutiny,
  replacing SaaS underwriting workbenches.

Phase 3 — Financial Core (Policy, Accounting, Compliance)

- Build the Agentic Policy Issuance & Premium Accounting Core
  for real-time ledgering, GST compliance, receivables, and
  policy document generation.
- Deliver native integrations with:
  - National Health Claims Exchange (NHCX)
  - Bima Sugam—without relying on middleware vendors.

2. Technical Requirements (GCP‑First, AI‑Native)

Agentic Orchestration (Core Skill)

- Expert-level capability in Vertex AI Agent Builder,
  LangGraph, or CrewAI.
- Ability to design multi-agent pipelines across pricing →
  underwriting → issuance with safe, deterministic
  execution.

Generative AI Expertise

- Mastery of RAG (Retrieval-Augmented Generation) pipelines.
- Experience fine-tuning Gemini models for:
  - Clinical document parsing
  - Regulatory adherence (IRDAI)
  - Complex reasoning in medical & financial decisioning

“SaaS‑Killer Stack” (Backend + Platform)

- Deep experience with:
  - GKE (high-scale microservices)
  - BigQuery ML (risk scoring, FWA models)
  - Apigee for hospital network APIs via HL7 FHIR
- Ability to design systems where AI agents safely trigger
  financial operations (SPDI-sensitive).

Infrastructure & Data Standards

- Experience with secure architectures handling PHI/SPDI.
- Comfort building for high auditability and traceability.

3. Domain & Regulatory Expertise

- Understanding of the Indian Health Insurance ecosystem and
  ABDM protocols.
- Familiarity with medical coding frameworks: ICD‑10 / ICD‑11.
- Experience migrating off SaaS platforms such as Salesforce,
  Duck Creek, Guidewire, or equivalent.

4. Why This Role?

Full IP Ownership

Every system you build directly replaces a SaaS
license—increasing valuation, autonomy, and long-term
defensibility.

Cloud-Agnostic Talent, GCP-Native Execution

Flexible work location, but leveraging the full capabilities
of Google Cloud’s AI-first ecosystem.

Strategic, High-Impact Engineering

You will design and own the “Nervous System” of the company
while working closely with the CTIO, architecture, and
business leadership.

Candidate Profile

The ideal candidate is a Builder who views SaaS as technical
debt and believes in first‑principles engineering.

You should have:

- 8+ years of high-scale backend engineering experience.
- 2+ years building or deploying production-grade AI agents.
- The mindset and discipline to operate in a Day‑1, high-
  intensity, IP‑first environment.
- A bias toward creating proprietary systems over stitching
  SaaS components.
