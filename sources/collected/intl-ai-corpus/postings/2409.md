## Description complète

Join us as an AI Engineer (Image & Video Generation) and
become part of a team building and scaling generative
pipelines for next-generation visual content at Zeely.

Zeely AI is a platform that uses artificial intelligence to
generate viral UGC videos and ad banners, as well as launch
effective ad campaigns on Facebook and Instagram, helping
small businesses become visible and scale.

We already have 1mln+ users in the US and are consistently
growing more than 4x year-over-year. Today, Zeely is ranked
among the Top-3 products in its category on the US market,
and our team has grown to over 160 specialists.

Your key responsibilities:

- Design and improve generative pipelines for image and video
  creation
- Tune and control the behavior of image/video models: visual
  quality, style consistency, and product identity
  preservation
- Optimize and scale image→video and text→video workflows for
  stable and reproducible production results
- Diagnose quality bottlenecks (artifacts, instability,
  product distortions) and implement fixes
- Extend generative systems for new products and marketing
  content scenarios
- Research and test new image/video models and tools, staying
  up to date with the latest industry solutions

What we’re looking for:

- 2+ years of hands-on experience in AI / Generative Systems
  Engineering, where prompts are part of the engineering
  logic rather than a standalone artifact
- Strong analytical and formal reasoning skills: ability to
  build cause-and-effect relationships when diagnosing and
  optimizing generation systems
- Ability to independently evaluate visual quality of
  generated outputs: composition, lighting, consistency, and
  product alignment—without external designer validation.
  Understanding of UGC/ad content and product-focused
  generation
- Experience with generative models across multiple
  modalities: text-to-image, image-to-image, image-to-video,
  video-to-video, multimodal scenarios
- Experience designing LLM agents or automated generative
  workflows
- Ability to build complex multi-step workflows, and
  optimize/debug generation pipelines
- Strong prompt engineering skills for image and video models
- Habit of documenting pipelines, experiments, and decisions:
  structured notes on models, prompts, parameters, and test
  results to ensure reproducibility and shared team
  understanding
- Keeping up with news and releases in image/video generation,
  understanding the current tooling landscape

Nice to have:

- Experience with ComfyUI
- Experience with LoRA tuning or fine-tuning image/video
  models
- Experience integrating generative pipelines into production
  systems (APIs, queues, storage, Docker, Kubernetes, etc.)
- Experience building automated workflows in n8n or similar
  tools
- Practical experience with Python AI/ML libraries
- Experience working with media processing in Python: ffmpeg-
  python, moviepy, imageio, pydub, and similar tools

Why it’s exciting to work with us:

- Work with cutting-edge technologies and contribute to
  building AI-powered tools
- Join a strong A-level team that motivates you to grow and
  achieve more
- Remote-first approach: work from anywhere, with the option
  to visit our offices in Warsaw and Kyiv
- Paid day offs and sick leaves — we care about your work-life
  balance
- Regular performance reviews to ensure your growth is
  transparent and rewarded
- Financial support — consultations for private entrepreneurs
  from our dedicated finance team
