## Description complète

We are seeking an experienced Senior Engineer to lead the
design, development, and implementation of Agentic AI
automation solutions and advanced Model Context Protocol
(MCP) integrations. This role is pivotal in shaping our
next-generation intelligent automation systems, enabling
autonomous decision-making, dynamic task execution, and
seamless, secure communication between AI models and diverse
enterprise data sources and tools.

Responsibilities

As a Senior Engineer, you will be driving the strategic
adoption and technical execution of Agentic AI and Model
Context Protocol. You will architect, develop, and deploy
sophisticated autonomous AI agents that can reason, plan,
execute multi-step tasks, and adapt to dynamic environments
with minimal human intervention. This involves building
robust, scalable, and secure agentic systems that move
beyond traditional automation to truly intelligent, goal-
driven behaviors.



You will lead efforts to integrate large language models
(LLMs) with enterprise APIs, databases, and internal
services, enabling them to perform actions, retrieve
information, and maintain context effectively.



This position requires a blend of hands-on technical
leadership, strategic thinking, and a commitment to best
practices in AI engineering, including governance, security,
and ethical considerations. You will mentor junior
engineers, drive technical discussions, and collaborate
cross-functionally with product managers and other
engineering teams to translate business challenges into
robust, production-ready AI solutions.



Qualifications

Bachelor’s degree in Computer Science, Data Science,
Statistics, Physics, Mathematics or Engineering degree or a
related field of study

5+ years of professional experience in software engineering,
Agentic AI, Gen AI, ML, or a data-focused role.

3+ years of hands-on experience developing and deploying
machine learning solutions using languages like Python,
PySpark, or Scala.

Proficiency with SQL and experience engineering data from
diverse, high-volume sources.

Even better, you may have...

MS or PhD in Computer Science, Electrical Engineering or a
related field

Demonstrated success applying Agentic AI to solve real-world
industrial problems with large-scale data.

Proficiency with version control (Git) and CI/CD practices
within an agile development environment.

Excellent programming skills, particularly interface
development to generate data visualizations and to extract
insights from large complex data sets.

Strong interpersonal and leadership skills, with proven
abilities to communicate complex topics to leaders and peers
in a simple, clear, plan- oriented manner

A self-starter mentality, with the ability to navigate
ambiguity and drive projects forward with minimal direction
in a fast-paced environment.

Ability to work in an environment where problems are not
always well-defined.
