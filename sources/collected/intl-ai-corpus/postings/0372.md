## Description complète

ABOUT THE TEAM

The AI Innovation Team at Mural is pioneering how generative
AI transforms visual collaboration and decision-making.
We’re a remote-first group of engineers, designers, and
product thinkers focused on helping teams work together more
effectively. Our goal isn’t to replace human creativity.
It’s to amplify it, building AI that enhances how people
align, communicate, and make decisions visually.

YOUR MISSION

You’ll bring AI-powered features to life for Mural’s
customers—building intuitive, high-impact capabilities that
make collaboration feel more fluid, visual, and human.
You’ll work across product and design to identify where AI
can provide real value and then deliver those experiences
end-to-end. You understand the trade-offs of rapid iteration
and production reliability, and you know how to ship safely,
quickly, and meaningfully.

WHAT YOU'LL DO:

- Build and refine early-stage AI systems and frameworks that
  enhance user experiences and deliver measurable
  improvements in performance and relevance.
- Help direction for AI innovation at Mural by working closely
  with cross-functional teams to define priorities and long-
  term strategy.
- Translate complex, open-ended problems into structured
  engineering approaches grounded in data analysis,
  experimentation, and machine learning principles.
- Champion technical excellence and mentorship, fostering best
  practices in AI development and nurturing a culture of
  inclusivity, curiosity, and collaboration.
- Shape the future of the team through thoughtful hiring,
  process improvements, and initiatives that attract and
  develop exceptional AI talent.

WHAT YOU'LL BRING

- 5+ years of experience of Software Engineering, with 2+ of
  developing generative AI products
- Comfort in prototyping and pathfinding new opportunities
  that led to successful 0→1 projects
- Proven experience implementing the full lifecycle of an
  early-phase AI development, from ideating on which models
  to use through productionizing and maintaining them
- Excellent written and verbal communication skills, including
  the ability to produce clear and concise technical
  documents
- Experience in developing deep product and business knowledge
  to connect abstract modeling and analysis tasks with
  business value

NICE TO HAVE

- A relevant advanced degree (Masters or PhD) in Machine
  Learning, Artificial Intelligence, Statistics, or a
  related field
- Proven track record of planning a multi-year roadmap in
  which shorter-term projects ladder up to the long-term
  vision
- Demonstrated expertise in AI indexing, retrieval systems,
  personalization, and/or natural language processing
- A growth mindset and experience in mentoring/aligning senior
  engineers

OUR STACK

Azure OpenAI, React, Node, MongoDB
