## Description complète

Job Description:

Ready to build the future of marketing with AI? We're
looking for an AI Marketing Engineer who's passionate about
automation, enjoys working with APIs, and gets excited about
turning innovative ideas into practical solutions. You'll be
instrumental in making AI truly valuable for our teams -
developing tools that streamline workflows, automate routine
tasks, and unlock new creative possibilities.

This role offers the opportunity to work on cutting-edge AI
projects that directly impact our business operations.
You'll collaborate with teams across operations, strategy,
and technology to implement practical AI solutions. While
not every project will succeed, those that do will become
essential tools that transform our workflows.

You'll report to our Software Development Director and work
within a supportive environment that values innovation and
practical problem-solving.

What You'll Do

- Build GenAI apps (briefing assistants, classifiers, creative
  generation tools) from prototype to production.
- Design and optimize prompts, fine-tune models when needed,
  and ensure reliable outputs.
- Create automation pipelines with tools like n8n, Make,
  Zapier, and Copilot Studio.
- Integrate AI models with our martech stack through APIs,
  webhooks, and custom scripts.
- Monitor outputs, implement guardrails for brand safety, and
  ensure compliance.
- Debug, document, and continuously improve solutions.

Build Innovative Solutions

You'll create GenAI applications that solve real business
challenges, including intelligent briefing assistants,
content classifiers, and creative generation tools. Your
work involves engineering prompts that actually deliver
results, fine-tuning models when needed, and deliver
reliable outputs. You'll turn proof-of-concepts into
production-ready solutions that teams can rely on.

Automation & Integration Master workflow tools like n8n,
Make, Zapier, and Copilot Studio to connect systems that
were never meant to talk to each other. You'll build
automation pipelines that make repetitive tasks disappear,
create seamless integrations between AI models and our
existing martech stack, and prototype rapidly while
iterating based on user feedback.

Deliver Quality Products

Monitor AI outputs and implement guardrails for brand safety
while ensuring models stay on-brand and compliant with data
regulations. When things break (because they will), you'll
debug and optimize for performance, documenting your work so
others can build on your innovations.

What We're Looking For

Practical skills matter more than degrees. Curiosity and
learning ability outweigh credentials.

- Strong Python coding skills and proven experience delivering
  functional tools.
- Hands-on with OpenAI/Anthropic APIs, open-source LLMs, and
  workflow automation platforms.
- Solid understanding of LLM capabilities/limitations and
  integration best practices.
- Comfortable with APIs, data integration, and version
  control.
- Problem-solver with self-directed learning ability and clear
  communication skills.
- 3 - 5 years working in a relevant technical environment

Nice to Have:

- Portfolio, open-source contributions, or personal
  AI/automation projects.
- Understanding of marketing/advertising workflows.
- Active in AI communities, curious about emerging tech, and
  eager to improve processes.

Why You'll Love It Here

- Meaningful Impact

Your tools will be used by hundreds of specialists and
strategists daily, giving you a direct impact on how one of
the world's largest agency networks operates. You'll have
access to dentsu's global AI initiatives and cutting-edge
projects, with opportunities to experiment on a daily basis
with the latest tools and models.

- Work Environment

Work with people who actually understand what you're
building in a hybrid setup where you come to our Amsterdam
office when it makes sense and work from home when it
doesn't.

What We Offer

- Competitive salary that reflects your technical expertise;
- 25 vacation days + 3 wellbeing days + 2 volunteer days;
- Regular hackathons and innovation time;
- Modern tech stack and development tools.

Apply

If you're someone who has strong convictions about effective
prompt engineering and AI implementation, gets excited about
new model releases and their potential applications, and
wants to work at a company that takes AI seriously and
implements it thoughtfully, we'd like to hear from you.

Please submit:

- Your CV highlighting relevant technical experience;
- Portfolio or GitHub showcasing AI/automation projects you've
  built;
- Brief description of the most interesting AI tool or
  automation you've created;
- Your thoughts on where most companies go wrong with AI
  implementation.
