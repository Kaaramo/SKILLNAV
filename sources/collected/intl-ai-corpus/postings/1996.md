## Description complète

What you can expect

As a Zoom AI DevOps Engineer, you will design and manage AWS
infrastructure supporting AI services. Using Terraform,
you’ll implement Infrastructure as Code and streamline
automation. Your work includes monitoring systems, managing
on-call rotations, and ensuring high reliability. You’ll
collaborate with cross-functional teams to boost performance
and guide engineers on automation best practices. Please
note this role includes on-call responsibilities and
occasional weekend deployments as needed.

About the Team

The team drives automation, CI/CD, observability, and cloud-
native operations to power Zoom’s AI services. We handle
service deployment and operations across multi-cloud and on-
premises environments. You’ll work in a fast-paced,
collaborative setting with Developers, Infrastructure
Engineers, and Product teams to build and maintain
resilient, high-performing systems.

What we’re looking for

- Have 5+ years of work experience as a Cloud or DevOps
  engineer
- Have 5+ hands-on experience working on Production service
  deployments using Kubernetes
- Have experience with AWS services and Kubernetes-based
  production deployments. Proficiency in Infrastructure as
  Code (Terraform) and configuration management tools
  (Ansible).
- Demonstrate experience in Kubernetes, Docker workload
  deployment, and monitoring for it for large-scale
  distributed systems deployed on prod
- Show high proficiency in programming/scripting languages
  like Python, Bash, or Go
