## Description complète

Job Purpose

We are seeking a highly motivated and skilled AI Engineer to
join our team and drive innovation in the fast-evolving
landscape of Generative AI. The ideal candidate will have
deep hands-on expertise in RAG (Retrieval-Augmented
Generation) pipelines and working familiarity with Frontier
Models (e.g., GPT-4, Claude, LLaMA, etc.). Exposure to
cutting-edge orchestration frameworks like CrewAI,
LangGraph, Autogen, or OpenAI SDK is highly desirable.

You will work closely with product, data, and engineering
teams to prototype, build, and scale AI-first solutions that
deliver tangible business impact. Strong communication
skills are key, as you’ll be working with cross-functional
teams and occasionally interfacing with clients.

Responsibilities

- Design and implement scalable RAG pipelines to enable
  contextual, high-quality responses using enterprise
  knowledge bases.
- Evaluate and integrate Frontier LLMs /Open source LLM and
  APIs for specific tasks, ensuring optimal performance and
  efficiency.
- Build autonomous agent workflows using tools like CrewAI,
  LangGraph, Autogen, or OpenAI SDK.
- Develop and optimize prompts, chains, and multi-agent
  systems.
- Stay updated on the latest in the GenAI ecosystem and make
  recommendations for internal adoption.
- Collaborate with stakeholders to understand use cases,
  define solution architectures, and iterate prototypes into
  production-ready systems.
- Ensure responsible AI practices, including data privacy,
  model explainability, andcompliance.

Required Skills & Experience

- 6 to 7 years of overall industry experience with at least
  1–2 years in Generative AI /LLM-focused projects.
- Strong understanding of Frontier LLMs and their application
  across domains.
- Hands-on experience in IDE’s such as Cursor
- Hands-on experience building Retrieval-Augmented Generation
  systems using vector databases (like FAISS, Weaviate, or
  Pinecone).
- Working knowledge of CrewAI, LangGraph, Autogen, or similar
  agent orchestration frameworks.
- Proficiency in Python, with experience using libraries like
  LangChain, Transformers, and OpenAI SDKs.
- Familiarity with API integrations, asynchronous task
  handling, and cloud-nativedeployment (Azure).
- Excellent problem-solving and analytical skills.
- Strong written and verbal communication skills.

Preferred Qualifications

- Exposure to US Healthcare systems, data formats (FHIR, HL7),
  or domain-specific use cases is a plus.
- Experience in fine-tuning or evaluating LLMs using domain-
  specific corpora.
- Understanding of security and compliance requirements in
  regulated industries.

What We Offer • Opportunity to work on cutting-edge AI
projects with meaningful real-world applications. •
Collaborative and inclusive work culture. • Flexible work
environment and competitive compensation.

Join us in shaping the future of AI-powered applications. If
you are passionate about solving complex problems with
elegant GenAI-driven solutions, we’d love to hear from you.

Required Education

Bachelor’s degree in Computer Science, Electrical and
Computer Engineering, Software Engineering, or Equivalent.
An MS or PhD in such technical fields is very desirable

Compensation & Benefits

Welldoc offers a competitive compensation package, including
generous PTO, pre-IPO stock options, and medical insurance
