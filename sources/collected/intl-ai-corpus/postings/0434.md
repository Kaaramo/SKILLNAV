## Description complète

At OneStudyTeam (a Reify Health company), we specialize in
speeding up clinical trials and increasing the chance of new
therapies being approved with the ultimate goal of improving
patient outcomes. Our cloud-based platform, StudyTeam,
brings research site workflows online and enables sites,
sponsors, and other key stakeholders to work together more
effectively. StudyTeam is trusted by the largest global
biopharmaceutical companies, used in over 6,000 research
sites, and is available in over 100 countries. Join us in
our mission to advance clinical research and improve patient
care.

One mission. One team. That’s OneStudyTeam.

As a Principal Engineer, AI Systems, you will serve as the
organization's senior expert in the implementation,
integration, and operational enablement of Artificial
Intelligence systems. You will guide the deployment and
support of OpenAI ChatGPT, Anthropic Claude, internally
developed AI-enabled solutions, and other large language
model (LLM) technologies across the enterprise.

This role blends strategy and execution. You will work
cross-functionally with engineering, operations, security,
and business teams to identify AI-driven efficiency
opportunities, implement solutions, and establish governance
practices that ensure safe, compliant, and ethical use of
AI.

You will operate as a hybrid product manager, systems
engineer, DevOps engineer, and enterprise AI advisor,
shaping the organization's approach to intelligent
automation and advanced tooling.

What You’ll Be Working On:

- AI Enablement and Strategy: Lead organization-wide AI
  adoption and enablement initiatives. Identify
  opportunities to leverage AI tools to improve efficiency,
  accuracy, and productivity across business units.
- Solution Design and Prototyping: Architect, configure, and
  prototype solutions using LLMs, APIs, and orchestration
  tools. Develop proof-of-concepts for department-specific
  AI use cases.
- Systems Integration: Integrate AI capabilities into existing
  applications, workflows, and cloud environments (e.g.,
  Azure, AWS). Manage connections between enterprise systems
  and AI platforms through APIs or automation layers.
- AI Platform Management: Oversee configuration and
  operational support of platforms such as OpenAI,
  Anthropic, Zapier, and Make.
- Security and Compliance Oversight: Ensure AI implementations
  adhere to internal policies and external standards,
  including HIPAA, ISO 27001/27002, and internal data
  governance frameworks.
- Responsible AI and Risk Management: Implement and promote
  compliance with frameworks such as NIST AI RMF 1.0 and the
  EU AI Act. Conduct AI risk assessments and recommend
  mitigation controls.
- Cross-Functional Collaboration: Partner with department
  heads and technical teams to evaluate requirements,
  recommend AI-enabled tools, and support implementation.
- Education and Advocacy: Serve as a trusted AI advisor to
  business and technical leaders. Provide internal guidance,
  training, and documentation on AI tool usage, safety, and
  best practices.
- Performance Monitoring: Establish metrics to evaluate AI-
  driven efficiency gains, user adoption, and business
  impact.
- Vendor and Tool Evaluation: Continuously research, evaluate,
  and recommend new AI tools, frameworks, and integration
  strategies aligned with enterprise goals.

What You’ll Bring to OneStudyTeam:

- Bachelor's degree in Computer Science, Information
  Technology, Engineering, or related field (or equivalent
  practical experience)
- Certifications in cloud platforms (AWS, Azure), AI/ML, or
  information security
- 10+ years of hands-on experience in software engineering,
  systems integration, or DevOps
- 3+ years of experience implementing and managing AI or
  automation tools in enterprise environments
- Deep understanding of AI deployment frameworks
- Experience integrating AI systems with enterprise
  applications, APIs, and data infrastructure
- Strong Python and scripting skills for automation and AI
  orchestration
- Proven ability to translate business problems into AI-
  enabled technical solutions
- Familiarity with HIPAA, ISO 27001/27002, and secure software
  development practices
- Working knowledge of NIST AI RMF 1.0 and EU AI Act risk
  categorization principles
- Demonstrated ability to work cross-functionally and
  communicate technical solutions to non-technical audiences
- Experience leading AI governance, security, or compliance
  initiatives in regulated industries
- Experience with MLOps, data pipelines, or enterprise
  automation platforms such as Zapier and Make
- Prior experience developing or managing chatbot workflows,
  prompt engineering, or AI knowledge bases
- Familiarity with productivity-enhancing tools such as
  Microsoft Copilot, Slack AI, Salesforce AI, or similar
  enterprise AI integrations
