## Description complète

About Us

Shepherd is the hybrid law firm for startups, combining AI-
powered technology with experienced attorney oversight to
deliver faster, more affordable, and transparent legal
services. Built for early-stage companies from pre-seed
through Series B, Shepherd provides fixed-cost pricing and
glass-box transparency- eliminating the unpredictability of
traditional billable hours. The firm's contextual AI learns
each client's business, risk appetite, and deal preferences,
enabling personalized legal solutions at a fraction of
BigLaw costs. Unlike pure technology platforms, Shepherd
clients work directly with dedicated attorneys who provide
strategic guidance and relationship-driven counsel. Shepherd
is building the future of legal services- where efficiency
increases client value, not just law firm margins.

The Opportunity

We’re looking for a Founding Engineer / Head of Engineering
to own the technical vision and build the core product from
the ground up. This is a true founding team–level role:
you’ll architect the AI engine, shape the product’s data and
workflow systems, and lead the technical execution that
powers the first AI-native law firm for startups.

As Founding Engineer / Head of Engineering, you’ll partner
closely with the CEO and CLO to turn complex legal workflows
into scalable, automated systems- blending LLMs, structured
data, and human-in-the-loop validation into a seamless,
modern startup experience.

This is a 0→1 product build, a leadership role, and an
opportunity to define the technical foundations of a
category-defining company.

What You’ll Do

Technical Leadership

- Architect the end-to-end technology stack for the Shepherd
  platform (LLM infrastructure, workflow engine, database
  design, API integrations (Slack, Docusign, ect).
- Establish engineering best practices, technical roadmap, and
  long-term architecture decisions.
- Build the foundation for a secure, compliant system that
  meets startup and enterprise legal standards.
- Own the evaluation, fine-tuning, and deployment of LLMs for
  contract analysis, summarization, redlining, and risk
  scoring.

Product & AI Development

- Build our core features:
  - AI contract review and redlines
  - Build and expand a context-aware AI
  - Smart drafting and document automation
  - Workflow automation (intake, tasking, approvals)
  - Legal hub for requests, docs, alerts, and lawyer interaction
- Design human-in-the-loop systems that blend AI speed with
  attorney validation.
- Translate ambiguous legal workflows into clean, reliable,
  repeatable technical systems.
- Work directly with early customers to validate and iterate
  on features in real time.

Security, Compliance & Infrastructure

- Set up infrastructure that meets SOC-2 and privacy
  standards.
- Implement secure document storage, access controls, role-
  based permissions, and audit trails.
- Ensure ethical, compliant, and trustworthy use of AI models
  across the platform.

Team & Culture

- Hire and lead a world-class engineering team as we scale
  (frontend, backend, ML/AI).
- Build a culture of velocity, quality, and ownership.
- Act as a technical co-founder who can communicate clearly
  with investors, partners, and customers.

Who You Are

- 6–12+ years of experience as a full-stack engineer or
  technical founder (AI/ML or workflow-heavy SaaS strongly
  preferred).
- Deep experience building production AI systems (LLM fine-
  tuning, embeddings, RAG, evaluation).
- Strong product instincts with a bias toward shipping quickly
  and iterating.
- Experience designing secure, scalable architectures for B2B
  or workflow products.
- Comfortable in ambiguity: able to turn undefined problems
  into structured, elegant solutions.
- Entrepreneurial DNA- you want to build, own, and scale
  something from the ground up.
- Bonus: experience with legal tech, fintech, compliance
  systems, or other highly regulated verticals.

Why Join Us

- Ground-Floor Equity: Ground-floor opportunity to build a
  generational company redefining legal infrastructure.
- Category Creation: Help build the first AI-native hybrid law
  firm + SaaS platform for startups.
- Technical Challenge: Blend LLMs, data, automation, and legal
  reasoning into one seamless workflow engine.
- Massive Market: Startups and SMBs spend $25B+ per year on
  legal- and the shift toward automation is accelerating.
- Mission-Driven Team: Work alongside founders building a more
  accessible, modern, and scalable legal system.

Compensation

- Equity: Significant founding team equity stake
- Cash: 140k-160k base to start, increasing with each
  subsequent funding round
