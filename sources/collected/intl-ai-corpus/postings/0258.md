Job Description

About You

Are you looking for an opportunity to work on meaningful,
cutting-edge projects in machine learning? Did you wonder
what it would be like to work at a company where your
contribution has a real, measurable impact – and you are
rewarded for it?

If you have a knack for data and aspire to contribute to

innovative ideas to fruition in an inclusive, feedback-

Your Responsibilities

As our new Senior AI Engineer, you will play a crucial role
in developing state-of-the-art agentic systems and AI
integrations within RepRisk’s Agentic delivery team. You
will contribute to the design, implementation and operation
of agentic and AI products within the company. Moreover, you
will be:

- Designing and building agentic AI features that leverage
  memory, planning, and tool-use to solve real-world
  business problems.
- Developing, integrating, and maintaining LLM-powered
  microservices and pipelines as part of broader production
  applications.
- Fine-tuning foundation models and training custom models for
  perception, reasoning, and decision-making tasks.
- Identifying, testing, and implementing state-of-the-art
  advancements in LLMs and autonomous agent architectures
  (e.g., tool-use, reflection, multi-agent coordination).
- Ensuring robust performance through continuous evaluation,
  optimization, and A/B testing of AI components.
- Maintaining a well-balanced tech stack that prioritizes both
  simplicity and efficiency.
- Collaborating closely with product managers, designers, and
  domain experts to align technical solutions with user
  needs and business goals.
- Mentoring and guiding team members, fostering a
  collaborative engineering culture.
- Participating in technical design reviews and code reviews
  to uphold high engineering standards.
- Contributing actively to a cross-functional Scrum team,
  sharing insights and driving innovation from concept to
  deployment.
