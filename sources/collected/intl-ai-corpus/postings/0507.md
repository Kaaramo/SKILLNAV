## Description complète

At Vanta, our mission is to help businesses earn and prove
trust. We believe that security should be monitored and
verified continuously, and we empower companies to practice
better security and prove it with ease. Vanta has a kind and
talented team, and while some have prior security
experience, many have been successful at Vanta without it.

At Vanta, our go-to-market (GTM) teams are the engine behind
our growth. We’re looking for a GTM Engineer who will sit at
the intersection of business systems, data, and AI and build
workflows that help Marketing, Sales, Customer Success, and
RevOps move faster and operate more intelligently.

You will own the strategy and execution of AI-driven
automations across our GTM stack using tools like
Salesforce, HubSpot, Gong, Clay, Workato, n8n, and
dbt/Snowflake.

This is a hands-on engineering role for someone who loves
solving complex business problems with elegant, maintainable
systems — and wants a direct, measurable impact on how Vanta
finds, wins, and grows customers. Your work will directly
influence pipeline generation, win rates, renewal velocity,
and rep productivity.

What you’ll do as a GTM Engineer at Vanta

- Systematize and Automate the GTM Motion: Identify high-
  leverage opportunities for AI and automation across GTM
  workflows (e.g. prospecting, pipeline generation, account
  research, renewal prep).
- Build AI-powered workflows & agents: Design, build, and
  maintain AI-driven workflows and agents using a mix of
  workflow tools (Salesforce flows and Workato) and code
  (e.g., Python, SQL)
- Build and Ship Internal Tools: Design, develop, and maintain
  internal-facing web applications leveraging Claude Code
  and Netlify.
- Partner with GTM Leadership: Work with Sales and Post Sales
  leadership to understand their needs and build custom AI-
  based solutions that align with their strategic goals
- Ship reliable, maintainable systems: Develop technical
  documentation, monitoring, and testing frameworks to
  ensure that GTM systems and AI workflows are reliable,
  observable, and easy to adopt.
- Experiment, evaluate, and iterate: Evaluate and implement
  new GTM and AI technologies, APIs, and tools (e.g.,
  enrichment platforms, orchestration tools, AI vendors)
  with a clear “build vs. buy” perspective.

How to be successful in this role:

- Experience 5+ years of experience in business systems, GTM
  engineering, data/analytics engineering, or a similar role
  in high-growth B2B SaaS.
- AI & systems mindset: Strong knowledge of how AI and
  automation can be applied in business and GTM systems
  (e.g., workflow automation, summarization, routing,
  scoring).
- Technical skill set: Prior software engineering experience,
  proficiency with Salesforce (data model, Flows,
  integrations) and experience integrating other GTM systems
  (e.g., Gong, Heap and other marketing automation tools),
  Strong SQL skills; experience with modern data stack tools
  like Snowflake and dbt is a plus.
- Collaboration: Excellent communication and interpersonal
  skills to work effectively with internal stakeholders and
  cross-functional teams.
- Open to using AI to amplify their skills and strengthen
  their work - demonstrating curiosity, a willingness to
  learn, and sound judgment in applying AI responsibly to
  improve efficiency and impact

About Vanta

We started in 2018, in the wake of several high-profile data
breaches. Online security was only becoming more important,
but we knew firsthand how hard it could be for fast-growing
companies to invest the time and manpower it takes to build
a solid security foundation. Vanta was inspired by a vision
to restore trust in internet businesses by enabling
companies to improve and prove their security. From our
early days automating security monitoring for compliance
standards like SOC 2, HIPAA and ISO 27001 to creating the
world's leading Trust Management Platform, our vision
remains unchanged.

Now more than ever, making security continuous—not just a
point-in-time check— is essential. Thousands of companies
rely on Vanta to build, maintain and demonstrate their
trust— all in a way that's real-time and transparent.
