### Raisonnement classification (LLM upstream)

This is a software engineering role building an AI adoption
platform for workforce training. The role involves full-
stack development (Python, TypeScript, React, Elixir, AWS)
to deliver AI upskilling products and personalized AI use
cases, but does not involve building, training, or deploying
AI models itself. The engineer is building the software
platform that delivers AI education and training content to
end users, working near AI rather than directly on AI
systems.
