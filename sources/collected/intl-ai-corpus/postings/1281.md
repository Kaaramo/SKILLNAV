## Description complète

We are looking for an AI QA Automation Engineer (L5) to
ensure the quality, reliability, and performance of AI-
powered systems, including APIs, agentic workflows, and
Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) pipelines.

This role goes beyond traditional QA automation. You will
work at the intersection of quality engineering and AI
systems, building automated validation frameworks for LLM-
driven behavior and partnering closely with ML and full-
stack teams to ship reliable AI features to production.

At Proximity, you won’t just test software - you’ll help
define how AI systems are validated, evaluated, and trusted
at scale.

Responsibilities:

- Build and maintain automation test suites for AI APIs,
  agentic workflows, and RAG-based systems.
- Implement LLM evaluation tests, covering accuracy,
  consistency, determinism, and performance characteristics.
- Collaborate closely with ML, backend, and full-stack
  engineering teams to validate releases and ensure
  production readiness.
- Contribute to regression, reliability, and performance
  testing across AI and platform services.
- Design test strategies for non-deterministic AI behavior,
  including guardrails, fallback paths, and quality
  thresholds.
- Continuously improve test coverage, tooling, and automation
  frameworks for AI-driven systems.

Requirements

- 5+ years of experience in QA automation or quality
  engineering roles.
- Strong hands-on experience with Python and/or JavaScript,
  and modern testing frameworks.
- Experience building and maintaining API-level and
  integration test suites.
- Ability to design robust tests for complex, distributed
  systems.
- Familiarity with LLM- or ML-based systems and their unique
  testing challenges is strongly preferred.
- Strong analytical and problem-solving skills, with attention
  to detail.
- Ability to work independently while collaborating
  effectively with cross-functional teams.

Desired Skills:

- Experience testing RAG pipelines, vector search, or AI agent
  workflows.
- Exposure to evaluation frameworks for LLMs or ML models.
- Familiarity with CI/CD integration for automated test
  execution.
- Understanding of performance and load testing for backend
  services.

Benefits

- Best in class compensation: We hire only the best, and we
  pay accordingly.

- High-impact role: Help define quality standards for
  production AI systems.
- Proximity Talks: Learn from experts across AI, engineering,
  and product.

Continuous learning: Grow your skills at the intersection of
QA, automation, and AI.
