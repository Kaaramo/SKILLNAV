About the role

Research has consistently shown that one-on-one tutoring
dramatically outperforms traditional classroom instruction—a
finding known as Bloom's 2 Sigma Problem. Despite decades of
awareness, no solution has successfully delivered
personalized tutoring at scale. Until now.

We’re building an AI Creator that turns learning into

solve real-world problems, transforming knowledge into
tangible impact from day one.. You will work on the core AI
system that delivers real-time, adaptive learning
experiences to learners. If you're excited about building
the future of human learning, this role is for you.

You’re a strong builder who thrives on turning cutting-edge

You’re comfortable working in ambiguity but don’t wait for

can transform learning.
