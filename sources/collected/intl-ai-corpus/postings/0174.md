## Description complète

🚀 Founding Engineer — Supernal

Remote (US & EU Friendly) | Full-Time | Equity + Salary

Website: getsupernal.ai

Supernal is building the operating system for businesses
powered by AI employees.

AI employees are here — and they’re redefining how
businesses operate. At Supernal, we turn any tedious, manual
process into an AI-powered workflow, managed through a
delightful interface and accessible from web to phone.
Businesses can now scale faster with smaller human teams
empowered by large teams of AI employees.

We believe this is a trillion-dollar opportunity. As a
founding engineer, you'll help invent the infrastructure and
interfaces that make it possible. Supernal has already
reached multi-million dollar revenue within months, and
we’re just getting started.

Why This Role Exists

We’re looking for a well-rounded, full-stack founding
engineer to help us build robust AI systems and product
experiences end-to-end. You’ll work directly with the
founders on everything from core architecture to shipping
new AI-powered features. If you’re a systems thinker, rapid
builder, and care deeply about great UX and AI performance —
we’d love to meet you.

🔧 What You’ll Do

- Architect and scale RAG pipelines, agent-based systems, and
  LLM-powered workflows
- Fine-tune large language models for structured data
  extraction and automation
- Build developer-friendly APIs and interfaces for web
  automation
- Ship product features fast — with real usage and feedback
- Work closely with product, design, and customers to build
  what matters
- Stay ahead of the curve on LLM tools, embeddings,
  orchestration, and training
- Create elegant, resilient systems that just work

🌟 Who You Are

- A full-stack engineer comfortable across Python/Django,
  React/TypeScript, or eager to learn fast
- A system thinker who sees architecture, UX, performance, and
  product as a unified challenge
- Obsessed with AI agents, LLMs, and building delightful
  automations
- Comfortable wearing many hats: engineer, product thinker,
  debugger, owner
- Thrive in fast-moving environments with a high degree of
  autonomy
- A natural builder: you ship, you own, you iterate

✅ Bonus Points

- Experience building LLM-based agents, fine-tuning models, or
  RAG pipelines
- Contributions to open-source AI/ML projects
- Experience with: TypeScript, PostgreSQL, Redis
- You've trained or fine-tuned your own model from scratch
- Worked on web automation, scraping, or structured data
  extraction before

✨ Why Join Supernal?

- Founding role in a rocketship startup with real revenue and
  real usage
- Ship fast: from idea to production in days, not months
- Autonomy: own your work, your time, and your decisions
- Remote-first across US and EU, with in-person offsites and
  workshops
- Competitive salary + meaningful equity
- Incredible teammates: builders, operators, musicians,
  filmmakers, and climbers
- Backed by invisible.co, and founded by repeat entrepreneurs

🧪 Our Interview Process

We prefer to pay you for a week to build something real with
us. We believe it’s the best way to evaluate fit, working
style, and what it’s like to collaborate — for both sides.

Supernal is for you if:

- You’re a deep systems thinker
- You constantly seek ways to grow yourself, your team, and
  the product
- You want to leverage LLMs to produce what used to take
  entire teams
- You care deeply about product excellence and technical
  elegance
- You thrive when wearing many hats and solving unstructured
  problems
- You value freedom and want to work from your favorite place
  on earth

Supernal is not for you if:
