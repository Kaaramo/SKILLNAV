## Description complète

AI Engineer

A Career with Point72’s COO Team

The AI Engineer, Investment Services will be responsible for
exploring opportunities to develop and build potential
solutions that leverage GenAI and Machine Learning in an
effort to deliver innovative, GenAI driven products that
seek to enhance the firm's business processes and enterprise
intelligence.

You will play a unique role by designing and implementing
advanced GenAI/ML techniques and applications to solve key
business problems across various departments with an aim to
enhance operational intelligence, efficiency, and decision-
making capabilities. This includes building prototypes to
prove out the potential value of proposed AI solutions and
transforming successful prototypes into robust, production-
grade capabilities to enable opportunities for broad value
creation for the firm.

Your projects will span both on-premises and AWS
environments, leveraging technologies such as Kafka, Spark,
Cloud warehouses, and Data Lakes, and employing programming
languages like Python, Java, and SQL.

As part of the AI/ML community at Point72, our teams
collaborate horizontally with one another and vertically
with our AI Platform and Data Teams, and Compliance to
define the AI Strategy for the firm including its
infrastructure, architecture, models, and data needs.  This
position offers immediate impact and growth opportunities,
allowing you to apply your skills while advancing in modern
AI integration and engineering.

What you’ll do

- Identify opportunities and develop technical roadmaps for
  GenAI solutions
- Scope, prioritize, prototype and deliver GenAI projects to
  help solve key business problems for Point72’s Investment
  Services teams. Your work/projects will span both on-
  premises and AWS environments, leverage technologies such
  as Kafka, Spark, Cloud warehouses, and Data Lakes, and use
  programming languages such as Java, Python, and SQL
- Lead collaborations across the firm, where appropriate, on
  GenAI/ML initiatives and techniques
- Deliver analytics and insights to measure the value of
  solutions delivered
- Partner with groups across the firm such as Technology and
  Compliance on GenAI initiatives
- Partner internally to establish rigorous model governance
  practices, including testing, monitoring, and
  documentation to ensure solutions remain accurate and
  bias-free
- Stay up to date on AI/ML market landscape, trends, and
  technological innovations to provide recommendations on
  new tools and methodologies for adoption

What’s required

- Bachelor's degree in computer science, engineering, or
  related field
- Minimum 5+ years of professional experience
- Demonstrated success leveraging AI/ML to drive/improve
  business outcomes
- The ability to develop, own, and provide technical
  leadership over AI solutions, including model selection,
  fine-tuning, and ideally model development
- Excellent written and verbal communication skills
- Commitment to the highest ethical standards
