About TechTorch

At TechTorch, we’re building the future of intelligent work.
Our mission is to help companies design, build, and deploy
AI agents that automate complex, real-world workflows —
delivering reliability, measurable ROI, and massive
efficiency gains.

Here, you won’t just be playing with prompts or running
endless proofs of concept. You’ll ship production-grade AI
systems that solve real problems across industries.

You’ll join a hands-on, fast-moving, ownership-driven team
that thrives on building quickly, iterating fast, and seeing
results in days — not months.

Role overview

We’re looking for an AI Developer who can design, implement,
and deploy agentic systems that actually work in production.

If you’ve been building AI agents for the last couple of
years and love the challenge of getting things done fast —
this is your place.

This is not a research role. It’s about building, testing,
and shipping real AI products that deliver measurable
business value.

What you’ll do

- Design and implement multi-step AI agents and orchestration
  workflows in Python
- Build using LangGraph, LangChain, n8n, AWS Bedrock, and
  similar tools
- Integrate and evaluate top models (Gemini, GPT, Claude,
  etc.)
- Connect agents to real systems (APIs, SaaS apps, webhooks,
  queues, events)
- Implement memory, retrieval, and planning for reliable agent
  behavior
- Take prototypes to production — with CI/CD, containers, and
  cloud deployment
- Build internal SDKs and reusable agent templates to speed up
  delivery
- Instrument everything — logging, tracing, guardrails,
  evaluation
- Work directly with clients to capture requirements, run
  experiments, and iterate fast
- Apply best practices for security, privacy, and safety in
  all deployments

What you bring

- 2+ years of hands-on experience building and shipping AI
  agents or complex automations
- Strong Python skills (async, testing, packaging)
- Experience with LangGraph or LangChain, plus orchestration
  tools (n8n, Bedrock, Airflow, Prefect, Temporal, etc.)
- Familiarity with OpenAI, Gemini, Claude, or similar model
  ecosystems
- Solid data foundations: embeddings, RAG, chunking, indexing
- Software engineering fundamentals: Git, CI/CD, Docker, AWS
- Bonus: TypeScript, FastAPI, Node.js, Supabase, or
  observability tools (Langfuse, W&B, OpenTelemetry)

How you work

- You like moving fast, but never at the cost of quality
- You communicate clearly and love solving problems
  collaboratively
