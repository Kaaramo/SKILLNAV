## Description complète

EverPro - Senior AI Engineer

At EverCommerce [Nasdaq: EVCM], we are on a mission to
digitally transform the service economy with tailored, end-
to-end SaaS solutions that simplify and empower the lives of
our 690,000+ customers.  As a leading service commerce
platform, our modern digital and mobile applications create
predictable, informed, and convenient experiences between
customers and their service professionals in the areas of
Home & Field Services, Health Services, and Fitness &
Wellness industries.

We are building an extraordinary company and looking for
talented, energetic, and motivated people to join our team.
You can learn more about our Company, Culture and Values
here: https://careers.evercommerce.com/us/en

We are seeking a highly experienced and visionary Senior
AI/ML Engineer III to serve as the technical leader for our
portfolio-wide agentic AI platform initiative. This
individual will architect and lead development of a shared
agentic platform (agents, tooling functions, orchestration
workflows) and an MCP (Model Context Protocol) server that
can be deployed into many of our portfolio of products. As
the senior-most technical member of the central AI team, you
will partner closely with the AI/ML Product Manager and
AI/ML UX Designer to shape strategy and drive execution of
the core technical foundation for all AI functionality
across our company.



Responsibilities:

Technical Leadership & Architecture

- Lead the design and implementation of a scalable, secure,
  multi-tenant agentic AI platform and MCP server
  infrastructure.

- Define technical strategy, architectural blueprints, and
  component-level specifications for shared AI services.

- Evaluate and select core technologies, frameworks, and tools
  to enable robust AI/ML development and deployment.

AI/ML Ops & Evaluation

- Build and maintain AI/ML Ops pipelines for model training,
  fine-tuning, deployment, monitoring, and lifecycle
  management at scale.

- Define methods to quantify performance and define desired
  model behavior in business-scoped areas/workflows.

- Drive comparative testing and benchmarking of LLMs, agents,
  tools, and supporting components to ensure optimal
  selections.

Team Leadership & Collaboration

- Lead and mentor a team of AI/ML engineers, fostering
  technical excellence and a culture of innovation.

- Collaborate with the AI/ML Product Manager and UX Designer
  to align technical execution with product strategy and UX
  principles.

- Partner with product engineering teams across the portfolio
  to integrate shared AI platform capabilities into their
  products.

Governance, Security & Standards

- Implement enterprise-grade security, compliance, and
  observability for all AI platform components.

- Establish coding, testing, and documentation best practices
  for reusable AI tooling and services.

- Champion responsible AI practices, ensuring ethical and
  trustworthy deployment of AI systems.



Skills and Experience needed for success in this role:

Required:

- 8+ years of software engineering experience, with 4+ years
  focused on AI/ML systems development.

- Deep hands-on experience with shipping agents in production,
  multi-agent orchestration, and LLM-based systems with
  tool-use and safety guardrails.

- Proven expertise in AI/ML Ops (CI/CD for models, model
  registries, monitoring, observability, and versioning).

- Strong background in designing and operating large-scale
  distributed systems or platform infrastructure.

- Familiarity with model evaluation methodologies,
  benchmarking, reinforcement learning, fine-tuning and
  experiment management.

- Demonstrated experience leading teams of engineers on
  complex, cross-cutting technical initiatives.

- Strong systems thinking, architecture design, and technical
  communication skills.

- Strong programming skills and experience in Python
  (PyTorch/TF, HF) and full-stack development. Node.js and
  TypeScript knowledge a plus.

Preferred:

- Experience implementing or managing MCP (Model Context
  Protocol) or comparable contextual orchestration servers.

- Prior work in multi-product SaaS environments or platform
  teams.

- Knowledge of security, compliance, and privacy frameworks
  relevant to enterprise AI deployments.

- Experience with tRPC (Type-Safe Backend)



Where:  The EverCommerce team is distributed globally, with
teams in the U.S., Canada, the U.K., Jordan, New Zealand,
and Australia. With a widely distributed team, we are used
to working remotely across different time zones. This role
can be based anywhere in the United States – if you’re close

can work 100% remotely. Please note that you must be
eligible to work without sponsorship to qualify for this
position, and this role may require travel to our Corporate
Headquarters in Denver, Colorado, or to other office
locations around North America.

  wellness stipend
