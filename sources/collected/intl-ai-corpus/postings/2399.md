About Us

Automation Anywhere is the leader in Agentic Process
Automation (APA), transforming how work gets done with AI-
powered automation. Its APA system, built on the industry’s
first Process Reasoning Engine (PRE) and specialized AI
agents, combines process discovery, RPA, end-to-end
orchestration, document processing, and analytics—all
delivered with enterprise-grade security and governance.
Guided by its vision to fuel the future of work, Automation
Anywhere helps organizations worldwide boost productivity,
accelerate growth, and unleash human potential.

About Role:

We are seeking an experienced Sr. AI Developer (AGENTIC) to
join our dynamic team, where you will contribute to the
successful delivery and ongoing support of automation and
GenAI solutions. This is an exciting opportunity for someone
with a strong development background who is passionate about
automation, AI, and collaboration with other teams.

Key Responsibilities:

- We are looking for candidates with experience in the latest
  features of Agentic Automations, AI Agents, MCP, A2A, MVP
  , and API Tasks.
- Candidates should have practical experience working with
  APIs in automation, demonstrating the ability to
  understand and utilize API documentation effectively.
- Additionally, candidates should have hands-on experience
  with IQ Bot or Document Automation, having built at least
  one learning instance and applied it in a real automation
  project (not just a proof of concept or learning
  exercise).
- A basic understanding of CR administration tasks, along with
  experience in support and deployment, is also desirable.
- Basic understanding for Gen AI.

Required Qualifications and Skills:

- Education: Bachelor’s in Computer Science, Data Analytics,
  Engineering, or related fields.
- Development Experience: About 5-10 years of hands-on
  experience in development of automation and AI projects.
  Proven experience in using Agentic Automations, AI Agents,
  MCP, A2A, MVP Expertise in tools development to support
  automation frameworks.
- AI/ML Knowledge: Working knowledge of Generative AI and how
  to integrate AI with automation solutions.
- Experience in APIs & Connectors: Proven experience in
  developing and integrating APIs, connectors, and working
  with third-party systems to expand automation
  capabilities.
- Cloud & Infrastructure: Experience in deploying automation
  solutions on virtual machines (VMs) on cloud environments
  (AWS)
- Agile Methodology: Familiarity with Agile development, Scrum
  processes, and experience in working within Agile teams.

Additional Competencies:

- Problem Solving: Strong analytical and troubleshooting
  skills to resolve critical production issues and provide
  L3 support.
- Collaboration & Communication: Excellent communication
  skills to work effectively with cross-functional teams,
  stakeholders, and internal customers.
- Customer-Focused: A strong understanding of customer needs,
  with the ability to support the automation initiatives
  impacting customer experience.
