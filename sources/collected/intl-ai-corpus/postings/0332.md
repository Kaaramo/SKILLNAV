About the Role

We’re hiring an AI engineer, a full-stack, strategic builder
who can take a large project from a working prototype to an
industry-defining product. You’ll lead development of our
agentic AI systems, owning model orchestration, retrieval,
real-time inference, evaluation, and production
infrastructure, and collaborating closely with the founding
team on product and technical strategy.

What You’ll Own

- Build the engine: Design and own end-to-end agentic
  systems—retrieval, orchestration, inference, eval, and
  feedback loops
- Scale the stack: Shape our architecture across model
  interfaces, data pipelines, vector DBs, and streaming
  infrastructure
- Ship fast: Partner with product and design to prototype,
  test, and launch AI-driven features that deliver real
  value
- Improve performance: Continuously optimize accuracy,
  latency, and robustness through fine-tuning, eval
  frameworks, and data iteration
- Learn from the field: Join sales and customer calls to
  understand real-world pain points, pressure-test features,
  and translate feedback into product improvements
- Support growth: Build infrastructure and tools that help the
  whole company move faster—debugging, observability, agent
  testing
- Build your team: Hire, mentor, and collaborate with
  engineers who care about craft, velocity, and real-world
  impact

You Might Be a Fit If You...

- Have 5–10 years of experience building AI or ML-powered
  systems, ideally in high-growth or early-stage
  environments
- Have shipped real products that use LLMs, retrieval,
  orchestration, or streaming inference—not just prototypes
- Think full-stack, end-to-end: You’re comfortable moving
  between modeling, infra, and product-facing code
- Have an entrepreneurial mindset and thrive on building from
  scratch—systems, processes, tools, and the team
- Can explain complex ideas clearly, especially to non-
  technical teammates, customers, and stakeholders
- Thrive in ambiguity and take ownership in fast-paced, high-
  trust environments
- Bonus: Experience with vertical software businesses or
