## Description complète

About Formant

Formant builds AI that helps the world's largest factories
make sense of their alarms. Semiconductor fabs,
manufacturing plants, and critical infrastructure generate
hundreds of alerts a day — and the people who know how to
interpret them are retiring faster than they can be
replaced. We embed with customers to learn their operations,
then build AI that automatically triages and prioritizes
alarms so operators can focus on what actually matters. Our
data compounds with every site and every customer. We're
pre-PMF, well-funded through mid-2027, and at the stage
where every hire shapes what this company becomes.

As a Series A startup, we value builders who thrive in
ambiguity, move quickly, and care deeply about customer
outcomes.

The Role

We're hiring a Senior Forward Deployed Engineer — a
technically elite, customer-obsessed builder who operates as
a startup within a startup.

This is not a traditional implementation or solutions
engineering role. The Sr. FDE is Formant's Pathfinder: the
person who goes first, goes deep, and shapes how our AI-
powered platform gets adopted across some of the world's
most complex industrial environments. You'll own the full
arc of an enterprise engagement — from initial discovery
through onsite bootcamp, POC, pilot, and production
deployment — while maintaining trusted relationships from
end users all the way to the C-suite. You will be supported
by a team of other FDEs and Engineers to implement the
customer strategy and solution.

You'll be Formant's most direct line to what our product
needs to become. The insights you surface in the field will
shape our roadmap, our blueprints, and how we scale every
engagement that follows.

NOTE This role requires 40-50% travel for onsite customer
engagements

What You'll Do

Pathfinding & Discovery

- Lead onsite discovery bootcamps with enterprise customers —
  living in their environment, learning their operations,
  and surfacing requirements that no requirements doc could
  capture
- Define measurable success metrics and establish clear
  checkpoints across POC, Pilot, and production deployment
- Translate ambiguous operational problems into concrete
  technical architectures and solution designs
- Build genuine buy-in with customer stakeholders at every
  level — from operators on the floor to engineering leads
  to executive sponsors

Technical Execution

- Own end-to-end technical delivery: sales engineering,
  solution architecture, rapid prototyping, integration with
  production environments, and full deployment
- Build and iterate on LLM-powered workflows, AI-assisted
  investigation pipelines, and automation tooling that
  operates under real-world constraints (latency,
  connectivity, hardware)
- Integrate deeply with customer systems, APIs, and existing
  operational infrastructure
- Produce reusable blueprints and integration patterns that
  accelerate all future deployments

Strategic Account Ownership

- Serve as the primary technical relationship owner for a
  portfolio of enterprise accounts
- Partner with Account Directors to connect technical outcomes
  to business value and renewal health
- Lead executive-level conversations with confidence —
  translating technical progress into ROI language
- Identify expansion opportunities and surface the product
  gaps that, if closed, unlock the next stage of the
  engagement

Field Intelligence & Product Influence

- Act as the feedback loop between the field and Formant's
  product and engineering teams
- Identify patterns across engagements and contribute to the
  evolving playbook, integration library, and deployment
  frameworks

Who You Are

Required Experience

- 5+ years in a customer-facing technical role — Forward
  Deployed Engineering, Solutions Architecture,
  Implementation Engineering, or equivalent field
  engineering experience
- Demonstrated ability to own complex enterprise engagements
  end-to-end, from discovery through production
- Experience with LLMs, AI workflows, or agentic systems in
  production environments
- Strong full-stack engineering skills — Python and/or
  TypeScript required; comfortable across the stack when
  needed
- Proven track record leading technical conversations with
  both engineering teams and senior business stakeholders
- Comfortable with significant travel for customer engagements
  (40–50% of the time spent in bootcamps onsite with
  customers)

Nice to Have

- Experience in robotics, industrial automation, or physical
  systems — including ROS, AMRs, manufacturing, agriculture,
  logistics, SCADA systems, or semiconductor fabrication
- Integrating LLMs and agentic systems with physical hardware
  and infrastructure
- Data curation and machine learning experience
- Background working with semiconductor, pharmaceuticals,
  automotive, oil & gas, electronics or other regulated
  verticals
- Experience building or scaling an FDE practice, onboarding
  playbooks, or deployment frameworks
- Startup experience — especially in a role with high autonomy
  and ambiguity

Who Will Thrive in This Role

- Engineers who want to be the person in the room when the
  hard problems get defined — not just solved
- People who find it energizing to move between a factory
  floor, an engineering whiteboard, and a C-suite meeting in
  the same day
- Builders with strong opinions, loosely held — who can design
  a system, prototype it fast, and throw it out when the
  customer teaches you something better
- People who understand that proximity to the real problem is
  the only sustainable competitive advantage

Salary range: $160,000–$180,000 based on experience and
geographical location.

This position is remote with significant onsite customer
travel. Formant currently hires in: AZ, CA, CO, IL, KS, MA,
NC, NY, OK, OR, and PA.
