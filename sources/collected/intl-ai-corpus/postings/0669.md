## Description complète

About the Team

The AI Flows team builds AI-powered workflows inside Miro
that help teams go from idea to outcome faster: turning
intent into structured steps, suggestions, and actions
directly on the canvas. We’re in a post–zero-to-one phase,
iterating quickly with real users, tightening UX loops, and
shaping what “AI workflows” should feel like in Miro,
reliable, delightful, and genuinely useful.

About the Role

We’re looking for a Senior Frontend Engineer who is
exceptional at building complex, highly interactive UIs and
who cares deeply about craft, clarity, and how things feel
to users.

This is not a “forms and dashboards” role. You’ll be working
in a rich, graphical environment with real performance
constraints, real users, and real scale.

If you enjoy hard frontend problems, visual complexity, and
shaping how AI shows up in a mainstream product , this is a
very good seat.

What you’ll do

- Design and build highly interactive, visual UI on top of
  Miro’s real-time canvas.
- Shape the core Flows experience: how users create,
  understand, run, and trust AI workflows.
- Turn fuzzy product ideas and design intent into clean,
  intuitive, polished interfaces.
- Work closely with product and design to explore new
  interaction patterns for AI in a collaborative
  environment.
- Take ownership end-to-end: from problem framing →
  implementation → iteration → refinement.
- Improve performance, architecture, and UX quality wherever
  you see friction.

Why this is interesting

- You’ll be working on AI in a real product, at real scale -
  not demos, not toys.
- Your work will directly shape how millions of people
  experience AI at work.
- You’ll be in a lean, low-bureaucracy team with real
  ownership and influence.
- You’ll combine hard technical challenges (performance, real-
  time UI, visual complexity) with meaningful product
  impact.
- The culture we’re aiming for is humble and straightforward:
  strong people, peer-level collaboration, and minimal
  corporate theater.

What you’ll need

- 6+ years of experience building frontend applications with
  modern languages, frameworks, and technologies.
- Strong JavaScript / TypeScript fundamentals.
- Proven ability to build complex, interactive web interfaces
  (not just CRUD).
- Good architectural instincts: you can structure frontend
  code so it scales and stays sane.
- You think in systems, not just components.
- You have a good visual and UX instinct, you can tell when
  something feels off.
- Experience in graphics-heavy products, editors, games, or
  other highly visual domains is a strong plus.
- Collaborative, team-first mindset with a strong sense of
  ownership and integrity.
- Clear and thoughtful communicator, comfortable asking
  questions and being transparent about uncertainty.
- Respectful and constructive in how you work with others,
  giving credit where it’s due and contributing to a
  positive team culture.
- Curious and open-minded, with a genuine interest in learning
  from teammates and exploring new approaches.
- Quick to ramp up on new domains and able to reason
  effectively in unfamiliar problem spaces.
- Proactive and self-motivated, you identify opportunities for
  improvement and take initiative to make things better.

What's in it for you

- Competitive equity package
- Corporate pension plan
- Lunch, snacks and drinks provided in the office
- Wellbeing benefit and WFH equipment allowance
- Annual learning and development allowance to grow your
  skills and career
- Travel allowance for your commute
- Opportunity to work for a globally diverse team
