Job Responsibilities

Role Summary:

The Senior Platform Engineer is a hands-on engineer
responsible for designing, implementing, and operating core
platform services that support application development and
deployment. This role focuses on building reliable CI/CD
pipelines, infrastructure automation, and shared tooling
that enables teams to deploy and operate services
consistently and securely. As a senior member of the
platform team, you will lead the design and delivery of
platform components, make architectural decisions, and
review technical designs. You will mentor junior engineers,
help troubleshoot complex system issues, and ensure the
platform scales with growing workloads while meeting
reliability and security requirements.

Key Responsibilities:

• Design, build, and maintain core platform services such as
CI/CD pipelines, infrastructure-as-code frameworks,
observability platforms, and secrets management. • Drive
platform standardization through golden paths, reusable
tooling, and reference architectures. • Own platform
reliability, scalability, and operational excellence,
including automation and incident response. • Embed security
and compliance best practices into pipelines using policy-
as-code. • Partner with application teams to enable self-
service infrastructure and smooth onboarding to the
platform. • Lead technical initiatives across squads and
mentor junior engineers.

Key Relationships:

• Reports to a Platform Engineering Manager or Director. •
Works closely with Platform, Software & Test Engineers, and
Security & Compliance teams. • Acts as a technical lead and
advisor for platform-related initiatives across teams.

Knowledge, Skills & Abilities:

• Strong experience with infrastructure-as-code tools such
as Terraform, Bicep or equivalent. • Hands-on expertise with
CI/CD systems including GitHub Actions, Azure DevOps, or
Gitlab. • Build, manage, and support containerized platforms
using Docker and Kubernetes. • Solid experience with cloud
platforms, with Azure preferred and AWS/GCP as a plus. •
Strong system design, debugging, and automation skills •
Hands-on experience developing and maintaining platform
automations using Python and Bash.

Education & Experience:

• Bachelor’s or Master’s degree in Computer Science,
Engineering, or equivalent experience. • 5+ years of
experience in platform engineering, DevOps, or
infrastructure roles. • Proven experience building and
operating large-scale internal platforms or developer
tooling. • Certifications in Azure Solutions Architect,
DevOps or Cloud Security are a plus.
