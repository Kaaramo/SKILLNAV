## Description complète

About Modo Energy

The energy transition is the biggest infrastructure buildout
in human history. Modo Energy is the data platform at the
centre of it.

We build the benchmarking, forecasting, and valuation tools
that the world's most serious energy investors, developers,
and operators depend on to make decisions. If a battery gets
financed, built, or traded anywhere in the world, there's a
good chance Modo data was in the room.

Founded in 2019, we're 75+ people across London, New York,
Sydney, and Madrid; $30M Series B, AI-native, and moving
fast.

This is a rare chance to join a category-defining company at
the moment it's scaling globally.

The Role

We're hiring a Frontend Engineer to lead the architectural
evolution of our UI. You'll define the design patterns,
component abstractions, and typing contracts that allow the
wider engineering team to ship features quickly without
compromising the architecture.

Our frontend is where massive datasets, predictive models,
and AI systems become usable - fast, data-heavy interfaces
that let users explore energy markets, interrogate datasets,
and interact with complex forecasting models in real time.
The stack is TypeScript, React, and Next.js in a modern
monorepo architecture built for rapid, AI-assisted
development.

What You'll Do

- Lead the technical direction for our frontend monorepo -
  state management, routing, and module boundaries within
  Next.js and TypeScript
- Design and develop our internal UI component library (built
  on MUI) - reusable, strictly typed abstractions that serve
  as building blocks for the engineering team and AI agents
- Build and improve high-performance, low-latency interfaces -
  profiling performance, minimising re-renders, and keeping
  the platform responsive under heavy data loads
- Set the architectural patterns and coding standards that
  govern development, including oversight of high-volume AI-
  assisted contributions
- Act as the primary technical authority for frontend -
  guiding the team on UI best practices and effective use of
  AI coding tools

What We're Looking For

The Essentials

- 3+ years of professional frontend development experience
- Strong proficiency in TypeScript, React, and modern
  JavaScript
- Hands-on experience with Next.js
- Experience with Material UI (MUI) or a comparable component
  library
- Strong eye for UI/UX - you can bridge the gap between design
  and engineering and build polished, production-quality
  interfaces
- Solid understanding of testing principles with frameworks
  such as Jest, Cypress, or Playwright
- Proficient with Git, Docker, and CI/CD workflows
- Familiarity with monorepo structures and tools (Turborepo,
  Yarn Workspaces, or similar)

Nice To Have

- Familiarity with Python and/or energy forecasting models
- Experience in energy markets or data-heavy analytical
  products
- Startup or scale-up background
