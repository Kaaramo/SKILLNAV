## Description complète

WHO WE ARE

At Basis, we empower agencies and brands with cutting-edge
software that automates digital media operations. Our all-
in-one platform supports seamless planning, reporting, and
financial reconciliation across direct, programmatic,
search, and social media.

We’re not just building advanced technology; we’re also
building a culture where passionate, motivated individuals
come together to drive meaningful change. Our commitment to
inclusivity and growth ensures our employees thrive both
professionally and personally.

With our headquarters in Chicago, we offer flexible work
options across the U.S. and Canada, including remote,
hybrid, or on-site at headquarters.

ABOUT THE TEAM

Technology is at the core of what we do. The Agent
Engineering team at Basis Technologies builds and scales
intelligent systems that power our programmatic advertising
platform. We work closely with Data Science, Product, and
Platform teams to deliver LLM-powered automation, media
planning intelligence, optimization, and analytics across
the media buying lifecycle.

Our mission is to build production-grade agentic systems
that drive measurable impact and redefine what's possible in
programmatic advertising.

WAYS YOU'LL CONTRIBUTE

As a Senior Engineer, you'll be at the forefront of building
agent systems that transform how media professionals plan
and execute campaigns. You will balance rapid prototyping
with production-quality delivery to ship solutions that
customers love. To be successful, you will contribute by:

- Collaborating with Data Science and Product teams to design,
  build, and ship LLM-powered features across our media
  buying platform.
- Designing and building agents using frameworks including
  LangChain and LangGraph to automate complex media planning
  workflows.
- Developing MCP servers and APIs that connect internal and
  external agents to our media buying toolkit.
- Building and tuning agent orchestration workflows, including
  evaluation and observability, to ensure agents reason,
  plan, and execute reliably.
- Rapidly experimenting with emerging agentic approaches while
  guiding stable, scalable architectural decisions.

WHAT YOU BRING TO THE TABLE

- 5+ years of experience in software engineering, with recent
  hands-on work building LLM-powered applications or agent
  systems.
- Strong proficiency in Python, with experience building
  production APIs, backend services, and integrations with
  emerging protocols such as MCP.
- Familiarity with agent frameworks such as LangChain or
  LangGraph, and experience with evaluation tools like
  LangSmith or Langfuse.
- Ability to move quickly from prototype to production,
  balancing speed with code quality and reliability.
- Strong communication skills, a collaborative mindset, and
  commitment to creating inclusive environments where all
  voices are valued.

BONUS POINTS

- Prior experience in ad tech, media planning systems, or
  programmatic advertising platforms.
- Experience designing skill-based or MCP tool sets for
  autonomous agents.
- Experience building, tuning, and evaluating production-grade
  agentic systems. Preference towards patterns such as
  asynchronous agents and event driven systems.
- Familiarity with prompt engineering, structured outputs, and
  techniques for improving agent reliability.
- Experience with RAG systems, vector databases, or knowledge
  retrieval approaches.
- Background in building developer tools, SDKs, or API-first
  products.

OUR TECH STACK

- Languages: Python
- Agent Frameworks: LangChain, LangGraph, Claude Agent SDK
- Protocols & Integrations: MCP, REST APIs
- Evaluation: Langfuse, custom evaluation frameworks
- Data: Snowflake, S3
- Infrastructure: AWS
