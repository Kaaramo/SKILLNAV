## Description complète

Our Company

Oaktree is a leader among global investment managers
specializing in alternative investments, with $208 billion
in assets under management. The firm emphasizes an
opportunistic, value-oriented and risk-controlled approach
to investments in credit, private equity, real assets and
listed equities. The firm has over 1400 employees and
offices in 25 cities worldwide.

We are committed to cultivating an environment that is
collaborative, curious, inclusive and honors diversity of
thought. Providing training and career development
opportunities and emphasizing strong support for our local
communities through philanthropic initiatives are essential
to our culture.

For additional information please visit our website at
www.oaktreecapital.com.

Responsibilities

We are seeking a Senior Power Platform Developer & Architect
with 10+ years of overall IT experience and at least 5 years
of deep, hands-on Power Apps and Power Automate expertise.
This senior role is responsible not only for development but
also for architecting scalable, secure, and enterprise-grade
applications and workflow using Power Apps, Power Automate,
Power Platform AI, and Dataverse.

The ideal candidate has strong experience designing Power
Platform architecture, defining governance models,
implementing enterprise ALM practices, and leveraging AI
Builder, Copilot Studio, and generative AI features to
deliver intelligent business applications. Experience with
JIRA or similar Agile tools is required.

Qualifications

•    10+ years of IT experience, with strong background in
application development, system integration, or enterprise
platforms. •    5+ years of deep experience in: o    Power
Apps (Canvas & Model-driven) o    Power Automate (Cloud +
Desktop) o    Power Platform solution architecture o
Dataverse schema and relational data design o    AI Builder,
Copilot Studio, and Power Platform AI •    Hands-on
expertise with solution architecture, including environment
strategy, ALM, governance, security, and DLP. •    Strong
understanding of SQL, Dataverse, REST APIs, Azure services,
and integration patterns. •    Experience with JIRA, Azure
DevOps, or similar tools for Agile execution and CI/CD.

Personal Attributes

•    Ability to lead architecture discussions, create
solution diagrams, and document platform decisions. •
Excellent communication skills with ability to influence
technical and business stakeholders.
