## Description complète

As a leading financial services and healthcare technology
company based on revenue, SS&C is headquartered in Windsor,
Connecticut, and has 27,000+ employees in 35 countries. Some
20,000 financial services and healthcare organizations, from
the world's largest companies to small and mid-market firms,

Job Description

SS&C is seeking an experienced Kubernetes and Cloud
Operations Platform Engineer to join our team and help us
build out our cloud infrastructure and operations
capabilities. This is a unique opportunity to join a growing
company and make a significant impact on our technology
stack and operations.

Job Summary:

As a AI Platform Engineer, you will be responsible for
designing, implementing, and maintaining our cloud
infrastructure, with a focus on Kubernetes and
containerization. You will also be responsible for Platform
Engineer on a daily basis and eventually building out a team
to handle release management and operations, ensuring that
our applications are deployed efficiently, reliably, and
securely. This is a hands-on leadership role that requires a
strong technical background, excellent communication skills,
and experience in building and managing high-performing
teams.

Key Responsibilities:

1. Cloud Infrastructure: Design, implement, and maintain our
cloud infrastructure, including Kubernetes clusters,
containerization, and cloud-native services.

2. Kubernetes Expertise: Develop and maintain expertise in
Kubernetes, including cluster management, deployment
strategies, and troubleshooting.

3. Release Management: Develop and implement release
management processes and procedures to ensure efficient,
reliable, and secure deployment of applications.

4. Team Building: Build out a team of engineers to handle
release management and operations, including recruiting,
training, and mentoring team members.

5. Operations: Develop and implement operational processes
and procedures to ensure high availability, scalability, and
security of our applications and infrastructure.

6. Collaboration: Collaborate with development teams to
ensure smooth deployment of applications and provide
feedback on application design and architecture.

7. Monitoring and Logging: Develop and implement monitoring
and logging strategies to ensure visibility into application
and infrastructure performance.

8. Security: Ensure that our cloud infrastructure and
applications are secure, compliant with industry standards,
and meet regulatory requirements.

9. Cost Optimization: Optimize cloud costs and ensure that
our infrastructure is running efficiently and effectively.

10. Documentation: Develop and maintain documentation on our
cloud infrastructure, release management processes, and
operational procedures.

Requirements:

1. Technical Expertise: 5+ years of experience in cloud
computing, with a focus on Kubernetes and containerization.

2. Leadership Experience: 2+ years of experience in building
and managing high-performing teams.

3. Cloud Platforms: Experience with cloud platforms such as
AWS, GCP, or Azure.

4. Kubernetes: Experience with Kubernetes, including cluster
management, deployment strategies, and troubleshooting.

5. Release Management: Experience with release management
tools and processes, such as GitOps, CI/CD pipelines, and
automated deployment.

6. Operations: Experience with operational processes and
procedures, including monitoring, logging, and security.

7. Communication Skills: Excellent communication skills,
with the ability to communicate technical concepts to non-
technical stakeholders.

8. Collaboration: Experience collaborating with development
teams and other stakeholders to ensure smooth deployment of
applications.

9. Problem-Solving: Strong problem-solving skills, with the
ability to troubleshoot complex technical issues.

10. Adaptability: Ability to adapt to changing technical
requirements and priorities.

Nice to Have:

1. Certifications: Kubernetes certifications, such as CKA or
CKAD.

2. Cloud Certifications: Cloud certifications, such as AWS
Certified DevOps Engineer or GCP Certified Professional
Cloud Developer.

3. Experience with other cloud-native technologies:
Experience with other cloud-native technologies, such as
serverless computing, service mesh, or cloud-native
databases.

4. Experience with agile development methodologies:
Experience with agile development methodologies, such as
Scrum or Kanban.
