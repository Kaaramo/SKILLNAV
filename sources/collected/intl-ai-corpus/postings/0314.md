## Description complète

ABOUT CORDIAL

We founded Cordial in 2014 on the belief that there should
be more humanity and empathy in marketing—both in how brands
communicate with their customers and in how technology
companies work with brands. We built our company and
platform purposefully, driven by a desire to inspire more
thoughtful communication and to create experiences that feel
more personal and human—for consumers, for the people at the
companies we work with, and for Cordial employees. Today,
brands like PacSun, Revolve, Abercrombie & Fitch,
Realtor.com, L.L. Bean and Forbes rely on Cordial to drive
revenue growth by sending a better message.

We chose the name Cordial to symbolize how we empower our
clients to communicate with their customers, as well as how
we do business: with transparency, collaboration, and trust.
We're building a passionate team of individuals willing to
learn, grow, and be thoughtfully challenged on a daily basis
to continuously improve our product, company, and culture
every single day.

OUR VALUES

- Communicate better than the rest
- Own it, every time
- Solve client problems tenaciously
- Make Waves

POSITION SUMMARY

We are looking for an experienced and ambitious builder to
join our team in the role of Applied AI Engineer. In this
role, you will be a primary contributor to our suite of AI
capabilities within the Cordial platform. You will work to
pull in frontier AI work to help our customers be more
efficient and leveraged within the platform. YOU WILL

- Be a primary contributor to our AI strategy, helping design
  and productize AI-powered solutions
- You’ll dive into massive datasets with the autonomy to
  choose what you work on and the ownership to turn ideas
  into real-world results for our clients.
- Serve as an expert sounding board on approaches and
  techniques for integrating LLMs into production
  applications
- Work in partnership with product management to drive
  compelling AI solutions and features
- Continuously explore and implement innovative approaches
  using frontier AI models to solve complex business
  challenges

ABOUT YOU

- 2+ years of professional experience building production
  applications with LLMs and AI technologies
- 5+ years experience with Python
- Track record of shipping and maintaining production services
  at scale
- A deep curiosity and excitement for where AI and applied AI
  systems are headed
- Experience building with LLMs (OpenAI, Anthropic, etc),
  prompt engineering, and RAG architectures
- Experience with LLM orchestration frameworks (LangChain,
  llamaindex)
- Experience with REST API development, NoSQL database design,
  and RDBMS design and optimizations
- Comfortable working in ambiguous environments and defining
  solutions to open-ended problems
