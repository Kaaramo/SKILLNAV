Job title: Data and AI Engineer

In this role, you

shape the digital platform of Philips Service Parts Supply
Chain with a focus on Artificial Intelligence (AI)
implementations, connecting /integrating elements such as
workflow applications, process automation, Control Tower,
and supplier collaboration applications.

You are responsible for

- Co-developing and implementing Service Parts Supply Chain
  (SPS) Digital Platform Strategy & Roadmap in collaboration
  with stakeholders.
- Securing a data and digital platform architecture that
  enables integration with existing applications and
  extensions with new technologies, especially with AI.
- Ensuring SPS data and digital platform architecture is
  documented and well understood by key stakeholders.
- Securing the right software quality through the execution of
  the software validation process as required.
- Acting as a point of contact in case of data and digital
  platform questions at the business level.
- Leading, designing, and co-developing AI solutions and other
  applications in collaboration with IT and other partners
  to enhance customer experience and ensure operational
  excellence while safeguarding SPS processes.
- Leading specific investigation and deployment projects.
- Supporting business and IT by sharing knowledge, experience
  and perspective.
- Supporting business with data and digital platform issues.
- Supporting business in clean-up following incident
  resolution.

You are a part of

- Philips Circular Lifetime Solutions organization
- Service Parts Supply Chain group
- Supply Chain Architecture team

Service Parts Supply Chain (SPS) is the single service parts
provider within Philips Healthcare. SPS is responsible for
the total service parts supply chain from the
factory/suppliers to the regions. The strategy is to
maximize the service part availability, minimize the total
cost of operation and minimize the inventory level. To
realize this strategy, SPS operates a global service parts
network, including supply management, strategic planning,
supply chain execution systems, forward Logistics
(warehouse/distribution), Business Partner Management,
reverse logistics and IT tools.

To succeed in this role, you should have the following
skills and experience

- Bachelor's / Master's Degree in Industrial Engineering,
  Operations, Supply Chain Management, Software Engineering
  or equivalent.
- 8+ years of Experience with and passion for data and digital
  platform architecture, and AI implementations, preferably
  in the Supply Chain domain.
- Strategic thinking, systems thinking and creativity.
- (8+ years) Hands-on experience in programming and databases,
  including scripting.
- Experience in software validation preferred.
- Able to work on multiple projects at once at both an
  individual and team level.
- Result oriented, persuasive, encouraging and motivating, and
  able to drive performance across functions.
- Fluent in English (verbal and written).
- Strong communication and presentation skills, and ability to
  interact effectively with colleagues on management and
  leadership level.
- Proven global skills; experience in a cross-cultural
  environment teamwork.
- Project management skills.
- Good prioritization skills, keeping what is most important
  to the success of the business always in view.
