The Opportunity

The SandboxAQ Cybersecurity R&D team is a diverse group
consisting of ML experts, cryptographers, mathematicians,
and physicists. We are not another security vendor chasing

The Cybersecurity R&D team is looking for a Full Stack
Software Engineer, AI to join our team. This role is central
to our efforts to: transform deep technical insights into
products that empower users and keep the world's AI safer.

You will own major pieces of our product, from sleek user
interfaces to resilient backend systems, working at the
intersection of innovation, craftsmanship, and impact. A
successful candidate will thrive in a fast-paced
environment, possessing the curiosity of a researcher and
the creativity of a hacker to implement features across the
full stack.
