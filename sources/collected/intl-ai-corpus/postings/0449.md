## Description complète

About WorkWhile WorkWhile is on a mission to help workers
earn a better living and live better lives. More than 80
million Americans work hourly jobs, often with unpredictable
schedules that pay close to minimum wage and offer limited
benefits, leading to financial instability.

WorkWhile is revolutionizing frontline work by creating a
more flexible and efficient labor market. Powered by AI, the
WorkWhile platform helps businesses quickly access high-
quality talent. Workers benefit from same-day pay, free
telehealth services, upskilling, and the ability to choose
shifts that match their skills and schedules.

We have the audacious goal to align incentives and solve
inefficiencies in the labor market, disrupting the $650
billion staffing and recruiting market. Backed by Khosla
Ventures and recognized by the Inc. 5000 for 10x growth,
WorkWhile is reshaping the future of work.

About the role

We are looking for a software engineer to lead the design
and deployment of AI-powered automation and intelligence
within our platform. This role sits at the intersection of
AI, product, and engineering, focusing on applying LLMs and
AI agents to streamline operational workflows, improve
worker-employer matching, and enhance the overall user
experience.

What you'll do

- Collaborate with founders, sales, design and other engineers
  to propose and implement solutions for our users - workers
  and businesses
- Build, ship and iterate on LLM-backed product features and
  internal evals to continually ensure quality
- Build, ship and iterate on LLM-backed agentic workflows,
  leveraging tools, resources, etc.

What we're looking for

- Experience building and deploying AI applications in
  production
- Strong proficiency in Python and familiarity with AI
  frameworks and APIs
- Understanding of AI infrastructure optimization and
  improving performance and output quality of LLM systems
- Passion for using AI to solve real-world problems and
  improving economic opportunities for hourly workers

Engineering @ WorkWhile

- We’re a small and mighty team with senior engineers from
  Rippling, Stripe, DoorDash, Faire among others
- We value user-centricity, competence, speed, and having fun

  yet are also great team players
- We deal in outcomes, not projects
- Read more about our team at https://tech.workwhile.ai/

  Toronto

WorkWhile has a diverse, mission-driven, and supportive
