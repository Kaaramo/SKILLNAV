This role is hands-on ML focused on 3D computer vision and
generative models for avatar creation. The role involves
designing, training, fine-tuning, and deploying ML models
using PyTorch/TensorFlow for 3D asset synthesis, mesh
generation, and graphics. While it works with AI for content
generation, it's centered on traditional ML/DL frameworks
and computer graphics rather than LLMs or agents, making it
ml-first.
