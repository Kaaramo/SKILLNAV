## Description complète

Amex GBT is a place where colleagues find inspiration in
travel as a force for good and – through their work – can
make an impact on our industry. We’re here to help our
colleagues achieve success and offer an inclusive and
collaborative culture where your voice is valued.

Amex GBT seeks an innovative AI/ML Solutions Engineer to
drive transformative artificial intelligence strategies and
implementations across our global enterprise. What You'll Do

- Develop end-to-end Data Science and Machine Learning
  solutions to support business imperatives – internal and
  external; descriptive and prescriptive.
- Be a hands-on guide; partner with business and technology
  customers to design and implement scalable big data
  solutions using AI/ML.
- Scan and capitalize on new products, platforms, processes,
  and algorithms which can bolster traveler, supplier,
  travel manager, travel consultant experience.
- Be passionate to develop, teach, learn, operationalize, and
  scale citizen decision science.

What We're Looking For

- Expertise with MLOPs and Big data solutions.
- Subject matter knowledge in statistics, analytics, big data,
  data science, machine learning, deep learning, cloud and
  full stack technologies.
- Hands-on experience analyzing large amounts of data to
  derive actionable insights
- The candidate is expected to have expertise in MLOPs,
  Python, Machine Learning, Cloud platforms(AWS/Azure/GCP),
  Gen AI, Hive, CI/CD pipelines (GitHub Actions, Jenkins,
  GitLab), Docker containerization for Flutter apps and
  Kubernetes mobile app deployment
- Good communication skills and partnerships with technology,
  product strategy, and data office.
- Experience collaborating and influencing senior leaders,
  business partners across time zones.
- Self-starter and autonomous; work with a distributed team
  and demonstrate a strong sense of ownership
- Degree or equivalent experience in statistics, computer
  science, mathematics, operations research, economics,
  engineering or information science
- A strong portfolio of GitHub, Kaggle, IP contributions.
- The candidate will be a member of the AI/ML and the Data
  Science team and will support building solutions in the
  big data, data science, data quality and ML space.
- An ideal candidate will have a growth-oriented, lean-startup
  attitude for deploying Data science and Machine Learning
  solutions .
