This is a DevOps/infrastructure role that supports AI
systems rather than building them directly. The role focuses
on cloud infrastructure, CI/CD pipelines, containerization,
and monitoring - all enabling work for the AI and Product
teams who build the actual AI features. The engineer
maintains the platform that AI runs on, but doesn't work on
models, agents, RAG, or ML systems themselves.

## Description complète

At Libra, we're transforming how legal professionals work.
Our AI platform combines deep legal reasoning with cutting-
edge generative technology to help lawyers research, draft,
and deliver legal work faster and smarter.

In just over a year, we became one of Europe's fastest-
growing legal AI companies—and in late 2025, we joined
Wolters Kluwer, the global leader in legal information
services. Together, we're combining Libra's AI capabilities
with Wolters Kluwer's authoritative legal content to build
the most powerful AI workspace for lawyers in Europe. We've
already launched in the Netherlands and Poland, and are
expanding across new markets with full localization and deep
local content integrations.

This is a unique opportunity to shape the next generation of
legal AI products—now at true European scale.

Role Overview

As a DevOps Engineer (m/w/d) at Libra, you’ll play a central
role in building, automating, and scaling the infrastructure
behind products trusted by top law firms and legal teams
across Europe. You’ll design and maintain robust, secure
systems that ensure our platform is fast, stable, and
compliant — while empowering our engineering teams to ship
quickly and confidently.

This is a high-impact role for engineers who thrive at the
intersection of reliability, security, and automation.
You’ll work hybrid with our team at the Merantix AI Campus
in Berlin, collaborating closely with our AI, Product, and
Security teams to ensure that Libra’s infrastructure scales
as fast as our customer base.

What You'll Do:

- Design, implement, and maintain Libra’s cloud infrastructure
  on Open Telekom Cloud (OTC), ensuring security,
  reliability, and scalability.
- Automate infrastructure setup and management using Ansible
  and Terraform.
- Build and optimize CI/CD pipelines with Jenkins, enabling
  fast, stable deployments.
- Containerize applications with Docker and manage
  orchestration across environments.
- Set up and maintain observability systems using Grafana and
  Prometheus to monitor system health and performance.
- Collaborate with developers to ensure seamless integration
  of infrastructure and application layers.
- Drive continuous improvement in performance, resilience, and
  security.
- Ensure compliance with the highest standards of data
  protection and reliability for enterprise-grade customers.

What You Bring:

- Strong experience managing cloud infrastructure on at least
  one major provider (e.g., Open Telekom Cloud, AWS, Azure,
  or GCP).
- Deep knowledge of Docker, Ansible, and Terraform for
  automation and orchestration.
- Experience with Jenkins or similar CI/CD tools.
- Proficiency in setting up monitoring and observability
  stacks (Grafana, Prometheus).
- Strong understanding of security best practices and
  compliance requirements.
- Excellent communication skills in English; German is a plus.
- Entrepreneurial mindset with a strong sense of urgency;
  self-starter who works independently while aligning to
  team goals.

What We Offer:

- Permanent employment from day one.
- Remote work & flexibility: Work remotely up to 3 days per
  week (home office) (= 8 days a month in the office) with
  flexible working hours.
- Work abroad flexibly: Work from anywhere within the EU for
  up to 20 days within a twelve-month period.
- Rest & time off: 26 vacation days.
- Show your commitment: 1 additional day off per year for your
  volunteer work (Volunteer Day).
- Support for development: E-learning via LinkedIn, online
  language training with goFluent, and other training and
  development opportunities.

Join us, at Wolters Kluwer, and be part of a global
technology company that makes a difference every day. Be the
difference. If making a difference matters to you, then you
matter to us.
