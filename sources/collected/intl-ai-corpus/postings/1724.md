This is a pre-sales Solutions Engineer role focused on the
AI Security portfolio, working with GTM to drive adoption
and sales. The role involves technical discovery,
positioning, executing POVs, and creating enablement assets
rather than building, training, fine-tuning, or deploying AI
models directly. While there is hands-on integration
experience with AI models, it's for demonstration,
prototyping, and supporting customer implementations rather
than developing AI systems.

About Netskope

Today, there's more data and users outside the enterprise
than inside, causing the network perimeter as we know it to
dissolve. We realized a new perimeter was needed, one that
is built in the cloud and follows and protects data wherever
it goes, so we started Netskope to redefine Cloud, Network
and Data Security.

Since 2012, we have built the market-leading cloud security
company and an award-winning culture powered by hundreds of
employees spread across offices in Santa Clara, St. Louis,
Bangalore, London, Paris, Melbourne, Taipei, and Tokyo. Our
core values are openness, honesty, and transparency, and we
purposely developed our open desk layouts and large meeting
spaces to support and promote partnerships, collaboration,
and teamwork. From catered lunches and office celebrations
to employee recognition events and social professional
groups such as the Awesome Women of Netskope (AWON), we
strive to keep work fun, supportive and interactive.  Visit
us at Netskope Careers. Please follow us on LinkedIn and
Twitter@Netskope.

About the Role:

As a founding member of the AI Consulting Engineering team,
you will be responsible to work closely with our GTM to
drive the adoption and ultimately sales of Netskope AI
Security portfolio of products.

Responsibilities:

Pre-Sale & Sales Activities:

- Assist the sales teams on technical discovery and
  positioning of Netskope AI Security portfolio of products
  with customers and prospects
- Prepare, initiate and execute POV’s (Proof of Value) with
  the aim of achieving technical win and ultimately winning
  the business.
- Work with the account teams to create a vision for prospects
  and customers that aligns with the overall Netskope vision
  but
- Help create a strategic vision for customers based on deep
  understanding of their AI strategy, expectations and
  required capabilities.
- Work with existing customers to understand efficacy and
  operational improvements that can be implemented

Evangelize the technology internally and externally:

- Enable the field on how to engage, qualify and position the
  portfolio through presentations, creation of assets and
  documentation
- Help create labs and other forms of enablement for the
  Systems Engineering team so they can engage and
  confidently present AI solutions to customers and
  prospects
- Engage with customers and partners through the Netskope
  Community to answer questions and create necessary
  material like articles and presentations

Partner with Product Management:

- Work with Product Management to help enhance the products
  based on requirements captured in the field and
  enhancements that promote better adoption and operation of
  those products.
- Help test, experiment and document new products and releases
  related to the AI portfolio and work with Product
  Management and Engineering on fixes and improvements

Requirements:

- At least 2 years of experience in pre-sales
- Previous experience in a technical pre-sales or consulting
  position with cybersecurity company
- Previous experience in an AI startup (cybersecurity is a
  plus) where you had constant customer and sales-
  interactions (SE, SA, Implementation Engineer)
- Good cloud computing experience in any of the three major
  Hyperscalers (AWS, Azure or GCP)
- Knowledge and hands on integration experience with relevant
  AI models (OpenAI, Anthophic, Meta, Microsoft Copilot,
  Google Gemini and AWS models) including prototyping and
  implementation.
- Understanding of AI prompt best practices and security
  testing is a plus.
- Strong full-stack development skills with the ability of
  testing integrations, supporting customers on
  implementation
- Experience building enablement assets through documentation,
  articles, videos and presentations
- Good presentation skills and experience presenting to
  technical and executive audiences
- Travel up to 50% of the time

Education:

- Bachelor's or Master's in computer science, engineering,
  mathematics, or related fields preferred
