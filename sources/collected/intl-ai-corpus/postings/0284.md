## Description complète

AI at Curai

At Curai, we believe that access to high-quality healthcare
is a fundamental human right, not a privilege. Our mission
is to radically transform healthcare delivery by harnessing
the power of artificial intelligence and clinical expertise
to make care more affordable, accessible, and effective for
everyone.  Patients interact with advanced AI systems at
every step of their care and follow-up. Licensed physicians
review each case with the patient for the clinical decision
in our integrated virtual clinic.

We ground our approach in rigorous research, continuous
learning, and a deep commitment to clinical integrity. We
focus on making a real impact: improving health outcomes,
expanding access to care, and setting new standards for what
trustworthy, patient-centered healthcare is. Read some of
our latest publications.

Who You Are

None of these individually are hard requirements but they do
describe the type of folks that we think would be most
effective and happy at Curai.

• Passionate about advancing AI in healthcare to create
meaningful impact and expand access to care.

• A self-starter with a growth mindset, eager to learn and
take initiative.

• Skilled at tackling complex, multifaceted problems using a
scientific and data-driven approach.

• Thoughtful in evaluating trade-offs, balancing immediate
needs with Curai’s long-term strategic goals.

• Strong at prioritizing and focusing on the most impactful
work; comfortable with ambiguity and challenging
assumptions.

• Prefer fast-moving, collaborative projects over extensive
solo efforts.

What You’ll Do

• Design, build, deploy and improve advanced ML and
generative AI (e.g. large language model) solutions for
patient and provider-facing products (e.g., conversational
AI, personalization, user understanding, clinical decision
support).

• Lead end-to-end AI initiatives from data exploration,
modeling, evaluation, A/B testing, deployment, and
continuous monitoring.

• Collaborate cross-functionally with clinicians,
researchers, engineers, product managers, data scientists,
and designers to drive product improvements.

• Mentor and provide technical guidance to interns and
junior team members, fostering a culture of learning and
innovation.

What You’ll Need

• 3+ years of industry experience building and deploying
robust ML/AI models for user-facing or data-driven products.

• Experienced with the full ML lifecycle: data analysis,
model development, evaluation, deployment, and monitoring.

• Hands-on experience building AI systems using large
language modelsProficient in Python and production-quality
software engineering practices.

• Skilled in working with real-world data, especially
healthcare data is a huge plus.

• Effective collaborator and communicator in fast-paced,
cross-functional teams.

• MS, or PhD in Computer Science, Engineering, or a related
field, or equivalent practical experience.

What We Offer
