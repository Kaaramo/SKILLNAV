About Company

Cracken is a fast-growing Silicon Valley-based startup built

and led teams at Apple, Google, Palo Alto Networks,

We tame Cracken, an AI Agent for Cybersecurity with human-

governments.

We are growing and seeking an AI/ML Engineer to help us
