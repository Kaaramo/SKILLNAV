## Description complète

About Anthropic

Anthropic’s mission is to create reliable, interpretable,
and steerable AI systems. We want AI to be safe and
beneficial for our users and for society as a whole. Our
team is a quickly growing group of committed researchers,
engineers, policy experts, and business leaders working
together to build beneficial AI systems.

About the Role

We're building Claude into the AI that everyone uses at
work. Not a demo, not a chatbot sidebar — a tool that
professionals across industries rely on every day to do
substantive work. We're looking for experienced software
engineers to join the Enterprise & Verticals team, where we
build the products that make that real.

This is a rare engineering role where you don't just build
on top of a model — you shape the model itself. Every
engineer on this team works directly with Anthropic's
research organization, including post-training, to make
Claude better at the specific tasks your products need to
solve. Whether you're working on general enterprise tooling
or a specific industry vertical, you'll identify capability
gaps, collaborate with researchers on domain-specific
improvements, and see those improvements ship in your
products.

This team owns two complementary surfaces:

Enterprise AI Products. General-purpose features that make
Claude useful for any knowledge worker — products like
Cowork, plugins, skills, and the connective tissue that lets
Claude operate effectively across workflows. A big part of
this work is understanding why users aren't activating,
what's blocking adoption, and building the capabilities that
close those gaps. You'll work on the systems that make
Claude a daily-use tool for enterprise customers across
industries.

Vertical AI Products. Purpose-built experiences for specific
industries where Claude can transform complex professional
workflows. We're currently building for three verticals,
with more to come:

- Financial Services — from data retrieval and analysis to
  institutional-quality report generation, building AI
  experiences that meet the standards of the world's largest
  financial institutions.
- Healthcare — from clinical documentation and prior
  authorization to care coordination and patient management,
  solving critical challenges for healthcare systems while
  ensuring accuracy and compliance.
- Legal — from document review and contract analysis to
  regulatory compliance and matter management, building
  tools that handle the precision, confidentiality, and
  nuance that legal work demands.

Representative Projects

- Build an Office add-in that lets Claude read, analyze, and
  generate content within Excel and Word — operating within
  the constraints of host application sandboxes and third-
  party platform APIs
- Design the plugins and skills infrastructure that lets
  Claude learn new domain-specific capabilities — from
  financial modeling templates to clinical documentation
  workflows — and make them discoverable and composable for
  enterprise users
- Build the customization and distribution layer to allow
  enterprise power users capture, refine, and share their
  enhanced workflows.
- Develop a document generation pipeline that produces
  institutional-quality investment research reports,
  translating Claude's analytical capabilities into outputs
  that meet the formatting, sourcing, and compliance
  standards of a top-tier bank
- Instrument and diagnose activation funnels to understand why
  enterprise users sign up but don't adopt Claude for daily
  work, then build the product changes that close those gaps
- Design the context and memory architecture that lets
  multiple Claude agents working across different product
  surfaces — Cowork, chat, Office add-ins, vertical tools —
  share a unified understanding of a user's projects,
  preferences, and in-flight work so that nothing falls
  through the cracks as people move between tools
- Identify high-toil workflows that enterprise users repeat
  daily — reformatting reports, reconciling data across
  systems, triaging inbound requests — and apply new model
  capabilities to automate or eliminate them, measuring the
  impact on time saved and user retention
- Build hybrid workflow systems where Claude agents and people
  collaborate asynchronously on complex tasks — for example,
  an agent that drafts a first-pass contract review
  overnight, flags items that need human judgment, and
  routes those decisions back through a structured interface
  so the attorney can review and approve in minutes rather
  than hours
- Collaborate with post-training researchers to improve
  Claude's performance on clinical documentation tasks,
  identifying specific failure modes in patient note
  summarization and helping design training data to address
  them

You May Be a Good Fit If You

- Have strong full-stack engineering skills with experience
  building products in enterprise or B2B contexts
- Care as much about whether users activate and get value as
  you do about the underlying systems — you're product-
  minded and user-oriented
- Have shipped products that real customers depend on, and are
  comfortable working across the stack (frontend, backend,
  APIs, integrations) and navigating ambiguity in an early-
  stage product environment
- Communicate effectively and can engage credibly with
  enterprise stakeholders — whether a portfolio manager, a
  general counsel, or a hospital CIO — and translate their
  needs into engineering work
- Work effectively across functions in a fast-moving
  organization

Especially Valuable Experience

- For Enterprise AI Products: Experience building developer
  tools, productivity applications, or platforms where
  adoption and activation are core challenges. Familiarity
  with skills, plugins, or extensibility systems.
- For Office Agents: Experience building Office add-ins,
  browser extensions, or plugins that integrate with
  existing productivity software. Familiarity with the
  Office JavaScript API, VSTO, COM, or similar frameworks
  for manipulating spreadsheets, documents, or presentations
  programmatically.
- For Financial Services: Experience with financial data
  providers, trading systems, or capital markets technology.
  Track record building systems that handle sensitive
  financial data, financial modeling, or document
  generation.
- For Healthcare & Life Sciences: Experience with clinical
  workflows or products for health records and clinical
  documentation. Familiarity with healthcare compliance
  requirements (HIPAA, HL7/FHIR, etc.).
- For Legal: Experience building legal technology products —
  document review, contract lifecycle management, compliance
  systems, or practice management platforms.
