Company Overview:

For more than 50 years, NISC has worked to develop
technology solutions for our customers, who we call our
“Members”. Those Members are comprised primarily of 960+
energy (utility) and communication (broadband) organizations
across North America. Our mission is to deliver technology
solutions and services that are Member-focused, quality
driven and valued priced. Our Members have over 18 million
end customers (residential and businesses who receive power,
internet, television and/or telephone services) that our
enterprise software solution enables our Member's employees
to compete effectively in the industry, while excelling in
providing customer service to their end customers.  

NISC exists to serve our Members and help them serve their
communities through our innovative software products,
services and outstanding customer support. We are an AI-
forward company committed to being a technology leader in
our industry. NISC has been ranked in ComputerWorld’s Best
Places to Work for 23 years, and we are looking for
qualified individuals to join our team. 

Position Overview:

We are looking for a high-agency, AI-native Software
Engineer who loves building real-world AI systems that make
a measurable difference.

This is not a research role. This is not a “play with models
in a lab” role. This is a builder role.

You will design, build, ship, and continuously improve
production-grade AI applications that are embedded directly
into mission-critical software used by utilities and
broadband providers across North America. Your work will
power customer service interactions, operational workflows,
and intelligent automation at scale.

We are looking for someone who:

- Gets excited about taking AI from idea → prototype →
  production → scale
- Loves experimenting with new models, frameworks, and agentic
  patterns
- Has a “there’s a way” mindset when solving hard technical
  problems
- Believes AI should drive tangible business impact, not just
  technical novelty
- Thrives in ambiguity and moves fast while maintaining
  engineering discipline

At NISC, AI is not a side initiative — it is a core
strategic priority. You will help shape how AI transforms
the way our Members serve their communities.

If you’ve built and deployed AI systems that real users
depend on — and you want to do it in an organization that
values ownership, innovation, and meaningful impact — we
encourage you to apply.
