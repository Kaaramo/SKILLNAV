## Description complète

Who We Are

Nava is on a mission to #fixhealthcare. Nearly 160M
Americans rely on their employers for healthcare — yet the
system is broken, bloated, and dominated by incumbents who
resist change. Nava fuses deep benefits expertise with
cutting-edge technology to deliver a modern, transparent,
and affordable healthcare experience.

Founded by seasoned entrepreneurs and backed by leading
investors, Nava is one of the fastest-growing benefits
brokerages in the country. We’re the first to combine
brokerage know-how with proprietary tech like HQ: our AI-
powered benefits platform, designed to help HR teams take
control of renewals, simplify strategy, eliminate
spreadsheet chaos, and empower employees to navigate their
benefits with ease.

In a $50B industry hungry for change, Nava is built to win —
lowering costs for employers, delighting employees, and
reshaping how healthcare works for millions of Americans.

🧠 Key Responsibilities:

- Work with product, engineering, and design to identify
  customer and team pain points and build generative AI
  solutions using software engineering best practices
- Work across the full stack - frontend, backend, and
  infrastructure - integrating AI capabilities directly into
  applications
- Build intelligent search and retrieval systems that help
  users find relevant information quickly
- Design effective interactions with foundation models (GPT-4,
  Claude, Gemini) to achieve reliable behaviors
- Ship AI-powered features with the same reliability and
  performance standards as any other application components

🖊️ Preferred Experience:

- 5+ years developing modern full-stack web applications on
  client- and server- side web applications with Python and
  JavaScript/TypeScript.
- Hands-on experience building applications with foundation
  models through direct API integration
- Proven ability to work with product, engineering, and design
  teams to identify user problems and translate them into
  technical solutions, leading the initiative for how to
  build delightful AI (generative) solutions
- Comfortable working across the full application stack
- Strong communication skills for working with cross-
  functional teams that are not on the technology side of
  the business
- An understanding of how to use tool calling, multi agents,
  etc. and some excitement about MCPs
- Working experience with Machine Learning (PyTorch, scikit-
  learn), training/deploying models with proprietary or Open
  Source models

☄️ Our Tech Stack:

- Frontend: React / Vite / Next.js
- Backend: Node / Nest.js / Express.js
- API: REST / Graphql
- AI: OpenAI / Anthropic / different AI platforms, Langfuse
- Database: Postgres
- Data Platforms: Salesforce, Airbyte, Dagster, Parabola, DBT
- Infrastructure: AWS
- Dev AI assistants: GitHub Copilot, Cursor, Claude Code

Working at Nava

As a remote-first company, Nava is committed to building a
dynamic and inclusive culture where you have the autonomy to
thrive. You’ll be supported by cutting-edge technology, a
collaborative team, and a shared mission to revolutionize
healthcare.

Benefits

- Health & Wellness: Medical insurance, including a $0 premium
  HDHP plan for individuals, Dental, Vision, Telehealth and
  Virtual Primary Care Visits, OneMedical membership,
  Wellhub, and a $50 monthly benefit to improve your mental
  or physical health.
- Financial Support: Stock options, 401(k) with a $2,000
  match, FSA, HSA, and access to Origin Financial.
- Work-Life: Flexible PTO, 12 weeks of paid parental leave
  with 2 weeks of transition time, $50 monthly connectivity
  reimbursement, and company-paid short-term disability,
  long-term disability, and life insurance, with additional
  voluntary benefits like supplemental life, AD&D, hospital
  indemnity, accident, and critical illness coverage.
- Professional Support: $1,000 home office setup stipend and
  access to an Employee Assistance Program (EAP)

believe that solving America's healthcare problem requires
leveraging America's greatest strength: our diversity.
