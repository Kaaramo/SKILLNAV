Ready to be pushed beyond what you think you’re capable of?

At Coinbase, our mission is to increase economic freedom in

To achieve our mission, we’re seeking a very specific

mission and who believes in the power of crypto and

want someone who is eager to leave their mark on the world,

Our work culture is intense and isn’t for everyone. But if
you want to build the future alongside others who excel in
their disciplines and expect the same from you, there’s no
better place to be.

While many roles at Coinbase are remote-first, we are not
remote-only. In-person participation is required throughout
the year. Team and company-wide offsites are held multiple
times annually to foster collaboration, connection, and
alignment. Attendance is expected and fully supported.

At Coinbase, our mission is to increase economic freedom in
the world. To achieve this, we are becoming an "AI-first"
company and fundamentally reimagining how a global financial
institution operates. AI is at the center of this strategy,
driving our core processes while human experts serve as the
"function calls" that the system consults for high-level
judgment.

The Role

We are looking for a visionary, AI-forward Principal
Software Engineer to serve as the technical North Star for
our AI Automation efforts. You will build Agentic AI systems
that will power our multiple org-wide initiatives and their
primitives, orchestration layers, and the tools that will
enable every team at Coinbase to automate their workflows.
You will report to the VP, Engineering and will operate with
the highest level of autonomy to define what a fully AI-
driven company looks like from the ground up.

What You’ll Do

- Architect Agentic Systems: Design and build sophisticated
  multi-agent architectures that move beyond simple chat to
  autonomous execution and complex reasoning.
- Solve the "Hard Problems" of AI: Define and implement
  reliable evaluation frameworks, eliminate hallucinations
  through rigorous data grounding, and build deep
  observability into LLM reasoning chains.
- Build the Core AI Platform: Develop the underlying
  primitives and orchestration tools that will allow the
  rest of the org to adopt AI, improving developer
  productivity and operational efficiency across the entire
  organization.
- Lead the AI-First Transition: Partner with leadership to
  identify high-impact automation opportunities across
  Product Foundations and Core functions, ensuring our AI
  strategy creates compounding leverage.
- Bridge Engineering and AI: Act as a "Software Engineer with
  deep AI expertise"—applying world-class backend
  engineering principles (scalability, reliability,
  performance) to the bleeding edge of Generative AI.
- Mentor and Elevate: Serve as a mentor to senior ICs and
  engineering teams, raising the bar for AI literacy and
  engineering excellence across the organization.

What We Look For

- Deep AI/LLM Expertise: Extensive experience working with
  LLMs, RAG, and Agentic frameworks. You understand the
  nuances of prompt engineering, fine-tuning, and the
  current limitations of frontier models.
- Backend Engineering Mastery: You are a seasoned Software
  Engineer at heart. You can build the robust, scalable
  systems required to put AI into production at a global
  scale.
- Proven Strategic Leadership: Experience operating with
  organizational level impact, leading technical strategy
  across multiple domains.
- Visionary Mindset: You are 18 months ahead of the industry
  curve. You can see how AI will transform business
  processes and have the technical chops to build that
  future today.
- Focus on Reliability: A passion for solving the "unsolved"
  problems of AI: evaluation, grounding, and deterministic
  outcomes in non-deterministic systems.

Nice to Have

- Multi-Agentic Architecture: Specific experience building or
  researching systems where multiple AI agents collaborate
  to solve complex tasks.
- Fintech or Compliance Background: Experience navigating the
  complexities of highly regulated environments.
- Open Source Contributions: Active contributions to the AI/ML
  community or major LLM frameworks (LangChain, AutoGPT,
  etc.).
