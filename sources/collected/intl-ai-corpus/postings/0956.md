## Description complète

Hey — I’m Rishabh, co-founder & COO at Flent. I lead all
things tech, platform, and cashflow here.

I feel the Flent is a lot more than a grid of homes on an
app. We are now building a platform for living.

Renting in REAL life is extremely messy. Most people come
with fuzzy requirements - “something close to my office,
maybe ₹60k budget, should’ve good ventilation, non-smoking
flatmates”. And on the supply side, every home is a new SKU
with 100+ attributes. Traditional marketplace interfaces and
journeys will never work.

I think of our platform as a human broker in a software form
- one that listens, interprets, and guides, rather than
dumping endless choices. It should be able to explain “why
this home” makes sense for you, do the trade-offs you’d
otherwise struggle with, and build trust through speed and
clarity.

Good software makes choices smooth. Great software makes
them vanish into a flow – that’s the bar I hold while
building things at Flent.

About the role

We’re hiring our foundation engineering squad — the team
that’ll lay the technical bedrock of Flent.

You’ll work with product and design to build Flent’s digital
renting ecosystem. Whether it’s search, payments, or real-
time notifications, your code will power how thousands of
people find and manage their homes. We are looking for AI-
native engineers who can multiply output-scaffold, test, and
refactor faster, while keeping quality and security high.

More than anything, this is your chance to curate the kind
of engineering culture you always wished for, built around
people who care about doing their best work.

What you’ll broadly do:

- Architect the AI Broker You will own the strategic direction
  of our conversational search platform. Your goal is to
  move beyond filters, designing the core logic that
  understands renter intent and delivers curated,
  explainable home matches.
- Build Spatial Home Tours You will lead the creation of a
  new-to-market virtual tour experience. This involves
  architecting the spatial interface from the ground up,
  capturing user interaction data within the 3D space, and
  using those signals to create a smarter, self-improving
  discovery engine.
- Build the "Flent for Living" Platform You will be
  responsible for the entire resident-facing ecosystem. This
  includes the end-to-end development of features like
  integrated payments, smart access control, maintenance
  ticketing, and lifestyle service upgrades.
- Define Foundational Engineering Culture As a member of the
  founding engineering team, you will establish the
  technical standards, security practices, and development
  methodologies that will define how Flent builds products
  for years to come.

What your day-to-day will look like:

- Develop backend APIs and services (Node/TS, Go, Python,
  Postgres, Redis, GraphQL/gRPC)
- Build and optimize responsive UIs and 3D interactions
  (React/Next.js, WebGL, Three.js)
- Integrate and productionize AI/NLP modules and ML-driven
  recommendation systems
- Implement authentication, authorization, validation,
  monitoring, and logging
- Profile, debug, and optimize 3D rendering, database, and API
  performance
- Automate deployments, run tests, maintain CI/CD, manage
  feature flags
- Analyze metrics, design/run A/B tests, iterate on user
  feedback
- Review code, document technical decisions and run PR
  feedback rituals.

We’re looking for someone who has:

- Has 4–6 years of experience in software engineering, ideally
  across high-growth startups.
- Is a product-minded builder — raises the bar beyond
  functionality, prioritizing UX, performance, and clarity.
- fast adaptability — moves across backend, frontend, AI, and
  3D; learns new tools quickly.
- Strong frontend chops: React/Next.js, modern state
  management, WebGL/Three.js for 3D, and performance
  budgets.
- Brings backend strength — comfortable with at least one of
  Node/TS, Go, or Python; Postgres; has some experience with
  Redis; queues/streams; caching; auth; rate-limiting;
  infra-as-code; and cloud (AWS/GCP).
- Is AI-native — uses copilots/agents for scaffolding, test
  writing, code search, and safe refactors — and knows when
  not to.
- Understands data & retrieval — familiar with embeddings/RAG,
  vector stores (pgvector/others), and building
  ranking/feedback loops.
- Practices strong engineering hygiene — testing strategy
  (unit/integration/property), static analysis, secure
  coding, privacy-aware logs, and clean rollbacks.
- Converts data to action — through clean event design,
  meaningful dashboards, disciplined A/Bs, and fast
  translation of signals into product updates.
- Operates with high agency and low ego — finds a way, moves
  fast, and keeps it real.

Educational background doesn’t matter to us. What does is
your attitude, your ability to take ownership, and your
willingness to do what it takes to make landlords feel taken
care of.

Apply now if you feel you are ready for your next challenge!
