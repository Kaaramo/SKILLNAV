### Raisonnement classification (LLM upstream)

This role builds the AI Gateway infrastructure platform that
routes and manages requests to various AI model providers
(OpenAI, Anthropic, etc.). The engineer works ON the
gateway/proxy layer (rate limiting, failovers, unified API)
rather than building, training, or fine-tuning AI models
themselves. It's infrastructure that enables other
developers to use AI, making it ai-support.
