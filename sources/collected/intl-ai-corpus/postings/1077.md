The Mission

Let's make healthcare free and accessible to everyone
through AI. We're building an AI doctor that already handles

billions while maintaining medical safety and accuracy.

What You'll Build

Core AI Systems

- Multi-agent consensus architectures where specialized models

- RAG pipelines processing thousands of clinical guidelines

  performance
- Real-time inference systems handling millions of medical

- Custom workflows for domain-specific medical reasoning

Clinical Data Infrastructure

- NLP pipelines extracting structured data from patient
  conversations (HIPAA-compliant)
- QHIN integrations for automated EHR data retrieval and
  reconciliation
- Intelligent triage systems routing between AI and human
  providers
- Documentation generation matching provider-specific
  protocols

Safety & Scale

- Emergency detection with sub-second escalation to human
  oversight
- Validation layers ensuring 99%+ treatment plan accuracy
- Distributed systems handling rapid growth and peak loads
- Fail-safe mechanisms for critical path decisions in
  regulated environments

You'll also work on voice/video AI interfaces, multilingual
medical NLP, predictive health modeling, and infrastructure
for the next billion patient interactions.

How We Work

- Engineering-first org: no project managers, just senior
  engineers
- Flat structure with direct CEO reporting and high autonomy
