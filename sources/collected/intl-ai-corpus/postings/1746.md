Job Description

As a Senior Software Engineer, you will be part of our
development team to help design, enhance and build our HR
platform in an agile development environment, you will work
independently with minimal insight to develop software that
conforms to Visa’s high standards. As part of this self-
driven team, you will have total ownership of the solutions
we provide for our HR customers making availability,
performance, security, and reliability critical to our
success. You will work with colleagues, who will support and
challenge you daily. You will play part of multiple teams
tasked with multiple projects ranging from building Custom
Workday reports and calculated fields, BIRT Layout designs,
Studio Integrations, EIB inbound and outbound integrations
to highly functional, secure, scalable, and resilient real
time enhancements. You will work on unit testing, code
reviews and regular check-ins to continuous integration that
will become part of your DNA. You will see the code written
and moved to production within just a few days, to then be
used by all Visa employees. If this sounds exciting, we want
to chat and tell you more about our work culture and
environment and see if this will be a good fit for both of
us.
