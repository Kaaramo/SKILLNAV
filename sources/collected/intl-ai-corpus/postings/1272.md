Core Responsibilities: Learning and Building

AI-Native Operations & Infrastructure You will lead the
deployment of our Generative AI infrastructure, moving
beyond "support tickets" toward self-service automation and
agentic resolution.

- Model Orchestration: Learn routing and orchestration for
  frontier models (Claude/Claude Code, Gemini, OpenAI) and
  open-source/distilled models (DeepSeek, Qwen, Kimi) using
  tools like LiteLLM, AWS Bedrock, and n8n.
- Agentic Frameworks & RAG: Build retrieval-augmented
  generation (RAG) systems and agentic workflows that
  integrate with our internal knowledge base to streamline
  operations.
- Developer Experience: Support the engineering team by
  integrating AI-driven CLIs and IDEs (Claude Code, Cursor,
  Codex) directly into the daily workflow.

Cloud & Systems Engineering

- Infrastructure as Code (IaC): Treat the entire corporate and
  product environment as a codebase. You will contribute to
  the design, reliability, and security of these
  environments using Terraform, Pulumi, ClawdBot, or Devpod.
- Cloud-First Architecture: We are an AWS-primary shop, but
  you must possess the eagerness to learn across GCP and
  Azure as needed. You will help build serverless, high-
  availability environments that scale.
- SaaS Integration: Act as the integration engineer for our
  core stack (Salesforce, HubSpot, Linear, Notion, Google
  Workspace, Pave). If it has an API, you will automate it.

Security & Identity

- Security-First Architecture: Wear a tin foil hat and partner
  with our internal Red Teams to implement Zero-Trust
  networking and secure build pipelines. You will learn why
  low-latency environments matter for cracking and why
  security must be woven into the fabric of the
  architecture.
- Identity Access Management (IAM): Help architect and
  automate the access management stack (Okta/Google
  Workspace/SSO). You will build the guardrails that allow
  rapid onboarding and access without sacrificing security.
- Offensive Tooling Support: Support high-performance
  computing (HPC) and specialized networking environments
  specifically tailored for deep-tier penetration testing
  and adversarial simulations.

Areas of Accountability: High Agency & Ownership

- The "Learn-by-Doing" Mandate: You are accountable for the
  "last mile" of execution. You will write code, configure
  VPCs, and debug CI/CD pipelines alongside a small, elite
  team.
- "Invisible IT" Execution: You will contribute to creating a
  friction-free employee experience—minimizing human
  intervention for access, hardware provisioning, and
  compliance.
- Scalability & Performance: Help ensure internal systems can
  handle massive data requirements and rapid growth without
  sacrificing performance.

Qualifications: The "Driven Builder" Profile

We prioritize High Agency and Individual Drive over years of
tenure. We want the person who builds side projects for fun.

- Relentless Autodidact: You don't wait for a training manual.
  You have a proven track record of teaching yourself new
  technologies and turning ideas into working projects.
- Polyglot Engineer: Strong foundation in Python or Go. You
  are eager to learn complex APIs, custom integrations, and
  low/no-code platforms to bridge gaps.
- Cloud Native Passion: Demonstrated interest in AWS
  (Primary), with eagerness to learn Azure/GCP. Specifically

- AI Pragmatist: You understand the difference between AI hype
