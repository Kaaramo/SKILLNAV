## Description complète

Senior Full‑Stack/AI Engineer (Frontend-leaning)

Who We Are:

AND Digital are a tech company focused on accelerating
digital delivery and dedicated to closing the digital skills
gap. We’ve been helping organisations build better digital
products and stronger digital teams since 2014.

We believe our work should always leave a legacy for the
client. We do this through close relationships with our
offices (or ‘Clubs’) so that our partners are always
prioritized by a regional team close to them.

This unique model has driven success for our clients and
ourselves, evidenced by our remarkable organic growth since
2014. Today we number more than 1,300 people with Clubs all
over the UK and Europe with plans for global expansion in
the next couple of years.

Join us - and help us fulfil our mission to close the
world’s digital skills gap.

What you’ll bring to the tableCore Technical Expertise

- Deep hands‑on experience delivering full‑stack
  JavaScript/TypeScript solutions, with strong proficiency
  in React, Next.js, Node.js, and the broader JavaScript
  ecosystem.
- Proven ability to build high‑quality, production‑ready
  systems, with strong fundamentals in performance,
  accessibility, security, and maintainability.
- Demonstrates technical leadership within a squad, shaping
  implementation approaches, influencing architectural
  decisions, and role‑modelling engineering excellence
  through hands‑on contribution.
- Strong understanding of cloud infrastructure (AWS
  preferable) and CI/CD pipelines, including how
  applications are built, deployed, monitored, and operated
  in production.
- Brings a DevOps mindset, ensuring smooth delivery workflows,
  observability, and operational readiness.
- Able to take ownership of complex technical problems, make
  pragmatic trade‑offs, and clearly articulate decisions to
  both technical and non‑technical stakeholders.
- Comfortable working across the stack, even if
  frontend‑leaning, with the ability to contribute
  meaningfully to backend services when needed.

AI & Emerging Technology

- Practical experience applying AI in real delivery
  contexts—e.g., integrating AI APIs/services, building
  AI‑augmented user experiences, or using AI‑assisted
  development tools responsibly.
- Understands where AI provides genuine value versus where
  traditional engineering approaches are more appropriate.
- Able to evaluate risk, cost, maintainability, and user
  impact when proposing AI‑driven solutions.
- Curious about emerging AI patterns, frameworks, and tooling,
  with the ability to apply them pragmatically rather than
  experimentally.
- Comfortable guiding clients on realistic AI opportunities,
  helping them navigate hype vs. practical value.

Consulting & Client Delivery

- Experience working in consulting or client‑facing
  environments, operating confidently in ambiguous problem
  spaces.
- Able to engage with stakeholders to shape requirements,
  clarify constraints, and contribute to solution
  discovery—not just implementation.
- Strong communication skills, capable of translating
  technical concepts into clear, outcome‑focused language
  for clients and business stakeholders.
- Brings a collaborative, delivery‑focused mindset, balancing
  technical quality with client priorities and timelines.

Nice To Have (but valuable)

- Experience with design systems, component libraries, or
  frontend architecture at scale.

Exposure to serverless, edge runtimes, or modern deployment
patterns (e.g., Vercel, CloudFront, Lambda@Edge).
