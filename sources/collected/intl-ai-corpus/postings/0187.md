### Raisonnement classification (LLM upstream)

This is a data engineering role focused on building the
infrastructure, pipelines, and platforms that support
GenAI/RAG systems. The role involves data ingestion, vector
databases, knowledge graphs, and document indexing for RAG
solutions, but does not include building, training, fine-
tuning, or deploying the actual AI models.

## Responsabilités

- Build robust data pipelines for document ingestion, indexing, and retrieval to support scalable RAG solutions
- Architect data platforms capable of processing petabytes of data with real-time and batch ingestion
- Design and manage infrastructure for optimal extraction, transformation, and loading from various data sources
- Integrate external databases, APIs, and knowledge graphs into RAG systems to improve contextualization
- Conduct experiments to evaluate RAG workflow effectiveness and iterate for optimal performance
- Curate and collect data from traditional and non-traditional sources for AI applications

---

## Use cases

- Retrieval Augmented Generation (RAG) systems for enterprise knowledge management
- Vector search and information retrieval for document-based AI applications
- Knowledge graph integration for enhanced AI contextualization
- Large-scale document processing and indexing for enterprise search
- Unstructured data management (text, audio, video, images) for AI workflows
