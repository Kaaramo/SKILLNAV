About Five9

Intelligence. Five9 has led the charge with its Genius AI
platform. Five9 offers a high-energy environment with lots
of room for personal and professional growth. Our innovative
AI team is seeking a talented and experienced Senior Full
Stack Software Engineer. In this role, you will be part of
the team that builds software that brings AI technologies to
users. Prior AI experience is not required - you will have

vendors like Azure, OpenAI, Google, Databricks, IBM, and
Amazon, and thus receive on-the-job experience for some of
the hottest skills on the market. An ideal candidate for us
is an experienced engineer who is passionate about building

problem solver and a creative self-starter.

Role Overview

As a Senior Software Engineer, you will be part of the team
that builds software that brings AI technologies to users.
You will work on building high performance web platforms and
applications, contributing to scalable and highly available
systems, and collaborating with cross-functional teams in an
agile environment while owning your code in production.

Key Responsibilities

- Implement UX designs by working with UX Designers on a day-
  to-day basis, using React/Redux based framework.
- Be part of an agile team to develop a scalable, highly
  available application platform using modern PaaS platforms
  such as GCP.
- Participate and contribute to requirements analysis and
  grooming.
- Understand how to leverage PaaS components, such as CDN,
  noSQL databases when developing software in order to
  achieve scalability, reliability, and operability.
- Ability to build custom widgets for dashboards developed
  using Google Looker Studio plugins is a plus, but not
  required.
- Document and communicate clearly architecture and
  implementation solutions.
- Have ownership of your code in production, including on-
  call, troubleshooting.

Qualifications & Skills

- 5+ years software development experience.
- React/Redux focus.
- Highly proficient in JavaScript and Java.
- Experience with PaaS hosted relational and no-SQL databases.
- Desirable, but not required, experience with custom widget
  development using Google Looker Studio.
- Experience in operational code ownership – on-call,
  troubleshooting, uptime.
- Experience in applying modern UI design patterns and
  separating concerns in a distributed system.
- Must have experience working within an Agile/Scrum team.
- Positive, self-motivated and enthusiastic attitude with a
  strong sense of ownership.
- Strong pro-active communication skills, both verbal and
  written.
- AI skills in speech recognition and natural language
  processing are a plus but not required.
- Experience in contact center software is a plus but not
  required.
