Job Description

We're building the AI Engineering team at AGS from the
ground up—and we need a technical anchor in India to make it
work. As Lead AI Solutions Engineer, you'll own day-to-day
technical decisions, mentor engineers, and drive projects
from concept to production. You're the Head of AI
Engineering's counterpart for delivery execution. While
leadership in North America sets architecture and strategy,
you'll translate that into working software and guide the
team through implementation. This is a hands-on technical
leadership role. You'll write code, review code, architect
solutions, and make other engineers better. You'll have real
authority over technical decisions and full accountability
for delivery outcomes.

If you want to lead technically without becoming a full-time
people manager, this is your role.



Key Responsibilities:

Technical Leadership

- Own day-to-day technical decisions for the India engineering
  team
- Lead design and architecture for agents, automations, and
  Foundation Layer capabilities
- Drive technical direction in partnership with the Head of AI
  Engineering
- Make build-vs-buy and technology selection recommendations
- Establish patterns, standards, and reference implementations
  the team will follow

Hands-On Delivery

- Design and build intelligent agents, conversational
  interfaces, and agentic workflows
- Develop Foundation Layer services—reusable AI capabilities
  exposed as REST APIs and MCP servers
- Create integrations between AI capabilities and enterprise
  systems
- Troubleshoot complex issues across the full stack

Team Development

- Mentor engineers through pairing, code review, and coaching
- Conduct technical interviews and help grow the team
- Set quality expectations and hold the team to them
- Build a culture of ownership where every engineer can
  deliver end-to-end

Stakeholder Collaboration

- Translate business requirements into technical solutions
- Provide estimates and communicate trade-offs clearly
- Partner with QA and Analysts to ensure quality and
  completeness
- Represent engineering in cross-functional discussions

Qualifications

- 5-8 years of software engineering experience with

- Strong programming fundamentals in Python and/or TypeScript
- Experience building AI/ML applications, conversational

  cloud platforms
