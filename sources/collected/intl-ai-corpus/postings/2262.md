🚀 About WRITER

📐 About the role

We’re looking for a staff software engineer to join our
enterprise admin team. This team owns the foundational
systems that enable large organisations to securely adopt
and operate Writer at scale, including identity,
authentication, authorization, administration, and billing
foundations.

You'll design and build the foundational platform services
that our largest customers depend on for secure, reliable
access to Writer across their organisations. These systems
power enterprise-grade access control, permissions, seat
management, usage governance, and subscription
infrastructure. The problems are non-trivial: multi-tenancy,
distributed systems, compliance constraints, scalability,
and long-term architectural maintainability.

This is a hands-on engineering role for someone who enjoys
owning systems end-to-end, from architecture and design
through production reliability and iteration.

This role is hybrid, based out of our London hub. You’ll
report to an engineering director.

🦸🏻‍♀️ What you’ll do

- Design and build robust, scalable services for enterprise
  administration, billing, identity management,
  authentication, and authorization
- Make meaningful system design decisions, own the
  architecture of core platform components from initial
  design through production
- Build and optimise high-performance APIs and distributed
  services that operate reliably at enterprise scale
- Collaborate with product managers and cross-functional
  engineering teams to translate complex business and
  compliance requirements into well-designed technical

- Partner with DevOps teams on continuous deployment, logging,

- Drive improvements in observability, reliability, deployment

- Navigate ambiguity, take loosely defined problem spaces and
  turn them into well-structured, maintainable

- Raise the bar on code quality, testing, documentation, and

⭐️ What you need

- 7+ years of hands-on software engineering experience
