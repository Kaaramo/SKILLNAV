### Raisonnement classification (LLM upstream)

This is a full-stack engineering role building user
interfaces and services that enable AI workflows (agents,
chatbots, copilots). The engineer collaborates with AI
experts and platform teams but focuses on building the

facing features rather than training, fine-tuning, or
deploying models directly.
