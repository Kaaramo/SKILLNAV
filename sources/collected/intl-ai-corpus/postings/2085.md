## Description complète

Location: Come and join us in Berlin!

Join us as a Senior Fullstack Engineer to build the
intuitive interfaces that power our support teams, drivers
and passengers. You will drive the development of reliable
and scalable solutions that power our AI assist features
end-to-end.

Be ready to join a multinational, diverse, highly motivated
and collaborative team who strives for excellence and likes
to have fun, while building great features and products.

YOUR DAILY ADVENTURES WILL INCLUDE:

- Architect, build, and maintain robust backend services.
- Craft intuitive, powerful web applications for both end-
  users and internal teams.
- Interest or experience with building AI assist features
- Own features end-to-end—from concept through to deployment
  and performance monitoring.
- Partner with engineers, designers, product managers, and
  support teams to deliver meaningful, user-centered
  solutions.
- Experiment with AI-enhanced tools and features to elevate
  our products and engineering practices.
- Bring your ideas to life and help shape how we work, grow,
  and innovate as a team.
- Ensure systems are observable, scalable, and rock-solid
  through modern DevOps practices.
- Actively engage in our engineering communities—frontend and
  backend—to share insights and stay ahead of the curve.
- Contribute to a culture built on transparency, continuous
  learning, and mutual respect.
- Share your knowledge with and mentor other engineers

Our Techstack: Kotlin | TypeScript | Spring | React | AWS |
Docker | Git | Kafka & more

TO BE SUCCESSFUL IN THIS ROLE:

- Strong experience in backend and/or frontend development.
- Hands-on with—or ready to dive into—Kotlin, Spring,
  TypeScript, and React.
- A true owner mentality—you care deeply about quality and
  continuous improvement.
- You thrive in a self-organized, agile team that values
  autonomy and open communication.
- You’re curious, and excited to learn from both deeply
  technical and product-savvy teammates.
- Purpose drives you—you want to do work that matters and grow
  in an environment that supports you.
- You bring creativity and a problem-solving mindset to
  technical challenges.
- You have experience in technical mentorship
- You have interest or experience in building AI agents
- You communicate clearly and confidently in English, both
  written and spoken

ABOUT FREENOW:

We are Freenow, the European taxi app with the broadest
multi-mobility offering, available in 9 markets and over 180
cities. Our app gives millions of passengers the freedom to
move with a wide choice of services including taxis, private
hire vehicles, carsharing, car rental, e-scooters, e-bikes,
e-mopeds and public transport.Headquartered in Hamburg and
led by our CEO Thomas Zimmermann, we are on a mission to
make urban mobility more efficient, sustainable and
inclusive.Since July 2025 we have been part of Lyft, a
global mobility platform connecting riders and drivers for

we are building a more caring and connected world, creating
