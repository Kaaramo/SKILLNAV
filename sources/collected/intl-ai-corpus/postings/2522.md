## Description complète

About the Role

Synechron is seeking a highly skilled AI/ML Engineer with
deep expertise in Generative AI, Large Language Models
(LLMs), and agentic AI solutions. This role will lead end-
to-end AI model development, from design and training to
optimization and deployment, with a strong focus on Google
Cloud Platform (GCP). You will play a key role in driving
innovation by integrating advanced AI capabilities into
scalable, enterprise-grade systems aligned with Synechron’s
AI and digital transformation strategy.

Key Responsibilities

- Lead the design, development, training, fine-tuning, and
  deployment of LLMs and Generative AI models
- Design and implement Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG)
  pipelines to improve contextual accuracy and response
  quality
- Convert AI models into scalable APIs and microservices for
  enterprise deployment
- Optimize models for latency, throughput, reliability, and
  cost efficiency in production environments
- Build reusable ML code, data pipelines, and feature
  engineering frameworks for training and inference
- Collaborate closely with data engineers, product managers,
  cloud architects, and application teams
- Conduct research on emerging AI technologies, benchmark
  tools and approaches, and recommend adoption strategies
- Ensure adherence to data privacy, security, and enterprise
  compliance standards
- Provide technical leadership, guidance, and mentoring to
  junior team members when required

Required Qualifications & Experience

- Minimum 3 years of hands-on experience in developing and
  deploying machine learning models
- At least 1 year of focused experience in LLMs, Generative
  AI, or agentic AI solutions
- Proven experience building and deploying RAG architectures
  in production environments
- Strong hands-on experience with GCP services, especially
  BigQuery and Vertex AI
- Demonstrated success in integrating AI models into
  enterprise systems and workflows
- Experience working in Agile delivery environments

Technical SkillsMust Have

- Python (3.8+) – expert level
- Java – proficient (for enterprise integration)
- SQL, including BigQuery, and NoSQL databases
- Deep learning frameworks: TensorFlow, PyTorch, Keras
- Version control: Git
- MLOps tools: MLFlow, experiment tracking, model registry

Good to Have

- Containerization and orchestration: Docker, Kubernetes
- API frameworks: FastAPI, Flask, Spring Boot
- Vector databases: FAISS, Pinecone, Chroma
- CI/CD pipelines for ML workloads

Cloud & Platform Expertise

- Google Cloud Platform: BigQuery, Vertex AI
- Cloud-based ML pipelines and scalable model deployment
- Understanding of model monitoring, performance tuning, and
  cost optimization

Education & Certifications

- Bachelor’s or Master’s degree in Computer Science, Data
  Science, AI/ML, or related field
- GCP or AI/ML certifications (e.g., Google Cloud Professional
  Data Engineer, TensorFlow Developer) are preferred

Professional Competencies

- Strong analytical and problem-solving mindset for complex AI
  systems
- Excellent communication skills to explain technical concepts
  to non-technical stakeholders
- Leadership and ownership mindset with ability to guide
  project delivery
- Adaptability to rapidly evolving AI technologies and
  priorities
- Strong organizational skills to manage experiments, models,
  and deployments
- Innovation-driven approach focused on scalable, sustainable
  AI solutions
