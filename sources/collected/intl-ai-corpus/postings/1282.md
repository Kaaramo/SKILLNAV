## Description complète

Job Description

As a leading company in the field of software solutions for
healthcare, we operate in 19 countries and employ over 9,000
dedicated staff members. You will work in a dynamic and
innovative environment full of opportunities. With your
commitment and passion, you have the chance to make a
lasting difference.

Together, we are shaping the healthcare system of the
future. Become part of our mission and make a difference—for
a world where knowledge saves lives!

Your Impact

- Development and operation of a microservice-based agent mesh
  – highly scalable, multi-tenant, and secure by design.
- Orchestration of agentic systems and multi-agent
  architectures with frameworks such as LangChain, CrewAI,
  or Semantic Kernel.
- Integration and productive deployment of foundation models
  (e.g., OpenAI, Azure OpenAI, Hugging Face, Mistral).
- Design and implementation of intelligent backend components
  for context management, Retrieval-Augmented Generation
  (RAG), memory, tool use, and agentic collaboration.
- Building pipelines for prompt optimization, inference
  control, and usage analytics.
- Close collaboration with frontend, UX, and infrastructure
  teams to bring AI solutions into end-to-end production.

Your Profile

- Degree in Computer Science, Artificial Intelligence, Data
  Science, or a comparable discipline.
- Hands-on experience in developing LLM-powered agentic
  applications with a strong focus on microservice
  architectures.
- Deep expertise in Python (LangChain, FastAPI, AsyncIO,
  Pydantic), as well as in working with model APIs, data
  integration, and CI/CD.
- Experience with Azure AI, Azure ML, or comparable cloud
  stacks (GCP, AWS is a plus).
- Knowledge of vector databases (e.g., Qdrant, Weaviate,
  Pinecone, FAISS) and the application of embedding models
  for RAG approaches.
- Solid understanding of scalability, multi-tenancy, and
  security requirements in the productive operation of AI
  systems.
