## Description complète

Garda Capital Partners (Garda) is a multi-billion dollar
alternative investment firm with over 22 years of experience
deploying relative value strategies across fixed income
markets for institutional investors. Garda's primary offices
are located in Wayzata, New York City, West Palm Beach,
Geneva, Zug, Copenhagen, Singapore, and Scottsdale.

Garda is looking to hire a Software Engineer (AI Tooling) to
be a part of our Research and Technology (R&T) group to
design and build systems that integrate modern AI
capabilities across the firm. The selected candidate will
develop backend services, automation workflows, and
integrations that help research, trading, and operations
teams work more effectively with AI-powered tools.

This is a hands-on engineering role focused on building
practical, production-ready systems—not research or model
training. You'll work with LLM APIs, build internal
applications, create automation workflows, and integrate AI
capabilities into existing systems. The ideal candidate is
excited about the potential of AI tools but wants to focus
on engineering and delivery rather than ML research.
Experience in financial services or capital markets is
strongly preferred.

Position Responsibilities

- Build internal applications and services that integrate LLM
  APIs and other AI capabilities into firm workflows
- Develop backend infrastructure for AI-powered tools (API
  gateways, vector search, chat backends, monitoring)
- Create automation workflows and integrations between AI
  platforms and internal systems
- Implement data processing pipelines that prepare and route
  information to AI services
- Work with stakeholders across trading, research, and
  operations to understand requirements and gather feedback
- Build and maintain integrations with vendor AI platforms and
  third-party tools
- Participate in design, development, testing, and deployment
  of new features and systems
- Support and improve existing AI-powered applications and
  workflows
- Collaborate with infrastructure teams to ensure solutions
  are secure, scalable, and well-monitored

Qualifications & Desired Skills

- Bachelor's Degree in Computer Science, Engineering,
  Mathematics, Finance, or related field
- 2-5 years of software engineering experience
- Strong programming skills in Python (primary language for
  this role) or ability to learn quickly
- Experience building backend services, APIs, or internal
  applications
- Comfortable working with REST APIs, databases, and data
  processing workflows
- Interest in AI and modern development tools; no ML/deep
  learning experience required
- Understanding of fixed income, trading operations, or
  investment management workflows is a plus
- Capital markets experience strongly preferred
- Strong problem-solving skills and ability to learn new
  technologies quickly
- Clear communication skills and comfort working with both
  technical and non-technical stakeholders
- Ability to work effectively in a fast-paced environment and
  manage multiple priorities
- Self-motivated with interest in building practical tools
  that people actually use
