The Principal AI Engineer is a hands-on senior technical
leader responsible for architecting and enabling AI
augmented software engineering across the enterprise. This
role drives the adoption of Generative AI, AI code
assistants, and agentic workflows to transform the software
development lifecycle (SDLC), accelerate engineering
productivity, modernize legacy systems, and improve software
quality.  The individual will combine deep expertise in
cloud-native architecture, distributed systems, and
enterprise software development with advanced proficiency in
AI-assisted engineering tools and agent frameworks. This
role will design and implement AI-driven development
platforms, agentic automation pipelines, and context-aware
code generation capabilities that enable engineering teams
to build and operate scalable, secure, and reliable software
systems. As a Principal AI Engineer, this individual will
influence enterprise architecture decisions, engineering
productivity strategies, AI governance frameworks, and
responsible AI adoption across the organization.
