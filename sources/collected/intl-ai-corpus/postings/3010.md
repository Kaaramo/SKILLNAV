- 5+ years in data science, data engineering, or ML
  engineering roles
- Strong proficiency in Python and SQL
- Hands-on experience with data tooling (pandas, Plotly,
  Streamlit, Dash)
- Practical experience working with LLMs and deploying ML
  solutions in production environments
- Experience integrating and working with APIs and technical
  systems
- Strong problem-solving skills with a bias toward
  implementation and delivery
- Excellent collaboration and communication skills in cross-
  functional teams

What you will do
