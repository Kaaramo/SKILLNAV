### Raisonnement classification (LLM upstream)

While this role involves building deployment pipelines for
AI agents and managing vector databases and model endpoints,
the core function is DevOps/infrastructure engineering. The
engineer is not building, training, or fine-tuning models
themselves — they are enabling and supporting AI workloads
through infrastructure, CI/CD, monitoring, and security.
This is platform/infrastructure work that supports AI teams
rather than hands-on AI/ML development.
