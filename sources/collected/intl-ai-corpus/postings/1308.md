## Description complète

ZoomInfo is where careers accelerate. We move fast, think
boldly, and empower you to do the best work of your life.
You’ll be surrounded by teammates who care deeply, challenge
each other, and celebrate wins. With tools that amplify your

About the Role:

We're seeking a Senior Software Engineer with strong Python
skills and a passion for building intelligent agentic
systems. This role is ideal for someone who thrives at
building applications powered by LLMs, bringing both
technical depth and entrepreneurial thinking to complex
problems. You'll work on cutting-edge AI agent systems that
leverage existing language models to solve real-world
challenges.

What You'll Do:

- Design, develop, and deploy production-grade agentic AI
  systems powered by LLMs
- Build scalable Python services and orchestration pipelines
  for complex multi-step agent workflows
- Architect frameworks that enable agents to reason, plan, use
  tools, and execute autonomous tasks
- Integrate LLM APIs (OpenAI, Anthropic, etc.) into robust,
  reliable production systems
- Collaborate with cross-functional teams to translate
  business requirements into technical solutions
- Optimize system performance, reliability, and cost-
  efficiency of LLM-powered applications
- Evaluate and integrate emerging LLM technologies and agentic
  patterns into our product stack
- Contribute to technical strategy and architectural decisions
  with an entrepreneurial mindset
- Mentor other engineers and promote best practices in
  building AI-powered systems

What you bring:

- 7+ years of professional software engineering experience
- Strong proficiency in Python with deep understanding of
  software design patterns and best practices
- Understanding of machine learning concepts (how models work,
  their capabilities and limitations)
- Hands-on experience working with Large Language Models
  (OpenAI, Anthropic, open-source models, etc.)
- Proven track record building AI agents or agentic systems
  (tool use, reasoning, multi-step workflows, function
  calling)
- Excellent analytical and problem-solving skills with ability
  to debug complex systems
- Strong communication skills and ability to work
  collaboratively in a team environment
- Entrepreneurial spirit - self-motivated, comfortable with
  ambiguity, and bias toward action

Nice to Have:

- Active contributions to open-source projects (especially in
  AI/agentic systems space)
- Experience with prompt engineering, RAG systems, or
  embedding-based search
- Familiarity with vector databases and semantic search
  technologies
- Knowledge of LLM orchestration frameworks (LangChain,
  LlamaIndex, etc.)
- Experience with modern workflow orchestration (Temporal,
  Airflow, etc.)
- Background in distributed systems or high-scale applications
- Experience with agent frameworks or MCP (Model Context
  Protocol)
- Publications, blog posts, or technical talks in AI/agents
  domains
- Experience building developer tools or platforms
