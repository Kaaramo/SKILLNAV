About the Role:

At DISQO, we’re redefining how companies turn data into
decisions. Our mission is to make advertising and consumer
behavior transparent, measurable, and actionable. We’re now
building the next evolution of analytics - an agentic, AI-
powered system that lets users explore complex data
intuitively and instantly.

This is a highly strategic and visible initiative at the
heart of DISQO’s future. You’ll work directly with our CTO
and a team of talented, driven engineers to architect and
build a next-generation AI analytics engine from the ground
up - leveraging Agentic AI to shape the foundation for how
data intelligence is delivered to customers worldwide.

What you will do:

- Architect and deliver a high-performance Advanced Analytics
  Engine – featuring vectorized query processing, columnar
  storage and OLAP capabilities – that redefines speed,
  scalability, and analytical depth while crunching
  marketing datasets with sub-second performance.
- Design and build an Agentic AI system that leverages this
  Advanced Analytics Engine to drive autonomous, intelligent
  decision-making.
- Partner with product, engineering and data teams to power
  agentic AI analytics systems that autonomously explore
  data and surface actionable insights.
- Profile, benchmark, and optimize Rust components for maximum
  throughput, low latency, and hardware-aware efficiency.
- Leverage AWS cloud services to architect scalable, reliable,
  and cost-efficient analytics infrastructure.
- Shape the evolution of DISQO’s broader data platform and its
  integration across our product ecosystem, leading
  architectural strategy and technical direction in close
  partnership with data and product engineering teams..
- Mentor and guide engineers, fostering a culture of technical
  excellence, innovation, and collaboration.
- Contribute to open-source or internal frameworks that
  advance analytical systems and distributed computation.

What you bring to the role:

- 12+ years of professional software engineering experience,
  including 5+ years architecting or building high-
  performance data systems or analytics platforms. 3+ years
  of product Rust experience.
- Deep expertise in Rust and strong experience in Java.
  Ideally looking for individuals who have transitioned from
  Java to Rust.
- Proven track record building large-scale data analytics or
  OLAP systems from the ground up.
- Deep understanding of columnar data engines, vectorized
  execution, and query/dataframe optimization.
- Hands-on experience with performance engineering, profiling,
  and hardware-aware optimization.
- Strong expertise with AWS -  designing, deploying, and
  optimizing large-scale data and compute systems in the
  cloud.
- A systems-thinking mindset - you see software, data, and
  infrastructure as one cohesive system to tune and
  optimize.
- Thrives in a fast-moving, startup environment - comfortable
  balancing hands-on execution with big-picture
  architectural thinking.
- Strong foundation in statistics and data analysis concepts.
- Familiarity with AI-driven analytics, or experience with
  open-source engines such as Apache DataFusion, DuckDB or
  ClickHouse/chDB are big pluses.
- Experience evolving / refactoring large brownfield solutions
  is a plus.
