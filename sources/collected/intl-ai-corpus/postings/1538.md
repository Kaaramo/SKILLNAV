Company Overview

KLA is a global leader in diversified electronics for the
semiconductor manufacturing ecosystem. Virtually every
electronic device in the world is produced using our
technologies. No laptop, smartphone, wearable device, voice-
controlled gadget, flexible screen, VR device or smart car
would have made it into your hands without us. KLA invents
systems and solutions for the manufacturing of wafers and
reticles, integrated circuits, packaging, printed circuit
boards and flat panel displays. The innovative ideas and
devices that are advancing humanity all begin with
inspiration, research and development. KLA focuses more than
average on innovation and we invest 15% of sales back into
R&D. Our expert teams of physicists, engineers, data
scientists and problem-solvers work together with the
world’s leading technology providers to accelerate the
delivery of tomorrow’s electronic devices. Life here is
exciting and our teams thrive on tackling really hard
problems. There is never a dull moment with us.

Job Description/Preferred Qualifications

Job Description Summary:

We are seeking an AI Engineer to lead the design and
development of LLM‑powered applications and enterprise‑grade
Generative AI solutions. This is a hands‑on engineering role
focused on building, integrating, and scaling
production‑ready GenAI systems, rather than research or
traditional data science.

The ideal candidate is a senior software engineer who has
evolved into Generative AI application development, with
strong practical experience in LLMs, prompt engineering, RAG
architectures, agentic AI workflows, and cloud‑based AI
platforms.

Key Responsibilities:

- Design and develop Generative AI applications using modern
  LLM platforms such as Microsoft Foundry (preferred), AWS
  Bedrock, Claude, OpenAI.
- Build Document Processing / Document Intelligence pipelines,
  including: OCR, LLM‑based summarization, Classification,
  data extraction, and validation workflows
- Design, build, and orchestrate multi‑step AI agents capable
  of tool usage, reasoning, and task execution.
- Develop custom copilots using Microsoft Copilot Studio.
- Design and implement RAG (Retrieval‑Augmented Generation)
  solutions using vector search and contextual grounding.
- Build agentic AI workflows and manage LLM orchestration.
- Develop REST APIs and microservices to operationalize AI
  capabilities using FastAPI and/or Node.js.
- Deploy, monitor, and operate GenAI solutions on Azure and
  other cloud platforms.

Required Skills:

- 5+ years of professional software development experience,
  with 2+ years of proven experience delivering
  enterprise‑scale Generative AI solutions.
- Strong experience with LLM orchestration tools such as
  LangChain, LangGraph, Semantic Kernel, etc.
- Experience building enterprise chatbots using Bot Framework,
  RAG pipelines, vector search, and multi‑turn conversation
  handling.
- Hands‑on experience with OCR engines (Azure Form Recognizer,
  AWS Textract, Tesseract, etc.).
- Strong Python development skills
- Experience with Microsoft Foundry and Copilot Studio
- Solid understanding of RAG architectures and vector search
- Experience with REST APIs, microservices, and cloud‑native
  development
- Familiarity with front‑end frameworks (React, Next.js,
  Angular) to deliver user-facing AI applications.
- Familiarity with CI/CD, Docker, and DevOps practices

Minimum Qualifications

Doctorate (Academic) Degree and 0 years related work
experience; Master's Level Degree and related work
experience of 3 years; Bachelor's Level Degree and related
work experience of 5 years
