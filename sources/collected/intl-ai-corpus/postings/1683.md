AI / LLM Engineer (Python)

Maven is a market-leading proprietary trading firm
allocating internal capital across discretionary,
systematic, and market-making strategies. Our collective
expertise spans traders, engineers, and technologists,
unified by an unwavering dedication to enhance our
efficiency as a premier liquidity provider for globally
listed derivatives. Our engineering teams design and build
groundbreaking execution, pricing, and data systems that
elevate and improve how financial markets operate.

Job Summary

As an AI Engineer you will be part of a small team
responsible for the design, implementation, and optimization
of AI workflows, as well as managing the organization’s AI
infrastructure. The role will involve working closely with
teams across the organisation to deliver robust, scalable,
and effective AI solutions that support key business
objectives. As an AI Engineer you are also expected to stay
abreast of the rapidly evolving landscape of large language
models (LLMs) and related AI technologies, applying this
knowledge to drive innovation and best practices throughout
the company.

Key Responsibilities

- AI Workflow Implementation: Work with business and IT
  stakeholders to identify and scope use cases for
  application of AI across the firm. Deliver technical
  solutions to implement the identified AI use cases.
  Provide support and maintenance.
- Infrastructure Evaluation: Analyse current AI infrastructure
  and deliver improvements in internal platforms, end user
  applications and tools.
- Cross-Department Collaboration & Training: Provide training,
  guidance, and support to stakeholders in all departments
  to facilitate understanding and adoption of AI systems.
  Work closely with product, engineering, and business units
  to integrate AI solutions into business processes.
- LLM & Industry Awareness: Stay current with advancements in
  large language models (LLMs) and emerging AI technologies.
  Evaluate and recommend adoption or integration of new
  models, frameworks, or tools as appropriate.
- Documentation & Best Practices: Maintain clear documentation
  on workflows, infrastructure decisions, and operational
  procedures. Champion best practices in reproducibility,
  scalability, and security.
- Performance & Cost Optimization: Assess and optimize AI
  workloads for performance and cost-effectiveness across
  different infrastructure options.

The Skills you will have

- Excellent Python skills, with significant experience
  designing, developing, testing, and deploying complex and
  robust production systems
- Solid experience using LLMs
- Very strong business communication skills and the ability to
  present information clearly and concisely
- Enthusiasm to rapidly learn new technologies and skills
- An efficient and diligent approach with a strong attention
  to detail
- The ability to solve new and unfamiliar problems quickly and
  creatively
- An interest in trading and finance

Ideal experience will include

- Deploying and managing key AI OSS projects such as LiteLLM,
  OpenWebUI, LibreChat, in a production environment
- Integrating LLMs in production systems
- Software engineering best practices for regression and
  integration testing
- Knowledge of tokenization, RAG, one-shot prompting, prompt
  engineering
- Containerisation and orchestration techniques (Docker,
  Kubernetes)
- Knowledge of financial markets & securities, especially
  equities and options

We pride ourselves on being able to rapidly deliver well-
engineered software into production. You will be able to see
the impact of your work immediately and will be in a unique
position to have a direct impact on trading revenue. We make

market short.
