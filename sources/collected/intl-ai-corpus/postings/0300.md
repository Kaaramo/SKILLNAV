### Raisonnement classification (LLM upstream)

This is a mobile engineering role focused on iOS development
for Search and AI features at Slack. The role involves
building mobile experiences and interfaces that integrate AI
capabilities, but does not involve building, training, fine-
tuning, or deploying AI models directly. The engineer is
creating context-aware mobile experiences that leverage AI
systems built by others, making this an AI-support role.

## Responsabilités

- Lead the Search and AI mobile efforts, owning the roadmap and technical strategy for high-impact user journeys

maximum flexibility in a constantly evolving product. What
