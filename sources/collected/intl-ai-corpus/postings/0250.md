## Description complète

⚙️What we’re making: We are creating a product that will
allow health insurance companies to automate prior
authorization document processing responsibly.

🔎What we're looking for: Humata Health Inc. is seeking a
skilled and passionate Staff Software Engineer to join our
innovative team, with a focus on our AI/ML Platform and
MLOps infrastructure. In this critical, high-leverage role,
you'll be a core contributor to the design, architecture,
and long-term vision of our scalable, secure, and multi-
language backend systems that power our healthcare platform.
You'll drive best practices across our ML Engineering and
MLOps disciplines, ensuring reliability and performance at
scale.

Location: Florida, US; Remote, US

✅ Responsibilities:

- Own the long-term technical roadmap, design, and
  implementation of highly available and performant services
  and infrastructure across the AI/ML platform, utilizing
  languages such as Python, Go, and/or
  JavaScript/TypeScript.
- Serve as a technical leader and subject matter expert,
  driving alignment across engineering, data science,
  product, and security teams on platform requirements,
  performance, and best practices.
- Define and implement the strategic vision for MLOps,
  including advanced model versioning, feature stores, drift
  monitoring, and CI/CD pipelines that handle complex,
  regulated healthcare data with rigorous security
  standards.
- Lead the development of resilient, high-throughput backend
  services and APIs (e.g., RESTful, gRPC) that seamlessly
  integrate various components of our ML system and other
  core services (Golang, Python).
- Mentor and coach senior and mid-level engineers, raising the
  bar for engineering excellence, system observability,
  testing, and documentation across the entire organization.
- Act as a critical escalation point for complex production
  issues, debugging and optimizing performance bottlenecks
  across services and infrastructure.

Role Requirements:

- 10+ years of professional experience in software
  engineering, with a significant focus on building and
  operating large-scale, highly available backend systems.
- 5+ years of experience in ML Engineering, MLOps, or building
  data-intensive platforms.
- Proficiency in at least one of Python, Go, or
  JavaScript/TypeScript, with a deep understanding of
  software design patterns and architectural best practices.
- Demonstrated experience in designing and scaling complex
  systems using microservices, containers (Docker), and
  orchestration (Kubernetes).
- Proficiency in at least one cloud platform (AWS, GCP, or
  Azure) and their ML services.
- Proven ability to lead technical projects independently from
  conception through deployment
- Exceptional problem-solving, communication, and
  interpersonal skills, with a track record of driving
  technical consensus and effective knowledge transfer.

⭐ Preferred Experience:

- Previous experience as a Staff, Principal, or equivalent
  technical leadership role.
- Deep practical knowledge of MLOps tooling and principles and
  their application in regulated environments (e.g.,
  experiment tracking, model serving frameworks, robust
  deployment strategies).
- Experience operating within healthcare technology or other
  highly-regulated industries, with familiarity with
  standards like FHIR, HL7, and PHI/HIPAA regulations.
- Experience with polyglot environments (e.g., Python for ML,
  Go for services).
- Advanced knowledge of security best practices, especially
  concerning data encryption and access control for
  sensitive data.
- A strong history of mentoring and contributing to open-
  source or internal engineering communities.

This role is expected to begin in January 2026.
