## Description complète

We are looking for an experienced frontend engineer to
strengthen our team. As a frontend engineer you will join a
Product team in our Digital Banking stream within the R&D
department and you will focus on building digital banking
products.



As part of the team, you will contribute to new and existing
functionalities, proof of concepts, and technical
improvements that will be implemented in the Digital
Banking. Your autonomous team is responsible for the entire
software development life cycle for the digital banking
product suite: from idea to release. You are expected to
take ownership of what you build and proactively collaborate
with architects, QA engineers and other team members to
ensure implementation is done according to our technical
vision and best practices. As a Frontend Developer, you’ll
use Angular, TypeScript, and JavaScript ES6 to create the
best omnichannel user experiences. You will also have the
opportunity to craft HTML templates and write SASS using our
Backbase Design System based on Bootstrap. We love clean,
testable, and maintainable code that is cross-browser
compatible. You are part of a multidisciplinary team being
end-to-end responsible for delivery, from concept to
production-ready software. We offer a hybrid working model
which is a location-flexible arrangement that allows people
to combine on-site and off-site work.



● You have 5+ years of relevant working experience working
on complex products; ● You have a solid background in
software development including design; ● You are able to
communicate clearly and confidently across all levels to get
buy-ins from technical stakeholders as well as from your
team members; ● Experience in the banking domain or Fintech
products will be considered an advantage; ● You have
excellent communication skills in English, spoken and
written; ● You have strong Angular and Typescript skills; ●
Having experience with Nx is a great asset; ● You have
worked with RxJS and understand design patterns; ●
Experience in Vue.js will be considered an advantage; ●
Having experience with Node and npm, and the Angular CLI
would be an advantage too in building toolchains; ● You
commonly use Gulp, Grunt and/or Webpack in your projects; ●
Having hands-on experience with build systems like Jenkins
is a plus; ● You have experience with non-functionals such
as security, performance, scalability etc.; ● Being a Full
Stack developer is a huge advantage; ● You have a solid
understanding of Enterprise Integration Patterns.
