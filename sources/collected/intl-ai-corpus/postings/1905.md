  workflow orchestration to evaluation tooling.

Raise the Quality Bar

- Design evaluation systems that measure retrieval quality,

- Build tooling that helps the team iterate confidently and

- Analyze production outputs to identify failure patterns and
  root causes.

Lead Across Teams

Build Responsible, Production-Ready AI

  auditability.
