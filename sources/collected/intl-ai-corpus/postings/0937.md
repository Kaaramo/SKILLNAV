## Description complète

Backend Engineer - AI Platform

Job Location: Bangalore, India

Experience : 3 - 5 years

About Unifize:

At Unifize, we’re building the AI-native product suite for
regulated manufacturing companies. Our platform is designed
to unify Quality, Operations, and R&D in a single
collaborative system.

Today, life sciences and other regulated manufacturers rely
on a patchwork of disconnected tools, eg, QMS, DMS, PLM,
MES, spreadsheets, and email, to manage their processes.
This fragmentation slows down innovation, increases
compliance risks, and forces teams to waste time manually
connecting the dots.

Unifize changes that by bringing process, documentation, and
communication together in one place. Whether it's managing
CAPAs, resolving deviations, launching new products, or
preparing for audits, Unifize enables teams to work faster
with full traceability and audit readiness built in.

What makes Unifize different:

- No-code configuration for rapid deployment and adaptation
- Collaboration embedded in every record, not siloed in email
  or chat
- Context-aware AI that suggests actions, detects risks, and
  enhances productivity
- Flexible architecture that supports expansion across QMS,
  DMS, MES, PLM, CMMS, and more

We’re already working with leading companies in the life
sciences and manufacturing sectors. Our customers start with
one use case and consistently expand, with 100% net
expansion to date.

Check out our website, case studies, videos to learn more.

The Role:

We're building a new AI Platform microservice that
integrates tightly with our core Clojure-based system. This
role involves leading backend development for this service,
primarily in Python, working on AI/ML integrations and
microservices architecture, and ensuring robust data
handling via PostgreSQL.

We don’t expect prior experience with Clojure but value
curiosity and a willingness to learn. This role is a good
fit for engineers who enjoy ownership, functional
programming principles, and building infrastructure that
powers intelligent applications.

Responsibilities:

- Own and build Python-based backend services that interface
  with AI models and core product workflows
- Design and manage scalable PostgreSQL and vector database
  schemas and queries
- Develop and maintain RESTful APIs
- Collaborate with the platform and frontend teams to ensure
  clean integration with the larger Unifize system
- Contribute to design decisions, including service
  boundaries, API structure, and data pipelines
- Write clean, well-tested code and proactively review others'
  work

Qualifications:

- Strong proficiency in Python, with emphasis on clean,
  modular design
- Solid understanding of relational databases, particularly
  PostgreSQL
- Experience designing and consuming LLM APIs and writing
  integrations with them – having understanding of RAG,
  vectorisation
- Familiarity with architectural principles behind modern web
  applications
- Ability to work in a fast-paced environment with a
  collaborative, product-focused team

Good to have:

- Understanding of AI/ML model integrations and workflows in
  production environments
- Experience with functional or functional-style programming
  (e.g., Clojure, Elixir, Scala, Java 8+)
- Exposure to distributed systems and microservices
- Familiarity with message queues, job orchestration, or
  asynchronous processing
