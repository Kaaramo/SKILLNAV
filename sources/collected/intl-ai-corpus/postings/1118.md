At Curriculum Associates, we believe in the potential of
every child and are changing the face of education
technology with award-winning learning programs like i-Ready
that serve a third of the nation’s K–8 students. For more

places, serving educators, and supporting accessible
learning experiences for all students has driven the
continuous improvement of our innovative programs. Our team
of more than 2,500 employees is composed of lifelong
learners who stand behind this mission, working tirelessly
to serve the educational community with world-class programs
and support every day.

Summary: This position is part of the expanding DevOps group
that combines teamwork, innovation, work/life balance, and
autonomy. You will contribute to the evolution of our AWS
cloud infrastructure that supports Curriculum Associates’

single program, i-Ready integrates powerful assessments and
rich insights with effective and engaging instruction in
reading and mathematics to address students’ individual
needs. i-Ready empowers teachers every day to make more

access to their own personalized path to growth. Want to be
part of our impact? Join us! Essential
duties/responsibilities: You will develop and advance our

Continuous Deployment. You will build, operate, scale, and
troubleshoot the Curriculum Associates SaaS infrastructure.

can shift quickly, and there is a need to be adaptable. You
will have an interest in and focus on AI and ML, as well as
traditional Dev Ops. You will enhance our monitoring tools,
build infrastructure, and source code management. In
collaboration with other teams. You’ll make sure our
infrastructure and applications that depend on it work

tools for systems and infrastructure automation. Required

ML lifecycle. Required Education and Experience: 7+ years of
DevOps experience Experience with AI/ML and the ML
Lifecycle. In depth knowledge of the AWS AI & ML suite of
services, such as... SageMaker Bedrock & Agentic AI
Experience with Continuous Integration / Continuous
Deployment (CI/CD) tools and processes. Knowledge of ML Flow
General AWS administrative functions, such as working with
IAM Infrastructure as Code approaches: Terraform or

Experience with languages such as Python. Experience with
