## Description complète

About the role

We are looking for senior and staff-level fullstack
engineers eager to join the team building our leading Magic
AI features.

At Hex we're deeply focused on building powerful, integrated
AI features that augment and accelerate analytics workflows.
The possibilities here are incredible, and we're uniquely
positioned to re-invent the way people think about
understanding data.

In this role, you’ll take the lead on building AI-powered
features into Hex, partnering closely with our Research team
on context and model prompting, and the rest of our product
engineering teams on integrating into the platform. The
fruits of your work will fundamentally change the way that
many thousands of people work with data, and give you a
front-row seat to the most exciting technological shift in
our lifetimes. As part of our Agent Platform team, you’ll
own the scalable agent infrastructure powering today’s most
powerful data agents, as well as the cutting-edge
experimentation tooling that allows the rest of the team to
rapidly deliver new AI features in the product.

We'd love to hear from you if you have:

- At least 4+ years of software engineering experience.
- Proficiency with React and Typescript in addition to
  knowledge around architecture and systems design.
- Experience leading and executing on major product
  initiatives.
- Excitement for getting your hands dirty and diving deep into
  how the browser works to develop high-quality and
  performant user experiences.
- An aptitude for how web infrastructure works to develop
  high-quality internal developer and external user
  experiences.
- An instinct for strategic thinking and aligning with
  business and product goals while keeping a healthy balance
  of velocity and engineering excellence.
- Interest in the data space, and a love of shipping great
  products and building tools that empower end users to do
  more.
- Experience maintaining a high quality bar for design,
  correctness, and testing.
- An ability to lead product initiatives while collaborating
  and mentoring fellow engineers.
- Curiosity and an interest in diving into the bigger picture
  of building a company, including go-to-market, customer
  development, people, and marketing.

This role will be hybrid (1-2 days per week) based out of
our San Francisco, California headquarters or NYC office.

In addition to our unique culture, Hex proudly offers a
competitive total rewards package, including but not limited
to, market-benched salary & equity, comprehensive health
benefits, and flexible paid time off.
