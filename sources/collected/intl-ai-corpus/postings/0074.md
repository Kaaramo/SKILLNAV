As a Senior or Staff AI Software Engineer at Caregentic, you

You will be responsible for building scalable, resilient

helping shape the technical direction of the platform and
mentoring other engineers. Our team operates with the

We’ve been a fully remote team from the start, distributed
across the U.S. We believe that great engineering happens
wherever people do their best work.
