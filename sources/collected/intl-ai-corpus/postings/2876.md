About the job

The primary goal of a Developer Relations is to engage,
educate, and inspire the developer community - building
trust, driving adoption of our technology, and ensuring
developers have an exceptional experience with our products.
This involves creating technical content, presenting at
events, gathering feedback, and serving as the voice of the
developer both externally and internally.

The role is focused on bridging the gap between developers
and our product teams, ensuring our solutions are
accessible, well-understood, and developer-friendly, while
fostering community growth, promoting best practices, and
driving continuous improvement in how developers discover,
learn, and build with our technology.

QVAC is a cutting-edge development platform designed for
building privacy-focused AI applications. Unlike traditional
AI tools that rely heavily on cloud-based infrastructure,
QVAC is designed to run entirely on local devices, giving
users full control over their data.
