## Description complète

About Nayya

Founded in 2019, Nayya is on a mission to connect people’s
most important information, so they can thrive in their
health and wealth. Powered by AI and advanced analytics,
Nayya’s platform transforms complex benefits experiences
into intuitive, seamless, and ongoing interactions—meeting
people's real world needs. As a trusted platform and partner
to leading employers, benefits solutions, and HR tech
providers, Nayya unlocks long-term value through helping
employees live more resilient lives. Backed by strategic
investors like ICONIQ, Felicis Ventures, SemperVirens,
Workday Ventures, MetLife Nextgen Ventures, and ADP
Ventures, Nayya is ushering in the future of health and
wealth for all.



About the Role

Nayya is looking for a Senior Software Engineer, AI
Applications to help design, build, and scale the core
systems that power our next generation of AI-native
products. In this role, you will be critical to developing
Nayya’s agentic platform to serve millions of employees
across their health and wealth management.  You will act as
a technical leader and AI business partner, translating
ambiguous needs into durable, production-grade AI-native
workflows that provide clarity for employees  and hundreds
of hours of savings for our partners.

What You’ll Do

- Develop AI-native features by building and integrating LLM-
  powered systems for extracting, structuring, and reasoning
  over complex healthcare and benefits data
- Build against AI infrastructure, including retrieval
  pipelines (RAG), embeddings, prompt orchestration, and
  internal agent frameworks
- Own end-to-end systems from design, application development,
  AI integrations and final product delivery.
- Collaborate cross-functionally with Product, Design and
  other functions to understand customer workflows and
  translate them into technical solutions
- Implement evaluation pipelines and benchmarking strategies
  to measure LLM performance, and design guardrails that
  enforce safety, accuracy, and compliance thresholds across
  AI-powered features

Who You Are

- 5+ years of software engineering experience building and
  operating complex applications in production
- Experienced integrating AI/LLM-powered systems (e.g.,
  OpenAI, Claude) into real products
- Strong proficiency with modern, object-oriented languages
  and frameworks (Typescript, Ruby etc)
- Comfortable completely owning backend systems and APIs and
  collaborating on frontend experiences
- Experience with AWS infrastructure or equivalent
- Experience with CI/CD pipelines, infrastructure as code, and
  monitoring tools
- Opinionated,  adaptable, and energized by working in fast-
  moving, ambiguous environments
- Have a track record of setting technical direction and
  influencing engineering standards across a team
- Strong communication skills and the ability to communicate
  with technical and non-technical stakeholders
