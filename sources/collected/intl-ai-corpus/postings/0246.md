## Description complète

Join a small, fast-moving team where you’ll research,
prototype, and ship quickly. We’re training foundational
models for financial applications on unique, multimodal
datasets and need engineering input at every step of the
process.

What you’ll do

-Design, build, and maintain data pipelines for unique and
multimodal datasets.
-Train and evaluate frontier-scale models; own the loop from
idea to prototype to production.
-Work shoulder-to-shoulder with researchers to discover new
methods.

You might be a fit if you

-Are a self-starter who thrives on coding autonomy and
project collaboration.
-Have deep ML chops (PyTorch/JAX), strong engineering
fundamentals, and a record of shipping research to
production.
-Prefer clear principles, low ego and prefer collaboration
over titles and politics.

Helpful backgrounds (any of the following)

-Frontier labs experience (e.g., Anthropic, OpenAI,
DeepMind); or
-Novel discovery through published work (NeurIPS/ICML/ICLR);
or
-Founded or led an AI/research company; or
-Standout independent achievement (leading major open-
source, top-tier role, etc.).

What we offer

-A well-funded trading firm expanding into AI research and
discovery - bring your best ideas and be rewarded for them.
-Real ownership and influence on roadmap, direction and
products.
-Competitive base compensation with significant upside tied
to results.
-A culture optimized for deep work, fast learning, and doing
the right thing.

If this sounds like you, apply.
