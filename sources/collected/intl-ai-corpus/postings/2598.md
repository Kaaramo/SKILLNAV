## Description complète

AI Product EngineerRaspberry AI

Raspberry AI is a leading provider of industry-defining AI
design software for fashion brands and retailers. Our
software empowers brands to rapidly understand consumer
demand and create unique designs within minutes. Leveraging
cutting-edge AI analytics and generative AI capabilities, we
help fashion brands revolutionize their design and
merchandising processes.

We are a Series A startup, backed by top-tier venture
capital firms such as Andreessen Horowitz, Khosla Ventures,
MVP and Greycroft.

About the Role

We are seeking a Senior AI Product Engineer to build the
agentic layer of Raspberry's platform. Today our product
gives designers powerful, individual AI tools — image
generation, editing, virtual try-on, and more. The next step
is making these tools work together autonomously: agents
that operate on our collaborative design canvas, chain
multi-step creative workflows, and keep designers in control
of the output.

This role sits at the intersection of AI engineering and
product engineering. You'll design agent architectures and
ship the product experiences around them — from model
orchestration and tool-use to the React interfaces designers
interact with.

Responsibilities

- Build AI agents that take actions inside Raspberry's design
  platform — triggering generations, chaining outputs across
  creative AI tools, and orchestrating multi-step workflows
- Design the orchestration and tool abstraction layer that
  exposes Raspberry's creative AI tools as agent-callable
  actions
- Architect end-to-end agentic workflows for batch design
  operations (e.g., sketch → render → colorway → on-body →
  tech flat)
- Build conversation, review, and approval interfaces that
  keep designers in control of agent-driven outputs
- Evaluate and integrate agent frameworks and LLM capabilities
  to find the best approaches for our domain
- Collaborate closely with ML engineers, product, and design
  to shape the roadmap for AI-powered creative workflows

Qualifications

- 5+ years of software engineering experience, with meaningful
  time building AI-native products end-to-end
- Hands-on experience with agent frameworks and orchestration
  (LangGraph, Anthropic Agent SDK, CrewAI, or similar)
- Strong full-stack product engineering skills — our frontend
  is React/Next.js/TypeScript, backend is FastAPI/Python
- Deep fluency with LLMs and generative AI — prompt
  engineering, function calling/tool use, multimodal models
- Excellent communication and collaboration skills

Nice to Have

- Experience building collaborative or real-time applications
  (CRDTs, WebSockets)
- Background in computer vision, image generation, or
  diffusion models
- Experience building tools or workflows for professional
  creatives (designers, artists, or adjacent domains)
- Contributions to open-source AI projects or published
  research
- Comfort operating in an early-stage startup with fast
  iteration cycles

Benefits

- Competitive salary and equity options
- Comprehensive benefits package including health, dental, and
  vision insurance
- Opportunity to work on cutting-edge AI technology with a
  high-impact team
- Fully remote with a supportive company culture
- Be part of a rapidly growing company with significant growth
  potential
