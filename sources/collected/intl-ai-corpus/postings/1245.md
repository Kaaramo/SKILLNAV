## Description complète

Design, implement, and support AI-driven automation
solutions leveraging Azure cloud services, Power Platform,
and RPA technologies. Collaborate with business stakeholders
and technical teams to deliver robust, scalable, and secure
process automation for financial workflows (e.g., wire
transfer and check balancing).

Requirements

Key Responsibilities:

- Develop and deploy automation workflows using Power
  Automate, Azure Functions (Python), and Azure OpenAI.
- Integrate data sources such as SFTP, OneDrive, Symitar, and
  other financial systems.
- Design, build, and maintain Power BI dashboards and reports.
- Ensure secure data handling, including encryption, RBAC, and
  compliance standards.
- Collaborate with cross-functional teams to gather
  requirements and deliver solutions.
- Monitor, troubleshoot, and optimize automation processes for
  performance and reliability.

Required Skills and Experience:

- 3+ years of experience with Azure cloud services, including
  Azure Functions, Blob Storage, and Azure OpenAI.
- Strong Python programming skills, with experience developing
  Azure Functions in Python.
- Hands-on experience with Power Platform (Power Automate,
  Power BI).
- Familiarity with RPA concepts and tools.
- Strong understanding of secure data integration (SFTP, API,
  database connectivity).
- Experience with financial systems (e.g., Symitar) and
  document processing.
- Knowledge of cloud security, RBAC, and compliance best
  practices.
- Excellent problem-solving, communication, and documentation
  skills.

Preferred:

- Experience in financial services or banking automation
  projects.
- Familiarity with legacy data systems and process
  modernization.

Flexible /Hybrid Work Hours
