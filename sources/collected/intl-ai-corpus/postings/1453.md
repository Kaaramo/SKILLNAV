## Description complète

Tabs is the leading AI-native revenue platform for modern
finance and accounting teams. Tabs agents automates the
entire contract-to-cash lifecycle, including billing,
collections, revenue recognition, and reporting, to help
teams eliminate manual work and accelerate cash flow.

High-growth companies like Cursor and Statsig rely on Tabs
to generate invoices directly from contracts, reconcile
payments in real time, and automate ASC 606 compliance.

Founded in 2023, Tabs has raised over $91 million from
Lightspeed Venture Partners, General Catalyst, and Primary.
The team is headquartered in New York and brings deep
expertise in finance and AI.

What We Do

We are enabling the one person finance team…

Artificial intelligence products that automate operational
management of accounting, communications, and reporting.
Finance leaders shouldn’t worry about updating new points of
contacts for an invoice; our systems enable them to deliver
strategy and vision to their business.

The Job

We are putting together a tight team of innovative product
engineers to join the AI team full stack. This is an in
person role in New York City.

- You will own building automation, AI, and ML features from
  concept, release, and integration into the product.
- We are looking beyond pattern matching, this isn’t a solved
  problem and we are looking for engineers who will generate
  new insights and solutions.
- You will work closely with company leadership and the
  founding team to deliver high impact projects with direct
  impact on market positioning of the business.

Who You Are

- Proven ability to build green field products, get it 80%
  right, and learn from the missing 20%.
- Experience taking ownership of projects and driving them
  through to completion
- 3-5 years of full stack experience in engineering that
  directly impacts the delivery of product and features to
  users
- 1+ year of experience on products that deliver AI/ML
  inference to end users. Can be anywhere in that lifecycle,
  from training machine learning systems, to creating the
  interfaces users use to navigate inference results.
- An interest and passion for AI/ML systems, if you haven’t
  had the opportunity at work to build AI/ML systems but
  have personal projects in the space we would love to hear
  about them.
- We don’t care what stack you’ve built in the past, but you
  will be building with Python, Typescript, and NextJs
  (bonus points if this is already your stack)

Exceptions to the above requirements are made for candidates
with exceptional stories. If this is you, we will consider
you regardless of the guidelines above.

Our Phenotype

We are not looking for people who like to coast or shop
offers for value. We are looking for gunners who are
attracted to non-traditional success. A specific type of
person does well here:

- You pride yourself on grit and creativity though you know
  you may not be the smartest in the room.
- You are obsessed with building and have a clear track record
  of ownership and shipping highly creative, innovative
  works.
- You are energized by working in-person with colleagues four
  or more days a week.
- You are opinionated on how product should be built and know
  when to fight for your ideas (and when to dissent and
  commit)
- When you see someone spilled coffee, you grab a mop.

We’re a NYC-based team working together in our Manhattan
office.

Even if you don’t meet 100% of the qualifications, we
encourage you to apply. We care most about curiosity, craft,
and drive.
