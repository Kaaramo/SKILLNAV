## Description complète

About us

At Inkeep, we power the only AI Agent platform designed for
engineers and non-engineers.

Our customers include leading AI companies like Anthropic
and Midjourney as well as high-growth tech companies like
PostHog and Postman. Our customers trust Inkeep to help them
provide a world-class support experience for their users and
a force multiplier for their employees.

We're growing quickly and looking for new team members who
like working at the forefront of AI with a fast and smart
team with high levels of ownership and impact.

Our investors include Khosla Ventures and Y Combinator.

About the role

Our customers are often highly technical teams who embed
Inkeep Agents into their customer-facing experiences (docs,
help center, marketing site, etc.) and internal workflows.
To enable this, we provide a full extensible Agent platform
which includes extensible UI components, AI and RAG APIs, an
Open Source Agent Framework. In this role, you'll be
partnering with both their technical and non-technical
business teams to help them on that journey.

The role involves being high-touch with customers to help
them succeed. If you want to become an expert on how
companies embed and deploy reliable AI systems, Inkeep is a
great fit.

Responsibilities

- Onboard and support customers through pilots
- Create templates, quickstarts, and other technical
  onboarding artifacts
- Use AI to make your work more efficient and our customers'
  lives easier

Qualifications

- 2+ years in solutions/sales engineering, support
  engineering, or other engineering roles with customer-
  facing responsibilities
- Strong experience with TypeScript (Python experience is a
  plus)
- Strong communication and organizational skills
- Located in NYC or willing to relocate (this role is in-
  office 5 days a week)

Bonus if you have...

- Experience with technical writing
- Experience working at a developer tool or B2B SaaS company

Benefits

- Health, Dental, and Vision insurance
- Unlimited PTO
