## Description complète

As a Staff Software Engineer on Datadog’s Cloud FinOps team,
you will help shape how the company understands, analyzes,
and optimizes its cloud infrastructure spend. This role sits
at the intersection of engineering, finance, and executive
leadership, building the systems and insights that drive
company-wide decisions about infrastructure usage, AI usage
and efficiency. You will design scalable solutions that
improve cost attribution, forecasting, and visibility across
Datadog’s multi-cloud environment. This is a high-impact
opportunity to influence strategic decisions, solve complex
infrastructure efficiency challenges, and help define the
future of FinOps capabilities at Datadog.

At Datadog, we place value in our office culture - the
relationships and collaboration it builds and the creativity
it brings to the table. We operate as a hybrid workplace to
ensure our Datadogs can create a work-life harmony that best
fits them.

What You’ll Do:

- Design and evolve internal platforms and tools that provide
  visibility into Datadog’s cloud infrastructure costs
  across AWS, GCP, and Azure.
- Partner with Finance, Engineering, and Executive Leadership
  to analyze cloud spend trends and inform strategic
  decisions.
- Develop scalable models and systems for cloud cost
  attribution across complex shared infrastructure.
- Build forward-looking FinOps capabilities that help teams
  proactively manage and optimize infrastructure usage.
- Work cross-functionally with engineering leaders to
  understand system architecture and translate
  infrastructure usage patterns into actionable cost
  insights.
- Influence company-wide initiatives around cost efficiency,
  forecasting, and infrastructure planning.
- Raises team performance by leading by example, building
  systems and patterns that improve productivity and
  autonomy by guiding with pragmatic technical judgment
  while being a hands-on contributor.

Who You Are:

- Experience designing or operating distributed systems at
  scale.
- Proven ability to lead large cross-organizational technical
  initiatives and influence senior stakeholders.
- Strong communication skills and ability to explain complex
  technical concepts to engineering, finance, and executive
  audiences.
- Experience working with cloud infrastructure platforms such
  as AWS, GCP, or Azure.
- Ability to operate autonomously in ambiguous problem spaces
  and develop scalable solutions.
- Interest in infrastructure efficiency, cost attribution, and
  FinOps-style problem domains.

Datadog values people from all walks of life. We understand
not everyone will meet all the above qualifications on day
one. That's okay. If you’re passionate about technology and
want to grow your skills, we encourage you to apply.
