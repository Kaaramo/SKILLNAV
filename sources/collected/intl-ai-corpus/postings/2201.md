## Description complète

Wati is the world's leading WhatsApp-first conversational
growth platform, empowering businesses to build deeper
customer relationships and accelerate revenue growth.

Trusted and loved by over 16,000 customers across 190+
countries, Wati has established itself as the premier choice
for businesses leveraging WhatsApp and other messaging
channels to connect with customers on their platform of
choice.

In a world where real-time interactions drive growth, Wati's
story is all about business growth through conversations. At
Wati, we believe that every conversation matters—whether
it's engaging a first-time prospect, nurturing a qualified
lead, or deepening relationships with loyal clients.

Our platform transforms these conversations into
opportunities, where strong relationships ultimately drive
measurable revenue outcomes.

Our Platform

Wati is building an AI-first GTM (Go-To-Market) engine to
scale revenue without scaling headcount.

We are evolving from structured growth to intelligent
automation at scale, where systems, data, and AI work
together to power revenue teams.

This role sits at the core of that transformation.

About the Role

We are looking for a GTM Engineer – Revenue Technology (AI &
Automation) to design and build the systems that power our
go-to-market engine.

You will work across Sales, Marketing, Customer Success, and
RevOps to understand how GTM operates in reality (not just
how it is documented), and translate that into scalable
systems.

Your goal is to turn fragmented tools, workflows, and data
into a unified, intelligent revenue engine, while leveraging
automation and AI to significantly improve team output.

What You’ll Do

- Design and build systems to automate lead routing, pipeline
  movement, and lifecycle workflows
- Sync data across CRM, billing, product, and support tools
- Create real-time signals for sales, expansion, and retention
- Own systems end-to-end: Discovery → Build → Rollout →
  Adoption → Iteration
- Build sales automation workflows, account intelligence
  systems, and pipeline infrastructure
- Develop expansion and churn detection engines
- Build AI-powered systems to qualify and enrich leads
- Detect buying signals and prioritize accounts
- Surface deal risks and recommend next-best actions
- Automate repetitive decision-making across GTM workflows
- Transform CRM, product usage, campaign, and conversation
  data into actionable insights
- Build systems that provide real-time intelligence to revenue
  teams
- Continuously improve conversion rates, speed to lead,
  pipeline velocity, and sales productivity
- Define success metrics and own outcomes end-to-end

Requirements

- Experience building automation, integrations, or AI-driven
  systems in a SaaS or GTM environment
- Strong understanding of business systems, workflows, APIs,
  and automation logic
- Hands-on experience with CRM platforms, automation tools, or
  data systems
- Comfort working with messy data and fast iteration cycles
- Strong ownership mindset with a focus on measurable business
  impact (pipeline, conversion, velocity)
- Builder mindset — you prefer creating systems over managing
  processes

Tech Stack

CRM & CX: HubSpot, Intercom Billing: Stripe, Chargebee
Automation: Clay, Zapier, Make, n8n Development: JavaScript,
Python, Node.js, REST APIs, Webhooks Data: SQL, BI tools,
dashboards AI: LLM APIs, Prompt Engineering, AI Agents

What Success Looks Like

- GTM operates on scalable, automated systems
- AI handles high-volume operational work
- Revenue teams operate with real-time intelligence
- Revenue becomes more predictable and data-driven
