Reporting to the Engineering Manager, Senior AI Engineer
Specialist to lead the design, development, and deployment
of cutting-edge AI solutions that leverage large language
models (LLMs), retrieval-augmented generation (RAG), and
agentic AI architectures. This is a hands-on leadership role
for a builder and systems thinker who can push generative AI
from experimentation into scalable, production-ready
capabilities.

This role is in Bangalore, India (Quarterly once visit to
the office for 2 weeks).

In this role, a typical day would include:

- Architectural Leadership: Design scalable, resilient AI
  architectures that support multi-tenant, global packaging
  solutions. Define the blueprint for how Esko handles
  agentic reasoning and distributed memory.
- Core Infrastructure & LLMOps: Lead the development of
  internal platforms for model lifecycle management,
  including automated retraining loops, deployment
  strategies (Canary/Blue-Green), and cost-attribution
  systems.
- Advanced R&D: Conduct feasibility studies on emerging tech
  (e.g., State Space Models, small language model
  distillation, or custom embedding architectures) to keep
  Esko ahead of the competition.
- Evaluation Strategy: Establish organization-wide standards
  for Model Governance and Red-Teaming. Build the framework
  that measures the "ROI of Accuracy" for every feature we
  ship.
- Technical Mentorship: Act as a force multiplier by mentoring
  Senior AI Engineers, conducting deep-dive code reviews,
  and fostering a culture of rigorous scientific
  experimentation.
- Cross-Functional Influence: Partner with executive
  leadership and Product VPs to translate business vision
  into a technical AI roadmap.

Required Skills & Qualifications

- Education: Master’s or PhD in Computer Science, Machine
  Learning, or a related field (or equivalent deep industry
  experience).
- Experience: 10+ years in Software Engineering, with 5+ years
  in AI/ML leadership. You have successfully taken multiple
  LLM-based products from "Alpha" to "Enterprise Scale."
- Expertise in Training & Alignment: Deep experience with
  Full-Parameter Fine-Tuning and Alignment Techniques (DPO,
  PPO, or KTO). Experience generating and filtering high-
  quality Synthetic Data for domain-specific model training.
- Systems Engineering: Expertise in high-throughput inference
  (vLLM, TensorRT-LLM) and low-latency vector retrieval at
  the scale of billions of vectors. Strong understanding of
  distributed computing (Ray, Kubernetes, or Spark).
- Strategic Benchmarking: Ability to design domain-specific
  evaluation moats that go beyond public benchmarks to
  measure real-world packaging design utility.

Preferred Qualifications

- Experience with Vision-Language Models (VLM) for analysing
  packaging graphics or 3D structural data.
- Knowledge of deploying distilled models to edge devices or
  client-side environments.
- A known contributor to the AI ecosystem (e.g., maintainer of
  a popular library or published research).
- Experience with serving frameworks like vLLM, NVIDIA Triton,
  or TGI (Text Generation Inference).
