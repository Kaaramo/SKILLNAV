## Description complète

About Acrisure

A global fintech leader, Acrisure empowers millions of
ambitious businesses and individuals with the right
solutions to grow boldly forward. Bringing cutting-edge
technology and top-tier human support together, we connect
clients with customized solutions across a range of
insurance, reinsurance, payroll, benefits, cybersecurity,
mortgage services – and more.

In the last twelve years, Acrisure has grown in revenue from
$38 million to almost $5 billion and employs over 19,000
colleagues in more than 20 countries. Acrisure was built on
entrepreneurial spirit. Prioritizing leadership,
accountability, and collaboration, we equip our teams to
work at the highest levels possible.

Acrisure is seeking a Data/AI Engineer to join our growing
team and focus on our International entities. In 2026, the
primary focus will be the design and delivery of global KPIs
across multiple international entities. This will involve
integrating data from over 20 systems, establishing
standardized KPI definitions, performing data transformation
and modelling, and developing scalable dashboards and
reporting solutions. Parallel and subsequent phases will
focus on workflow automation, the rollout of AI-enabled
capabilities, and broader operational optimisation
initiatives.

Responsibilities:

- Design and deliver scalable data pipelines using a mix of
  lowcode and codebased tooling to establish a robust,
  governed data foundation.

- Translate complex insurance and brokerage workflows into
  scalable data and technology solutions, ensuring they are
  aligned to business processes and measurable outcomes.

- Model core business concepts and relationships in a unified
  data model, using structured objects, links,
  transformations, and governed logic.

- Build decision centric operational applications focused on
  business value, workflow efficiency, and KPI visibility,
  leveraging modern development frameworks and analytics
  tooling.

- Develop and deploy safe, reliable, and auditable AIdriven
  solutions, including agents, automations, and decision-
  support capabilities, using appropriate governance,
  testing, and monitoring practices.

- Collaborate with cross-functional technical and business
  teams to ensure solutions are scalable, reusable, and
  deliver clear return on investment



Required Qualifications

- 5–10+ years of experience building data-driven products

- Demonstrated success delivering production use cases in
  regulated or data-sensitive domains (financial services or
  insurance preferred).

- Strong coding experience in Python and
  TypeScript/JavaScript; SQL proficiency.
- Bachelor’s or Master’s degree in Computer Science,
  Engineering, Mathematics, or a related technical field.
- Equivalent industry experience building production-grade
  data and AI systems accepted.

Preferred Qualifications

- Prior experience in the insurance domain, particularly in
  underwriting, placement, claims, or finance.

- Familiarity with ML/LLM concepts such as retrieval-augmented
  generation, agent design, and human-in-the-loop models.

- Demonstrated enablement leadership: building reusable
  templates, documentation, and coaching cross-functional
  teams.

- Experience with event-driven architectures, API
  integrations, CI/CD, and infrastructure-as-code in cloud
  environments.

- Effective communicator with a systems-thinking mindset and
  ability to translate business workflows into scalable
  solutions.
- Exposure to Palantir Foundry and AIP is advantageous and
  will enhance your impact across our data ecosystem.
- Advanced degree or certifications in cloud computing, AI/ML,
  data engineering, or solution architecture.
- Familiarity with Spark, Databricks, or similar distributed
  processing frameworks.
