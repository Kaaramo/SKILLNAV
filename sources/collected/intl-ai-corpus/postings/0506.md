## Description complète

Opportunity

In 2025, tools like Cursor, Devin, and Claude gave engineers
superpowers, allowing them to ship faster than ever before.
But for the rest of the world — Sales, Ops, Marketing, and
Finance — work largely hasn't changed. They're still
drowning in Slack threads, manual data entry, and "good
enough" ChatGPT responses.

We're here to fix that.

Vellum is building the platform where anyone can describe a
goal in plain language and generate reliable, working agents
in seconds (think "Lovable for agents" and "Devin for
everyone else").

Our Values

We're a high-performance team in one of the most competitive
software markets of all time, so we're looking for S-tier
builders who align with our core values:

Lean into the future

We don't just build AI tools; we live in the future.

- We use AI tools (Cursor, Claude, etc.) extensively in our
  daily workflows.
- We skate to where the puck is going. You should be excited
  to build what customers need before they even ask for it.
- We cut the right corners to go fast, but we build our
  products and processes for the long run.

Say what you mean; mean what you say

We operate in an extremely high-trust environment.

- We speak openly and honestly, even when it's hard.
- We receive feedback without getting defensive.
- If we say we're going to do something, we do it. If we can't
  keep a commitment, we communicate it immediately.

Play to win

There are going to be huge companies built in this space,
and we're going to be one of them. We've raised $25M from
top-tier investors and we intend to spend it aggressively to
win this market. In other words, we're swinging for the
fences.

- We love winning and know it won't be easy. This means we
  move extremely fast, make sacrifices, and do whatever it
  takes.
- We demand executional excellence from ourselves and each
  other.
- We push ourselves hard to maximize long-term output and are
  resilient to burnout.

Who You Are

- You've founded or worked at an early-stage startup.
- You have deep experience building coding agents or other
  complex AI systems.
- You're not a researcher - you're an engineer applying AI in
  the real world.
- You're an early adopter of AI tools that make you more
  effective.
- You're ready to build alongside us in our New York office.
- You crave ownership and are ready to take on significant
  responsibility from Day 1.
- You're excited to build the tools that will redefine how
  people work.

If this sounds like you, welcome home.

Let's get to work.
