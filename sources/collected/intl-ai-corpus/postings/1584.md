## Description complète

We're looking for a Staff Software Engineer with deep
experience in AI/ML to join the Notebooks team. Datadog
Notebooks provide a flexible tool for ad-hoc data analysis,
collaborative investigations, and technical documentation.
Its fluidity makes it an ideal surface for agents and humans
to interact. The Notebook team is building AI-driven
workflows for natural-language data analysis, assisted graph
generation, automated investigations, and more. You will act
as a technical leader in this domain, focused on building
industry-leader AI-powered features. We’re looking for a
product-minded ML engineer with strong technical expertise,
excellent communication skills, and a track record of
driving impactful initiatives end to end.

At Datadog, we place value in our office culture - the
relationships and collaboration it builds and the creativity
it brings to the table. We operate as a hybrid workplace to
ensure our Datadogs can create a work-life harmony that best
fits them.

What You’ll Do:

- Spearhead AI initiatives within Notebooks, serving as the
  technical owner for the design, development, and
  deployment of ML/AI-driven features.
- Shape the long-term technical direction and strategy for AI
  workflows within Notebooks and related products.
- Champion the full development cycle: from rapid prototyping
  and defining success metrics/evaluations to running
  experiments, iterating, and achieving production-ready
  scale and reliability.
- Drive product direction with a strong user focus, always
  advocating for the best end-user experience.
- Coordinate with other leaders and product teams interested
  in leveraging Notebooks in their AI-powered workflows.
- Develop robust agent systems, including tools, retrieval and
  planning strategies, guardrails, prompt
  management/evaluations, fallbacks, and human-in-the-loop
  processes.
- Stay up to date with industry trends, guiding the adoption
  of new technologies and evolving best-practices.

Who You Are:

- You have a BS/MS/PhD in a scientific field or equivalent
  experience
- 10+ years of relevant engineering experience, as well as
  experience acting as a technical lead
- Proven track record of leading large-scale AI/ML initiatives
  in a product-driven environment
- You have significant experience in model deployment,
  development, training, fine-tuning, and evaluation

- You are passionate about mentoring others and cultivating a
  high-performing engineering culture.
- You’re excited about leveraging AI tools to enhance how you
  code, solve problems, and build

Datadog values people from all walks of life. We understand
not everyone will meet all the above qualifications on day
one. That's okay. If you’re passionate about technology and
want to grow your skills, we encourage you to apply.
