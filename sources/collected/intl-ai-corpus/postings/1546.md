Lead AI/ML Engineer

Purpose

To lead the design, architecture, and delivery of
enterprise-grade AI/ML solutions using NLP, NLU, LLM, and
Generative AI technologies, while providing technical
leadership, mentoring engineers, and ensuring scalable,
production-ready AI systems.

Responsibilities & Authorities

- Lead architecture and development of advanced AI/ML
  solutions
- Define and implement end-to-end AI pipelines (data → model →
  deployment → monitoring)
- Establish AI engineering standards, best practices, and
  governance
- Mentor AI/ML engineers and enforce code quality and
  technical excellence
- Collaborate with product and business teams to translate
  requirements into AI solutions
- Optimize models for performance, scalability, and production
  reliability
- Evaluate and adopt new AI tools, frameworks, and
  technologies
- Own delivery timelines, risk management, and quality
  standards
- Communicate technical trade-offs and solutions to
  stakeholders
- Authority over technical design, model approach, and AI
  architecture decisions

Requirements

- Bachelor’s/Master’s degree in CS/IT/Data Science or related
  field
- 6+ years of AI/ML experience with production deployments
- Strong expertise in Python, OOP, FastAPI, Prompt Engineering
- Hands-on experience with NLP, NLU, LLMs, and Generative AI
- Experience with Docker, Jenkins, CI/CD, and MLOps practices
- Experience leading AI/ML engineering teams

- Strong problem-solving and communication skills
