About the Role:

Gartner is looking for passionate and motivated Senior
Software Engineers who are excited to foray into new
technologies and help build / maintain data driven

deployment etc.

What you’ll do:

- Support AI/ML application development to ensure delivery of
  desired business outcomes.
- Build sophisticated AI-powered systems that provide
  intelligent, personalized client experiences at scale
- Design and implement Model Context Protocol (MCP) servers to
  enable seamless integration between AI agents, enterprise
  systems, and external tools
- Build user profiling and personalization models to deliver
  tailored experiences
- Take ownership of algorithms and drive
  enhancements/optimizations based on business requirements
  with proper documentation and code-reusability
- Work on Cloud Providers like AWS and Azure.
- Explore and create POC of AI/ML technologies and frameworks
- Setting up best practices for coding standards w.r.t. AI/ML
  applications
- Work in a globally distributed team in an Agile/Scrum
  approach

What you’ll need:

Strong IT professional experience in ML and Gen AI
application development. The candidate should have strong
qualitative and quantitative problem-solving skills along
with high ownership and accountability.

Must have:

- 5 to 7 years of Information Technology experience with 2
  years of relevant experience building conversational AI
  systems, chatbots, LLM applications, or other advanced
  machine learning/artificial intelligence solutions to
  drive business impact
- Practical, intuitive problem solver with proven ability to
  translate business objectives into actionable data science
  tasks and implement state-of-the-art ML research into
  production systems
- Working experience in some of the following areas:
  - Large Language Models (LLMs) and Generative AI
  - Conversational AI, chatbot development, and dialogue systems
  - Natural Language Processing and text mining
  - Search and Recommendation systems
  - Prompt engineering, LLM fine-tuning, and model optimization
  - AI agent architectures and orchestration
- Experience and proficiency with Python, deep learning
  frameworks (e.g., pytorch, tensorflow, huggingface), LLM
  frameworks (e.g., LangChain, LangGraph, LlamaIndex),

- Experience in Python and related API frameworks.
- Excellent problem solving and troubleshooting skills.
