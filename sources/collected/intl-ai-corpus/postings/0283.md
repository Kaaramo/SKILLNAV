## Description complète

About Beli

By changing the way people explore restaurants, Beli has
emerged as one of the fastest-growing consumer platforms in
the country. Beli helps you build organized restaurant lists
and maps, share them with friends, and discover new spots
around the world according to your personal taste.

Job Overview

As an AI/ML Engineer Intern at Beli, you'll have the unique
opportunity to apply cutting-edge artificial intelligence to
enhance how people discover and experience restaurants. Over
the course of a 10-week, high-impact internship, you’ll
research, prototype, and integrate AI-driven features that
elevate Beli’s user experience—from personalization and
content understanding to search, discovery, and beyond.
You’ll work closely with the CTO, CEO, and software
engineers in a fast-paced, collaborative environment where
your ideas will directly shape the future of the product.

Responsibilities

- Analyze & Research: Explore Beli’s rich dataset of user
  behavior and restaurant information to uncover
  opportunities for applying AI across the product.
- Build & Test: Develop and refine machine learning models and
  AI-powered features (e.g., natural language understanding,
  content summarization, personalization, or ranking).
- Deploy & Scale: Implement solutions that are performant,
  cost-effective, and ready for production—bringing your
  work to millions of Beli users.
- Collaborate & Innovate: Work with product and engineering
  teams to brainstorm creative applications of AI and
  evaluate their potential impact.

Qualifications

- Currently pursuing a Master’s or PhD in Computer Science,
  AI, ML, Statistics, or a related field
- Experience with applied machine learning, data science, or
  AI product integration
- Strong analytical skills with the ability to work with large
  datasets
- Excellent communication and collaboration skills
- A hands-on, problem-solving mindset with intellectual
  curiosity and creativity
