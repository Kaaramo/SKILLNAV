Your Opportunity

We are seeking an experienced AI Engineer with a strong
foundation in software engineering and a proven track record
of building Generative and Agentic AI solutions. This new
role is ideal for someone who thrives at the intersection of
software development, applied AI research, and practical
solution design. You will be responsible for designing,
implementing, and deploying production-grade AI systems that
leverage the latest in large language models (LLMs),
orchestration frameworks, and agentic architectures.

Key Responsibilities

- Design & Development
  - Build scalable and robust AI-driven applications using LLMs
    and agentic frameworks.
  - Implement vector database solutions to enable semantic
    search, RAG (retrieval augmented generation), and
    knowledge management.
  - Develop orchestration pipelines with frameworks like
    LangChain, Semantic Kernel, AutoGen, or emerging agentic
    frameworks such as Agno and Parlant.
- Automation & Integration
  - Integrate workflow automation platforms (n8n, dify.ai Power
    Automate, etc.) with AI systems to deliver end-to-end
    business solutions.
  - Ensure seamless integration with existing enterprise
    software ecosystems and APIs.
- Quality & Evaluation
  - Apply or contribute to LLM evaluation methodologies,
    leveraging platforms like Arize, DeepEval, or IBM
    WatsonX for model benchmarking, monitoring, and
    continuous improvement.
  - Establish best practices for testing, performance tuning,
    and evaluation of agentic/LLM-based workflows.
- Collaboration & Delivery
  - Work closely with cross-functional teams including data
    scientists, software engineers, and product owners.
  - Translate business requirements into technical designs and
    deliver working AI-powered applications in production
    environments.

Required Skills & Qualifications

- Strong background in software engineering (e.g., system
  design, distributed systems, APIs, CI/CD).
- Proven experience in building Generative AI and/or agentic
  systems.
- Hands-on expertise with:
  - LLMs and model fine-tuning or integration.
  - Vector databases (e.g., Pinecone, Weaviate, Milvus, FAISS,
    etc.).
  - LLM orchestration frameworks (LangChain, Semantic Kernel,
    AutoGen, etc.).
  - Agentic frameworks (Pydantic AI, Agno, Parlant, or similar).
- Proficiency in at least one major programming language such
  as Python or .NET (strong engineering fundamentals matter
  more than specific syntax).
- Hands-on experience architecting, deploying, and operating
  cloud-native solutions on one or more major hyperscale
  platforms (Azure, AWS, or GCP)
- Experience with workflow automation tools like n8n, dify.ai,
  Power Automate, or similar platforms.

Nice-to-Have Skills

- Familiarity with LLM evaluation frameworks (Arize, DeepEval,
  IBM WatsonX).
- Contributions to open-source AI frameworks or communities.
- Experience in MLOps, monitoring, and model deployment
  pipelines.

Who You Are

- A problem-solver with strong engineering rigor and curiosity
  for cutting-edge AI.
- Comfortable moving between architecture, coding, and
  deployment.
- Passionate about transforming abstract business needs into
  intelligent, production-ready systems.
