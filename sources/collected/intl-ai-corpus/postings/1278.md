The Senior AI Automation Engineer, Business Systems designs,
builds, and operates GenAI-powered automations and agents

What you’ll do:

- Design, build, and ship GenAI automations and agents into
  internal business workflows
- Write and maintain application code, orchestration logic,
  and integration components
- Integrate with business systems and tools (e.g., CRM, ERP,
  HCM, contact center, collaboration tools) via APIs,
  events, and workflows
- Leverage shared data/platform capabilities (data access
  patterns, connectors, reliability practices) rather than
  rebuilding foundations
- Define and apply quality checks for agent behavior; iterate
  based on usage, feedback, and failure modes
- Engineer for production: reliability, security,
  observability, and cost awareness
- Partner with stakeholders to clarify requirements, validate
  outputs, and support rollout into operations
- Capture reusable patterns, templates, and technical
  blueprints to speed up future delivery

What you’ll bring:

- 5+ years of professional experience in software engineering,
  automation engineering, or systems integration

  tool/function calling; retrieval patterns where needed)
- Solid engineering fundamentals: testing, debugging, code
  quality, and operational readiness
- Strong problem-solving and ability to move from ambiguity to
  implementation with disciplined scoping
- Effective communication across technical and non-technical
  stakeholders
