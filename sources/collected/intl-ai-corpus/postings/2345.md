## Description complète

About Us

Hawk is the leading provider of AI-supported anti-money
laundering and fraud detection technology. Banks and payment
providers globally are using Hawk’s powerful combination of
traditional rules and explainable AI to improve the
effectiveness of their AML compliance and fraud prevention
by identifying more crime while maximizing efficiency by
reducing false positives. With our solution, we are playing
a vital role in the global fight against Money Laundering,
Fraud, or the financing of terrorism. We offer a culture of
mutual trust, support and passion – while providing
individuals with opportunities to grow professionally and
make a difference in the world.

Your Mission

As a Senior UX/UI Engineer at Hawk, your mission is to
design, architect, and build the user experience for our
next-generation, AI-powered Anti-Money- Laundering
platform—specifically focusing on our new agentic AI
Investigation Agent and other new agentic products. You will
operate at the critical intersection of UI, UX, and Product,
shaping the vision for how human analysts interact with
autonomous AI workflows. You will draft and implement
cutting-edge UX capabilities for complex case management,
customer-centric data views, and dynamic grouping, ensuring
that AI-driven insights are surfaced intuitively. This is a
high-ownership role requiring you to look at problems
holistically, establish best-in-class human-machine
interfaces, and deliver scalable, high-quality frontend
solutions.

Key Responsibilities

- Drive the UX strategy, design, and end-to-end frontend
  implementation for completely new product capabilities,
  including our agentic AI workflow automation and case
  management tools.
- Build strong, intuitive human/machine interfaces that
  effectively surface complex agentic AI results and
  explainability metrics, making fraud and AML data
  actionable for human analysts.
- Lead the development of complex UI topics, such as
  comprehensive 360-degree customer-centric views
  (aggregating key metrics across different systems) and
  dynamic entity grouping workflows.
- Architect and build scalable, maintainable frontend
  components using React. You will leverage shadcn/ui for
  our newest solutions (like the Investigation Agent) while
  maintaining and bridging components in our existing
  Material UI ecosystem.
- Step outside the traditional "developer box." Partner
  closely with Product Management, Backend Engineering
  (FastAPI/LangGraph), and Data Science to shape the product
  roadmap, translate user needs into technical reality, and
  question the"why" behind features.
- Actively leverage and champion the use of agentic coding
  tools (e.g., Claude Code, Google Antigravity) to
  accelerate development, improve code quality, and
  establish modern engineering workflows.
- Support strategic decision-making, establish frontend
  coding/design best practices, and ensure a consistent,
  high-quality user experience across the application suite.

Your Profile

- 4+ years of software development and UI/UX design
  experience.(Note: We are open to exceptional candidates
  with 2-3 years of experience if theydemonstrate a
  remarkably strong track record).
- Proven experience working seamlessly at the intersection of
  UI engineering, UX design, and product strategy. You can
  conceptualize a user journey and write the code to make it
  real.
- Deep expertise in frontend development using React. Strong
  hands-on experience architecting and bridging modern
  design systems and component libraries—specifically
  shadcn/ui and Material UI—is highly preferred.
- Demonstrated ability to design and implement user interfaces
  for data-heavy, B2B, or enterprise applications (e.g.,
  workflow automation, analytics dashboards, or case
  management systems).
- Hands-on experience and comfort utilizing modern AI coding
  assistants (Claude Code, Cursor etc.) as an integrated
  part of your daily development workflow.
- Excellent holistic thinking and problem-solving skills. You
  are a self-starter who helps shape the product vision
  rather than just executing pre-defined tickets.
- Excellent communicator with a collaborative mindset, capable
  of articulating design decisions and technical trade-offs
  to both technical and non-technical stakeholders
- Committed to user-centric design and delivering applications
