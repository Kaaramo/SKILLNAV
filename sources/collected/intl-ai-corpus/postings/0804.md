## Description complète

About the Team

We’re a builder team at Pattern that ships products to
streamline operations and open new markets. We move quickly,
iterate directly with users, and give engineers real
autonomy and ownership. AI is part of our daily toolkit—used
to speed up product management, elevate experience design,
and increase engineering velocity. We share what works and
continually raise the bar together.

The Role

- We’re looking for a Senior Software Engineer who enjoys
  building innovative, scalable solutions for ambiguous
  problems. You’ll work across the stack, collaborate with
  product and design, and own projects from architecture
  through launch and iteration.

Problem Spaces You May Work In

- You won’t be limited to a single domain. Recent and ongoing
  initiatives include:
- Short-Form Video Intelligence & Creative Tooling -Analyze
  patterns in social video and power data-driven creative
  workflows (insight extraction, content scoring, generation
  pipelines).
- Marketplace Content Optimization at Scale -Synthesize/enrich
  product listings, normalize attributes and taxonomies, and
  enable rapid experimentation to improve conversion.
- Applied Automation & Robotics Enablement - Connect
  perception, workflow orchestration, and operator tools to
  improve reliability and throughput in physical operations.
- AI Platform & Agentic Workflows - Retrieval, evaluation,
  guardrails, and observability services that integrate AI
  deeply into product experiences with the right
  cost/latency/quality trade-offs.

Our Preferred Stack

- We hire senior engineering ability and adaptability. If
  you’re willing to adopt our tools, we want to talk—even if
  your background differs.
- Languages & Frameworks: Python, FastAPI, React/Next.js(Good
  to know)
- Cloud & Infrastructure: AWS (Terraform (Good to know))
- Data: SQL

What You’ll Do

- Design, build, and operate production systems with clear
  interfaces, tests, and observability.
- Partner directly with users and stakeholders to validate
  value and iterate quickly.
- Apply AI thoughtfully to accelerate development and improve
  outcomes, and share your approach with the team.
- Make pragmatic architecture decisions and own projects end-
  to-end.

What We’re Looking For

- Senior-level experience delivering and operating production
  software (typically 4+ years).
- Strong backend skills (APIs, data modeling, cloud services)
  and comfort working across the stack.
- Product mindset: bias to action, care for UX, reliability,
  and maintainability.
- Curiosity and sound judgment in applying AI to real
  workflows.
