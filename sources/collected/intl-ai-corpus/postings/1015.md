## Description complète

Airbnb was born in 2007 when two hosts welcomed three guests
to their San Francisco home, and has since grown to over 5
million hosts who have welcomed over 2 billion guest
arrivals in almost every country across the globe. Every
day, hosts offer unique stays and experiences that make it
possible for guests to connect with communities in a more
authentic way.

The Community You Will Join:

The Tools & Technologies Experience (TNT) team sits at the
center of Airbnb’s digital employee experience
transformation. We bring together engineering, product
thinking, business processes, and knowledge management to
reimagine how employees interact with their work. Our
mission is to make internal tools smarter, workflows more
seamless, and employee decisions more data-driven and
personalized.

As part of TNT, you’ll join a curious, collaborative, and
ambitious team committed to building the future of employee
experience through the strategic use of AI.

The Difference You Will Make:

In this role, you will architect the next generation of
Airbnb’s internal operating system by designing and building
intelligent, AI-powered solutions that address real Employee
Experience (EX) pain points. You will translate ambiguous HR
and workforce needs into scalable products, stitch together
systems and data flows, and rapidly build prototypes that
demonstrate what’s possible.

You’ll be expected to move fluidly between engineering,
product strategy, systems architecture, and narrative
storytelling. You’ll independently build working prototypes
- agent flows, lightweight apps, LLM-driven assistants -
that can progress from concept to stakeholder testing in
days, not months, and that can evolve into production-ready
solutions with minimal lift from partner teams.

This is a highly entrepreneurial role that requires zooming
between high-level EX systems thinking and hands-on
implementation. You’ll partner closely with EX, BizTech,
Legal, Privacy, and InfoSec to ensure solutions are
innovative, ethical, and securely designed.

A Typical Day:

- Translate employee and manager workflows into well-defined
  AI product opportunities
- Rapidly prototype and test working solutions (LLM agents,
  workflow automations, microapps, embedded tools, etc.)
- Architect systems integrations across our HR tech stack
  (Workday, Greenhouse, Qualtrics, Airtable, RightAnswers,
  Asana, and more)
- Lead discovery sessions with stakeholders to uncover
  automation and augmentation opportunities
- Evaluate third-party AI tools and determine build vs. buy
  options
- Design and execute fast-cycle pilots across knowledge
  management, decision support, communications, and resource
  planning
- Define success metrics, measure impact, and iterate based on
  user feedback
- Translate complex AI architectures into clear narratives
  that help stakeholders understand value and adoption paths
- Ensure alignment with Airbnb’s privacy, security, and
  ethical AI guardrailsContinuously evolve EX’s AI strategy
  as organizational needs and technologies shift
