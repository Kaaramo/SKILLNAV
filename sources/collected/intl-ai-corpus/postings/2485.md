ABOUT THE TEAM

We are hiring an AI-Native Quality Engineer to help
establish the future of our software validation and
verification capabilities in the age of AI.

This role is designed for an early-career engineer with
strong experience in artificial intelligence, machine
learning workflows, or modern automation tooling who wants
to apply those skills to one of the hardest unsolved
problems in all of software engineering: turning product
intent into trustworthy, testable, verifiable system
behavior.

You will work at the intersection of application
engineering, product development, and applied AI to help
translate ideas into structured specifications, executable
examples, and scalable validation systems. Along the way,
you will learn modern Quality Engineering disciplines such
as Definition of Ready, Example Mapping, Specification by
Example, and behavior-focused acceptance criteria – while
helping us explore how AI can transform those practices.

This role is a discipline-building apprenticeship with
meaningful technical ownership from day one and mentorship
from senior engineering leaders shaping next-generation
specification-driven delivery systems.

WHAT YOU’LL DO

Software teams now build faster than ever–but confidence
still depends on how clearly behavior is defined, validated,
and verified.

We are investing in a new generation of engineers who can
combine:

- AI-native thinking
- structured system reasoning
- specification clarity
- automation fluency
- validation and verification disciplines

to help modernize how software organizations measure
correctness and readiness.

You will help shape the future here.

Automation & Verification Engineering (~30%)

- Contribute to API verification using REST testing tools and
  schema-based checks
- Help translate structured examples into executable
  verification artifacts
- Support UI and integration automation aligned with
  documented behavior
- Assist with test data setup and environment readiness
- Contribute to CI/CD verification workflows
- Analyze unexpected behavior discovered during automated
  execution
- Improve signal quality from automated feedback loops

Specification & Behavioral Clarity (~30%)

- Support structured refinement activities that make software
  behavior explicit before implementation begins
- Participate in Example Mapping and specification workshops
  with engineers and product managers
- Translate feature ideas into structured scenarios and
  acceptance criteria
- Capture assumptions, edge cases, and behavioral expectations
  early in development
- Help create reusable specification templates and example
  libraries
- Apply AI-assisted workflows to improve requirements clarity
  and consistency
- Identify ambiguity before it becomes rework

AI-Enabled Quality Engineering Exploration (~40%)

- Prototype AI-assisted approaches to specification generation
- Experiment with prompt-driven verification workflows
- Explore semantic regression detection techniques
- Evaluate machine-interpretable documentation strategies
- Integrate AI-assisted tooling into requirements and
  specifications, test generation, maintenance, failure
  analysis, and reporting workflows
- Help identify innovative opportunities to improve
  engineering confidence using modern AI tooling

WHO YOU ARE

- 0-2 years of experience in software engineering, AI/ML
  workflows, or applied automation
- Strong proficiency in Python, JavaScript/TypeScript, or
  another modern programming language
- Hands-on experience working with APIs or modern web systems
- Demonstrated interest in artificial intelligence, prompt

- Ability to reason about system behavior through structured
  examples
- Strong written communication skills
- Curiosity about how complex systems behave under changing
  conditions
- Interest in learning modern validation and verification
  disciplines

Preferred Experience
