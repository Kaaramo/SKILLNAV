About the team

We are seeking an AI Engineer to join our Data Science team

About the role

You will own the systems that safely streamline business
processes using LLMs and generative AI. You’ll work on
projects including:

- Improving our internal chatbot that supports customer ops
- Expanding our in-house schema extraction pipelines for
  faster, more accurate document parsing automation
- Designing LLM-driven workflows that streamline various case
  management workflows
- Identifying new opportunities where GenAI can radically
  simplify how we operate

This role blends applied AI engineering, business-process
design, and hands-on Python development. You’ll be working
on problems that don’t have perfect definitions, where
ambiguity is normal and creativity is essential.

In this role, you will

- Build real, production GenAI systems - not demos - that
  directly shape how a leading fintech operates
- Work with LLMs, retrieval systems, agents, and automation
  pipelines on a daily basis
- Own your solutions from idea → prototype → production
  deployment → monitoring
- Work in small, autonomous teams that ship fast and give you
  real decision-making power
- Redesign legacy processes from scratch using AI instead of
  patching around them
- Bring curiosity, resourcefulness, and a desire to replace
  manual work with smarter systems

Key skills

- Strong Python engineering skills, comfortable building
  production-grade systems
- Hands-on experience using LLMs in real workflows
- Ability to design, evaluate, and iterate on prompts,
  retrieval pipelines, and agentic flows
- Understanding of business processes and an instinct for
  simplifying or automating them
- Comfortable working with ambiguity, quickly testing
  hypotheses, and refining ideas with partial information
- Skilled at breaking poorly defined problems into structured
  steps and deciding when/how to bring AI into the loop
- Clear communicator who can articulate trade-offs and
  influence stakeholders
