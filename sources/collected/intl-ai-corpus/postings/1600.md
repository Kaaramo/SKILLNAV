### Raisonnement classification (LLM upstream)

This is a backend engineering role focused on integrating AI
APIs into production systems and building backend services
that bring AI capabilities to payroll workflows. The
engineer collaborates with ML engineers but does not
directly build, train, fine-tune, or deploy models - they
integrate existing AI services and build the infrastructure
to support AI-powered tools.
