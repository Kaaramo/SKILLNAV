## Description complète

About this role

Role Overview

We are seeking a hands-on, execution-oriented Vice President
to work directly with the Chief Strategic Advisor to Aladdin
on identifying, exploring, and applying AI and new
technologies and tools to a broad set of business problems
at BlackRock.

This role is focused on experimentation, learning, and
prototyping rather than ownership of said tools or platform.
The prototypes may lead to production implementation which
will be done through the appropriate existing teams at
BlackRock.  The successful candidate may be exposed to a
wide range of business problems—spanning investing,
operations, client service, distribution, and corporate
functions—and will help brainstorm, prototype, and
demonstrate how AI can improve productivity and unlock new
business opportunities.

The position will sit within the broader Portfolio Analytics
Group (PAG) team to enable collaboration and sharing of best
practices, while reporting both to Strategic Advisor and
PAG.

Key Responsibilities

Applied AI & Problem Solving

• Partner with senior leadership to explore AI-driven
opportunities across the firm, with exposure to a wide range
of business challenges

• Independently prototype, test, and iterate on AI-enabled
solutions using existing firm infrastructure and the latest
tools

• Translate business questions and pain points into
practical proofs of concept with tangible business impact

• Embrace an experimental mindset—rapid iteration, learning
from results, and adjusting direction as needed

Collaboration & Influence

• Engage with business teams across all parts of the firm to
brainstorm and refine use cases

• Influence adoption by demonstrating value through working
examples

• Serve as a connector between business teams and technical
partners, helping shape ideas and approaches

Knowledge Sharing & Enablement

• Capture and share learnings, patterns, and best practices
across the organization

• Contribute to an internal community of practice around AI
and emerging technologies

• Stay current on AI, fintech, and productivity trends and
proactively surface ideas relevant to the firm

What Success Looks Like (12 Months)

• A diverse set of AI-driven experiments tested across
multiple business areas

• Tangible productivity improvements or new capability
demonstrations in select workflows or business problems

• Strong working relationships with business leaders and
increased comfort engaging with AI tools

• A clearer organizational understanding of where AI can—and
cannot—add value

Qualifications & Experience

• B.S./M.S. in Computer Science, Engineering, or related
discipline preferred, though technical fluency and hands-on
comfort with technology is key

• 4+ years of professional experience, with proven
experience in using technology to deliver innovative and
analytical solutions

• Experience in financial services, investing, fintech, or
consulting is helpful but not required

• Deep technical fluency in Python with hands-on experience
building scalable, production-grade services

• Strong knowledge of agentic design patterns, such as
planning/execution loops, routing/specialist agents,
retrieval + tool orchestration, verification/reflection, and
human-in-the-loop approvals using frameworks like Google
ADK, LangChain and LangGraph or similar

• Ability to learn new skills and concepts quickly, highly
adaptable

• Strong communication skills and interpersonal skills in
working with a diverse set of stakeholders
