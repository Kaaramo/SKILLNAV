## Description complète

Location Designation: Hybrid - 3 days per week  We are
seeking a Senior Software Engineer with AI expertise to help
design and deliver modern, cloud-native insurance platforms.
This role sits within our core systems team and partners
closely with engineers, architects, data scientists, and
product teams to build scalable, secure, and highly
available solutions leveraging AWS, APIs, DevSecOps, and AI.
You will play a key role in advancing automation, developer
productivity, and AI-enabled capabilities while contributing
to enterprise standards and best practices. What You'll Do

- Design, build, and deploy cloud-native applications and
  platforms on AWS
- Apply AI and automation to improve developer experience and
  productivity
- Build and optimize CI/CD pipelines and DevSecOps automation
- Design and consume APIs and reusable services
- Ensure solutions meet security, performance, resilience, and
  compliance standards
- Collaborate with cross-functional teams in an Agile delivery
  environment
- Contribute to architecture evolution, technical standards,
  and engineering best practices

Required Qualifications

- Bachelor's degree in Computer Science, Engineering, or
  related field
- 5-7 years of software engineering experience building
  production-grade systems
- Hands-on experience with AWS, cloud-native architecture, and
  infrastructure as code (Terraform)
- Experience with DevSecOps, CI/CD pipelines, and automation
- Experience building or deploying AI or Agentic AI use cases
- Proficiency with modern programming and scripting languages
  (e.g., Python, Java, .NET)
- Experience with containerization (EKS/ECR), APIs, and modern
  development tools (GitHub, Jira)
- Strong communication skills and ability to work effectively
  with technical and business partners

Preferred Qualifications

- Experience in financial services, insurance, or wealth
  management
- Experience with LLM-based solutions and emerging AI
  technologies
- Familiarity with DevSecOps platforms (e.g., Harness)
- Relevant certifications in cloud, DevSecOps, or AI
