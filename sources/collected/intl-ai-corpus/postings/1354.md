About the role:

Seeking a Lead AI DevOps Engineer to oversee design and
delivery of advanced AI/ML/GenAI solutions. The role

leadership in deploying and integrating LLM/SLM models into
enterprise applications, ensuring security, scalability, and
operational excellence.



Tasks/ Responsibilities:

The person will act as a senior member of the Data Science &
AI Competency Center, AI Engineering team, guiding delivery
and coordinating workstreams.



Leading architecture and deployment of AI/ML/GenAI solutions
(LLM/SLM at scale).

Driving automation of infrastructure, model lifecycle and
inference pipelines.

Overseeing CI/CD processes for AI/ML/GenAI workloads.

Designing secure, scalable cloud infrastructures (Azure-
