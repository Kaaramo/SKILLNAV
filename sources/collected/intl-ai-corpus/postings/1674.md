Job Description

Job Description

Visa’s People Technology team is seeking a Senior Software
Engineer in Bangalore to deliver world-class software
solutions for our functional partners. You will report to an
Engineering Manager and work as a senior individual

This role requires close collaboration with People team

The portfolio includes SaaS and hosted applications such as
Workday, Cornerstone, ADP, ServiceNow, as well as custom-
built solutions.

You will be expected to quickly understand business
requirements, think proactively, and identify opportunities
where technology can solve complex problems. The role also

and Learning system integrations.

If this sounds exciting, we’d love to connect and share more
about our work culture and how this role can make an impact.

- Design, develop, document, and maintain full stack
