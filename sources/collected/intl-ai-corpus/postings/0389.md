This role is a Solutions Engineer focused on customer
success, adoption, and feedback collection. The
responsibilities involve guiding customers, monitoring
engagement, and acting as a bridge between customers and the
internal AI team - not building, deploying, or fine-tuning
models directly. While the company works with LLMs and AI
agents, this role supports AI solutions rather than working
on them hands-on.

## Description complète

Company Description

Newton Research is a fast-growing software start-up founded
by repeat entrepreneurs and well-funded by blue chip venture
capital firms. We are working with cutting edge technology
including LLMs and generative AI. We build products that
leverage AI agents to handle complex marketing analytics
workflows for our customers and partners.

Role Description

This team drives product growth by focusing on customer
adoption and utilization of Newton's AI solutions. This role
is key to increasing customer engagement, showcasing the
platform's value, and keeping the voice of the customer at
the forefront of the product roadmap.  The ideal candidate
will have advertising technology experience and a diverse
skill set encompassing growth, product, marketing, Gen AI,
and customer success. This multifaceted role requires a
motivated, highly organized, and process-driven individual
with an attention to detail, strategic mindset, and the
ability to excel in a collaborative, fast-paced startup
environment. As an early team member, you will have a unique
opportunity to shape our product and establish yourself as a
leader in the AI and advertising analytics industry.

Responsibilities:

- Identify opportunities to integrate platform usage into user
  workflow habits by guiding customers toward high-value use
  cases
- Conduct regular check-ins with customers to understand their
  evolving needs and provide guidance on leveraging Newton’s
  solutions effectively, acting as the communication bridge
  between customers and internal AI team
- Monitor customer engagement through various platform
  utilization metrics
- Serve as voice of the customer, gathering insights from
  customers interactions and translating them into
  actionable feedback for the product and AI teams

Qualifications:

- 5+ years of experience in the advertising technology
  industry, preferably in analytics or data operations
- Deep understanding of the analytics and AI landscape, mainly
  how advertisers and publishers think about use cases in
  analytics, attribution, and profitability
- Strong problem-solving skills with a passion for
  communicating and delivering technical solutions
- Experience collaborating with clients and cross-functional
  teams, including product, engineering, and sales.
- Understanding of SQL and Python is preferred
