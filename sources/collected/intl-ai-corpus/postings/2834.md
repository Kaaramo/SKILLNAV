## Description complète

The Pitch

Slate’s product already uses AI across the stack —
background removal, active speaker detection, auto-
captioning, content suggestions, and image generation — but
each of these is its own independent integration. We need a
Senior Backend Engineer focused on AI to bring this
together: unifying how we integrate models, building the
infrastructure that makes AI features reliable at scale, and
helping us move faster on the next wave of AI-powered
capabilities.

What You’ll Actually DoBuild the AI platform layer

- Design and build the shared infrastructure for AI
  integrations across Slate’s backend: model routing, prompt
  management, response caching, fallback handling, and cost
  observability
- Refactor existing one-off AI integrations (background
  removal, captioning, etc.) onto a unified platform
- Own the reliability, performance, and cost-efficiency of
  Slate’s AI systems

Enable new AI features

- Work closely with the AI Product Engineer and product team
  to bring new AI capabilities to production
- Build the backend systems that power real-time AI features —
  handling streaming, async processing, and latency
  constraints
- Evaluate and integrate new models and APIs as they become
  available

Raise the quality bar

- Define evaluation frameworks and monitoring to track AI
  feature quality over time
- Build systems to detect and alert on model regressions or
  degraded outputs
- Ensure AI features meet the same reliability bar as the rest
  of Slate’s infrastructure

You’d Be a Great Fit IfYou have the experience:

- 5+ years of backend engineering experience, with a track
  record of shipping production systems
- Hands-on experience integrating and operating LLMs or ML
  models at scale
- Strong systems thinking — you design for reliability,
  observability, and cost from the start

You’ve done work like this before:

- Built shared AI infrastructure used by multiple teams or
  features
- Designed async processing pipelines for ML workloads
- Worked with OpenAI, Anthropic, Replicate, or similar APIs in
  production

You work the way we work:

- You own the outcomes, not just the tasks
- You communicate clearly and raise issues early
- You use AI tools in your own workflow and think about how to
  apply them to hard engineering problems

Tech Stack

Node.js, TypeScript, PostgreSQL, Redis, AWS, OpenAI /
Anthropic APIs, Replicate
