Role:  AI Engineer – Gen AI Specialist

Location:  London, England, United Kingdom

About the role:

We’re seeking a highly capable Gen AI Specialist to support
the development and delivery of AI-driven solutions, with a
strong focus on visual content creation. This role centres
on image and video generation, model training, and creative
AI experimentation. You will work closely with our Gen AI &
AI Technology Lead to prototype, refine, and deliver AI
outputs that integrate seamlessly into our CGI workflows.

You will collaborate with creatives, producers, and CGI
artists to bring AI-powered visual concepts to life,
enabling faster, more flexible content creation for global
clients. You will also apply data-driven methodologies to
support content production, tools, and automation,
leveraging AI wherever possible.

What you will be doing:

- AI Visual Generation
  - Support rapid prototyping using:
    - Text-to-Image / Text-to-Video
    - Image-to-Image / Video-to-Video
    - Image-to-Video & Video-to-Image workflows
  - Produce high-quality, product-accurate AI imagery and video
    content.
- Model Training & Optimisation
  - Train and fine-tune LoRA models.
  - Optimise models using a combination of CGI datasets,
    photographic references, look-dev materials, etc.
  - Explore emerging image/video generation techniques and model
    architectures.
- Workflow Collaboration
  - Work with CGI Artists to ensure outputs meet client-approved
    visual standards.
  - Help translate experimental results into reliable,
    repeatable workflows.
  - Contribute to testing, validating, and refining AI tools
    used by the wider team.
- R&D and Exploration
  - Stay informed about new developments in the Gen AI and ML
    landscape.
  - Identify and evaluate new tools, methodologies, and models.
  - Document findings clearly for internal use and knowledge-
    sharing.
  - Actively share expertise and learnings with the team.

What you need to be great in this role:

- Strong enthusiasm for AI, Machine Learning, and emerging
  technologies.
- Hands-on experience generating image or video content using
  AI models.
- Familiarity with LoRA training and model fine-tuning.
- Interest in LLM development and integration.
- Understanding of modular, data-driven workflow design.
- Strong visual sensibility and an interest in CGI aesthetics.
- Experience with tools such as ComfyUI, Qwen, WAN, or
  equivalent platforms.
- Ability to thrive in a fast-paced, iterative R&D
  environment.
- Confidence to experiment—explore, learn from setbacks, and
  iterate quickly.
- Nice to Have
- Python skills for automation and tooling.
- Familiarity with CGI pipelines, VFX workflows, or look
  development.
- Understanding of GPU compute usage and optimisation.
