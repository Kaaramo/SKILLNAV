Role Intent

This role is intended for experienced software developers,
not traditional automation engineers. The focus is on
building and evolving automation frameworks and platforms
used by multiple teams across our CXone division, including
developing a new agentic AI–driven automation framework and
unifying existing Playwright-based frameworks. Contractors
will contribute to shared automation and framework backlogs
under the direction of a Tech Manager based in Pune.

Key Responsibilities

Design, develop, and maintain shared automation frameworks
and platform components.

Translate production-derived workflows into reusable
automation assets and framework capabilities.

Contribute to CI/CD-integrated automation and service-level
validation.

delivery.

systems or frameworks.
