Job Description:

Johnson & Johnson Innovative Medicine R&D IT is seeking
Product Lead Engineers - AI located in Hyderabad, India. At
the Johnson & Johnson Innovative Medicine, we take patient
health and well-being to our core values. From clinical
trials to regulatory approval and ongoing safety monitoring,
we ensure every step of your drug's journey is safe and
effective. We bring together the best minds and pursue the
most promising science.

The Product Lead Engineer is responsible for supervising
product engineering and making software engineering
decisions. They fulfill dual responsibilities as both an
entrepreneur and implementer of technical solutions. They
tackle complex business problems and intricate engineering
challenges and shape the technical design. Their
responsibilities include ensuring the product's technical
architecture, software development aligns with the
technology roadmap and adheres to software engineering
standards.

Key Responsibilities:

- Lead the design, development, and implementation of AI and
  generative AI solutions to address complex business
  problems in Safety, Regulatory, and Clinical functions.
- Collaborate with multi-functional teams to integrate cloud
  computing and automation technologies into our products
  and services.
- Stay abreast of the latest advancements in AI, cloud
  computing, and automation, and apply this knowledge to
  enhance our product offerings.
- Develop and maintain product roadmaps, ensuring alignment
  with customer needs and market trends.
- Work hands-on with business partners, data scientists,
  software engineers, and other partners to define technical
  requirements and specifications.
- Optimize product performance and scalability using cloud
  infrastructure and automation tools.
- Analyze product performance metrics and user feedback to
  drive continuous improvement.
- Provide technical guidance and mentorship to junior
  engineers, fostering a culture of learning and innovation.
- Communicate product vision and progress to partners,
  ensuring alignment with business objectives.
