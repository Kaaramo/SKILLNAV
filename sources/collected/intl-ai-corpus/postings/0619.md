## Description complète

About Kiddom

Kiddom is a groundbreaking educational platform that
promotes student equity and growth by uniting high-quality
instructional materials with dynamic digital learning.
Through unparalleled curriculum management functionality,
Kiddom empowers schools and districts to take ownership of
their curriculum – resulting in learning experiences
tailored to meet the unique needs and goals of local
communities. Kiddom’s high-quality curriculum is layered
with robust teacher and leader data insights to drive the
continuous improvement of instructional decisions,
school/district programming, and professional learning.

As a Senior QA Engineer, you will play an important role in
ensuring the quality and reliability of our educational
platforms. You will work at the intersection of traditional
quality assurance and cutting-edge AI technology,
contributing to our testing infrastructure and improving
learning outcomes for our users. You are expected to
exemplify company values and possess the capabilities to
keep the team on track through independent project
management and process adherence.

What You'll Do...

- Project Leadership: Keep team projects on track,
  demonstrating strong ownership and initiative, while
  effectively managing interactions with cross-functional
  partner groups.
- Technical Estimation & Strategy: Independently and
  accurately estimate the QA level of effort (costing) for
  projects, considering maintenance, timelines, and business
  pressures.
- Requirement Analysis & Design Feedback: Deeply understand
  and analyze user stories, product specifications, and
  requirements to ensure testability.  Provide critical
  feedback on functional issues, acceptance criteria, and
  design flaws early in the development lifecycle.
- Testing & Debugging: Design and execute comprehensive tests,
  and debug complex issues, in possibly unfamiliar areas of
  the application.
- AI Tooling: Proactively integrate AI tools into your QA
  workflows to improve efficiency and accuracy, while
  evaluating and recommending specific testing tools (e.g.,
  self-healing scripts, AI-driven test generation) to
  increase team velocity.
- Testing AI Features: Validate AI-driven educational
  features, such as personalized learning paths or automated
  grading systems, ensuring accuracy and bias mitigation.
- Automation & Code Quality: Create complex test scripts, and
  conduct high-quality code reviews for automated scripts
  with actionable feedback.
- Documentation: Develop thorough test plans that prioritize
  maintainability and address immediate business needs.
  Utilize LLMs to generate test data, summarize bug reports,
  and create initial drafts of test documentation.
- Defect Management: Conduct in-depth defect analysis to
  identify root causes and trends, improving overall product
  quality.
- Support: support customers by triaging user-reported issues.

What we're looking for...

- 5+ years of professional experience in functional manual
  testing.
- Expert Knowledge: Expert understanding of core QA concepts,
  testing strategies, and the software test cycle process.
- Automation Proficiency: Deep experience with functional
  automation tools (Playwright, Selenium, and JUnit, etc.)
- AI Tooling: Proven ability to use AI tools for test
  automation, bug triage, and test data generation to
  streamline repetitive tasks.
- Communication & Influence: Excellent verbal and written
  communication skills with a track record of contributing
  materially outside of individual work to assist the wider
  team.
- Debugging Skills: Strong ability to solve technical issues
  in both familiar and unfamiliar functionality.
- Work Style: Excellent attention to detail, high
  accountability, and the ability to handle problems from
  start to finish with an appropriate response.

Salary range is dependent on geographic location, prior

during the interview process.
