## Description complète

At Broadridge, we've built a culture where the highest goal
is to empower others to accomplish more. If you’re
passionate about developing your career, while helping
others along the way, come join the Broadridge team.

Broadridge is seeking a Senior Principal Software Engineer
within BTCS to lead the adoption of AI across software
development and quality assurance, while also helping
prepare Broadridge’s core platforms for the next generation
of digital assets, including crypto and tokenized
securities.

This role is designed for a senior technologist who
understands enterprise-scale, mission-critical systems in
highly regulated financial markets and who can drive
measurable improvements in engineering productivity, code
quality, and platform readiness.

Key Responsibilities

AI Adoption Across Development & QA

• Lead the responsible adoption of AI-assisted development
and QA capabilities.

• Define governance, standards, and controls for AI usage.

• Track AI prompt usage and code adoption into production.

• Embed AI across the SDLC including coding, testing, and
defect detection.

Engineering Productivity & Efficiency

• Define metrics for developer productivity and delivery
efficiency.

• Improve CI/CD pipelines and developer tooling.

• Promote consistent engineering standards.

Code Quality & Defect Reduction

• Use AI-driven analysis to improve code quality and
resilience.

• Reduce defect leakage and technical debt.

Crypto & Tokenization Readiness

• Define engineering readiness for crypto and tokenized
assets.

• Assess architecture, security, ledger integration, and
compliance.

• Partner with product, legal, and compliance teams.

Cross-Functional Leadership

• Drive execution across engineering, product, QA, DevOps,
security, and business teams.

• Translate strategy into executable plans with measurable
outcomes.

Key skills

- Hands on experience of crypto, tokenization of assets
- Real world experience of AI deployment and efficiency
  measurement
- Experience of OMS, Execution management lifecycle
- Ability to converse and influence senior developers across
  the SDLC lifecycle
- Knowledge of C++/Java
- Critical thinker- ability to influence senior leaders.
