### Raisonnement classification (LLM upstream)

This is a UI-focused Staff Engineer role that builds user
interfaces and API gateways for an Agentic AI platform. The
role connects AI backend models to user interfaces but does
not involve building, training, or deploying the AI models
themselves - it's focused on the frontend software
engineering that supports the AI product.

About Netskope

Today, there's more data and users outside the enterprise
than inside, causing the network perimeter as we know it to
dissolve. We realized a new perimeter was needed, one that
is built in the cloud and follows and protects data wherever
it goes, so we started Netskope to redefine Cloud, Network
and Data Security.

Since 2012, we have built the market-leading cloud security
company and an award-winning culture powered by hundreds of
employees spread across offices in Santa Clara, St. Louis,
Bangalore, London, Paris, Melbourne, Taipei, and Tokyo. Our
core values are openness, honesty, and transparency, and we
purposely developed our open desk layouts and large meeting
spaces to support and promote partnerships, collaboration,
and teamwork. From catered lunches and office celebrations
to employee recognition events and social professional
groups such as the Awesome Women of Netskope (AWON), we
strive to keep work fun, supportive and interactive.  Visit
us at Netskope Careers. Please follow us on LinkedIn and
Twitter@Netskope.

About the role:

Please note, this team is hiring across all levels and
candidates are individually assessed and appropriately
leveled based upon their skills and experience.

We are seeking an exceptional Staff Software Engineer to
build next-generation security analytics and AI-powered
capabilities within Netskope's platform. This is a high-
impact individual contributor role for an engineer who
excels at solving complex technical problems at the
intersection of artificial intelligence, large-scale data
systems, and cybersecurity.

As a Staff Engineer, you will design and implement
sophisticated systems that leverage cutting-edge AI/ML
techniques, graph-based analytics, and real-time data
processing to solve critical security challenges. You will
work on problems spanning threat detection, risk assessment,
behavioral analytics, and intelligent automation—building
the systems that help security teams at the world's largest
enterprises detect and respond to threats.

This role requires deep technical expertise with strong
hands-on implementation skills. You will collaborate closely
with Distinguished Engineers, Senior engineers, Product
managers, and security researchers to deliver production-
quality solutions that operate at massive scale. Your code
and architectural decisions will directly impact how
thousands of organizations protect their critical assets.

What’s in it for you

We are looking for a highly motivated, skillful, and hands-
on Staff UI Developer to take ownership of the next
generation of user experiences for our Agentic AI platform.
You will drive the technical direction, ensure the
scalability of the UI architecture, and set the bar for code
quality. If you possess the technical depth of a developer,
the foresight of an architect, and the proven ability to
lead complex initiatives, we would like to speak with you
about this exciting, impactful opportunity.

What you will be doing

- Participate actively in deep technical design discussions,
  driving the product direction and long-term strategy
  specifically for the Agentic AI platform's user experience
  (UI) and API Gateway.
- Maintain code quality and design integrity by conducting
  rigorous code reviews and diving into the codebase as
  necessary to optimize architecture and troubleshoot
  complex distributed issues.
- Provide technical leadership and strategic direction for the
  overall Netskope UI and the API Gateway components
  interacting with the Agentic AI models.
- Collaborate effectively across organizational boundaries
  (Product Management, Backend AI/ML teams) to ensure a
  shared roadmap and streamlined, end-to-end delivery of
  Agentic AI features.
- Refine and grow existing processes, or develop new
  workflows, to enable the smooth and efficient functioning
  of the UI engineering team supporting the AI initiatives.
- Collaborate with the geo-distributed team to own and deliver
  high-impact projects that connect the Agentic AI backend
  to the user interface, ensuring successful end-to-end
  deployment.
- Collaborating with the geo-distributed team to own and
  deliver projects end-to-end.
- Communicating across functional teams and driving
  engineering initiatives.

Required skills and experience

- 8+ years of deep, hands-on experience developing
  sophisticated, large-scale web user interfaces.
- Expert-level, hands-on experience building and architecting
  web UI applications using React, or similar modern
  frameworks.
- A consistent track record of collaborating across geo-
  distributed and cross-functional teams to design and
  deliver world-class UI applications.
- Experience working with Node.js and Express (knowledge of
  NestJS a plus).
- Experience with AI-assisted coding platforms (example -
  GitHub Copilot, Cursor, Claude, Codex etc,) for code
  generation, debugging and documentation.
- Knowledge of secure web development practices.
- Experience working with Cloud Security and/or cloud
  technologies is a plus.
- An energetic, self-starter personality and attitude, with
  the desire to work in a dynamic fast-paced environment.
- Excellent verbal and written communication skills

Education

- BSCS or equivalent required, MSCS or equivalent strongly
  preferred
