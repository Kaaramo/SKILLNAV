## Description complète

Senior AI Platform Engineer – SolarEdge

Join SolarEdge’s Next-Gen AI Platform Team

About SolarEdge: SolarEdge is a global leader in smart
energy technology, delivering innovative solutions for solar
power, energy storage, and intelligent energy management.
Our mission is to drive the future of clean energy by
leveraging advanced technologies, including artificial
intelligence, to create scalable and sustainable solutions
for our customers.

Mission

Build the internal AI platform to standardize, scale, and
productionize LLM- and agent-based systems, empowering
SolarEdge to harness the full potential of AI across our
products and services.

What You’ll Do

·      Design reusable agentic AI infrastructure, including
Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG), tool orchestration,
and planning/execution flows.

·      Build robust retrieval services, embedding pipelines,
and intelligent model-routing solutions.

·      Implement comprehensive evaluation, monitoring, and
cost-control frameworks to ensure system reliability and
efficiency.

·      Optimize for latency, reliability, and token
efficiency at scale, ensuring high performance in production
environments.

·      Productionize advanced ML models and seamlessly
integrate them with enterprise systems across SolarEdge's
global operations.

·      Collaborate closely with Data Science & Engineering
teams, contributing to system architecture and driving
projects forward with a clear technical direction.

What We’re Looking For

·      5+ years of experience in backend, ML, or AI
engineering roles.

·      Proven expertise in building scalable AI/ML
infrastructure and deploying models in production
environments.

·      Strong programming skills (e.g., Python, Java, or
similar), with deep understanding of distributed systems and
cloud platforms.

·      Experience with LLMs, agent-based systems, and modern
AI frameworks.

·      Ability to design and implement monitoring,
evaluation, and cost optimization strategies for AI systems.

·      Excellent collaboration and communication skills;
able to work effectively with multidisciplinary teams.

·      Passion for clean energy and leveraging technology
for global impact.
