About the Role

DDS Marketing is looking for an AI Agent Engineer to own the
end-to-end development of AI-powered automation modules that

healthcare practices.

You will work directly with the founder, who oversees

This role is ideal for someone who enjoys building
production AI systems quickly, integrating APIs, and
shipping autonomous agents that deliver real-world outcomes.

Ad Deployment Agent
