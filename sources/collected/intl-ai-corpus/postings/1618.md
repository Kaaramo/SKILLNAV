## Description complète

About the Role

Cigna is accelerating responsible AI adoption across the
enterprise. We’re seeking a Prompt Engineer & AI
Applications Lead to partner with product, data,
engineering, and business stakeholders to discover,
evaluate, and scale AI use cases—while ensuring safety,
compliance, and measurable value. This role blends hands‑on
prompt design with business relationship management and
vendor collaboration to drive outcomes across multiple time
zones.

What You’ll Do (Key Responsibilities)

AI Use Case Discovery & Shaping

- Identify, qualify, and prioritize high‑impact AI
  opportunities across functions (operations, clinical,
  customer support, finance, IT).

- Facilitate discovery workshops to define problem statements,
  success metrics, guardrails, and adoption plans.

- Translate business needs into AI solution patterns (e.g.,
  retrieval‑augmented generation, summarization,
  classification, extraction, assistants/agents).

Prompt Engineering & Experimentation

- Design, test, and optimize prompts, chains, and system
  instructions for performance, safety, and reliability
  across multiple LLMs and tools.

- Develop prompt evaluation frameworks (A/B tests, golden
  sets, bias & hallucination checks, cost/latency tracking).

- Create reusable prompt templates, style guides, and prompt
  libraries for enterprise use.

Vendor & Partner Management

- Assess AI vendors for capability, compliance, total cost of
  ownership, integration complexity, and roadmap fit.

- Co‑develop POCs/pilots with vendors; define SLAs, acceptance
  criteria, and transition plans to scale or sunset.

- Coordinate security, privacy, legal, and procurement
  reviews; document decisions and risks.

Solution Delivery & Governance

- Partner with Data/ML Engineering to integrate RAG pipelines,
  guardrails, and observability (prompt logs, red‑teaming
  results, monitoring).

- Ensure adherence to responsible AI, data privacy, and
  healthcare compliance requirements (e.g., PHI handling,
  HIPAA‑aligned processes in consultation with compliance
  teams).

- Contribute to model cards, risk assessments, and
  human‑in‑the‑loop controls.

Stakeholder Enablement & BRM

- Serve as a trusted advisor to business leaders—clarify value
  cases, manage expectations, and align roadmaps and
  budgets.

- Build enablement materials (playbooks, demos, FAQs) and
  deliver training for non‑technical and technical
  audiences.

- Communicate progress, risks, and ROI via clear narratives
  and dashboards.

What You’ll Bring (Qualifications)

Must‑Have

- ~5+ years of relevant experience in AI/ML product work,
  data‑driven solutions, or technical consulting, with at
  least 2+ years hands‑on prompt engineering (or
  demonstrable expertise).

- Business Relationship Management (BRM) experience:
  stakeholder alignment, requirements shaping, roadmap
  facilitation, and value realization.

- Proficiency with LLMs and tooling (e.g., prompt design,
  few‑shot techniques, function calling/tools, vector
  search/RAG, evaluation methods).

- Practical knowledge of data fundamentals (data quality,
  governance, metadata, PII/PHI concepts) and safe AI
  patterns.

- Strong communication: can translate complex AI concepts for
  executives and non‑technical teams; excellent storytelling
  and documentation.

- Collaborative mindset and experience working across multiple
  time zones and cross‑functional teams.

- Bachelor’s degree in a relevant field (Computer Science,
  Data/AI, Engineering, or related); equivalent experience
  considered.

- Location: Madrid, Spain

Nice‑to‑Have

- Experience in healthcare/insurance processes and analytics.

- Familiarity with evaluation frameworks (e.g., golden
  datasets, rubric‑based scoring), guardrails/red‑teaming,
  and observability.

- Exposure to MLOps/LangChain/LlamaIndex, vector databases,
  and API integration patterns.

- Practical understanding of responsible AI principles and
  risk controls; experience partnering with
  Legal/Privacy/Compliance.

- Comfort with one or more languages or tools (Python, SQL,
  REST APIs, YAML), and with cloud platforms
  (Azure/AWS/GCP).

Success Metrics (KPIs)

- Validated use cases discovered per quarter and percent
  advancing from POC → Pilot → Scale.

- Model quality gains (e.g., accuracy, factuality, reduction
  in hallucination) and experience metrics (latency, cost
  per task).

- Risk reduction (guardrail coverage, incident rate, red‑team
  findings addressed).

- Stakeholder satisfaction (NPS/CSAT) and enablement reach
  (teams trained, assets reused).

- Vendor outcomes (SLA adherence, value realization, cost
  savings/avoidance).

Ways of Working

- Hybrid work with core collaboration hours overlapping
  European and India time zones; occasional evening/morning
  meetings to align with global teams.

- Limited travel for workshops or vendor evaluations as
  needed.

- Partner closely with Product Owners, Data/ML Engineering,
  Architecture, Security, Privacy, Legal, and Procurement.
