This role is focused on data infrastructure, pipelines, and

pipelines, and supporting analytics workflows for others.

As a Principal Data Engineer, you will lead technical
strategy and execution for our robust, scalable, and modern
data ecosystem. You'll collaborate cross-functionally with
data analysts, engineering teams, and business stakeholders
to architect sophisticated data solutions powering
analytics, operational insights, and innovative products.

This high-impact role is designed for an experienced
technical leader who excels in strategic thinking, long-term
planning, and driving the maturity of data systems and
practices. Your deep understanding of data infrastructure,
business context, and modern data tooling will help position
Guild's data engineering capabilities for future growth and
innovation.

What You Bring

- 12+ years of data engineering or relevant experience, with
  demonstrated leadership in complex technical environments.
- Expert-level SQL skills, including advanced performance
  optimization, query tuning, complex CTEs, window
  functions, and data orchestration.
- Advanced proficiency in Python for sophisticated pipeline
  development, automation, API integrations, and scalable,
  maintainable code design.
- Data Trust is important to us, so significant experience
  implementing Change Data Capture (CDC) at scale is a must
- Extensive hands-on expertise with Databricks architecture
  and optimization, specifically around Unity Catalog,
  Medallion Architecture, MLFlow, and the Databricks MCP
  Platform
- Extensive hands-on expertise with Snowflake architecture and
  optimization, advanced dbt modeling strategies, and
  comprehensive integration using Fivetran.
- Strong command of infrastructure as code (Terraform),
  Unix/Linux environments, and version control (Git).
- Deep expertise in relational databases (Postgres, MySQL,
  AuroraDB) and substantial experience with NoSQL databases
  like DynamoDB.
- Advanced understanding of data modeling, warehousing
  methodologies, and modern ETL/ELT frameworks.
- Proven track record aligning technical designs to strategic
  business objectives and commercial outcomes.
- Experience leading technical initiatives within agile, high-
  growth environments utilizing modern data stack tools.
- In-depth knowledge of data governance, privacy, compliance
  standards, and regulatory frameworks.
- Expert understanding of CI/CD, continuous delivery,
  automated testing, and change management best practices.

Responsibilities

- Lead the architecture, design, and development of
  sophisticated, highly scalable data pipelines leveraging
  tools such as Fivetran, dbt, AWS Glue, Airflow, custom
  Python applications, Snowflake, Databricks, and cloud
  storage solutions (e.g., S3)
- Provide technical leadership in integrating and transforming
  complex data sets from diverse sources including AuroraDB,
  Snowflake, Postgres, MySQL, and DynamoDB.
- Drive collaboration with analytics, engineering, and
  commercial stakeholders to design and refine data models
  and marts that empower high-impact decision-making.
- Champion and implement rigorous data quality, validation,
  observability, and reliability standards across data
  workflows.
- Mentor and upskill team members, influencing the development
  of Guild’s data engineering practices and standards.
- Serve as a trusted advisor, guiding enterprise-wide data
  strategy, prototyping key data initiatives, and ensuring
  alignment of data engineering practices with long-term
  business and technical objectives.
