## Description complète

We are looking for a Lead Gen AI Engineer to play a key
hands-on technical role in designing, building and scaling
production-grade Generative AI solutions across the
business.

This role sits within MLOps function and focuses on hands-on
technical execution and architecture, working closely with
platform, security and business teams to bring GenAI use
cases from concept into reliable, secure, and production-
ready systems.

What you will Deliver

- Design, architect and deliver production-grade GenAI
  solutions using LLMs, RAG, vector search, and prompt
  engineering, ensuring scalability, security, reliability
  and maintainability.
- Drive GenAI projects end-to-end, evolving solutions from
  proof-of-concept to production with strong technical
  quality, operational readiness, and robust
  monitoring/observability.
- Provide hands-on technical leadership, mentoring engineers,
  defining engineering standards, leading design reviews,
  and guiding key architectural and build-vs-buy decisions.
- Collaborate across the organisation, integrating GenAI
  services with enterprise systems, cloud platforms, and
  working closely with business, platform and infrastructure
  teams.
- Lead technical execution of real-world GenAI applications
  (e.g., support assistants, case handling automation, fraud
  investigation, decision support) while identifying risks,
  dependencies and trade-offs.

Skills & Experience

- Proven experience leading the development and deployment of
  GenAI systems in enterprise environments.
- Deep hands-on expertise with LLMs, RAG architectures, vector
  databases, and orchestration frameworks.
- Strong understanding of prompt design, context management,
  and GenAI-specific risks such as hallucination and data
  exposure.
- Experience integrating GenAI solutions with enterprise
  systems and cloud platforms (AWS, Azure)
- Proven ability to work independently and collaboratively
  across technical and business teams to deliver high-
  quality solutions with clear business value.
- Strong communication skills, able to explain technical
  decisions and trade-offs clearly in cross-functional
  environments.

Desirable

- Background in AI/ML engineering, AI solution architecture,
  or software engineering, with strong hands-on experience
  designing and building production-ready GenAI systems.
- Experience working within CI/CD and observability frameworks
  to deploy, monitor and iterate GenAI systems in production
  environments.
- Experience working in large-scale or regulated enterprise
  settings.
- Proven track record delivering GenAI solutions across areas
  such as knowledge assistants, customer support, commercial
  automation, and engineering tools.
- Understanding of practical evaluation approaches for GenAI
  in production, including user feedback mechanisms,
  performance considerations, and assessing response
  usefulness and relevance.
- Exposure to advanced GenAI orchestration patterns and
  frameworks such as tool use, memory management, or agentic
  workflows.
