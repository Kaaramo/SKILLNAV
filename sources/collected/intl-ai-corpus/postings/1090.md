Position Overview


Autodesk is undertaking a bold transformation to build an
AI-first application platform that delivers a seamless,
unified experience—fundamentally reshaping how users and
internal teams operate within an agent-driven workspace.
This platform will serve as the foundation for the next
generation of design-and-make software.


We are looking for a skilled Principal Software Engineer –
Agentic AI to architect, build, and deploy advanced AI
agents capable of autonomous reasoning, decision-making, and
self-directed execution. The ideal candidate brings deep
expertise in modern AI architectures, reinforcement
learning, and multi-agent systems, along with a strong
commitment to the responsible and ethical deployment of
agentic AI. In this role, you will collaborate closely with
cross-functional partners to develop domain-aware
intelligent systems that accelerate innovation and deliver
meaningful business impact across the Autodesk platform.


Responsibilities

- Agentic AI Design & Development: Architect, develop, and
  optimize agentic AI systems, including autonomous agents,
  multi-agent frameworks, and LLM-powered agents. Integrate
  these solutions seamlessly into existing Autodesk
  platforms and workflows
- Research & Experimentation: Assess emerging agentic AI
  models, frameworks, and tooling. Prototype, evaluate, and
  benchmark solutions to ensure scalability, robustness,
  safety, and performance
- Deployment & Monitoring: Deploy agentic AI solutions into
  production environments with a focus on reliability and
  operational excellence. Monitor agent behavior, detect and
  diagnose anomalies, refine agent policies, and establish
  feedback loops for continuous learning and improvement
- Ethics & Governance: Ensure all agentic AI systems adhere to
  ethical guidelines, regulatory requirements, and internal
  governance standards throughout their lifecycle
- Cross-Functional Collaboration: Partner closely with
  architects, software engineers, product managers, and
  business stakeholders. Clearly communicate complex
  technical concepts and trade-offs to both technical and
  non-technical audiences

Minimum Qualifications

- Bachelor’s or Master’s degree in Computer Science,
  Engineering, or a related technical discipline
- 10+ years of experience designing and building scalable,
  distributed software systems
- 3–6 years of hands-on experience in Artificial Intelligence,
  Machine Learning, or a related domain
- Proven experience delivering GenAI or AI/ML solutions end-
  to-end, from use case definition through production
  deployment, with exposure to agentic or autonomous systems
- Strong understanding of infrastructure and DevOps practices
  for GenAI workloads, including cloud environments, LLM
  hosting, CI/CD pipelines, containerization (Docker), and
  orchestration with Kubernetes

Preferred Qualifications

- Proficiency in Python and experience building APIs along
  with working knowledge of SQL
- Familiarity with agentic AI frameworks and concepts such as
  LangChain, LangGraph, AutoGen, Model Context Protocol
  (MCP), large language models (LLMs), chain-of-thought
  prompting, knowledge stores, and embeddings
- Experience developing autonomous agents using cloud-based AI
  services
- Knowledge of prompt engineering techniques and model fine-
  tuning
- Solid understanding of reinforcement learning, planning
  algorithms, and multi-agent systems
- Experience deploying and operating AI solutions at scale on
  major cloud platforms, including AWS, Azure, or Google
  Cloud Platform
