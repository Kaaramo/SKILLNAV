## Description complète

Who We Are

Imprint is reimagining co-branded credit cards & financial
products to be smarter, more rewarding, and truly brand-
first. We partner with companies like Crate & Barrel,
Rakuten, Booking.com, H-E-B, Fetch, and Brooks Brothers to
launch modern credit programs that deepen loyalty, unlock
savings, and drive growth. Our platform combines advanced
payments infrastructure, intelligent underwriting, and
seamless UX to help brands offer powerful financial
products—without becoming a bank.

Co-branded cards account for over $300 billion in U.S.
annual spend—but most are still powered by legacy banks.
Imprint is the modern alternative: flexible, tech-forward,
and built for today’s consumer. Backed by Kleiner Perkins,
Thrive Capital, and Khosla Ventures, we’re building a world-
class team to redefine how people pay—and how brands grow.
If you want to work fast, solve hard problems, and make a
real impact, we’d love to meet you.

Learn more about us on Imprint's Technology blog.

The Role

We’re looking for a creative, technical self-starter to
build the platform that runs agents at Imprint, along with
the first 50 agents running on that platform to transform
the efficiency of Imprint’s internal teams and processes. As
an AI Engineer, you’ll develop Imprint’s agent platform and
iterate on taking those agents from toys to critical
capabilities. We already benefit from an array of off-the-
shelf AI tools internally - ChatGPT, Claude, Notion AI,
Gemini, etc. - and are looking for a dedicated engineer to
apply AI (by which we really mean LLM) techniques across the
company.

You’ll work end-to-end, partnering with organizations across
Imprint to integrate with internal systems and ship
valuable, secure agent-based interfaces. You’ll be trusted
to move fast, experiment ambitiously, and scale what works.

The ideal candidate is a self-starter who thrives in
ambiguity, loves building, and understands how to balance
pragmatism with technical vision. You should be comfortable
stitching together APIs one day and wrangling embeddings or
RAG pipelines the next. This work will require a mix of
customer-centric product development, hands-on experience
with modern AI frameworks, software engineering
fundamentals, and a passion for automation. If you're
excited by the opportunity to create leverage across every
corner of a growing company, we'd love to meet you.

What You'll Do

Platform & Agent Development

- Design Imprint's internal agent platform from the ground up
- Create 50+ specialized AI agents automating workflows across
  engineering, operations, customer success, finance, and
  business development

Cross-Functional integration

- Build secure, scalable agent orchestration systems
  integrating with our existing tech stack
- Partner with teams across Imprint to identify high-impact
  automation opportunities
- Integrate AI with internal systems (CRMs, data warehouses,
  communication tools, proprietary platforms)
- Transform manual processes into intelligent, self-improving

- Create intuitive interfaces, making advanced AI accessible

Technical Innovation

  models
- Implement RAG pipelines, embedding systems, and multi-modal

- Balance experimental innovation with production reliability
  and security

What We Look For

- Strong proficiency in programming languages such as Go (our
  primary backend language), Java, Python, or similar
  languages
- Experience with AI/ML frameworks (OpenAI API, Claude API,
  Gemini)
- Production experience with cloud platforms (AWS or GCP) and

- Deep understanding of LLMs, prompt engineering, and agent
