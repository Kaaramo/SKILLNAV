### Raisonnement classification (LLM upstream)

This role is building infrastructure, deployment automation,
and platform tooling for an AI product, not developing the
AI models or systems themselves. The Platform Engineer
focuses on Terraform, Kubernetes, cloud infrastructure, and
observability to deploy and maintain the Unstructured AI
application, which is enabling work for others who work on
AI.

## Responsabilités

- Design, implement, and maintain infrastructure blueprint for deploying Unstructured AI application in customer cloud environments
- Codify infrastructure as code using Terraform with modular, secure configurations for flexible deployment scenarios
- Manage Kubernetes and Helm-based deployment stacks including databases, secrets, authentication, and SSO components
- Set up and maintain monitoring, alerting, and observability systems using Datadog, Prometheus, and Grafana for operational visibility
- Participate in incident response and troubleshooting for customer-deployed environments using observability data
- Collaborate with application teams to implement Python code and configuration changes for infrastructure and deployment patterns
- Build tools and services to automate product updates, upgrades, and ongoing maintenance of the Unstructured AI application

## Description complète

Joining Collibra’s Engineering team

We’re shaping the way some of the largest organizations in
the world manage data by helping customers connect the right
data and insights for all Data Citizens. We are building a
team of exceptional people to help us deliver on that
promise. As a Platform Engineer, you are the guiding force
behind bringing Collibra's product and vision to customers
and prospects. Responsible for both net-new and expansion
opportunities in existing accounts. Platform Engineers
establish knowledgeable and trusted relationships from day
one and serve as thoughtful technical advisors.

This is a hybrid role based in our New York office. Our
hybrid model means you’ll work from the office at least two
days each week. This setup helps us stay connected, work
more closely together, and keep making progress as a team.

Platform Engineers at Collibra are responsible for

- Designing, implementing, and maintaining the infrastructure
  blueprint that enables customers to deploy our
  Unstructured AI application in their own cloud
  environments (AWS, GCP, Azure).
- Codifying infrastructure as code (Terraform), building
  modular, secure, and reproducible configurations that
  support flexible deployment scenarios, including bring-
  your-own VPC, custom networking, and varied customer
  setups.
- Managing and evolving the deployment stack, including
  Kubernetes and Helm-based deployments of the Unstructured
  AI application, and supporting components such as
  databases, secrets, and user authentication and SSO.
- Set up and maintaining monitoring, alerting, and
  observability systems (e.g. Datadog, Prometheus, Grafana)
  to ensure operational visibility and proactive incident
  detection.
- Participating in incident response and troubleshooting for
  customer-deployed environments, using observability data
  to diagnose and resolve issues quickly.
- Collaborating with the application team to implement python
  code and configuration changes supporting evolving
  infrastructure, authentication, and deployment patterns.
- Building tools and services to automate product updates,
  simplifying upgrades and ongoing maintenance of the
  Unstructured AI application..
- Working closely with product and field engineering teams to
  ensure reliable, scalable, and easily deployable
  infrastructure for every customer.

You are

- Someone who takes pride in designing clean, modular
  infrastructure that scales across clouds and deployment
  models.
- Comfortable working at the intersection of application and
  infrastructure, ensuring the Unstructured AI product runs
  smoothly and efficiently.
- Someone who thinks in automation and maintainability,
  building systems that are resilient, secure, and easy to
  operate.
- Actively taking ownership in rapidly evolving environments,
  adapting infrastructure and deployments as the
  Unstructured AI product grows and changes.
- Someone who prioritizes observability, reliability, and
  performance as integral parts of product delivery.
- Experienced in collaborating effectively with engineers
  across domains to deliver robust, production-ready
  systems.

You have

- 2+ years of engineering or technical experience with strong
  exposure to infrastructure, cloud, or platform work.
- Strong experience with Terraform or similar infrastructure-
  as-code tools and modular deployment best practices.
- Good understanding of Kubernetes, Helm, and container
  orchestration.
- Experience deploying and managing workloads in at least one
  major cloud platform (AWS, GCP, or Azure).
- python application code experience to align infrastructure
  and deployment patterns.
- Familiarity with networking, IAM, and security
  configurations, including VPC design and private
  networking.
- Experience with deployment automation and CI/CD pipelines,
  including container image management (ECR, GCR, ACR).
- Hands-on experience with monitoring, observability, and
  incident response, debugging distributed systems using
  tools like Datadog, Prometheus, or Grafana.
- A background in DevOps, SRE, or platform engineering for
  complex, customer-deployed applications.
- A bachelor’s degree or equivalent related working experience
  is required.
- This position is not eligible for visa sponsorship.

Measures of Success

- Within your first month, you’ll understand the Unstructured
  AI deployment architecture across clouds and begin
  contributing small improvements to Terraform and Helm
  configurations.
- Within your third month, you’ll lead enhancements that
  improve the reliability, visibility, and maintainability
  of customer deployments across different environments.
- Within your sixth month, you’ll drive major automation
  initiatives that simplify ongoing operations, upgrades,
  and scalability for the Unstructured AI platform.
