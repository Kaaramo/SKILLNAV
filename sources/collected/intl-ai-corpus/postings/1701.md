## Description complète

We are seeking a highly skilled Machine Learning Engineer
with 4+ years of experience to bridge the gap between
Generative AI and actionable business intelligence. You will
be responsible for optimizing Large Language Model (LLM)
performance and building sophisticated clustering models to
drive customer and product segmentation.

Key Responsibilities:

- LLM Optimization: Maximize the accuracy and minimize the
  latency of LLM-powered applications.
- Performance Evaluation: Implement robust LLM evaluation
  frameworks to ensure high-quality outputs.
- Advanced Analytics: Segment customers and products into
  meaningful clusters by incorporating complex business
  logic into ML models.
- Data Orchestration: Build and maintain efficient data
  pipelines using SQL and Python DAGs.

Technical Requirements:

Mandatory Skills:

- LLM: Proven experience in LLM Evaluation; familiarity with
  LLM APIs is a strong plus.
- Machine Learning: Expertise in Clustering techniques.
- Data Processing: Strong proficiency in SQL and developing
  Python-based DAGs (e.g., Airflow).

Preferred (Bonus) Skills:

- Advanced LLM: Experience in self-hosting and fine-tuning
  LLMs.
- Extended ML: Proficiency in Time Series Forecasting,
  Dimension Reduction, and Market Basket Analysis.
- Big Data Tools: Hands-on experience with Spark and Alteryx.
