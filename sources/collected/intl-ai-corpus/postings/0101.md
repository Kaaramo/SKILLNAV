About Man Group

Man Group is a global alternative investment management firm
focused on pursuing outperformance for sophisticated clients
via our Systematic, Discretionary and Solutions offerings.
Powered by talent and advanced technology, our single and
multi-manager investment strategies are underpinned by deep
research and span public and private markets, across all
major asset classes, with a significant focus on
alternatives. Man Group takes a partnership approach to
working with clients, establishing deep connections and
creating tailored solutions to meet their investment goals
and those of the millions of retirees and savers they
represent.

Headquartered in London, we manage $213.9 billion* and
operate across multiple offices globally. Man Group plc is
listed on the London Stock Exchange under the ticker EMG.LN
and is a constituent of the FTSE 250 Index. Further
information can be found at www.man.com

* As at 30 September 2025

The Team

At Man Group, our ambitions for AI are significant. We have
a long history of pioneering technology in finance, and this
role presents the opportunity to join us, as we work towards
leveraging scalable intelligence to fundamentally change how
we identify and execute on investment opportunities.

We are looking for a high-performing Senior Python Engineer
to build solutions that make use of the latest GenAI and
Machine Learning techniques and capabilities. This role is
at the intersection of technology, generative AI and
finance, helping to deliver high-impact, novel solutions
that touch every part of our business.

Working in our Machine Learning Technology team, you will
build out the core AI platform and infrastructure used
across Man Group. You will also partner and consult with
teams across the business to create agents and tools that
augment, automate and transform our investment and
operational workflows.

Role Responsibilities

- Design, build, and maintain the firm's core AI and Machine
  Learning platform
- Partner with stakeholders in research, trading, technology,
  and operations to design, build, and deploy bespoke AI/ML
  applications and agents
- Actively monitor the latest developments in applied AI to
  identify, propose, and implement novel AI-powered
  solutions
- Proactively engage with teams across the business to promote
  understanding and adoption of AI tools and capabilities

Key Competencies

Essential

- Advanced proficiency in Python, with extensive professional
  experience in building production applications and
  services
- Proven experience working in a formal Python development
  environment alongside other developers, following software
  development best practices
- Demonstrable enthusiasm for AI engineering and Machine
  Learning
- Strong academic record to include a degree with high
  mathematical and computing content e.g. Computer Science,
  Mathematics, Engineering or Physics
- Self-organised with the ability to effectively manage time
  across multiple projects and with competing business
  demands and priorities
- Strong interpersonal skills with the ability to establish
  and maintain a close working relationship with traders,
  quantitative researchers, and other stakeholders

Advantageous

- Familiarity with front-end frameworks/languages e.g.
  TypeScript, React, Next.js
- Experience integrating with databases such as PostgreSQL and
  Redis
- Experience using GenAI to build agentic, knowledge
  processing, or automated coding tools
- Experience training or fine-tuning generative models
- Experience building tools and technologies within systematic
  or discretionary investing units
- Experience working with applied mathematics, financial
  markets and instruments, with awareness of underlying
  concepts
- Experience with our tooling stack: Linux/UNIX, Git, Jira,
  Slack
