Who We Are

Pindrop is the Real Human + Right Human® Identity Trust
Platform for the AI era. As AI-driven fraud and deepfakes
erode trust in digital communication, Pindrop delivers
continuous identity verification and deepfake detection

Enterprises rely on Pindrop to secure billions of high-risk
customer interactions each year, including top U.S. banks,
as well as leading insurers and healthcare providers.
Powered by models trained on more than 1.5 billion real-

- Build automated systems that drive efficiency across the

  conversion rates.

Cross-Team Collaboration & Process Improvement

- Partner with Sales, Customer Success, and Revenue Operations

  process alignment.
- Train and support internal teams on marketing tools, best
  practices, and analytics.
- Continuously improve and automate workflows to increase
  efficiency and scalability.
