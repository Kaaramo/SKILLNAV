### Raisonnement classification (LLM upstream)

This role uses generative AI tools (Adobe Firefly,
Midjourney) as part of the design workflow to augment
creative output, but the primary function is graphic design
and visual storytelling rather than building, training, or
deploying AI systems. The role is AI-support because it uses
AI tools as a creative assistant within a traditional design
role, not as an ML engineer or AI developer.

Job Description

We’re looking for a Creative Graphic Engineer who’s equal
parts designer, innovator, and generative AI experimenter.
If you're someone who loves pushing pixels, but also loves
pushing boundaries, this is your role.

This is not your typical graphic design job. We want someone
who’s excited about reimagining visual storytelling using
emerging AI tools, breaking out of the static eBook mold,
and designing with data-backed intent. You'll be a key part
of a fast-paced Marketing team that’s redefining how
creative works in a modern B2B world.

What You’ll Do

- Design digital assets that move the needle. Think high-
  impact visuals for eBooks, ads, social media, interactive
  web assets, landing pages, and more.
- Use generative AI tools to augment, experiment, and scale
  creative output, always testing what works while upholding
  our brand integrity.
- Translate strategy and messaging into scroll-stopping,
  performance-driven creative across digital and print.
- Collaborate with writers, video, and campaign strategists to
  bring stories to life visually, while evolving our brand
  expression.
- Work inside our creative tech stack with tools like Adobe
  CC, Adobe Firefly, Figma, Bynder, Seismic, Google Drive,
  and Monday.com to manage and deliver assets.
- Bring ideas to planning sessions, test new formats, and
  track how your creative designs perform.
- Help define and evolve visual standards, working across
  Product, Brand, and Demand Gen.

Qualifications



- 5+ years in graphic design, ideally in a B2B tech or SaaS
  environment (agency or in-house).
- Ability to use generative AI (e.g., Adobe Firefly,
  Midjourney, Canva) as part of your design process.
- A strong portfolio that showcases AI generation experience,
  versatility, experimentation, and an understanding of
  performance-driven design.
- Mastery of design fundamentals, but a willingness to break
  the rules when the concept calls for it.
- A curious mindset. You’re constantly exploring new tools,
  trends, and creative formats.
- Comfort working in a fast-moving, cross-functional team that
  iterates quickly and values creative risk-taking.

Bonus Points If You

- Have motion/animation chops.
- Have worked on brand evolutions or helped build a visual
  system from the ground up.
- Bring a point of view on the future of AI and creativity.
