Job Description

Job Description

Visa’s People Technology team is seeking a Senior Software
Engineer – Automation with strong expertise in Microsoft
Power Platform, Digital Process Automation, and Generative
AI, combined with hands‑on experience in IT Operations,
Application Support, and Governance, Risk & Compliance
(GRC).

This role focuses on designing and scaling intelligent
automations while also ensuring operational stability,
security, compliance, and resilience of People Technology
applications. You will work closely with business
stakeholders, architects, infrastructure, security, and
platform teams to deliver secure, scalable, and reliable
solutions across the enterprise.

Key Responsibilities

Automation & Engineering

- Design, develop, and deploy end‑to‑end automation solutions
  using Microsoft Power Platform (Power Automate, Power
  Apps, Dataverse, Power Virtual Agents / Copilot Studio)
  and Generative AI
- Build intelligent process orchestrations integrating
  low‑code workflows, RPA, APIs, and AI‑driven components
- Apply Power Platform architectural best practices, including
  governance, security, scalability, and environment
  strategy
- Develop and deploy RPA components, including bots,
  connectors, logging, monitoring, and code repositories
- Support solution rollout, hyper‑care, and operational
  stability of deployed automations
- Create and maintain technical and functional documentation,
  including solution designs, UML diagrams, and process maps
- Ensure automation quality through testing, validation, and
  QA processes
- Partner with business stakeholders to capture requirements
  and translate them into technical designs and
  implementation plans
- Promote reusability, modular design, and performance
  optimization
- Integrate systems using SFTP, HTTP, SMTP, REST/SOAP, and
  enterprise APIs
- Measure and report automation KPIs and ROI, driving
  continuous improvement
- Stay current with emerging trends in Power Platform, RPA,
  and Generative AI

IT Operations & Application Support

- Provide Level 2 / Level 3 application support for assigned
  People Technology systems
- Develop and maintain IT Configuration Management practices
  and methodologies
- Administer configuration and asset management tools,
  ensuring CMDB accuracy, completeness, and integrity
- Manage server patching for operating systems and middleware
  in coordination with Infrastructure teams
- Plan, schedule, and execute vulnerability remediation
  activities using tools such as Qualys
- Monitor, identify, and report unauthorized or non‑compliant
  changes to configuration items
- Collaborate closely with Infrastructure, Platform, and
  Security teams to support evolving technical requirements
- Participate in incident, problem, and change management,
  including root cause analysis and corrective actions

Governance, Risk & Compliance (GRC)

- Participate in internal and external audits and remediate
  audit findings for assigned applications
- Ensure compliance with Identity & Access Management (IAM)
  and Data Privacy policies using approved enterprise tools
- Conduct application risk assessments and maintain security
  and privacy documentation
- Support operational resiliency planning , including:
  - Disaster recovery and failover strategies
  - Service impact and dependency assessments
  - Business continuity readiness

Application Ownership

- Serve as Application Technology Coordinator (ATC) for
  assigned systems
- Ensure ongoing compliance, configuration accuracy, and data
  quality
- Act as a key coordination point between engineering,
  operations, security, and business stakeholders
- Drive continuous improvement in operational maturity,
  reliability, and automation

This is a hybrid position. Expectation of days in the office
will be confirmed by your Hiring Manager.
