About us

Harmonic is the startup discovery engine.

It pains us to see great startup opportunities consistently
go undiscovered. So, we dedicated ourselves to mapping out
the startup landscape and building the tools that ensure the
most promising founders get found and funded.

The world's largest and most prolific venture capital firms
(as well as the up-and-comers you haven’t heard of yet) rely
on us to find and invest in the next Google, AirBnB, Uber,
Stripe, and Anduril. We play a crucial part in ensuring
hundreds of billions of dollars get routed efficiently and
that the innovations the world would most benefit from
materialize.

We’re growing revenue at a rate of 100%+ annually and
already power thousands of investors' workflow. Backed by
$30M from investors like Craft, Floodgate, and Sozo
Ventures, we want to power the entire investment workflow
from discovery to term sheet. If you resonate with our
values and want to fundamentally evolve how venture capital
markets work, come join us.

About Discovery & Scout

The Discovery & Scout (Search) team sits at the core of
Harmonic’s product and data engine. We make it possible to
search, research, analyze, compare, and evaluate across the
entire universe of startups (we track tens of millions of
companies, hundreds of millions of operators and, tens of
thousands of investors along with their deal activity, and
counting). We source and ingest data from first-, second-
and third-party sources, including hundreds of fragmented
and unstructured sources, and turn them into structured
insights and searchable indices. Recently, we built the
industry leading agentic layer for startup search and
research, that’s unlocking new paradigms for VCs to make
sense of this vast amount of data on startups and automate
critical tasks in their sourcing and diligence workflows.

We’re in the middle of a massive platform shift where AI and
LLMs are transforming what "software" is. Agentic product-
and user-experience patterns will be spelled out in
playbooks before long – and we’re building those playbooks,
not waiting for them. This includes conversational
interfaces, deep research, dynamic agentic frameworks
purpose built for venture, and AI-first product workflows.
We’re looking for folks that want to help redefine software
and push on this frontier with deep curiosity, an insatiable
growth mindset, and high agency.

The role

Scope of the role:

- Build AI-fueled products for VC: We have a tight-knit, fast-
  paced team working across the stack on the cutting edge of
  AI frameworks, LLM models, and the next-gen of AI-first
  product experiences.
  - You’ll build and ship end-to-end AI capabilities – from core
    features in our LangGraph backend, to critical user
    journeys in the frontend React UI.
- Agentic experimentation & evaluation: The industry is
  rapidly evolving, and new models and frameworks emerge
  weekly. We’ve built systems and evaluation frameworks that
  enable us to quickly determine whether new models perform
  better in pockets of the product, and are relentless about
  experimenting with new frameworks that emerge if we see a
  not-before-possible use case it can service.
  - You’ll be hands on rapidly prototyping, benchmarking, and
    tuning usage of LLM models and agentic architectures.
    Establishing and building new evaluation frameworks, and
    partnering with human (+ LLM) evaluators to constantly
    improve user outcomes for AI-driven experiences.
- Shape AI-native experiences: We’re in the very early innings
  of an HCI evolution, and are part of shaping AI-native
  design principles and patterns. We’re stoked to be at the
  forefront, proving out new functional patterns.
  - You will collaborate with product, design, data, and go-to-
    market teams to design and build AI-first product
    experiences that make investors 10x more productive.
