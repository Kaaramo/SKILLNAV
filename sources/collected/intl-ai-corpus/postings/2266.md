## Description complète

This isn’t your regular job. Almedia is a place where those
who want to push harder can accelerate their careers faster
than anywhere else. We’re aiming to become Germany’s second
bootstrapped unicorn. Almedia is already Europe’s #3
fastest-growing company in 2025 (FT1000).

We are building the future of marketing by rewarding our
community of over 70 million users for engaging with our
advertisers’ products. We are offering a new way to acquire
users for the biggest companies in the world.

About the Role

You will build and iterate on internal tools and products,
integrating AI capabilities into our systems. The focus is
on strong engineering with a clear AI angle: working across
the stack, connecting to AI APIs, and collaborating with
business stakeholders to improve their workflows.

What You’ll Do

- Develop and maintain full stack applications (front end,
  back end, databases)
- Build and maintain back-end services in Node.js or Python
- Integrate and orchestrate AI APIs into existing and new
  systems
- Use prompting and current AI tools to design useful AI-
  powered features
- Work across multiple systems and data sources, making sense
  of fragmented data
- Collaborate with business teams (e.g. marketing, UA,
  operations) to:
  - Understand their work and tools
  - Identify where AI can improve their workflows
  - Translate needs into technical solutions and iterate on them

Must-Haves

- Production experience with Node.js or Python
- Experience integrating and working with AI APIs
- Strong coding skills and solid engineering fundamentals
- Full stack capabilities: front end, back end, databases
- Good prompting skills and ability to use current AI tools
  effectively
- Ability to communicate clearly with non-technical
  stakeholders
- Curiosity, initiative, and strong learning mindset
  (especially around AI)

Nice-to-Haves

- Experience with TypeScript
- Experience with n8n or similar automation/orchestration
  tools
- Familiarity with agentic/system-thinking approaches for AI
  solutions
- Experience partnering closely with marketing, UA, or other
  business/ops teams

What We Value

- Engineering-first mindset, with the ability to understand
  business context
- Proactive approach: going to people, understanding their
  work, and suggesting improvements
- Comfort with ambiguity and evolving requirements
- Strong communication and collaboration across teams

Why Almedia?

- Own Our Growth: We offer all Berlin-based employees equity
  in Almedia to truly be a part of our success.
- Scale With Almedia: Grow alongside a startup that has been
  profitable from day one.
- Central Berlin Office: Work from a fully-stocked modern
  office built for collaboration, accessible from all around
  Berlin.
- Other Benefits: Transport subsidy, breakfasts and lunches,
  language learning, Urban Sports Club, and more.

We believe in fostering talent, evaluating all skill levels
during the hiring process, and providing a clear path for
growth. Almedia is an equal opportunity employer. We embrace
and celebrate diversity, and encourage individuals from all
backgrounds to apply.
