Position Summary

As a Software Engineer IV, you will work closely with the
team's senior engineers, Product Managers, and Designers.
You’ll also collaborate closely with our Pune in-office
Customer Operations team and teams in the Netherlands and
North America to solve real-world problems for our global
customers.

In this role, you will lead complex initiatives and own end-
to-end delivery of projects with moderate to large scope.
You anticipate delivery risks, align stakeholders early, and
maintain quality under tight timelines. Together with the
Senior Engineering Manager, you will develop excellent

HackerOne is investing in a CTEM roadmap that includes

also raise the bar for platform health and influence
HackerOne’s engineering practices to accelerate execution.

You’ll combine your technological expertise, domain
knowledge, and an unquestionable dedication to business
objectives as you intersect with the engineering
organisation. You identify and make systematic improvements
to technical weaknesses you’ll find in your squad’s
