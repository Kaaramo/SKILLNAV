## Description complète

Job Description

As a Senior Software Engineer – AI, you will collaborate
with a cross-functional team of product managers, UX
designers, and engineers to design, build, and deliver AI-
powered solutions.

About the role:


Build AI-Driven Systems:

- Architect and implement advanced AI systems, including
  multi-component pipelines, retrieval-augmented generation
  (RAG), and custom AI agents with multi-step reasoning.
- Integrate AI models into production software through robust
  APIs and scalable data pipelines.
- Adapt AI models and techniques to specialized domains,
  tailoring solutions for expert systems in areas such as
  legal, tax, and compliance.

Innovate:

- Evaluate and prototype cutting-edge AI techniques to solve
  business challenges.
- Conduct proof-of-concept projects for new AI-driven
  features.
- Stay current with AI research and emerging technologies.

Provide Technical Leadership:

- Break down ambiguous requirements into scalable technical
  specifications.
- Mentor junior engineers and facilitate technical
  discussions.
- Contribute to ML blogs and LLMOps practices within the team.
- Present thought leadership at internal and external forums
  representing the organization in emerging technology
  areas.

Ensure Quality & Operations:

- Implement comprehensive testing frameworks and monitoring
  systems for AI model performance.
- Ensure strong data governance, bias analysis, and security
  standards.
- Conduct scalability analysis and recommend operational
  improvements.

Collaborate Across Functions:

- Work closely with AI researchers, designers, and product
  teams to translate AI capabilities into real-world
  applications.
- Optimize AI system performance considering factors like
  latency and resource usage.
- Drive collaboration to feature enhancements through AI
  capabilities.
