### Raisonnement classification (LLM upstream)

This is a Staff Frontend Engineer role focused on building
UI/UX interfaces for AI-powered features ("OpsAI"), not
building, training, or deploying AI/ML models themselves.
The engineer works on frontend architecture, interaction
patterns, and user experience to present AI capabilities
from backend/ML teams, which is enabling AI work rather than
directly working on AI systems.
