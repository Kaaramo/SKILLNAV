## Description complète

Company Overview

300+ media companies as clients, $40+ billion in revenue
processed, 25,000+ worldwide users.

Operative is a revenue accelerant for media companies around
the world. No other software company in AdTech space, brings
a comparable depth of experience to create truly innovative
software that performs across all platforms, revenue models
and business units. We are a SAAS (Software as a Service)
platform which helps clients manage advertisements both in
the linear (TV) and digital space. We have been in the
market for over two decades and have 1100+ employees with 12
offices spread across the globe. Operative is proud to play
a pivotal role in the way advertising is bought, sold and
managed across media industry.

JOB SUMMARY:

We are seeking an experienced Senior AI Engineer to join our
team and accelerate the development of Adeline, our agentic
AI platform that automates business workflows for media
companies managing billions in advertising revenue. You'll
work directly with our AI Engineering team to build and
scale backend systems that integrate cutting-edge AI
capabilities into our core AOS, and other platforms. This is
a hands-on product engineering role where you'll collaborate
closely with our product team and customers to identify
high-impact AI use cases and translate them into production-
ready solutions that help the world's largest media
companies optimize their advertising operations.

MAIN DUTIES AND RESPONSIBILITIES:

- Design and develop scalable Python/Java services that power
  AI-driven features within Operative's advertising
  management platform, handling systems that process $8.5B
  in digital and $60B+ in global TV ad revenue
- Collaborate with AI teams to integrate Large Language Models
  using frameworks like Langchain, LangGraph, and other
  modern AI toolkits to create agentic workflows that
  automate complex advertising operations
- Build and maintain data pipelines and backend APIs that
  empowers our AI agentic capabilities, for powerful
  workflow and process automations
- Deploy and scale AI applications using Docker containers
  across AWS/GCP/Azure cloud environments, ensuring high
  availability for mission-critical advertising operations
  serving 300+ clients across 25+ countries
- Work directly with media company customers (FOX, NBCU,
  Disney, HBO, etc.) to understand their workflow pain
  points and translate business requirements into technical
  AI solutions that drive measurable revenue impact
- Contribute to architecture decisions, code reviews, and
  engineering best practices for AI systems, ensuring
  robust, scalable solutions that can handle enterprise-
  scale advertising operations
- Develop concrete roadmap items and lead implementation
  efforts for AI features that advance customers from proof-
  of-concept to production deployment of agentic AI
  workflows

SKILLS AND COMPETENCIES:

Must Have :

- Strong experience with web frameworks including
  FastAPI/Flask for API development and data manipulation
  tools like Pandas and SQLAlchemy for handling complex
  advertising datasets
- Hands-on Docker experience with demonstrated ability to
  containerize applications and manage deployment pipelines
  in cloud environments
- Proven experience running LLMs locally and in containerized
  environments, with practical knowledge of model
  deployment, optimization, and scaling challenges
- Direct experience integrating LLMs, system prompts, and
  Model Context Protocol (MCP) into existing production
  applications, not just proof-of-concepts
- Expertise with leading AI toolkits and frameworks including
  Langchain, LangGraph, Langfuse, InspectAI, LangSmith,
  GenAI Toolbox, LlamaIndex, and OpenSearch
- Strong debugging, collaboration, and communication skills
  with ability to work effectively across engineering,
  product, and customer-facing teams
- Product engineering mindset with understanding of how to
  translate customer needs into technical requirements and
  measurable business outcomes

Preferred:

- Experience with Streamlit for rapid dashboard and demo
  development
- Background in media, advertising technology, or high-volume
  transaction processing systems
- Experience with agentic AI workflows and automation of
  complex business processes
- Knowledge of advertising operations, inventory management,
  or programmatic advertising concepts

EDUCATION, CERTIFICATION AND EXPERIENCE:

- Bachelor's degree in Computer Science, Software Engineering,
  or related technical field
- 5+ years of professional software development experience
  with focus on backend systems and APIs
- Demonstrated experience shipping AI-powered products or
  features to production environments
- Experience working with enterprise customers and translating
  business requirements into technical solutions

Preferred:

- Advanced degree in Computer Science, Machine Learning, or
  related field
- Experience in advertising technology, media technology, or
  other high-volume transaction processing domains
- Previous experience in a product engineering role where
  AI/ML capabilities directly impacted customer workflows
  and business metrics
- Experience leading or mentoring engineering teams in AI
  implementation projects
