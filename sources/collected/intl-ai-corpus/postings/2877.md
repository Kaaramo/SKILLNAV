About the job

The AI & Optimization Lead — Bitcoin Mining Operations will
play a crucial role in defining and executing the AI vision
for MOS (Mining Operating System) and MDK (Mining
Development Kit) — the core FOSS platforms powering Tether’s
Bitcoin-mining operations worldwide.

This role is responsible for designing the data foundation
and AI models that drive real-world operational efficiency
across mining sites, with a focus on performance
optimization, predictive insights, and intelligent
automation. You will work closely with engineering, data,
and operations teams to define a structured data baseline,
ensure high-quality data modelling, and develop AI-driven
solutions that are fully integrated into MOS/MDK.

Your mission is simple and ambitious: turn data into
intelligence — and intelligence into site-level efficiency.

A strong background in data architecture, modelling, ML/AI
systems, and mathematical optimization is essential. Prior
exposure to Bitcoin mining or industrial/IoT environments is
highly valuable, but not mandatory.
