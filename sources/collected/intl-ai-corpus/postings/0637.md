## Description complète

About Galileo

Galileo is the leader in agent observability and run-time
protection. We help AI teams ship reliable AI with evals,
monitoring, and guardrails that work at production speed. In
a few years since inception, Galileo has earned the trust of
Fortune 10 banks, Fortune 50 telecommunications companies,
as well as AI teams at ServiceTitan, Comcast, NTT, Reddit,
and Headspace Health. We are backed by top-tier investors,
have offices in San Francisco and New York, and are growing
quickly.

We believe AI should empower builders, not slow them down.
That's why we say Galileo is the fastest way to ship
reliable AI.

Role Description

We’re looking for customer-facing AI Engineers to educate
the technical community with hands-on demos & content. As
part of our Developer Relations team, you’ll create
technical videos, demos, blogs, and workshops that help
developers learn to ship reliable AI applications.

We’re looking for an Engineer who loves diving deep into
code, building compelling demos, and explaining complex
workflows in ways that make other developers say “got it!”
You’ll be creating the kind of resources you wish someone
had made for you.

The ideal candidate combines deep chops in AI/ML with a
passion for teaching and building. You’ll spend most of your
time designing and delivering technical content. About 20%
of your time will go to representing Galileo at events,
either virtually or in-person.

You’ll also serve as a bridge between our product team and
the developers who use our platform — synthesizing developer
feedback into actionable product changes.

This is a chance to join the first 100 Galileans and shape
the future of agentic AI.

Main Responsibilities

- Build, document, and share sample agents using the latest
  models and frameworks, teaching the community how to build
  reliable AI.
- Create deep technical posts, videos, and tutorials that
  illustrate practical use cases for developers.
- Represent Galileo at conferences and virtual events,
  showcasing demos and connecting with developers in
  authentic conversation.
- Develop and maintain sample code, reference apps, and SDK
  enhancements that help developers get started quickly.
- Build relationships with AI thought leaders, influencers,
  and open-source communities to strengthen Galileo’s
  community.
- Partner with product, engineering, and growth teams to
  launch new products & features. Synthesize user feedback
  to shape our roadmap.
- Stay current with the latest in AI research, LLM models, and
  developer tooling.

What We're Looking For

- 2+ years as a Software Engineer building technical products.
  An active Github profile is a must.
- Hands-on experience with AI tools, LLMs, agents, and
  frameworks.
- Proficiency in Python and TypeScript; able to prototype and
  ship small projects quickly.
- B.S. in Computer Science or equivalent
- Passion for teaching others how to build.
- Track record of engaging with developers on social media.
- Nice-to-have: experience in high-growth startups.
