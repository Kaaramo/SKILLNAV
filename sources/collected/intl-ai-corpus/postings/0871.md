About GlobalFoundries

Introduction:

The Modern Workplace Engineer – Employee Productivity AI
role provides operational expertise for Microsoft 365
services, focusing on AI-driven productivity solutions. This
role will report into the Director of Intelligent Workspace
that includes administration and governance of SharePoint,
Power Platform, Viva Suite, and Copilot. The role ensures
compliance, service reliability, and adoption of modern
workplace technologies to enhance employee experience.

Key Responsibilities:

- Hands-on development of Copilot Agents.  Familiar with pro-
  code development methodology and copilot connectors using
  Copilot Studio

- Implement governance policies for Copilot agents and Power
  Platform environments.

- Administer Power Platform environments (Dev/Test/Prod),
  enforce DLP policies, and monitor compliance.

- Configure and maintain Viva Connections/Home and SharePoint
  portals, including audience targeting and content
  governance.

- Operate service reliability runbooks for SharePoint/Teams
  and validate Copilot readiness for meeting experiences.

- Collaborate with Security teams to package evidence for
  compliance audits and manage platform-security
  escalations.

- Maintain analytics dashboards for adoption and compliance
  across Copilot, Viva, and Power Platform.

Required Qualifications:

- 4–7 years of experience in Microsoft 365 engineering with
  hands-on Power Platform administration.

- Experience operating Copilot Studio per governance standards
  and managing ALM in Managed Environments.

- Strong knowledge of SharePoint architecture, Viva Suite
  configuration, and Teams integration.

- Experience implementing security-aligned governance (labels,
  DLP, audit) and compliance frameworks.

Preferred Qualifications:

- Experience with AI-driven productivity tools and automation
  including emerging integration options including MCP
  (Model Context Protocol)

- Familiarity with enterprise governance frameworks and
  security best practices.
