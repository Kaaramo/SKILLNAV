## Description complète

AI Engineer

Location: Offshore (Remote)

Experience: 2-4 years

Engagement Type: Full-Time

About the Role: Smartbridge is looking for an AI Engineer to
develop and optimize AI-driven solutions using a range of AI
frameworks, cloud platforms, and large language model
services. The ideal candidate will have 2-4 years of
experience in AI engineering, machine learning models, and
cloud-based AI services, with a strong understanding of
integrating AI into business processes.

Responsibilities:

- Develop and customize AI-driven solutions to enhance
  business workflows and automate processes.
- Integrate AI services from providers such as Microsoft,
  OpenAI, and other leading platforms into enterprise
  applications.
- Design, train, and optimize AI models for predictive
  analytics, automation, and intelligent decision-making.
- Implement natural language processing (NLP), machine
  learning (ML), and automation models to improve user
  experiences.
- Collaborate with business users and developers to define AI
  integration needs and solution requirements.
- Perform data preprocessing, feature engineering, and fine-
  tuning of AI models.
- Ensure AI models are secure, scalable, and optimized for
  performance.
- Stay current on emerging AI tools, frameworks, and best
  practices.

Technical Skills Required:

- Experience with AI/ML frameworks and tools such as
  LangChain, OpenAI, Azure AI, or similar.
- Python or R for AI/ML model development.
- Cloud AI services such as Azure AI, AWS AI, or Google Cloud
  AI.
- Large language models (LLMs) and prompt engineering for AI-
  driven applications.
- Natural Language Processing (NLP) for text and conversation
  modeling.
- Power Automate or similar workflow automation tools for AI
  integration.
- Data Engineering: SQL, Power Query, and ETL operations.
- Familiarity with Git, DevOps, and Agile methodologies.

Additional Requirements:

- Bachelor's degree in Computer Science, AI, Data Science, or
  related field.
- AI-related certifications such as Azure AI Engineer, AWS
  Machine Learning Specialty, or equivalent preferred.
- Experience in industries such as Oil & Gas, Healthcare, or
  Food & Beverage is a plus.
- 100% remote role – must be available for the second shift to
  overlap with the US team until Noon CST.
- 3-month probation period before full-time confirmation. If
  certification is not already held, it must be completed
  during probation.

Recruitment Process & Technical Testing: Candidates will
undergo a 45-60 minute TestGorilla assessment, including:

- Intro Video Section – Candidate introduction & motivation.
- Analytical & Problem-Solving Skills – Scenario-based
  questions.
- Technical Test – Covering AI engineering, Python, SQL, and
  cloud AI services.
