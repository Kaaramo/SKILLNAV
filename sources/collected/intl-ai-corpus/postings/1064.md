## Description complète

What success looks like in this role:

- Rapidly converting ideas, business problems, and hypotheses
  into working demonstrations & PoCs
- Designing, building, and orchestrating Agentic AI systems,
  including autonomous and semi-autonomous agents capable of
  reasoning, planning, tool usage, and goal execution.
- Leading the design and development of Generative AI
  applications leveraging LLMs, embeddings, vector
  databases, RAG, and multi-agent frameworks.
- Working with Scrum/Agile teams to develop rapid prototypes
  and work toward scalable enterprise solutions.
- Evaluating, selecting, and integrating AI frameworks, agent
  orchestration tools, and model providers relevant to
  agentic and generative AI development.
- Staying current on LLMs, agent architectures, reasoning,
  memory, and AI best practices.
- Learning, evaluating, and leveraging emerging AI tooling
  ecosystems including copilots, agent builders,
  orchestration platforms, and observability tools.
- Self-starter working independently as well as
  collaboratively to deliver high-impact outcomes.
- Providing technical leadership, guidance, and mentorship to
  junior AI engineers, fostering a culture of continuous
  learning and innovation.
- Demonstrating strong ownership of quality, reliability,
  security, and productivity, using efficient development
  practices, reusable components, and enterprise standards.

You will be successful in this role if you have:

- BS/BE Degree with 5-8 years of relevant experience.
- Strong hands-on experience with Large Language Models (LLMs)
  and their application in agent-based and autonomous
  systems.
- Solid understanding of Agentic AI concepts, including
  planning, reasoning, and multi-agent collaboration.
- Solid understanding and hands-on experience agent

  CrewAI).
- Proficiency with Azure AI Services, including Azure OpenAI,
  Azure AI Doc Intelligence, Translator, etc

  capabilities, limitations, prompt engineering, function

- Strong programming skills in Python, with experience
  integrating AI services, APIs, and data pipelines.

  Golang, with a focus on API-driven architecture.

  familiarity with vector databases.
- Working knowledge of at least one front-end framework such
  as React or Angular for UI
- Hands-on experience with cloud-native development,
  containers, and microservices.
- Demonstrated experience in cluster using Kubernetes, Docker,
  Helm & platforms like Rancher
- Familiarity with UI/UX tooling such as Figma for rapid
  prototyping and design collaboration.
- Cloud certifications (Azure Developer, Azure AI Engineer, or
  equivalent) are an added advantage.
