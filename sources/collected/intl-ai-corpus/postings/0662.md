## Description complète

About Numeral:

Numeral is building the automation backbone for internet
commerce — starting with the painful world of sales tax
compliance. We handle everything from registration to
remittance, delivering a white-glove service so e-commerce
businesses can stay laser-focused on what they do best:
growing their products, customers, and teams.

We’re one of the fastest-growing companies from Y
Combinator’s W23 batch, backed by top-tier investors
including Benchmark Capital. Our team has deep roots from
the early days at Stripe, Airbnb, Notion, and other breakout
companies — and now we’re bringing that same level of craft,
speed, and ambition to a space that’s long overdue for
reinvention.

Numeral is small but mighty. Growth is already borderline
unmanageable — which means every hire we make now will
directly shape the trajectory of the company. If you’re
excited about joining as an early team member and want the
kind of ownership that defines careers, we want to meet you.

Mission:

Running an online business today means wearing a thousand
hats — most of them not the reason founders started their
company. Our mission is to eliminate the administrative and
accounting burdens that distract businesses from doing what
they love.

We’ve already helped hundreds of merchants avoid the
headache of building giant finance teams just to manage tax
compliance. Tomorrow, we’re scaling that impact even
further: building the automation layer that lets internet
businesses stay nimble, compliant, and future-proof.

Numeral has raised over $57M, now serves over 2,500 paying
customers and we have more than tripled our revenue every
year in our history.

About the role:

We’re looking for a Foundational AI Engineer who thrives in
ambiguity, enjoys building real-world AI systems end-to-end,
and wants to define the technical and architectural
foundations of an early-stage startup.

This is a hands-on role for someone who wants to design and
implement LLM-powered automation systems: model
orchestration, tool calling, MCP-style integrations, agentic
workflows, and automation-driven tooling, while being
familiar with a traditional full-stack development
environment. You should also extensively use AI-powered
tools for development.

You’ll work closely with product and leadership to
concretize AI ideas into production-grade systems that
customers rely on daily.

Responsibilities:

- Design, build, and operate production AI systems, including
  tool-calling agents, model orchestration layers, and RAG.
- Implement and maintain AI integrations using modern
  frameworks, MCP servers, and external model providers.
- Build and evolve scalable backend services and APIs that
  power automated workflows and customer-facing features.
- Think in automation and workflows: design systems using
  tools like n8n, Claude Code-style agents, or similar
  orchestration approaches where appropriate.
- Make architectural decisions that balance latency, cost,
  reliability, and iteration speed.
- Own infrastructure, observability, and performance for both
  AI and non-AI services.
- Collaborate closely with product, design, and data teams to
  ship features that solve real customer problems.
- Help define engineering best practices, AI
  safety/reliability norms, and our overall technical
  culture.

Qualifications:

- 7+ years of experience building products in high-growth
  environments.
- 2+ years of experience building with LLMs.
- Avid use of state-of-the-art AI development tools such as
  Cursor and Claude Code.
- Fluency with backend technologies like PostgreSQL, Redis,
  and cloud services (AWS preferred).
- Experience with frontend development, especially TypeScript
  and its ecosystem.
- Strong product sense, balancing performance, flexibility,
  and speed.
- Comfortable working in dynamic startup environments with
  evolving needs.
- Excellent communication and collaboration skills.

Bonus Points For:

- Experience in payments, tax, accounting, or regulatory tech.
- Experience with automation tools (n8n, Zapier-style systems,
  workflow engines) or building internal automation
  platforms.
- Familiarity with MCPs, tool calls, agent frameworks, or
  model orchestration patterns.
- Understanding of vector databases and concepts (such as
  Pinecone, HNSW).

Why Join Us?

- Competitive salary and equity - you'll share directly in the
  company's success
- Full medical, dental, and vision coverage
- Wellness perks like Headspace and the Peloton One App
- 401(k) to help you build long-term financial security
- Lunch and snacks when you're in the office
- Regular team offsites and company events as we grow
- A culture built on ownership - your work matters and people
  will see it!
