## Description complète

Who We Are

Maybern is transforming the way private fund managers
effectively manage their funds through cutting edge
technology. Maybern is founded by top engineering experts
with deep knowledge of the fund management space.

Private funds manage $15T in capital and are growing at 20%
YoY, but with increasing regulatory scrutiny and investor
demands for transparency, the need for world class software
to help private fund CFOs is crucial.

This is where Maybern comes in.

We are equipping fund managers with intuitive, flexible fund
management software, enabling private investment managers to
focus on what they do best: driving returns for their
investors. We are backed by leading venture capital firms
and a large number of strategic investors.

Maybern is an NYC based company with a strong in-office
culture to drive collaboration, and we are looking to make
strong additions to our team.

What We're Looking For

We are looking for a Senior or Staff Applied AI Engineer to
build our implementation engineering function from the
ground up. This role requires independence, cross-functional
collaboration, and creativity through experimentation and
applied AI. You’ll work closely with our internal
implementations team to understand the systems through which
they process years of customer data to onboard new customers
to the Maybern platform. You’ll dig through complex problems
and deliver elegant technical solutions and automation.

This role is critical to Maybern’s success and highly
quantifiable: faster, smoother implementations mean faster
value for our customers and faster revenue capture for us.
Depending on seniority and fit, this role can offer a clear
path to team management.

What You'll Do

- Build and lead the Implementation Engineering team, setting
  technical vision and standards for high-impact client
  deployments.
- Collaborate directly with our clients to understand their
  most pressing operational challenges, then design and
  build tailored solutions that address them.
- Architect scalable systems that integrate client data,
  deeply leverage AI, and drive measurable business
  outcomes.
- Partner closely with Product and Engineering leadership to
  influence Maybern’s roadmap based on field insights.
- Own the end-to-end delivery of complex implementations,
  ensuring technical excellence, client satisfaction, and
  long-term success.

This Could Be a Great Fit If You…

- Have 6+ years of experience in software engineering with at
  least 1+ years in a technical leadership role.
- Are curious about or open to managing a team in the future.
- Have led in early-stage or high-growth environments, and
  know how to scale both systems and teams.
- Have experience building internal tools for operations-heavy
  businesses.
- Have experience influencing team and product roadmaps
  through thoughtful prioritization.
- Are comfortable working with server-side languages and
  frameworks (we’re tech agnostic in interviews, but use
  Python and Django in practice).
- Have experience designing and deploying large-scale systems
  that integrate data, AI/ML, or advanced analytics.
- Bring a track record of success working directly with
  customers, executives, and technical stakeholders to
  deliver solutions.
- Are excited to work in-office with a great team!

Benefits

- We offer a competitive salary + equity package,
  comprehensive benefits, and a flexible family-friendly
  work environment. Some of our perks include:
  - Comprehensive healthcare benefits
    - Maybern covers 100% of medical, dental, and vision premiums
    - HSA & FSA plans
  - Family-Friendly policies
    - Paid parental leave
  - Flexible schedule
    - Flexible PTO / sick leave
    - WFH as needed to fit your needs
  - 401k
