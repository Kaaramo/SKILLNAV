About the role:

As a Staff Software Engineer (AI), you will lead the design

Build & Architect AI-Driven Systems

- Design and lead the implementation of complex, enterprise-
  scale AI systems, including sophisticated multi-component
  pipelines, advanced retrieval-augmented generation (RAG)
  architectures, and autonomous AI agents with multi-step
  reasoning capabilities.

- Architect scalable AI model integration strategies for
  production-ready prototypes using robust APIs, distributed
  data pipelines, and microservices architectures.

- Lead the adaptation and customization of AI models for
  specialized internal domains, driving innovation in
  business process transformation.

- Define technical standards and best practices for rapid
  prototyping and solution discovery.

- Contribute directly to hands‑on delivery, leveraging AI
  development tools to rapidly build and iterate on the
  systems you design.

Drive Innovation & Technical Strategy

- Lead evaluation and strategic adoption of cutting-edge AI
  techniques to solve complex business challenges for
  internal teams.

- Oversee proof-of-concept projects and guide technical
  feasibility assessments for new AI-driven features.

- Stay at the forefront of AI research and emerging
  technologies, translating insights into actionable
  technical roadmaps.

- Drive innovation initiatives and establish TR’s position in
  emerging AI technology areas.

Provide Senior Technical Leadership

- Lead technical architecture decisions and translate high-
  level business requirements into comprehensive, scalable
  technical specifications.

- Foster technical growth and career development of the team.

- Champion MLOps and LLMOps practices in the prototyping and
  delivery phases.

- Serve as a senior thought leader, driving technical
  discussions in executive forums and representing Forward
  Engineering at internal events.

Lead Cross-Functional Collaboration

- Embed directly with prioritized business units to develop
  deep domain expertise and build stakeholder relationships.

- Partner with business leaders, functional SMEs, and product
  teams to co-create solutions and drive adoption.

- Document solutions and architectural decisions for knowledge
  transfer and capability building.
