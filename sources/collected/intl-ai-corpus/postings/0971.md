Lead AI Engineer About the Role

We're looking for a Tech Lead to own the technical direction
of proAgent's core architecture. You'll lead a small, high-
agency team building AI Agents that handle nuanced financial
conversations-where every millisecond of latency and every
reasoning decision matters.

This is a player-coach role. You'll write code, make
architectural decisions, and set the technical bar for how
we build intelligent, low-latency voice agents.

What You'll OwnAgent Loop & Reasoning

- Design the agentic runtime that powers natural, multi-turn
  conversations while executing complex business logic
  (payment negotiations, compliance guardrails, objection
  handling)
- Build primitives that balance conversational fluidity with
  structured reasoning-the agent needs to feel human while
  making verifiable decisions
- Create context management systems that track consumer state,
  conversation history, and business rules across long,
  branching dialogues

Voice AI Systems

- Architect our real-time voice pipeline for sub-1s latency
  across transcription, reasoning, and synthesis
- Own VAD tuning, and turn-taking logic that makes
  conversations feel natural
- Evaluate and integrate speech models (STT, TTS, speech-to-
  speech) as the landscape evolves-we currently work with
  ElevenLabs and Cartesia for Text to Speech, and Deepgram
  for Speech to Text, and are always exploring what's next
- Debug and optimize streaming audio and WebRTC

LLM Infrastructure

- Design our prompting, orchestration, and model routing
  strategies
- Build evaluation frameworks that measure agent quality
  beyond accuracy-conversation quality, empathy, compliance
  adherence
- Stay current on model capabilities and make pragmatic build-
  vs-integrate decisions

Technical Leadership

- Set architectural direction for proAgent platform and
  surrounding applications
- Mentor engineers and establish engineering practices that
  scale
- Partner with Product and Client teams to translate customer
  problems into technical roadmaps
- Make tradeoffs between shipping fast and building durable
  systems-we need both

What You Bring

- 4+ years building production systems, with 1+ years in a
  Lead for a project
- Hands-on work with LLMs in production: prompt engineering,
  orchestration, evaluation
- High bias for action to see things to production - real-time
  systems can get gnarly, and truly high bias is needed to
  take things to production
- Strong fundamentals in Python and TypeScript
- Clear technical communication-you can explain complex
  tradeoffs to engineers and stakeholders alike

Even Better

- Experience with voice AI: speech recognition, synthesis,
  telephony systems
- Background in fintech, lending, or collections
- Experience with Twilio, LiveKit, ElevenLabs, or similar
  real-time infrastructure
- You've built agentic systems that combine LLM reasoning with
  structured actions
- Track record of building and scaling small teams

Why This Role

- High leverage: proAgent is poised to handle millions of live

- Frontier work: Voice AI that reasons, decides, and acts is
  one of the hardest problems in applied AI. We're building

- Ownership: Small team, big scope. You'll shape what proAgent
