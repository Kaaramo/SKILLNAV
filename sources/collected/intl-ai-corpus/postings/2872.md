## Description complète

At Too Good To Go, we have an ambitious mission: to inspire
and empower everyone to fight food waste together.

More than 1/3 of all food produced in the world is wasted.
And that has a huge impact on the health of our planet. 10%
of greenhouse gas emissions come from food waste and loss.

Through our marketplace app, we connect businesses that have
unsold, surplus food, with consumers who can buy and enjoy
it at ⅓ of retail value. We are a certified B Corporation
with a mission to empower everyone to take action against
food waste. Alongside our marketplace app, we create
educational tools, explore new business solutions, and
influence legislation to help reduce food waste.

We’re growing fast: Our community of 120 million registered
users and 180,000 active partners across 21 countries, have
together already saved over 500 million meals from going to
waste - avoiding almost 1.3 million tonnes of CO2e!

In 2023, Too Good To Go was recognised on Fortune's
acclaimed 'Change the World' list, named as one of
Deloitte's 'Best Managed Companies' and won a coveted
'Cultural Impact Award' at the annual Apple App Store
Awards.

We’re looking for an exceptional AI Solutions Developer to
join the Commercial superstar team at Too Good To Go.

Your mission

Your mission is to transform how we operate by turning
cutting edge AI into a global operational reality. As our AI
Solutions Developer, you won't just be "implementing AI" you
will be the architect of the agentic systems and intelligent
workflows that allow us to scale our mission of saving food
across 21 countries.

You will sit at the high impact intersection of technology
and commerce. By partnering with stakeholders in Product,
Engineering, and Commercial teams, you will hunt for
operational bottlenecks and replace them with elegant AI-
driven solutions. You aren't just looking for "process
optimization"; you are redefining what is possible in our
daily operations.

By combining your technical skills and commercial
understanding, you will help our teams work more effectively
and focus on what truly matters, ensuring that every
automated process translates into more food saved.

- Location: Copenhagen or London (Hybrid)

The Role in Detail

- Strategic Opportunity Discovery: You will proactively
  interrogate existing operations to identify high-value
  opportunities for automation. You act as a strategic
  sparring partner for the Commercial organization,
  challenging the status quo and prioritizing use cases
  based on ROI and scalability.
- End-to-End AI Architecture: You will lead the design and
  implementation of commercial AI systems. This includes
  defining data requirements, selecting optimal models
  (LLMs, SLMs, or ML), and architecting the full stack -
  from prompt engineering and tool development to deployment
  and monitoring.
- Ecosystem & Tooling Strategy: As the internal authority on
  AI, you will curate our commercial AI toolkit. You will
  evaluate, procure, and integrate third-party services,
  ensuring our tech stack remains cutting-edge while
  maintaining a tight partnership with Tech and Legal teams.
- Governance & Responsible AI: You will ensure all AI
  initiatives are built on a foundation of integrity. This
  includes maintaining strict compliance with GDPR and
  championing ethical AI practices and robust organizational
  security policies.
- Cross-Functional Synthesis: You serve as the bridge between
  technical complexity and business value. You will
  translate vague business pain points into elegant, high-
  performing technical solutions that align with broader
  company goals.
- Change Leadership & Adoption: Beyond technical deployment,
  you are responsible for the human element of AI. You will
  drive organizational transformation by designing new ways
  of working, ensuring sustainable adoption, and measuring
  the lasting impact on productivity.

Requirements

- The "Agentic" Expert: You have hands-on experience building
  autonomous or semi-autonomous agents using frameworks like
  LangGraph, CrewAI, or AutoGen.
- Technically Versatile: You are proficient in Python, with
  experience in web frameworks (FastAPI/Node.js) and
  building/consuming RESTful APIs.
- Reliability Obsessed: You know that non-deterministic
  systems can be unpredictable. You have expertise in
  Advanced Prompt Engineering and establishing LLM Evals to
  ensure systems meet commercial quality standards.
- Data-Grounded: You have practical knowledge of RAG
  architectures, vector embeddings, and function-calling to
  connect LLMs to real-time business data.
- Engineering Disciplined: You live by software best practices
  - CI/CD, Git, unit testing, and structured documentation
  are second nature to you.
- Strategic Communicator: You can explain "what is feasible"
  to peers and prioritize projects based on a clear-eyed
  view of ROI vs. effort.
- Commercial Architect: You possess a "Product Mindset." You
  can lead discovery sessions with non-technical
  stakeholders and build "UI/UX Lite" interfaces (e.g.,
  Streamlit, Retool) to make your solutions accessible.
