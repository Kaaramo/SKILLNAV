## Description complète

AWS Cloud & Generative AI Engineer

Location: Remote / Hybrid

Experience Level: Senior (5+ years)

Summary

We are seeking a seasoned Cloud engineer to build full-
stack, cloud-native applications that merge modern AI
innovation with scalable, sustainable architecture. This
role blends hands-on AWS development and driving enterprise-
grade GenAI adoption across diverse products and teams.

Key Responsibilities

• Implement serverless, event-driven cloud applications
using AWS services such as Lambda, API Gateway, DynamoDB,
Step Functions, and S3.

• Integration of Generative AI workflows leveraging Amazon
Bedrock, RAG pipelines, and knowledge-base-driven context
engineering to deliver intelligent, reliable, and extensible
AI capabilities.

• Develop internal frameworks and reusable modules that
enhance developer experience, maintainability, and cloud
efficiency.

• Collaborate cross-functionally with product, design, and
data science teams to bridge business goals with scalable
cloud solutions.

• Champion accessibility-first design and inclusive
engineering practices across products and internal tooling.

• Mentor engineers and influence architectural direction
aligned with organizational growth and sustainability
objectives.

Required Qualifications

• 5+ years of experience in software engineering, cloud
architecture, or full-stack application development.

• Deep hands-on experience with AWS (e.g., Bedrock, Lambda,
Step Functions, Redshift, Glue, ECS/EKS, IAM).

• Proficiency in Python, TypeScript/Node.js, or similar
languages for serverless development.

• Strong grasp of context engineering, prompt orchestration,
and LLM application design.

• Proven success leading cross-functional engineering teams
to innovate at the intersection of technology and business.

• Commitment to accessibility and inclusive design
principles.

• Experience integrating agent-based architectures and
retrieval-augmented generation (RAG) on AWS.

• Understanding of application-level security, compliance,
and cost optimization best practices in the cloud.

• Background in developing internal developer platforms,
pipelines, or observability frameworks.

• Prior contributions to AI/ML product teams or open-source
cloud frameworks.

We offer you a competitive total rewards package, continuing
education & training, and tremendous potential with a
growing worldwide organization.
