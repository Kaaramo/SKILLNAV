## Description complète

For more than 40 years, Accelya has been the industry’s
partner for change, simplifying airline financial and
commercial processes and empowering the air transport
community to take better control of the future. Whether
partnering with IATA on industry-wide initiatives or
enabling digital transformation to simplify airline
processes, Accelya drives the airline industry forward and
proudly puts control back in the hands of airlines so they
can move further, faster.

Role Context

As a Senior AI Engineer – Applied AI, you will lead the
design and implementation of AI-powered capabilities across
FLX ONE Cargo (F1C), focusing on embedding AI into core
platform workflows and engineering systems.

This role is centred on turning AI capabilities into
production-grade systems at scale. You will work on
integrating LLMs, retrieval systems, and AI-assisted
workflows into backend services and developer tooling.

You will operate as a senior individual contributor, driving
technical direction for AI integrations, shaping
implementation patterns, and ensuring solutions are
scalable, reliable, and aligned with platform architecture.

Hands-On AI Engineering

● Design and build scalable AI-enabled services integrated
into core Cargo platform workflows.

● Lead implementation of AI-assisted automation across
engineering and operational use cases.

● Drive production readiness of AI systems, balancing
experimentation with reliability.

Model Integration & Enablement

● Integrate language models or ML components into backend
services via clean APIs.

● Apply techniques such as prompt engineering, retrieval-
augmented generation (RAG), and lightweight orchestration
where appropriate.

● Ensure AI components are modular, observable, and safe to
operate in production.

Platform & Developer Enablement

● Work with engineers to adopt AI-assisted tooling that
improves development speed, testing, or debugging.

● Collaborate with architects to ensure AI integrations
align with platform standards and security expectations.

● Support gradual adoption of AI capabilities without
disrupting core delivery.

Operational Readiness

● Contribute to monitoring, evaluation, and continuous
improvement of AI-enabled features.

● Help define basic guardrails around performance, cost, and
reliability of AI components.

● Participate in production support and incident resolution
for AI-related services.

What You Must Bring

● 5+ years of professional software engineering experience,
with strong hands-on experience building or integrating
AI/ML systems into production environments.

● Strong proficiency in Python, with hands-on experience
working with LLMs, RAG pipelines, or AI service integration.

● Solid understanding of APIs, cloud services, and
distributed systems.

● Practical mindset with a focus on building usable,
reliable solutions.

Nice to Have

● Experience with LLM platforms or frameworks (e.g. AWS
Bedrock, OpenAI APIs, LangChain).

● Familiarity with retrieval-augmented generation (RAG) or
vector search concepts.

● Exposure to MLOps practices such as monitoring,
versioning, or deployment pipelines.

● Experience in airline, logistics, or other data- and
integration-heavy domains.

Why This Role Matters

AI Engineers help FLX ONE Cargo move faster by turning AI
ideas into working software. This role ensures AI is applied
where it adds real value—improving productivity,
reliability, and decision-making—without compromising
platform stability.

Hands-on experience with LLMs, RAG, or AI service
integration is required.

Profiles focused only on research or experimentation without
production experience will not be considered.
