## Description complète

About this role

Aladdin is BlackRock’s unified technology and data platform
used by BlackRock and many of the world’s largest Asset
Managers and Investors. The Aladdin Platform Engineering
organization is the Engineering Pillar within Aladdin
responsible for building and operating the technology
platform that powers Aladdin and includes the Developer
Platform, the Core Platform and the Control Platform. Within
Aladdin Platform Engineering, we are looking to bring on a
senior technology leader to be the Head of AI Developer
Platform.

Responsibilities

1. Own AI Native SDLC & Engineering Productivity

- Define and operationalize an AIfirst, agent driven SDLC
  embedded into the Developer Platform, spanning design,
  build, test, release, and operate phases, including:
  - Agent assisted design, code generation, refactoring, and
    documentation
  - Agent driven test generation, coverage analysis, defect
    intelligence, and built-in quality and resilience
    signals
  - Intelligent release validation, change risk assessment, and
    deployment readiness
  - Continuous functional, integration, regression, performance,
    and resilience testing orchestrated as first-class SDLC
    stages rather than post hoc controls
  - Continuous learning loops that improve software, delivery
    workflows, and AI agents over time
- AI Vendor, Tooling & Platform Integration
  - Drive evaluation, selection, and governance of AI developer
    tools and vendors integrated into the AI Developer
    Platform, ensuring capabilities are secure, production
    grade, and aligned to Aladdin’s architecture, risk
    posture, and engineering standards.
  - Ensure AI tools are platform integrated rather than point
    solutions, delivered through standardized onboarding,
    quality gates, observability, lifecycle management, and
    Responsible AI controls in partnership with Risk,
    Security, Legal, and Architecture.
- Lead adoption of PDLC agents delivered through the Developer
  Platform, aligned to core engineering personas and
  architecture/design principles:
  - Developers:
  - Testers / QE:
  - Operators / SREs:
  - Ensure quality, resilience, and risk prevention are embedded
    as default behaviors within PDLC agents, rather than
    treated as separate processes or late-stage controls.

2. Own the evolution of continuous validation capabilities
across the SDLC, including:

- Performance, load, spike, and concurrency testing integrated
  into CI/CD pipelines
- Chaos engineering and fault injection experiments executed
  in preproduction environments
- Game Day testing and operational readiness simulations to
  validate incident response, observability, and recovery
  paths
- Establish clear skill expectations and enablement paths for
  developers, testers, and operators working in an AI
  augmented delivery model,



3. AI Developer Platform Context Engineering & Enablement

- Own the workforce enablement and distribution layer for the
  Developer Platform (through IDEs), ensuring AI
  capabilities and PDLC agents are delivered as secure,
  reusable, composable platform services within Engineering
  Services rather than fragmented tools.
- Lead context engineering and context graph strategy for the
  Developer Platform , including:
  - Defining and evolving enterprise context graphs
  - Ensuring AI agents and PDLC agents have governed access to
    the right context at the right time, with controlled
    blast radius
- Driving reuse of context primitives across SDLC, agent-based
  validation, and operational workflows

4. Own platform level capabilities for test data and
environment management, including:

- Production aligned preproduction environments that enable
  reliable end-to-end validation
- Repeatable, governed test data strategies (refresh,
  reset/replay, synthetic data) that support functional,
  performance, and resilience testing
- Pre-Prod Environment stability, drift detection, and
  telemetry-based health signals as inputs to release
  decisions

- Be a Champion and Lead governance for context ownership,
  freshness, versioning, and reuse to prevent drift and
  repeated failures at scale.

5. Engineering Excellence, Culture & Leadership

- Build and lead a high-performance, globally distributed AI
  Developer Platform organization within Engineering
  Services.
- Evangelize AI native, agent driven delivery practices across
  Aladdin while balancing innovation, operational stability,
  and risk discipline.

7. Governance, Risk & Responsible AI

- Own policy, governance, and control frameworks for AI usage
  and PDLC agents within the Developer Platform, ensuring
  alignment with firmwide risk, compliance, and Responsible
  AI standards.
- Own and mitigate technology and AI related risks for the
  Developer Platform, representing Engineering Services in
  risk and control governance forums.

8. Collaboration & Enterprise Impact

- Work closely with business leaders, product managers, domain
  architects, and delivery teams to translate business
  requirements into scalable, secure AI Developer Platform
  and PDLC agent capabilities.
- Collaborate with Platform Engineering, Product Engineering,
  Product leadership to ensure the Developer Platform meets
  the growth demands of Aladdin’s clients and partners.
