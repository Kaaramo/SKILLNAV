## Description complète

The Moveworks AI Agent Developer is responsible for
configuring, enhancing, and supporting AI-driven HR
solutions to enable various Human Resources–centric
processes. Within the HR Technology team, the AI Agent
Developer will partner with the ServiceNow HR Technology
Leader and Digital Content/AI Product Manager to support
requirements gathering, configure Moveworks, assist with
integrations to other HR applications, and deliver solutions
aligned with JCI standards.

This role will primarily focus on hands-on configuration and
development of enhancements, supporting integrations with
other applications (ServiceNow and Workday), and
troubleshooting issues related to the Artificial
Intelligence (AI) Assistant (Moveworks). This position is a
hybrid role based in Pune, India, combining in-office and
remote work, and collaborates closely with global HR
technology teams.

How you will do it

• Hands-on configuration and enhancement of the Moveworks AI
Assistant / Chatbot • Partner with ServiceNow and Workday
technical teams to support and maintain integrations •
Analyze chatbot interaction data and assist in updating AI
Assistant skills • Configure Moveworks paths and
conversations based on defined requirements • Enable and
support Moveworks plugins and platform features • Partner
with Moveworks support on technical issues and platform-
related questions • Support enhancement delivery from
concept through deployment, Including Coordination of
testing as necessary. • Stay informed about Moveworks
updates, new features, and best practices • Document
technical configurations, system changes, and enhancement
activities • Provide technical support to HR stakeholders
and assist with end-user guidance as needed • Assist with
unit testing and participate in system and integration
testing to ensure solution stability

What you will need

Required

• Bachelor’s degree or equivalent practical experience • 5
years of relevant technical experience • Experience
supporting or configuring enterprise SaaS platforms • Basic
experience working with integrations and APIs • Experience
supporting AI Assistants, chatbots, or similar automation
platforms • Strong analytical and problem-solving skills •
Basic understanding of HR processes and workflows (e.g.,
onboarding, offboarding, employee self-service) • Ability to
work collaboratively with global teams in a structured
environment • Willingness to work in a hybrid model based in
Pune, India

Preferred

• Experience configuring chatbot conversations, skills, or
workflows (Moveworks or similar platforms) • Exposure to
ServiceNow and/or Workday integrations • Ability to manage
multiple tasks and follow defined development standards •
Strong documentation skills and attention to detail •
Interest in building deeper expertise in HR technology and
AI-driven solutions
