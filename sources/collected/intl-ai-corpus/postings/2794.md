About the Role

We are building a Hybrid Agentic AI Platform that operates
across AWS, multi-cloud, and customer environments. This
role will design and implement the core intelligence layer,
including LLM integration, agent orchestration, and context-
aware decisioning.

Responsibilities

- Design and build agentic workflows and orchestration systems
- Implement RAG pipelines (retrieval, embeddings, context
  building)
- Integrate LLMs (hosted/private) into enterprise workflows
- Build QA scoring, evaluation, and feedback loops
- Optimize prompts, latency, and response quality
- Ensure secure data handling (no-retention, in-memory
  processing)
- Collaborate with platform and FDE teams for real-world
  deployments

Required Skills

- Strong Python programming
- Hands-on experience with LLMs / GenAI systems
- Deep understanding of RAG, embeddings, vector databases
- Experience with APIs and distributed systems
- Familiarity with AWS (Bedrock, Lambda, S3, etc.)

Nice to Have

- Experience with agent frameworks (LangChain, LangGraph,
  etc.)
- LLM evaluation frameworks and guardrails
- Exposure to multi-tenant SaaS architectures
