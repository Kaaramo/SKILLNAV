## Description complète

✈️ About AirNxt

AirNxt is building the next generation of AI-powered MRO
software to transform aviation maintenance, operations, and
management. Our platform is designed to be intuitive,
scalable, and high-performance, simplifying complex
workflows and enhancing efficiency across the aviation
industry.

As we scale, we’re looking for passionate professionals who
thrive in agile environments and are eager to solve real-
world aviation challenges using cutting-edge technology and
innovative solutions. If that sounds like you, we’d love to
hear from you!

🎯 The Role

This is not a research role, it’s a build-and-ship role. You
will lead the design and implementation of AirNxt’s AI
capabilities, from document understanding to agentic
workflows and early fault prediction.

You’ll partner closely with our CEO and Data Architect to
define what “intelligent aviation software” actually means
in practice.

You will not be buried in infra or tooling. This role is
about shipping AI systems that work in production, in the
real world.

🧠 What You'll Build:

- Design and deploy AI workflows that turn raw maintenance
  documents into structured, validated data
- Agent-based automation workflows using LangGraph (or
  equivalent), orchestrating validation, transformation, and
  human-in-loop approval
- Develop lightweight predictive models based on time-series
  or log patterns that flag potential issues before they
  become unscheduled events
- Implement NLP + LLM chains that extract meaning from
  technical and regulatory documents, pilot logs, work
  orders, and assist in summarizing or generating checklists
- Build with clear traceability, fallbacks, and an execution
  path that earns trust from ops teams and regulators

⚙️ What You Bring

You’ve likely done some or all of the following:

- 4–8 years of experience building and shipping real-world
  AI/ML systems (not just prototypes or research)
- Strong Python developer with ability to own services end-to-
  end
- Hands-on with LLM workflows, prompt engineering, and
  chaining frameworks (LangGraph, LangChain, Haystack, or
  similar)
- Integrated LLMs, embeddings, and vector search into real-
  world workflows
- Designed custom NLP chains for document understanding, with
  schema enforcement and confidence scoring
- Working knowledge of time-series or anomaly detection (e.g.,
  Prophet, LSTM, transformers)
- High ownership mindset—you debug in production and think in
  first principles
- Clear communicator who thrives in lean, fast-paced teams
  without layers of abstraction

✅ Bonus if you’ve touched:

- Aviation, logistics, or other physical/regulated systems
- Graph data (Neo4j, property graphs, entity relationships)
- Safety reporting systems or compliance-heavy workflows
- Smart-contracts or audit-proof data systems

🧭 What Success Looks Like in 6 Months

- You’ve built an intelligent pipeline that turns raw PDFs
  into structured, validated records
- Agents are live, automating complex data flows with human
  oversight where needed
- A predictive model flags early risk conditions based on
  real-world log and usage data
- You’re the technical owner of our AI system, and a respected
  voice in the team
- We're not guessing, your systems are improving speed,
  quality, and safety on the ground

🎁 What We Offer

- Competitive salary, Health insurance
- The chance to own a core product stream from the start, and
  grow into Head of AI if that’s your path
- Early access to problems that are hard, real, and high-
  impact
- A team that moves fast, thinks clearly, and backs each other
- A mission that matters: keep aircraft safer, smarter, and
  better maintained

🚫 What We Don’t Offer

- Endless sprints to nowhere
- AI hype without grounding
- Legacy drag, middle-management layers, or AI theater
- Burnout-as-a-badge or chaos as culture

If you’re a builder who codes, simplifies, and cares about
execution, this is yours.

We’re not looking for a perfect resume. We’re looking for
the person who can build the system.

If that’s you, let’s talk.
