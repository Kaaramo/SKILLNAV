## Description complète

About Skiffra

Skiffra is building the intelligence layer for the physical
world. We translate complex, real-world environments into
clear, actionable data. While most AI companies focus on
digital industries, we design AI native orchestration
systems for the sectors that extract, move, and build
ecosystems around us. We turn messy, high stakes operations
into intelligent, adaptive workflows that improve decisions
in real time. Our first proving ground is mining and natural
resources, but the platform is modular by design and built
to scale into any industry where decisions are expensive and
the consequences are real.

The Role

As a Founding Full-Stack Engineer, you will build across
backend, frontend, and agentic layers to turn messy
operational reality into fast, AI-native systems. This is a
hands-on founding role with real users, real environments,
and real consequences.

What You’ll Do

- Build end-to-end product features across backend, frontend,
  and agentic layers
- Integrate LLMs and design safe, pragmatic tool interfaces
- Prototype quickly and iterate based on live operational
  feedback
- Debug across the entire stack and remove bottlenecks
- Collaborate closely with product, domain experts, and early
  customers
- Influence architecture, engineering standards, and the early
  roadmap
- Help shape hiring and engineering culture from the ground up

What We’re Looking For

- 7+ years of relevant engineering experience
- Strong fluency in Python or TypeScript
- Proven ability to ship full-stack features end-to-end
- Experience integrating LLM APIs or building AI-augmented
  features
- Strong debugging skills across backend and frontend
- Clear communicator with a bias toward action and simplicity

Nice to Have

- Experience with agentic workflows or tool-use patterns
- Familiarity with React, Node, FastAPI, Next.js, or similar
  frameworks
- Exposure to workflow engines or event-driven systems
- Experience in operations-heavy domains
- Prior zero-to-one or startup experience

How We Work

We value speed, clarity, and technical rigor. We favor
simple systems over clever ones, prototypes over over-
planning, and ownership over handoffs. You’ll work directly
with founders and ship things that matter quickly.

Location & Work Model

This is a hybrid role with regular in-person collaboration
in Los Angeles or the San Francisco Bay Area. We are not a
remote-only company.

Work Authorization

Candidates must be authorized to work in the United States.
We are not able to offer visa sponsorship for this role.
