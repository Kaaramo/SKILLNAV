### Raisonnement classification (LLM upstream)

This role is primarily a data engineering position that
builds data pipelines and platforms to support downstream
AI/ML consumption. While it involves leveraging Snowflake
Cortex AI for embeddings/classification/summarization and
integrating AWS Bedrock, the core focus is designing and
maintaining data infrastructure rather than building,
training, or fine-tuning AI models directly. The role
enables AI work rather than owning AI system development.
