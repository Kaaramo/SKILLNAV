This is an AI Platform Engineer role focused on building
infrastructure and platforms to enable AI/ML work rather
than directly developing, training, or deploying models. The
role emphasizes platform development, infrastructure
optimization, and cross-functional integration - all
enabling capabilities for others to work with AI systems.

## Description complète

Key Responsibilities:

- Platform Development: Design, develop, and maintain a
  scalable AI platform for integrating, training, and
  deploying ML models.
- Infrastructure Optimization: Manage and optimize cloud
  infrastructure (e.g., AWS, GCP, Azure) to balance high-
  performance AI workload requirements with cost-
  effectiveness.
- Cross-Functional Integration: Collaborate across teams to
  integrate AI capabilities with blockchain systems,
  ensuring data security and adherence to decentralization
  principles. Contribute to DApp and smart contract
  development as needed.
- Security & Documentation: Implement robust data security and
  privacy measures for the platform. Create and maintain
  comprehensive technical documentation to foster knowledge
  sharing and support platform scalability.
- Innovation: Stay current with emerging trends in AI,
  blockchain, and cloud technologies to drive continuous
  innovation.

Required Qualifications:

- Bachelor's degree or higher in Computer Science or a related
  field.
- Proven experience in building and deploying large-scale
  AI/ML platforms.
- Strong proficiency in Python and familiarity with AI/ML
  frameworks (e.g., TensorFlow, PyTorch).
- Hands-on experience with major cloud platforms (AWS, GCP, or
  Azure).
- Solid understanding of distributed systems and cloud-native
  technologies.
- Excellent problem-solving, attention to detail, and
  communication skills.

Preferred Qualifications (Bonus Points):

- Familiarity with blockchain technology and smart contract
  development (e.g., Solidity).
- Practical experience with blockchain security best
  practices.
- Proficiency in DevOps tools and CI/CD pipelines.
- A history of contributing to open-source projects.

What we offer

  good
- Growth: A self-directed environment where you can take
  initiative to shape your role and career
- Compensation: Market-competitive compensation including, for
  most roles, exposure to pre-launch tokens

In addition, 0G Labs is committed to the health and well
