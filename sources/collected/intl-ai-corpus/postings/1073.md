Job Details

The Senior AI Engineer advances InvoiceCloud’s generative AI
strategy by designing, developing, and deploying large
language model (LLM) and AI-powered capabilities that
enhance platform intelligence and customer experience. This
role operates at the intersection of research, engineering,
and product impact, owning the full lifecycle of AI
solutions from rapid prototyping through scalable production
deployment.



As a senior technical contributor, the role partners closely
with Product, Data Science, Data Engineering, and Platform
teams to translate business goals into reliable, secure, and
measurable AI implementations. The Senior AI Engineer
defines reusable frameworks and standards, improves
developer velocity through automation and observability, and
applies applied experimentation to drive meaningful
outcomes.



Success Profile

At InvoiceCloud, success is anchored in our core
competencies. These competencies guide how every employee
delivers impact across their role





Takes Ownership

- Owns end-to-end development and delivery of AI, ML, and LLM-
  powered solutions from design through production.
- Acts as a technical authority for AI architecture,
  frameworks, and model lifecycle management.
- Ensures data governance, security, compliance, and
  operational stability across AI deployments.
- Proactively identifies blockers, defines mitigation
  approaches, and drives disciplined execution.
- Maintains accountability for reliability, scalability, and
  long-term maintainability of AI systems.



Drives Efficiency

- Improves platform intelligence, latency, scalability, and
  operational performance through optimization and
  observability.
- Defines and applies technical KPIs for accuracy, inference
  performance, and system health.
- Builds reusable components, services, and standards that
  accelerate AI development and reduce duplication.
- Implements automation through MLOps pipelines, monitoring,
  and evaluation workflows.
- Uses data-driven feedback loops to continuously improve
  model behavior and system efficiency.



Innovative

- Evaluates and prototypes emerging AI technologies including

- Uses GenAI tools such as Azure OpenAI, LangChain, and
  Semantic Kernel to accelerate experimentation and
  delivery.
- Leads applied experimentation to validate new approaches and

- Advances self-learning, monitoring, and automation
  strategies that strengthen AI delivery at scale.
- Applies creative problem-solving to complex AI challenges

Customer Centric

- Designs AI-powered capabilities that enhance payer

- Collaborates with Product and Client-facing teams to ensure

Requirements

- 9+ years of software engineering experience, including 3+
  years building AI, ML, or LLM-powered applications
- Proven success delivering scalable, production-grade AI

- Experience designing, deploying, and operating AI pipelines

- Strong analytical, communication, and collaboration skills
- Experience mentoring engineers or influencing technical
  design and standards
