## Description complète

Axiad is an identity security company tackling the critical
threats posed by compromised credentials, which account for
over 70% of enterprise breaches.  As human and non-human
identities multiply across disparate systems, traditional
IAM tools fall short, leaving organizations with fragmented
visibility and significant security gaps.

Axiad bridges this divide by uncovering hidden identity
risks and credential vulnerabilities, providing actionable
insights to strengthen security—without requiring a complete
system overhaul.  Our solutions integrate seamlessly with
existing IAM infrastructures, enabling organizations to
shrink their attack surface and adopt phishing-resistant,
strong authentication methods.

At Axiad, we make identity security simple, effective, and
real for a passwordless, more secure world.  Discover more
at axiad.com or follow us on LinkedIn.

Location: India, Hybrid Job Type: Full-time

Axiad is seeking a skilled AI-First Golang Developer with
6-8 years of hands-on software development experience,
specializing in Golang and PostgreSQL. The ideal candidate
will have a strong AI-First mindset, leveraging AI-assisted
development tools and methodologies to enhance productivity
and code quality while building scalable, resilient
microservices solutions.

This role requires deep expertise in Go programming and
database design along with practical experience in
microservices architecture. The developer will actively
participate in AI-driven coding, testing, and deployment
processes to accelerate software delivery and innovation.

Role Responsibilities

- Design, develop, and maintain microservices using Golang and
  PostgreSQL in an AI-augmented development environment.
- Embrace and advocate AI-First development workflows by
  utilizing AI coding tools like Claude Code, Cursor or
  Windsurf, automated code generation, and AI-based code
  reviews.
- Collaborate with cross-functional teams to translate complex
  business requirements into scalable microservices
  solutions.
- Develop RESTful APIs and event-driven services with robust
  security, scalability, and performance.
- Implement database schemas, optimization strategies, and
  query tuning in PostgreSQL to ensure data integrity and
  efficiency.
- Participate in continuous integration and deployment (CI/CD)
  pipelines integrating AI-tools for automation.
- Troubleshoot and resolve production issues related to
  services and databases, leveraging AI-powered debugging
  tools.
- Contribute to architectural reviews, design discussions, and
  prototype development with a focus on AI-powered
  innovation.
- Mentor junior developers to adopt AI-first methodologies and
  best practices in Golang microservices development.

Skills and requirements

- 6-8 years of professional experience in software development
  with deep expertise in Golang.
- Strong knowledge of PostgreSQL database design, performance
  tuning, and querying.
- Proven experience designing and working with microservices
  architectures.
- Demonstrable adoption of AI-First development mindset and
  tools (Claude Code, Cursor or Windsurf). Knowledge of one
  of the AI development tools is a must
- Expertise in RESTful APIs, message queues, and event-driven
  systems.
- Experience with containerization (Docker) and orchestration
  platforms like Kubernetes.
- Proficiency in Git, CI/CD tools, and automated deployment
  practices.
- Strong problem-solving skills and collaborative mindset.
- Excellent communication skills within Agile/Scrum teams.
- Experience with cloud platforms like AWS or Azure.
- Knowledge of advanced microservices patterns, such as CQRS,
  event sourcing, and circuit breakers.

Preferred Qualifications

- Exposure to observability tools (Prometheus, Grafana, ELK
  stack).
- Understanding of DevOps culture and infrastructure as code
  tools (Terraform, CloudFormation).
- Prior experience in identity and access management (IAM)
  systems is a plus.
