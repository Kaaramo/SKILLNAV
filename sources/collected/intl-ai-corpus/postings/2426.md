## Description complète

About the company

Hi, we're Ondo Finance. Our mission is to provide
institutional-grade, blockchain-enabled investment products
and services. We have both a technology arm that develops
decentralized finance technology, and an asset management
arm that creates and manages tokenized funds. We were the
first company to tokenize exposure to US Treasuries, and
have since expanded into several other assets. We are also
focused on incubating protocols that can support both
tokenized real-world assets and traditional crypto.

Founded by folks from Goldman Sachs Digital Assets Team,
we’re backed by some of the best investors in the world
including Founders Fund, Coinbase Ventures, Pantera Capital,
Tiger Global, and more. We are currently the leaders in the
space in terms of AUM and are well capitalized to continue
growing the firm. We're fully remote, with team members
across the U.S.

About the role

We’re looking for a senior, hands-on Engineer who will be
the primary builder and architect of our internal AI copilot
platform. This platform will be used daily by every team to
ship faster, automate workflows, and make better decisions.
In this role, you will be a is a force-multiplier IC today
with a clear path to owning an internal AI platform/team as
we scale.

This is not a research-focused role. We're looking for a
pragmatic engineer with strong product judgment, capable of
designing and shipping internal tooling systems from
prototype to production, iterating quickly based on real
user needs.

Responsibilities

- Architect and build internal AI-powered systems that connect
  company data, tools, and workflows
- Own solutions end-to-end — from backend services and data
  pipelines to lightweight user interfaces
- Apply modern AI capabilities (LLMs, retrieval,
  orchestration) to real operational problems across teams
- Rapidly prototype and iterate based on real user feedback,
  balancing speed with sound system design
- Collaborate cross-functionally to identify high-leverage
  automation opportunities
- Help define and evolve Ondo’s internal AI roadmap as usage
  scales
- In the first 6 months, you might:
  - Ship an internal ‘Ondo Copilot’ that answers cross-
    functional questions and routes workflows
  - Automate operational workflows end-to-end between Slack,
    Notion, and internal services
  - Build a small RAG platform that other engineers can plug
    into

Requirements

- 6–12+ years of experience as a backend or full-stack
  software engineer, operating at a Senior or Staff IC level
- Strong systems design experience building scalable services,
  data pipelines, and integrations across multiple internal
  tools
- Experience shipping production-grade software end-to-end,
  from architecture through deployment and monitoring
- Demonstrated ability to apply modern AI tools (e.g., LLM
  APIs, retrieval systems, automation frameworks) to real-
  world workflows — without overengineering
- Strong product judgment and ability to independently
  identify high-leverage problems and deliver pragmatic
  solutions
- Comfortable operating as a founding IC with high autonomy
  and minimal oversight

Nice to haves

- Experience building internal platforms, developer
  productivity tools, or business enablement systems
- Familiarity with AI experimentation and emerging tooling
  (e.g., Claude, OpenAI, Copilot, agent frameworks)
- Experience integrating enterprise systems (Slack, Notion,
  CRM, finance tools, data warehouses)
- Background in regulated or security-conscious environments
  (fintech, enterprise SaaS, etc.)
- Prior experience scaling an internal tool from prototype to
  company-wide adoption

What we offer

• Competitive compensation including salary, future token
rights, and/or equity (according to your preferences) —
we're well-funded and believe that great talent deserves
great compensation

• Full benefits (medical, vision, and dental) and flexible
vacation policy (PTO)

• Small remote-first team across many countries — you'll be
an early team member helping shape our vision, culture, and
design practices

• A+ colleagues — our team includes alumni from: Goldman
Sachs, Blackrock, Two Sigma, Bridgewater, SpaceX, AWS, Meta,
Google, Pinterest, McKinsey, Circle, Uniswap, Phantom

• Best-in-class investors — we are proud to be backed by
leading crypto experts and VCs, including Pantera Capital,
Founders Fund and Coinbase Ventures
