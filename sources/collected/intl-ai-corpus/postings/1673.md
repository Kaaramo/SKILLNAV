As part of the Thermo Fisher Scientific team, you’ll

world healthier, cleaner and safer. We provide our global

challenges, like protecting the environment, making sure our
food is safe or helping find cures for cancer. DESCRIPTION:
Join our innovative team as a Senior Developer and make a
meaningful impact on global science and healthcare. In this
role, you'll design and develop sophisticated AI &
Automation solutions that contribute to improving health and
environmental outcomes. You'll work with cutting-edge
technologies across cloud platforms, data integration,
AI/ML, and enterprise systems to deliver robust applications
that power our essential operations. Working in an agile
environment, you will operate as a hands-on software
engineer within the intelligent automation and AI ecosystem,
responsible for designing, developing, and scaling
enterprise-grade agentic AI and automation solutions. This
role blends Python-based AI development, agentic
orchestration, and RPA technologies (UiPath) with Microsoft
Power Platform, focusing on robust engineering practices
rather than low-code configuration alone.

You will design agentic systems that combine LLM-powered
reasoning, autonomous decision-making, and Human-in-the-Loop
(HITL) controls to deliver reliable, explainable, and
compliant automation across complex business processes. The
role requires deep coding expertise, system design thinking,
reusable frameworks, and integration with enterprise
platforms, data sources, and AI services.
