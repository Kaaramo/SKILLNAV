## Description complète

Role Title: AI Developer

Location: Bangalore

Working Hours: 1:30 pm to 10:30 pm IST

Years of Experience: 3years

Role Purpose: We are looking for an AI Developer with a
strong delivery mindset; someone who combines technical
depth with practical execution, takes ownership of outcomes,
and thrives in an environment that values innovation,
accountability, and continuous improvement.

As an AI Developer, you’ll play a crucial role in shaping
and delivering our AI-driven strategies, bringing innovation
to our business processes. This position is expected to get
involved in the end-to-end lifecycle of AI project, right
from problem framing to deployment and maintenance in
production. We’re looking for someone with a positive,
entrepreneurial mindset who can collaborate effectively with
cross-functional teams to build and maintain AI capabilities
into our existing business functionalities.

AI Development:

• Contribute to the ongoing maintenance and enhancement of
our Generative AI models and implementations.

• Identify opportunities to use AI to enhance our internal
business processes.

• Stay up to date with advancements in AI and machine
learning techniques to drive innovation.

• Educate and foster culture on using AI to the colleagues.

• Continuously identify opportunities to innovate,
streamline workflows, and improve business outcomes using
AI-driven solutions.

• Apply emerging AI techniques pragmatically, balancing
experimentation with production readiness.

Python Coding:

• Write efficient, maintainable code for our AI products.

• Write clean, well-documented, and testable code,
contributing to long-term platform stability and
scalability.

• Proactively identify technical debt and participate in
refactoring and quality improvement initiatives.

Platform Delivery:

• Collaborate with Infrastructure team to maintain our AI
products deployed in Cloud environment.

• Ensure that our AI capabilities meet our security and
legal standard.

Key Accountabilities:

• Develop, maintain and enhance our current Generative AI
products and tools deployed in our cloud infrastructure.

• Monitor and evaluate our AI product in production and
proactively identify and troubleshoot issues and driving
continuous improvement.

• Collaborate with cross-functional teams to understand
business objectives and translate them into new AI tools and
features.

• Build Generative AI or predictive models, based workflow
for various business applications (e.g., Research Pricing,
recommendation systems).

• Ensure that our AI solution development and deployment
comply with internal security, legal and governance
standard.

• Communicate findings and results to both technical and
non-technical stakeholders.

• Contribute to building an internal culture of effective
and responsible AI usage.

• Demonstrate ownership and accountability for delivering
high-quality outcomes aligned with product and business
objectives.

• Communicate progress, risks, and outcomes clearly,
ensuring transparency and alignment across teams.

Knowledge, Skills and Experience Required:

Essential:

• Minimum of 3 years of hands-on experience in AI/ML
development.

• Proficiency in Python with experience developing
production-grade AI or ML applications.

• Experience deploying and operating AI workloads in a cloud
environment, preferably AWS.

• Familiarity with Git-based workflows and CI/CD pipelines.

• Solid understanding of core AI/ML concepts, including
supervised and unsupervised learning.

Desirable:

• Familiarity with Generative AI technologies and frameworks
(e.g. OpenAI API, Bedrock)

• Familiarity with Agents and RAG.

• Familiarity with containerisation (e.g. Docker)

• Knowledge of deploying python-based API (e.g. FastAPI)

Familiarity with front-end UI for Generative-AI based
application (e.g. Typescript, streamlit)

Education and Knowledge
