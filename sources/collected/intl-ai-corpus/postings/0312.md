## Description complète

Are you passionate about building AI-driven software that
transforms how professionals work? Join a dynamic and highly
skilled team at Thomson Reuters, where we invest deeply in
AI technologies and explore emerging fields with the backing
of a global leader.

As a Senior Software Engineer – AI, you will play a vital
role in developing intelligent systems that power the next
generation of expert solutions across legal, tax, risk, and
compliance domains. In this role, you will be an embedded AI
expert on the Contract Express team, advancing our document
automation platform with cutting-edge AI capabilities.

About the Role  As a Senior Software Engineer – AI, you
will:

- Lead the technical execution of AI initiatives for our
  document automation platform, including architecture
  decisions, roadmap alignment, and delivery oversight.

- Design, build, and deploy scalable AI/ML solutions
  leveraging emerging technologies and best practices in
  MLOps and LLMOps.

- Optimize AI performance through advanced techniques,
  including domain-specific adaptation and fine-tuning of
  third-party models and algorithms.

- Integrate AI components within a modern software stack
  (.NET, Azure, Angular) to deliver robust, secure, and
  performant features inside Contract Express.

- Design and implement continuous monitoring of application
  and user behavior to generate product insights, inform
  prioritization, and improve AI-driven experiences.

- Collaborate closely with product managers, engineers,
  designers, and subject-matter experts (legal, tax, risk,
  compliance) to translate business needs into effective AI
  solutions.

- Stay abreast of AI developments (with a focus on LLMs,
  retrieval-augmented generation, and prompt engineering)
  and promote the learning and adoption of relevant
  technologies within the team.

- Act as a thought leader by contributing to internal forums,
  representing the team in areas of AI expertise, mentoring
  other engineers, and influencing the broader technical
  direction.

About You  You are a fit for the role if you have:

- Solid, hands-on experience building and deploying AI/ML
  solutions in production environments.

- Strong software engineering background, ideally with
  experience in:

- .NET (C#) for backend services and APIs

- Azure cloud services (e.g., App Services, Functions,
  Storage, AI-related services)

- Frontend development with Angular or similar frameworks

- Practical experience with MLOps / LLMOps practices (CI/CD
  for models, experiment tracking, monitoring, evaluation,
  and observability).

- Experience working with large language models (LLMs) and/or
  NLP techniques, including integration of third-party AI
  platforms and services.

- Proven ability to make sound architectural decisions and to
  balance innovation with maintainability, security, and
  performance.

- A collaborative mindset and strong communication skills,
  with the ability to partner effectively across disciplines
  and explain complex AI concepts to non-technical
  stakeholders.

- Curiosity and a continuous learning mindset, staying current
  with the rapidly evolving AI landscape and bringing new
  ideas back to the team.

If you're excited about shaping the future of AI-powered
document automation and want to work at the intersection of
advanced AI and mission-critical professional workflows,
we'd love to hear from you.
