Role Overview

The AI Engineer is a hands-on builder who develops and
deploys agentic AI solutions for AHEAD’s clients. You will
work on multi-agent workflows, retrieval pipelines, and
system integrations that automate real business processes
like document handling, SDLC tasks, service desk actions,
reporting, and decision support.

This role focuses on implementing and improving components
within larger agentic systems. You will collaborate closely
with senior engineers, solution managers, and client teams
to translate ideas into working automations. It is an ideal
role for someone eager to deepen their skills in applied AI,
workflow orchestration, and enterprise integration.

Key Responsibilities

- Agentic Solution Development
- Build and customize agents using frameworks such as
  LangChain, LangGraph, Autogen, CrewAI, or LlamaIndex.
- Implement components for document processing, workflow
  automation, data retrieval, and structured output
  generation.
- Develop and maintain vector DB pipelines (Pinecone,
  pgvector, Elasticsearch) and RAG-based retrieval
  workflows.
- Configure LLM/SLM endpoints, tool-calling functions, and
  agent memory/state handling.
- Workflow & Integration Engineering
- Implement real-time and batch workflows using Python, Kafka,
  EventBridge, Airflow, Celery, Snowflake, S3, or n8n.
- Build integrations with enterprise tools (SharePoint,
  Salesforce, ServiceNow, Jira, APIs).
- Support data transformations, validation, and versioning
  needed for downstream agent operations.
- Quality, Testing & Observability
- Write tests for agent behavior, pipelines, and model output
  consistency.
- Contribute to monitoring, logging, metrics, and guardrail
  configurations for agentic systems.
- Participate in debugging sessions and post-incident reviews
  with senior engineers.
- Client Delivery Support
- Participate in client workshops, demos, and working sessions
  as part of the engineering team.
- Translate requirements from senior engineers or solution
  managers into technical tasks and working components.
- Help document solution behavior, configurations, and
  deployment steps for clients.
- Security, Governance & Responsible AI
- Follow established patterns for access control, data
  handling, safe model usage, and auditing.
- Ensure compliance and guardrail configurations are
  consistently applied in implementations.
- Team Collaboration & Growth
- Contribute to internal documentation, code templates, and
  reusable building blocks.
- Learn and apply AHEAD’s reference architectures, best
  practices, and agentic patterns.
- Support onboarding of new engineers and collaborate in agile
  ceremonies.

Qualifications

- Ability to learn quickly in fast-moving, client-facing

- Familiarity with MLOps, containerization, or observability

- Exposure to cloud services (AWS, Azure, GCP).
