## Description complète

Sxaler is the new global standard in exceptional talent
acquisition for category-defining technology companies. We
partner with the brave and the bold during the periods which
matter most, and nothing less than exceptional talent is
required to win.

Our leaders are operators from unicorns for which they've
scaled. Our clients choose us for the quality of our people,
the wisdom of experience, and our record for rapid impact.

We believe that AI-natural recruiters who balance input,
intervention and empathy will redefine our profession. We
look for people with unreasonable standards for excellence —
high-agency operators who take ownership and win.

We're hiring Founding Engineers for an AI startup in stealth
mode, looking to disrupt a $100b+ market. The opportunity
includes the chance to become the VP Engineering/CTO for the
right person.

The Founders are highly ambitious and experience operators,
who have been part of multiple unicorn success stories
previously and deeply understand the problem space.
Sufficient private funding/runway to reach PMF and avoid
external capital/dilution.

What You’ll Do

- Work directly with the founders and functional experts to
  shape our early product offerings
- Build a scalable, AI-native platform with potentially
  millions of users
- Prototype and ship quickly, with direct input from early
  customers
- Translate real-world workflows into intuitive user
  experiences
- Help define the technical architecture and engineering
  culture from day one
- Contribute to product strategy, customer discovery, and
  everything in between

You Might Be a Fit If…

- You’re a full-stack Product Engineer (or close to it) who
  loves building fast and shipping
- You are tinkering constantly with AI, seeking to understand
  its utility and limitations
- You default to action, care deeply about product, and are
  comfortable in ambiguity
- You challenge assumptions and think from first principles
- You want to build cutting-edge products which meaningfully
  accelerate humans and challenge archaic norms
- You’ve worked in early-stage startups/scaleups and/or taken
  ideas from 0 → 1

Why Join Now?

- You’ll be among the first engineers
- You’ll have tremendous influence around engineering choices
  and culture
- You’ll work directly with thoughtful, scar-tissued operators
  and sharp engineers
- You’ll build products that shapes the future of AI
- Competitive base salary + meaningful early equity
