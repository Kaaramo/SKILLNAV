### Raisonnement classification (LLM upstream)

This role is titled "AI & Automation" but the actual work
involves integration development using Boomi and Workato
(iPaaS platforms). The developer builds integration
processes between third-party applications and supports
existing integrations. While it falls under the company's
"Digital Solutions" umbrella, there is no hands-on work with
AI/ML models, LLMs, or any AI systems — it's primarily an
integration/automation developer role that supports business
automation initiatives.
