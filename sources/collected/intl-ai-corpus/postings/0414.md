Job Description

The Software Engineer (Back End) position at Blueflame AI
plays a critical role in producing high-quality software
solutions to support the Blueflame AI platform. This
position requires close collaboration with our Client
Success and Product teams and other members of our
Engineering team to develop and implement superior software
components, and ensure the reliability and performance of
the platform. The ideal candidate will be organized,
energetic, and comfortable working in a fast-paced startup
environment.

This is a hybrid position in NYC with 3 days in office.

Responsibilities

- Development: Design and implement the server-side logic and
  functionality for the Blueflame AI platform, ensuring
  efficient data processing and management
- Database Management: Create, maintain, and optimize
  databases, ensuring data integrity and security
- API Development and Integration: Develop and maintain APIs
  to enable communication between the server and client-side
  applications, as well as with third-party services
- Security Implementation: Implement security measures to
  protect data and applications from unauthorized access and
  vulnerabilities, including encryption and authentication
  protocols
- Collaboration with Front End Engineers: Work closely with
  front end software engineers to ensure seamless
  integration of server-side logic with user interfaces
- Optimize Performance: Implement best practices for
  optimizing platform performance
- Troubleshoot: Identify and fix bugs in the code
- Documentation: Write and maintain documentation for code,
  processes, and best practices to facilitate knowledge
  sharing within the team
- Stay Updated with Trends: Keep abreast of the latest trends
  and technologies in back end development, including new
  frameworks, libraries, and tools

What You Bring

- Advanced knowledge of Python
- Experience building/working with RESTful APIs
- Experience with Mongo & Redis
- Interest in LLMs
- Familiarity of distributed systems and multi-threading
- Ability to problem solve and work collaboratively
- Minimum 3-7 years of industry and/or technical experience
- Bachelor’s or master’s degree in computer science or an
  equivalent engineering discipline
