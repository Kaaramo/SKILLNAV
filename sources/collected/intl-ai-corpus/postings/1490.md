## Description complète

Join us in bringing joy to customer experience.  Five9 is a
leading provider of cloud contact center software, bringing
the power of cloud innovation to customers worldwide.

Living our values everyday results in our team-first culture
and enables us to innovate, grow, and thrive while enjoying
the journey together. We celebrate diversity and foster an
inclusive environment, empowering our employees to be their
authentic selves.

The Team

The Product Engineering team drives the development and
realization of practical AI solutions for the contact‑centre
environment. Our existing Studio product stack is built on
PHP/Laravel, Python, and Vue.js, deployed across both
private and public clouds. We leverage leading cloud AI
services, including Azure OpenAI, Google AI APIs, IBM
Watson, and Amazon Lex.

Our vision is to reimagine the connection experience between
end users and the brands that serve them. Our AI agents will
deliver delightful, efficient self‑service at super‑human
speeds, while seamlessly integrating live human agents
whenever a human touch is required. These AI agents will
also assist live agents and help supervisors and managers
optimize the blend of human and AI workforce.

The Staff Software Engineer role is part of the Studio
Innovations team, which focuses on modernizing the existing
Studio product. This team ensures our production systems
remain stable, secure, and high‑performing, and is
responsible for enhancements that transition the Studio
platform into Java microservices on GCP.

This position is based out of an affiliate office of Acqueon
Technologies in India, following the hybrid work model of
that location.

Role Purpose

The Staff Software Engineer in the R&D AI Automation Team is
responsible for designing, developing, and maintaining the
Studio product — a drag‑and‑drop flow builder and execution
engine that integrates multiple channels such as voice, SMS,
and chatbots with the Five9 contact‑centre voice platform.

This role plays a key part in modernizing and
re‑architecting the Studio product into Java-based
microservices on GCP. Responsibilities include:

- Software design, development, testing, and documentation
  across backend, frontend, and interaction management
  platforms.
- Collaborating with product owners and designers to expand
  the feature set and deliver high‑quality user experiences.
- Taking operational ownership of service performance and
  quality, including possible participation in an on‑call
  rotation.
- Exploring cutting-edge technologies and partnering with
  product teams to convert ideas into production‑ready
  solutions.

How You Contribute

- Lead the design, development, and implementation of new
  features and change requests.
- Write high-quality, efficient, and maintainable code with a
  focus on scalability and multi‑tenancy, while adhering to
  coding best practices.
- Serve as the go‑to expert for ambiguous or time‑sensitive
  challenges.
- Contribute to production incident management and debugging
  of complex issues using monitoring, logging, and APM tools
  such as New Relic, Grafana, and Prometheus.
- Conduct thorough code reviews and maintain high coding
  standards.
- Identify technical debt and propose actionable improvements.
- Apply strong understanding of multiple programming
  languages, frameworks, and development methodologies to
  solve complex technical problems.
- Act as a natural coach and mentor, providing technical
  guidance and support to peers.
- Fill functional gaps across engineering and cross‑functional
  teams to maintain continuity and efficiency.
- Ensure project milestones are met and development aligns
  with business objectives.
- Contribute to documentation, specifications, processes, and
  APIs.

Skills, Competencies, and QualificationsRequired

- 8+ years of experience designing and developing
  high‑traffic, distributed, and scalable systems on cloud
  platforms such as GCP, AWS, or Azure within a SaaS
  environment.
- Hands-on expertise in cloud‑native backend microservices
  development using Java and frameworks like Spring Boot.
- High proficiency in multiple programming languages,
  including but not limited to Java, PHP, JavaScript, and
  Python.
- Experience with web technologies such as HTML, CSS,
  JavaScript, and modern front-end frameworks like Vue.js or
  React.
- Strong experience with observability and monitoring tools:
  New Relic, Grafana, Prometheus, and similar systems.
- Expertise in SQL and NoSQL databases, along with
  optimization techniques.
- Expertise in CI/CD, containerization, and Kubernetes.
- Solid understanding of web security best practices and the
  ability to implement application and data protection
  measures.
- Ability to deploy, monitor, and troubleshoot applications in
  production.
- A portfolio demonstrating experience in architecture,
  design, and full-stack development.
- Ability to work both independently and collaboratively.
- Strong commitment to on-time delivery of high-quality code
  and solutions.

Educational Qualifications

- Bachelor’s degree in Computer Science, Engineering, or a
  related field (Master’s preferred).

Desirable

- Experience with PHP.
- Experience working on multi-tenant systems.
- Expertise with sharding using MySQL, Redis, or MongoDB.
- Experience in Contact Centre, IVR, Virtual Agents, VoIP, or
  telecom service provider environments.

Other Requirements
