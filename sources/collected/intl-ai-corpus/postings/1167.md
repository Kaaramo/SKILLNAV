Notion helps you build beautiful tools for your life’s work.

About The Role:

We are seeking a Staff Software Engineer with 10+ years of
experience to work in the AI Connectors team. AI Connectors
power AI agentic search, enabling customers to find answers
across applications like Slack, Google Drive, Salesforce,
and GitHub through Notion AI connectors. You'll build the
end-to-end AI agentic search experience: Design and ship
connectors from scratch, keeping third-party data
continuously in sync. Build semantic search infrastructure
powered by vector embeddings and efficient storage systems.
Transform natural language questions into intelligent
queries across multiple data sources. Implement ranking
systems that surface the most relevant results while
respecting user permissions and access controls. Scale to
millions of documents across thousands of customers. You're
a builder who thrives in fast-paced environments with a
track record of shipping frequently: Expert in distributed
systems, data pipelines, vector databases, and production
infrastructure. You write clean code, take end-to-end
ownership, and make pragmatic architectural decisions under
ambiguity. You mentor teammates and deliver projects that
enable intelligent agentic search experiences at enterprise
scale.
