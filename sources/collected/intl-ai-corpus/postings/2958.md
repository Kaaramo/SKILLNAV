## Description complète

At DataSnipper, we’re transforming the way audit and finance
teams work and design plays a critical role in that mission.
As AI rapidly reshapes the design and development landscape,
we’re evolving with it.

We’re looking for a Design Engineer who thrives at the
intersection of design, frontend development and AI. Someone
who can empower teams, accelerate experimentation, and help
us build smarter, faster, and better products.

About the role

As a Design Engineer, you will be a key enabler for our UX
and product teams. Helping them ideate, prototype, and ship
high-quality experiences at speed. You’ll bridge the gap
between design and engineering, making it easier for teams
to turn ideas into reality.

You’ll also play a central role in shaping how we use AI in
our workflows, creating environments where teams can “vibe
code” their way through challenges and continuously gather
feedback.

Your work will directly contribute to freeing up more time
for meaningful user research and deeper product insights.

About DataSnipper

Audit and finance are still massively manual — and we’re
changing that. DataSnipper is a $1B, bootstrapped unicorn
with 600,000+ users across 180+ countries, already embedded
in the daily workflows of top audit and accounting firms.

Now, we’re taking things further with our Excel Agent —
bringing AI directly into where the work actually happens.
Unlike generic AI tools, we don’t sit on the sidelines. Our
AI operates inside Excel, with access to real documents and
audit evidence — meaning it doesn’t just generate answers,
it does the work, with full traceability.

We’re not just applying AI — we’re redefining how audit gets
done. If you want to build something category-defining at
scale, this is the place.

What you will do

- Build and foster an AI-enabled product and design team at
  DataSnipper
- Create and maintain AI design / vibe coding environments
  using tools like Figma MCP, Claude Code, and others you
  find valuable
- Help build our AI SDLC, specifically for the design steps
- Set up and manage repositories for AI-led design
- Own and evolve the frontend of our design system, ensuring
  scalability and consistency
- Connect the design system with tools so designers,
  engineers, and product managers can all actively use and
  experiment with components
- Establish strong collaboration channels between design,
  engineering, and product teams
- Work closely with designers, teaching them code while they
  generate user insights you can leverage in development

What you bring

- 3–5 years of experience in design engineering, frontend
  development, or a similar role
- Strong understanding of design systems and frontend
  architecture
- Experience working with Claude Code, Cursor, or similar for
  engineering and/or design work
- Interest or experience in AI-driven workflows and tools
- Experience working with modern design and prototyping tools
- Ability to bridge design and engineering and communicate
  effectively across teams
- A proactive mindset with a passion for enabling others and
  improving workflows
