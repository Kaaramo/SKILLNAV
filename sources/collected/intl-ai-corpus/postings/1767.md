## Description complète

StubHub is on a mission to redefine the live event
experience on a global scale. Whether someone is looking to
attend their first event or their hundredth, we’re here to
delight them all the way from the moment they start looking
for a ticket until they step through the gate. The same goes
for our sellers. From fans selling a single ticket to the
promoters of a worldwide stadium tour, we want StubHub to be
the safest, most convenient way to offer a ticket to the
millions of fans who browse our platform around the world.

StubHub is seeking a Software Engineer II to join a new
engineering team building AI-powered consumer experiences
that will shape the next evolution of our marketplace.

This team is focused on reimagining how fans discover,
evaluate, and purchase live events using AI-native
capabilities embedded directly into our platform. Rather
than layering AI onto existing workflows, we are building
intelligent systems and modern application architectures
that enable dynamic, adaptive, and highly personalized user
journeys.

This is a highly hands-on, fullstack role for engineers who
enjoy building new systems from the ground up, moving
quickly from prototype to production, and solving complex
technical problems at scale.

You’ll work on foundational AI-driven capabilities that
power real consumer experiences used by millions of fans
globally.

Location: Hybrid (3 days in office/2 days remote) – New
York, NY



About the team:

This team sits within our Consumer App Foundations
organization and operates at the intersection of AI,
platform engineering, and consumer product innovation.

We partner closely with product, design, data science, and
infrastructure teams to:

- Build intelligent consumer-facing systems
- Develop scalable services supporting AI-driven workflows
- Rapidly prototype and validate new interaction models
- Embed experimentation deeply into the product lifecycle
- Deliver production-ready AI experiences at marketplace scale

We operate with a startup mindset inside a global consumer
platform.

What You'll Do:
