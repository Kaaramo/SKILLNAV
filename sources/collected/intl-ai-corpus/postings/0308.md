## Description complète

Who We Are:

At Sixfold, we’re building the AI platform that transforms
how insurers evaluate and price risk. Not just another tool
that generates summaries or flags issues—we're creating AI
agents that actually understand risk the way veteran
underwriters do. Our platform is processing billions in
premium for some of the world's largest carriers, and we're
just getting started.

The technical challenges are wild. We're teaching AI to
understand that a bakery in Florida faces different risks
than one in Montana. To know when a manufacturing company's
pivot from toys to medical devices fundamentally changes
their risk profile. To make million-dollar decisions with
the same intuition as someone who's been underwriting for 20
years.

What makes Sixfold special isn't just the technology—it's
that we're building it with people who deeply understand
insurance. Our team includes folks who've built and scaled
carriers, researchers who've pushed the boundaries of AI,
and engineers who just love solving seemingly impossible
problems.

We're still early, but the impact is already real. If you
want to build AI that matters—that affects real businesses,
real people, and billions in economic activity—Sixfold is
where you should be. We're not just digitizing insurance.
We're reimagining what it can be.

Who You Are:

- A smart self-starter: You have a bias for action. You orient
  yourself around solutions and outcomes and don’t wait for
  others to tell you what to do. But you also understand how
  to build alignment and conviction for a decision that
  can’t be taken back easily.
- A force multiplier: You look for ways to magnify your impact
  and your team’s. When you find a productivity hack, you
  share it with your teammates and build a tool to make it
  easier. You document knowledge for the benefit of future
  teammates. And you lean into AI and automation to improve
  your productivity.
- An empathetic communicator: You communicate nuanced ideas
  clearly, whether you're explaining technical decisions in
  writing or brainstorming in real time. In disagreements,
  you engage thoughtfully with other perspectives and
  compromise when needed.
- A learner: You thrive on learning new things. You can name
  your favorite sources of what’s new in tech and AI, and
  you’re excited to tell us about your latest hack or
  discovery.

What You’ll Do:

As a software engineer at Sixfold, you will:

- Build and deliver quality software. You will write, test,
  and deploy code across our AI pipeline using Python and a
  variety of generative AI and other machine learning tools
  & techniques. You'll work with large language models,
  fine-tune prompts, design evaluation metrics, and
  implement responsible AI practices. You’ll work on team-
  level projects, applying engineering best practices and
  contributing to the team’s technical decision-making.
- Grow as a full-stack developer. You'll have opportunities to
  build features end-to-end by expanding your skills with
  Ruby on Rails, React and TypeScript. You'll collaborate
  with our product engineers to understand our tech stack
  and how AI capabilities integrate with our web app and
  APIs.
- Collaborate with cross-functional partners. Our teams
  include product managers, designers, and engineers working
  together to accomplish shared goals. You will collaborate
  with these partners, beginning with problem discovery
  through solution design and delivery. We work in two-week
  cycles and release to production at least once a week.
- Own your software through its entire lifecycle. In addition
  to shipping code, you will assess our most pressing
  product and engineering problems, identify and design
  solutions, write technical specs, and scope and break down
  projects. You will also spend approximately one week per
  quarter on call, serving as the first line of defense
  against service outages and critical bugs. If you don’t
  have experience with all of this yet, don’t worry - we’ll
  teach you!

Qualifications:

- You have 3+ years of professional software engineering
  experience.
- You are familiar with Python and FastAPI and know their
  patterns well enough to build features and APIs without
  significant learning time. Extra credit if you have
  experience with Pydantic, Notebooks, or Streamlit.
- You have experience building Retrieval-Augmented Generation
  (RAG) systems using vector databases (e.g., Weaviate,
  Pinecone, FAISS, or pgvector) and large language models
  (LLMs). You understand embedding generation, chunking
  strategies, and retrieval optimization to improve response
  accuracy and performance. Extra credit if you have
  experience fine-tuning or orchestrating LLM workflows.
- You want to work in an early-stage, fast-growth tech
  company. You are comfortable with ambiguity and change.
  You can own problems and proactively devise solutions, not
  just wait for somebody to feed you tasks to execute.
- You love communication, collaboration, and execution. You
  value meaningful commit messages and pull request
  descriptions and prefer to merge your PRs within a day or
  two. You appreciate healthy engineering debates but know
  how to make pragmatic decisions. You take feedback well
  and are candid with the feedback you give.
