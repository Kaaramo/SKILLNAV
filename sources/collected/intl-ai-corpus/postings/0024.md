## Description complète

Mutiny Overview

Mutiny is building a first-class agentic GTM platform that
unifies sales and marketing to help companies close
enterprise deals faster. Our customers are enterprise
marketing and sales teams who face a fundamental challenge:
what works isn’t scalable, and what’s scalable doesn’t work.
Mutiny is solving this by combining AI agents, creative
workflows, and deep integrations to deliver personalized
marketing and sales experiences at scale.

Our customers include some of the fastest-growing B2B
companies—Uber, Amplitude, Qualtrics, and LaunchDarkly. We
are backed by Sequoia Capital, Tiger Global, Insight
Partners, and Y Combinator.

About the Role

We’re looking for a Senior Software Engineer to join our
engineering team in New York. You’ll architect, build, and
own key functionality of our Campaign Builder and AI Agent
experiences—core surfaces that let marketers and sellers
generate, edit, and launch personalized campaigns across
accounts.

This role is central to how Mutiny brings our agentic vision
to life: helping enterprise teams go from idea to
personalized assets with speed, trust, and quality. You’ll
partner closely with PM and Design to define the end-to-end
experience—from conversational AI collaboration to
guardrails for brand consistency.

📍 This role is based in NYC and requires being in-person 5
days a week.

What you’ll do:

- Build all across the stack to deliver a world class AI agent
  that helps sales and marketing teams create breakthrough
  experiences.
- Work with top notch AI tooling from cloud based coding
  agents, agent networks
- Collaborate closely with design and product to build AI-
  powered features that drive real customer value.
- Build scalable and resilient systems on top of the
  inherently non-deterministic nature of LLMs.
- Help grow our engineering team to tackle a huge opportunity.
- Contribute to our culture of engineering excellence and work
  at the bleeding edge of AI development.

What you bring:

- 5+ years of experience in software engineering
- Proficiency in a backend language like Python, TypeScript,
  Kotlin, or Ruby.
- You have a “full-stack” mindset — you can be autonomous in
  implementing features e2e which helps you design features
  that deliver customer value.
- You communicate nuanced ideas clearly, whether you're
  explaining technical decisions in writing or brainstorming
  in real time.
- You love to collaborate with engineers across the spectrum
  of seniority and experiences.
- Someone who is energized by ambiguity and can create
  structure in a dynamic, fast-paced environment.
- Familiarity with LLM techniques (RAG, vector databases,
  LangChain, agent frameworks) is a plus.

What you’ll get out of it:

- You will create a name for yourself by getting to work with
  and support some of the fastest-growing companies in B2B
  SaaS.
- You will get exposure to real business problems every
  company faces (growth) that you can take with you to start
  your own company (or to help scale another).
- You will work directly with our founders and leaders to
  deliver product outcomes that have substantial impact on
  company trajectory.
- You will contribute to our in-person culture, being a
  critical voice in hiring and defining who we are.
- You will have fun, plain and simple. There is a reason our
  first company value is that work should feel like play.

Hiring process for this role:

The hiring process for this role looks like this:

- Hiring Manager Phone Screen (30 minutes): you’ll meet with
  our CTO to discuss your experience in engineering AI or
  data powered systems and how this role aligns with your
  career goals.
- Technical Screen (60 minutes): you'll meet with one of our
  engineers to work through a coding challenge to understand
  your technical ability.
- Virtual Onsite (two 90 minute technical interviews, one 60
  minute culture interview): you’ll meet with more members
  of the team to dive deeper into your experience and see if
  there’s potential for a great fit at Mutiny.
