Job Details

Why this job is important

Generative AI has moved quickly from experimentation to
expectation — but making it work well, safely, and at scale
is hard. In this role, you’ll help turn

GenAI into something teams across Kantar can rely on, not
just prototype with.

You’ll design and build the foundations that enable
production‑grade AI: reusable GenAI components, strong MLOps
practices, and cloud‑native services that balance quality,
cost, and reliability. Your work will help teams move
faster, avoid common pitfalls, and deliver AI solutions that
stand up in real‑world environments.

This is a hands‑on, individual‑contributor role. You won’t
be managing people — instead, you’ll lead through technical
expertise, curiosity, and a passion for solving complex
engineering problems.

What you’ll be doing

- Designing and building GenAI platform components that help
  teams create and deploy AI solutions safely and
  efficiently
- Implementing LLM‑based systems, including RAG, embeddings,
  semantic search, and agent‑based workflows
- Shaping how GenAI systems handle prompting, grounding,
  evaluation, reliability, and cost control
- Building and evolving MLOps pipelines, from experimentation
  through deployment, monitoring, and iteration
- Engineering cloud‑native, API‑driven services that integrate
  AI into production platforms
- Supporting and guiding other engineers through design
  reviews, coaching, and shared standards

The skills & experience we’re looking for

We don’t expect you to tick every box — if most of this
sounds like you, we’d love to hear from you.

- Hands‑on experience building production AI or ML systems,
  with recent focus on Generative AI / LLMs
- A strong understanding of RAG architectures, embeddings,
  vector databases, and semantic retrieval
- Experience with MLOps, including deployment, monitoring,
  evaluation, and CI/CD for AI systems
- A solid software‑engineering background, building scalable
  backend services
- Experience working in cloud environments (Azure preferred,
  but AWS or GCP also welcome)

It’s a bonus if you’ve also:

- Designed AI platforms used by multiple teams
- Worked with responsible AI, governance, or secure AI
  deployment
- Tuned GenAI systems for performance, reliability, or cost
