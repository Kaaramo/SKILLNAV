## Description complète

Jasper is the leading AI marketing platform, enabling the
world's most innovative companies to reimagine their end-to-
end marketing workflows and drive higher ROI through
increased brand consistency, efficiency, and personalization
at scale.

Jasper has been recognized as "one of the Top 15 Most
Innovative AI Companies of 2024" by Fast Company and is
trusted by nearly 20% of the Fortune 500 – including
Prudential, Ulta Beauty, and Wayfair. Founded in 2021,
Jasper is a remote-first organization with team members
across the US, France, and Australia.

About The Role

As Jasper undergoes an agentic AI shift, this role is
critical for ensuring our IT infrastructure is robust,
secure, and fine-tuned to handle advanced AI workflows.
Spending 70-80% of your time directly on AI enablement, you
will be deployed across the organization to tackle hands-on
technical implementations, bridging the gap between
traditional IT systems management and cutting-edge AI
deployments. You will design and deploy intelligent systems
that automate cross-platform workflows, preparing Jasper for
AI-driven scale by enabling agentic AI to trigger real
actions across our entire tech stack.

This fully remote role reports to the Director of AI
Enablement & IT and is open to candidates located anywhere
in the continental US.

 What you will do at Jasper

- Agentic IT Infrastructure & Automation: Modernize and fine-
  tune our IT systems to ensure they securely and
  efficiently support autonomous and agentic AI workflows.
  Build reliable, scalable automation infrastructure that
  significantly enhances team efficiency and reduces manual
  work.
- AI Implementation & Cross-Platform Workflows: Partner with
  the Director of AI Enablement & IT to operationalize AI
  initiatives using tools like Claude, ChatGPT, and Zapier.
  Design intelligent workflows that connect platforms like
  Google Workspace and Slack, allowing AI agents to trigger
  real, multi-step actions.
- Core Systems Management: Administer and optimize core IT
  domains, including Identity Providers and Mobile Device
  Management. Leverage advanced features like Okta Workflows
  to streamline identity and access operations.
- Cross-Functional Technical Support: Deploy across various
  departments to assist directly with the technical rollout
  of AI enablement projects.
- SaaS Ecosystem Operations: Manage and streamline our broader
  SaaS stack, including Google Workspace, Linear, and tens
  of other applications.
- Enablement & Training: Assist in creating playbooks and
  training resources to help teams adopt new AI tools
  seamlessly into their existing workflows.

What you will bring to Jasper

- 4–6 years of experience in IT systems engineering, IT
  support, or a related technical role.
- Demonstrated experience designing and deploying intelligent
  systems that automate cross-platform workflows and prepare
  infrastructure for AI-driven scale.
- Strong, hands-on administrative experience with Identity
  Providers (Okta) and Mobile Device Management (Rippling
  IT).
- Proven track record of managing and integrating modern SaaS
  applications (Google Workspace, Linear, etc.).
- Ability to act as a multifaceted technical specialist,
  comfortably pivoting between core IT operations and cross-
  functional AI implementation projects.
- Strong communication skills with the ability to translate
  complex technical concepts and business impact clearly to
  non-technical stakeholders.
