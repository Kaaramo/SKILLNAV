## Responsabilités

- Design and evaluate information retrieval and reasoning strategies across RAG, agents, and ML including chunking, embedding models, hybrid search, and multi-step workflows
- Prototype GenAI workflows and agentic systems that map and reason over compliance objects like controls, risks, and requirements
- Explore ML and probabilistic approaches including classifiers, ranking models, graph prediction, and calibration where GenAI is not optimal
- Build and maintain evaluation frameworks including golden datasets, automated quality metrics, and regression detection
- Implement and tune ranking and reranking systems using cross-encoders, LLM-based rerankers, and learning-to-rank approaches
- Run experiments to validate hypotheses and quantify improvements before production rollout
- Debug failure modes and build error taxonomies across retrieval, reasoning, and generation
- Collaborate with AI and Software Engineers to hand off validated approaches for productionization
