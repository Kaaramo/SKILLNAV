### Raisonnement classification (LLM upstream)

This is a data engineering leadership role that builds and
manages the data platforms and pipelines that ENABLE AI/ML
work, but does not directly build, train, fine-tune, or
deploy models. The role involves feature engineering and
data preparation for model training, and partnering with
data science teams to provide curated data. This is classic
"enabling" work that supports AI initiatives rather than
hands-on AI/ML model development.

## Description complète

About Citi:

Citi, the leading global bank, has approximately 200 million
customer accounts and does business in more than 160
countries and jurisdictions. Citi provides consumers,
corporations, governments, and institutions with a broad
range of financial products and services, including consumer
banking and credit, corporate and investment banking,
securities brokerage, transaction services, and wealth
management. Our core activities are safeguarding assets,
lending money, making payments, and accessing the capital
markets on behalf of our clients.

Role Summary:

We are seeking a highly skilled and forward-thinking Vice
President to lead our Intelligent Data Platforms team in
Pune. This leader will be responsible for the evolution and
management of our foundational client and account data,
bridging the gap between high-throughput transactional
systems (OLTP) and large-scale analytical platforms (OLAP).
This is a critical engineering leadership role, owning the
core data assets that power every client and advisor
interaction and serve as the foundation for our AI-driven
initiatives.

The ideal candidate is a seasoned data expert with a deep
background in managing massive, mission-critical data
ecosystems within premier financial institutions. You must
have proven experience not only in delivering high-
performance, real-time data via APIs but also in enabling
advanced analytics and AI on that data. This role requires a
leader who is driven by metrics, possesses an end-to-end
view of the entire data lifecycle, and has a passion for
leveraging modern data technologies and AI to unlock new
value from our core data assets.

Key Responsibilities:

- Unified Data Platform Ownership: Lead a team of skilled
  engineers responsible for the design, development, and
  24/7 operation of the unified client and account data
  platform, encompassing both high-throughput OLTP systems
  and large-scale OLAP analytical stores.
- AI & Advanced Analytics Enablement: Pioneer the application
  of AI/ML models on core client and account data to
  generate actionable insights, identify patterns, and
  create intelligent features for advisor and client
  applications. Partner with data science teams to provide
  them with high-quality, curated data.
- Real-Time API Management: Oversee the entire lifecycle of
  the high-throughput, low-latency APIs that provide real-
  time data to advisor workstations, client-facing portals,
  and other critical applications, ensuring they are
  performant, reliable, and secure.
- Data Integrity & Scale: Take ultimate responsibility for the
  integrity, availability, and performance of massive
  volumes of data across both transactional and analytical
  systems.
- Measurement & Analytics: Champion a culture of measurement.
  Define, monitor, and report on key performance indicators
  (KPIs) across the entire ecosystem, from database
  performance and API latency to data quality and the
  efficacy of AI models.
- End-to-End Ecosystem View: Serve as the subject matter
  expert for the entire client and account data ecosystem,
  from the foundational OLTP databases and real-time APIs to
  the analytical data warehouses/lakes and the AI/ML models
  they feed.
- Technical Strategy: Partner with global architects and
  business leaders to define the technical roadmap for the
  core data platforms, including strategies for data
  replication, ETL/ELT processes, and the integration of
  AI/ML capabilities.

Qualifications:

- Experience: 10+ years of technology experience, with a
  significant track record in wealth management technology
  at leading financial institutions.
- Technical Expertise:
  - Deep, hands-on experience managing large-scale, high-
    throughput OLTP databases (e.g., Oracle, PostgreSQL, SQL
    Server).
  - Extensive experience with analytical data platforms, data
    warehousing, and data lakes (e.g., Snowflake, BigQuery,
    Redshift, Hadoop ecosystem).
  - Hands-on experience with the data engineering aspects of
    AI/ML, including feature engineering, data preparation
    for model training, and deploying models into production
    pipelines. Familiarity with MLOps principles.
  - Proven track record of designing, building, and operating
    mission-critical, real-time RESTful APIs at scale.
  - Strong data modeling skills for both transactional and
    analytical use cases.
  - Expertise with performance measurement and monitoring tools
    (e.g., AppDynamics, Splunk, Prometheus, Grafana).
- Leadership: Proven ability to lead, mentor, and manage a
  high-performing engineering team.
- Work Ethic & Ownership: A strong, demonstrable work ethic
  and a deep sense of ownership, with a history of seeing
  complex systems through from conception to long-term
  operational stability.
- Systems Thinker: The ability to maintain a holistic, end-to-
  end view of a complex data ecosystem, understanding the
  flow of data from real-time transaction to analytical
  insight.
- Communication: Excellent communication skills, with the
  ability to effectively collaborate with technical teams
  and business stakeholders across different geographic
  locations.

Education:

- Bachelor’s degree in Computer Science, Engineering, or a
  related technical discipline.
