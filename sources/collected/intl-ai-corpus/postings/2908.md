### Raisonnement classification (LLM upstream)

This is a platform engineering role focused on building
infrastructure and tooling that enables other teams to
deploy AI solutions. The core work involves Kubernetes,
Terraform, cloud architecture, API gateways, and CI/CD —
classic platform/infrastructure engineering. While the
platform serves GenAI use cases (fine-tuned models, vector
stores, agent orchestration), the engineer is building the
platform that supports AI rather than directly building,
training, or fine-tuning models themselves. The "nice to
have" section mentions GenAI experience, indicating it's
secondary to the platform engineering focus.
