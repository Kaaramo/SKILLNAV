## Description complète

Company overview

Consumer Edge is a data innovation and AI company redefining
how professionals understand consumer and business behavior.
Our mission is to create the most actionable data products
that transform how our clients make decisions. Working with
world-class data partners, we deliver real-time,
transaction-based intelligence enriched by deep industry
expertise. We believe that in the right hands, data has the
power to change the way we work.

Technology is the foundation of Consumer Edge, and lies at
the heart of everything we do. We are a group of passionate
technologists who enable the company mission to derive
insights from a growing number of datasets, and create
actionable data products.

Role summary

We are looking for a Senior Engineer who is motivated to
help us create the next generation of AI Products.
Responsibilities will include development of backend
services, interaction with state of the art AI models, and
data retrieval from internal and external sources. You will
work with Product Managers, Data Scientists and other
Engineers both in the team and in the whole technology
organization to bridge the gap between product requirements,
AI, and our rich data sources. You will deal with non-
trivial and non-deterministic flows, entirely dictated by
the user’s requests and the AI interpretation of those,
within the guardrails we create to ensure they achieve their
goals.

About the team

You’ll be joining Consumer Edge’s AI Products Team. We are a
distributed cross-disciplinary team located across Europe
and the US. Our mission is to build a proactive intelligence
layer that seamlessly integrates with user workflows, acting
as a copilot for exploration, surfacing insights, and
suggesting next steps. This will be achieved through
unified, embedded, and proactive intelligence. You’ll be
joining a group of software engineers, product managers and
data scientists and working closely with stakeholders to use
data to answer real-world business questions. The product is
still at an early stage with a huge roadmap ahead and a lot
of innovation happening in the field so you’ll have the
opportunity to influence technical design, features and
implementation details.

We believe in building a culture of collaboration, with
everyone contributing to build something great, sharing both
the successes and overcoming the challenges together.

Your main responsibilities

- Develop new user-facing features
- Work on backend services, data pipelines, and cloud
  infrastructure
- Keep up to date with the latest developments in AI models
  and architectures
- Contribute to the vision and ensure the technical
  feasibility of product requirements
- Optimize the system for accuracy, speed and user experience
- Collaborate with other team members and stakeholders
- Collaborate with other teams both at the input side and the
  output side of our systems

What you’ll bring to the team

- 10 years experience working with backend systems and cloud
  architectures
- You are proficient in parallel and concurrent programming
- You know how to build software in a robust and testable way
- You know how to design APIs and interact with other teams to
  evolve them effectively
- You are familiar with Python and SQL
- You are great at collaborating with the team and
  communicating with stakeholders

Nice to have

- You have experience working with AI APIs
- You have experience building agentic workflows
- You have experience working with data pipelines

What our stack looks like

- We write our code exclusively in Python, with type checking
  and great test coverage. Our services use FastAPI and our
  pipelines use Dagster and DBT.
- We deploy our infrastructure on Google Cloud using Terraform
- We work with a variety of AI providers
- We use GraphQL and REST to query our APIs, and SQL to read
  data from our tables

Benefits & perks

- We are a remote-first company with a distributed environment
  and flexible working arrangements. We believe that
  distributed workers should be first-class citizens. We
  also have an office in New York if offices are your thing.

Salary

The annual base salary for this role is between $140,000 -
$180,000 based on experience, with the opportunity for a
performance-based bonus, company equity, 401(k) matching,
paid parental leave, flexible and generous time off, work-
from-home flexibility, and subsidized health benefits
