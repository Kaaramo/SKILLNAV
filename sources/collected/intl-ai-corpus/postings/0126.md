## Description complète

Capsule is building the world’s most intuitive AI-powered
video editing tool, designed for modern teams. We’re a
Series A startup led by two serial founders who have built
and scaled creative platforms used by hundreds of millions
of people. We’re backed by top-tier investors behind
Webflow, Figma, Slack, and Replit.

We’ve grown quickly. In less than a year since leaving
public beta, 100+ enterprise teams—including HubSpot,
ServiceNow, and Instacart—use Capsule to create high-
quality, on-brand video content at scale.

We are seeking a skilled AI Engineer to join our team and
help develop the next generation of our video editing tool.
In this role, you will work closely with our founders and
product and engineering teams to design, implement, and
deploy ML models that power new and improved features for
our product.

‍

Responsibilities

- Research and evaluate new ML techniques and technologies
  that can be applied to our product
- Collaborate with cross-functional teams to identify and
  prioritize ML feature development opportunities
- Develop and implement ML models and algorithms to power
  these features
- Optimize model performance and accuracy through testing and
  iterative development
- Participate in code reviews and provide technical guidance
  to other team members
- Staying up-to-date with the latest trends and best practices
  in ML engineering
- Work fast and enjoy the rapid pace of an early stage startup
- Have fun working with other highly-skilled team players in a
  zero-ego work environment
- Bring new ideas to the table that expand the capabilities of
  the product and delight customers

Requirements

- 3+ years of experience as an AI/ML engineer in a commercial
  software development setting
- Computer Science or related degree
- Strong proficiency in Python and at least one deep learning
  framework (e.g. PyTorch)
- Experience with data preprocessing, feature engineering, and
  model selection and optimization
- Experience building and deploying ML models in production
  environments
- Strong problem-solving and communication skills
- Highly-motivated, entrepreneurial-minded, high EQ
- Strong communication and organization skills
- Interest in video technology in general
- Experience with CI/CD a plus

Benefits

- Competitive compensation including salary and stock options
- Comprehensive insurance benefits — medical, dental, vision,
  life
- Generous paid time off
