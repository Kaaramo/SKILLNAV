## Description complète

Frazier & Deeter (FD) is an award-winning accounting &
advisory firm. FD and our family of brands serve clients
worldwide, from the Fortune Global 500 companies to growing
small businesses. Frazier & Deeter cultivates a growth
mindset and instils in our people the belief that we must be
adaptive and entrepreneurial, and we make a difference for
our clients and each other. We focus on our brand promise of
Investing in Relationships to Make a Difference.

FD offers a full range of tax, audit, accounting, and
advisory services through our offices in Atlanta, Charlotte,
Las Vegas, Nashville, Alpharetta, and Tampa, in addition to
London and India. We have been recognized repeatedly as a
Top 50 firm, a Best of the Best Accounting firm, a Best Firm
to Work For® and a Best Firm for Women in Leadership.

FD recently opened its own entity in India to support our
expanding international and cross-border client base. We are
not your typical firm of accountants and we like doing
things differently. Our team members have the opportunity to
be involved in global consulting and advisory client facing
roles in a fast growing and exciting new business in India,
which is part of our established and highly regarded
accountancy firms in the US and UK.

About the Role

The Agentic AI Developer is responsible for building
intelligent automations and streamlining processes across
the firm using Microsoft Copilot Studio, Power Automate, and
other generative AI tools. As part of a global development
team, this individual contributor will help automate manual
workflows, integrate AI-driven efficiencies, and support
agentic AI capabilities at scale.

Responsibilities

- Design and implement AI-augmented automations using
  Microsoft Copilot Studio and Power Automate.
- Develop solutions that streamline manual tasks across
  compliance, audit, and operational workflows.
- Leverage AI tools to parse documents, validate policies, and
  automate routine decision-making steps.
- Integrate agentic AI capabilities into existing
  applications, enhancing overall firm productivity.
- Work within a CI/CD pipeline, ensuring code is reviewed,
  tested, and deployed following best practices.
- Collaborate with internal development teams to align AI
  solutions with broader initiatives.
- Document automation designs, conduct testing, and iterate
  based on team feedback.

Skills & Experience

- Proficiency in Python for automation tasks and scripting.
- Experience developing or integrating solutions using AI
  technologies and RESTful APIs.
- Experience using Microsoft Power Automate and low-code/no-
  code platforms.
- Familiarity with CI/CD pipelines, version control, and code
  reviews.
- Understanding of AI application patterns, though model
  building isn’t required.
- Ability to collaborate with technical teams while working
  independently on implementation.

Additional Qualifications

- Prior experience with business process automation or
  operational efficiency projects is a plus.
- Familiarity with compliance workflows (e.g., audit
  processes) is beneficial.
- Strong communication for test/feedback phases, though
  primary focus is on development.

What FD offers

- Competitive salary
- Clear career growth path within the firm
- Personal and professional skills development and training
  support
