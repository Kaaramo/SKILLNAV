The Role:

We are seeking an innovative AI & Automation Engineer to

You'll build intelligent assistants that serve HR, Finance,

  organizational needs
- Performance & Governance
- Measure and report on automation ROI, adoption metrics, and
  user satisfaction
- Partner with IT Operations and Security teams to ensure

- Establish best practices for AI governance, data privacy,

You have:
