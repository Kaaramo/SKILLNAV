### Raisonnement classification (LLM upstream)

This is an IT Operations/Application Support role focused on
monitoring, maintaining, and supporting AI and Data
platforms in production. The engineer ensures availability,
performance, and reliability of AI services and model
inference layers, but does not build, train, fine-tune, or
deploy AI models themselves. They work near AI systems to
provide operational support rather than working directly on
AI/ML development.

## Responsabilités

- Own the day-to-day operational health of compliance approved AI and Data platforms in production, ensuring high availability, performance, and reliability

- Conduct post-incident root cause analysis and drive corrective and preventive improvements
