## Description complète

Hi, We're Centerfield!

Supercharged customer acquisition. Centerfield delivers
outcome-based digital marketing solutions and personalized
omnichannel experiences for the world’s leading brands.
Powered by our proprietary Dugout platform, Centerfield
acquires customers at scale for leading residential service,
insurance, e-commerce, and B2B brands. Centerfield’s digital
experiences and digital brands, such as Business.com and
BroadbandNow.com, reach more than 150 million in-market
shoppers annually. Centerfield is headquartered in Silicon
Beach and is proud to be recognized by Built in LA as a Best
Place to Work in Los Angeles.

The Opportunity... At Centerfield, we’re building smarter,
faster, and more scalable platforms - and we want you to
lead the way. As a Lead .NET Full Stack Engineer, you’ll
design and deliver high-impact applications, guide technical
direction, and mentor a talented team of engineers. This is
a role for someone who loves solving complex problems,
thrives in modern cloud environments, and wants their work
to make a direct impact on millions of users.

How You'll Contribute...

- Drive technical initiatives while staying hands-on in
  development
- Partner with cross-functional teams to translate business
  needs into technical solutions
- Mentor and support junior developers through coaching and
  collaboration
- Define and drive system architecture and design for
  scalable, high-performance applications
- Document development phases and monitor systems for quality
  and goals alignment
- Refine team processes and best practices
- Evaluate and recommend new technologies and tools to improve
  our platforms

What We're Looking For...

- Expertise in cloud platforms (AWS, GCP), REST & RPC APIs,
  and database design
- Strong proficiency in C# & .NET
- Strong experience with relational databases such as
  PostgreSQL or MySQL
- Experience with Service-Oriented Architectures and/or
  MicroServices.
- Clear understanding of software engineering design
  principles and working knowledge of commonly used design
  patterns
- Messaging & queues/event streams (e.g., RabbitMQ, Kafka, AWS
  SQS/SNS)
- Familiarity with CI/CD tools and practices
- Experience with Redis
- Strong interpersonal presence with the ability to build
  trust and foster collaboration across teams
- Clear technical communicator - able to whiteboard ideas on
  the fly, translate concepts into digital diagrams, and
  produce roadmaps that guide execution.
- Curious, passionate, teachable, continuous learning mindset
  (especially with how fast things move).

Bonus Points...

- Experience with Python, Node.js, or JavaScript
- Hands-on experience with JavaScript frameworks such as
  React, etc
- Experience with building applications using machine learning
  and artificial intelligence.
- Understanding of the MCP vs A2A frameworks.
- Understanding of AI agents vs RAG
- Understanding of semantic search approaches, vector
  databases, and libraries such as Redis VL or Pinecone
- Understanding of prompt engineering basics.
- Understanding of transformers, embeddings, and parameters
  when working with agentic frameworks.
- Chatbot experience working with inferences, pattern
  recognition, reasoning, generative, and multi-modal.
- Experience with training and optimization tactics such as
  fine-tuning, feedback loops, reinforcement, data labeling,
  and tagging.
- Understanding of tools such as Cursor, Claude, Perplexity,
  and ChatGPT.
