## Description complète

Job Purpose:

This role is responsible for building AI centric solutions
within Cendyn; helping to build and deploy intelligent
features across Cendyn's hospitality technology products.
You'll work across our polyglot stack (.NET, Node.js,
Python) to integrate LLMs, build RAG systems, and develop
AI-powered APIs. We’re looking for someone hungry to learn,
eager to dive into new technologies, and excited about the
opportunity to grow their AI engineering skills while
contributing to real production systems. You’ll collaborate
closely with senior engineers while taking ownership of
meaningful work from day one.

Job Responsibilities:

Essential Functions:

- Collaborate with senior engineers to implement AI features
  using LLMs (OpenAI, Anthropic, Azure OpenAI) integrated
  into Cendyn products
- Help build and maintain RAG (Retrieval-Augmented Generation)
  systems using vector databases
- Develop prompt engineering strategies and contribute to
  evaluation frameworks for AI output quality
- Build user interfaces using modern frameworks (React,
  Vue.js, Angular) that surface AI capabilities through
  intuitive experiences
- Develop backend services across our technology stack
  (.NET/C#, Node.js, Python) implementing AI orchestration
  workflows
- Write clean, testable, maintainable code following
  development standards across all layers of the application
  stack
- Contribute to data pipelines that prepare and serve data to
  AI systems
- Help configure cloud infrastructure (AWS, Azure) for AI
  workloads with guidance from senior team members
- Contribute to CI/CD pipelines for AI-powered applications
  using containerization (Docker, Kubernetes)
- Participate in code reviews, architectural discussions, and
  continuous improvement of development processes
- Stay current with AI/ML technologies and frameworks,
  bringing new ideas to the team
- Document AI patterns and learnings to share knowledge with
  teammates
- Follow and help improve software development standards and
  best practices
- Troubleshoot issues and help implement fixes across the full
  application stack
- Participate in on-call rotation with support from senior
  engineers

Requirements:

Required Education and Experience:

- Bachelor’s degree or equivalent combination of education and
  experience
- 1-3 years professional software development experience
  (internships and personal projects count)
- Familiarity with at least one of: Python,
  JavaScript/TypeScript, C#/.NET with willingness to learn
  others. Bonus points for experience with: LLM APIs
  (OpenAI, Anthropic), vector databases, modern frontend
  frameworks (React, Vue.js, Angular)
- Exposure to AI/ML concepts or LLM APIs (personal projects,
  coursework, or professional experience all count)
- Comfort working in Agile (Scrum) environments
- Understanding of RESTful API design and database basics (SQL
  and/or NoSQL)
- Familiarity with Git and basic branching/merging practices
- Awareness of cloud platforms (AWS, Azure, or GCP) and
  containerization (Docker) is a plus
- Strong problem-solving skills and genuine curiosity about
  how things work
- Good communication skills with ability to ask clarifying
  questions and explain your work

Preferred Education and Experience:

- Bachelors degree or higher

Expected Competencies:

- Hungry to learn and eager to dive into new technologies
- Self-starter who takes initiative and flags blockers early
- Collaborative mindset—you enjoy working with others and
  learning from teammates
- Genuine excitement about AI technologies and their potential
  to transform hospitality

Work Timings:

Monday through Friday from 12 PM to 9 PM IST to provide
healthy overlap between India team and US team and
supporting both to ensure adequate collaboration. This role
will be working in Hybrid Mode and will require at least 2
days’ work from office at Hyderabad. Occasional evening and
weekend work may be expected in case of job-related
emergencies or client needs.
