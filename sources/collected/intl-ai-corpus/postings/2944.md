## Description complète

About Us: EvolutionIQ’s mission is to deliver state of the
art technology that helps insurance claims teams make claims
handling more accurate, fair, and efficient, so that more
people impacted by injury or illness can continue their
lives with dignity and stability. We are currently
experiencing massive growth and to accomplish our goals, we
are hiring world-class talent who want to help build and
scale internally, and transform the insurance space. Our
team is our #1 priority, and we have been named one of
Inc.’s Best Workplaces 3 years in a row and Built In’s Best
Places to work in 2025 and 2026!

The Opportunity & Role Value: We are currently seeking a
Solutions Engineer to serve as a dedicated owner of data
onboarding, relationships, maintenance, and health. You will
align priorities with Client Services schedules while
coordinating across Product, Engineering, and Sales teams.
You’ll centralize validation of client data within Solutions
Engineering, partnering with Engineering to develop tools
and processes that boost data quality and streamline
validation operations. By elevating data management at EIQ,
you’ll collaborate with Product to enhance operational
efficiency and mitigate risks to time-to-value and overall
project health. In this role, clients will benefit from your
expertise as you bridge infrastructure gaps and integrate
multiple systems to keep implementations on track. You will
also serve as the key technical liaison who will
troubleshoot, build custom workarounds, and reduce the need
for Product and Engineering teams to engage directly with
clients.

About You:

- 5+ years of experience working in a Solutions Engineering /
  Solutions Architect capacity across enterprise B2B SaaS
  for a high growth technology company
- Proven experience as a business and technical leader for
  complex and sophisticated data solutions for enterprise
  clients
- Deep familiarity with the data tools including Pandas,
  Google BigQuery, DBT, and Data Analytics / BI reporting
  tools such as Looker or Looker Studio.
- Proactive, self starter that can own and elevate function in
  a fast paced, agile startup environment
- Dedicated problem solver who can leverage technical
  expertise, analytical skills and business acumen to find
  creative solutions
- Superb written and oral communication skills
- Excellent relationship building and interpersonal skills
- Familiarity with RDBMS, database schema concepts, and ETL
  pipelines is a plus
- Familiarity with AI/ML methodologies is a plus

In this Role You Will:

- Lead data onboarding and data transfer for client
  implementations
- Develop and document data onboarding requirements and
  processes while developing operational framework to drive
  continuous improvement to client delivery models and
  client value
- Collaborate with Product, Engineering and ML teams to
  deliver and evolve scalable data onboarding and data
  health solutions while aligning with overall EIQ strategy
  and goals
- Contribute to the design, clean up and maintenance of client
  data and data architecture for EIQ data ingestion
  processes
- Stay current and provide feedback on systems to monitor and
  improve client data health in collaboration with CS and
  Product teams
- Collaborate closely with Sales to provide technical and
  operational guidance during the sales cycle
- Partner with Product, Engineering and CS teams to support
  Client custom integrations and scope custom product
  requests
- Provide additional implementation support to the Client
  Services team to ensure clients can successfully utilize
  all product features and functionality
- Identify opportunities for tools and automation to scale
  workflows for EIQ's CS and other teams
- Provide technical triage and escalation oversight across all
  product modules to client business and technical
  stakeholders as needed
- Provide data management training and promote data fluency
  across EIQ

Your Impact:

- Data onboarding, data relationships, data maintenance and
  health will have a clear owner and their priorities will
  fully align with Client Services priorities and schedules
  while coordinating across Product, Engineering and ML team
  needs
- Data management at EIQ will be elevated as a dedicated
  resource is focused on collaborating with product to
  improve the operational efficiency and mitigate risk to
  TTV and Project / Client health
- Clients will have an expert, dedicated resource who can
  streamline the process and bridge gaps in their
  infrastructure while working across multiple systems to
  ensure implementation and projects are successful and on
  schedule
- Product, Engineering and ML teams at EIQ will be able to
  collaborate with a CS resource to troubleshoot and build
  custom workarounds and processes which will greatly reduce
  the need for them to work directly with Clients
- SE role can also provide technical and product expertise
  during the Sales process and properly manage Client
  expectations around resource allocation, effort and time
  required to ensure a successful data transfer process
