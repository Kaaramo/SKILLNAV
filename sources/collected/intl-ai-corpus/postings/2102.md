## Description complète

ABOUT BESTOW

Life insurance is one of the world's most important
products. It's also one of the hardest to build, distribute,
and modernize. Bestow exists to change that.

Bestow is a leading vertical technology platform serving
some of the largest and most innovative life insurers. Our
platform unifies the fragmented, legacy value chain,
enabling carriers to launch products in weeks instead of
years. Carriers choose us to scale and operate at
unprecedented speed, powered by AI and automation.

Bestow isn't selling policies. We're building the
infrastructure that helps an entire industry move faster,
reach more people, and deliver on its promise.

Backed by leading investors (Goldman Sachs, Hedosophia, NEA,
Valar, 8VC) and trusted by major carriers, Bestow is powered
by a team that moves with precision, purpose, and heart. If
you want to help reimagine a centuries-old industry with
lasting impact, join us.

Bestow offers flexible remote/hybrid work, meaningful
benefits, equity, and substantial growth opportunities.

Bestow participates in the E-Verify Program.

ABOUT THE TEAM

The AI Automation team is Bestow's internal function
dedicated to how the business operates. We embed AI into the
workflows of engineering, product, sales, legal, finance,
and other parts of the business to eliminate process
friction and drive measurable efficiency gains. We work
cross-functionally to identify high-friction processes,
rapidly prototype LLM-powered solutions, and harden them
into production-grade systems that drive measurable business
impact. The team partners closely with business operations,
data science, data engineering, and product teams to
translate research and operational intelligence into tools
that achieve organic adoption. You'll be expected to move
fast, build pragmatically, and treat AI not just as a
technology but as a lever for redesigning how work gets done
across the organization.

WHAT YOU'LL DO

As an AI Automation Engineer, you will identify, build, and
scale AI-powered automation across Bestow's internal
operations, serving as a catalyst for practical innovation
that frees teams to focus on strategic and creative work.

Build & Scale

- Rapidly prototype MVPs using LLM APIs (Gemini, Anthropic),
  agent frameworks, and workflow orchestration platforms
  (n8n, Zapier, Make) to address business bottlenecks
- Develop production-grade internal applications with reliable
  frontends, robust backends (Python), and proper
  observability
- Design and implement RAG architectures, multi-step agent
  workflows, and structured output pipelines grounded in
  company data
- Build automated evaluation frameworks (evals) to ensure the
  reliability, accuracy, and safety of AI-generated outputs
- Use AI tooling to accelerate your own development process,
  supervising AI's architectural choices and debugging where
  it falls short

Scale & Harden

- Evolve successful prototypes into durable production systems
  with comprehensive error handling, logging, retry logic,
  and monitoring
- Research and propose solutions that handle sensitive and
  business-critical data with appropriate security controls
  (PII handling, SOC2 awareness, HIPAA considerations,
  human-in-the-loop safeguards)
- Optimize for cost, latency, and token efficiency across LLM-
  powered workflows
- Design resilient systems with comprehensive monitoring and
  alerting that contribute to overall automation governance

Advocate and Influence

- Communicate complex technical concepts to non-technical
  stakeholders, building alignment and driving informed
  decision-making

- Host workshops, create playbooks, and train team members
  across all departments, ensuring they can safely and
  effectively use, maintain, and get value from the tools
  you ship.
- Document automation patterns and best practices to ensure
  long-term maintainability of processes with increasing
  criticality.

- Champion responsible AI practices across the organization,
  ensuring the automations you build include appropriate
  human oversight, observability, and safeguards, especially
  for business-critical or sensitive data workflows.

WHO YOU ARE

- You are a hands-on engineer who is highly collaborative
  across the company, writes production code, owns systems
  in production, and is accountable for the reliability of
  what you ship.
- Proven track record of shipping tools or automations that
  achieved real adoption, not just demos.
- Strong cross-functional communicator who is comfortable
  interviewing a sales rep, ops manager, or legal
  coordinator about their workflow and translating it into a
  technical spec. You help bridge the gap between business
  problems and engineering solutions.
- High level of ownership and accountability, particularly
  when building systems that handle sensitive or business-
  critical data.
- Ability to connect what you ship to measurable outcomes;
  you’re comfortable working with business partners to
  define success metrics and report on results.
- Adaptability to learn new technologies and products as the
  job demands

Requirements

- 5+ years of experience in full-stack development or software
  engineering with strong skills in Python and
  JavaScript/TypeScript.
- 1+ years of hands-on experience building LLM-backed tools
  involving prompt engineering, API integration, and
  automated evaluations.
- Demonstrated experience with at least one
  orchestration/agent framework (LangChain, LangGraph,
  CrewAI, AutoGen, or similar).
- Familiarity with RAG architectures, including vector
  databases (Pinecone, Weaviate, pgvector) and embedding
  pipelines.
- Experience with workflow and integration platforms (n8n,
  Zapier, Make, Temporal, or Airflow).
- Familiarity with cloud infrastructure on Google Cloud
  Platform (GCP), including Cloud Run, Cloud Functions,
  Vertex AI, Cloud Storage, and IAM, or comparable AWS or
  Azure infrastructure and tooling.
- Demonstrated experience with AI Coding assistants. AI tools
  are heavily ingrained in Bestow culture.
- Strong debugging skills; you can diagnose failures across
  prompts, integrations, APIs, and business logic.
- Knowledge of data security practices relevant to AI (PII
  handling, HIPAA, SOC 2).
- Experience with BigQuery and advanced SQL for data analysis
  and pipeline integration
- Experience building CI/CD pipelines using tools such as
  Docker, CircleCI, git, etc.
- Nice to have: Experience with LLM observability tools
  (LangSmith, Helicone, Braintrust) and MLOps practices.
- Nice to have: Background in insurance, fintech, or other
  regulated industries.

What Success Looks Like

In your first 90 days, you'll have shipped at least one
meaningful automation that saves a team measurable time.
Within six months, you'll have a portfolio of deployed
solutions across multiple functions and a reputation as the
person who makes things faster and easier.
