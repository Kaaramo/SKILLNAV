## Description complète

Octane is unlocking the power of financial products for
merchants and consumers. Our cutting-edge technology and
innovative financial products empower businesses with more
control and flexibility, enabling them to deliver seamless
digital experiences, drive customer loyalty, and build long-
term value.

Octane supports merchants throughout the sales cycle:
connecting dealerships with high-intent buyers, driving
transparent, fast, and easy closings with award-winning
technology, and providing on-going customer care with
superior loan servicing.

Founded in 2014, Octane supports over 60 OEM partner brands
and over 4,000 dealer partners, and has a team of over 600.
Visit www.octane.co.

At Octane, we’re building innovative technology solutions
that power smarter, faster, and more efficient decision-
making. We are seeking an accomplished AI/ML Technical Lead
to drive the design, development, and deployment of advanced
machine learning solutions that scale seamlessly in
production environments. In this role, you’ll shape the
strategy for how AI is leveraged across the organization,
partnering with cross-functional teams to translate business
goals into technical solutions that are not only state-of-
the-art, but also ethical, secure, and reliable.

This position is ideal for a hands-on leader who thrives at
the intersection of research and engineering. You will
mentor engineers and data scientists, establish best
practices for MLOps, and ensure models deliver consistent
value long after deployment. With opportunities to explore
cutting-edge fields like generative AI, large language
models, natural language processing, and computer vision,
you’ll play a pivotal role in setting the technical standard
for Octane’s AI initiatives while guiding the team toward
innovation and measurable impact.

Responsibilities:

- Lead the end-to-end design, development, and deployment of
  advanced AI/ML systems, ensuring production environments
  are scalable, efficient, and highly performant.
- Collaborate with cross-functional teams to define AI
  strategy, evaluate use cases, and translate business
  objectives into actionable technical solutions.
- Build, optimize, and maintain machine learning pipelines,
  covering data ingestion, preprocessing, model training,
  evaluation, deployment, and ongoing monitoring.
- Establish and champion MLOps best practices, including CI/CD
  for ML workflows, automated testing frameworks, and
  continuous model performance monitoring to prevent drift.
- Serve as a technical mentor and leader, guiding engineers
  and data scientists on system architecture, coding
  standards, and efficient development practices.
- Research and evaluate emerging AI methodologies, frameworks,
  and tools, integrating innovative approaches into the
  organization’s technology stack where applicable.
- Ensure AI solutions meet ethical, security, and compliance
  standards, including model explainability, bias
  mitigation, and data privacy requirements.
- Partner with engineering leadership to set technical
  strategy, establish architectural guidelines, and define
  standards that shape the future of AI initiatives at
  Octane.

Requirements:

- Master’s degree in Computer Science, Artificial
  Intelligence, Machine Learning, or related field with 5+
  years of professional experience, or Bachelor’s degree
  with 8+ years of experience in AI/ML engineering.
- Proven expertise with leading ML/DL frameworks such as
  TensorFlow, PyTorch, or JAX, with hands-on experience
  training and deploying models.
- Strong programming proficiency in Python, with additional
  experience using languages such as C++ or Java for
  performance-critical applications.
- Practical experience with MLOps tools and workflows,
  including MLflow, Kubeflow, SageMaker, or Vertex AI to
  streamline model lifecycle management.
- Deep understanding of data engineering concepts such as data
  pipelines, preprocessing, and distributed computing, as
  well as hands-on experience with cloud platforms (AWS,
  Azure, or GCP).
- Demonstrated success designing, deploying, and optimizing
  AI/ML systems at scale, including model serving,
  monitoring, and iteration.
- Strong communication and collaboration skills, with the
  ability to influence technical direction and align diverse
  stakeholders.

Preferred Skills:

- Experience with generative AI and large language models
  (LLMs), including prompt engineering and fine-tuning
  approaches.
- Knowledge of advanced ML domains such as natural language
  processing (NLP), computer vision, reinforcement learning,
  or recommendation systems.
- Familiarity with containerization and orchestration
  technologies, including Docker and Kubernetes, to support
  scalable deployment.
- Hands-on experience with infrastructure-as-code (IaC) tools,
  such as Terraform or Ansible, to enable automated and
  reproducible infrastructure management.
- Contributions to the AI/ML community, such as open-source
  projects, conference presentations, or peer-reviewed
  research publications.
