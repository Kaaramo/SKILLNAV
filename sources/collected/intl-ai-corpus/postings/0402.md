About Blooming Health

Blooming Health is a mission-driven, venture-backed health
tech company transforming the social care landscape. We’re
building an intelligent platform that helps people navigate
and access the resources essential for holistic well-
being—enabling CBOs, health systems, and health plans to
deliver more effective and equitable care.

The Opportunity

We’re hiring an AI Engineer to help us design and build the
systems that power our AI experiences—especially LLM-driven
workflows, conversational patterns, and orchestration/agent
layers. You’ll work closely with the Director of AI
Engineering and product/engineering partners to ship
reliable AI features into production, with strong attention
to evaluation, data, and software quality.

What You’ll Do

- Brainstorm and design AI orchestration patterns: how agents
  hand off, how state flows, how to handle uncertainty and
  edge cases.
- Build and maintain our AI orchestration layer (Master OS,
  Engines, agent workflows).
- Think through tradeoffs in prompt design, tool architecture,
  and conversation flow.
- Design data pipelines that capture, transform, and surface
  insights from patient/member interactions.
- Write clean, tested, production-ready Python (APIs,
  services, orchestration code).
- Be a thought partner on technical direction—we’re building
  something new and need someone who enjoys solving hard
  problems together.

What You’ll BringMUST HAVE

AI / LLM Foundations

- Hands-on experience with LLM APIs (OpenAI, Anthropic, or
  similar).
- Understanding of prompt engineering patterns and
  limitations.
- Familiarity with conversational AI / chatbot architectures.
- Ability to reason about AI system design: when to use
  agents, how to structure workflows, how to handle
  uncertainty.

Python Proficiency

- Comfortable with async Python (asyncio, httpx, aioredis).
- Experience building APIs and backend services.
- Strong grasp of clean code principles and testing.

Data Engineering Fundamentals

- Strong SQL (PostgreSQL preferred).
- Experience building ETL pipelines / data workflows.
- Understanding of data modeling and schema design.

Nice-to-Haves

AI / LLM Depth

- Orchestration patterns: multi-agent systems, tool use,
  function calling.
- RAG, embeddings, retrieval systems.
- Voice AI / real-time audio (Twilio, WebRTC, speech-to-text).
- Familiarity with evaluation/testing strategies for AI
  systems.

Data Engineering Depth

- Streaming data (Kafka, event-driven architectures).
- Orchestration tools (Airflow, Prefect, Dagster).
- dbt or similar transformation frameworks.

Healthcare Domain

- Healthcare workflows, HIPAA considerations.
- SDOH, care management, patient engagement experience.

Infrastructure

- Docker, Kubernetes, container orchestration.

Why Join Blooming Health?
