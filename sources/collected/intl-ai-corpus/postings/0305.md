We are Loop

Loop Earplugs is reinventing how people experience the world

making history as the first company to win Deloitte’s Fast
50 Belgium twice.

But this is just the beginning. As we continue to develop
disruptive products, we’re constantly on the lookout for the
next big idea - and building a team who can make it happen.

Being a Looper means making a difference. Whatever your
role, this is your stage to make a real impact and bring
your ideas to life. Better yet, you’ll join a fast-growing,
global team of 250+ people who are passionate, down-to-earth
and eager to tackle challenges together.

5+ years in AI | Hybrid I Antwerp or Amsterdam

As an AI Engineer at Loop, you’ll play a critical role in
transforming how our teams work by developing AI-driven
tools that solve business challenges. Collaborating closely
with internal stakeholders, you’ll turn complex needs into
simple, scalable solutions. Whether it’s automating customer
feedback analysis, streamlining campaign workflows, or
building tools that improve asset creation, your work will
directly influence how teams operate. By blending technical
expertise with a deep understanding of our business, you’ll
empower every department to achieve more—and faster. This is
your chance to create tools that drive impact across the
company.

Build AI-driven tools that transform how we work

We’re looking for an AI Engineer who takes full ownership of
building impactful AI solutions—from scoping to deployment.
You’ll design, develop, and ship production-ready systems
that automate workflows, accelerate decision-making, and
save hours across teams. Working closely with stakeholders,
you’ll translate real business challenges into intelligent,
scalable tools using NLP, LLMs, and agentic workflows.
You’ll choose the right stack—Streamlit, FastAPI, Azure, or
GCP—and ensure adoption through seamless integration and
measurable outcomes. This is a hands-on, high-autonomy role
where you’ll help define both the technical direction and
engineering standards.

Your responsibilities:

- Develop AI-driven software to solve business challenges,
  integrating technologies like NLP, machine learning, and
  automation.
- Build and deploy applications using open-source frameworks
  (Streamlit, Gradio, or FastAPI), so they can be used by
  other teams.
- Create custom integrations using APIs and webhooks to
  connect AI solutions with internal systems.
- Fine-tune LLMs for specific business applications.
- Own projects and products end-to-end, from identifying
  opportunities to deployment, using first-principle
  thinking.
- Stay ahead of AI trends and recommend new technologies to
  enhance our capabilities.

How you’ll succeed as an AI Engineer

Success in this role means combining technical excellence
with creativity, autonomy, and leadership. You’ll take full
ownership of projects—from defining scope and technical
direction to driving measurable business outcomes like
adoption, automation impact, and efficiency gains. You’ll
set engineering standards, mentor teammates, and foster a
culture of experimentation and excellence. Clear
communication and collaboration with stakeholders will be
essential as you translate complex challenges into simple,
scalable solutions. By staying ahead of AI trends and
sharing your knowledge, you’ll elevate the team and shape
how Loop builds with AI.

These key competences will set you up for success:

- 5+ years of experience building AI/ML algorithms and
  solutions, and a degree in Computer Science or related
  field.
- Highly proficient with Python, and with ML frameworks like
  PyTorch and scikit-learn.
- Hands-on experience with Natural Language Processing (NLP),
  traditional ML, and LLMs.
- Experience with data preprocessing, feature engineering, and
  model evaluation techniques essential for machine learning
  projects.
- Familiarity with the latest in GenAI, including RAG, agentic
  workflows, MCP, A2A, tool use, function calling, and
  multi-modal systems.
- Experience with DevOps practices, including CI/CD pipeline
  tools such as Docker, Kubernetes, Github Actions.
- Experience with deployment on one of the major cloud
  providers (preferably Azure/GCP).
- Understanding of various machine learning algorithms,
  including supervised and unsupervised learning,
  reinforcement learning, and neural networks.
- Experience with MLOps, including monitoring and optimizing
  AI models for performance, scalability, and efficiency.

Job evolution:

- Within your first month, you’ll immerse yourself in Loop’s
  workflows and AI ecosystem—learning how different teams
  operate and identifying opportunities where AI can make
  the biggest impact. You’ll also help establish best

  setting the foundation for scalable AI delivery.
- By your third month, you’ll take ownership of an internal AI
  product, working closely with stakeholders to scope

  first working prototypes.
- By your sixth month, you’ll be leading multiple AI products
  from a technical perspective —owning one from concept to
  production deployment. You’ll have contributed to Loop’s
  growing library of internal AI tools, mentoring teammates
  and helping define the standards for how we build, deploy,
  and scale AI solutions across the organization.
