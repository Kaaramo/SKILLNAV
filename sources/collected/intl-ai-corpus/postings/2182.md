Required Qualifications



AI / ML‑Specific Qualifications

- Experience building AI‑driven applications, including
  solutions that leverage large language models (LLMs), AI
  Agents, agent tools and agent skills.

- Hands‑on experience with LLM frameworks such as LangChain,
  LlamaIndex, or Semantic Kernel.

- Experience with Google Agent Development Kit (ADK) or
  comparable agent frameworks, including designing,
  orchestrating, and operating agentbased systems in
  production.

- Experience leveraging AInative code editors and IDEs (e.g.,
  Copilotenabled IDEs, agentassisted development

  developer productivity, code quality, and delivery
  velocity.

- Understanding of agentic AI architectures, including
  multi‑step reasoning, orchestration, and tool‑based
  execution.

- Experience with prompt engineering and prompt tuning,
  including iterative improvement based on feedback and
  performance.

- Familiarity with ML model evaluation, including monitoring
  accuracy, reliability, and behavioral drift over time.

- Experience working with or integrating vector databases
  (e.g., Faiss, Chroma) as part of AI solutions.

- Understanding of MLOps concepts, including ML lifecycle
  pipelines, deployment, observability, and
  operationalization.

- Awareness of responsible AI considerations, including
  governance, safety, and reliability when deploying AI
  systems at scale.

  field.

- Strong hands‑on experience with object‑oriented programming,
