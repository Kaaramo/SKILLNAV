## Responsabilités

- Design and build Generative AI prototypes using frameworks like LangChain, LlamaIndex, and APIs from OpenAI and Anthropic
- Develop RAG pipelines using vector stores and knowledge graphs for client solutions
- Build and test agentic architectures on PA's Genie Platform enabling AI agents that reason, retrieve, and act across systems
- Explore and implement LLMOps practices including evaluation tools and model observability platforms like TruLens and LangSmith
- Deploy AI solutions on cloud-native environments (AWS, Azure, GCP) with appropriate DevOps practices
- Work cross-functionally with product, design, and other experts to deliver innovative software solutions for clients across seven sectors
- Collaborate with internal AI community through knowledge-sharing, coaching, and mentoring
- Integrate LLMs with APIs, data sources, and production systems in distributed microservices architectures

- Retrieval-augmented generation (RAG) systems for enterprise knowledge access
- Agentic AI architectures that reason, retrieve, and act across multiple systems
- Client-facing AI prototypes and solutions across consumer, defence, energy, financial, government, health, and transport sectors
- LLM-powered workflows with production deployment and observability
- Cloud-native AI solution deployment and evaluation
