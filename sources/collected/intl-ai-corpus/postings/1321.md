## Description complète

Datadog is building the platform that empowers engineers to
observe, understand, and act on their systems. As we expand
into the world of intelligent systems, our Simulation
Environments team is pioneering high-fidelity, synthetic
environments to safely train and evaluate AI agents. As a
Staff Engineer on this team, you’ll play a foundational role
in designing and scaling this simulation platform. You'll
collaborate across AI research and product teams to deliver
environments where AI agents can learn, reason, and take
action—pushing the boundaries of observability and
automation.

At Datadog, we place value in our office culture - the
relationships and collaboration it builds and the creativity
it brings to the table. We operate as a hybrid workplace to
ensure our Datadogs can create a work-life harmony that best
fits them.

What You’ll Do:

- Design and build high-fidelity simulation environments that
  model complex distributed systems with realism and
  scalability
- Develop synthetic data generation tools and evaluation
  pipelines to train and assess AI agents across Datadog
  products
- Collaborate with AI research and product engineering teams
  to integrate models into simulation and production
  workflows
- Define architecture and technical direction for Datadog’s
  emerging simulation platform
- Mentor engineers and contribute to shaping the culture of a
  new, cross-functional team

Who You Are:

- You have a BS, MS, PhD, or equivalent experience in Computer
  Science, Engineering, or a related technical field
- Strong experience in distributed systems, backend, or
  infrastructure engineering
- Hands-on experience building or deploying ML/AI systems,
  beyond consumption of existing models
- Familiarity with synthetic data, simulation frameworks, or
  generative modeling approaches
- Proven ability to turn ambiguity into clarity and define new
  systems from the ground up
- Skilled communicator and team leader, especially in cross-
  functional and early-stage environments
