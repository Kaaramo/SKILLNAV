Job Description

For Visa to deliver on the vision of the best way to pay and
be paid for everyone, everywhere, the Operations &
Infrastructure (O&I) group must provide best-in-class
infrastructure services that are secure, scalable,
continuously available, and run on software-defined
infrastructures. The O&I group offers an exciting
opportunity to work on latest technologies where the
industry is going through major innovations such as cloud
computing. As part of O&I, the PaaS Middleware Engineering
team provides comprehensive design, build, and support
services for web and middle-tier platforms.

The selected candidate will join the PaaS Middleware
Engineering team, working alongside other engineers to
design, build, support (L3), and enhance internal and
external middleware services built on various Middleware and
Integration platforms.

We are seeking a highly skilled and motivated Middleware
Engineer to join our team. The ideal candidate will have
extensive experience working with middleware technologies,
specifically Hazelcast, Qlik-Striim, Flink and Temporal. The
Middleware Engineer will be responsible for designing,
building, and supporting middleware solutions that enable
seamless integration and high performance of our business-
critical applications.

- Design and Development:
  - Design and develop middleware solutions using Hazelcast,
    Qlik, Flink, Temporal, and Striim to meet the business
    requirements.
  - Collaborate with application developers, architects, and
    other stakeholders to design scalable and efficient
    middleware architectures.
- Implementation and Integration:
  - Implement and integrate middleware components to ensure
    seamless data flow and communication between different
    systems and applications.
  - Configure and fine-tune middleware platforms for optimal
    performance and reliability.
- Support and Maintenance:
  - Provide Level 3 support for middleware platforms, addressing
    complex issues and ensuring high availability and
    stability of the systems.
  - Perform regular maintenance tasks, including software
    updates, patches, and performance tuning.

This is a hybrid position. Expectation of days in office
will be confirmed by your hiring manager.
