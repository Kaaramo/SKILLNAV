About the Role

As a Developer Efficiency Engineer in SBU Connectivity, you
will contribute hands‑on engineering solutions that improve
software development efficiency across detailed design, unit
construction, and unit verification. You will work on
automation, internal tooling, and AI‑assisted productivity
improvements that help development teams reduce manual
effort and improve consistency and quality.

This role is suited for a technically strong software
engineer who enjoys solving workflow and tooling problems,
has a good understanding of the software development
lifecycle, and is motivated to improve developer experience
through practical, engineer‑driven solutions.

What You Will Do

Process & Workflow Efficiency

- Analyze software development workflows related to design,
  implementation, and unit verification to identify
  inefficiencies and improvement opportunities.
- Propose and implement practical, engineering‑driven
  improvements to development practices and workflows.
- Apply engineering judgment to balance productivity, quality,
  and compliance needs.

Automation & AI-Based Productivity

- Leverage AI‑based productivity tools to improve developer
  workflows, consistency, and efficiency where they provide
  clear value.
- Assess the impact of solutions using measurable indicators

  quality gains.

Metrics & Engineering Insights

- Create and maintain dashboards and reports that provide
  visibility into development efficiency, throughput, and

- Use data from development tools to support technical
  decisions and improvement proposals.

Continuous Improvement
