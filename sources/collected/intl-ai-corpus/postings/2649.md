## Responsabilités

- Design, build, and deploy custom Copilot agents using Microsoft Copilot Studio and Azure AI Foundry
- Architect and implement AI solutions integrating enterprise data across Microsoft 365, SharePoint, and Power Platform
- Develop custom connectors, grounding data pipelines, and AI orchestration logic to extend Copilot capabilities
- Leverage Azure OpenAI, Azure Cognitive Search, and Azure AI Services to deliver RAG-based agent solutions
- Build and optimize prompt flows, semantic memory, and contextual grounding for AI agents
- Develop automation workflows using Power Automate integrated with AI and enterprise data sources

- Collaborate with business teams to identify use cases and translate them into scalable AI applications, including building POCs and prototypes
