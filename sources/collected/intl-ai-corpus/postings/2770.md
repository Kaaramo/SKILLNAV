About the Role:

As a Senior(+) Engineer (Typically equivalent to Staff or Sr
Staff titles in other companies), you'll join this Core
Platform Team with bottom-up visibility and ownership with a
fast-expanding codebase (aimed at AI-based Detection &
Response) and a far-reaching edict for service resiliency,
with several key feature additions and critical re-
architecture initiatives underway: Deployment of core
services to several new clouds, modularizing into reusable
components, improvements to core libraries, frameworks,
observability tooling (tracing, profiling, alerting, SLOs
etc), and automation to reduce manual toil in all the above.



Recently this group introduced adaptive concurrency to our
core kafka library which implements flow control to protect
database performance. In terms of overarching goals, it
builds and maintains the next-generation infrastructure and
security platform for CrowdStrike which include an
application and service delivery platform, massively
scalable distributed data storage and replication systems,
and a cutting-edge search and distributed graph system. Help
us scale CrowdStrike’s infrastructure and products to handle
massive growth in traffic and data as we continue to
experience dramatic growth in the usage of our products.



Crowdstrike Falcon is THE industry standard in cloud-native
cyber security and threat hunting, processing Trillions of
events/day. You’ll be positioned for optimal widespread
impact with  bottom-up ownership of our core codebase,
driving architectural choices that affect every feature
development team, providing shared architectural components
to be leveraged throughout the Falcon Platform (things like
Unified Search, Protobuf libraries and other shared-tier
architectural components).



What You'll Do:

- Develop and maintain services to meet reliability and
  scalability demands
- Extend and build new libraries for cross-cutting concerns
  that comprise/extend to the entirety of CrowdStrike's
  cloud platform, which consists of hundreds of libraries
  and services.
- Constantly re-evaluate our product to improve architecture,
  knowledge models, user experience, performance and
  stability.
- Provide technical leadership and guidance for large-scale
  architectural changes and complex incident management.
- Lead initiatives around reliability, scalability,
  performance, and cost-efficiency in distributed systems.
- Design and implement automation tools to improve the
  reliability and efficiency of our infrastructure
- Develop and enhancement monitoring services.
- Use and give back to the open source community.
- Evangelize best practices for building software, especially
  as pertains to Go.
- Obsess about learning, and champion the newest technologies
  & tricks with others, raising the technical IQ of the
  team.
- Drive strategic decisions and influence infrastructure and
  operational improvements across the organization.
- Brainstorm, define, and build collaboratively with members
  across multiple teams
- Be an energetic ‘self-starter’ with the ability to take
  ownership and be accountable for deliverable.



What You'll Need:

- 7+ years of full-time experience in a service-oriented
  backend developer role
- 5+ years developing microservices for a SaaS product, in one
  of the following object-oriented languages: Golang, Java,
  Scala, Kotlin, Python, Node.js.
- Degree in Computer Science (or commensurate experience in
  data structures/algorithms/distributed systems).
- Expert-level knowledge of Golang
- The ability to scale backend systems – sharding,
  partitioning, scaling horizontally are second nature to

- The desire to ship code and the love of seeing your bits run
  in production.
- Deep understanding of distributed systems and scalability

- Team player skills – we embrace collaborating as a team as

- A thorough understanding of engineering best practices from

- The ability to thrive in a fast paced, test-driven,

- The skills to meet your commitments on time and produce high

Bonus Points:
