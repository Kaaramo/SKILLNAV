## Description complète

About Foundation Health

Foundation Health is transforming healthcare through an AI-
powered digital pharmacy platform that seamlessly connects
operational infrastructure with high quality patient
experiences. Our mission is to transform patient centric
care by connecting fragmented infrastructure, optimizing
care coordination, and removing friction from the patient
journey.

This ambitious vision is only achievable with the dedication
of the right team propelling us forward. We firmly believe
that a supportive and inspiring work environment fuels
creativity, transforming it into groundbreaking innovation.
It is this very innovation that not only benefits our
organization but also positively impacts our people,
partners, and most importantly, our patients.

The Role

As a Senior AI Engineer at Foundation Health, you will play
a pivotal role in designing and delivering production-grade
AI solutions that power and differentiate our product suite.
This role combines technical leadership with solution
architecture, requiring not just strong engineering
capabilities, but also the ability to understand customer
needs, identify opportunities, and design scalable,
reliable, and compliant AI systems that create business
value.

You’ll work cross-functionally with product, engineering,
sales, and operations teams, and engage directly with
enterprise clients to translate their needs into AI-powered
solutions. This is a high-impact role, ideal for someone
eager to own entire solution verticals and lead
implementation end-to-end from concept and experimentation
to deployment and optimization.

We are a lean, fast-paced team, and you’ll be joining us at
a foundational stage, playing a key role in shaping our AI
strategy, infrastructure, and internal automation efforts.
Your contributions will directly influence how we build,
deploy, and scale intelligent systems, while also helping
define our engineering culture and technical practices.
While we primarily work remotely, we value strong
collaboration and make time for regular in-person connection
to stay aligned and build momentum as a team.

This is a high-impact individual contributor role, ideal for
someone focused on deep technical and functional growth.
While future leadership opportunities may arise, this role
is primarily for those who want to own and deliver impactful
work hands-on.

What You'll Be Doing

As Senior Engineer focused on AI you’ll:

- Lead the design and implementation of production-grade AI
  systems across diverse problem spaces (e.g.,
  classification, personalization, generation, search,
  forecasting).
- Work across the stack: data pipelines, model training,
  evaluation, deployment, and monitoring.
- Collaborate with product and engineering teams to apply
  ML/AI effectively to real-world use cases, balancing
  ambition with practicality.
- Define and evolve our ML infrastructure and tooling: model
  tracking, CI/CD for ML, serving patterns, A/B testing,
  etc.
- Build lightweight prototypes to test ideas, and production
  systems to scale impact.
- Participate in code reviews, technical planning, and
  architectural decisions
- Share on-call responsibilities for the systems you build

Who We're Looking For

We’re looking for candidates with strong real-world
experience building AI systems that ship and deployed to add
value. You should be confident across the stack, from
experimentation to production.

Essential

- 6+ years of total software development experience
- 4+ years of professional experience building and deploying
  ML/AI systems
- Solid programming experience in Python (e.g., pandas,
  scikit-learn, PyTorch, etc.) and familiarity with modern
  backend stacks (e.g., Typescript, Node.js, Go, or
  similar). Excellent writing and editing skills, with the
  ability to create clear, compelling content tailored to
  professional audiences
- Strong understanding of machine learning principles
  (supervised/unsupervised learning, evaluation,
  overfitting, data leakage, etc.)
- Experience deploying models in production environments using
  cloud infrastructure (GCP preferred).
- Familiarity with containerized workflows (Docker preferred)
  and serverless architectures
- Exposure to modern MLOps tooling (e.g., MLflow, Vertex AI,
  SageMaker, or equivalents).
- Comfortable working autonomously in a remote-first team,
  with excellent written and verbal communication skills.
- Available to travel to the US for onsite client meetings

Desirable

- Experience across multiple AI subdomains: NLP,
  recommendation systems, time series, or computer vision
- Experience with retrieval-augmented generation (RAG) or
  integrating with foundation models (OpenAI desirable)
- Experience in event-driven systems and streaming data
  architectures
- Familiarity with A/B testing and experimentation platforms
- Exposure to regulated industries, especially healthcare
  (HIPAA, HL7, FHIR, etc.) - a bonus, not a requirement
- Experience with ethical AI practices, model fairness, and
  explainability tooling.

Benefits we offer

- Life assurance at 4x your annual salary
- Auto-enrolment in the company pension scheme
- 25 days of paid holiday plus bank holidays
- 10 full paid sick days in addition to statutory sick pay
- Flexible working arrangements
- Opportunity with a path to move to the US
