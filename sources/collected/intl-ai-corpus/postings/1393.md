### Raisonnement classification (LLM upstream)

This is a DevOps Engineer role focused on infrastructure and
platform support for an AI team. The responsibilities
involve deploying and managing infrastructure, CI/CD
pipelines, and observability for AI/LLM solutions, but not
building, training, or fine-tuning models directly. The role
enables the AI/ML work of others by providing the underlying
cloud infrastructure, automation, and deployment systems.
