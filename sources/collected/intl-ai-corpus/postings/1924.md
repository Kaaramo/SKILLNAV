The Role

As a Senior ML/AI Engineer, you’ll build and deploy the
intelligent systems at the core of Merciv. Our platform
doesn’t just surface insights. It reasons, forecasts, and
acts autonomously across complex enterprise data landscapes.
You’ll develop the models and agentic architectures that
power demand forecasting, consumer intelligence, competitive
analysis, and autonomous decision-making for the world’s
largest retailers.

This is applied AI at its most impactful. You’ll work at the
intersection of cutting-edge research and real enterprise
deployment, building systems that generate tens of millions
in value for our clients.

What You’ll Do

- Design, build, and deploy ML models for demand forecasting,
  time series prediction, consumer sentiment analysis, and
  anomaly detection at enterprise scale
- Develop and iterate on Merciv’s agentic AI architecture
  (building systems that reason across heterogeneous data
  sources and take autonomous action)
- Build and maintain robust ML pipelines: data preprocessing,
  feature engineering, model training, evaluation, and
  production deployment
- Architect RAG systems and LLM integrations that power
  Merciv’s natural language interfaces and autonomous
  workflows
- Collaborate with backend engineers to ensure models are
  production-grade (optimized for latency, reliability, and
  scale)
- Own model performance end-to-end: monitoring, retraining,
  and continuous improvement in production
- Stay at the frontier of AI research and bring relevant
  innovations into our platform

Who You Are

- 5+ years of experience in applied machine learning and AI,

- M.S. or Ph.D. in Computer Science, Machine Learning,
