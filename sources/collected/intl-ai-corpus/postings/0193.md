### Raisonnement classification (LLM upstream)

This is a safety and validation leadership role for
autonomous vehicle systems, focused on setting safety
standards, assurance processes, and validation strategies
for AI/ML models rather than building, training, or
deploying the models themselves. The role involves
establishing safety frameworks (ISO 8800, ISO 21448),
validating AI-driven systems, and providing technical
guidance to engineering teams - all support functions that
enable safe AI development rather than hands-on model work.
