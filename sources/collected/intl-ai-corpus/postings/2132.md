Role Overview

Roller is looking for a talented AI Engineer with 3-5 years
of experience to help build the AI platform and intelligent
automation systems enabling AI powered business verticals.
The ideal candidate will have hands-on experience designing
and deploying GenAI platform and solutions using modern LLM
frameworks, RAG architectures, and scalable AI pipelines.

Key Responsibilities

- Develop Enterprise GenAI platform and services for various
  business verticals using Python and modern frameworks such
  as LangChain.
- Design and implement Retrieval Augmented Generation (RAG)
  systems using vector databases and LLMs.
- Build scalable AI pipelines for ingestion, embedding,
  retrieval, and generation workflows.
- Integrate LLMs with enterprise data sources and APIs.
- Implement LLM evaluation frameworks to measure model
  performance, hallucination, and response quality.
- Establish AI governance and Guardrails practices including
  safety checks, monitoring, and responsible AI controls.
- Optimize prompt engineering strategies and model
  interactions for accuracy and performance.
- Implement observability for AI systems and build AI usage
  dashboards for prompt tracing, token usage monitoring,
  latency tracking, Agent usage and evaluation metrics.
- Collaborate with cross functional teams to deploy
  production-grade AI solutions.

Required Skills

- Strong programming experience in Python.
- Experience with Agentic AI framework such as LangGraph
- Hands-on experience building RAG-based applications.
- Experience working with Vector Databases (Pinecone,
  Weaviate, OpenSearch, etc.).
- Knowledge of AI pipeline architecture and model
  orchestration.
- Experience with LangChain or similar LLM orchestration
  frameworks.
- Experience implementing LLM evaluation frameworks and
  benchmarking methods.
- Understanding of AI governance, responsible AI, and model
  guardrails.

Nice to Have skills

- Experience with workflow automation tools such as n8n.
