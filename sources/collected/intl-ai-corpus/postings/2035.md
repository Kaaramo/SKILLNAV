## Description complète

﻿Who We Are?

The 4M story is one you likely haven’t heard before. We're
on a mission to create a global map of the subsurface -
doing for the world below ground what Google Maps did for
the world above. Achieving this vision requires cutting-edge
products and technology, which come to life through strong,
collaborative teams of talented people who thrive on
innovation and tackling complex challenges.

The Opportunity

As the AI Revenue Operations Engineer, you will serve as the
AI nerve center for 4M’s entire go-to-market organization.
Reporting directly to the CRO, you will be responsible for
identifying, deploying, and scaling the most impactful AI
capabilities across every corner of the revenue team — from
the way we research prospects and run pipeline reviews to
how leaders communicate strategy and reps execute in the
field. This is not a support role. You will shape how 4M
competes in an AI-first world by staying on the cutting edge
of what’s possible, translating that into practical
capabilities the team can use today, and building the
systems that make AI an enduring competitive advantage.

Key Responsibilities

﻿

AI Strategy & GTM Innovation

- Own 4M’s GTM AI roadmap, continuously scanning the AI
  landscape to identify emerging tools, models, and
  workflows that can create an unfair advantage for the
  revenue team.
- Serve as the organization’s primary expert on frontier AI
  capabilities, with deep hands-on fluency in large language
  models (especially Claude), agentic workflows, and AI-
  native sales and marketing tooling.
- Define the architecture for how AI integrates across the GTM
  stack, from CRM enrichment and forecasting to outreach
  automation, content generation, and deal intelligence.
- Build proof-of-concepts quickly, evaluate real-world impact
  rigorously, and scale what works across the full team.

﻿Leader & Team Enablement

- Act as a hands-on AI coach and enablement partner for CRO
  and GTM leadership - ensuring leaders are equipped with
  AI-powered workflows, decision-support tools, and real-
  time intelligence to lead more effectively.
- Design and deliver enablement programs that build genuine AI
  fluency across the GTM team, driving measurable adoption
  and behavior change at every level — not just awareness.
- Create AI-powered templates, playbooks, and automation that
  reduce friction in high-volume workflows: account
  research, outreach personalization, pipeline preparation,
  QBR storytelling, and more.
- Partner with sales, marketing, and customer success
  leadership to embed AI into their day-to-day rhythms, so
  that using AI becomes the default, not the exception.
- Run regular lunch-and-learns, working sessions, and one-on-
  ones to surface what’s working, unblock adoption, and
  introduce new capabilities as they emerge.

﻿AI-Powered Revenue Operations

- Own the GTM tech stack with an AI-first lens - evaluating,
  implementing, and continuously optimizing tools across
  sales automation, forecasting, CRM enrichment, competitive
  intelligence, and customer lifecycle management.
- Build and maintain AI-augmented reporting infrastructure
  that gives leadership real-time visibility into pipeline
  health, conversion trends, and GTM performance - surfacing
  insights automatically rather than requiring manual
  analysis.
- Develop AI-assisted forecasting and territory planning
  processes that make revenue predictions faster, more
  accurate, and more actionable.
- Maintain data integrity across CRM and GTM systems,
  establishing governance standards that ensure AI outputs
  are trustworthy and decision-grade.
- Continuously identify manual, repetitive GTM workflows and
  replace them with intelligent automation — freeing the
  team to focus on high-judgment, high-value work.

﻿CRO Partnership & Strategic Execution

- Serve as a trusted execution partner to the CRO - leveraging
  AI to dramatically accelerate the preparation of board
  materials, pipeline reviews, QBRs, and executive
  briefings.
- Use AI tools to synthesize signals from across the business
  - win/loss data, customer feedback, market trends,
  competitive moves - into crisp strategic recommendations.
- Manage cross-functional GTM initiatives from inception
  through execution, using AI-powered project management,
  communication, and tracking to move faster with fewer
  bottlenecks.
- Help the CRO stay on the cutting edge by serving as an
  internal resource on what’s new in AI, translating
  developments in the AI space into strategic implications
  for 4M.

- 2-5 years of experience in GTM operations, revenue strategy,
  or sales enablement within a high-growth B2B technology
  company - with a clear and demonstrable focus on AI
  adoption and innovation.
- Deep, hands-on expertise with AI tools in a GTM context —
  including proficiency with Claude (Anthropic) for account
  research, proposal development, competitive analysis,
  content generation, and workflow automation; experience
  with other frontier LLMs and AI-native GTM platforms is
  strongly preferred.
- A proven track record of enabling GTM teams on new AI tools
  and workflows, driving measurable adoption and lasting
  behavior change - not just rolling out software, but
  changing how people work.
- Genuine curiosity about and enthusiasm for the AI landscape
  - you follow model releases, read the research, experiment
  relentlessly, and bring what you learn back to the team.
- Strong ability to translate complex AI capabilities into
  practical, accessible workflows that non-technical GTM
  professionals can use confidently and consistently.
- Experience designing and building agentic AI workflows,
  prompt libraries, or custom AI integrations within CRM or
  GTM systems is a significant plus.
- Proficiency with CRM platforms (e.g., Salesforce, HubSpot),
  sales engagement tools, and analytics platforms; comfort
  with APIs and no-code/low-code automation tools.
- Exceptional communication skills - able to influence senior
  stakeholders, run engaging enablement sessions, and
  translate AI concepts clearly for audiences at every
  level.
- A bias toward action: you build fast, test quickly, learn
  from results, and iterate without waiting for perfect
  conditions.
