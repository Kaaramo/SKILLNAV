### Raisonnement classification (LLM upstream)

This is a Developer Advocate role focused on promoting
MongoDB's AI capabilities to the community, not building AI
systems. The role involves teaching, creating content,
speaking at events, and helping developers use MongoDB's AI
features like vector search. While it requires AI/ML
expertise and experience building AI solutions, the day-to-
day work is advocacy and education, not hands-on AI/ML
development.

## Description complète

We are looking for an AI Developer Advocate to join our
Developer Relations team. In this role, you will help
promote MongoDB's artificial intelligence (AI) capabilities.
As more developers choose MongoDB as their data platform,
your responsibilities will include directly engaging with
the community, building strategic partnerships with AI
companies, creating inspiring resources and content, and
collaborating with a global team of skilled Developer
Advocates.

You will empower developers—from startups to Fortune 500
companies—to integrate advanced data and AI capabilities
into their applications and solutions. If you are passionate
about helping builders explore the possibilities of AI and
enjoy cultivating a vibrant developer ecosystem, we invite
you to join us in shaping the future of development on
MongoDB.

Our Developer Relations teams are distributed across the
globe, with most members working remotely. This position
will be based in the San Francisco Bay Area. To see what our
team is doing, check out our content on YouTube, Dev, and
Medium.

We are looking to speak to candidates who are based in San
Francisco for our hybrid working model.

Candidate Profile

We are excited to welcome an influential individual to our
team who is already a well-established voice in AI. From
your experience over the past few years, we’ll see you in
videos and at events sharing technical insights, leading
discussions, and inspiring others to effectively use AI and
ML capabilities. You’ve also created articles, tutorials,
and hands-on workshops to teach builders best practices and
equip them to leverage AI in their apps.

At the foundation of your passion and expertise is a love to
equip others. Our ideal candidate will have at least 3 years
of experience in a builder role, such as a data scientist or
developer, where they crafted AI solutions in production.
They will have 8+ total years of experience through any
combination of builder role (such as data scientist or
developer), educator role (such as developer advocate or
developer educator), or customer-facing role (such as
solution architect).

Our ideal candidate will also have connections with AI
companies and communities in the San Francisco Bay Area. You
will work with key AI partners to create content and partner
on virtual and in-person events that showcase how MongoDB is
part of the larger AI ecosystem.

You are an effective teacher who can explain complex topics
in a simple and easy-to-understand way. Along with an
appreciation for the key AI-related features of our
platform, such as vector search and Voyage AI, you will also
develop a strong understanding of MongoDB's core
capabilities, data modeling, and emerging trends in our
industry.

Though the expertise you bring will be unique, our team is
built to work together to share knowledge and help each
other grow. You will work with others at all levels,
supporting peer reviews of code, demos, sessions, and other
content. You should have a genuine passion for equipping
other developers to be more productive and successful.

Position Expectations

Below are examples of the types of work you will be doing.
Your mix of activities will vary week to week based on
priorities and event schedules.

- Build on a solid foundation of ML/AI expertise, adding to
  your database experience to become a MongoDB expert
- Expand your knowledge of embedding models and rerankers to
  become an expert in Voyage AI
- Collaborate with our events and field teams to engage
  developers and founders in the Bay Area at meetups,
  events, hackathons, and startup houses
- Speak at strategic AI events and lead workshops at high-
  profile conferences such as AWS re:Invent
- Create and update tutorials, articles, videos, and workshops
  along with their associated workflows or workflows or code
  examples
- Provide feedback to our Product and Technology team, serving
  as the voice of the developer as new products are scoped
  and planned
- Regularly interface with Partner Managers to identify
  opportunities to work with key AI partners to create
  content and partner on virtual and in-person events that
  showcase how MongoDB is part of the larger AI ecosystem
- Partner with our community team to support engagement with
  third-party and first-party user groups (MUGs), Champions,
  and influencer programs across the region
- Lead one-hour sessions (both virtual and in-person) with
  customer teams to evaluate their AI use cases and provide
  recommendations on how they can better utilize MongoDB

Requirements

- Expertise in building AI or ML solutions and comfortable
  building agentic solutions
- Advanced familiarity with several AI coding and workflow
  tools, and how platforms are recommended through content,
  preference, and integration
- Expertise with one or more complementary programming
  languages (Python or JavaScript preferred)
- Knowledgeable and connected in the Bay Area AI ecosystem
- Prolific author of content (tutorials, how-to guides,
  readmes) and associated code to help developers learn
- Established portfolio or active contributor to AI examples
  and Agent Skills in GitHub
- Engaging presenter at in-person events, videos, webinars, or
  live streams
- Up to 25% of time traveling to Bay Area engagements (like
  meetups, events, hackathons, and startup houses)
- Up to 10% of time traveling outside of the Bay Area for
  industry events
