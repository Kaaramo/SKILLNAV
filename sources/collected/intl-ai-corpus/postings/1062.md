Description About the Team: Our Data & Analytics team

- AI Agentic Model Development: Build intelligent agents using

  enhance analytics.
- Semantic Modeling & Dashboard Design: Create optimized
  semantic layers and interactive dashboards using Power BI
  and Snowflake.

You'll collaborate cross-functionally to deliver scalable,
high-impact solutions that improve efficiency, consistency,
and business outcomes. What you get to do in this role:

- Translate business questions into scalable BI solutions in
  partnership with Sales Operations and Analytics teams.
- Support and guide stakeholders in developing optimized
  reports and dashboards.
- Design and maintain semantic models in Power BI and
  Snowflake for analytics and LLM integration.
- Develop ETL/ELT pipelines to ensure data readiness and
  quality for self-service analytics.
- Optimize Snowflake objects (SQL scripts, views, procedures,
  UDFs, tasks/streams) for performance and reliability.
- Implement row-level security (RLS), incremental refresh, and
  performance tuning.
- Conduct data quality checks, write unit tests, and
  contribute to documentation and runbooks.
- Prepare enterprise data for LLM consumption with semantic
  models and prompt-friendly outputs.
