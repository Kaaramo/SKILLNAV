## Description complète

Welcome to the future of cloud networking and security!

Cato Networks is the first company to converge enterprise
networking and security into one centralized and global
service that is delivered by cloud. It is led by networking
and security pioneer Shlomo Kramer (Check Point, Imperva)
and early investor (Palo Alto Networks, Exabeem, Trusteer
and more). Cato’s unique technology inspired a brand-new
product category, later named “SASE” by Gartner and a market
expected to reach $28.5 billion by 2028. This is your
opportunity to get on the rocket ship and join a company
that is building a cutting-edge enterprise network and
secure cloud platform, and is on a fast track to becoming
the worldwide market leader – don’t miss it!

We are looking for a unique, hands-on Machine Learning / AI
Developer to design and implement AI-powered solutions that
improve business processes across the company. This role
focuses on delivering custom-built tools and workflows that
off-the-shelf products cannot provide, working closely with
multiple departments such as Customer Success, Sales, HR,
and more.



Responsibilities:

- Collaborate with stakeholders across global teams to
  identify opportunities where AI can enhance business
  processes.
- Perform deep research and analysis on organizational data to
  understand its structure, quality, biases, and
  limitations, ensuring AI solutions meet business
  expectations.
- Design, develop, and deploy AI-based solutions tailored to
  organizational needs (e.g., customer success agents, HR
  process automation, sales optimization).
- Build workflows, agents, and ML pipelines, ensuring
  solutions are reliable, scalable, and production-ready.
- Integrate diverse data sources and leverage multiple data
  types to create actionable insights.
- Work closely with business units' management to address
  specific operational challenges.

Requirements:

- 6+ years of hands-on software development experience with a
  focus on AI/ML applications.
- Strong proficiency in Python and experience with ML
  workflows and MLOps.
- Familiarity with workflow orchestration tools and/or process
  automation tools.
- Solid understanding of data integration and data-driven
  solution design.
- Ability to work cross-functionally and interface effectively
  with multiple departments.
- Strong initiative and problem-solving skills, with the
  ability to independently gather requirements and propose
  solutions.
- Passion for AI-driven process optimization, ideally in
  business domains as mentioned above.
- Familiarity with cloud platforms and modern AI frameworks is
  an advantage.
- Strong research skills focused on data analysis — ability to
  investigate datasets, validate data quality, identify
  inconsistencies or gaps, and ensure the data foundation
  supports high-quality AI solutions.
