## Description complète

Jahnel Group’s mission is to provide the absolute best
environment for software creators to pursue their passion by
connecting them with great clients doing meaningful work.
This is a full time position with one of our closest
clients.

Who We're Looking For

The Senior Backend Engineer (AI/Data Platform) will join a
high-caliber engineering team building the foundation of an
AI-powered procurement platform. This individual will take
the lead in architecting scalable systems that integrate
LLMs and manage complex data ingestion pipelines. You’ll
work closely with the founding team to shape technical
direction, contribute to core product development, and help
define engineering culture at a fast-paced, early-stage
startup.

This is a high-impact role for someone with deep backend
experience, exposure to AI/LLM technologies, and a passion
for building intelligent, data-driven systems.

Primary Responsibilities

- Backend System Architecture and Implementation: Designs and
  implements scalable systems for data ingestion,
  transformation, and AI workflows using Python, NestJS, and
  PostgreSQL.
- LLM Integration and Workflow Orchestration: Integrates LLM
  APIs (OpenAI, Anthropic, etc.) into production
  environments using frameworks like LangChain to enable
  intelligent sourcing decisions.
- API Development and Data Pipelines: Owns the development and
  maintenance of REST APIs, services, and background jobs
  while building robust ETL pipelines and analytics-ready
  data formats.
- Infrastructure Automation and Deployment: Implements
  infrastructure-as-code using Terraform and manages
  deployments via AWS (ECS, Lambda, S3, API Gateway).
- Performance Optimization and Reliability: Optimizes data
  models and queries, ensures system reliability, and leads
  initiatives to enhance observability, monitoring, and
  CI/CD practices.
- Team Collaboration and Mentorship: Works cross-functionally
  with product and AI teams to ship impactful features and
  provides mentorship to junior engineers, driving best
  practices.
- Architectural Decision Making: Makes critical technical
  decisions to ensure system scalability, performance, and
  extensibility as the platform evolves.
- Other duties and projects as assigned

Some Must-Haves

- 5+ years of backend or full-stack engineering experience,
  preferably in high-growth or startup environments.
- Strong proficiency in Python and Node.js (NestJS).
- Deep experience with PostgreSQL, including data modeling and
  query optimization.
- Hands-on integration of LLM APIs such as OpenAI or Anthropic
  into real-world applications.
- Familiarity with LangChain or similar LLM orchestration
  frameworks.
- Proven experience building scalable data pipelines and ETL
  systems.
- Infrastructure expertise with AWS services (ECS, Lambda, S3,
  API Gateway).
- Proficiency with Terraform and Docker.
- Solid understanding of CI/CD pipelines and testing
  frameworks.
- Excellent problem-solving and debugging skills.
- Strong communication, collaboration, and ownership mindset.

Where We're Looking For It

- New York
