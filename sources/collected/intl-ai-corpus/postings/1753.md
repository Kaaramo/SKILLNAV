## Description complète

About FLORA

We build creative tools for the new creative class—an
intelligent canvas where the best AI models enable
professional craft. We're a team of ~20, backed by
exceptional investors like Hanabi Capital, Menlo Ventures,
Justin Kan, and Gabe Whaley (founder of MSCHF). Our
customers span a variety of creative industries and include
Pentagram, Lionsgate, and Nike.

We’re looking to bring on an Applied AI Engineer who will
architect and build the agentic systems powering FLORA.

As part of the founding team, you will work closely with the
founders to invent how creative professionals interact with
AI in a generative age. This is a first-principles role:
you’ll design, implement, and scale the backbone of agentic
workflows that let creatives orchestrate text, image, and
video models seamlessly on FLORA’s infinite canvas.

What You’ll Do As an Applied AI Engineer, you’ll design and
build the intelligence layer of FLORA — systems that make
multi-modal creative workflows powerful, reliable, and
intuitive. You’ll work on problems at the intersection of
AI, orchestration, and interaction design: bringing together
text, image, and video models into seamless workflows that
creatives can actually trust and enjoy using.

You’ll collaborate across product, infra, and design to
build agentic features that let creatives speak their idea
into existence while maintaining control.

Requirements

- Hands-on experience building agentic systems — e.g. multi-
  step pipelines, tool-using agents, assistants with memory,
  or similar.
- Familiarity with evaluation frameworks (automated checks,
  regression testing, human-in-the-loop scoring) to ensure
  system reliability.
- Experience with memory and retrieval (vector DBs,
  embeddings, summarization, context management).
- Strong background in orchestration across models, APIs, and
  tools — including handling retries, fallbacks, and edge
  cases.
- Comfortable reasoning about multi-modal workflows (text,
  image, video) and their integration in a user-facing
  product.
- Ability to work across the stack (frontend, backend, infra)
  and collaborate closely with product and design.
- Product-minded — you care as much about usability and
  creative control as you do about technical novelty.
- 3+ years of engineering experience
- Bonus: experience with creative tools, generative models, or
  node-based interfaces.

Working at FLORA

Office policy

We work in-person at the Domino Refinery building in
Williamsburg, Brooklyn—with views of the East River.

Compensation and benefits

Competitive salary and meaningful equity. We also offer an
Equinox membership and comprehensive health and dental
coverage.
