### Raisonnement classification (LLM upstream)

This role is fundamentally a data platform engineering
position that supports AI systems rather than building them
directly. The engineer builds data pipelines, canonical
models, and schemas that enable AI agents and RAG systems to
function, but does not train, fine-tune, or deploy ML
models. The core work is data engineering and ontology
design in service of downstream AI/LLM consumers.

The Role

As an AI-Native Data Platform Engineer at Farther, you will
design and own the canonical data foundations powering our
financial AI systems. This role sits at the core of our
platform — building the ontology, data contracts, and
reconciliation frameworks that enable intelligent agents to
operate safely and autonomously.

AI systems only perform as well as the structure beneath
them. You will architect custodial data pipelines, canonical
financial models, and AI-ready schemas that support
embeddings, retrieval systems, and agent-driven workflows in
a regulated wealth management environment.

We are building autonomous agents that reason over and act
on platform state — and you will define the data layer that
makes that possible.
