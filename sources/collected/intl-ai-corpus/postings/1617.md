About the Team The AI Center of Excellence is a

who partner with Detection and Response teams to turn

- Data Science, your primary responsibility will be to drive
the end-to-end delivery of ML and LLM-based solutions on
AWS. Specifically, your focus will be to:

- Design and implement sophisticated ML pipelines for security
  use cases, including malware detection and user behavioral
  analytics.
- Architect and maintain RAG pipelines and agentic AI
  workflows using toolchains like LangChain and LangGraph to
  automate complex security tasks.
- Optimize and deploy models within the AWS ecosystem,
  utilizing SageMaker and Bedrock while ensuring robust
  monitoring, drift detection, and retraining workflows.
- Collaborate and mentor across the team to establish
  architectural standards, conduct code reviews, and support
  the technical growth of junior engineers.

The skills and qualities you'll bring include: Core Skills

- Technical Ownership: Ability to take a project from a
  conceptual prototype to a high-quality production system.
- Analytical Problem Solving: Expertise in applying scientific
  computing and statistical modeling to unlabeled, large-
  scale data.
- Continuous Learning: A proactive approach to mastering
  emerging technologies in LLMs, Generative AI, and agentic
  systems.

Functional Skills

- Python Proficiency: Advanced experience with numpy, pandas,
  and scientific computing libraries.
- Machine Learning Frameworks: Hands-on expertise with scikit-
  learn and Deep Learning frameworks (PyTorch or
  TensorFlow).
- Generative AI Toolchains: Proven experience with
  HuggingFace, Transformers, LangChain, and LangGraph.
- Cloud Infrastructure: Strong experience deploying and
  operating ML workloads on AWS (SageMaker, Bedrock, Lambda,
  EKS).
- Vector Databases & RAG: Practical experience designing and
  tuning Retrieval-Augmented Generation pipelines.

- Security Domain Knowledge: Familiarity with threat

  model transparency tools like SHAP or LIME.
- LLM Evaluation: Experience using evaluation frameworks such
