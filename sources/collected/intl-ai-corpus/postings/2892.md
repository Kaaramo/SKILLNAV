## Description complète

Senior Developer (Application Modernization / AI
Transformation)

Location: London, UK

Type: Full-time

Department: Technology

Job Summary

eClerx is seeking a Senior Developer to support a premier
global financial institution on a multi-year transformation
journey. This role sits within a global eClerx team (New
York, London) focused on modernizing legacy applications,
re-engineering platforms onto modern technology stacks, and
using AI-enabled development and testing tools to accelerate
analysis, migration, refactoring, testing, and delivery. The
ideal candidate is a senior software developer with strong
experience in software modernization, AI-assisted SDLC, LLM-
based solutions, and enterprise-grade application delivery.
This role is well suited for someone who enjoys solving
complex engineering problems and building practical AI
solutions in a regulated, large-scale environment.

Responsibilities

- Partner with business and technology stakeholders to
  identify opportunities for AI-driven automation and
  modernization.
- Analyze legacy applications, architecture, codebases, and
  integrations to define re-engineering and migration
  strategies.
- Leverage AI-enabled development tools to improve code
  analysis, refactoring, documentation, test generation, and
  migration productivity.
- Modernize legacy systems into modular, API-driven,
  microservices-based, or cloud-ready architectures.
- Perform hands-on development across .net, Python, Java, and
  Go, delivering scalable and maintainable solutions.
- Contribute to SDLC activities end to end, including design,
  development, testing, deployment, and release support.
- Strengthen CI/CD, automated testing, and DevOps practices to
  improve release quality and speed.
- Document solutions clearly and communicate recommendations
  to both technical and non-technical stakeholders.

Eligibility Requirements

- Technical experience, with a strong understanding of AI
  concepts, architectures, and methodologies.
- Software development experience, with strong exposure to
  enterprise application design, delivery, and
  modernization.
- Proven experience across the full SDLC, using AI-enabled
  development and testing tools for code analysis,
  refactoring, test generation, defect remediation, and
  delivery.
- Strong hands-on experience with LLMs, including RAG,
  LangChain, LlamaIndex, agentic AI frameworks, and vector
  database concepts.
- Experience building intelligent systems using Python,
  working knowledge of Java, .net and/or Golang preferred.
- Experience with legacy application transformation, including
  code remediation, platform migration, and architecture
  modernization.
- Good understanding of software architecture patterns, APIs,
  integration patterns, microservices, and event-driven
  design.
- Experience with automated testing, CI/CD pipelines, and
  DevOps practices in enterprise environments.
- Familiarity with graph databases such as Neo4j/Cypher and
  applying graph-based reasoning is preferred.
- Experience working with one of the cloud platforms such as
  Azure, AWS, or GCP and delivering secure, scalable
  solutions.
- Financial services experience preferred; ability to work
  effectively in Agile and global delivery teams is
  essential.
- Bachelor’s degree in Computer Science, Information
  Technology, Engineering, or a related field.
