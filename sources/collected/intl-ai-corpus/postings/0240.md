## Description complète

Join us as an AI Engineer (ComfyUI) and become a driving
force in scaling Zeely’s LLM initiatives and generative
capabilities. We are looking for a specialist who will build
a systematic approach to prompt creation and become a key
player in the development of our AI platform. You will be
responsible for creating scalable templates that work across
hundreds of different products, as well as testing new
generative models, identifying their use cases, and
integrating them into Zeely’s pipelines.

Zeely AI is a platform that uses artificial intelligence to
generate viral UGC videos and ad banners, as well as launch
effective ad campaigns on Facebook and Instagram, helping
small businesses become visible and scale.

We already have 1 million users in the US and are
consistently growing more than 4x year-over-year. Today,
Zeely is ranked among the Top-3 products in its category on
the US market, and our team has grown to over 130
specialists.

We are part of the Google for Startups Alumni community and
official partners of Meta and TikTok. In 2024, Zeely was
recognized by Forbes Ukraine as one of the Top 25 fastest-
growing startups.

Your key responsibilities:

- Design, build, and continuously optimize complex generative
  content pipelines across image, video, audio, and text
  formats.
- Configure and fine-tune generative model parameters to
  achieve an optimal balance between quality, consistency,
  and performance.
- Analyze, optimize, and improve existing workflows to
  increase stability, efficiency, and scalability in
  production environments.
- Develop and maintain advanced ComfyUI pipelines, including
  working with custom nodes.
- Proactively troubleshoot generation issues, quickly
  identifying root causes and implementing effective, long-
  term fixes.
- Extend and evolve the capabilities of internal generative
  systems to support new use cases and product needs.
- Collaborate closely with Product and Content teams to
  translate feature requirements into reliable, production-
  ready generative solutions.
- Continuously research, benchmark, and test new models and
  tools, staying on top of market trends and emerging
  technologies, including: Flux, Nano Banana, OpenAI, Kling
  AI, Hailuo MiniMax, Veo 3, Runway, Pika Labs.

What we’re looking for:

- 2+ years of hands-on experience in AI / Generative Systems
  Engineering, with a strong focus on prompt engineering
  within multi-step, production-grade generative pipelines.
- Practical experience working with generative models across
  multiple modalities, including: text-to-text, text-to-
  image, text-to-audio, video-to-video, and others.
- Strong expertise in ComfyUI as a core working environment,
  including developing custom nodes.
- Deep understanding of the generative AI ecosystem, including
  model architectures, strengths/limitations, inference
  behavior, and rapid evaluation of new releases.
- Practical experience integrating open-source models and
  tools into existing pipelines, including evaluation,
  optimization, and maintenance.
- Experience designing and configuring LLM-based agents or
  automation flows (prompt logic, chaining, orchestration,
  tool usage).
- High adaptability and strong learning velocity — ability to
  quickly test, validate, and adopt new models, tools, and
  techniques in a fast-moving AI landscape.
- Strong communication skills, with the ability to collaborate
  effectively with Product, Content, and Engineering teams,
  and clearly explain technical constraints and trade-offs.

Nice to have:

- Experience with an intuitive “vibe coding” approach to
  content generation.

Why it’s exciting to work with us:

- Work with cutting-edge technologies and contribute to
  building AI-powered tools.
- Join a strong A-level team of experienced professionals who
  set a high bar and push each other to do better work.
- Remote-first approach: work from anywhere, with access to
  our offices in Warsaw and Kyiv.
- We take care of the essentials. Paid time off, sick leaves,
  and financial support with private entrepreneurship
  matters.
- Regular performance reviews to ensure your growth is
  transparent and rewarded.
