## Description complète

Meet Ascertain

Ascertain is building AI agents to automate the
administrative work that burdens care teams. We are in major
health systems and large specialty groups, saving hundreds
of staff hours every week.

Our backers include Northwell Health, New York’s largest
health system, and Deerfield Management, a leading
healthcare investment firm.

Together, we’re on a mission to restore time, trust, and
focus to the people who keep healthcare running. Our work is
urgent — not because of startup timelines, but because our
customers rely on us to drive financial resilience and
operational clarity in a system under strain.

About the Job

As a Product Engineer - Voice AI at Ascertain, you will own
the development and optimization of voice agents that
automate healthcare back-office workflows. This is a hands-
on role: you'll configure and iterate on voice systems,
write production code when needed, and work directly with
customers and our operations team to deeply understand user
workflows and continuously improve performance.

You'll be responsible for building reliable voice agents,
establishing QA processes, analyzing call data, and using
what you learn to optimize every aspect of how we deliver
phone calls. Over time, you'll grow into owning the voice
agent strategy for the organization.

This role reports to the Head of Engineering and is based in
our NYC or can be remote for the right candidate.

What You'll Do

- Build, configure, and optimize voice agents using third-
  party platforms and in-house tooling
- Own quality assurance for voice interactions
- Work directly with our operations team and customers to
  understand workflows, gather feedback, and translate
  insights into product improvements
- Collaborate with engineers to build reliability and
  monitoring infrastructure as we scale
- Write production code in Python to support integrations and
  custom functionality
- Contribute to the broader product development process,
  bringing a user-centric perspective to everything we build

What You'll Need

- 3-4 years of experience in software engineering, with
  proficiency in Python
- A track record of being deeply user and product-centric
- Comfort with ambiguity and significant iteration; you're
  energized rather than frustrated by the process of getting
  something right through repeated refinement
- High attention to detail, especially when it comes to edge
  cases and quality
- Experience working cross-functionally with non-technical
  stakeholders
- Familiarity with FastAPI, telephony platforms, or voice AI
  tools is a plus
- Healthcare experience is preferred

What You'll Bring to the Table

- You're a builder who cares as much about the user experience
  as the technical implementation
- You take ownership of outcomes, not just tasks
- You communicate clearly and build trust with operations,
  engineering, and customers alike
- You're excited to work at the intersection of product and
  engineering, and you see this blended role as a feature,
  not a compromise
- You thrive in early-stage environments where scope evolves
  and you're expected to figure things out

Benefits & Perks

- Cutting-edge technology: Work directly with the latest
  advancements in generative AI and automation, tackling
  challenging problems that meaningfully impact healthcare
- Ownership & growth: You'll join Ascertain at a pivotal early
  stage, shaping our products, technical direction, and
  culture, with significant opportunities for career
  advancement
- Collaborative team: Join a talented, passionate, and
  supportive team with backgrounds from top tech companies
  and clinical institutions
- Flexible hybrid work: We support a flexible hybrid working
  model from our NYC office, trusting you to manage your own
  schedule
- Competitive compensation: We offer competitive salary,
  meaningful equity, comprehensive health insurance, and
  more

Why Ascertain? Make a real difference: Your work will
directly impact healthcare organizations and improve patient
care. Join Ascertain in transforming healthcare with AI!
