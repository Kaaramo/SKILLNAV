## Description complète

Chaos Labs is the first automated, on-chain economic
security system enabling crypto protocols to optimize risk
management and capital efficiency while safeguarding user
funds. We analyze protocol mechanics and scenario
implications using world-class custom EVM simulations to
provide actionable insights. Our platform is trusted by
leading protocols, including Aave, GMX, dYdX, Venus, Jupiter
and others, to manage and secure billions of dollars in
assets.

We are seeking a proactive and results-driven Full-Stack
Engineer to join our AI team. Reporting directly to the Head
of Product & AI you will play a critical role in working
closely with our CEO and AI team to develop our financial AI
agent.

Responsibilities:

- Design and build full-stack systems for our AI financial
  agent.
- Develop scalable APIs and backend services to support agent
  workflows.
- Build high-performance, user-facing interfaces to visualize
  agent reasoning and surface insights, as well as enable
  real-time interaction.
- Collaborate with AI researchers to integrate and
  productionize agent logic.
- Connect and manage data pipelines from financial sources.
- Implement testing, monitoring, and performance best
  practices.
- Stay up to date with LLM frameworks, full-stack tools, and
  financial trends.

- 5+ years of full-stack development experience.
- Strong proficiency in Python (FastAPI),
  JavaScript/TypeScript, Node.js, and React.
- Proven experience building scalable APIs and web
  applications.
- Solid knowledge of relational and non-relational databases.
- Hands-on experience with LLMs and frameworks like LangChain
  or similar.
- Familiarity with vector databases, RAG pipelines, and async
  programming.
- Experience with financial data and APIs.
- Comfortable working in fast-paced, self-directed
  environments.

.
