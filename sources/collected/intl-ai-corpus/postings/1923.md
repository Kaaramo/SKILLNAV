## Description complète

Join us in bringing joy to customer experience.  Five9 is a
leading provider of cloud contact center software, bringing
the power of cloud innovation to customers worldwide.

Living our values everyday results in our team-first culture
and enables us to innovate, grow, and thrive while enjoying
the journey together. We celebrate diversity and foster an
inclusive environment, empowering our employees to be their
authentic selves.

We are looking for Staff AI Forward Deployment Engineer to
establish and grow our presence in India. In this role, you
won't just be building chatbots; you will be the technical
architect and strategic lead for our most critical customer
deployments. You will bridge the gap between complex AI
research and real-world business impact, serving as the
technical face of Five9 for our clients in the region.

What You’ll Do

- Strategic Execution: Lead the design and deployment of
  sophisticated NLP models and conversational AI agents for
  high-stakes enterprise clients.
- Team Leadership: Mentor a team of AI engineers and data
  scientists, providing technical guidance, code reviews,
  and career development.
- Customer Partnership: Act as the primary technical point of
  contact for customers, translating vague business
  requirements into robust technical roadmaps.
- Data Strategy: Oversee the end-to-end data pipeline—from
  cleaning and organizing messy real-world text data to
  implementing advanced RAG (Retrieval-Augmented Generation)
  patterns.
- Optimization & Benchmarking: Define the standards for
  testing virtual agent performance, ensuring our models
  meet high accuracy and ethical AI standards.
- Cross-functional Collaboration: Work closely with US-based
  Engineering and Product teams to ensure the "voice of the
  customer" in India influences our global product roadmap.

What We’re Looking For

- NLP & LLM Expertise: Deep experience with NLP systems, Large
  Language Models (LLMs), and prompt engineering. You should
  know how to move beyond "out of the box" solutions to
  create production-grade AI.
- Statistical & Experimental Rigor: A mastery of A/B testing
  and model evaluation metrics. You don’t just build models;
  you prove they work using data.
- Consultative Mindset: Exceptional communication and
  consultative skills with a user-first mindset.

Requirements

- Education: Bachelor’s or Master’s degree in Computer
  Science, Data Science, or a related quantitative field.
- Experience: 6+ years of professional experience in Data
  Science/ML, with at least 1-2 years in a lead or senior
  capacity.
- Python Mastery: Expert-level Python skills and familiarity
  with the ML ecosystem (PyTorch, HuggingFace, LangChain, or
  similar).
- Customer-Facing Background: Proven track record of working
  directly with external clients or stakeholders to deliver
  technical products.
- Leadership Presence: Experience working with global teams
  across different time zones (specifically India and US).
