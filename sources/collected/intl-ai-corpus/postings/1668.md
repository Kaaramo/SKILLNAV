Job Description Summary:

Rocket's Data Modernization Business Unit aids customers in
managing, governing, and maximizing the value of their
enterprise data assets. Our clientele comprises many Fortune
500 companies, including some of the world's largest banks,
insurance companies, and manufacturing organizations. We are
currently seeking motivated and innovative individuals to
join our applied machine learning team. This is an
opportunity to play a crucial role in the continued
evolution of our world-class solutions. Data Scientist is a
highly motivated and experienced individual proficient in
advanced machine learning models, particularly language
models, document understanding and analysis, and code
generation. They will be responsible for understanding and
adapting current research. The position requires a deep
understanding of computational linguistics, image
processing, and Python programming. The selected candidate
will be part of a collaborative team dedicated to building,
fine-tuning, and deploying state-of-the-art models to solve
real-world problems in information management, data
intelligence, content automation, audit, and analytics. This
presents an opportunity to create tremendous business value
as we assemble a world-class applied machine learning team.

Essential Duties and Responsibilities:

· Consult with key internal and external stakeholders to
understand and frame model requirements and potential
applications.

· Design, develop, and deploy machine learning models using
tools and platforms optimized for distributed machine
learning.

· Recommend ongoing improvements to methods and algorithms
that lead to findings, including new information.

· Evaluate the applicability of current research to our
problem domain and software solutions.

· Collaborate closely with software engineers to integrate
the trained models into end-user applications.

· Develop sound analytic plans based on available data
sources, business partner needs, and required timelines.

· Manage deliverables across multiple projects in a
deadline-driven environment and projects related to a wide
variety of business settings.

· Present results, insights, and recommendations to both
technical and non-technical stakeholders.

Required Qualifications:

· 5+ years of industry experience.

· Experience in the professional management of junior data
scientists within projects and their continuous professional
development.

· Expertise in Python, Linux, and ML, familiarity with SQL.

· Demonstrated ability to find creative and practical
solutions to complex problems.

· Proven experience in applying Data Science methodologies
to extract, process, and transform data from multiple
sources.

· Demonstrated ability to deliver outputs in a comprehensive
format that highlights major trends, avoids
misinterpretations, and values the conclusions.

· A quick learner, highly adaptable to the needs of internal
collaborators in a dynamic environment.

· Efficiency in managing multiple projects.

· Excellent communication and interpersonal skills.

· Must be comfortable working in a team-oriented
environment.

Preferred Qualifications:

· Master’s degree in Statistics, Computer Science,
Mathematics, Economics, or directly related fields.

While all the above qualifications are important, we also
value candidates who display a strong passion for continued
learning, possess a spirit of innovation, and show high
