The Junior Product Manager will join the mission to
transform how Citi manages Wholesale Credit Risk (WCR),
utilizing Emerging Technologies, among others Generative and
traditional AI.

Institutional Credit Management (ICM) was formed to
transform the way Citi manages the extension of wholesale
credit to institutional clients. ICM is a global group
focused on re-imaging credit control process, policies, and
technology to manage the wholesale lending process end to
end. Within ICM we are responsible for building a
comprehensive platform that will create a user centric
experience that meets the needs across differing business
lines, regions, and regulatory demands.

With the new Emerging technologies available in current
market key responsibility of ICM Product Manager is to
manage current use cases and capabilities which utilizes
these new technologies within current platforms and
processes.

If you are an individual who takes ownership, strives for
client and operational excellence, and learns from different
perspectives to surpass stakeholder expectations, then this
is a unique leadership opportunity for you. This role will
work in collaboration with cross-functional and cross-
business stakeholders throughout the lifecycle of the
initiative. To be successful in this role, the candidate
will have previously worked in an agile focused development
organization, with experience driving technology change in
an iterative manner.  The candidate will be a seasoned user
experience design professional who can partner with
technology to transform WCR activities in scope for ICM.

In this role, you’re expected to perform:

Credit Risk Activities in Scope for Transformation:

- Originations & Amendments – Transformation of the deal
  management lifecycle from prospecting through
  underwriting, credit administration, and booking
  operations.
- Periodic Assessment and Client Monitoring – Transformation
  of the credit review processes including Annual Reviews
  and Problem credit management
- Portfolio Limit Setting and Management – Transformation of
  how Citi Manages Limits across Lending and Counter Party
  Credit products, including implementation of a
  comprehensive monitoring structure.
- Client Management and Servicing – Transformation of how our
  clients interact with Citi to execute on their business
  operation needs, including statements, draws, etc.
- Core Services – Transformation of the underlying foundation,
  data, and functional services that support the above
  business activities (e.g. Financial Spreading, Collateral
  Management, Covenant Monitoring, etc.)

Key Responsibilities:

- Support digital transformation by re-engineering operational
  business processes through process visualization and
  documentation exercises.
- Challenge current state to design a target state that drives
  controls, efficiency, and a better user experience.
- Utilize modern Emerging technologies for simplification,
  automation, quality control leading to improvement in
  Efficiency of internal processes and systems.
- Lead and manage ongoing Generative and traditional AI
  products and projects to their full delivery to
  stakeholders.
- Lead end users through training, user testing and desktop
  procedure updates.
- Participate in design workshops that enable collaboration
  and conceptual solutions, while serving diverse personas
- Translate requirements into detailed user stories that can
  be leveraged to drive software development.

As a successful candidate, you’d ideally have the following
skills and exposure:

- Bachelor’s degree in business or equivalent or technology
  related field
- 5-8 years of relevant experience
- Experience in executing technology change, either as an end
  user, product manager, or business analyst
- Knowledge about Generative AI and Traditional AI. Key
  features, strong and weak points of these technologies and
  Experience with their implementation in existing
  processes.
- Familiarity with the different software development
  methodologies and project management concepts for managing
  change in technology (e.g., requirements creation, feature
  identification, and user story writing)
- Familiarity in a front office, middle office, or operational
  capacity supporting the credit lifecycle execution or
  related transformation efforts in wholesale lending
- Demonstrated ability to translate end user needs into
  tangible work products that technology engineers can move
  forward to develop (e.g. Process Flows, Requirements,
  Design Artifacts, Screen Mock Ups, etc.)
- Track record of working across multiple stakeholders and
  collaborating with various technology partners
- Basic knowledge of programming in Python is advantage
