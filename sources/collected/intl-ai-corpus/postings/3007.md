## Description complète

Mission Statement & Summary

We’re hiring a Senior AI Engineer to design and deliver AI
systems across the business.

This is a hands-on role focused on building production-grade
solutions using LLMs and agentic systems, embedding AI
directly into core business processes rather than building
isolated prototypes. You’ll take high-value problems and
turn them into scalable, reliable systems used in real
workflows, driving measurable business outcomes.

You’ll contribute significantly to technical direction,
architecture, and implementation, translating defined
business priorities into robust, production-ready systems.
You’ll make key technical contributions and work hands-on
alongside engineers to deliver solutions that are reusable,
scalable, and built for long-term impact.

How You’ll Contribute

· Design and implement AI systems end-to-end, contributing
to technical direction and architecture

· Build LLM-powered and agent-based solutions from concept
through to production

· Contribute to engineering standards, patterns, and
reusable components across use cases

· Make informed technical decisions on models, tooling,
infrastructure, and system design within agreed
architectural direction

· Ensure systems are robust, observable, and production-
ready (evaluation, monitoring, guardrails)

· Provide technical guidance and mentorship to engineers
contributing to AI initiatives

We're Looking For These Essential Skills

· Proven experience delivering production-grade AI systems
end-to-end

· Experience designing and implementing complex AI systems
across multiple use cases or domains

· Strong expertise in generative AI / LLMs, including
orchestration, prompting, and evaluation

· Experience building agentic or multi-step AI systems

· Strong software engineering fundamentals including APIs,
system design, and cloud-based architectures

· Ability to operate effectively in ambiguous or fast-moving
problem spaces

· Experience contributing to and influencing engineering
standards and practices across projects

It’s a plus if you also have these skills

· Experience contributing to AI platforms or reusable
frameworks and patterns

· Familiarity with LLMOps / MLOps and production evaluation
practices

· Experience embedding AI into core business processes or
enterprise systems

· Experience working in large-scale or regulated enterprise
environments
