## Description complète

About this role

The AI Integration Engineer will lead the build and delivery
of AI-powered features that improve data and application
workflows across GIP and BlackRock. This role focuses on
integrating large language model (LLM)-based technologies
such as ChatGPT, Microsoft Copilot and Snowflake Cortex into
business intelligence platforms (Power BI), application
environments (Power Apps) and data pipelines. The engineer
will be responsible for prompt design, API integration,
experimentation and embedding intelligent automation
throughout the data stack in collaboration with BI, app
development and infrastructure teams.

ResponsibilitiesAI Feature Development & Integration

- Design, prototype and implement AI-powered assistants for
  data analysis, reporting and decision support
- Integrate Microsoft Copilot into Power BI dashboards and
  Power Apps workflows to enhance user efficiency
- Develop and optimize prompt libraries for ChatGPT and other
  LLM-based assistants
- Leverage Snowflake Cortex and BlackRock-approved AI
  platforms to embed intelligent automation into the data
  ecosystem
- Work closely with business intelligence, application, and
  data engineering teams to find opportunities for AI-
  powered features
- Lead rapid experimentation with AI tools, evaluating
  feasibility, accuracy and business impact
- Translate business use cases into structured AI workflows
  and solutions guided by specific inputs
- Collaborate with external vendors and internal technology
  teams to integrate new AI capabilities

Governance & Performance

- Implement guidelines for AI usage, including security,
  governance, and alignment with BlackRock standards
- Supervise AI feature performance, analyze usage trends and
  drive improvements through iteration
- Ensure cost-efficient use of AI interfaces and cloud
  platforms
- Produce documentation and training materials for end users
  on AI-enabled tools and workflows

Requirements

- Bachelor’s degree in computer science, software engineering,
  data science, information systems or a related field;
  Master’s degree preferred
- 4-6 years of experience in software development, data
  engineering or business intelligence with exposure to
  AI/ML applications
- Proven experience integrating APIs and cloud services into
  enterprise applications
- Familiarity with AI/LLM platforms (e.g., ChatGPT, Copilot,
  Snowflake Cortex) and embedding them into BI or
  application workflows
- Experience working in financial services or large-scale
  enterprise environments (preferred)

Skills

- Strong skills in API integration, workflow automation and
  application development
- Hands-on experience with Microsoft Power Platform (Power BI,
  Power Apps, Power Automate)
- Proficiency in SQL, Python or JavaScript for data and app
  integrations
- Understanding of prompt engineering, LLM behavior and AI
  feature optimization
- Knowledge of Snowflake and modern cloud architectures (AWS,
  Azure, GCP)
- Excellent problem-solving skills and ability to work across
  technical and business teams
- Strong communication and documentation skills, with the
  ability to explain technology-based solutions to non-
  technical collaborators
