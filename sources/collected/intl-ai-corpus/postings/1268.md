## Description complète

Overview:

- Blue Yonder is looking for an experienced Sr Gen AI Engineer
  to contribute to the design, development, and
  implementation of agentic AI-driven solutions at Blue
  Yonder.
- This role is not a research role and requires being hands-on
  and building agentic AI POCs from the ground up to
  demonstrate the “art of the possible” and business value.
- The Sr Gen AI Engineer will design, prototype and deploy
  agentic AI solutions that address customer pain points
  across Blue Yonder’s global customer base.
- This role will work on cutting-edge projects that push the
  boundaries of Generative AI and machine learning in the
  supply chain and retail domains.

What you’ll do:

- Design, build and rapidly iterate agentic AI solutions
  (including orchestration, MCP, A2A) logic and evals using
  proprietary/open source LLMs and open source agentic
  frameworks (Autogen).
- Demo your solutions to wider business and to customers to
  get feedback and rapidly iterate to improve the solutions.
- Collaborate with other team members and supply chain experts
  to refine Agentic AI solutions and customise your solution
  to improve fit to a given business problem.
- Research and establish coding standards, best practices, and
  technical documentation for building reliable and
  trustworthy agents.
- Evaluate and recommend new technologies to improve our
  development ecosystem.
- Integrate AI solutions with our existing platform and
  products.

What we are looking for:

- Bachelor’s degree in computer science, Artificial
  Intelligence, Data Science, or a STEM related field.
- Minimum 3+ years of experience in data science/machine
  learning/Gen AI with a strong understanding of the Gen AI
  development ecosystem.
- MUST have strong proficiency in development using Python and
  ML libraries (pandas, numpy, sklearn, Pytorch,
  transformers, Hugging Face, LangChain etc.)
- Strong knowledge of LLMs, prompt engineering, RAG, LLM
  evaluations/ monitoring.
- Experience with cloud platforms (AWS, GCP, or Azure) and
  Github.
- Proven track record of shipping products powered by LLMs,
  RAG systems, and/or AI agents is a big plus.
- Comfortable with working in a startup environment embedded
  within an enterprise.
- Passion for pushing the boundaries of AI and a commitment to
  staying at the cutting edge of industry trends and
  technologies.
- Familiarity with CI/CD pipelines and automated testing
  strategies.
- Familiarity with monitoring, logging, and observability
  tools for LLMs.
- Previous experience with working on data science problems in
  supply chain context
- Strong communication skills and ability to explain complex
  technical concepts.
- Contributions to open-source projects in the AI/ML space
