## Description complète

Konovo is a global healthcare intelligence company on a
mission to transform research through technology- enabling
faster, better, connected insights.

Konovo provides healthcare organisations with access to over
2 million healthcare professionals—the largest network of
its kind globally. With a workforce of over 200 employees
across 5 countries: India, Bosnia and Herzegovina, the
United Kingdom, Mexico, and the United States, we
collaborate to support some of the most prominent names in
healthcare. Our customers include over 300 global
pharmaceutical companies, medical device manufacturers,
research agencies, and consultancy firms.

As we transition from a service-oriented model to a product-
driven platform, we are expanding our hybrid Bengaluru based
team. We are looking for a Lead Applied AI Engineer to help
advance the next chapter of Konovo’s AI-native platform.

AI is already embedded in our product and internal tooling.
Your role will be to deepen, scale, and operationalise those
capabilities across our platform, data systems, and
engineering workflows with production reliability and
enterprise discipline. You will partner with global
engineering, data, and product teams to translate real
business problems into production-ready AI solutions.

This is a hands-on role for someone who understands both the
promise and limits of modern AI. You will design
thoughtfully, build pragmatically, and ship responsibly.
Your work will directly shape how our customers and
employees experience AI at Konovo. You will operate in an
environment where AI decisions impact enterprise customers
globally and must meet high standards of reliability,
compliance, and performance. AI is a strategic
differentiator for Konovo. As a globally scaled healthcare
intelligence company service hundreds of enterprise
customers, AI at Konovo must accelerate innovation while
protecting reliability, data integrity, and customer trust.

How You’ll Make an Impact:

- Design, build, and deploy production-grade AI and LLM-
  powered features within our intelligent platform
- Improve model performance, reliability, observability, and
  cost efficiency in enterprise-scale production
  environments
- Collaborate with product and engineering teams to translate
  business requirements into scalable AI solutions
- Advance our provider-agnostic AI architecture to enable
  secure access to multiple LLM ecosystems
- Build internal AI tooling that enhances engineering
  productivity and broader employee workflows
- Establish best practices for model evaluation, prompt
  engineering, testing, and governance
- Ensure responsible AI implementation aligned with data
  privacy, security, and compliance standards
- Design AI systems with strong guardrails around privacy,
  auditability, global regulatory requirements, and long-
  term maintainability
- Contribute to architectural decisions as we evolve our
  modern data stack and AI platform capabilities
- Mentor engineers and raise the technical bar for applied AI
  across the team
- Evaluate emerging AI capabilities rigorously, adopting what
  delivers measurable value while avoiding unnecessary
  complexity or hype

What We’re Looking For:

- A proactive and strategic thinker with strong analytical and
  problem-solving skills.
- 5+ years of experience building and deploying AI/ML systems
  in production environments
- Strong hands-on experience with LLM integration, prompt
  engineering, model evaluation, and performance
  optimisation
- Experience building AI systems where reliability,
  compliance, and uptime are business-critical
- Proficiency in Python and experience working with modern AI
  frameworks and libraries
- Experience building systems in cloud environments such as
  AWS, Azure, or GCP. AWS preferred.
- Strong understanding of data architecture, APIs, and
  scalable backend systems
- Experience working in product-focused engineering teams, not
  just research environments
- Ability to balance innovation with product stability, cost
  discipline, and measurable business impact
- Comfort explaining AI trade-offs and feasibility clearly to
  both technical and non-technical stakeholders
- Experience working across timezones and collaborating with
  global teams
- Bachelor’s in Computer Science, Artificial Intelligence,
  Machine Learning, or a related field
- Adaptability to thrive in a fast-changing environment while
  navigating challenges collaboratively.

Bonus Points For Experience with:

- Vector databases, retrieval-augmented generation (RAG), or
  model orchestration frameworks
- Databricks, Snowflake, or modern lakehouse architectures
- Healthcare, research, or data-intensive domains
