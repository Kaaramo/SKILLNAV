Accrete is a dynamic and innovative company focused on

About the Role

We are seeking a Software Engineer to join our AI Platform
team, responsible for building the core systems and
infrastructure that support Accrete’s AI-driven products.
This role is focused on backend and platform
engineering—designing reliable, scalable services and
tooling that enable our product and research teams to move
quickly and safely.

This is an ideal role for an engineer with strong Python
experience who enjoys working on foundational systems and
collaborating closely across engineering, product, and
research teams.

What You’ll Do

- Design, build, and maintain scalable backend services and
  platform components in Python
- Develop APIs and internal services that support core product
  functionality
- Build and improve data pipelines, workflows, and
  infrastructure used across the platform
- Collaborate with product, research, and engineering teams to
  translate requirements into robust technical solutions
- Improve system reliability, performance, and observability
  in production environments
- Contribute to architectural decisions and help evolve
  platform best practices
- Participate in code reviews and uphold high standards for
  code quality and maintainability

What We’re Looking For

- 3–5 years of professional software engineering experience
- Strong proficiency in Python and React and experience
  building backend systems or services
- Solid understanding of software engineering fundamentals
  (system design, APIs, data modeling)
- Experience working with cloud environments (AWS, GCP, or
  Azure)
- Familiarity with databases, data stores, and service-
  oriented architectures
- Intellectual curiosity demonstrated through side projects,
  independent experimentation, reading industry
  research/articles, or hands-on exploration of new tools
  and technologiesDemonstrated passion for AI and software
  engineering, with genuine curiosity about emerging
  technologies
- High level of initiative and ownership; proactively
  identifies problems and drives solutions end-to-end
- Humble, low-ego approach with a practical, no-nonsense
  mindset and strong sense of accountability
- Enthusiasm and energy for building, experimenting, and
  iterating in a fast-moving environment

Nice to Have

  teams
