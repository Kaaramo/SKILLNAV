### Raisonnement classification (LLM upstream)

This is a Product Manager role focused on building developer
tools, infrastructure, and platforms that support AI
workflows (MCP servers, APIs, SDKs, integration with AI
IDEs). The role does not involve hands-on work building,
training, fine-tuning, or deploying AI models - rather, it
enables developers who build with AI by creating the tooling
and integration layers around Anaconda's package management
platform.
