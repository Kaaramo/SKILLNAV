Sr. AI Solutions Engineer

About This Role

Built’s new Agentic AI product helps lenders automate high-
leverage operational workflows. As our Sr. AI Solutions
Engineer, you’ll be the hands-on technical owner for driving
customer onboarding and adoption — designing, building, and
tuning agent workflows, prompts, and evaluations.

This role sits within Built’s AI Solutions & Implementations
Team and reports to the Head of Implementations. It exists
to scale customer delivery and adoption for our new Agentic
AI platform as we move from pilot to broad rollout —
ensuring our clients achieve measurable value while shaping
how the product evolves.

You’ll partner with an Implementation Manager and an Agent
Analyst and act as the technical expert to ensure customers
love the product and get tremendous value.

What You’ll Do

- Own prompt engineering & agent workflow design
  (tool/function calling, structured outputs, guardrails).
- Translate client SOPs into production workflows; ensuring we
  account for the specific nuances of each client.
- Operate and iterate with real client data to launch;
  troubleshoot and resolve issues quickly. Back-test new
  changes to existing data to ensure nothing breaks.
- Act as the technical escalation point for 6–8 concurrent
  customers; enable clients and our Agent Analysts on best
  practices.
- Instrument and report performance, create reusable
  templates/runbooks, and feed product requirements to
  Engineering.

What Success Looks Like

30 Days: You’ve learned the platform, shadowed multiple
customer implementations, and built your first workflow end-
to-end.

60 Days: You’re owning technical delivery for active
implementations and driving measurable value for customers.
90 Days: You’ve built repeatable patterns, contributed to
product feedback loops, and are trusted as the technical
point person for new customer launches.

What You’ll Bring

- Experience in a customer-facing technical role (Solutions
  Engineer / Sales Engineer / Implementation Engineer) at a
  data/AI/infra company (e.g., Snowflake, Databricks,
  similar).
- Deep, practical prompt engineering expertise (few/zero-shot
  design, tool/function calling, structured outputs, agents,
  retrieval).
- Experience building agentic workflows with LLM providers
  (OpenAI/Anthropic/Azure).
- Strong software engineering fundamentals; fluent in Python
  and/or TypeScript, REST/GraphQL APIs, and SQL.
- Comfort creating evaluation pipelines, A/B tests, and
  analytics to measure quality, safety, and cost.
- Excellent client communication—able to translate messy SOPs
  into deterministic workflows and drive to outcomes on
  tight timelines.
- Bonus: Fintech/banking or B2B enterprise SaaS background;
  Snowflake/Databricks integrations; prompt
  observability/guardrails (e.g., Langfuse/MLflow-like
  tools); security & compliance awareness (SOC2, PII
  handling).
