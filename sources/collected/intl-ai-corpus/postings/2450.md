## Description complète

We are looking for an software engineer to join our team in
New York, NY. As a founding member of our team focused on
AI, you'll have the opportunity to shape our AI strategy and
be instrumental in building our core technology platform.

About Niva

At Niva, we’re building a universal identity layer that
enables trust between businesses worldwide. We leverage
cutting-edge language models and machine learning to deliver
instant, reliable, and borderless business verification.
Leading fintechs, banks, and public companies already use
Niva to verify businesses, prevent fraud, and meet Know Your
Business (KYB) requirements in minutes.

Read more about how Niva’s platform reduced time spent to
verify businesses by 98% with a customer here. We’re a fast-
moving team on a mission to make it effortless to trust
businesses anywhere in the world and we’re looking for eager
new teammates to help us accelerate our mission.

What You'll Be Doing

- Work closely with the CEO and CTO to define our AI strategy
  and influence the product roadmap
- Design and develop our multi-agent AI platform that
  autonomously extracts, validates and reconciles business
  identity information from numerous sources across the
  globe
- Build robust evaluation frameworks to benchmark agent
  performance and ensure high-quality AI outputs
- Engage directly with customers to understand their needs and
  gather feedback, to improve the ways in which AI can
  improve their workflows
- Continuously staying up to date with the latest in the AI
  ecosystem
- Help set the culture of the company, promoting a
  collaborative and fun environment. You'll be part of the
  founding team and integral for establish not just what we
  work on, but "how" we work

You're Probably a Match If You Have

- Hands-on experience building agent-based LLM applications
  using open-source or closed-source models (GPT, Claude,
  etc.)
- Knowledge of RAG techniques and how to optimize them for
  domain-specific knowledge, including chunking strategies,
  vector embeddings, and relevance scoring
- Strong Python skills with experience building reliable,
  scalable production systems
- Customer centric thinking - you're energized by translating
  user problems into AI solutions that deliver real-world
  value
- Comfort with ambiguity - you can navigate the challenges of
  working with emerging technologies where best practices
  are still evolving
- Excitement for how AI is changing the world and being part
  of that change

What We Offer

- Competitive salary range of $180k - $250k
- Substantial equity in the company
- Comprehensive health benefits (medical, dental, vision)
- Flexible working hours and flexible time off
- A chance to be a part of a pioneering company with a mission
  to revolutionize global trust and commerce
