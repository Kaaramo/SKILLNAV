If that sounds exciting—and the job description below feels

We are a hybrid company with a focus on in-person
collaboration when necessary. Employees are expected to be
available to work from one of our office hubs at least two
days per week, or eight days per month. Our US hub locations
include: Austin, TX; Chicago, IL; New York City, NY; San
Francisco, CA; and the Washington, DC metro area. If this is
a role based in Europe, our Europe hub locations are London,
UK and Amsterdam, NL. Please ensure you can realistically
commit to this structure before applying.

We’re looking for an AI Automation and Integration Engineer
to join our rapidly evolving IT & AI Workspace Technology
team. This role is for a hands-on builder who thrives on
creating smarter, more connected systems—someone who can
transform manual workflows into intelligent, AI-enabled
automations using cutting-edge tools like Model Context
Protocol (MCP), Slack workflows, and secure system
integrations.

You’ll design and implement scalable automations across our
SaaS ecosystem, partner closely with our AI product and
infrastructure teams, and bring a high degree of technical
rigor and curiosity to every problem you solve.

This is a key role on a small, fast-moving team where your
work will directly impact how we scale, automate, and
future-proof our internal operations.

What you’ll do:

MCP & AI Workflow Engineering

- Implement and scale our Model Context Protocol (MCP) to
  support secure, composable AI workflows across Slack,
  Jira, Salesforce, GitHub, AWS-based infrastructure, LLM
  providers (e.g., Claude), and internal APIs.
- Design agentic systems that enable AI tooling to integrate
  seamlessly with operational data and user identity
  services.
- Author requests for comments (RFCs), technical requirements
  documents (TRDs), and internal documentation to align MCP
  features with enterprise use cases.

Automation & Integration Development

- Build and maintain scalable automations and secure APIs to
  reduce operational overhead in onboarding, provisioning,
  and data lifecycle management.
- Own development and maintenance of reusable modules for
  identity sync, data uploads (e.g., CSV bulk importers),
  and AI-to-API orchestration.
- Drive continuous improvement across automation platforms by
  collaborating with stakeholder teams across the business.

Security, Identity & System Governance

- Work with InfoSec and IT teams to enforce role-based access
  controls and SAML/SSO integrations
- Harden identity workflows across US and EU regions, ensuring
  compliance with internal and external standards.
- Support audit readiness through traceable change management
  and data integrity practices.

Cross-Functional Collaboration

- Attend cross functional standups, Architecture Roundtables,
  and AI working groups to ensure engineering solutions
  align with company goals.
- Partner closely with stakeholders to balance speed and
  safety in fast-moving pilot initiatives.
- Advocate for test-driven development (TDD), documentation,
  and open source-inspired code reusability.

What Success Looks Like

- MCP server scaled and adopted across multiple workflows with
  stable, secure integrations
- Automation measurably reduced manual data entry through bulk
  import and automation tooling (e.g., 50% reduction).
- Onboarding and provisioning latency improved through
  identity automation (e.g., 30% faster completion).
- Clear documentation, playbooks, and dashboards enable
  stakeholders to self-serve with AI tools and automation
  templates, not rely on ad-hoc IT custom work.
- All automation systems have strong test coverage, robust
  monitoring, and meet security review requirements, with
  audit-ready logs for provisioning and data movement.
- Impact is visible and quantified through simple time-saved
  and adoption metrics layered on top of Jira, dashboards,
  and usage telemetry.

- 3–6 years of professional experience in software or systems

- Hands-on experience designing AI-assisted automations that

- Demonstrated experience integrating APIs and building

- Strong scripting or development skills (e.g., Python, ROR or

- Familiarity with common SaaS platforms including Okta, Jira,

Preferred:
