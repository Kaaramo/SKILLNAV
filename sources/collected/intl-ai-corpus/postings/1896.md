AI Forward Deployed Engineer

Summary

As an AI Forward Deployed Engineer (FDE), you are a hands‑on
bridge between SensibleAI capabilities and real‑world
customer outcomes. You design, implement, and harden
cross‑product AI solutions—spanning SensibleAI Forecast,
Studio, and Agents, Genesis Blocks, and the OneStream core
platform. You partner with Delivery and Engineering to
validate value, improve reliability, and codify reusable
patterns that others can adopt.

Primary Duties and Responsibilities

- Apply OneStream, AI, and enterprise software expertise to
  help customers deliver transformative AI solutions within
  the OneStream ecosystem.
- Play a key role in hands-on delivery for AI-focused
  implementations, expansion projects, and established
  deployments—not just early-stage POCs.
- Interact, collaborate, and present to a diverse set of
  stakeholders (developers, sponsors, executives) to deliver
  AI-focused implementations.
- Play a growing role in orchestrating the evolving SensibleAI
  Portfolio (Forecast, Studio, Agents) with OneStream core
  to deliver modular solutions tailored to complex financial
  and analytics requirements.
- Produce reusable demos, implementations, checklists,
  workflows, best practices, and concise instructional
  videos that enable Presales, Delivery, Partners, and
  customers to leverage your learnings.
- Diagnose and resolve AI solution field issues; document
  decisions and trade-offs; support Delivery and Partner
  teams with structured guidance.
- Collaborate with AI Delivery to harden solutions for
  production and with AI Engineering to channel learnings
  and product gaps back into roadmaps.
- Engage directly with finance and data teams; work with
  partner FDEs to increase capability and consistency across
  the ecosystem; facilitate workshops and office hours.

Required Education and Experience

- Bachelor's degree in computer science, data science, or
  relevant combination of education and experience.
- 0-2 years of software engineering experience, with a focus
  on data science, AI, or machine learning concepts.
- 1-2 years of industry experience in Applied Artificial
  Intelligence and/or Machine Learning.
- 1-3 years of experience delivering/implementing enterprise
  software solutions in FP&A/CPM/EPM or data/AI platforms,
  including at least 1 year in customer-facing build roles.
- Demonstrated success contributing to POCs or early
  implementations that transitioned to scaled rollout or
  productized assets.
- Strong SQL; data modeling and ETL/ELT; REST APIs,
  identity/security basics.
- Experience in Python and/or C#; automation and packaging;
  familiarity with containerization is a plus.
- Experience in producing insightful data visualizations of
  complex concepts and dataset.

Preferred Education and Experience

- Direct experience with OneStream implementations or building
  Genesis Blocks / Marketplace content.
- Direct experience within a prior Forward Deployed
  Engineering role.



Knowledge, Skills, and Abilities

- Translate complex technical concepts into business value for
  finance stakeholders.
- Depth in one or more areas (e.g., forecasting, LLM apps, CPM
  modeling) with breadth across data, integrations, and
  platform operations.
- Hypothesis-driven approach; comfort operating with
  ambiguity; strong documentation and diagramming habits.
- Work cross-functionally with Product, Engineering, Delivery,
  CS, Presales, and Partners; give/receive design feedback.
- Design for reuse; produce assets others can adopt;
  contribute to internal standards.
- Apply data privacy and governance guidelines in solution
  design.
- Comfortable engaging and presenting to management level
  executives from time to time.



Travel (remove if not applicable)

- Up to 25% travel may be required for discovery, workshops,
  readouts, and early implementation milestones.
