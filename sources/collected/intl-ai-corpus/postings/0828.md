## Description complète

About the Role

You’ll lead the architecture, development, and deployment of
next-generation agentic AI systems that transform enterprise
operations. These intelligent agents will autonomously
orchestrate workflows, analyze real-time data, and embed
decision-making across domains like finance, sales, and
operations. You’ll guide cross-functional teams, mentor
engineers, and ensure technical excellence across all stages
of delivery. Microsoft AI Foundry will be a key platform for
agent development, and Copilot Studio may be used optionally
for few use cases.

Day-to-Day Work Responsibilities

- Architect and lead development of scalable, production-grade
  agentic AI systems.
- Design serverless, event-driven workflows using Azure
  Functions, Logic Apps, and Power Automate.
- Build and optimize ML models for predictive analytics,
  anomaly detection, and recommendations using Azure ML,
  Databricks, and Python.
- Integrate agents with enterprise platforms such as SAP HANA,
  Salesforce, Snowflake, Kafka, and UiPath.
- Ensure observability and reliability through Azure Monitor,
  logging, and alerting frameworks.
- Define and enforce architecture patterns, reusable
  components, and governance standards.
- Lead code reviews, technical design sessions, and agile
  ceremonies.
- Leverage Microsoft AI Foundry to build and deploy agents
  that meet enterprise-grade standards.

Leadership Responsibilities

- Mentor and coach junior engineers, fostering a culture of
  innovation and technical excellence.
- Drive cross-team collaboration and alignment on agentic AI
  strategy and execution.
- Own delivery timelines, quality benchmarks, and stakeholder
  communication.
- Champion best practices in DevOps, CI/CD, and model
  lifecycle management.
- Stay ahead of trends in AI, machine vision, and IoT to guide
  strategic decisions.

Cross-Functional Collaboration

You will work closely with:

- Data Scientists & ML Engineers – for model development and
  integration
- Product Managers & UX Designers – aligning agent
  capabilities with user needs
- Enterprise Architects & DevOps Engineers – ensuring
  scalability and deployment efficiency
- Security & Compliance Teams – meeting regulatory and
  governance requirements
- Business Stakeholders & SMEs – defining use cases and
  validating outcomes
- Change Management & Enablement Teams – supporting adoption
  and training efforts

Technology & Tools

- Languages & Platforms: Python, Power Apps, Power Automate,
  Databricks, Snowflake
- Enterprise Systems: SAP HANA, Kafka, UiPath
- AI Platforms: Microsoft AI Foundry (highly preferred),
  Copilot Studio (optional)
- Cloud & ML Tools: Azure Machine Learning, Azure DevOps,
  GitHub
- Data & Observability: Vector Databases, Azure Monitor
- Vision & IoT: Azure IoT Hub, OpenCV, TensorFlow, PyTorch
  (for edge and vision-based agents)

What you bring with you:

- Proven experience leading AI engineering teams and
  delivering enterprise-grade solutions
- Strong technical depth in AI/ML, cloud platforms, and
  agentic system design
- Ability to navigate ambiguity, drive clarity, and influence
  across functions
- Passion for building transformative AI systems that solve
  meaningful problems
