### Raisonnement classification (LLM upstream)

This role focuses on building and maintaining infrastructure
that supports AI/ML platforms rather than directly building,
training, or fine-tuning models. The engineer enables AI
services through infrastructure engineering, automation, and
operational reliability - supporting model hosting,
inference environments, and deployments but not working
hands-on with the models themselves.
