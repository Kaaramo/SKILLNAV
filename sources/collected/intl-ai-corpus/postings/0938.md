### Raisonnement classification (LLM upstream)

This is a Full Stack Developer role that builds web
applications integrating AI/ML models and LLM services. The
developer works alongside data scientists and ML engineers
to embed AI capabilities into applications, but is not
directly building, training, or fine-tuning models
themselves. Their primary focus is on full stack
development, API integration, and infrastructure to enable
AI capabilities for end users.

- Design, develop, and maintain full stack web applications that integrate AI/ML models, knowledge graphs, and search interfaces for financial research
