## Responsabilités

- Partner with stakeholders to scope problems and identify appropriate AI solutions, leveraging existing tools or building custom workflows
- Design and implement agentic systems using RAG, grounding, prompt engineering, and orchestration on a GCP-first stack
- Build and maintain production ML pipelines and services for recommender systems, customer segmentation, and marketing optimization
- Develop APIs and microservices for AI/ML solutions ensuring security, scalability, and observability
- Implement CI/CD for ML services, write infrastructure as code, and monitor model/data drift and performance
- Establish robust guardrails for safe AI usage including prompt security, evaluation frameworks, and compliance with privacy regulations
- Drive best practices, reusable templates, and documentation to scale AI/ML delivery across the business
- Collaborate with data engineers, scientists, product managers, legal, and infosec to deliver end-to-end solutions
