## Description complète

7AI is seeking a Senior AI Sales Engineer to join our
growing team as a key technical leader. This is a strategic
role for a seasoned professional with deep cybersecurity
expertise and a passion to learn and advocate for AI. As one
of the first senior hires in our go-to-market team, you will
play a critical role in shaping how we engage with
customers, influence product direction, and scale our
technical sales motion.

You will partner closely with Sales, Engineering, Product,
and Customer Success teams to drive high-impact engagements
with prospects and customers. You’ll lead technical
discovery, design sophisticated solutions, and communicate
the business value of our AI-powered cybersecurity platform
to both technical and executive stakeholders.

Key Responsibilities:

- Own and lead the technical sales discussions for high-value
  customer engagements.
- Serve as a trusted advisor to senior IT and security
  executives, deeply understanding their threat landscape
  and technology environment.
- Design and deliver compelling technical demonstrations and
  POCs that highlight differentiated capabilities of the 7AI
  platform.
- Collaborate with Product and Engineering teams to translate
  customer feedback into product features and roadmap
  priorities.
- Tailor solutions during Proof of Value to ensure long-term
  customer success
- Mentor and guide junior Sales Engineers, contributing to
  team development and knowledge sharing.
- Represent 7AI at industry conferences, panels, and partner
  events as a technical subject matter expert.
- Drive continuous improvement of pre-sales processes, tools,
  and documentation to support scale.

Requirements:

- 6+ years of experience in cybersecurity-focused Sales
  Engineering, Solutions Architecture, or similar customer-
  facing technical roles.
- Demonstrated success in complex B2B enterprise sales,
  preferably in fast-paced startup environments.
- Expert-level knowledge of cybersecurity principles,
  frameworks, and modern threat landscapes.
- Experience working with or evangelizing AI/ML technologies
  within the security domain.
- Exceptional communication skills, with the ability to tailor
  complex technical topics to both engineering teams and
  executive stakeholders.
- Strong leadership presence with a collaborative mindset and
  a drive to take ownership.
- Proven record in scripting / engineering and customization /
  implementation
