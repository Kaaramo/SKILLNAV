### Raisonnement classification (LLM upstream)

This role focuses on building foundational tooling and
platform capabilities that SUPPORT AI agents (like Browser
Rendering API, agent orchestration primitives, and agent-to-
agent interaction frameworks), rather than building,
training, or fine-tuning the AI models themselves. The
engineer is creating infrastructure and APIs that enable
others to build and deploy AI agents, which is the
definition of ai-support work.
