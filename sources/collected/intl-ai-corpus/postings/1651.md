### Raisonnement classification (LLM upstream)

This role builds and maintains infrastructure/platform that
supports data scientists and ML engineers, rather than
directly building, training, or fine-tuning models. The
focus is on the platform layer (model registry, inferencing
systems, Kubernetes orchestration) that enables others to do
AI/ML work.
