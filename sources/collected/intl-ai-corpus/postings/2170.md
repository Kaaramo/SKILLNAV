Company Description

We specialize in building intelligent AI agents that

Job Description

Job Overview We are seeking a highly skilled Senior AI
Engineer specializing in agent-based systems and advanced AI

implementing advanced reasoning capabilities, and building
robust, secure, and intelligent AI solutions.

Key Responsibilities:

Architecture & System Design

- Designing multi-agent architectures (supervisor,
  hierarchical, collaborative patterns)
- Building fault-tolerant, production-grade agent systems with
  error recovery, fallback strategies, and human-in-the-loop
  escalation
- Architecting scalable RAG systems (hybrid search, query
  decomposition, agentic retrieval)
- Designing agent evaluation and benchmarking frameworks from
  scratch
- Security and safety patterns for autonomous agents
  (sandboxing, permission scoping, content filtering)

Advanced Agent Capabilities

- Complex workflow orchestration — agents that plan, delegate,
  self-correct, and iterate
- Multi-modal agent development (vision, audio, document
  understanding)
- Implementing advanced reasoning patterns (ReAct, reflection,
  tree-of-thought, self-critique loops)
- Building custom tool ecosystems — MCP servers, plugin
  architectures, dynamic tool selection
- Knowledge graph integration and structured knowledge
  management for agents
- Fine-tuning and distillation of models for domain-specific
  agent behaviors

Qualifications

- Bachelor’s or Master’s degree in Computer Science,
  Artificial Intelligence, or a related field
- 2- 5 years of experience in AI/ML engineering, with a focus
  on LLMs or agent-based systems
- Strong experience in system design and scalable architecture
- Proficiency in Python and modern AI frameworks
- Hands-on experience with RAG pipelines and vector databases
- Familiarity with model fine-tuning, evaluation, and
  deployment
