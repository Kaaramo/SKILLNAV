## Description complète

Software Engineer (Applied AI)

At Column Tax, we’re building the next generation of tax
software. Our mission is to make it possible for every
taxpayer to file confidently in just one click. As the
fastest-growing tax e-filer of all time, we’ve already
helped millions of Americans. Come help us build delightful,
transparent, and easy-to-use tax products — and bring that
experience to many, many millions more!

What we are looking for at Column Tax

- Improvise, adapt, overcome. We’re a startup — everyone jumps
  in across teams to solve problems and create forward
  motion.
- Empathy, communication, and teamwork. Tax software isn’t a
  solo effort. You’ll collaborate closely with engineers,
  tax analysts, designers, and GTM on nearly every project.
- Ownership mindset. Everyone shapes the product and
  engineering roadmap
- Fierce execution. We focus week to week, to deliver real
  value to taxpayers and partners.

What we are looking for in the role

- 4-6+ years of professional experience in software
  engineering or a related role.
- Experience exploring, developing, and implementing advanced
  AI technologies into shipped software such as our AI tax
  development agent.
- Experience building and deploying reinforcement learning
  algorithms and machine learning pipelines.
- Track record of taking full ownership of projects from spec
  to ship to scale.
- Regularly takes on work beyond their current comfort zone,
  learning new languages, systems, or domains in order to
  deliver real value.
- Comfortable working and reasoning across the stack whether
  frontend, backend, or infrastructure.
- Bonus points for experience in tax, bookkeeping, embedded
  fintech.
- Bonus points for a blend of startup scrappiness and working
  in high-scale systems.
- Based in San Francisco 🌉 / New York City🗽, and excited to be
  in-office at least 3 days a week. If you're not local but
  are open to relocating, let us know!

Day-to-day

- Build agentic tooling that is able to understand tax law
  turn it into software code, and test itself to achieve
  correctness.
- Ingest and process millions of data points from uploaded
  documents, partner integrations, and external APIs to
  accurately auto-fill tax returns so taxpayers don’t have
  to.
- Build and extend our APIs for our partners - Our partners
  are key to our success. We empower our partners by
  creating APIs that make integration seamless and
  intuitive.
- Create UI for our embedded tax platform - Work with Design
  and the Tax teams to translate complicated tax code into a
  delightful UI that anyone can use.

Week-to-week

- Week 1: Meet the team, ship to prod on day one, fix 1–2 bugs
  to get acclimated.
- Month 1: Lead and ship a small feature, review PRs, and demo
  at all-hands.
- Month 3: Own a larger project, help scope future work,
  mentor teammates, and lead process improvements.

Interview ProcessStep 1: Intro call with our Head of
Engineering (or a team member)

This is a two-way conversation. We’ll talk about your
background, what you’re looking for next, and the kinds of
problems you enjoy solving. You’ll also get a chance to
learn more about Column — how we work, what we value, and
how we’d support you if you joined. Bring questions!

Step 2: Take home challenge

We’ll ask you to complete a ~3-hour project that mirrors the
kind of integration or tooling work we do every day. You’ll
have full freedom to do it your way — this is about
decision-making, clarity, and implementation, not code golf.

Step 3: Interview panels

The interview panels are where you’ll meet several of us and
work through collaborative challenges. The interviews are
designed to mimic real work — like redesigning a creaky
system, debugging in a sandboxed environment, and walking
through a past project. The interview panels are over video
calls and ~4.5 hours total over two days.

Step 4: Offer

Before making a formal offer, we like to speak with a few
people you’ve worked closely with — usually a manager or
peer. It helps us understand how to support you best. If all
looks good, we’ll move quickly to extend an offer and kick
off what we hope is a long, rewarding partnership.

— even if you’re not sure you meet every requirement — we
