## Description complète

This role owns the technical vision and execution for
applying AI at scale across DevOps and productization
workflows, with a mandate to fundamentally improve how
software and firmware are built, tested, released, and
operated. The position sits at the intersection of AI
systems, developer platforms, and operational excellence,
driving the design and rollout of intelligent, automated
pipelines that move beyond static CI/CD toward predictive,
self-healing, and data-driven execution. The focus is on
replacing high-cost manual processes with scalable AI-
powered mechanisms, improving end-to-end quality and
velocity, and enabling reliable delivery across large,
multi-team engineering environments. Success in this role
requires operating at platform and ecosystem scale, setting
architectural direction, and ensuring AI-enabled systems are
secure, observable, governable, and resilient in long-lived
production environments.



In This Role, Your Responsibilities Will Be:

- Lead the design and delivery of AI-enabled solutions for
  building, test, and release workflows of software products
  using approved enterprise technologies and enterprise-
  ready solution patterns.
- Identify engineering workflows that can be fully or
  partially offloaded to AI systems, reducing or eliminating
  repeated manual engineering effort.
- Design and implement scalable AI-powered solutions in
  engineering workflows such as build and test triage,
  failure analysis, flaky test intelligence, debugging
  assistance, and release decision support.
- Work hands-on with engineering teams to turn ideas into
  working solutions that can be rolled out in production.
- Own key initiatives from concept and architecture through
  development, rollout, validation, adoption, and ongoing
  improvement.
- Help define technical direction, propose practical
  architecture improvements, and influence how AI is
  introduced into critical engineering systems.
- Develop tools, supporting systems, and infrastructure that
  improve engineering productivity and enable sustainable AI
  adoption.
- Review technical designs and implementations, provide strong
  feedback, and mentor junior engineers.
- Establish practical approaches for evaluation, telemetry,
  monitoring, observability, and continuous improvement of
  AI-enabled systems.
- Design solutions that account for operational constraints
  such as rate limits, retry behavior, error handling, and
  production supportability.
- Collaborate with internal teams, domain experts, and junior
  talent to build solutions that can scale across the
  organization.
- Ensure all automation and intelligence align with Emerson’s
  reliability, safety, and regulatory commitments
- Act as a key technical leader, shaping the long-term roadmap
  for AI driven DevOps transformation across T&M. • Partner
  with engineering leaders, architects, and operational
  teams to drive adoption of intelligent automation and data
  driven decision-making.
- Mentor teams on AI/ML fundamentals, emerging practices, and
  how to leverage automation to enhance product quality and
  operational performance.



Who You Are:

- You are an application-focused AI engineering leader who
  enjoys solving real workflow and engineering problems.
- You are comfortable operating as both an architect and a
  builder.
- You can take an ambiguous problem, create a practical
  solution approach, and drive it through execution.
- You understand what it takes to deploy systems at an
  organizational scale, including rollout, adoption,
  governance, observability, and long-term maintainability.
- You work well across teams, influence technical direction,
  and help raise the capability of the engineers around you.
- You are self-driven, pragmatic, and able to make sound
  trade-offs in evolving situations.



For This Role, You Will Need:

- B.E / B.Tech / M.Tech / MCA in Computer Science /
  Electronics / Electrical / Instrumentation / Artificial
  Intelligence / Machine Learning / related technical
  discipline.
- 8+ years of proven experience in software engineering,
  systems design, AI application engineering, or related
  technical roles. Minimum 3+ years  experience developing
  AI based applications.
- Hands-on experience with retrieval-augmented generation
  architectures, workflow automation, evaluation methods,
  platform integration, agentic systems, cost-aware AI
  design, and operationalizing AI-enabled systems in
  production, including monitoring, observability, feedback
  loops, and lifecycle management.
- Strong understanding of AI training fundamentals, including
  how models are developed, adapted, evaluated, and
  improved, even if your primary industry experience has
  been in building and deploying AI-enabled applications
  rather than training foundation models directly.
- Strong experience building AI-enabled applications or
  workflow solutions in enterprise environments.
- Strong understanding of DevOps and modern software delivery
  systems, including CI/CD pipelines, build and release
  workflows, automation, observability, and production
  operational considerations.
- Proven experience designing, deploying, and operating
  systems or workflow solutions on an organizational scale,
  including rollout across multiple teams or large
  engineering groups.
- Strong problem-solving ability, including diagnosing
  technical issues and proposing practical, high-quality
  solutions.
- Good understanding of enterprise constraints such as
  governance, approved technologies, security,
  observability, and production-readiness.
- Ability to work effectively with engineering teams that have
  deep domain expertise but limited AI maturity.



Preferred Qualifications That Set You Apart:

- Experience applying AI to build systems, test systems, CI/CD
  workflows, release engineering, software delivery, and
  engineering productivity workflows.
- Experience with enterprise AI platforms, internal developer
  platforms, or similar shared engineering platforms used to
  operationalize AI solutions at scale.
- Familiarity with Agile software development practices, CI
  pipelines, and workflow automation.
- Understanding of NI software tools or related internal
  engineering ecosystems.
- Exposure to fine-tuning, model adaptation, data preparation,
  evaluation pipelines, or other AI training-related
  workflows.
- A strong track record of driving large, ambiguous technical
  initiatives to successful completion.
- Ability to work effectively in a diverse, multi-cultural
  engineering environment and communicate well with both
  engineering teams and management.



Why This Role Is Important:

- This role is intended to bring external AI expertise into
  the organization and apply it in practical, high-value
  engineering workflows.
- The focus is not on research alone, but on delivering
  enterprise-ready AI systems that save developer time,
  improve decision-making, and create reusable internal
  capability.
- This is a Principal-level individual contributor role for
  someone who can influence architecture, build solutions
