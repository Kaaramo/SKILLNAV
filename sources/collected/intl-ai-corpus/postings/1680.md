About us

Appnovation is a global, full-service digital partner that
combines Strategy, Experience & Design, Engineering and
Managed Services. We build digital solutions that deliver
real impact today and serve as foundations for future
growth.  Bold ambition. Practical action. Endless

About the Role

Join forces with Appnovation to deliver high-impact results
for one of our key strategic partners - a global leader in
their field. This engagement offers a unique vantage point
into a world-class organization with exceptional
opportunities for professional growth and long-term career
advancement

As a Senior AI Software Engineer, you'll transform business
ideas into production-ready applications using cutting-edge
AI design tools and modern full-stack frameworks. With a
strong bias toward frontend development, you specialize in
the critical early stages—from concept through pre-totype to
prototype—where design systems meet AI-augmented
development. You are a visionary technologist who sits at
the intersection of engineering excellence and AI
innovation. Your mission is to oversee the entire "concept-
to-production" pipeline, ensuring our teams leverage
cutting-edge AI tools to deliver enterprise-grade
applications at unprecedented speeds.

While you maintain a deep technical foundation in full-stack
development, your primary focus is strategic leadership:
defining design system standards, optimizing AI-augmented
workflows, and mentoring a high-performance team of
developers and AI builders.

Key Responsibilities

- Strategic AI Integration: Direct the adoption of AI design
  tools (v0.dev, bolt.new, Lovable) to compress the
  development lifecycle from weeks to days without
  sacrificing quality.
- Architecture & Standards: Define and enforce the technical
  standards for production-ready full-stack applications,
  ensuring scalability, performance, and accessibility
  across all projects.
- Design-Dev Synthesis: Lead the integration of Figma-based
  design systems with AI-driven development to create
  seamless, automated handoff processes.
- Operational Excellence: Manage a ticket-driven workflow in
  Jira, optimizing team velocity and ensuring technical
  documentation meets enterprise-level requirements.
- Cross-Functional Partnership: Act as a bridge between
  Strategy, Creative, and Technology departments to ensure
  AI-powered solutions meet complex business objectives.
- Backend & Data Strategy: Oversee the implementation of
  robust backend services (Supabase, SQL/NoSQL) and secure
  authentication frameworks (OAuth, JWT) across the
  portfolio.
- Design and implement RESTful APIs and backend services using
  platforms like Supabase to support frontend functionality.
- Collaborate with UX Designers, AI Builders, and Innovation
  Leads throughout the development lifecycle.

Qualification

- 8–12+ years in full-stack development, with at least 3+
  years in a leadership or management capacity. Bonus points
  if you have experience working in a startup or rapid
  prototyping environment.
- Proven track record of implementing AI design tools(such as
  v0.dev, bolt.new, Lovable, or similar platforms) and
  workflow automation (e.g., N8N) within a professional
  engineering team.
- Deep expertise in modern frameworks (React, Vue, Nuxt),
  TypeScript, and multiple backend languages including
  Python, PHP, and NodeJS.
- Expert-level knowledge of design systems, design tokens, and
  CSS frameworks (Tailwind) to ensure brand consistency at
  scale.
- Comprehensive understanding of cloud platforms (AWS, Azure,
  GCP), deployment workflows, and enterprise security
  patterns.
- Solid understanding of backend development, including API
  design (REST, GraphQL), server-side logic, and database
  management.
- Proficiency with authentication mechanisms (OAuth, JWT) and
  security best practices.
- Expert-level proficiency with Figma, including design system
  management and component libraries.
- A portfolio demonstrating not just individual code, but the
  leadership of complex, full-stack applications built using
  AI-augmented methodologies.
- Strong attention to detail and a passion for innovation.
- Ability to work in a fast-paced and dynamic environment.
