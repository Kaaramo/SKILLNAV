### Raisonnement classification (LLM upstream)

This is an Ontology Engineer role focused on designing
semantic models, knowledge graphs, and data schemas that
enable AI systems to function. The work involves building
the infrastructure and data architecture that supports AI
teams (contextual retrieval, RAG, entity linking) rather
than directly building, training, or deploying models
themselves. The role sits at the intersection of data
architecture and applied AI, providing the foundational
structure that makes data "ready for automation and AI."

## Description complète

About Applico Capital

Applico Capital is the leading venture capital firm focused
on the $8 trillion B2B distribution industry. Through our
learnings and understanding of the industry, we are building
a tech startup, currently in stealth, to solve the
industry's biggest problems as it comes to unlocking AI-
enabled synergies.

Our mandate is to leverage AI and modern technologies to
reimagine the role of the traditional distributor and
transform how the entire industry operates.

We are looking for highly technical builders who thrive in
entrepreneurial, scrappy, and collaborative environments.

About the Role:

We’re seeking an Ontology Engineer to help design and
implement the semantic and structural backbone of this new
data platform.

You’ll model how products, suppliers, customers, and
transactions interconnect — defining the schemas,
relationships, and standards that make enterprise data
interoperable, trustworthy, and ready for automation and AI.

This role sits at the intersection of data architecture,
knowledge representation, and applied AI. You’ll work
closely with data engineers, architects, and AI teams to
ensure every dataset aligns to a consistent, machine-
understandable model that scales across multiple
distributors and business systems.

Key Responsibilities:

- Design and maintain domain ontologies and semantic schemas
  that describe key business entities (e.g., product,
  supplier, customer, location, transaction)
- Map and align internal data structures (ERP, CRM, PIM, CMS)
  to open data standards and taxonomies (e.g., schema.org,
  GS1, UNSPSC, ETIM)
- Build and manage knowledge graph models and associated
  metadata structures
- Define and enforce data modeling conventions, relationships,
  and validation rules (RDF, OWL, SHACL, JSON-LD)
- Partner with data engineers and architects to embed semantic
  models into pipelines and APIs
- Contribute to data governance, lineage, and interoperability
  practices across business domains
- Collaborate with AI teams to support contextual retrieval,
  entity linking, and enrichment using LLMs and embeddings
- Evaluate and adopt open-source tools for ontology
  management, graph storage, and reasoning (e.g., Protégé,
  Neo4j, Stardog, Weaviate, ArangoDB)
- Help design developer-facing documentation and schemas to
  make complex data understandable and reusable

Requirements

- 5+ years of experience in ontology engineering, data
  modeling, or knowledge graph development
- Proficiency with semantic web standards and technologies
  (RDF, OWL, SHACL, JSON-LD, SPARQL)
- Experience designing or maintaining domain ontologies or
  taxonomies for real-world business applications
- Familiarity with enterprise data integration (ERP, CRM, PIM,
  MDM, CMS) and how to model heterogeneous data
- Knowledge of open data standards such as schema.org, GS1,
  ETIM, UNSPSC, or eCl@ss
- Hands-on experience with graph databases (Neo4j, ArangoDB,
  Weaviate, Ontotext GraphDB, or similar)
- Strong understanding of data lineage, governance, and
  interoperability principles
- Experience collaborating with data engineers and AI/ML teams
- Bonus: exposure to vector search, RAG pipelines, or LLM-
  based enrichment
- Excellent communication skills — able to explain semantic
  concepts to non-technical audiences

Why Join Us

- Build the semantic foundation powering one of the most
  ambitious AI and data transformations in B2B commerce
- Work alongside experts in data architecture, AI engineering,
  and automation
- Help define how modern enterprises structure and share data
  in an open, extensible way
- Operate with startup agility inside a large, stable, and
  forward-thinking business

Preferred Stack

- Standards: RDF, OWL, SHACL, JSON-LD, schema.org, GS1, ETIM
- Databases: Neo4j, Weaviate, ArangoDB, Ontotext GraphDB,
  Stardog
- Languages: Python, SPARQL, SQL
- Tools: Protégé, dbt, Airbyte, Dagster/Prefect
- AI & Enrichment (Optional): LangChain, LangGraph, DSPy,
  OpenAI APIs
