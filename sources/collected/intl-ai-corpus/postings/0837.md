Company Description

EsyConnect is a prominent and innovative job portal that has
established itself as a leading platform for connecting job
seekers with employers. With a user-friendly interface and a
comprehensive range of features, EsyConnect offers a
seamless and efficient job search experience for individuals
looking for employment opportunities and companies seeking
talented professionals.

The primary objective of EsyConnect is to bridge the gap
between job seekers and employers by providing a centralized
platform where both parties can connect, communicate, and
collaborate effectively. The platform boasts a vast database
of job listings across various industries and sectors,
catering to a wide range of job preferences and
qualifications.

Job Description

We are seeking a talented and innovative AI/ML Engineer to
join our team in Navi Mumbai, India. As an AI/ML Engineer,
you will be at the forefront of developing cutting-edge
artificial intelligence and machine learning solutions that
drive our company's technological advancements.

- Design, develop, and implement machine learning models and
  algorithms to solve complex business problems
- Collaborate with cross-functional teams to identify and
  prioritize AI/ML opportunities
- Preprocess and clean large datasets, perform feature
  engineering, and conduct exploratory data analysis
- Develop and maintain scalable ML pipelines for model
  training, evaluation, and deployment
- Optimize existing ML models for improved performance and
  efficiency
- Stay up-to-date with the latest advancements in AI/ML
  technologies and implement best practices
- Contribute to the development of AI/ML strategies and
  roadmaps
- Mentor junior team members and share knowledge through
  documentation and presentations

Qualifications

- Master's or Ph.D. in Computer Science, Machine Learning, or
  a related field
- 3+ years of experience in AI/ML development
- Strong proficiency in Python and experience with R or Java
- Expertise in machine learning frameworks such as TensorFlow,
  PyTorch, and scikit-learn
- Experience with deep learning techniques, natural language
  processing, and computer vision
- Familiarity with big data technologies like Hadoop and Spark
- Proficiency in working with cloud platforms (AWS, Azure, or
  GCP)
- Strong understanding of statistical analysis and
  mathematical concepts
- Experience with data preprocessing, feature engineering, and
  model deployment
- Proven track record of implementing and deploying ML models
  in production environments
- Excellent problem-solving skills and attention to detail
- Strong communication skills and ability to explain complex
  concepts to non-technical stakeholders
- Experience with version control systems, preferably Git
- Knowledge of current AI/ML trends and best practices
- Relevant certifications (e.g., AWS Machine Learning
  Specialty, Google Cloud Professional Machine Learning
  Engineer) are a plus
