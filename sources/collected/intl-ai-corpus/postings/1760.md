This is a Staff Backend Engineer role on the Conversational
AI team, but the work focuses on building infrastructure,
APIs, and systems that support AI rather than directly
building models. The role involves working with the Data
Science team to leverage MLMs in tools like DataRobot, but
the engineer's primary responsibilities are backend
architecture, API design, and platform scalability rather
than model development, fine-tuning, or deploying LLMs
directly.
