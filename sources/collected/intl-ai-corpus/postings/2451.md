About our Company

We’re a small team of engineers, creatives, and researchers
building new kinds of interactive experiences powered by AI.
We’re launching The Interface: a platform for expressive,
real-time characters that people can talk to and explore
shared worlds alongside. It’s a mix of entertainment and
social.

We just wrapped up YC (S25) and raised a sizeable seed round
from top investors. Max (CEO) studied MechE at MIT,
previously led AI initiatives at McKinsey; Peyton (CTO) is
an MIT CS grad and former quant at Citadel. We’re setting up
in NYC — a hacker house-style space — and moving quickly
toward launch.

About the Role

We’re looking for a Founding AI Engineer to build the
intelligence layer behind our real-time characters. You’ll
be developing low-latency, expressive AI systems — powering
voice, personality, and narrative consistency — so that
users can have rich, dynamic interactions with characters.

This is a highly creative engineering role: you’ll run
experiments across voice models, prompting strategies, and
generative systems; help define how characters maintain
memory and personality; and ensure everything runs in real
time with minimal latency.

What You’ll Do

- Build and optimize real-time voice models and pipelines for
  natural, low-latency speech
- Develop personality systems, context management, and
  narrative consistency tools
- Experiment with prompting, fine-tuning, and custom model
  integration
- Explore generative techniques (e.g. diffusion, multimodal
  tools) to launch new product features
- Collaborate with rendering engineers to tightly connect
  voice, visuals, and behavior
- Create metrics and frameworks for evaluating reliability,
  latency, and engagement
- Prototype and ship AI features directly into the product
  with fast iteration cycles

You Might Be a Fit If You

- Have 3–6+ years of experience in ML/AI engineering
  (especially real-time systems)
- Are comfortable with voice models / conversational AI
- Have worked with prompting, fine-tuning, or custom model
  deployment
- Understand how to balance performance, cost, and latency in
  production AI systems
- Have experience with generative models (text, voice, image,
  or multimodal)
- Are excited by fast-paced, hands-on experimentation in new
  AI applications
- Enjoy building at the intersection of engineering and
  creativity

Why Join Us

- You’ll be one of the first 5 team members
- You’ll work directly with the founders (MIT backgrounds,
  bias to action)
- We just raised a sizeable seed round and are backed by top
  investors
- You’ll own the intelligence layer of our characters and
  product
- We’re building an entertainment platform and want to have
  fun doing it
- You’ll help shape the foundation of how AI feels, sounds,
  and interacts in real time
