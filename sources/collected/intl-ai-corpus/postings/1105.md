### Raisonnement classification (LLM upstream)

This role focuses on traditional computer vision and
perception systems including SLAM, sensor fusion,
calibration, multi-view geometry, and localization using
cameras, LiDAR, and IMUs. The work involves implementing
GPU-enabled solutions for detection, segmentation, and
localization on embedded platforms, which aligns with
classical ML/CV rather than generative AI or LLMs.

## Responsabilités

- Develop innovative perception systems for manufacturing automation, mobile devices, cabin features, and flight-deck automation
- Own end-to-end development of perception pipelines for robotic and mobile platforms including mobile robots, hand-held, and airborne systems
- Implement GPU-enabled solutions for indoor and outdoor localization, mapping, detection, and segmentation using cameras, LiDAR, IMUs, and satellite navigation
- Design perception rigs and associated lifecycle software including calibration and health management for field applications
- Use rapid-prototyping techniques to model and simulate perception systems with software and hardware in the loop testing
- Collaborate with ML teams to produce scalable perception solutions suitable for large datasets while minimizing labeling needs
