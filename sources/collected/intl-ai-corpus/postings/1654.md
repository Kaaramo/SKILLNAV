Mission

Architect the company’s proprietary “SaaS‑Killer” Agentic AI
Stack—the intellectual backbone that will replace external
platforms and secure our long-term technology sovereignty.

1. The “Total Replacement” Roadmap

I. Agentic Claims Adjudication (SaaS Replacement)

Objective: Build an in-house, AI-native Claims Management
System to replace third‑party CMS platforms. Execution:

- Design backend pipelines to ingest and validate medical data
  via NHCX.
- Automate clinical scrutiny and rule-based adjudication using
  agentic workflows.Outcome:
- Straight-Through Processing (STP) with near-zero manual
  intervention.
- Decommissioning of legacy SaaS workbenches.

II. Predictive FWA & Real-Time Risk Sovereignty

Objective: Replace external Fraud, Waste & Abuse (FWA)
platforms with an in-house predictive risk engine.
Execution:

- Build scoring models for every pre-auth and claim using
  proprietary fraud signatures.
- Develop a real-time “risk brain” that analyzes patterns at
  the point of care.Outcome:
- A live Provider Trust Score that auto-triggers approvals or
  investigations.
- Zero dependency on third-party FWA suites

III. Autonomous Provider Network & Portal

Objective: Build a proprietary “Network Control Tower” as a
replacement for external hospital portals and network tools.
Execution:

- Architect secure, bidirectional data interoperability using
  HL7 FHIR and ABDM standards.
- Enable automated onboarding, tariff updates, and network
  insights.Outcome:
- Dynamic package pricing, automated negotiations, and self-
  updating network data—powered by agentic backends.
