### Raisonnement classification (LLM upstream)

This is an infrastructure/platform role supporting AI
systems rather than building AI models directly. The focus
is on architecting and operating the underlying platforms
(Kubernetes, cloud, CI/CD) that enable AI workloads, not on
training models, fine-tuning LLMs, or implementing AI
algorithms.
