## Description complète

Who Is Nisos?

Nisos is the human risk management company specializing in
unmasking threats before they escalate. We are a trusted
advisor who operates as an extension of security,
intelligence, legal, and human resource teams to protect
their people and business. Our intelligence-led solutions
help enterprises make critical decisions, manage human risk,
and drive real world consequences for digital threats.

For more information, visit: https://www.nisos.com.

What Is The Opportunity?

Join an AI/ML team designing and shipping production ML
systems that combine Large Language Models (LLMs),
retrieval-augmented generation (RAG), and modern agent
frameworks for multi-step decision making. You will help
turn massive, noisy data into actionable intelligence for
analysts and customers.

What Are The Responsibilities?

- Integrate and fine-tune LLMs for natural-language
  understanding, reasoning, and multi-step tool
  orchestration.
- Build agentic workflows (e.g. using LangGraph) to coordinate
  complex tasks across data sources and services.
- Architect and implement AI/ML features for pattern
  recognition, entity extraction, and automated intelligence
  gathering at scale.
- Develop scalable data pipelines and streaming architectures
  that process billions of heterogeneous data points.
- Create and optimize vector databases and retrieval systems
  for semantic search and RAG.
- Deliver production-ready APIs and microservices supporting
  real-time analysis and model inference.
- Own model deployment and monitoring using modern ML/LLMOps
  practices; drive continuous improvement in accuracy,
  latency, and cost.
- Evaluate LLM systems with offline metrics, golden test sets,
  prompt A/B tests, latency & cost optimisation.
- Collaborate with analysts and product teams to translate
  intelligence needs into robust ML solutions.

Who You Are

- Expert proficiency in Python, with experience of at least
  one of deep learning frameworks, PyTorch and TensorFlow
- Hands-on with LangChain, LangGraph, AutoGen or similar agent
  LLM frameworks
- Deep experience with prompt engineering, foundation model
  API integration and LLM finetuning
- Expertise in RAG systems with vector databases (Pinecone,
  Weaviate) and embedding technologies
- Strong experience with LLMOps practices for observability
  and traceability and LLM evaluation.
- Deep understanding of distributed systems, microservices,
  and real-time streaming architectures
- Strong containerization and infrastructure skills (Docker,
  ECS, Terraform, CloudFormation)
- Experience building production AI systems with strong
  safety, privacy, and ethical practices
- Familiarity with computer vision and NLP techniques for
  OSINT
- Understanding of adversarial AI and security implications

Required Education and Experience

- Degree in Computer Science, AI/ML, or equivalent experience
- Substantive experience in building AI/ML products at scale
  with proven experience in LLMs, RAGs or Agentic AI
  solutions
- Proven experience shipping production AI systems at scale
- Track record of building systems that process and analyze
  large-scale unstructured data
- Experience in security, intelligence, or OSINT platforms
  preferred

Where will you work?

- Hybrid office schedule: employees balance working remotely
  and working at our office in Belfast City Centre.
- Flexible schedule: employees generally work normal business
  hours. We routinely meet in the afternoon with US-based
  colleagues, but we rarely schedule meetings earlier than 8
  am or past 6 pm local time.

What equipment will you use?

The position requires use of Macbooks and MacOS. We ensure
you can work well from home by providing Macbook Pros,
monitors, peripherals, and noise-canceling headphones.
