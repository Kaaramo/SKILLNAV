### Raisonnement classification (LLM upstream)

This SDET role is specifically titled for "Agentic AI" and
involves testing multi-agent LLM systems, prompt validation,
response evaluation, hallucination detection, and agent
orchestration. While the core role is test engineering, the
focus is directly on evaluating and testing AI/LLM/agent
systems — working hands-on with non-deterministic AI
components. This qualifies as ai-first because the engineer
is directly interfacing with and validating agentic AI
systems, not merely building infrastructure for others who
work on AI.

Who you are:

TraceLink is looking for talented full-time software

Responsibilities :

The Software Development Engineer in Test assists in
building and executing automated tests for

the Company's software products. The SDET will build testing
tools to enable automated testing of

the TraceLink platform and application.

- Execute tests on the TraceLink platform and application.
- Extend and maintain automation frameworks.
- Prepare and present clear reports on defect findings that
  allow developers to quickly and accurately reproduce
  problems and allow the Quality Engineering team to verify
  that fixes are complete.
- Verify defect fixes.
- Build tooling for automated testing of the TraceLink
  platform and application.
- Programmatically interface testing tools with the TraceLink
  platform and application.
- Create testing tools that can be configured and used by non-
  developers and can simulate real-world transaction volumes
  involving potentially millions of transactions per day.
- Provide high-level analysis to apply appropriate
  technologies and architecture to solve conceptual problems
- Research and remain informed of new technology and tools,
  and how such technologies could be applied at TraceLink.
- Work closely with engineers building the core TraceLink
  product to ensure that testability concerns are factored
  into the design of the product.
