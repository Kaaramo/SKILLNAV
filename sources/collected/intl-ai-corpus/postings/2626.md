## Description complète

Position Overview

Atlas Obscura is building a platform that helps millions of
people discover the world's most extraordinary places and
experiences. We're looking for a scrappy, design-obsessed
engineer who builds consumer products the way great product
teams do — fast, feel-first, and AI-to-the-core.

This isn't a traditional engineering role. We don't need
someone who's spent years maintaining legacy systems. We
need someone who wakes up thinking about how to make a
product feel magical, reaches for AI tools before reaching
for boilerplate, and ships things that make people go "wait,
how did they build that so fast?"

You'll report to the SVP, Head of Engineering & Technology
and work closely with Product and Design to build our new
native mobile app from the ground up — alongside our web
experience.

Who You Are

You're an AI-native builder. Claude, Codex, or Cursor aren't
novelties for you. They're how you prototype, build,
refactor, and ship. You've probably already built things
with AI that have surprised people.

You're a native mobile developer first. You know Swift and
understand what makes an iOS app feel genuinely great versus
one that feels like a website in a wrapper.

You care deeply about how things feel. You've shipped
consumer apps and sweated the details: animation curves,
responsiveness, and the difference between a tap that feels
good and one that doesn't. You think like a designer even if
your title has never said so.

You move fast and you like it that way. You're not waiting
for a perfect spec. You prototype, learn, test, and iterate.
You've probably iterated on something multiple times in the
time it would take someone else to finish the first version.

You come from the startup world. You know what it means to
do a lot with a little. You're scrappy in the good sense:
resourceful, low-ego, outcome-focused.

What You’ll Build

- A new native iOS app from scratch — this is a greenfield
  opportunity
- AI-powered product experiences that feel like the future,
  not like a chatbot bolted on
- Consumer-facing features on mobile that delight millions of
  people
- Fast prototypes that go from idea to something real in hours
  or days
- Brand partnership and editorial experiences that look great
  and work beautifully

What We’re Looking For

- You build with AI by default. Claude, Codex, Cursor, agents,
  and orchestrators are your everyday toolkit
- Strong native iOS development in Swift — this is a must
- React Native or similar experience for cross-platform
  flexibility
- You have shipped at least one app to the App Store — you
  know the submission process, crash analytics, push
  notifications, and what it actually takes to get something
  live in people's hands
- You think like a designer: obsessed with feel, flow, and
  delight
- You're strong on front-end and comfortable enough on the
  back end to not be blocked, but you don't need to be an
  infrastructure expert
- You've worked at a startup or early-stage product team
- You can prototype something compelling faster than most
  people can write a spec
- NYC-based or open to it (we're hybrid)

Nice to Have

- Android / Kotlin experience
- Experience with AWS and basic DevOps
- Experience building APIs or working with geospatial data

Experience Level

We genuinely don't care how many years are on your resume.
If you've shipped beautiful consumer apps using AI-
accelerated workflows, you're exactly who we're looking for.

Our Stack

Swift · React Native/Hotwire · AI coding agents (Claude,
Codex, Cursor) · Ruby on Rails · Postgres · AWS · Tailwind ·
Redis

About Atlas Obscura

Atlas Obscura is a community platform for travelers looking
to explore the world’s hidden wonders. Through our website,
mobile app, books, podcasts and other channels, we celebrate
the world's most curious places, extraordinary experiences,
and passionate communities. We blend high-quality editorial
content with public submissions to create a unique platform
that we are building out to feature more planning, memory
sharing, and real-time travel tools.

- A collaborative culture that values curiosity, creativity,

- Competitive salary of $115,000-$120,000 commensurate with
  experience
- Comprehensive health, dental, and vision insurance

Atlas Obscura is committed to building a diverse team that
