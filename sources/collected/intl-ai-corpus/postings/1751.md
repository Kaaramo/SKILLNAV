Seekho, a Series B, fast-scaling startup , is redefining

- Drive Seekho’s AI direction from an engineering lens

  error analysis)
- Rapid prototyping skills — ideas to POCs in days
- Applied research mindset — signal > hype
- Production awareness — latency, cost, guardrails, monitoring
- Strong Python + modern AI stack (OpenAI/Anthropic APIs,
  vector DBs, orchestration frameworks)

  decisions
