## Description complète

About Hypha

Hypha is an AI-native platform transforming asset management
across the full lifecycle—acquisition, management, and exit.
Focused on healthcare facilities and multifamily real
estate, we're revolutionizing the industry through three key
pillars:

- Observability: Providing real-time visibility into asset
  performance, deal progress, and market conditions for all
  stakeholders
- Intelligence: Enabling AI-driven multidimensional
  optimization with risk scores, tax strategies, and
  benchmarking
- Orchestration: Creating seamless workflows and a single
  source of truth to eliminate inefficiencies

Role Overview

As a Backend Engineer, Applied AI at Hypha, you’ll build and
improve the AI systems that power the core of the platform.
This includes designing and operating document processing
pipelines, building extraction agents, developing entity
resolution and computed attribute systems, and shaping the
conversational query interface that allows users to interact
with complex data.

You’ll work hands-on with LLMs and agent frameworks to
design multi-step workflows that transform unstructured
information into structured, actionable insights. A large
part of the role is building the infrastructure that makes
these AI systems reliable, observable, and performant as
they operate at scale.

We’re hiring across multiple experience levels for this
role, from engineers early in their careers to more senior
engineers with deeper systems and architecture experience.
Level and compensation will be determined through the
interview process.

Core Competencies

- Design and build AI agent workflows — document extraction,
  entity resolution, and data structuring
- Build reliable backend infrastructure — queues, pipelines,
  and orchestration using Inngest and Supabase
- Work with PostgreSQL at depth — schema design, query
  optimization, and data modeling for complex financial data
- Instrument, monitor, and debug AI pipelines — understanding
  failure modes and building guardrails
- Develop and optimize prompt chains, tool-use patterns, and
  retrieval strategies
- Collaborate with product and frontend engineers to expose AI
  capabilities through the product

Ideal Candidate

- Strong backend fundamentals — You're experienced with
  Node.js/TypeScript, PostgreSQL, and building reliable
  server-side systems. You understand concurrency, queuing,
  and data pipeline design.
- Applied AI experience — You've built products with LLMs;
  whether that's agent architectures, RAG systems,
  extraction pipelines, or tool-use patterns. You understand
  the practical challenges of working with non-deterministic
  systems.
- Systems thinking — You can reason about how components
  interact at scale. You think about failure modes, retries,
  observability, and cost when designing AI workflows.
- Pragmatic engineering — You ship working systems, not
  research prototypes. You know when to optimize and when
  good enough is good enough.
- AI-fluent — You use AI coding tools daily and know how to
  wield them effectively as a multiplier on top of strong
  fundamentals.

What Sets You Apart

- Experience with workflow orchestration tools (Inngest,
  Temporal, Step Functions)
- Experience with OCR, document processing, or financial data
  extraction
- Familiarity with OpenAI and Anthropic APIs, function
  calling, and structured outputs
- Early-stage startup experience

Why Join Hypha

- Be at the cutting edge of applied AI
- Have a large scope and massive impact on the company
- Work directly with the leadership team
- Strong career growth opportunities as we scale
- Competitive compensation package: $180-$225k base salary and
  equity

If you're ready to help build the future of asset management
and are excited by the challenge of bringing AI-driven
solutions to complex financial workflows, we want to hear
from you.
