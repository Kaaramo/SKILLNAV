## Description complète

At MCG, we lead the healthcare community to deliver patient-
focused care. We have a mission-driven team of talented
physicians and technical experts developing our evidence-
based content and innovating our products to accelerate
improvements in healthcare. If you are driven to enhance the
US healthcare system, MCG is eager to have you join our
team. We cultivate a work environment that nurtures personal
and professional growth, and this is a thrilling time to
become a part of our organization. With dynamic roles that
offer meaningful impact, you'll be able to fully realize
your potential. Plus, you'll enjoy world-class benefits and
the security, stability, and resources of our parent
company, Hearst, with over 100 years of experience.

As a Senior Gen AI Engineer, Salesforce Platform you will
lead the development of AI-powered solutions across our
Salesforce platform to streamline end-to-end business
operations. Partnering with the Manager of Salesforce
Operations and the Principal AI Architect, you will
implement orchestrated AI agents using Salesforce Einstein,
Agentforce, and DataCloud, and deliver secure, scalable
solutions via Apex, Visualforce, Lightning Web Components,
and robust APIs.

You will leverage generative AI to enhance efficiency,
reduce manual work, and create smarter systems and workflows
across our organization.  Candidates with a deep
understanding of business operations and workflows are
strongly preferred.  You will play a critical role in
delivering AI solutions and frameworks that drive
transformative business value.  This is a unique opportunity
for full-stack development to experiment and solve for
multiple use cases, as well as an opportunity to help
educate business stakeholders on the power of AI solutions.

You will:

- Design, develop, and deploy generative AI solutions that
  modernize business operations—automating workflows,
  surfacing insights, and delivering intelligent
  recommendations with a balance of speed, functionality,
  and long-term scalability.
- Build, integrate, and operationalize AI agents and
  automations using Salesforce Agentforce (skills, actions,
  orchestration, guardrails) embedded within Sales, Service,
  and CPQ workflows.
- Implement Einstein/Agentforce solutions, including prompt
  flows, tools, and custom actions grounded in Salesforce
  Data Cloud for personalized retrieval and contextual
  experiences.
- Develop and secure APIs (REST/SOAP, Platform Events,
  Pub/Sub) to connect Salesforce with internal and external
  systems such as CPQ, CLM, provisioning, licensing, ERP,
  and finance platforms.
- Engineer data interoperability across systems—collaborating
  with architects and data teams to break down silos, build
  ETL pipelines, and establish automated data workflows.
- Build and deploy retrieval-augmented generation (RAG)
  patterns within Salesforce, implementing data indexing,
  grounding, safety measures, and evaluation loops.
- Collaborate closely with the Principal Architect and
  Principal AI Engineer to translate high-level business
  objectives into production-ready AI-powered solutions,
  ensuring alignment with architectural and engineering best
  practices.
- Prototype rapidly to validate new generative AI use cases
  and transition successful proofs-of-concept into stable,
  scalable, maintainable production applications.
- Contribute to full-stack development efforts—from API design
  to front-end UI—when implementing AI features across
  various business systems.
- Drive and contribute to CI/CD pipelines, code reviews, and
  release management using modern tooling (SFDX, GitHub
  Actions, Azure DevOps, etc.).
- Ensure compliance, data security, and governance across all
  AI and integration solutions, including considerations for
  PII, data residency, consent management, and auditability.
- Partner with business stakeholders (technical and non-
  technical) to understand requirements, gather feedback,
  and continuously improve AI-driven capabilities.
- Work alongside data scientists and AI specialists to
  evaluate and optimize prompt strategies, fine-tune models,
  and implement organization-specific AI enhancements.
- Lead knowledge sharing and enablement, mentoring teams and
  fostering adoption of AI technologies throughout the
  organization.
- Monitor, evaluate, and iterate on AI solution performance to
  ensure quality, accuracy, and measurable business value.
- Stay current on industry advancements in AI, automation, and
  Salesforce innovation, integrating relevant learnings into
  the organization’s technology roadmap.
