This is a Data Engineer role focused on building data
pipelines and infrastructure (ETL/ELT with Dagster, dbt,
Snowflake) to power an existing optimization engine. The
role supports ML systems by providing data infrastructure
and platform capabilities, but does not involve building,
training, or deploying models directly.
