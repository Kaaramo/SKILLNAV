## Description complète

About Hypha

Hypha is an AI-native platform transforming asset management
across the full lifecycle—acquisition, management, and exit.
Focused on healthcare facilities and multifamily real
estate, we're revolutionizing the industry through three key
pillars:

- Observability: Providing real-time visibility into asset
  performance, deal progress, and market conditions for all
  stakeholders
- Intelligence: Enabling AI-driven multidimensional
  optimization with risk scores, tax strategies, and
  benchmarking
- Orchestration: Creating seamless workflows and a single
  source of truth to eliminate inefficiencies

Role Overview

We're looking for Software Engineers to help build Hypha

In this role you’ll build cutting-edge AI agents and work
closely with our leadership team, product manager and
designer to help translate customer needs and product
requirements into software.

Some of the challenging engineering problems we’re working
on include:

- Extracting structured data from unstructured at large scale
  and accuracy
- Agentic information retrieval over both structured and
  unstructured data
- Synthesizing structured and unstructured data into
  actionable business insights

Technology Stack

- Fullstack Typescript
- React
- Next.JS + Vercel
- PostgreSQL

Qualifications

Must have

- 3+ years of experience in software engineering
- TypeScript experience
- A proven track record of delivering mid to large impact
  features as the sole or lead developer
- Self-starter mentality with the ability to work in a fast-
  paced environment that values speed and iteration
- Comfortable with ambiguity—must be able to take vague
  requirements and clarify them to deliver the right
  intersection of quality and speed
- Strong communication skills including the ability to work
  well with non technical team members

Must have EITHER (or both!)

- Strong React & frontend development skills
- Experience building AI agents and/or working with LLM APIs

Ideal Candidate

- Values in-person collaboration and thrives in the energy of
  working alongside driven, high-performing teammates.
- Deeply product-minded—builds with clear awareness of user
  needs, business goals, and product impact.
- Relentless focus on impact—ruthlessly prioritizes and
  executes the most important work at any given time.
- Strong sense of ownership—takes personal responsibility for
  product quality and user outcomes.
- Embraces change—constantly refines their craft and
  integrates new tools (like AI) to amplify results.

Why Join Hypha

- Be at the cutting edge of applied AI
- Have a large scope and massive impact on the company
- Work directly with the leadership team
- Strong career growth opportunities as we scale
- Competitive compensation package: $175-$220k base salary and
  equity

If you're ready to help build the future of asset management
and are excited by the challenge of bringing AI-driven
solutions to complex financial workflows, we want to hear
from you.
