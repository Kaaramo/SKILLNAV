Position Details:

- Job Title: Third Party Risk (TPCR) Engineer
- Location:  Hyderabad - Hybrid
- Employment Type: Full-time

Role Purpose:

Chubb is seeking a skilled and experienced Third-Party Risk
Engineer to design, test, and help oversee the
implementation of innovative solutions what will enhance and
strengthen the third-party cyber risk process. The ideal
candidate will focus on leveraging advanced AI-driven
solutions to optimize TPRM control assessments, streamline
processes, and improve decision-making.

To be successful in this role, the candidate will need to
gain a strong understanding of the TPRM process which
includes reviewing the inherent risk ranking of suppliers,
responsible for completing assessments of higher risk third
parties, and managing issues to completion. The candidate
will be involved in multiple work streams across risk
disciplines with focus on third-party cyber security and
operational resilience.

Roles and Responsibilities:

- Engineer and enhance tools and processes which support the
  third party risk management program
- Identification of opportunities to incorporate AI into the
  TPRM program and processes.
- Perform and execute of TPRM assessments initiated by the
  business.
- Identify, track and manage issues and control deficiencies
  relating to Third Parties, including liaising with the
  business owners to support remediation activities.
- Assist with internal security reporting, including steering
  committees and updates for senior management.
- Provide support to the Third Party Cyber Risk Regional Lead
  and engage with the wider Global Information Security
  team.

Experience:

- Good knowledge of AI concepts and experience working with
  GPT models, particularly in constructing effective prompts
  and RAG framework. Familiarity with AI frameworks and
  protocols such as CrewAI, LangChain, MCP, and A2A is a
  plus.
- Prior on the job experience of process automation and re-
  engineering.
- Education: Degree in Computer Science, Information
  Technology, Cybersecurity, or a related field.
- Communication: Strong verbal and written communication
  skills, with an ability to work collaboratively in a team
  environment.
- Proactive Attitude: Demonstrated problem-solving skills and
  initiative taking in a dynamic work environment.
