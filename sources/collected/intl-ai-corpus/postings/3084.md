This is Adyen

Adyen provides payments, data, and financial products in a
single solution for customers like Meta, Uber, H&M, and
Microsoft - making us the financial technology platform of
choice. At Adyen, everything we do is engineered for

For our teams, we create an environment with opportunities

Engineer - ML/AI

At Adyen, we are the financial technology platform of choice
for the world's leading companies. Our Payment Solutions
team is the engine behind this, processing billions of
transactions while empowering businesses to create seamless
payment experiences. As Adyen and our merchants scale
globally, the complexity and volume of our operational
knowledge are growing exponentially.

Our team’s mission is to accelerate Adyen’s internal AI
transformation and make critical information and insights
instantly accessible so every team can make informed
decisions, when and where it matters most. We build the
targeted AI solutions and custom tools that our teams need
to thrive, filling the critical gaps. We're looking for an
entrepreneurial builder who can uncover these unique
operational challenges and deliver the high-impact products
that will redefine how Adyen works.

What you’ll do:

- Discover and Build: You will be an explorer within Adyen,
  proactively engaging with product and engineering teams to
  uncover their most critical challenges. You won't wait for
  instructions; you'll find high-impact opportunities and
  rapidly design and build AI prototypes (MVPs) to
  demonstrate value.
- Develop Strategic AI Products: You will own the end-to-end
  development of bespoke AI tools that solve problems unique
  to Adyen's scale. Whether it's optimizing merchant
  experience, enhancing pricing models, or improving
  internal workflows, you will build intelligent solutions.
- Drive the AI Roadmap: Functioning as an AI champion, you
  will translate ambiguous business problems into a tangible
  roadmap. You will own initiatives from ideation and
  stakeholder alignment through to deployment and impact
  assessment, focusing relentlessly on delivering measurable
  results.
- Lead and mentor: You will initiate, lead, and guide others
  in the full cycle of complex features or systems. You will
  proactively share knowledge, inspire other engineers
  through code reviews, and raise the bar on our software
  creation process within and beyond the team
- Push the Boundaries of Our Tech Stack: You will become an
  expert on our internal systems and external AI platforms,
  identifying their limits and building the custom
  enhancements and integrations that unlock their full
  potential for our specific payment-processing context.

Who you are:

- You have 5+ years of experience in a Data Science, Data
  Engineering, or Software Engineering role, with at least 2
  years dedicated to building and deploying production-ready
  GenAI models.
- You possess practical experience in building and deploying
  RAG systems using vector databases like Pinecone,
  Weaviate, or Qdrant, along with deep knowledge of LLM
  integration, embedding models, and semantic search
  techniques.
- You are a technical expert proficient in Python (LangChain,
  LangGraph or similar), SQL, and big data frameworks
  (Spark, Hadoop, Kafka).
- You have a strong understanding of software engineering best
  practices and MLOps principles, utilizing tools like Git,
  Airflow, CICD, Docker, and Kubernetes to ensure robust and
  scalable solutions, and you are capable of leading
  incident response and ensuring live environment
  reliability.
- You are a proactive self-starter who takes initiative on
  projects, from initial ideation and experimentation to
  final deployment and iteration.
- You are an exceptional communicator, capable of translating
  complex data-driven insights and outcomes to a diverse
  range of stakeholders.
- You possess an innovative and experimental mindset,
  embracing a "launch fast and iterate" approach to problem-
  solving and product development.
- You are a champion for a data-centric culture, skilled in
  advocating for best practices, ensuring documentation is
  complete and up-to-date, and driving continuous
  improvement within technical teams

Challenges you could potentially tackle :

- Engineering an NLP-powered chatbot to democratize data
  discovery across Adyen's data landscape, enabling users to
  query table metadata using natural language.
- Building a knowledge management system that ingests and
  indexes conversations from our primary communication tool,
  decreasing repetitive questions and making undocumented
  technical solutions and business logic discoverable.
- As we are in a discovery phase, we also expect you to find
  your own opportunities!
