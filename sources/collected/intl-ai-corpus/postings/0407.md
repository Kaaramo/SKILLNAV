About the Role:

Grade Level (for internal use):

12

Team: S&P Global Ratings

The role: Agentic AI Senior Software Engineer

Location: NJ or NY (2 days onsite a week)

Role Summary

This Senior role anchors the Agentic AI strategy within the
S&P Global Ratings Quality Engineering organization. You
will design and implement AI-driven solutions that transform
QE practices—enabling autonomous testing, predictive
insights, and intelligent decision-making.

As a hands-on technical architect, you will collaborate
across Engineering, Product, Data Science, and
Transformation teams to embed Agentic AI into the QE
lifecycle—without direct people management responsibilities.

Key Outcomes

- Architect and implement Agentic AI platforms for autonomous
  testing and defect triage.
- Integrate AI copilots and LLM-powered agents into QE
  workflows.
- Drive adoption of AI decision frameworks to enhance
  engineering effectiveness.
- Collaborate with customer experience advocates to align AI
  quality insights with customer journeys.
- Partner and embed AI maturity benchmarks (e.g., TMMi).
- Enable QE through intelligent environments, data
  observability, and ethical AI governance.

Strategic Responsibilities

- Define and execute the Agentic AI architecture roadmap for
  QE transformation.
- Evaluate and integrate LLMOps, GenAI, and autonomous agent
  frameworks.
- Lead AI experimentation labs to validate use cases and
  proofs of concept.
- Ensure AI solutions are scalable, secure, and compliant with
  enterprise standards.
- Stay current with advancements in Agentic AI, GenAI, and AI-
  powered DevSecOps.

Required Skills & Experience

- 15+ years in Quality Engineering or Software Engineering,
  with deep hands-on expertise in AI/ML.
- Proven experience in AI/ML applications for QE, including
  GenAI, LLMOps, and intelligent automation.
- Strong proficiency in AI model testing, data observability,
  and AI governance.
- Hands-on skills in Python, Azure AI, ML pipelines, and
  containerized environments.
- Familiarity with data privacy regulations, ethical AI, and
  secure engineering practices.
- Ability to work across Product, Engineering, CX, and
  Transformation teams.

Preferred Qualifications

- Experience in regulated industries (e.g., finance,
  healthcare, telecom).
- Certifications in AI/ML, Generative AI, or Enterprise AI
  Strategy.
- Knowledge of DevSecOps, TMMi, and AI adoption frameworks.

What’s in it for You?

- Lead the Agentic AI transformation within a global Quality
  Engineering function.
- Shape the future of AI-augmented enterprise quality, setting
  industry benchmarks.
- Drive measurable outcomes in efficiency, engineering
  effectiveness, and user-centric quality.
- Collaborate with global teams in a dynamic, inclusive, and
  innovation-driven environment.
