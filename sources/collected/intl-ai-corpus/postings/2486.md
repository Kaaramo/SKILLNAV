## Description complète

About Crosby AI

Crosby is an AI-first legal platform reimagining corporate
legal services from the ground up. We are a team of
technologists and legal experts who build proprietary
technology and human-in-the-loop workflows that meaningfully
improve how lawyers and machines work together — delivering
speed, consistency, and quality across high-stakes work.

Our systems review complex documents faster and with
exceptional accuracy, combining advanced AI with structured
legal expertise. Clients receive AI-powered redlines,
commentary, and negotiation guidance within hours, at a
predictable, volume-based price.

Backed by Sequoia, Index Ventures, and Bain Capital
Ventures, we're building the end-to-end contracting platform
for the next generation of fast-growing companies.

The Team

The Engineering team builds the core systems,
infrastructure, and developer tooling that power Crosby’s
AI-first platform. We work across backend, frontend, and AI
systems to enable fast iteration, high-quality outputs, and
reliable production systems.

We prioritize speed, leverage, and strong engineering
foundations — investing in systems that make the entire team
more effective.

The Role

As an AI Developer Experience Engineer at Crosby, you'll
play a critical role in building the internal systems that
enable our team to develop, evaluate, and ship AI features
quickly and reliably. You’ll focus on improving the
developer experience around LLMs, data, and experimentation
— creating tools and infrastructure that accelerate
iteration while maintaining quality.

You’ll partner closely with engineers, data scientists, and
researchers to streamline workflows across prompting,
evaluation, fine-tuning, and deployment. This role is for
someone who is obsessed with leverage — building systems
that make other engineers dramatically more productive.

Responsibilities

- Build AI developer tooling: Design and implement internal
  tools for prompt management, evaluation, experimentation,
  and model iteration.
- Improve iteration speed: Create systems that enable rapid
  testing, debugging, and comparison of model outputs across
  different approaches.
- Own evaluation infrastructure: Develop frameworks for
  benchmarking, regression testing, and monitoring AI system
  performance.
- Streamline data workflows: Build pipelines and tooling to
  support data collection, labeling, and feedback loops for
  model improvement.
- Support production systems: Partner with engineering teams
  to ensure AI features are reliable, observable, and easy
  to maintain in production.
- Enable the team: Identify bottlenecks in the development
  process and build systems that increase developer velocity
  and confidence.

Who You Are

- 3–7+ years of software engineering experience, ideally
  working on developer tools, infrastructure, or platform
  teams
- Strong proficiency in Python and experience building backend
  systems and internal tooling
- Hands-on experience working with LLMs, APIs (e.g., OpenAI,
  Anthropic), and modern AI workflows
- Experience building systems for experimentation, evaluation,
  or data pipelines
- Strong systems thinking with the ability to design tools
  that scale across teams
- Highly pragmatic with a focus on improving developer
  velocity and real-world outcomes
- Strong communicator who can work closely with engineers,
  researchers, and product teams
- Proactive, resourceful, and excited about building
  foundational systems in a fast-moving environment
