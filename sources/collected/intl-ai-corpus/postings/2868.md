## Description complète

Principal AI Engineer

Backbase has ushered in a new era of digital banking with
the global launch of its AI-powered Banking Platform,
recently lighting up Times Square. This milestone marks a
bold step in reshaping the digital banking
landscape—empowering banks to move beyond generative AI
experiments and into full-scale execution. By automating
critical operations and amplifying productivity across both
front and back offices, Backbase is focused on transforming
the promise of AI into measurable business impact.

To drive this vision forward, the Ecosystems department in
Backbase is building the Agentic AI Automation Platform—a
next-generation platform that leverages advanced agent-based
architectures to automate and optimize complex banking
workflows. We operate with a bold startup mindset
prioritizing speed, autonomy, and breakthrough innovation.

What We’re Looking For

We’re looking for a Senior AI Engineer with a passion for
building intelligent, context-aware agentic systems. You’ll
help shape the core of our Agentic AI Automation Platform
that orchestrates and automates complex agentic workflows to
deliver measurable outcomes for global banks.

You should be:

- 🚀 Experienced: Minimum 6 years in software engineering, with
  3+ years developing and delivering enterprise-grade AI
  applications.
- 🧠 Technically Strong: Proficient in Python, with deep hands-
  on experience in Agentic AI frameworks, architectures,
  design patterns, knowledge-base, AI-search, retrieval-
  augmented generation, context engineering, evaluation, and
  guardrailing.
- 🧩 Agentic Thinker: Well-versed in modern AI agent patterns
  including MCP, A2A, ACP. You understand how to build
  modular systems where agents collaborate, reason, and
  adapt to context dynamically.
- 🛠️ MLOps / LLMOps Savvy: You’re comfortable with model
  lifecycle management, prompt versioning, fine tuning,
  context management, agent monitoring, and AI
  experimentation using platforms like Azure AI Foundry,
  HuggingFace, and LangChain.
- 🌟 Startup Mindset: You're a self-starter—proactive,
  resourceful, curious, and comfortable moving fast.
- 🗣️ Excellent Collaborator: Strong communicator and
  documentation advocate who believes in sharing knowledge
  and building for scale.
