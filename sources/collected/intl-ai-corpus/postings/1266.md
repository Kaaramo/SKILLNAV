COMPANY OVERVIEW

KKR is a leading global investment firm that offers
alternative asset management as well as capital markets and
insurance solutions. KKR aims to generate attractive
investment returns by following a patient and disciplined
investment approach, employing world-class people, and
supporting growth in its portfolio companies and
communities. KKR sponsors investment funds that invest in
private equity, credit and real assets and has strategic
partners that manage hedge funds. KKR’s insurance
subsidiaries offer retirement, life and reinsurance products
under the management of Global Atlantic Financial Group.
References to KKR’s investments may include the activities
of its sponsored funds and insurance subsidiaries.

POSITION OVERVIEW

The Senior Digital / AI Engineer designs, builds, and scales
advanced digital and AI-driven solutions that create
measurable business impact. This role combines strong
software engineering skills with applied machine learning,
and modern cloud-native architectures. The Senior Engineer
acts as a technical leader, translating business problems
into robust, production-grade AI systems while mentoring
junior engineers and influencing platform standards.

Key responsibilities will include the following:

- New Requests/Builds
  - Collaborating on solutions with Solution Architects and
    other supporting IT areas.
- Provide technical advice and guidance to assigned business
  area teams.
- Be a consultant/analyst on strategic projects.
- Lead initiatives that span strategy and road map exercises,
  architecture definition, product selection, application
  and integration design and implementation.
- Responsible for the entire lifecycle of IT Solutions,
  including regular maintenance, patching, updates, and
  fixes.
- Manages all aspects of the technical activities (including
  software development requirements, architectural
  requirements, hardware, technical models, vendor
  interaction, technical designs, etc.) and ensures the
  solutions and applications align with the overall
  architecture and infrastructure.
- Participate in testing and verification of technical
  solutions.
- Communicate the effectiveness and task progress of the
  technical project team members.
- Design, develop, and deploy AI/ML-powered applications and
  digital platforms in production environments.
  - Implement end-to-end AI systems, including data ingestion,
    feature engineering, model training, evaluation,
    deployment, and monitoring.
  - Implement scalable APIs, microservices, and event-driven
    architectures supporting AI workflows.
  - Ensure solutions meet security, privacy, and compliance r
  - Establish engineering best practices: testing, CI/CD,
    observability, and documentation.
  - Contribute to reusable libraries, internal platforms, and
    reference architectures.
  - Apply machine learning, deep learning, NLP, or generative AI
    techniques where appropriate.
  - Work with structured and unstructured data at scale.
  - Collaborate with data scientists to productionize models and
    experiments.
  - Implement MLOps practices: versioning, model lifecycle
    management, drift detection, and retraining.
- General Support
  - Partner with product managers, business stakeholders, third-

  - Translate ambiguous business problems into technical
