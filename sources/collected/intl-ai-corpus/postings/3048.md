## Description complète

Foley is hiring an Applied AI Engineer to build and scale
AI-driven revenue systems within our Small Market segment.
This role sits at the intersection of AI, growth, and
revenue execution—designing systems that convert inbound
demand into measurable revenue outcomes.

You will work directly with Growth, Sales, and RevOps to
build production-grade systems that automate, augment, or
replace traditional sales workflows. This is a builder role
focused on shipping real systems, not strategy or
coordination.

What you’ll do

- Build AI-driven revenue systems that capture, qualify, and
  convert inbound demand into measurable revenue
- Design self-service and e-commerce experiences using AI to
  guide customers from interest to purchase
- Automate and augment sales workflows, reducing manual effort
  and accelerating deal velocity
- Develop multi-channel engagement systems across email, SMS,
  and web that adapt to user behavior in real time
- Own measurement and optimization, using data and AI to
  continuously improve conversion and funnel performance
- Partner cross-functionally with Growth, Sales, and RevOps to
  build scalable, production-ready systems

Who you are

- Demonstrated ability to build and ship real systems
  (portfolio, GitHub, or deployed work)
- Strong proficiency in Python (APIs, automation, data
  workflows)
- Experience building with LLMs or AI systems (agents, RAG,
  workflows, etc.)
- Ability to translate business and revenue problems into
  technical solutions
- Strong communication skills with technical and non-technical
  stakeholders

Nice-to-haves

- Experience with CRM or marketing automation platforms (e.g.,
  HubSpot, Salesforce)
- Experience building lead scoring, routing, or conversion
  systems
- Background in growth, marketing, sales, or RevOps
  environments
- Familiarity with AI orchestration frameworks (e.g.,
  LangChain, LangGraph, CrewAI)
- Experience with telemetry, observability, or performance
  tracking systems

Location: This role is primarily remote (US based) with the
