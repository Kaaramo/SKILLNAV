SII Group Morocco, a subsidiary of SII Group, is a Technology Consulting and Digital Services company created in 2008. We support major clients in adopting new technologies and driving innovation.

**Our Talents First**

We offer an inclusive, flexible (remote‑friendly), and fulfilling work environment across Casablanca, Oujda, and Rabat — where ideas and ambitions truly matter.

We are looking for a skilled and results‑driven **Data Analyst** able to deliver high‑quality dashboards, insights, and data models. The ideal candidate has strong technical mastery of Power BI and Qlik Sense, and can quickly transform data into clear, actionable outputs.

**Key Responsibilities**

- Develop interactive dashboards and reports using Power BI and Qlik Sense.
- Perform data analysis, data modeling, and KPI tracking.
- Transform raw data into clear, business‑oriented visual insights.
- Ensure data accuracy, consistency, and quality.
- Work closely with business teams to understand requirements and deliver rapid, high‑impact outputs.

**Required Technical Skills**

- Advanced expertise in Power BI (DAX, data models, interactive dashboards).
- Strong command of Qlik Sense (data loading, scripting, visualization).
- Solid understanding of data workflows, ETL concepts, and data quality.
- Experience with SQL is a plus.

**Profile**

- English‑speaking Data Analyst.
- Strong analytical mindset.
- Ability to deliver quickly in a fast‑paced environment.
