We are looking for a Senior Consultant – Forecaster / Data Scientist to support demand forecasting and replenishment activities for retail customers.

The role requires strong analytical thinking, hands-on technical capability, and the ability to identify trends, troubleshoot issues, and recommend continuous improvements to forecasting processes.
