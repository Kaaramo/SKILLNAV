**The Opportunity**

Altus Group is currently seeking a candidate in the position of a Real Estate **Associate Analyst** to join our Altus Global Service Center. The Associate Analyst will work within a dynamic team gaining experience in (but not limited to) commercial real estate data analytics.

The Associate Analyst will be responsible for the collection and organization of commercial real estate data, as part of our integrated team deliveries within our Intelligence as a Service solutions.

The role will afford the Associate Analyst opportunities to gain exposure to commercial real estate data and contribute to the reliability and accuracy of our database, enter information and play an active part in the generation of reports and presentations for the CRE market supporting Altus Group data products.

**Key Responsibilities:**

- Support in research, data collection, and ingestion of key commercial real estate data

- Responsible for the accuracy of information entered and responsible to review discrepancies in data received, request clarification or notify appropriate team member of issues related to data
- Proof entries as required and compile information to complete files from a variety of sources to reach a conclusion. Collaborate with team members to confirm information required and assist others in the delivery of their work as needed to ensure accurate and timely data entry.
