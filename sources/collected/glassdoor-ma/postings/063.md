We’re hiring a **Senior / Lead AI Engineer** to join **AytmaTech’s** outsourcing pool and lead AI-driven solution development on engagements for international clients. This is a fully remote, contract-based/permanent role focused on building, integrating, and deploying modern AI systems across a variety of business domains and products. Professional English communication (written & spoken) is required.

**What you’ll do**

- Serve as the primary AI engineering lead on client contract engagements (outsourced/embedded role).
- Design, build, and deploy AI-powered solutions, applications, and automation workflows for client projects.
- Develop and integrate machine learning, generative AI, and LLM-based features into web platforms, internal tools, and business systems.
- Build and optimize AI pipelines for data processing, model inference, evaluation, and monitoring.
- Work with structured and unstructured data sources to prepare datasets, improve quality, and support AI workflows.
- Implement prompt engineering, retrieval-augmented generation (RAG), embeddings, vector search, and agent-based workflows where applicable.
- Collaborate with backend, frontend, product, and business teams to translate requirements into scalable AI solutions.
- Evaluate AI models, tools, APIs, and frameworks to recommend practical and cost-effective implementations.
- Optimize performance, reliability, and operational efficiency of AI systems in production environments.
- Support deployment, testing, monitoring, and troubleshooting of AI-powered applications.
- Create technical documentation, implementation guidelines, and knowledge transfer materials for client teams.
- Provide clear status updates and lead communication with international stakeholders throughout engagements.

**Required (must-have)**

- 4–7 years of professional experience in software engineering, machine learning, AI engineering, or related roles.
- Strong programming skills in Python and experience building production-grade applications and APIs.
- Hands-on experience working with AI/ML frameworks, LLM APIs, or modern AI tooling ecosystems.
- Experience integrating AI services and models into real-world applications and workflows.
- Strong understanding of prompt engineering, embeddings, vector databases, retrieval systems, and LLM limitations.
- Experience working with data processing, APIs, asynchronous workflows, and backend integrations.
- Familiarity with model evaluation, testing, observability, and performance optimization.
